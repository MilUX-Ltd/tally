# Gary Linton Services International

> Specialist global supplier of CBRN, forensic, mobile laboratory and modular facility equipment to military, police and security customers.

## What they do

GLSI designs, sources and delivers specialist equipment packages and turnkey solutions for defence, law enforcement and security organisations in more than 60 countries. The company operates as a full turnkey provider, managing design, build, equipping, delivery, installation, commissioning and training against a client brief. It has direct experience supplying MOD-funded programmes including an urban training village and training tower built for the MOD, comprising 14 relocatable habitable units.

## Products and solutions

- **Mobile Laboratories** - BSL2 and BSL3 containerised mobile laboratories (20ft and 40ft configurations) for forensic, biosafety and field science applications.
- **CBRN Equipment** - Rapid deployment kits, PPE kits across all four protection levels, and decontamination equipment for police and military CBRN response.
- **Mobile Biometric Kit (MBS-01)** - Biometric identification station, biometric reader, and ID check unit for field identification operations.
- **Modular Training Village** - Relocatable habitable units configurable for urban operations training; previously supplied to the MOD.
- **Crime Scene Investigation Equipment** - Forensic and police equipment packages including disaster victim identification (DVI) capability.
- **Mobile Protective Shield** - Personal protection solutions for law enforcement and security operations.
