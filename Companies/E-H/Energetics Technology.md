# Energetics Technology Ltd

> Specialist energetics and pyrotechnics company designing, developing, testing and manufacturing explosive, propellant and blast protection products for defence and security customers.

## What they do

Energetics Technology Ltd (ETL) researches, develops and manufactures energetic materials products and blast mitigation equipment. Capability areas include high-explosive and pyrotechnic compositions, pyrotechnic payloads and actuators, rocket propellants, cable cutters, gas generators, igniters and delays, and IED-resistant containment and mitigation products. Customers include defence manufacturers, EOD teams, police and special forces. ETL is part of the Etienne Lacroix Group, a French defence and pyrotechnics group.

## Products and solutions

- **Pyrotechnic payloads and actuators** - designed for integration into ordnance and weapons systems.
- **Cable cutters** - electrically initiated cutters for braided steel, armoured cabling and fibre-optic lines.
- **Rocket propellants** - melt-castable, low-cost formulations designed for high-volume production.
- **Blast mitigation products** - IED isolation units, bomb-resistant litter bins and detonator containment units for use in public spaces and by EOD and police teams.
- **CIED training and EOD equipment** - training aids and equipment for counter-IED and explosive ordnance disposal roles.
- **40mm medium-velocity ammunition** - design and evaluation of medium-velocity ammunition for small arms and support weapons.
