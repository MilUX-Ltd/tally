# EWST Ltd

> Electronic warfare simulation hardware manufacturer producing multi-spectral threat and countermeasure simulators for test, evaluation, and training.

## What they do

EWST Ltd (Electronic Warfare Simulation Technologies) designs and manufactures multi-spectral threat and countermeasure simulators used for EW test, evaluation, and training by naval, air force, army, and civilian defence organisations across 30 countries. The company has over 40 years of product history, operating previously as EW Simulation Technology Ltd under Ultra Electronics before being carved out of the Ultra I&C (Cobham) portfolio. It was registered as an independent company in April 2023 under the name Ultra EWST Limited, then renamed EWST Ltd in May 2025 following a change of ownership to EWST Holdings Ltd. Over 100 simulation systems have been delivered since 1984. The operational base is Building A8, Cody Technology Park, Farnborough, Hampshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Chameleon** - radar target generator and ECM signal generator; multi-channel, multi-DRFM architecture; 500 MHz to 40 GHz coverage; supports hardware-in-the-loop and radiating applications.
- **MERTS (Mobile EW and Radar Test System)** - fully mobile system integrating ECM (Chameleon) and radar threat generation; includes antenna azimuth/elevation positioner with TV camera and GPS tracking.
- **PRS (Portable Radar Simulator)** - portable radar simulation system.
- **RSS8000** - radar threat simulation system.
- **EMES9000** - high-fidelity electromagnetic spectrum simulation system.
