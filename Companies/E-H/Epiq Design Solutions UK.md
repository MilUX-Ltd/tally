# Epiq Design Solutions UK

> Commercial off-the-shelf software-defined radio platforms for electronic warfare, signals intelligence, and counter-UAS applications.

## What they do

Epiq Design Solutions is the UK subsidiary of Epiq Solutions Inc., a US defence-technology company founded in 2009 with heritage from the former radio division of G3 Technologies. The company designs and manufactures COTS software-defined radio transceivers and rugged embedded computing platforms for RF sensing, signal processing, electronic warfare, and uncrewed platform payloads. The UK entity, registered in February 2023, serves as the company's UK and European market presence. The parent holds a portfolio of SDR product families addressing SWaP-constrained defence mission requirements, electronic attack, spectrum awareness, direction finding, and signals intelligence.

## Products and solutions

- **Matchstiq Z4** - Compact SDR transceiver targeting small uncrewed platform payloads, electronic attack and jamming, and counter-UAS applications; announced January 2026.
- **Matchstiq X40** - SDR platform combining Analog Devices AD9084 MxFE with AMD Versal Adaptive SoC, supporting up to 2 GHz instantaneous bandwidth for AI/ML applications at the RF edge.
- **Matchstiq V40** - High-bandwidth SDR variant for advanced signal processing.
- **SOSA/MOSA-aligned VPX solutions** - Open-architecture embedded computing for defence integration.
- Spectrum awareness, signal classification, direction finding, and geolocation software.
