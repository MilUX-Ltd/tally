# Ghost Shift
> UK boutique consultancy providing online intelligence collection, influence operations design and offensive cyber advisory, led by former UK Intelligence Community and law enforcement practitioners.
## What they do
Ghost Shift specialises in online intelligence gathering and influence operations. The leadership team brings over 20 years of experience from the UK Intelligence Community, offensive cyber and international law enforcement, including coalition operations. Service lines cover intelligence collection from open and online sources, design and delivery of influence campaigns, and advisory on balancing operational delivery against deniability. Customers span government, defence and large enterprise. Ghost Shift Ltd was incorporated in 2025.
