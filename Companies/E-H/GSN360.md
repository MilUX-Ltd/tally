# GSN360

> A technology channel consultancy for the defence and security sectors, helping technology vendors grow through channel strategy, distribution partnerships and market entry.

## What they do

GSN360 (registered as Global Solutions Network(s) Ltd) is a channel consultancy rather than a reseller, systems integrator or distributor in its own right. It describes its role as accelerating technology growth through tailored channel strategies, distribution partnerships and strategic guidance, acting as an extension of client businesses as they expand into new markets and territories. The company works across sectors including defence, security, green technology, insurance, maritime and data centres, and states it has helped clients write over £50 million in contracts across around 80 countries. GSN360's LinkedIn profile describes areas of work including secure communications, robotics and synthetic environments; this framing does not currently appear on the company's own website, so it is carried here with that caveat.

## Products and solutions

- **Vendor enablement** - channel strategy development, market positioning, partner and reseller engagement, sales acceleration, and tender or bid writing support.
- **Distribution partnerships** - distributor identification and engagement, introductions, co-selling and joint marketing, and exclusive distribution agreement negotiation.
- **Deal fulfilment and local market enablement** - unblocking stalled deals, local market penetration, cross-border transaction management and regulatory compliance support.
- **Global partnerships** - cross-border market entry facilitation across the USA, Canada, the Middle East, Australia and Europe.
- **Consulting services for major projects** - sector consulting across defence, security, green technology, insurance, maritime and data centres, drawn from a curated associates network.
