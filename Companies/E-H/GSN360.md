# GSN360

> A technology channel specialist for the defence and security sectors, helping technology companies grow through channel strategy and distribution partnerships.

## What they do

GSN360 helps technology companies grow through tailored channel strategies, distribution partnerships and go-to-market guidance, with a focus on the defence and security sectors. The team works across areas including secure communications, robotics and synthetic environments, and supports vendors in reaching defence and security customers through the IT channel.
