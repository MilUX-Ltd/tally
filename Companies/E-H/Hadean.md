# Hadean

> London developer of distributed spatial computing software for simulation, wargaming and command and control.

## What they do

Hadean builds a distributed spatial computing platform for massively scalable simulations, synthetic environments and virtual worlds. Its technology supports defence customers with digital wargaming, command and control, and AI-driven synthetic training, alongside applications in gaming, space and enterprise. The company is venture-backed and works with the UK Ministry of Defence and partners across the UK defence technology sector.

## Products and solutions

- **Spatial computing platform** - distributed engine for large-scale, interoperable simulation and synthetic environments.
- **Wargaming** - simulation tooling that models realistic human behaviours and evolving scenarios.
- **Command and control** - software integrating diverse data sources into a single operational picture for real-time situational awareness.
