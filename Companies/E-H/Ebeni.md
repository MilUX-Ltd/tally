# Ebeni

> Independent safety and engineering assurance consultancy for defence, aerospace, nuclear and rail; MoD Safety Adviser on several large aircraft programmes.

## What they do

Ebeni is an independent specialist engineering company focused on the assessment and assurance of safety, mission and business-critical systems. Its core work spans defence, air traffic management, nuclear and rail. In defence, Ebeni works across air, land, maritime and C4ISTAR domains, providing engineering and management expertise for safety-critical and mission-critical applications. The company is Safety Adviser to the UK MoD on several large aircraft programmes and is Independent Technical Evaluator for the GA-ASI Protector RPAS being delivered to the RAF.

## Products and solutions

- **Safety assurance and certification** - independent safety case development, review and certification for defence platforms, aircraft and complex systems under MoD and civil regulatory frameworks.
- **Airworthiness and regulatory support** - regulatory compliance, airworthiness substantiation and independent technical evaluation for military and civil aviation programmes.
- **Risk and systems engineering** - risk management, system safety analysis, reliability engineering and functional safety assessment across high-integrity domains.
