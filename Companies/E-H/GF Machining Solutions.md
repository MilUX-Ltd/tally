# GF Machining Solutions

> UK subsidiary of the Georg Fischer Group providing precision machining technology, including EDM, milling, laser and automation solutions, to aerospace and defence manufacturers.

## What they do

GF Machining Solutions Ltd is the UK entity of GF Machining Solutions, a division of the Georg Fischer Group headquartered in Biel/Bienne, Switzerland. The company supplies machine tools and complete precision machining solutions to manufacturers of precision components and tooling, with aerospace and defence among its key served sectors. The UK subsidiary (trading as United Machining UK Limited following a 2026 rebranding of the global division) serves customers from its Midlands base. Products and solutions are used in the manufacture of highly challenging aerospace components including engine parts such as blisks and discs, as well as aerostructure components.

## Products and solutions

- **EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining)** - wire, die-sinking, and hole-drilling EDM machines under the Agie Charmilles brand, used for tight-tolerance aerospace and defence components.
- **High-speed milling** - 5-axis and high-performance milling centres under the Mikron Mill brand for precision aerospace parts.
- **Laser texturing and micromachining** - surface texturing and laser micromachining systems.
- **Turbomachinery airfoil machining** - specialist machining of blisks, discs, and airfoil components for aerospace engines under the Liechti brand.
- **Automation and tooling** - workholding, automation, and tooling systems under the System 3R brand.
