# Greenjets
> UK manufacturer of electric ducted-fan propulsion systems and the CarryAir delivery drone, with over 300 years of combined team experience from Rolls-Royce, BAE Systems and Formula 1.
## What they do
Greenjets designs and manufactures next-generation electric propulsion systems for advanced air mobility, from 2 kW to 1 MW output. The core technology is a ducted-fan system claimed to be quieter, safer and more efficient than competing designs. The company's primary product is the CarryAir drone, a logistics-capable unmanned aircraft demonstrated at industry events including Future Force Demo Day. The team draws on deep domain knowledge from aerospace and motorsport primes.
## Products and solutions
- **CarryAir** - Logistics and delivery drone built around Greenjets' ducted-fan propulsion technology.
