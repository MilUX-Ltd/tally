# Elma Electronic UK

> UK subsidiary of the Swiss Elma Electronic group; designs and manufactures rugged chassis, backplanes, and embedded computing sub-systems for defence C5ISR, EW, and SIGINT applications.

## What they do

Elma Electronic UK Ltd is the British trading entity of the Elma Electronic group, headquartered in Switzerland, with over 35 years of experience in rugged embedded computing hardware for defence and aerospace. The Bedford-based UK operation supplies and supports chassis, backplane, and enclosure products deployed across Royal Navy submarines and surface ships, as well as land and air platforms. The company holds SC21 Bronze status and is an active ADS Group member. The wider Elma group operates across the USA, UK, Germany, Switzerland, and other regions.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged VPX and OpenVPX chassis** - VITA 46/65, SOSA-aligned, CMOSS and HOST-compatible chassis for C5ISR and EW applications.
- **Backplanes and enclosures** - VME, CPCI, CPCI Serial, VNX+ backplane and sub-system designs for land, naval, and air platforms.
- **Custom embedded sub-systems** - configured and integrated computing systems for radar, SIGINT, and sensor processing.
