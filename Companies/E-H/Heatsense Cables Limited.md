# Heatsense Cables Limited

> Specialist manufacturer of high-performance thermocouple and signal cables engineered for extreme environments, supplying defence and aerospace applications.

## What they do

Heatsense Cables designs and manufactures specialist cables operating across a temperature range of -196°C to +1250°C, making them suited to harsh defence and aerospace environments. The company has technology capability in fluoropolymer extrusion, high-performance taping, cable wrapping, cover sintering, colouring, etching and the application of specialist coatings. Its range includes cables resistant to flame, oils and chemicals, as well as cables rated for ultra-high vacuum, low outgassing, cryogenic and high radiation environments. Bespoke design and manufacturing services are available alongside standard product lines.

## Products and solutions

- **Thermocouple cables** - precision temperature measurement cables for extreme-environment defence and industrial applications.
- **Signal cables** - high-performance signal transmission cables for harsh environments including radiation and cryogenic conditions.
- **Specialist coated cables** - cables with fluoropolymer and other specialist coatings for flame, chemical and vacuum resistance.
