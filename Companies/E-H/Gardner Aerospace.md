# Gardner Aerospace

> UK manufacturer of precision metallic aerospace components and assemblies for civil and defence aerospace programmes.

## What they do

Gardner Aerospace manufactures machined parts, sheet metal fabrications, and sub-assemblies for the aerospace industry, including defence programmes. The company holds recognised industry approvals and manufactures to AS9100 and NADCAP quality standards. It operates multiple UK facilities, with its principal site in Derby, and has expanded operations internationally including a facility in Bengaluru, India.

The company is owned by Ligeance Aerospace Technology Co. Ltd, a Shenzhen-listed Chinese entity. A 2022 proposed increase in indirect Chinese state investment (via Sichuan Development Holding Co. Ltd) was called in by the UK Government under national security legislation. The acquisition was permitted subject to conditions, including the appointment of an HM Government Observer to the board of the UK subsidiary and the removal of Chinese board members from that entity.

## Products and solutions

- **Machining** - 5-axis CNC milling and high-precision CNC mill/turn of complex metallic aerospace parts.
- **Fabrications** - Precision sheet metal fabrication for aerospace structural and system components.
- **Assembly** - Sub-assembly and full assembly work combining machined, fabricated and welded parts.
- **Speed Shop** - Rapid-turnaround machining capability for urgent aerospace component requirements.
