# Goaco
> Award-winning UK digital consultancy delivering cyber security, AI, cloud, software development and user-centred design for public sector and defence customers.
## What they do
Goaco is a UK digital consultancy with over 15 years of experience on complex government programmes. Capabilities span digital delivery, cyber security, data and AI services, cloud, software development, managed services and user-centred design. The firm describes itself as a recognised UK Government digital expert and is a member of the Aurora Provider Network, open for collaboration on defence and security programmes.
