# Goaco

> UK digital transformation and cyber security consultancy, established 2010, delivering cyber security, data and AI, cloud, software development and user-centred design to public and private sector customers.

## What they do

Goaco is a UK-headquartered digital consultancy, formerly trading as Level 5, delivering services across digital delivery, cyber security, software development, data and AI, cloud, managed services and user-centred design. The company states it partners with UK Government departments and cites case studies with the Cabinet Office, the Ministry of Justice, the National Library of Wales and the Scottish Courts and Tribunals Service. It has also expanded into the Middle East with a Dubai presence.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber Security Services** - including penetration testing, a 24/7 security operations centre, data protection and managed security services.
- **DFIR (Digital Forensics and Incident Response)** - a named solution line for forensic investigation and incident response.
- **Goidentity** - a cloud-based digital identity verification platform launched July 2025, built with Thales, supporting verification of thousands of ID document types worldwide and biometric facial matching with passive liveness detection.
- **Data and AI Services** - data and artificial intelligence consulting and delivery.
- **Managed Services** - 24/7 ITIL-aligned IT service desk and support.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** G-Cloud 14; Digital Outcomes and Specialists (DOS 6 and DOS 7); Technology Services 4 (Crown Commercial Service); Cyber Security Services 3
- **Accreditations:** JOSCAR registered supplier; Cyber Essentials Plus certified (and a Cyber Essentials Plus certification body); ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO 27001
- **Other:** Armed Forces Covenant signatory; Living Wage Employer
