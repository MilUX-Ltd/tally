# Faculty

> London applied-AI company building and deploying AI systems for defence, national security and law enforcement; acquired by Accenture in March 2026.

## What they do

Faculty is a UK applied artificial intelligence company that designs, builds and deploys AI systems for customers across defence, national security, government and commercial sectors. In defence and security it builds bespoke AI algorithms and customised off-the-shelf solutions spanning sensor enhancement, data exploitation and multi-domain command and control, with a stated emphasis on AI safety and ethical deployment. The company works with the UK Ministry of Defence, UK Strategic Command and national security and law enforcement partners. Faculty was acquired by Accenture, with the transaction completing 16 March 2026; it now operates as part of Accenture.

## Products and solutions

- **Cognitive Intelligent Sensing (CoInS)** - machine learning algorithms to autonomously process sensor data and orient sensing systems in real time; in joint development with Leonardo for unmanned platforms and ISR.
- **Electronic warfare AI** - AI capability acceleration for EW applications, developed in collaboration with Leonardo.
- **Defence AI systems** - bespoke and customised AI solutions spanning sensor enhancement, data exploitation and multi-domain command and control.
- **National security and law enforcement** - AI systems designed, built and deployed for operational use with UK security partners.
