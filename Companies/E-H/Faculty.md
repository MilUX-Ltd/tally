# Faculty

> London applied-AI company building and deploying AI systems for defence, national security and law enforcement.

## What they do

Faculty is a UK applied artificial intelligence company that designs, builds and deploys AI systems for customers across defence, national security, government and commercial sectors. In defence and security it builds bespoke AI algorithms and customised off-the-shelf solutions spanning sensor enhancement, data exploitation and multi-domain command and control, with a stated emphasis on AI safety and ethical deployment. The company works with the UK Ministry of Defence and national security and law enforcement partners.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence AI** - bespoke and customised AI systems for use cases from sensor enhancement to multi-domain command and control.
- **National security and law enforcement** - AI systems designed, built and deployed for operational use with security partners.
- **AI safety and assurance** - dedicated R&D and safety practice underpinning deployed systems.
