# Electric Twin

> AI startup building synthetic audiences that model how populations think and behave, with stated applicability to military campaign planning and strategic influence operations.

## What they do

Electric Twin combines large language models with real-world survey data and social science research to generate synthetic human populations. Clients run simulations against these audiences to predict how real people will respond to messages, policies, or decisions. Independent validation by the London School of Economics found the platform achieves 95% accuracy across 40,000 evaluations in 155 countries. Co-founder Alex Cooper is a former British Army officer and has explicitly positioned the capability for military planning. Simon Case, former Cabinet Secretary and Non-Executive Director at the Ministry of Defence, sits on the company's ethics board.

## Products and solutions

- **Synthetic audience platform** - AI-generated population models enabling simulation of human responses to communications, policies, and campaign messages across demographic and geographic segments.
