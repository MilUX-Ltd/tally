# Gattaca Projects

> Mechanical engineering consultancy delivering project-based solutions across defence, energy, aerospace and transportation.

## What they do

Gattaca Projects is a mechanical engineering consultancy providing end-to-end project delivery across design, manufacturing, structural analysis, quality control, R&D, maintenance and project engineering. The business operates as a statement-of-work partner, drawing on a network of specialist associates to scale for larger programmes. It is a subsidiary brand within Gattaca PLC, a listed engineering and technology staffing group.

## Products and solutions

- **Engineering project delivery** - turnkey and modular project support across the design-to-manufacture lifecycle for defence and security clients, including BAE Systems and AWE.
- **Structural analysis and quality control** - engineering assurance services applied to safety-critical and defence programmes.
- **R&D and maintenance support** - specialist technical resource deployed at defined programme phases.
