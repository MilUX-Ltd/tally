# Global Invacom Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of satellite ground equipment serving commercial, government and defence Satcom, SatTV and GNSS networks in over 120 countries.

## What they do

Global Invacom designs and manufactures satellite ground equipment from multiple UK sites, including its headquarters at Whitstable, Kent. The company serves commercial, government and defence customers with antennas, RF electronics, transceivers, fibre distribution equipment, waveguides, and complete satellite terminal solutions. Its defence portfolio includes products for Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (ISR) applications and man-portable terminals suited to military operations. The company holds Joscar, Cyber Essentials Plus and ISO 9001, 14001 and 45001 accreditations. It is listed on the Singapore Exchange (SGX) and AIM.

## Products and solutions

- **XRJ Ka-band transceiver** - single-unit transmit and receive transceiver operating across the full extended Ka-band, supporting WGS, GovSat, Skynet and Inmarsat GX networks; Mil-STD 164C compliant and ITAR-exempt; awarded Best Government Solution at Strategies in Satellite Ground Segment 2025.
- **XY multi-orbit antenna** - steerable terminal for rapid-response and mission-critical applications, supporting GEO, MEO, LEO and High Earth Orbit.
- **ISR ground segment solutions** - satellite ground equipment designed for data capture in intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance roles.
- **Transportable and fixed satellite terminals** - manpack VSAT terminals, steerable and tracking terminals, and long-term fixed deployments.
- **RF over Fibre and waveguide products** - distribution and switching components for satellite networks.
