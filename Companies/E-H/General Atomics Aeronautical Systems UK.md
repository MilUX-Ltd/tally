# General Atomics Aeronautical Systems UK

> UK-registered entity of GA-ASI, providing dedicated support for the RAF's Protector RG Mk1 remotely piloted aircraft system and other UK MOD RPAS requirements.

## What they do

General Atomics Aeronautical Systems UK Ltd (GA-UK) is an affiliated entity of General Atomics Aeronautical Systems, Inc. (GA-ASI), incorporated in England in 2010 and headquartered in London. It supports UK Ministry of Defence Remotely Piloted Air Systems (RPAS) requirements and works with European systems and support providers to enhance the capabilities of GA-ASI's Predator-series UAS. The parent company, GA-ASI, is headquartered in San Diego, California, and is a subsidiary of General Atomics, a privately held US defence technology company.

## Products and solutions

- **Protector RG Mk1** - the RAF's new RPAS, based on the GA-ASI MQ-9B SkyGuardian; provides intelligence, surveillance, target acquisition and reconnaissance (ISTAR) capability and replaces the RAF's Reaper fleet. Achieved UK Military Type Certificate from the Military Aviation Authority in May 2025.
- **UK Protector Availability and Support Solution (UK PASS)** - sustainment and logistics contract signed with UK MOD in June 2025, covering the RPA, Certifiable Ground Control Stations and Synthetic Training Systems.
- **Predator B / MQ-9 Reaper support** - historical support for RAF Reaper operations from RAF Waddington and Creech Air Force Base, Nevada.
