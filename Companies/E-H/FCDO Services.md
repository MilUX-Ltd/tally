# FCDO Services
> UK government Crown service providing technical security, secure IT, secure speech and logistics for diplomacy and defence.
## What they do
FCDO Services is an arms-length body of the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office, delivering bespoke services to protect UK government assets, data and people at home and overseas. Capabilities span technical, building and perimeter security; secure IT and digital services; secure speech environments; and logistics and deployment support. Customers are primarily other UK government departments and their global partners.
