# FCDO Services
> UK government trading fund providing technical security, secure IT, secure speech, logistics and translation services for diplomacy and defence, at home and overseas.

## What they do

FCDO Services is a trading fund and arm's-length body of the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office, delivering secure services to protect UK government people, data and assets worldwide. It traces its origins to secure communications work for British embassies from the 1930s, became an executive agency (FCO Services) in 2006, moved to trading fund status in 2008, and took its current name in September 2020 when the FCO and DFID merged. FCDO Services operates in more than 250 destinations across 168 countries and reported revenue exceeding £252 million in 2025/26. Customers are primarily other UK government departments, alongside foreign governments and international organisations closely linked to the UK. The organisation employed around 797 people as of February 2026.

## Products and solutions

- **UK NACE** - the UK National Authority for Counter-Eavesdropping, the National Technical Authority for technical security, providing threat identification, policy and advice, and operational technical security support to UK government, the armed forces, law enforcement and critical national infrastructure.
- **Digital and cloud services** - secure IT, digital and cloud solutions for government and partner customers.
- **Securing buildings and spaces** - technical, building and perimeter security for diplomatic and government premises overseas.
- **Secure speech** - secure speech environments protecting sensitive discussions from technical and physical eavesdropping.
- **Logistics** - global logistics, freight and deployment support for government and partner customers, run through the NetCourier logistics management system.
- **Translation and interpreting** - language services supporting UK government engagement overseas.
