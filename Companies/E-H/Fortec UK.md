# Fortec UK

> Specialist supplier of displays, embedded computing, monitors and power solutions, including value-added design for defence applications.

## What they do

Fortec UK provides displays, embedded computing, monitors, components and power solutions for demanding technical applications. Alongside standard products, it offers value-added services including bespoke design, optical bonding and custom manufacturing. It is the UK operation of the wider FORTEC Group and serves defence among its market sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **TFT displays and industrial monitors** - display modules and ruggedised monitors.
- **Embedded computing** - panel PCs and embedded systems.
- **Power supplies** - AC-DC and DC-DC units across a wide power range.
- **Touch and optical bonding** - touch controllers and display customisation services.
