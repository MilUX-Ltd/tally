# FarX

> Early-stage biometric AI company, based in the Malvern cyber cluster, developing fused voice and face recognition for continuous multi-factor identity authentication.

## What they do

FarX (trading as FARx, legally FarX Group Limited) is a Great Malvern-based company founded in September 2022 by a team of voice and face biometric specialists. It builds a proprietary machine learning technology that fuses speaker, speech and face recognition to deliver continuous, frictionless multi-factor biometric authentication of a user. FARx 1, released October 2023, is described by the company as the first fused voice-face ("talking face") recognition technology. The company states its applications include banking and financial services, retail, healthcare, education, gaming, online conferencing, and government citizen and employee authentication. FARx is delivered as an on-premises server solution or as a cloud service, accessed via API.

## Products and solutions

- **FARx 1** - fused voice, speech and face recognition engine providing continuous multi-factor biometric identity verification.
- **Sector applications** - packaged use cases for banking, retail, healthcare, education, gaming and government/citizen authentication, plus embedded use in chatbots, help desks and online conferencing.
