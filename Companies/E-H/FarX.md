# FarX

> Early-stage biometric AI company developing fused voice, face, and speech recognition for continuous multi-factor identity authentication.

## What they do

FarX builds biometric authentication technology that fuses voice, speech, and face recognition into a continuous multi-factor identity verification system. The company is based in Great Malvern, Worcestershire, within the UK's established cyber and signals intelligence cluster. Its technology targets government and law enforcement applications, with explicit capability for deepfake and synthetic voice detection.

## Products and solutions

- **Fused biometric authentication** - continuous identity verification combining voice recognition, speech analysis, and facial recognition into a single multi-factor system.
- **Synthetic media detection** - capability to identify deepfake video and synthetic voice audio, relevant to counter-disinformation and identity assurance applications.
