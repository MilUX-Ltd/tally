# Escend

> Cyber security and GRC consultancy focused on defence, space and government sectors.

## What they do

Escend has over 22 years of expertise in cyber security, data protection, and governance, risk and compliance (GRC). The company delivers consultancy services to clients across defence, space and government, with a stated focus on enhancing security frameworks and implementing advanced cybersecurity protocols to safeguard critical assets. Services include strategic security planning, managed detection and response, and 24/7/365 SOC operations across multiple sites. The company describes delivering certifications required by the UK Ministry of Defence and the US Department of Defence.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed Detection and Response** - 24/7/365 SOC monitoring and incident response across multiple client sites.
- **Strategic Security Planning** - security framework design and implementation for defence and government clients.
- **GRC Consultancy** - governance, risk and compliance advisory including NIST and COBIT 5 frameworks.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001; Cyber Essentials; Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
