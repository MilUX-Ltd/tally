# Eclipse Digital Solutions

> Physical security systems integrator serving Ministry of Defence, government, justice sector, and critical national infrastructure clients.

## What they do

Eclipse Digital Solutions designs, installs, and maintains integrated electronic security systems for government, defence, justice, and critical national infrastructure clients. The company works across MOD installations, HM Prison Service sites, UK Border Agency facilities, and CPNI-advised organisations. In June 2025 it launched a dedicated Custodial Division to formalise its justice sector presence. It holds a strong accreditation stack including Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 27001, and SSAIB certification, and is a signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Perimeter Intrusion Detection Systems (PIDS)** - perimeter detection integrated with thermal CCTV and video analytics.
- **Drone detection and mitigation** - RF-based detection systems with a range of up to 500 metres.
- **Under-vehicle scanning** - deployed at high-security entry points.
- **CCTV and video management systems** - design, installation, and maintenance across secure sites.
- **Access control** - installation and managed maintenance of access control infrastructure.
- **ANPR** - automatic number plate recognition systems.
- **Control room design and integration** - integrated security control room design for government and defence clients.
- **Managed maintenance and support** - ongoing support services for installed security systems.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 9001:2015, ISO 27001:2017, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, SSAIB, Armed Forces Covenant signatory
