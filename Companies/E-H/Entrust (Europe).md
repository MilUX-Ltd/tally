# Entrust (Europe)

> UK and European entity of Entrust Corporation, providing digital identity, PKI, hardware security modules and border-control technology to government and defence-adjacent customers.

## What they do

Entrust (Europe) Ltd is the UK and European legal entity of Entrust Corporation, a US-headquartered digital security company. Through this entity the Entrust portfolio is delivered to UK government, financial services, and critical national infrastructure customers. The UK product footprint includes hardware security modules, PKI and certificate lifecycle management, AI-powered identity verification, and government credential and travel-document issuance platforms. The registered UK office is in London; principal operational presence is in Reading.

## Products and solutions

- **nShield Hardware Security Modules** - physical cryptographic devices certified to FIPS 140-3 Level 3 and Common Criteria EAL4+, used to protect PKI, encryption keys and classified infrastructure. Acquired from Thales (nCipher) in 2019.
- **PKI and certificate lifecycle management** - TLS/SSL, code signing and document signing certificates and management platforms.
- **Digital identity verification** - AI-powered biometric IDV and document verification, expanded through the acquisition of Onfido in April 2024.
- **Government credential and border-control solutions** - ePassport and national identity card issuance, biometric smart corridors, eGates, and the Electronic Travel Authorisation platform deployed for the UK Home Office.
- **Identity and access management** - enterprise authentication, MFA and zero-trust enablement.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022 (Entrust Corporation organisation-wide); nShield HSMs certified to FIPS 140-3 Level 3 and Common Criteria EAL4+.
