# HENSOLDT

> German sensor-solutions group for defence and security, spanning radar, electronic warfare, optronics and avionics, with a UK arm.

## What they do

HENSOLDT AG is a German multinational focused on sensor technologies for defence, security and aerospace, with main product areas in radar, optoelectronics and avionics. It originated from the defence electronics unit of Airbus, was sold to KKR in 2017, and listed on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange in September 2020. The group has production sites in Germany, France and the United Kingdom. Its UK arm, HENSOLDT UK (formerly Kelvin Hughes, acquired in 2017 and renamed in 2019), is based in Enfield, North London, and develops maritime navigation and surveillance radar.

## Products and solutions

- **Radar systems** - surveillance, reconnaissance, air traffic control and air defence radars used on platforms including the Eurofighter, German Navy F125 frigates and US Navy littoral combat ships.
- **Electronic warfare** - the Kalaetron family of airborne EW systems, including Kalaetron Attack for radar jamming.
- **Optronics** - daylight cameras, thermal imagers, image intensifiers and laser rangefinders for military and civil security.
- **Kelvin Hughes SharpEye** - solid-state maritime navigation and surveillance radar developed by HENSOLDT UK.
