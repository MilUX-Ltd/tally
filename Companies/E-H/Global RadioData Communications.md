# Global RadioData Communications Ltd

> Hereford-based satellite, RF and IP networking integrator developing mission-critical communications systems for defence, government, emergency response and energy customers worldwide.

## What they do

Global RadioData Communications Ltd (GRC) designs, integrates and supports satellite, GSM/LTE, cloud and IP connectivity solutions for users operating in contested and remote environments. The company is a Tier 1 Value-Added Reseller with major satellite hardware and airtime providers. Its customer base includes defence and government agencies, emergency responders, oil and gas operators and NGOs. GRC operates its own Network Monitoring Centre (NMC) in Hereford, providing 24/7 hardware support and network assurance. The company also develops the Scytale family of proprietary products for tactical and government communications. GRC is a signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Scytale** - rapidly deployable communications device connectable to any bearer of opportunity (satellite, GSM, local Wi-Fi), providing VoIP telephony, Wi-Fi coverage up to 300 m and data networking within minutes of power-on; supports next-generation encryption and is interoperable with defence, government and commercial networks.
- **Scytale Tactical Cloud** - cloud-based security layer providing traffic obfuscation, digital signature protection and remote-user data security.
- **GRM rugged modem series** - in-house designed satellite modems for demanding environments.
- **6-SAT managed SATCOM service** - fully managed end-to-end satellite connectivity service aggregating multiple satellite hardware and airtime providers including Ovzon SATCOM-as-a-Service.
- **SigmaNet SD-WAN** - next-generation network resilience for assured communications.
- **Satellite terminals** - VSAT manpack terminals, hotspots and satcom-on-the-move solutions for fixed and mobile deployments.
