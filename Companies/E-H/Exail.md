# Exail Ltd

> UK subsidiary of Exail Technologies SA, a France-headquartered group supplying autonomous mine countermeasure systems, unmanned maritime vehicles and inertial navigation systems to navies and defence forces worldwide.

## What they do

Exail Ltd is the UK entity of Exail Technologies SA, listed on Euronext Paris and formed in 2022 from the merger of ECA Group (drone and robotics) and iXblue (navigation and photonics). The group employs approximately 2,000 people globally and operates in over 80 countries, with the UK registered office in Petersfield, Hampshire, and operational offices in Aberdeen and Wokingham. The company's defence focus is on autonomous mine countermeasures, unmanned maritime vehicles, and fibre optic gyroscope-based inertial navigation systems, with over 70 navies and armies among its customers. The UK entity serves the Royal Navy and allied defence customers and is actively engaged in UK MCM programme work.

## Products and solutions

- **UMIS (Unmanned MCM Integrated System)** - a fully unmanned mine countermeasure system integrating autonomous surface, underwater and aerial vehicles for end-to-end MCM missions.
- **A18-M AUV** - mid-size autonomous underwater vehicle for long-endurance MCM operations; up to 24 hours endurance, covering 20-40 sq km per mission.
- **A9-M AUV** - smaller autonomous underwater vehicle for MCM tasks.
- **DriX USV** - unmanned surface vessel series used for hydrographic survey, surveillance and mine countermeasures; in service in 19 countries.
- **Inertial navigation systems (INS)** - fibre optic gyroscope-based navigation and motion sensing for ships, submarines, UUVs, UAVs and land platforms.
- **Photonics and fibre optic components** - optical components and laser systems for space, telecoms and scientific applications (legacy iXblue capability).
