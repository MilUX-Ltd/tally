# Hale Hamilton

> Uxbridge manufacturer of high-pressure valves and gas control systems for naval and defence platforms; part of CIRCOR.

## What they do

Hale Hamilton manufactures high-pressure valves and fluid and gas control equipment for naval defence, industrial gas and high-technology industrial markets. Its valves are fitted across the UK naval fleet, including submarines and surface warships, and on platforms operated by other navies, with a long-standing relationship with the UK Ministry of Defence. All products are designed and manufactured in the UK at Uxbridge, and the company operates as part of the US-headquartered CIRCOR International group.

## Products and solutions

- **High-pressure valves** - valves and flow-control equipment for naval submarines and surface ships.
- **Gas control systems** - high-pressure fluid and gas control equipment for defence and industrial applications.
- **Industrial and hydrogen valves** - valve systems for industrial gas, power and hydrogen markets.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Ownership:** subsidiary of CIRCOR International, within its Instrumentation and Thermal Fluid Products segment.
