# Etherstack

> Tactical radio and mission-critical communications technology; UK-incorporated, ASX-listed.

## What they do

Etherstack plc is a wireless communications technology company that designs, develops and deploys radio products for defence, public safety and utility networks. It specialises in waveform software, radio protocol stacks and deployable tactical communications systems, supplying defence organisations, public-safety agencies, carriers and utilities. The UK operating entity is Etherstack Wireless Limited; the parent is incorporated in the UK and listed on the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX: ESK), with R&D across the UK, Australia, the USA and Japan.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence waveforms** - P25, TETRA, DMR and NXDN protocol stacks plus NATO and sovereign waveforms, ported across multiple radio platforms.
- **Tactical communications** - deployable repeater and push-to-talk-over-IP systems for resilient field communications.
- **LMR-IWF** - 3GPP-compliant interworking function for migrating land mobile radio to LTE and 5G.
- **Cryptography and key management** - AES-256 encryption for secure voice and data.
