# Ford Aerospace Limited

> Specialist manufacturer of precision components, laminated shims and assemblies for aerospace, defence and high-technology industries.

## What they do

Ford Aerospace manufactures and supplies precision machined and fabricated components, assemblies, and adjustable laminated shim products to the aerospace and defence supply chain. The company holds approved supplier status with several major defence and aerospace primes including BAE Systems, Leonardo, Airbus, GKN, Moog Aerospace, Rolls-Royce and Safran. Its portfolio covers surface-bonded and edge-bonded parts in metallic and non-metallic materials, with customisable shim products that replace traditional shim sets in complex assembly applications.

Founded in 1910 and incorporated in 1944, the company is part of the SPIROL Group, a global manufacturer of engineered fasteners, disc springs and shims.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision machined components** - make-to-print components for aerospace and defence assemblies.
- **Laminated shim products** - adjustable and peelable surface and edge-bonded shims in metallic and non-metallic materials, in customisable thicknesses and lamination configurations.
- **Fabricated assemblies** - fabricated metal parts and sub-assemblies for high-technology industry customers.
