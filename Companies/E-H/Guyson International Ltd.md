# Guyson International Ltd

> Yorkshire-based manufacturer of blast finishing and ultrasonic cleaning equipment for the aerospace and defence sectors, covering both OEM manufacturing and MRO operations.

## What they do

Guyson International manufactures surface preparation and precision cleaning solutions for aerospace, defence, medical, and industrial applications. Its blast systems support aerospace airframe structures, turbine blades, propulsion components, landing gear, and additive manufactured parts. Defence applications include selective removal of conformal coating from printed circuit boards and surface preparation of military components. The company also designs fully robotic and automated blasting systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Manual Blast Machines (Euroblast range)** - Industrial blast cabinets for a wide range of surface preparation tasks, including ATEX-certified variants for hazardous environments.
- **Automated Blast Machines** - Rotary indexing, rotary spindle, in-line, and robotic blasting systems for high-volume and precision applications.
- **Shot Peening Systems** - Automated shot peening equipment for aerospace and defence components.
- **Automated Powder Recovery Systems** - Equipment for metal additive manufacturing and 3D printed component finishing.
- **Ultrasonic Cleaning Systems (Kerry range)** - Multi-stage aqueous and solvent cleaning systems, ultrasonic baths, and benchtop cleaners.
