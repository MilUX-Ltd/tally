# Geocento

> Earth observation SME providing satellite imagery selection, procurement and virtual constellation services.

## What they do

Geocento provides web-based technologies and services for selecting, planning and accessing satellite imagery, and for designing and managing earth observation constellations. Its platforms let organisations procure imagery and operate their own virtual constellations without reselling intermediaries. Earth observation supports defence, security and intelligence applications including surveillance and geospatial analysis.

## Products and solutions

- **Virtual Constellation Design** - consultancy for designing earth observation constellations.
- **Virtual Constellation Management** - technology and support to operate customer constellations.
- **Pyxis** - customer-controlled constellation systems.
- **Lyra** - white-label offering for resellers.
