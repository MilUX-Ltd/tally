# GKN Aerospace

> Tier-one global aerospace and defence manufacturer of aerostructures, electrical wiring systems, landing gear, transparencies, and engine components.

## What they do

GKN Aerospace Services Limited is a leading global tier-one supplier of airframe and engine structures, landing gear, electrical wiring interconnection systems (EWIS), transparencies, and aftermarket services. The company serves both civil and defence markets across fixed-wing aircraft, rotorcraft, UAVs, missiles, and space launch vehicles. It is owned by Melrose Industries plc, a UK-listed engineering company. GKN Aerospace has a significant UK manufacturing footprint, including facilities in Filton (Bristol), Luton, and Western Approach, with further sites across Sweden, the Netherlands, and the United States.

## Products and solutions

- **Composites and metallic aerostructures** - Fuselage structures, flaperons, in-flight opening doors, wing trailing and leading edges, for platforms including the F-35 Lightning II, C-130, and Tempest sixth-generation fighter.
- **Electrical wiring interconnection systems (EWIS)** - Wiring harness packages designed and produced for all F-35 aircraft in production and a broad range of military aircraft engines. GKN holds a proprietary wiring design and manufacturing system.
- **Landing gear** - Design, development, production, and MRO of landing gear systems for helicopters and fixed-wing platforms including the Chinook CH-47, Apache AH-64, NH90, and Black Hawk.
- **Transparencies and canopies** - Fighter canopies produced from single-piece stretched acrylic with low-observable coatings, supplied ready for frame installation.
- **UAV structures** - Composite landing gear and composite tail sections for platforms including the General Atomics MQ-9B SkyGuardian, flown by the RAF as Protector.
- **Missile components** - Lightweight reusable missile canisters, structural components, and missile EWIS for customers including Raytheon and Lockheed Martin.
- **Ice protection systems** - Electro-thermal ice protection systems and in-house icing wind tunnel test facilities.
