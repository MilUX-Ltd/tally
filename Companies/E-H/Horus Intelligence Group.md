# Horus Intelligence Group

> Global security and intelligence consultancy providing risk management, investigations and security sector reform.

## What they do

Horus Intelligence Group is a security and intelligence consultancy providing risk management, corporate intelligence and investigations, and security sector reform (SSR) programmes. It supports international development and security programmes and corporate clients across multiple regions, drawing on military and intelligence expertise. The group operates through UK, Asia Pacific, North America and global entities.

## Products and solutions

- **Security consultancy** - security risk management and advisory services.
- **Corporate intelligence and investigations** - intelligence gathering and analysis for clients.
- **Security sector reform** - international development programmes in SSR.
