# Filtronic

> AIM-listed designer and manufacturer of RF, microwave, and millimetre-wave components and subsystems for defence, space, and telecoms.

## What they do

Filtronic is a UK public company that designs and manufactures high-performance radio frequency, microwave, and millimetre-wave components and subsystems. It serves the defence and aerospace, space, telecoms infrastructure, and critical communications markets. Core capabilities include RF design, solid-state power amplifiers (SSPAs), transmit/receive modules, hybrid manufacturing, GaAs and GaN semiconductor assembly and test, plastic encapsulation of compound semiconductor devices, and electromechanical manufacturing. Principal sites are at NETPark Sedgefield in County Durham; additional UK design offices are in Leeds, Manchester, and Cambridge. The company also operates a manufacturing site in Salisbury, Maryland, USA. It is listed on the London Stock Exchange AIM market under the ticker FTC.

## Products and solutions

- **Transmit/receive modules** - radar T/R modules for defence customers; the company passed 60,000 units in production as of 2025.
- **Solid-state power amplifiers** - GaN-based SSPAs for space (including E-Band SSPAs for SpaceX Starlink) and defence applications.
- **Electronic sensor system components** - bespoke RF assemblies for defence prime contractors; a £13.4 million contract for an electronic sensor system was announced in July 2025.
- **Compound semiconductor packaging** - plastic encapsulation of GaAs and GaN devices for high-density System-in-Package designs; DASA DTEP award for low-cost, low-SWAP packaging for future radar applications.
- **Satellite communications RF assemblies** - RF assemblies for low-earth-orbit satellite constellations including Airbus LEO programmes.
