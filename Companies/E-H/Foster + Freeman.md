# Foster + Freeman

> UK designer and manufacturer of forensic examination equipment for questioned documents, fingerprints, trace evidence, identity verification, and border control.

## What they do

Foster + Freeman designs and manufactures systems for the forensic examination of questioned documents, the detection and enhancement of latent fingerprints, and the elemental analysis of trace evidence. The company also produces identity verification and border control technology for travel document authentication. Products are built to order at the company's headquarters in Evesham, Worcestershire, and exported to more than 170 countries. End-users include the Ministry of Defence, law enforcement agencies, border forces, and military organisations worldwide.

## Products and solutions

- **VSC Range (Video Spectral Comparator)** - Workstations for forensic examination of identity, travel, and questioned documents; used at borders and in forensic laboratories.
- **Crime-lite Range** - Forensic alternate light sources for crime scene examination and fingerprint evidence recovery, including handheld and laboratory imaging systems.
- **DCS 6** - Fingermark imaging workstation providing multi-spectral photography for latent print capture and enhancement.
- **VSC ForeFront** - Portable triple-layer identity authentication device for frontline border operations.
- **ffTA (Trace Analysis System)** - Laboratory platform for glass refractive index measurement, Raman spectroscopy, and micro spectrometry of trace evidence.
- **CBRNE and TSCM solutions** - Tactical products for chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear, and explosive environments, and technical surveillance countermeasures.
