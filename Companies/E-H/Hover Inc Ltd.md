# Hover Inc Ltd

> Cambridge-based aerospace hardware company developing ruggedised avionics computers for manned and unmanned aircraft.

## What they do

Hover Inc designs ruggedised Integrated Modular Avionics computers built around a proprietary ARINC 653 real-time operating system, running safety-critical and non-safety-critical software in isolated partitions on the same hardware. The company states its products are TSO-C153A approved and conform to DO-160G, DO-254, DO-178C (Level A) and DO-297. Hover is ATI-backed (Aerospace Technology Institute) and targets Advanced Air Mobility and other crewed and uncrewed aircraft manufacturers, positioning itself as an open-systems alternative to vertically integrated Tier 1 avionics suppliers, with customers retaining IP ownership. It operates from Cambridge, with a further office in Seattle, US.

## Products and solutions

- **H1.0** - dual-channel avionics computer for redundant applications.
- **H2.0** - single-channel avionics computer.
- **H3.0** - single-channel avionics computer optimised for remote I/O and battery or fuel-cell management.
- **Detect and Avoid computer** - onboard computing platform supporting ACAS XU/XR collision-avoidance logic for cooperative and non-cooperative air traffic, developed with avionics partner Sagetech Avionics.
