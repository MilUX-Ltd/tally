# Hover Inc Ltd

> Cambridge-based aerospace hardware company developing high-performance computing platforms for manned and unmanned aircraft.

## What they do

Hover Inc designs ruggedised single-board computers integrated with advanced AI processors for airborne autonomy, flight control, and machine-learning tasks. The company is ATI-backed and focuses on the demanding computational requirements of both crewed and uncrewed aviation, including a proprietary real-time operating system suited to safety-critical embedded applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Ruggedised SBCs** - Compact, high-performance single-board computers with integrated AI processors for airborne autonomy and flight control.
