# Envitia

> UK geospatial and data software and services firm serving defence, national security and central government.

## What they do

Envitia is an IT services and consulting company specialising in complex data challenges. Its consultancy and technical engineering teams deliver data solutions that enable defence, national security and central government customers to make better decisions. Stated specialties include software engineering, mapping and geospatial, data modelling, data quality, data governance, analytics, and AI and machine learning. The operating entity is Envitia Ltd, under holding company Envitia Group Limited.

## Products and solutions

- **MapLink Pro** - a cross-platform suite of geospatial software development toolkits used as a foundation for command and control, ISR, situational awareness, and training and simulation applications. MapLink Pro 11 was released in 2025. Publicly named integrators include BAE Systems, Lockheed Martin, Northrop Grumman, General Dynamics and IBM.
- **Data Discovery Platform (DDP)** - a modular geospatial data discovery, cataloguing and processing platform with open-standards APIs to discover, harvest, transform and visualise data, used in UK and US military systems.
- **Service lines** - AI and machine learning, architecture, cloud services, data analytics and visualisation, data quality and management, digital and data strategy, geospatial, and software engineering.
