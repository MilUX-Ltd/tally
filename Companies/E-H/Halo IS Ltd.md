# Halo IS Ltd

> Leeds-based cybersecurity consultancy specialising in security testing, compliance audits and strategic consultancy.

## What they do

Halo IS is a cybersecurity consultancy established in 2019 in Leeds. Its core competencies are security testing, compliance audits and strategic consultancy, delivered by a team of CREST-accredited ethical hackers and account managers. Engagements range from a first network security assessment through to annual testing schedules and full red team exercises. The company describes a tailored, cost-effective approach to security testing across organisations of any size.

## Products and solutions

- **External and internal infrastructure assessment** - network reconnaissance, port scanning and vulnerability scanning from the perspective of an unauthenticated attacker, with manual verification and exploitation of confirmed vulnerabilities.
- **Web application and API assessment** - OWASP-based testing covering configuration management, transport layer security, authentication, authorisation, session management, data validation and business logic.
- **Mobile application assessment** - testing based on the OWASP Mobile Testing Guide, covering platform usage, reverse engineering, server-side controls, cryptography and data storage.
- **Cloud service review** - assessment of IaaS, PaaS and SaaS tenant configurations (for example Microsoft 365 or AWS) against NCSC Cloud Security Principles and CIS Benchmarks.
- **Wireless infrastructure assessment** - testing of access point configuration, signal bleed, encryption standards and network segmentation.
- **Server and workstation build reviews** - hardening reviews against NCSC guidance, CIS Benchmarks or Cyber Essentials Plus requirements.
- **Firewall review** - ruleset and configuration audits against best practice.
- **Social engineering** - physical access testing (site surveillance, access control bypass, tailgating) and phishing/vishing assessments, combined with user training.
- **Cyber Essentials certification support** - Cyber Essentials Basic and Cyber Essentials Plus assessments.
- **PCI DSS Requirement 11.3 testing.**
