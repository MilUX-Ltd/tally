# Exsel Group

> Independent defence technology group providing engineering design and integration, electronics, training and simulation, ballistic protection, and field services to defence and industry.

## What they do

The Exsel Group is an independent defence technology solutions company delivering engineering solutions, technology systems, and support services to defence and industry where reliability, safety, and protection are paramount. Originating in 1947, the group now operates across three UK sites and serves a global customer base spanning government, defence, and commercial interests. Exsel Group is the official sole representative and approved agent for ND Defense's complete range of vehicles and parts across the UK, Europe, and West Africa.

## Products and solutions

- **Design and integration** - electromechanical, electronic, and communication systems integration into vehicle and mobile platforms; container-based solutions for rapid deployment and severe environment applications; build-to-print and full design-and-development programmes.
- **Electronics** - software and electronic engineering for defence systems including obsolescence management, through-life support, repair and maintenance, and data-centric security (delivered through the Exsel Electronics division).
- **Training and simulation** - design, production, assembly, and installation of training and simulation hardware devices for high-fidelity representative training environments.
- **Dyna Armour** - ballistic and blast protection solutions for personnel, platforms, and assets, designed and developed in partnership with Dynasystems.
- **Field services** - global maintenance, servicing, and parts supply for defence vehicle fleets including ND Defense legacy and current vehicles.
