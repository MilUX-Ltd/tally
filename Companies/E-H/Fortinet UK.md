# Fortinet, Inc.

> Cyber security vendor providing network and platform security, with a UK operating company serving public sector and defence.

## What they do

Fortinet is a cyber security company founded in 2000 and headquartered in Sunnyvale, California. It develops firewalls, secure networking, SASE and security operations products, integrated through its Security Fabric platform. The UK operating company, Fortinet UK Limited (Companies House 04570027), serves customers across UK industry and government, including public sector and defence applications.

## Products and solutions

- **FortiGate / FortiOS** - next-generation firewall hardware and operating system. FortiOS 8.0 was announced in 2026 with capabilities for AI security and SASE.
- **Security Fabric** - integrated platform connecting Fortinet's security and networking products.
- **Defence solutions** - products positioned to extend cyber security across the battlespace at the operational level.
