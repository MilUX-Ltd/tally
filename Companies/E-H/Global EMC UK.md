# Global EMC UK Ltd

> Manufacturer of electromagnetic shielding enclosures, anechoic test chambers and Faraday cages, serving military and defence, medical, academic and automotive customers across 24 countries.

## What they do

Global EMC UK designs, manufactures and installs magnetic field shielding, Faraday cages and anechoic test chambers from an 1,800 m² factory in Nottinghamshire. The company delivers bespoke, turnkey solutions to military and defence agencies, commercial research facilities and medical institutions. Its engineering team includes Security Cleared (SC) designers and is Cyber Essentials Plus accredited.

## Products and solutions

- **Military and defence test chambers** - anechoic and conducted EMC chambers designed and manufactured to MIL-STD-461, DEF STAN 59-411 and MIL-STD-285.
- **Tempest shielded rooms** - Faraday cage enclosures meeting NATO SDIP-27 and Tempest standards, independently tested and certified to ISO 17025.
- **Anechoic test chambers** - shielded enclosures lined with high-performance absorber material for EMC and RF measurement across L, S, C, X, Ku and Ka bands; 5G and automotive CISPR12/25 variants also available.
- **Magnetic field shielding** - rooms and enclosures for TEM/SEM/E-Beam equipment and EEG applications, reducing 50 Hz AC and quasi-DC interference.
- **Faraday cages and shielded rooms** - RFI/EMI enclosures including forensic room shielding, high voltage laboratories, and control and amplifier rooms.
