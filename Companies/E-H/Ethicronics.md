# Ethicronics

> Cambridge deep-tech startup developing software to verify the integrity and authenticity of electronic hardware components across defence and critical supply chains.

## What they do

Ethicronics develops a software-as-a-service platform to establish trust in electronics hardware, addressing the risk of counterfeit, tampered, cloned or maliciously modified components. Its solution generates hardware integrity certificates by applying unforgeable digital stamps at PCB to nanometer-level resolution, enabling supply chain assurance for defence, aerospace, space, transport, finance and critical infrastructure. The company was incorporated in 2022 and is based at the Allia Future Business Centre, Cambridge.

## Products and solutions

- **Hardware Integrity SaaS platform** - a scalable software solution that certifies and authenticates electronic components, producing hardware integrity certificates for defence and critical-sector supply chains.
