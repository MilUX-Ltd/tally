# FSP Consulting Services Limited

> Digital transformation and cyber security consultancy with specific expertise in the defence sector, including secure cloud, Secure by Design and penetration testing.

## What they do

FSP provides digital transformation and cyber security consulting to defence, government, critical national infrastructure and regulated industries. The company integrates data and AI, cyber security, enterprise cloud and change delivery, with security-cleared teams supporting classified environments. FSP is NCSC assured for cyber security consulting in risk management and holds CREST approval for penetration testing.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber Security Consulting** - NCSC assured risk management consulting and penetration testing, including CHECK-scheme capability for public sector and CNI clients.
- **Secure by Design** - advisory and delivery support for secure cloud and classified solution design in defence programmes.
- **Digital Transformation** - business strategy, change and adoption, and digital solution delivery for defence and government organisations.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** NCSC Assured (Cyber Security Consulting), CREST approved penetration testing, ISO 27001 (UKAS certified), ISO 9001 (UKAS certified), Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
