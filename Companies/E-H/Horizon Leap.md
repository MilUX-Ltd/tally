# Horizon Leap

> Consultancy for remote and autonomous air systems, specialising in BVLOS operations, UAS strategy and regulatory guidance.

## What they do

Horizon Leap Innovations provides consultancy for remote and autonomous systems, with a focus on unmanned aircraft systems (UAS). It advises on Beyond Visual Line of Sight (BVLOS) operations, Atypical Air Environment guidance, UAS strategy and implementation, and test and evaluation. The work supports operators and organisations navigating regulation and operational planning for autonomous air capabilities.

## Products and solutions

- **BVLOS advisory** - guidance on Beyond Visual Line of Sight operations and regulation.
- **UAS strategy** - technology strategy and implementation for unmanned aircraft systems.
- **Test and evaluation** - support for trialling and evaluating autonomous air systems.
