# Extreme Cases

> UK manufacturer of bespoke rugged protective cases, containers, and backpacks for defence, military, and specialist field use.

## What they do

Extreme Cases designs and manufactures custom protective cases and carrying solutions for sensitive equipment transported and operated in demanding environments. The company supplies directly to defence customers including the UK MoD, Bundeswehr, NATO, and European Special Forces. Based in Barnstaple, North Devon, it holds an exclusive European distribution agreement with Zero Manufacturing, a US specialist case brand.

## Products and solutions

- **Bespoke protective cases** - custom-designed rigid cases for sensitive equipment, built to withstand transit and field conditions.
- **Rugged containers** - heavy-duty storage and transport containers for military use.
- **Field backpacks** - specialist carrying solutions for operational use.
- **Zero Manufacturing distribution** - exclusive European distributor for the Zero Manufacturing range of protective cases.
