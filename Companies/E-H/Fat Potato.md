# Fat Potato

> Defence-sector software consultancy specialising in cloud-native development, AWS migration and secure-by-design engineering.

## What they do

Fat Potato is a Cheltenham-based software consultancy that has operated primarily in the defence sector since its founding in 2016. The company delivers bespoke cloud-native software development, legacy system migration to AWS, and secure-by-design engineering for government and defence clients. It is an AWS Partner and is approved on the MOD's Neutral Vendor Framework for Innovation (Constellia NVFi). The company also develops an in-house HR platform product, Chinchill.HR.

## Products and solutions

- **Bespoke software development** - cloud-native, microservices-based software for defence and government customers.
- **AWS cloud transformation** - migration of legacy systems to Amazon Web Services, including architecture design and delivery.
- **Chinchill.HR** - in-house HR platform product.
- **Cyber resiliency and secure design** - Cyber Essentials Plus certified; applies secure-by-design principles across its delivery work.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** MOD Neutral Vendor Framework for Innovation (Constellia NVFi)
- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus; AWS Partner (AWS Partner Network)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; JOSCAR registered
