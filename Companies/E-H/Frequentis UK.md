# Frequentis (UK) Limited

> UK subsidiary of Frequentis AG, an Austrian-listed supplier of communication and information systems for safety-critical control centres, with a dedicated defence portfolio covering military ATC, secure voice, C2 and networks.

## What they do

Frequentis AG is headquartered in Vienna and listed on the Vienna and Frankfurt stock exchanges. It develops communication and information systems for safety-critical environments, serving air traffic management, defence, public safety, maritime and public transport sectors. The UK entity, Frequentis (UK) Limited, is registered in Twickenham. The Group employs more than 2,600 people and operates in over 50 countries. Frequentis is a long-standing supplier to UK air navigation service provider NATS, and has assumed delivery responsibility for the NATS Main Voice System and Arrivals Manager System as part of the Deploying SESAR project.

## Products and solutions

- **Military air traffic control (MilATC)** - modular ATC systems for military heliports, transport hubs, fighter, naval and joint-use airbases; includes tower automation integrating weather, flight data processing, surveillance and airbase lighting.
- **Secure voice** - communication systems for military and government control centres requiring security-accredited voice over IP and radio integration.
- **Remote digital tower** - replaces out-of-window tower views with a visualisation system; applicable to military ATC where physical tower presence is impractical.
- **Command and control** - information and communications systems enabling coordinated decision-making in air defence and multi-domain operations.
- **Networks** - mission-critical communications networks for defence and air defence applications.
- **Recording** - voice and data recording for safety-critical and evidential use.
