# Frost Business Management Partnership FBMP Ltd

> Commercial management and project management consultancy serving the defence, aerospace, maritime, cyber and national security sectors.

## What they do

FBMP provides commercial management, project management and business leadership services to organisations working in defence, aerospace, maritime, cyber and national security. The firm focuses on programme delivery, commercial performance improvement and internal capability development, and works with large OEMs and their supply chains in complex delivery environments.

## Products and solutions

- **P3M (Portfolio, Programme and Project Management)** - structured project and programme management services across complex defence and aerospace programmes.
- **Commercial Management** - commercial advisory and delivery support for organisations working with MOD and prime contractors.
