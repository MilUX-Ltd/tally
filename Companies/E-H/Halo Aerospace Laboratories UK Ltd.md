# Halo Aerospace Laboratories UK Ltd

> Non-destructive testing and surface treatment specialist serving the aerospace, defence, medical, nuclear and gas industries from a facility in Morecambe, Lancashire.

## What they do

Halo Aerospace Laboratories UK Ltd provides non-destructive testing (NDT) and materials testing services alongside a range of surface treatment processes for aerospace and defence customers. The company operates in-house and on-site facilities at its Heaton Business Park site in Morecambe and offers site inspection services throughout the UK and Europe. It holds NADCAP accreditation covering its NDT and surface treatment processes and works to customer-specific quality standards.

## Products and solutions

- **Non-destructive testing (NDT)** - eddy current (ET), magnetic particle inspection (MPI), penetrant flaw detection (PFD), hardness testing, ultrasonic testing (UT) and visual inspection.
- **Surface treatments** - chromic and sulphuric anodising, chromate conversion coating, passivation, wet and dry blasting, neutral salt spray (NSS) and humidity testing, and chemical analysis.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** NADCAP (NDT and surface treatment processes)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
