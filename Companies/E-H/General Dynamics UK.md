# General Dynamics UK

> UK subsidiary of US defence prime General Dynamics, delivering land systems and mission systems for the British armed forces and international allies.

## What they do

General Dynamics United Kingdom Limited operates two main business lines: Land Systems and Mission Systems. Land Systems designs, manufactures and supports armoured fighting vehicles and protected patrol vehicles for the British Army from a 57-acre site in Merthyr Tydfil, the largest active armoured vehicle production facility in the UK. Mission Systems, based in Hastings and Oakdale, delivers avionics, tactical communications, situational awareness systems, and secure data solutions for fixed-wing aircraft, rotary-wing platforms and ground vehicles. The company is a lead supplier to the Ministry of Defence and has been operating in the UK since 1962.

## Products and solutions

- **AJAX Armoured Fighting Vehicle** - family of tracked armoured reconnaissance vehicles for the British Army, assembled and integrated at Merthyr Tydfil.
- **Foxhound** - protected patrol vehicle for light infantry and specialist troops; a SHORAD variant has been demonstrated with Moog.
- **Bowman** - tactical communications and information system equipping British Army vehicles, naval vessels and aircraft; upgrades ongoing.
- **Multi Role Vehicle Protected (MRV-P)** - protected vehicle programme for the British Army.
- **Mission systems for air platforms** - avionics, stores management, video and data recording, and data-at-rest security integrated onto Eurofighter Typhoon, Hawk, AW101, AW159 Wildcat, M-346, F-16 and others.
- **Robotic Vehicles** - Tracked Robot 10-Ton (TRX) and Multi-Utility Tactical Transport (MUTT) unmanned ground vehicles.
