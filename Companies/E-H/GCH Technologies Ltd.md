# GCH Technologies Ltd

> Developer of physics-native software that enhances existing defence and security systems.

## What they do

GCH Technologies develops software that upgrades existing defence platforms rather than replacing hardware. Its capabilities span sensor detection and tracking, camouflage detection, GPS-independent navigation, unmanned platform survivability and synthetic training. The company is based in Amersham, Buckinghamshire.

## Products and solutions

- **VEGA** - detection and tracking optimisation for electro-optical and infrared sensors.
- **TrueSight** - camouflage and concealment detection.
- **TRINITY** - unified defensive system coordination.
- **ALT NAV** - GPS-independent autonomous navigation.
- **NEO** - unmanned platform survivability enhancement.
- **SCAR** - synthetic threat injection for training.
