# HashiCorp UK Ltd

> Developer of open-source and enterprise infrastructure automation software used to provision, secure, connect, and run cloud and on-premises environments; now operating as an IBM company.

## What they do

HashiCorp builds tools that address development, operations, and security challenges in infrastructure, enabling organisations to automate provisioning and manage secrets, networking, and application deployment. Its open-source and enterprise products are used across government, defence, and enterprise environments, including by US Department of Defense and US Air Force programmes for cloud migration and configuration management in air-gapped environments. HashiCorp is now an IBM company, following IBM's acquisition, and continues to operate under the HashiCorp brand.

## Products and solutions

- **Terraform** - infrastructure-as-code tool for provisioning cloud and on-premises resources, including Sentinel governance controls in the enterprise tier.
- **Vault** - secrets management and data encryption platform operating a zero-trust security model, used to manage tokens, passwords, certificates and encryption keys; the platform supports FIPS 140-2 and FIPS 140-3 validated cryptographic modules.
- **Consul** - service networking and service mesh solution, supporting FIPS 140-2 validated cryptographic modules.
- **Packer** - automated machine image creation tool.
