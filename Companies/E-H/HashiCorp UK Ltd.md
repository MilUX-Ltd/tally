# HashiCorp UK Ltd

> Developer of open-source and enterprise infrastructure automation software used to provision, secure, connect, and run cloud and on-premises environments.

## What they do

HashiCorp builds tools that address development, operations, and security challenges in infrastructure, enabling organisations to automate provisioning and manage secrets, networking, and application deployment. Its open-source products are widely used across government and enterprise environments, including defence.

## Products and solutions

- **Terraform** - Infrastructure-as-code tool for provisioning cloud and on-premises resources.
- **Vault** - Secrets management and data encryption platform.
- **Consul** - Service networking and service mesh solution.
- **Packer** - Automated machine image creation tool.
