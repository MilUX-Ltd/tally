# Helix

> A Milton Keynes-based manufacturer of high-power-density electric powertrains for aerospace and high-performance applications, formerly trading as Integral Powertrain.

## What they do

Helix designs and manufactures compact, power-dense electric motors and powertrains for aerospace, automotive, and industrial applications. Rebranded from Integral Powertrain, the company operates a flexible manufacturing facility in Milton Keynes and supplies machines under three Product Levels based on its Scalable Core Technology (SCT). In aerospace, the company focuses on performance-critical requirements where power density, efficiency, and reliability are primary considerations. Helix is currently engaged in hybrid-electric supersonic propulsion development and UK aerospace decarbonisation programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Helix Scalable Core Technology (SCT) motors** - High-power-density electric motors supplied across three product levels, from prototype to production, for aerospace and industrial customers.
- **Hybrid-electric powertrain systems** - Systems for aerospace applications including participation in the Astro Mechanica Duality hybrid-electric supersonic engine programme.
- **Custom electric drivelines** - Engineered-to-order electric and hybrid drivetrain solutions for specialist vehicle, marine, and aerospace platforms.
