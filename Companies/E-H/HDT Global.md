# HDT Global

> US manufacturer of deployable military shelters, environmental control, power generation, CBRN filtration and robotic systems.

## What they do

HDT Global designs and manufactures fully integrated deployable solutions for US and allied military units, including shelters, generators, heaters, environmental control units and air filtration systems. The company also operates a robotics division producing manipulators, actuators and mobile robotic vehicles for military, commercial, healthcare and underwater applications. Manufacturing of shelters, ECUs, generators, CBRN filters and robotic components is carried out in-house.

## Products and solutions

- **Military shelters** - soft-wall and rigid-wall deployable shelters and expeditionary command and control systems.
- **Environmental control units (ECUs)** - military-grade heating, cooling and air handling for deployable systems.
- **Power generation** - military generators for expeditionary use.
- **CBRN filtration** - chemical, biological, radiological and nuclear air filtration systems.
- **Robotics** - Adroit manipulators and actuators, plus mobile robotic vehicles.
