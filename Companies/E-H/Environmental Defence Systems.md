# Environmental Defence Systems

> Huddersfield manufacturer of blast protection, explosive ordnance disposal support equipment and aviation fire containment products, serving the British MoD, EOD departments and commercial aviation operators worldwide.

## What they do

Environmental Defence Systems Limited, trading as EDS Limited, invents, manufactures and supplies protective products used by military forces, emergency services and commercial aviation operators. Its BlastSax product is in active service with the British MoD and EOD departments; its AvSax product has become the world's best-selling aviation lithium battery fire containment bag. The company holds ADS Group and Counter EO UK membership.

## Products and solutions

- **BlastSax** - sandless sandbags that absorb water to form a blast and fragmentation protection barrier. Deployed by the British MoD and EOD departments, and used by police forces worldwide to contain small explosive devices during detonation, including two World War Two bombs disposed of by Hong Kong Police.
- **AvSax** - fire containment bags for in-flight lithium battery emergencies. Carried on more than 15,373 aircraft operated by 80 airlines. Winner of the Queen's Award for Enterprise (Innovation) in 2018.
- **FloodSax** - alternative sandbags for civil flood protection and liquid spill absorption.
