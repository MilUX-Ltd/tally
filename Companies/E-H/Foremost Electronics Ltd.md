# Foremost Electronics Ltd

> Engineering-led distributor of electromechanical components and electronic enclosures, including MIL-compliant solutions for defence and aerospace.

## What they do

Foremost Electronics (trading as 4Most) is an engineering-led importer and specialist distributor of electromechanical components and electronic systems. It supplies cabinets, enclosures, subracks, power and thermal management, and control and monitoring systems, including bespoke designs. The company serves the aerospace and defence sectors with MIL-compliant, ruggedised solutions for mission-critical applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Cabinets and enclosures** - standard and bespoke housings for electronic systems.
- **Subracks and systems** - modular packaging including MIL-compliant options.
- **Power and thermal management** - power supplies and thermal control components.
- **Aluminium transit cases** - ruggedised cases for field and transport use.
- **Switches and thermal fuses** - control and protection components.
