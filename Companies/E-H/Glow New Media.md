> Small UK software business specialising in rapid proof-of-concept development for mobile, location-aware, real-time bespoke systems.

Glow New Media is an agile software firm focused on building rapid proof-of-concept applications for mobile and location-aware use cases. The company emphasises real-time, bespoke system development and is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
