# Glow New Media

> Small UK software and mobile app development agency, based at Sci-Tech Daresbury, specialising in real-time, location-aware bespoke systems and a member of the North West Regional Defence & Security Cluster.

## What they do

Glow New Media is a small, agile UK software and mobile app development agency, describing itself as pragmatic and highly capable across mobile app design and development (iOS and Android), website design and build, and bespoke software production. The company states particular strength in real-time positioning, mobile and web architecture, and enterprise integration, with healthcare and healthtech named as an area of specific expertise. Past clients cited publicly include NHS trusts and National Rail.

## Products and solutions

- **SafeTeam Guardian** - a lone-worker safety system and app, described as adopted by Global Digital Exemplar NHS trusts and community services; available via G-Cloud.
- **SafeTeam NetZero** - an extension of the SafeTeam platform for real-time tracking of NHS "Grey Fleet" vehicle use against Net Zero performance.
