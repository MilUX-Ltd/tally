# Hanwha Aerospace UK Ltd

> UK entity of South Korean defence and aerospace manufacturer Hanwha Aerospace, covering land systems, artillery, aerospace propulsion and space.

## What they do

Hanwha Aerospace UK Ltd is the British registered subsidiary of Hanwha Aerospace Co. Ltd, headquartered in Seoul, South Korea. The parent company develops and produces self-propelled artillery systems, infantry fighting vehicles, aircraft engines, aerospace components and space launch equipment. The UK entity supports Hanwha's strategic expansion across Europe and engagement with NATO member states, and was incorporated in September 2023. Hanwha Aerospace has appointed a UK-based CEO to lead its European and UK defence business.

## Products and solutions

- **K9 Thunder** - self-propelled 155mm howitzer, in service with multiple NATO allies and partner nations.
- **AS21 Redback** - tracked infantry fighting vehicle with a 30mm Mk44S Bushmaster II cannon, offered to allied nations including Australia.
- **Aerospace propulsion** - aircraft engines and propulsion components for civil and military applications.
- **Space systems** - launch vehicle components and space-related equipment.
