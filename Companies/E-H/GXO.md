# GXO Logistics

> Contract logistics provider operating an active UK defence supply chain business, including warehousing and 4PL services for BAE Systems' Type 26 frigate programme.

## What they do

GXO Logistics is a US-listed, pure-play contract logistics business operating across more than 970 facilities globally. In the UK, GXO provides warehousing, materials handling and 4PL control-tower services to aerospace and defence customers. The business expanded its UK defence footprint significantly following the acquisition of Wincanton in 2024. GXO holds a six-year agreement, announced in February 2026, with BAE Systems to support shipbuilding operations at the Scotstoun and Govan yards on the River Clyde for the Type 26 frigate programme. It is also a founding member of the Torus alliance, alongside Amentum, Accenture and Maersk, established to provide integrated supply chain solutions to the UK defence sector. GXO holds Gold Award status under the UK Defence Employer Recognition Scheme.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence logistics operations** - warehousing and materials handling at defence manufacturing and shipbuilding sites, including BAE Systems' Clyde yards.
- **4PL control tower** - coordination of inbound and outbound transport operations across a distributed defence supply chain.
- **Aerospace and defence supply chain solutions** - end-to-end logistics management for aerospace and defence OEMs, drawing on over two decades of sector experience across more than 30 global A&D sites.
