# HazmatLINK Ltd

> UK supplier of CBRN and hazardous materials detection equipment to first responders, military specialists, and emergency services.

## What they do

HazmatLINK, established in 2004, provides detection products and integration solutions for hazardous materials management and emergency response. The company supplies equipment that identifies unknown substances, including chemical and biological hazards, drugs, and explosive precursors, to first responders and military specialists. It also provides consultancy and planning advice to emergency services and government clients on managing incidents involving hazardous materials, including deliberate CBRN releases.

## Products and solutions

- **FirstDefender RM / RMX** - handheld Raman spectrometer for chemical identification in hazmat and CBRN contexts.
- **TruDefender FTX** - portable FTIR analyser for chemical threat identification.
- **TruNarc Tau** - narcotics and chemical identification device with a library of over 1,200 substances, ruggedised with a user-replaceable battery and Wi-Fi connectivity.
- **Gemini** - dual-technology analyser combining Raman and FTIR for chemical identification.
- **Spotfire** - modular biological detection system with a base station and up to four concurrent sample modules, replacing the FilmArray system.
- **SEEKERe / Chameleon / TraceX / ERG 2024** - additional detection and emergency response tools for CBRN, EOD, and hazmat markets.
