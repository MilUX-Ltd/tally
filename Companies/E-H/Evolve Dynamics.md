# Evolve Dynamics

> British designer and manufacturer of all-weather, long-endurance unmanned air systems.

## What they do

Evolve Dynamics is a UK engineering company that designs, develops and manufactures unmanned air systems, payloads and software. Its systems are built for extreme-weather, persistent operation and are used across defence, police and security, search and rescue, and infrastructure inspection. The company reports field experience supporting Ukrainian forces in contested airspace.

## Products and solutions

- **Sky Mantis** - all-weather, payload-agnostic long-endurance UAS, capable of battery or tethered persistent flight for ISR and force protection.
- **Sky Mantis 2** - upgraded variant unveiled in 2024 with anti-jamming datalinks, improved GNSS resilience and high-resolution imaging, developed with front-line user feedback.
