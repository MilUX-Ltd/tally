# EW Solutions Ltd

> Electronic warfare, CEMA, and OSINT consultancy and intelligence products company serving defence organisations and government agencies.

## What they do

EW Solutions Ltd (trading as EWS) is a security-cleared SME founded in 2009, specialising in electronic warfare, Cyber and Electromagnetic Activities (CEMA), and OSINT-driven intelligence. The company provides vendor-agnostic consultancy, proprietary open-source intelligence products, and EW training and engineering support. Customers include armed forces, national agencies, government bodies, and defence industry OEMs. Registered and operational at Long Bennington Business Park, Nottinghamshire.

## Products and solutions

- **OSTD (Open Source Threat Database)** - unclassified, daily-updated global repository of IED and RCIED events; over 40,000 entries; used by national agencies for threat intelligence.
- **OverSite** - UxV threat database tracking UAV, UGV, USV, and UUV events, tactics, techniques and procedures, and ECM development data; available standalone or alongside OSTD.
- **Country Reports and RFI service** - tailored regional threat assessments with multilingual analysis.
- **EW and CEMA consultancy** - capability development, procurement support, red team assessments, bid support, market and competitor analysis, and technology integration for OEMs seeking routes to market.
- **EW and CEMA training** - covers JSP822 Training Needs Analysis, CEMA doctrine development, and EOD support; delivered in the UK and in deployed environments.
- **Engineering and Field Service** - technical support and Field Service Representatives (FSRs) for EW system lifecycle support.
