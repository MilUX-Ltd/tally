# ESET UK

> Global cybersecurity company with a dedicated defence, military and government tier and a UK office in Bournemouth.

## What they do

ESET is a Slovak-headquartered cybersecurity company, founded in 1992, with a UK entity (ESET Software UK Limited) based in Bournemouth since 2004. The UK office is ESET's regional head sales office. The company provides endpoint protection, managed detection and response (MDR), extended detection and response (XDR), and threat intelligence products to home, SME, enterprise and public-sector clients. ESET PRIVATE, launched globally in 2026, is a dedicated tier for defence organisations, military, government agencies and critical national infrastructure operators, supporting air-gapped, on-premise and restricted-bandwidth environments.

## Products and solutions

- **ESET PRIVATE** - custom-engineered security solutions for defence, military, government and CNI clients; supports air-gapped and fully isolated deployments.
- **ESET PROTECT** - XDR platform for extended detection and response across endpoints, cloud and network; ranked in G2 Winter 2026 XDR Report.
- **Threat Intelligence** - ESET is recognised as a leading publisher of APT research, particularly on state-sponsored groups targeting critical infrastructure in Europe.
