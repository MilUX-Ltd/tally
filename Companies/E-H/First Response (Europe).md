# First Response (Europe)

> Digital forensics and cyber incident response firm serving law enforcement, public sector, and corporate clients.

## What they do

First Response (Europe) provides digital forensics, cyber incident response, and cybercrime investigation services. Their capabilities cover acquisition and analysis of electronic evidence, server breach investigation and remediation, malware reverse engineering, forensic readiness planning, and litigation support. The company also delivers cybercrime investigation and computer forensics training internationally to law enforcement and corporate audiences.

## Products and solutions

- **Digital forensics** - acquisition and analysis of electronic evidence for legal proceedings and internal investigations.
- **Cyber incident response** - 24/7 breach investigation, server remediation, and malware reverse engineering.
- **Forensic Readiness Planning** - advisory and policy work to prepare organisations for evidence preservation requirements.
- **Cybercrime training** - investigative and forensic training programmes for law enforcement and corporate teams.
