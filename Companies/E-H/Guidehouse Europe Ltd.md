# Guidehouse Europe Ltd

> Global professional services firm delivering advisory, technology, and managed services to national security and defence clients, with a UK entity based in London.

## What they do

Guidehouse is an AI-led professional services firm providing advisory, technology, and managed services to government and commercial clients including the national security and defence markets. The firm operates across healthcare, financial services, energy, infrastructure, and national security sectors, with approximately 18,000 professionals globally. The UK entity, Guidehouse Europe Ltd, supports defence and national security engagements from its London office.
