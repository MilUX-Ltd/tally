# Guidehouse Europe Ltd

> Global professional services firm delivering advisory, technology, and managed services to national security and defence clients, with a UK entity based in London.

## What they do

Guidehouse is a global AI-led professional services firm delivering advisory, technology, and managed services to commercial and government clients, with an integrated business technology approach across healthcare, financial services, energy, infrastructure, and national security markets. Its Defense & Security industry group supports diplomatic, intelligence, law enforcement, homeland security, and defence agencies, applying strategy, management, technology, and risk guidance to operational optimisation, technology modernisation, warfighting capabilities, and financial management. The firm states it employs approximately 18,000 professionals globally. Guidehouse Europe Ltd is the UK entity, based in London, and the firm maintains a wider UK and Europe, Middle East and Canada regional presence alongside operations in Germany, India and Lithuania.

## Products and solutions

- **Defense, Diplomacy & Intelligence** - strategy, management, technology, and risk advisory for defence, diplomatic and intelligence agencies.
- **Homeland Security & Law Enforcement** - advisory support for border security, customs, cybersecurity, and crisis management challenges.
- **AI & Data** - services helping clients apply AI, data, and analytics to decision-making, productivity, and strategic objectives.
- **Cybersecurity** - tailored services to protect critical assets and reduce vulnerabilities, aligned to client risk profiles and operational requirements.
- **Financial Management** - data and technology-led advisory across risk, controls, compliance, and financial systems.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Other:** prime supplier on 7 of 9 lots of the UK government's Management Consulting Framework Three (MCF3), covering strategy and policy, complex transformation, finance, HR, procurement and supply chain, infrastructure and transport, and environmental sustainability and socio-economic development.
