# Elbit Systems UK

> UK subsidiary of Israeli defence prime Elbit Systems, delivering UAS, electronic warfare, optronics, battle management, and military training systems to the UK Armed Forces.

## What they do

Elbit Systems UK Limited is the UK holding entity for Elbit Systems' British operations, comprising six subsidiaries and two joint ventures across sixteen sites and approximately 680 employees. The company is engaged in multiple major UK defence programmes spanning uncrewed aerial systems, electronic warfare, optronics, command and control, and military flight training. It operates manufacturing, design, and support facilities in England.

## Products and solutions

- **Watchkeeper WK450 UAS** - tactical uncrewed aerial system delivered to the British Army through UAV Tactical Systems Ltd (UTacS), which Elbit fully acquired in January 2026.
- **MORPHEUS Battle Management Application** - command and control software for British Army headquarters and tactical units, based on the TORC2 H platform.
- **MEWSIC (Maritime Electronic Warfare Systems Integrated Capability) Increment 1** - electronic warfare suites for the Royal Navy, provided under a 13-year, £100 million contract with Babcock and QinetiQ.
- **UK Military Flying Training System (MFLTS)** - elementary and basic fast-jet training aircraft and simulators delivered through the Affinity Flying Training Services joint venture with KBR.
- **Night Vision Goggles** - produced at a Centre of Excellence for electro-optics in Kent, supplying the British Army and allied nations.
- **Joint Fires Synthetic Training (JFST)** - simulators supporting Joint Terminal Attack Controller and Fire Support Team training for the British Army.
