# Fleetwind

> ISO 9001-accredited naval systems engineering consultancy specialising in platform integration, safety assurance, and in-service support for complex warship programmes.

## What they do

Fleetwind Ltd is a maritime systems engineering company formed in 2019 and registered in Polruan, Cornwall. The company works directly with the Royal Navy, UK MOD, and defence primes across the supply chain, with its business predominantly in the defence sector. Fleetwind specialises in the integration and sustainment of complex naval platforms, covering the transition of ships and systems from the build environment into service. The team is drawn from ex-Naval personnel, business leaders, and specialist engineers with experience on high-profile programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Platform integration and assurance** - systems engineering support for the transition of naval platforms and systems into the in-service environment, including Type 26, Type 31, and QE-Class programmes.
- **Safety case and certification** - delivery of Safety Case Reports, Naval Authority Certification submissions, and supporting assurance across Ordnance, Munitions and Explosives (OME), aviation safety, and integrated platform assurance for surface and sub-surface environments.
- **Ship-Air Integration** - development of Interface Control Documents, Ship Aviation Safety Case Reports, and Ship Air Release recommendations, supporting Naval Authority certification submissions.
- **In-service support** - integrated logistic support (ILS) disciplines, in-service support solution development, and business management advice.
