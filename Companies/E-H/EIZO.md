# EIZO

> Japanese display manufacturer producing rugged, TEMPEST-compliant and MIL-STD-810 qualified monitors for defence C4ISR, air traffic control, and maritime applications.

## What they do

EIZO Corporation develops high-end display solutions across professional, healthcare, maritime, and defence markets. Its dedicated defence subsidiary, EIZO Rugged Solutions Inc. (Orlando, Florida), has over 30 years' experience in military ISR display and embedded processing, supplying rugged LCD monitors and GPGPU cards qualified to MIL-STD-810 and TEMPEST standards. The UK entity, EIZO Limited (Companies House 07738824, Ascot, Berkshire), handles commercial and professional display distribution; defence-specific UK sales are supported by Sarsen Technology as a UK integration partner.

## Products and solutions

- **Talon rugged LCD monitors** - MIL-STD-810 qualified, TEMPEST-compliant, sunlight-readable monitors in 24-, 28-, and 32-inch formats for vehicle, shipborne, and ground station applications.
- **Embedded GPGPU and video processing** - SOSA-aligned XMC and VPX cards for OpenVPX C4ISR integration, including H.264/265 encoders and video splitters.
- **Sonar and radar display variants** - specialist 2K×2K and stereoscopic display configurations for sonar visualisation, radar, and chart table navigation.
