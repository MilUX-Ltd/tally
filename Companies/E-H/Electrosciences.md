# Electrosciences Ltd

> UK materials science consultancy specialising in piezoelectric, ferroelectric, and multifunctional materials, with applications in military sensors, sonar, and structural materials.

## What they do

Electrosciences provides scientific consultancy and contract R&D in multifunctional materials, covering piezoelectric, ferroelectric, pyroelectric, multiferroic, magnetoelectric, and thermoelectric systems. Services include measurement, characterisation, metrology, standards development, and the development of bespoke instruments. The company also supplies commercial instruments for piezoelectric materials characterisation.

## Products and solutions

- **Piezoelectric Materials d33 Analyser** - instrument for measuring piezoelectric coefficients in materials and components.
- **LIMM Analyser** - laser intensity modulation method instrument for pyroelectric and piezoelectric profiling.
- **StressCell (Multifunctional Stress Rig)** - test rig for combined mechanical and electrical characterisation of materials.
- **ESPY33** - piezoelectric measurement tool developed in collaboration with aixACCT Systems GmbH.
- **Contract R&D and characterisation services** - testing and modelling of multifunctional materials for defence, aerospace, and industrial clients.

## Defence and aerospace applications

The company's publicly stated military and defence applications include sonar transduction, adaptive camouflage materials, self-healing structural materials, sensor and actuator arrays for military platforms, functionally graded armour materials, and super-hydrophobic surface coatings for military equipment.
