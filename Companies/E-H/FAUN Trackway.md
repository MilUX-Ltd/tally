# FAUN Trackway

> Designer and manufacturer of portable aluminium Trackway ground stabilisation systems for military and specialist civil applications.

## What they do

FAUN Trackway is a Welsh manufacturer of portable, recoverable Trackway panel systems used by military engineers worldwide to provide rapid access across soft ground, mud, marsh, snow and sand. Originating in wartime aluminium fabrication on the Isle of Anglesey from 1940, the company has supplied the UK MOD since the 1960s and exports to numerous NATO and partner-nation armed forces. It operates three divisions: Trackway Defence, Trackway Access (civil) and Trackway Engineering. The company is part of the German KIRCHHOFF Group (FAUN KIRCHHOFF GRUPPE). A US subsidiary, FAUN Trackway USA Inc., is based in Washington DC.

## Products and solutions

- **Military Trackway panels** - aluminium alloy portable roadway panels rated to MLC 30, MLC 60, MLC 70 and MLC 150; recoverable and redeployable.
- **Heavy Ground Mobility System (HGMS)** - vehicle-mounted rapid deployment and recovery system for high-load Trackway.
- **Medium Ground Mobility System (MGMS)** - vehicle-mounted system for medium-load Trackway deployment.
- **Airfield Damage Repair mats** - aluminium matting for rapid airfield surface repair.
- **Helicopter landing systems** - Containerised Helipad ("Helipad in a Box") and UAV and aircraft landing pads.
- **Over-the-shore access matting** - marine ingress and egress systems.
- **Engineering services** - bespoke manufacturing for defence and nuclear sectors.
