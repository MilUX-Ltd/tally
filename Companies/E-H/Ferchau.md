# Ferchau

> German-headquartered engineering services group delivering specialist aerospace and defence engineering from its UK base at Bournemouth Airport; Airbus Preferred Supplier for engineering and customer services.

## What they do

Ferchau Limited is the UK entity of FERCHAU GmbH, a privately held German engineering services group founded in 1966. The UK operation, incorporated in January 2011, is based at Aviation Park West, Bournemouth Airport, with a second site in Bristol. It delivers specialist engineering services to the aerospace and defence supply chain, including structural analysis, systems design (fuel, hydraulics, and electrical systems), vibration and operational stability analyses, aerospace certification support, and embedded engineering and testing. The group's Aviation Group subsidiary RST Rostock manufactures harness test systems and cabin simulators for military platforms including Eurofighter. FERCHAU is not listed as an ADS Group member.

## Products and solutions

- **Structural and systems engineering** - stress calculations, design verification, systems design for aerospace and defence applications, delivered from Bournemouth and Bristol.
- **Certification support** - specialist assistance through the Airbus and wider aerospace regulatory certification process.
- **Embedded engineering and test** - embedded development and testing capability for aerospace and defence programmes.
- **Harness test systems and cabin simulators** (RST Rostock, group subsidiary) - manufactured test equipment and simulators for civil, military, and space programmes including Eurofighter.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, AS9100 (aviation division), ISO 27001 (aviation division), ISO 14001, ISO 45001
