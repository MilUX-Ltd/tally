# EP90 Group

> Specialist consultancy in security and intelligence, automotive cyber security, and private mobile networks for government and defence clients.

## What they do

EP90 Group operates across three practices: security and intelligence, automotive cyber security, and mobile telecoms. Its founders bring backgrounds in UK government interception agencies and complex security investigations, and its client portfolio spans military, policing, government, and adjacent industries. The telecoms practice focuses on 4G LTE and 5G standalone private network design, with a national cyber capacity building strand.

## Products and solutions

- **Security and intelligence consultancy** - Communications intelligence, cyber threat intelligence, industrial control system (ICS) security, and confidential investigations with a digital dimension.
- **Automotive cyber security testing** - Bespoke component, sub-assembly, and whole-vehicle testing; Attacker-in-the-Loop (AIL) testing integrating security into the embedded-system development lifecycle for connected and autonomous vehicles.
- **Private network design** - 4G LTE and 5G standalone private networks; national cyber capacity building.
