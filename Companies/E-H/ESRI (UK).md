# ESRI (UK) Limited

> UK distributor and services partner for Esri's ArcGIS platform, and the MOD's Strategic Geospatial Partner.

## What they do

Esri UK provides geographic information system (GIS) software, location intelligence and mapping services to public sector, defence and commercial customers across the United Kingdom. It is the authorised distributor and services arm of Esri Inc (US parent) in the UK and Ireland. The company holds a long-standing strategic relationship with the UK Ministry of Defence, most recently confirmed under a £46 million enterprise agreement covering the full suite of ArcGIS products across the MOD's information architecture, including land, maritime and air domains.

## Products and solutions

- **ArcGIS platform** - full suite of Esri GIS products covering field operations, mapping, data science, imagery, situational awareness and data collection across MOD domains.
- **Defence and National Security services** - professional services, integration and support for defence customers including battle management, ISR, air operations and maritime operations use cases.
- **Strategic Geospatial Partnership** - managed GIS technology access, licencing and support under the MOD enterprise agreement, enabling location-based intelligence and digital transformation across the Armed Forces.
