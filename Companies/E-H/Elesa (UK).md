# Elesa (UK) Ltd

> UK subsidiary of Elesa S.p.A. (Italy), supplying certified standard machine elements and industrial components to the defence, aerospace, and advanced manufacturing sectors.

## What they do

Elesa (UK) Ltd is the UK subsidiary of Elesa S.p.A., established in 1991 and based in Metheringham, Lincolnshire. The company supplies standard machine elements in plastic and metal, including knobs, handles, levers, hinges, fasteners, clamps, levelling elements, and position indicators, marketed to defence and aerospace manufacturers and MRO suppliers.

Defence and aerospace-relevant product lines include ATEX-certified control elements for explosive-atmosphere environments, INOX stainless steel ranges (AISI 303, 304, 316L) for corrosive and naval environments, Visual Detectable (VD) components for inspection-critical applications, and vibration-damping parts for platform-mounted equipment. Elesa (UK) exhibited at DSEI 2025 to market these product lines directly to the defence and security sector.

## Products and solutions

- **ATEX-certified components** - IECEx/ATEX zone 1 and zone 21 certified control elements, knobs, and levers for explosive-atmosphere defence and offshore applications.
- **INOX stainless steel range** - AISI 303, 304, and 316L components for naval, offshore, and aerospace environments requiring corrosion resistance.
- **Visual Detectable (VD) components** - safety-oriented components designed for inspection visibility in defence and critical-industry applications.
- **Vibration-damping elements** - components for mobile and platform-mounted military and industrial equipment.

## Ownership note

Elesa (UK) Ltd is a subsidiary of Elesa S.p.A., headquartered in Monza (Milan), Italy, founded 1941. The UK entity was established in 1991 with operational facilities at Metheringham, Lincolnshire.
