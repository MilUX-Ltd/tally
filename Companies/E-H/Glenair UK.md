# Glenair UK

> UK subsidiary of Glenair Inc (Glendale, California), manufacturing and supplying high-reliability interconnect solutions for military aerospace, land systems, and space applications.

## What they do

Glenair UK Limited is the UK manufacturing and sales entity of Glenair Inc, a US privately-held interconnect manufacturer headquartered in Glendale, California. The Mansfield, Nottinghamshire site has supplied the military aerospace sector with interconnect solutions for over 40 years. The company manufactures and stocks connectors, backshells and accessories, fibre optic interconnects, harness solutions, hermetic connectors, RF and microwave connectors, and space mechanisms. Its products are used in military aircraft, ground vehicles, soldier systems, and space vehicles. Glenair UK holds JOSCAR registration, AS/EN/JISQ 9100, Cyber Essentials Plus, and ISO 14001.

## Products and solutions

- **Military-specification connectors** - Circular and rectangular Mil-Spec qualified connectors in environmental, filtered, hermetic, and fibre optic configurations, for military aerospace and land systems.
- **Backshells and accessories** - Conduit fittings, EMI/RFI shielding accessories, and strain relief backshells for defence wiring systems.
- **Fibre optic interconnects** - Optical connector and cable assembly solutions for military and space platforms.
- **RF and microwave connectors** - High-frequency interconnect solutions for radar, electronic warfare, and communications applications.
- **Hermetic connectors** - Sealed connector solutions for demanding military and space environments.
- **Space mechanisms** - Hold-down and release mechanisms (HDRM) and related space interconnect products.
- **STAR-PAN harness solutions** - Integrated harness panel systems for complex military wiring installations.
