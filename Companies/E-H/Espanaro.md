# Espanaro

> Veteran-founded defence engineering consultancy specialising in autonomous system security, C5I architecture and the Consilium command orchestration platform.

## What they do

Espanaro is a systems engineering and software engineering company based in Newtown, Wales, incorporated in 2020 and founded by a veteran-led team with backgrounds in mathematics, theoretical physics and computer science. The company delivers systems integration and secure engineering solutions to the UK defence sector, with stated clients including the Ministry of Defence, Defence Science and Technology Laboratory (Dstl) and the Nuclear Decommissioning Authority (NDA). Capability areas include autonomous system security, life-critical systems security, data-centric security, cyber security and C5I solutions and architectures.

## Products and solutions

- **Consilium** - automated technology policy management system providing decision support and command orchestration, integrating cameras, fixed sensors, autonomous drones and UGVs into a sense-reason-decide-effect capability. Deployed on a 12-month T&E programme for a UK Government CNI client and completed final acceptance testing with the NDA.
