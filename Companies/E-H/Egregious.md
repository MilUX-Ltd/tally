# Egregious

> London-based AI startup providing narrative intelligence and SOCMINT capabilities for national security, defence, and regulated industries.

## What they do

Egregious builds AI-powered analysis tools to detect and counter disinformation, malign influence operations, and AI-driven deception. Their platform maps the relationships between online narratives to deliver actionable intelligence. The company serves national security and defence clients alongside finance and corporate security customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Narrative Graph** - an AI-driven map of online narratives used to identify and track influence operations and disinformation campaigns.
- **SOCMINT Platform** - private and secure social media intelligence for national security and defence use cases.
- **Narrative Intelligence** - tools for detecting AI-generated content, polarisation, and coordinated inauthentic behaviour across open-source channels.
