# Fischer Connectors

> Swiss-headquartered manufacturer of high-performance electrical and fibre-optic connectors for military, aerospace, and industrial applications; UK entity based in Waterlooville, Hampshire.

## What they do

Fischer Connectors designs, manufactures, and distributes electrical connectors, fibre-optic connectors, cable assemblies, and connectivity systems for demanding environments. Defence is a primary vertical: products are tested to MIL-SPEC and IEC standards and are used across dismounted soldier, ground vehicle, aerospace, and marine applications. The company operates under the parent group brand Conextivity Group, with the Swiss parent Fischer Connectors SA as the global headquarters. The UK entity (Fischer Connectors Ltd, incorporated 1988) handles sales and distribution for the British market.

## Products and solutions

- **KEYSTONE tactical hub** - a connectivity platform built for the Internet of Battlefield Things (IoBT), with NATO STANAG compatibility and Nett Warrior integration.
- **MiniMax 06** - an ultra-miniature connector targeting handheld defence devices and UAVs.
- **SS Series and SSC Series** - short-plug miniature connectors for high pin-density, space-saving military and instrumentation use (launched January 2026).
- **Fibre-optic connectors and cable assemblies** - for harsh-environment military communications and sensor systems.
