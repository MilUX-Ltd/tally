# heliguy

> UK drone specialist and DJI supplier providing drone hardware, CAA-approved training, surveying, rental and repairs.

## What they do

heliguy is the trading brand of Colena Ltd, a UK drone specialist established in 2006 and based in North Shields. It provides end-to-end drone programmes spanning hardware supply, CAA-approved training, rental, surveying, repairs and regulatory consultancy. It positions itself as a drone partner supporting commercial and public-safety operations, including BVLOS and drone-in-a-box deployments.

## Products and solutions

- **Drone supply** - DJI enterprise and consumer drones (including Matrice series, Dock 3 drone-in-a-box and FlyCart 30 delivery drone) plus accessories.
- **Training** - CAA-approved and Ofqual-regulated courses, including BVLOS and UK SORA pathway support; states over 9,000 candidates trained.
- **Drone hire** - drone rental fleet for short-term and project use.
- **Survey Assist** - end-to-end drone surveying with in-house GIS specialists, LiDAR and photogrammetry payloads, and data processing.
- **Repair centre** - in-house DJI-accredited repair and servicing.
- **Consultancy** - account-managed drone-programme build from proof of concept to regulatory support.
- **Sector solutions** - utilities, Drone as First Responder, security and surveillance, and reality capture.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** UK CAA-approved training provider (Recognised Assessment Entity); DJI-accredited technicians and DJI Enterprise partner; Ofqual-regulated qualifications including a TQUK Level 5 award.
