# Guartel Technologies Ltd

> UK manufacturer of metal, mine, and command wire detectors and Counter-IED systems for military and law enforcement operations worldwide.

## What they do

Guartel Technologies Ltd designs, develops, and manufactures metal detection, mine detection, and command wire detection equipment. The company also supplies bespoke Counter-IED and EOD systems, including specialist operational training courses. Its products are in service with police forces, security services, NGOs, the UK MoD, NATO, and military forces worldwide.

## Products and solutions

- **Metal Detectors** - Range includes the HD7, MD4, MD5, MD8+, MD3000, and DIVETECT, designed for security and Counter-IED operations.
- **Command Wire Detectors** - WD10, WDTX, and WireTect systems for detecting command-initiated IED threats.
- **EOD and CIED Equipment** - H&L Kit, Mini H&L Kit, LCR CIED, MDA 450, demining visor, IEDD van, and mine prodder products.
- **CIED Training** - Specialist EOD and CIED operational training courses.
