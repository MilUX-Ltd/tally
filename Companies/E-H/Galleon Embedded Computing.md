# Galleon Embedded Computing Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of rugged data recorders, mission computers, and network-attached storage for aerospace and defence platforms.

## What they do

Galleon Embedded Computing develops high-performance, ruggedised data recording and storage solutions for military and commercial aerospace applications. Products span airborne, ground vehicle, and remotely operated vehicle platforms. The company became part of the Spectra Aerospace and Defense group in August 2021 and now trades under the Spectra brand, with the UK registered entity (Companies House 09962683) providing a UK presence.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged data recorders** - IRIG-106 compliant recorders supporting high-speed multi-sensor data capture, with removable storage up to 64TB.
- **Mission computers and servers** - VPX and XSR-series systems including the XSR Tactical Secure Server, meeting MIL-STD-810, MIL-STD-704, MIL-STD-461 and DO-160 standards.
- **Network-attached storage (NAS)** - High-capacity NAS devices for deployed military environments across land, sea, air, and space domains.
- **C5ISR integrated systems** - End-to-end data capture, processing, storage, and display solutions built to work across platforms without separate integration risk.
