# Helsing

> Software-native defence company building AI-enabled autonomous systems across air, land, sea and space.

## What they do

Helsing develops AI software and autonomous systems for defence, connecting existing hardware into AI-enabled networks and designing its own platforms across all domains. The company describes defence as having become a software problem, requiring a software-native company to develop advanced AI algorithms and architectures. It is headquartered in Munich and has built a UK presence, including a manufacturing facility in Plymouth.

## Products and solutions

- **Altra** - reconnaissance-strike software platform for land combat, providing AI mission planning and command and control for autonomous systems.
- **HX-2** - software-defined, mass-produceable AI strike drone with a range up to around 100 km.
- **Centaur** - AI air-combat software acting as an autonomous pilot for existing and future fighter aircraft.
- **CA-1 Europa** - autonomous multi-role uncrewed combat aerial vehicle.
- **SG-1 Fathom** - autonomous underwater glider designed and manufactured in Britain.
- **Lura** - maritime AI platform for persistent underwater surveillance and submarine detection, paired with SG-1 Fathom.
