# Gibson Robotics

> Developer of autonomous uncrewed aerial systems for counter-UAS and critical infrastructure protection.

## What they do

Gibson Robotics designs and manufactures autonomous fixed-wing and multirotor UAS for security applications at sensitive sites and critical national infrastructure. The company focuses on detecting, tracking, and neutralising aerial and ground threats in real time. Its systems deploy in seconds and use patented net-capture mechanisms to effect aerial threats with low collateral risk.

## Products and solutions

- **Red Kite** - High-speed autonomous fixed-wing UAS capable of intercepting aerial threats and capturing them using a patented net-capture mechanism. Equipped with sensors for visual detection, tracking, and threat identification.
- **Shadow Hawk** - Quadcopter UAS with stabilised pan-tilt-zoom thermal and RGB camera, active internal heating and cooling, triple-redundant hardware and software, and navigation and strobe lighting.
