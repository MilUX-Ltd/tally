# Halochrome
> Bath-based IT and business-change consultancy specialising in sovereign high-grade encryption, post-quantum cryptography, and complex defence and security programme delivery.
## What they do
Halochrome works with defence and security organisations on IT consultancy and business change, with a focus on outcome and benefit realisation across complex programmes. The firm's technical speciality has matured toward sovereign encryption and post-quantum cryptography, including securing communications downlinks on larger surveillance platforms where imagery remains sensitive over extended periods. The company reports direct contracts with NCSC and the MOD. Incorporated May 2020; an Armed Forces Covenant signatory.
## Products and solutions
- **Encryption and post-quantum cryptography** - Sovereign high-grade encryption implementation for classified and sensitive networks.
- **Security assurance** - Risk assessments, security audits, ISMS design, policy and training.
- **Programme and portfolio management** - Delivery of complex government programmes including systems engineering and project management.
