# Heruon

> Developer of AI-driven facial and object recognition technology and cell-phone signal detection systems for policing, counter-terrorism, and security applications.

## What they do

Heruon develops proprietary AI and hardware for ethical and accurate facial and object recognition, with applications in counter-terrorism, policing, and public and private sector security. The company also produces technology capable of identifying signals emitted by mobile phones and other devices, and has received recognition from the UK Home Office for its approach to security innovation.

## Products and solutions

- **Facial and object recognition systems** - AI-powered identification technology for policing and counter-terrorism, built on proprietary and patented approaches.
- **Device signal detection** - Technology for identifying signals emitted by mobile phones and other electronic devices.
