# ExTrac AI

> British OSINT company providing an AI-powered intelligence platform for defence and security customers tracking conflict, grey zone, and influence activities.

## What they do

ExTrac AI Limited is a British Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) company serving allied nations. It provides both an AI SaaS platform and bespoke intelligence reporting, sourcing, cleaning, and structuring hard-to-access adversary data to deliver expert insight at machine speed. The platform finds, curates, and ingests high-relevance data sources including communications and kinetic behaviours of hostile state and non-state actors, mining them for real-time insights on conflict, grey zone, and influence activities using AI that is both secure and compliant by design. Defence and security applications include force protection, situational awareness, operational planning, and Information Environment and Battle Damage Assessment.

## Products and solutions

- **ExTrac platform (web and mobile)** - AI SaaS platform combining curated OSINT data, domain-specific AI, and human expertise; accessible via web app, mobile app, and API, with configurable dashboards, alerts, and reporting.
- **Co-Analyst** - domain-specific agentic AI built into the ExTrac platform that retrieves, analyses, and contextualises data on instruction, producing visualisations and reports.
- **Bespoke intelligence reporting** - customer-specific reporting products drawing on ExTrac's curated data combined with client data for tailored analytical output.
