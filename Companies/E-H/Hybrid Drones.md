# Hybrid Drones

> UK manufacturer of heavy-lift hybrid jet-electric uncrewed aerial systems, trading as HYDRA.

## What they do

Hybrid Drones Ltd is a UK heavy-lift hybrid UAS manufacturer, founded in 2019 to develop a hybrid jet-electric VTOL drone concept. Its platforms are designed to carry large payloads over tactical distances, with a modular design offering fully-electric and multi-jet variants. Stated use cases include logistical resupply, ship-to-ship transfer, casualty evacuation and weapons employment.

## Products and solutions

- **HYDRA-400** - heavy-lift VTOL UAV using hybrid propulsion combining electric rotors with micro jet turbines, with a maximum lift of up to 400 kg and rapid field assembly.
- **Platform variants** - four configurable variants of the platform (fully-electric, 2-jet, 4-jet and 6-jet), with maximum lift rising from 260 kg to 400 kg.
- **Stores carriage** - fitted with an L3Harris Gnat stores release system, able to carry casualty evacuation pods, cargo racks, under-slung loads and weapons.
