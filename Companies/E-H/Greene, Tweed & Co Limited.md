# Greene, Tweed & Co Limited

> US-headquartered manufacturer of high-performance sealing, composite and connector components for aerospace and defence platforms, with a UK facility in Nottinghamshire.

## What they do

Greene Tweed designs and manufactures custom sealing assemblies, thermoplastic composite structural components, and electrical and fibre optic connectors for commercial and military aerospace and defence applications. The company holds over 60 years of aerospace experience and states that 80 per cent of the world's aircraft rely on its custom-engineered components. Its UK entity is based in Ruddington, Nottinghamshire. Defence applications include flight control systems, hydraulic utility systems, fuel systems, brake systems, landing gear and engines. The company also serves the defence sector directly with products engineered for improved operational reliability, reduced system weight, and survivability in mission-critical environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Sealing solutions** - elastomer and thermoplastic O-rings, rotary seals, rod and piston seals, scrapers and wipers for high-pressure, high-temperature aerospace and defence environments.
- **Xycomp DLF thermoplastic composites** - discontinuous long-fibre thermoplastic composite components delivering 30 to 50 per cent weight savings against aluminium; used in structural airframe brackets, enclosures and airflow structures.
- **Seal-Connect electrical and fibre optic connectors** - rated to 260°C and 45,000 psi; designed for critical power and data transmission in aerospace and defence platforms.
- **Chemraz FFKM elastomers** - perfluoroelastomer sealing materials for chemically aggressive environments.
