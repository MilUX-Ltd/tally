# Fenrir Solutions

> Electronic warfare modelling, simulation and testing consultancy serving the defence sector.

## What they do

Fenrir Solutions is a specialist consultancy in electronic warfare modelling, simulation, and testing. The company draws on over 20 years of military experience and over a decade of electronic warfare expertise, delivering modelling and simulation services to defence customers. It is listed in the ADS Group member directory under simulation and modelling services, based in London.
