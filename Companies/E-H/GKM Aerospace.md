# GKM Aerospace

> Precision CNC machining and turnkey component manufacture for the aerospace and defence sectors.

## What they do

G.K.M. (Aerospace) Limited is a precision engineering manufacturer based in Basildon, Essex, supplying finished components and assemblies to leading OEMs and tiered suppliers in the aerospace, defence, and communications industries. The company operates a multi-shift CNC machining facility and works from engineering drawings and 3D models to deliver complex metallic and composite parts. Customers include prime contractors in the defence sector such as BAE Systems, Martin-Baker, Airbus, and Babcock.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC precision machined components** - High-complexity turned and milled parts in aluminium, steel, titanium, invar, and exotic alloys, supplied to defence and aerospace OEMs and their supply chains.
- **Turnkey assemblies** - Complete component solutions from CAD/CAM design through to quality-controlled finished assemblies, including R&D and proof-of-concept manufacturing.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100 (BSI Cert No. FM11462), Cyber Essentials Plus, Airbus UK Approval, BAE Systems Certificate, MOD Defence Contractor Registration (Reg 4FFG01)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Make UK Defence, JOSCAR Registered
