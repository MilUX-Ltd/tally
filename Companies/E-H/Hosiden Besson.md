# Hosiden Besson

> UK-based designer and manufacturer of acoustic, electronic and mechanical products, including robust telecoms equipment for extreme environments.

## What they do

Hosiden Besson (trading as HBL) designs and manufactures acoustic, electronic and mechanical ODM and OEM products for customers worldwide. Its acoustic electronic products and components serve the fire and safety, automotive, telecoms and hearing protection markets, and it has designed and built durable telecoms equipment for use in extreme environments. The company maintains onsite manufacturing, research and development, testing and quality facilities at its Hove site, including environmental, anechoic and EMC test capability. HBL is a wholly owned subsidiary of Hosiden Corporation of Japan.

## Products and solutions

- **Acoustic electronic products and components** - speakers, microphones, receivers and sounders for fire and safety, automotive and telecoms applications.
- **Robust telecoms equipment** - durable communications hardware designed for use in extreme environments.
