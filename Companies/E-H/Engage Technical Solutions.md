# Engage Technical Solutions

> Independent UK defence technical consultancy delivering logistics information systems support and programme management inside DE&S and the wider MoD supply chain.

## What they do

Engage Technical Solutions is a defence and security technical consultancy providing expert advice and support to the MoD and its supply chain. Its core specialisms are logistic information systems, engineering management across the CADMID cycle, and programme and portfolio management. The firm has been structured as an Employee Ownership Trust since March 2022. SIC code 84220 (Defence activities) on Companies House.

## Products and solutions

- **Information Systems and Services** - architecture, data analytics, service delivery management and requirements elicitation for UK Defence logistic IS, including MJDI, JAMES, LOGFAS, BIWMS+, DEEAMS and DFMS. The team holds ITIL qualifications and manages full service delivery lifecycles.
- **Engineering Management** - Integrated Logistic Support, safety and environmental engineering, and systems engineering across the CADMID cycle.
- **Programme, Project and Portfolio Management (P3M)** - lead Delivery Agent for DE&S Digital, including the Bridging the Gap (BtG) programme and the Defence Equipment Engineering Asset Management System (DEEAMS) project. Also supports the Business Modernisation for Support (BMfS) Programme.
