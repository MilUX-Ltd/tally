# Horstman Defence Systems Limited

> Land vehicle mobility specialist delivering suspension systems and drivetrain components for military platforms.

## What they do

Horstman Defence Systems designs and manufactures suspension, track and drivetrain solutions for armoured and tracked military vehicles, operating as part of the RENK Group. The company supplies battlefield mobility components from its Bath facility, with an emphasis on reducing maintenance burden and improving crew survivability through reliable suspension performance.
