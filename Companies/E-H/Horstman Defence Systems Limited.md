# Horstman Defence Systems Limited

> Land vehicle mobility specialist delivering suspension systems and drivetrain components for military platforms.

## What they do

Horstman Defence Systems designs and manufactures suspension, track and drivetrain solutions for armoured and tracked military vehicles, operating as part of the RENK Group since April 2019. The company describes itself as the pre-eminent mobility solution provider in the global heavy armoured and tracked vehicle suspension market, tracing its suspension design lineage through post-war British armour including Conqueror, Chieftain and Challenger 1. It employs over 250 people across the UK, US and Canada, and supplies battlefield mobility components from its Bath facility with an emphasis on reducing maintenance burden and improving crew survivability.

## Products and solutions

- **Hydrostrut** - hydro-pneumatic suspension strut for armoured and tracked vehicles.
- **HydroCore** - a cost-effective Hydrostrut variant designed for scalable mobility architecture across commercial and military use.
- **Programme support** - Horstman states it supports the British Army's Challenger 3, Boxer and Ajax programmes, and has a long production and support heritage on Warrior. For Challenger 3 it is refurbishing and upgrading the Hydrogas suspension to third-generation standard under the Heavy Armour Automotive Improvement Programme, covering the main battle tank plus engineering and recovery variants.
