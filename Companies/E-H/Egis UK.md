# Egis UK

> Engineering and consulting firm with active UK defence-nuclear work including the Sellafield decommissioning programme and Sizewell C civil design.

## What they do

Egis is a French engineering, project management, and mobility services group with approximately 23,500 employees across more than 100 countries. The UK entity, headquartered in London at Valentine Place in Southwark, employs approximately 1,000 people across offices in London, Bristol, Farnborough, Edinburgh, Glasgow, and Oakham.

UK work spans civil and defence nuclear engineering, aviation advisory and engineering, rail and urban transit, and roads. Defence-relevant work is concentrated in the nuclear domain: Egis holds positions on the £4.6bn Sellafield Decommissioning and Nuclear Waste Partnership (Lot 1, through the Amentum-led DNWP consortium), the Sizewell C civil design contract (extended September 2025), and through its subsidiary Galson Sciences Ltd, a 10-year appointment as Major Permissions Delivery Partner for Nuclear Waste Services' Geological Disposal Facility programme. The parent group holds explicit defence infrastructure capability including naval bases, air bases, and secure environments, primarily for the French Ministry of Armed Forces.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence and civil nuclear engineering** - safety case, civil design, and decommissioning engineering for nuclear sites including Sellafield, Hinkley Point C, and Sizewell C.
- **Nuclear waste and geological disposal** - technical and permitting support for the UK Geological Disposal Facility programme (Galson Sciences subsidiary).
- **Aviation engineering and advisory** - airport operations, airspace design, and engineering services; long-standing base at Farnborough.
- **Rail and transport engineering** - digital rail transformation and transit infrastructure; client includes Transport for London.
- **Infrastructure and buildings** - civil and structural engineering, including roads (M25, M40 operations).
