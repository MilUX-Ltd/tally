# Emergency Protection Ltd

> UK and Ireland distributor of the Bolawrap less-lethal restraint device, supplying police and military organisations.

## What they do

Emergency Protection Ltd is the exclusive UK and Ireland distributor of Bolawrap, a less-lethal restraint tool that allows officers to restrain a subject from a distance without pain compliance. The company supplies police and military customers, and its staff draw on backgrounds in mental health nursing, police service, military, and commercial sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Bolawrap** - a handheld, less-lethal restraint device that deploys a Kevlar cord to restrain a subject from a distance; supplied to law enforcement and military users across the UK and Ireland.
