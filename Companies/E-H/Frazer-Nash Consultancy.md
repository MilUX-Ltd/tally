# Frazer-Nash Consultancy

> Systems, engineering and technology consultancy serving UK defence; part of the KBR group.

## What they do

Frazer-Nash Consultancy is a UK systems, engineering and technology company that delivers engineering and technology solutions across defence, nuclear, transport, energy and other sectors. In defence it provides systems engineering, procurement support, design assurance and through-life support, with a long-established practice in submarine and surface-ship work. The company operates from a network of UK and Australian offices and is part of the US-headquartered KBR group, which acquired it in 2021.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems engineering and assurance** - design, integration and safety assurance for defence platforms including submarines and surface ships.
- **Procurement and acquisition support** - technical advisory and programme support to defence customers.
- **Technology and digital services** - modelling, simulation, data and digital engineering for complex systems.
