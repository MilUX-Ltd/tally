# HD Thinking
> Cheltenham-based advisory practice focused on cyber strategy, AI and national security, led by a former GCHQ Director Cyber who created the National Cyber Force.
## What they do
Human Digital Thinking (HD Thinking) is the consulting practice of Sally Walker, who spent 25 years at GCHQ. Her final senior role was Director Cyber, where she ran the National Offensive Cyber Programme from 2016 and led the cross-government creation of the National Cyber Force, the joint GCHQ-MOD partnership operating alongside US Cyber Command and NCSC. HD Thinking advises senior leaders in defence, intelligence and security on the impact of big data and AI on human decision-making, cyber strategy and organisational design.
