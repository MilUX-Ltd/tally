# Flowcopter

> Scottish developer of hydraulic-drive heavy-lift cargo drones for long-range missions.

## What they do

Flowcopter designs and manufactures heavy-lift uncrewed air systems built around digital hydraulic drive technology, a spin-out from Artemis Intelligent Power. Its platforms are aimed at markets traditionally served by helicopters, including offshore logistics, renewables, agriculture, emergency response and humanitarian aid. The company pairs combustion engines with digital displacement pumps feeding hydraulic motors that regulate propeller speed.

## Products and solutions

- **FC100** - heavy-lift cargo drone designed to carry payloads up to 100 kg over distances around 200 km with multi-hour endurance, positioned as a lower-fuel alternative to helicopters.
