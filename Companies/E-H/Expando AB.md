# Expando AB

> Swedish specialist in rugged COTS computing, embedded systems, and space and avionics components for defence and aerospace.

## What they do

Expando AB designs, integrates, and supplies rugged computing hardware and specialist electronic components to defence, aerospace, and space customers. Founded in 2000 and owned by the Tisenhult Group, the company operates from Sollentuna, north of Stockholm, and serves Nordic and European prime contractors. Its work spans naval platforms, missile systems, surveillance systems, and satellite applications. Named customers include Kongsberg, SAAB, BAE Systems, and Patria.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged servers and board computers** - mission computers and small-form-factor tactical systems for naval, missile, and surveillance platforms.
- **High-performance embedded computing (HPEC)** - SIGINT, EW, mission computing, and C5ISR applications, including collaboration with EIZO Rugged Solutions and NVIDIA Jetson.
- **Space and avionics components** - radiation-hardened power supply, memory, computer cores, and space cameras; ARINC components (429, 571, 575).
- **Custom ASIC and bespoke semiconductor solutions** - flight-critical software and power supply subsystems.
