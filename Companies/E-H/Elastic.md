# Elastic
> US-listed search and analytics platform with a defined UK Defence and Public Sector practice, deployed across MOD, intelligence and central government workloads.

## What they do
Elastic produces the Elasticsearch engine and the wider Elastic Stack (Elasticsearch, Kibana, Beats, Logstash), used for search, observability, security and generative AI workloads at scale. In the UK, Elastic operates a dedicated Defence and Public Sector practice and supports government-aligned deployments including air-gapped and on-premises options. The platform underpins log search, threat detection (Elastic Security / SIEM) and observability of mission systems across MOD and intelligence customers.

Elastic is listed on the NYSE (ESTC) and has significant UK presence alongside its Mountain View headquarters.

## Products and solutions
- **Elastic Stack** - search, observability and security analytics platform (Elasticsearch, Kibana, Beats, Logstash).
- **Elastic Security** - SIEM and threat detection, deployed across government and defence environments.
- **Air-gapped and on-premises deployment** - government-aligned options for classified and sensitive environments.
