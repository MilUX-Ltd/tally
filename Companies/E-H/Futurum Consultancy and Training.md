# Futurum Consultancy and Training Ltd

> Specialist intelligence, surveillance, and covert tradecraft training and consultancy for defence, law enforcement, and government clients.

## What they do

Futurum provides discreet, practitioner-led training and consultancy to defence, law enforcement, and government clients internationally. The company has served clients in over 50 countries, with expertise spanning intelligence analysis, covert tradecraft, countering corporate espionage, counter-terrorism, and crisis management. All trainers are drawn from the UK police, NCA, military, special forces, and government.

## Products and solutions

- **Intelligence Analysis Training** - instruction in i2 Analyst Notebook, Palantir, open-source intelligence, and social media exploitation.
- **Covert Tradecraft Consultancy** - training across conventional and technical surveillance, specialist interviewing, and elicitation techniques.
- **Counter-Terrorism and Crisis Management** - advisory and training services for government and law enforcement clients.
- **Leadership and Management Development** - programmes for military and public sector clients.
