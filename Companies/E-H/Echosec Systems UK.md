# Echosec Systems UK

> OSINT and geospatial intelligence platform for national security, defence, and law enforcement, operating as part of Flashpoint.

## What they do

Echosec Systems UK Ltd is the UK subsidiary of Echosec Systems, a Canadian-originated open-source intelligence and geospatial intelligence platform company acquired by Flashpoint in August 2022. The platform aggregates real-time data from social media, fringe networks, messaging applications, news sources, and dark web forums, then maps it geospatially to support physical threat intelligence, situational awareness, counterterrorism, and geopolitical risk assessment. The product is now delivered as "Echosec by Flashpoint" within the Flashpoint Ignite unified intelligence platform. The UK operational office is in Cardiff, Wales.

## Products and solutions

- **Echosec by Flashpoint** - geospatial OSINT platform providing real-time social and dark web data mapped to an interactive interface, with geofencing and proximity alerting.
- **Flashpoint Ignite** - unified intelligence platform integrating Echosec capabilities with cyber threat intelligence, vulnerability data, and OSINT.
- **Echosec Assist** - AI summarisation assistant within the Echosec platform, launched May 2024.
- **Echosec Optimize** - AI-powered location-based keyword generation for geospatial OSINT searches, launched December 2025.
- **APIs** - data delivery for integration into customer SIEM, SOAR, and GIS systems.
