# Elite Electronic Systems Ltd

> Northern Ireland-based contract electronics manufacturer specialising in high-reliability PCB assembly, cable and harness assembly, and box-build for the aerospace and defence sector.

## What they do

Elite Electronic Systems is a contract electronics manufacturing services (EMS) provider focused on high-reliability and regulated markets, with aerospace and defence as a primary sector. The company offers PCB assembly, cable and harness assembly, high-level and box-build assembly, and control panels, serving OEMs from prototype and NPI through to production scale. It operates three UK manufacturing sites: Enniskillen (Northern Ireland), Livingston (Scotland), and Chesterfield (England), totalling approximately 150,000 sq ft of manufacturing space. The company is backed by private equity investor Lonsdale Capital Partners.

## Products and solutions

- **PCB assembly (PCBA)** - high-reliability surface-mount and through-hole assembly for aerospace, defence, medical, and industrial OEMs.
- **Cable and harness assembly** - from prototype to volume production for complex defence and aerospace wiring systems.
- **Box-build and systems integration** - high-level assembly including panel wiring, mechanical assembly, and functional test.
- **NPI and prototype services** - new product introduction support from design for manufacture through to first articles.
