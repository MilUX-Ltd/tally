# Global Satellite Vu Ltd

> UK space company delivering high-resolution thermal infrared imagery from a satellite constellation for national security, economic monitoring and climate resilience applications.

## What they do

SatVu designs, owns and operates a constellation of thermal infrared Earth observation satellites, capturing imagery at up to 3.5 metres ground sample distance. The data supports activity-based intelligence, critical infrastructure monitoring and situational awareness in low-light and night-time conditions. The company sells thermal imagery, tasking services and a catalogue product direct to government and commercial customers.

## Products and solutions

- **HotSat Satellite Constellation** - thermal infrared imaging satellites; HotSat-1 launched June 2023, HotSat-2 launched March 2026 and declared commercially operational June 2026; HotSat-3 under contract.
- **Thermal Imagery** - calibrated high-resolution thermal infrared data products delivered via the SatVu platform.
- **Tasking** - on-demand satellite collection scheduling for specific targets or areas of interest.
- **Catalog** - archive thermal imagery accessible via API.
