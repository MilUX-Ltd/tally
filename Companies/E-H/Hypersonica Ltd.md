# Hypersonica Ltd

> Anglo-German defence and aerospace company developing a sovereign European hypersonic precision strike capability, with a UK subsidiary registered in Ashford, Kent.

## What they do

Hypersonica is a hypersonic missile company focused on precision strike, developing what it describes as Europe's first privately funded sovereign hypersonic strike capability. Founded in December 2023, the group is headquartered in the Munich area with a wholly owned UK subsidiary, Hypersonica Limited, registered in Ashford, Kent since February 2025. The company states its approach uses rapid iteration from design to flight-ready hardware, combining simulation, prototyping and flight testing, with a modular architecture intended to shorten development timelines and reduce costs against conventional defence development programmes. Hypersonica states its goal is to deliver hypersonic strike capability by 2029.

## Products and solutions

- **Hypersonic strike missile** - a manoeuvrable hypersonic precision strike system under development, with a phased flight test programme working towards full mission requirements.
