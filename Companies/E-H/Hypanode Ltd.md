# Hypanode Ltd

> Scottish cleantech company developing a high-performance electric power system for air, sea and space platforms.

## What they do

Hypanode is developing a clean power system it describes as breaking the link between high performance, material use and global warming emissions. It positions the technology as foundational, transforming capabilities across air, sea and land applications, and states its mission is to break the efficiency barrier and power a cleaner future. The company registers itself under the tagline "Motion unbound" and describes its work as being carried out by a team of scientists and engineers. Hypanode is a micro-enterprise incorporated in Scotland in November 2022, registered at Westhill, Aberdeenshire, with a stated delivery address at the Dundee University Incubator, Dundee Technopole.

## Products and solutions

Hypanode's public materials describe the technology at a conceptual level only (a green, high-performance electric power system for electric and hybrid propulsion across air, maritime and space platforms). The company has not published named products, specifications, or a dedicated products page as of this verification date.
