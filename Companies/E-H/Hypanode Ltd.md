# Hypanode Ltd

> Scottish cleantech company developing a high-performance electric power system for air, sea and space platforms.

## What they do

Hypanode is developing a clean power system it describes as breaking the energy barrier for electric and hybrid propulsion, targeting applications across air, maritime and space. The technology aims to deliver significantly higher performance than existing electric systems with substantially lower emissions than conventional engines, and is designed for new mission profiles where existing power solutions are inadequate.
