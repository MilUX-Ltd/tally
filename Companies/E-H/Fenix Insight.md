# Fenix Insight

> Open-source intelligence company specialising in munitions, weapons systems, and global conflict event data, serving defence, government, and humanitarian customers.

## What they do

Fenix Insight combines explosive ordnance expertise with AI and data analytics to produce structured intelligence on munitions, weapons platforms, and conflict events. Founded in 2011, the company serves UK and international civil and military government departments, humanitarian organisations, and commercial risk management customers. In addition to its data products, the company delivers specialist consultancy, capability development, incident investigation, and evidence-based policy design.

## Products and solutions

- **METIS** (Munitions, Events and Technical Information System) - the company's primary OSINT platform, launched in 2015 and rebranded to METIS in 2025. Covers over 470,000 geospatial conflict events, 6,700 technical munition, platform, and CBRN records, and 45,000 images. Delivered via secure web interface or API.
- **IRIS** (Image Recognition Identification System) - machine vision software that detects landmines and explosive ordnance from UAV video feeds in real time. Field-trialled with Ukrainian Army Engineers and the Ukrainian Ministry of Emergencies in January 2024.
- **Intelligence briefings** - monthly SITREPs, munitions newsletters, and CBRN bulletins.
