# Hydro Aluminium UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of Hydro ASA, supplying aluminium extrusions, profiles and fabricated components to aerospace and defence customers.

## What they do

Hydro Aluminium UK is part of Norsk Hydro ASA, a global aluminium and renewable energy company. UK operations encompass manufacturing plants, fabrication and components, extrusion dies, casthouses, surface treatment, and thermal break facilities, providing bespoke aluminium profiles and additional value-added services to aerospace and industrial customers.
