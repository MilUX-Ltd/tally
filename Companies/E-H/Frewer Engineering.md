# Frewer Engineering
> UK specialist engineering design and analysis consultancy supporting complex, safety-critical programmes across defence and other regulated sectors.
## What they do
Frewer Engineering provides engineering design and analysis services for complex, safety-critical programmes. The firm is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park, and participates in the Aurora Provider Network, indicating an active position in the UK defence supply chain. Specific programme experience has not been publicly disclosed; the positioning is across multiple sectors with defence as a confirmed element.
