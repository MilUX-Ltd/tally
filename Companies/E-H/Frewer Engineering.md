# Frewer Engineering

> Engineering design, analysis and consultancy firm serving aerospace, defence, marine, nuclear and space sectors, with accredited UK security clearance and Cyber Essentials Plus certification.

## What they do

Frewer Engineering, trading name of Frewer & Co. Engineers Limited, was founded in 2003 and is based in Ockley, Surrey. The company provides engineering design and analysis consultancy services across demanding sectors including aerospace, defence, gas turbines, marine, nuclear and space. Its defence work spans land-based military equipment, airborne propulsion systems, ballistic protection and fluid dynamics for military platforms. The team holds full UK security clearance and the company holds Cyber Essentials Plus accreditation. It is registered on JOSCAR and is a member of Make UK Defence.

## Products and solutions

- **Structural analysis for military platforms** - design and structural assurance of land vehicles, weapons components and airborne structures, including lightweight metallic and composite structures.
- **Ballistic protection analysis** - ballistic modelling and light-weighting for composite body armour and helmet systems.
- **Fluid flow analysis** - aerodynamic and hydrodynamic performance optimisation and flow-induced loading for military equipment.
- **Gas turbine and propulsion engineering** - design and analysis of military aircraft propulsion systems.
- **Composites design** - carbon composite structures for aerospace and defence applications.
- **Rotor dynamics** - engineering analysis for rotating systems across defence and aerospace programmes.
- **UAS programme support** - engineering support to unmanned aerial systems development (referenced in January 2026 news).
