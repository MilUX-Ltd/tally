# GE Aviation UK

> UK operations of GE Aerospace, supplying propulsion, avionics, electrical power and propeller systems for military and commercial aircraft.

## What they do

GE Aviation UK is the UK-registered entity and operating presence of GE Aerospace (NYSE: GE), a global aerospace propulsion, services and systems company. The UK footprint spans Gloucestershire and Cheltenham (propeller, electrical power and avionics systems), plus maintenance, repair and overhaul (MRO) sites in Scotland and South Wales. More than 60% of UK business serves defence and military customers. The UK employs over 2,000 people, making it one of the largest concentrations of aviation systems engineers in the country.

## Products and solutions

- **Propulsion systems** - military jet engines including the F110, F404 and F414 for combat aircraft, and the T700 turboshaft for rotorcraft platforms including the AH-64E Apache Mk2.
- **Avionics and electrical power systems** - integrated avionics and power systems for platforms including the C-130J, Eurofighter Typhoon, F-35, Boeing P-8 Poseidon and E-7 Wedgetail.
- **Dowty Propellers** - electronically controlled, all-composite propeller systems for military airlifters, commercial aircraft and specialist platforms; manufactured in Gloucestershire.
- **MRO services** - engine maintenance, repair and overhaul for military and commercial operators at UK sites.
