# Heligan Group

> A defence and security-sector financial services group, combining private equity investment, M&A advisory, wealth management, and intelligence-led insight services for the security technologies and defence market.

## What they do

Heligan Group operates as an intelligence-driven financial services business focused exclusively on the security technologies and defence sector. The Group is structured around four integrated divisions: private equity investment in defence and security technology firms, mid-market M&A advisory, private banking and wealth management, and specialist intelligence and investigations advisory. It draws on deep sector experience from backgrounds in defence, national security, and law enforcement.

## Products and solutions

- **Heligan Investments** - Private equity fund (Heligan Private Equity I) targeting buyout and minority investments in defence and security technology companies with enterprise values of £5M to £50M.
- **Heligan Corporate Finance** - Mid-market M&A advisory covering business sales, acquisitions, capital raising, and complex transactions in the defence and security sector.
- **Heligan Wealth Management** - Private banking and wealth management services for clients in the defence and security sector.
- **Heligan Strategic Insights** - Intelligence-led advisory drawing on senior sector networks to support decision-making in complex and high-risk environments.
