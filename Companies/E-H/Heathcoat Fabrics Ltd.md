# Heathcoat Fabrics Ltd

> UK technical textile manufacturer supplying parachute fabric, CBRN suit materials, and specialist defence textiles from its site in Tiverton, Devon.

## What they do

Heathcoat Fabrics has manufactured textiles on its Tiverton site for over 200 years and employs more than 430 people across knitting, weaving, dyeing and finishing. The company is the UK's only weaver of parachute fabric, covering paratrooper chutes, aircraft brake chutes, ejector seat chutes and naval decoys; it also supplies NASA. Heathcoat supplies the active element of NATO CBRN suits and holds international research funding in related areas.

## Products and solutions

- **Parachute fabric** - sole UK weaver of parachute canopy fabric for military and aerospace applications including paratrooper, brake, ejector seat and naval decoy chutes.
- **CBRN suit materials** - active-element textiles for NATO chemical, biological, radiological and nuclear protective suits.
- **Technical textiles** - specialist woven and knitted fabrics for platform interiors, PPE, field accommodation, sleeping equipment and equipment bags.
