# Exotopic Ltd

> London-based space and defence accelerator and mission-delivery company, running the UK Space Agency Accelerator and delivering in-orbit demonstration missions for defence-relevant SMEs.

## What they do

Exotopic Ltd is a London SME operating at the intersection of space mission delivery, investment and defence-sector accelerator management. The company is the lead partner delivering the UK Space Agency Accelerator programme and has won contracts with both the European Space Agency and the Defence and Security Accelerator (DASA). Its primary commercial offering is an in-orbit demonstration (IOD) service, "Give Me Some Space!", which provides defence and aerospace SMEs with affordable access to space to test and validate spacecraft technologies. Exotopic is also a technical advisory business, supporting companies with investment readiness and business development in the defence and space sectors. The company is registered at the British Interplanetary Society building in Vauxhall, London.

## Products and solutions

- **Give Me Some Space! IOD missions** - rideshare in-orbit demonstration service giving SMEs access to low-Earth orbit to test propulsion, sensors, computing and other spacecraft payloads; first mission targeted Q1 2026, second (targeting Q1 2027) open to European and international entrants.
- **UK Space Agency Accelerator** - Exotopic delivers this programme as lead partner, supporting early-stage space and defence-adjacent companies.
- **SAC Technical Expert Support (TES)** - free access to technical expert advice for SMEs, delivered as part of Exotopic's accelerator remit.
- **MachLab** - a propulsion test and research facility supporting rocket engine testing up to 10 kN across a range of technology readiness levels.
