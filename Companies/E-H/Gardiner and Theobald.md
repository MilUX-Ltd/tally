# Gardiner & Theobald LLP

> Independent construction and property consultancy delivering cost, project, and programme management services to defence, national security, and space clients.

## What they do

Gardiner and Theobald is an independent global consultancy providing cost management, project management, and construction management services across the built environment. Its Defence and National Security practice supports MOD, the Defence Infrastructure Organisation (DIO), prime contractors, and private operators on programmes spanning land, sea, air, cyber, and space domains. Security-cleared teams manage programmes from £100,000 to £100 billion in value. The firm is regulated by RICS and has signed the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence infrastructure programme management** - Cost, commercial, and project management for MOD and DIO-led construction and estate programmes, including appointments as commercial delivery partner on the Defence Equipment and Support (DE&S) Paragon joint venture.
- **Satellite and space launch programme support** - Cost control, risk management, and regulatory readiness for LEO, MEO, and GEO satellite launch programmes and UK spaceport development.
- **Construction cost management** - Quantity surveying and cost advisory for defence facilities including Royal Navy, British Army, and Royal Air Force estate.
