# Howco Group Plc

> Global distributor and processor of high-performance alloys supplying raw materials and precision components to the aerospace and defence sectors.

## What they do

Howco Group distributes and processes nickel alloys, titanium alloys, chromium alloys, and other superalloys for use in the world's most demanding industrial environments, including aerospace and defence. Operating from ten sites across six countries, the company provides the full supply chain from raw material to finished precision part, with additive manufacturing capability alongside conventional processing. Howco Group Plc is registered in Scotland; company records show current control held by Eryngium Limited, a private UK-registered company, since August 2023, having previously been controlled by Sumitomo Corporation of Japan. The "Plc" in the company name denotes its legal form; the company is not listed on a public stock exchange.

## Products and solutions

- **Alloy distribution and processing** - nickel, titanium, chromium and other superalloys supplied from raw material through to finished precision part, including sawing, mechanical testing and non-destructive testing.
- **Aerospace-accredited processing** - its Sheffield facility holds AS9100D accreditation covering material management, procurement, stocking, sawing of non-ferrous metal, and mechanical and non-destructive testing.
