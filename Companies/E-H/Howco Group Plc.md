# Howco Group Plc

> Global distributor and processor of high-performance alloys supplying raw materials and precision components to the aerospace and defence sectors.

## What they do

Howco Group distributes and processes nickel alloys, titanium alloys, chromium alloys, and other superalloys for use in the world's most demanding industrial environments, including aerospace and defence. Operating from ten sites across six countries, the company provides the full supply chain from raw material to finished precision part, with additive manufacturing capability alongside conventional processing.
