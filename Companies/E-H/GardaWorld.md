# GardaWorld

> Private security and risk management company providing government, diplomatic, and defence security services in complex and high-threat environments.

## What they do

GardaWorld is the largest privately-owned security company in the world, headquartered in Montreal, Canada. Its International Protective Services division operates in complex, high-threat, and emerging markets, delivering physical security, close protection, risk analysis, crisis response, and training services to diplomatic, defence, development, and infrastructure clients. In the UK, GardaWorld operates under the entity GW Consulting UK Limited and is a founding signatory of the International Code of Conduct for Private Security Providers.

## Products and solutions

- **Government and defence security services** - Physical security for embassies and consulates, mobile security teams, risk analysis reporting, and emergency planning for MOD and allied-nation government clients.
- **International protective services** - Close protection teams drawing on former US and UK Special Forces personnel, operating in high-threat environments.
- **Counter-terrorism and intelligence services** - Threat assessment, intelligence reporting, and advisory services.
- **Crisis response** - Emergency planning, incident management, and evacuation support.
