# Flann Microwave

> Designer and manufacturer of RF, microwave and millimetre-wave components and precision test instrumentation.

## What they do

Flann Microwave is a UK manufacturer of precision passive RF, microwave and millimetre-wave components, sub-systems and calibration instrumentation, operating from around 300 MHz to 1.1 THz. Trading since 1956 and based in Bodmin, Cornwall, it serves aerospace and defence, space and satellite communications, metrology and test, telecommunications, and research markets, with the majority of production exported. It employs around 65 staff.

## Products and solutions

- **Waveguide components** - flexible and sub-millimetre waveguide, assemblies, bends, transitions and waveguide-to-coaxial transitions.
- **Antennas and horns** - standard gain horns, corrugated and dual-polarised horns, feed systems and orthomode transducers.
- **RF components** - attenuators, couplers, filters and diplexers, polarisers, switches, phase changers, loads and terminations.
- **Calibration and metrology** - calibration and verification kits, reflectometers and multi-port standards.
