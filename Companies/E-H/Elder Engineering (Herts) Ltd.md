# Elder Engineering (Herts) Ltd

> CNC machining and engineering materials supplier serving aerospace and security sectors.

## What they do

Elder Engineering supplies engineering plastics and materials and provides CNC machining and fabrication of components. It holds AS9100 certification to serve the aerospace sector and has worked on airport security products, including X-ray security test pieces produced in connection with the Department for Transport.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC machined components** - precision machined parts in plastics and other materials.
- **Engineering plastics and materials** - rod, sheet, plate, tube and strip stock, tapes and silicone products.
- **X-ray security test pieces** - test artefacts for airport and security screening equipment.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100; ISO 9001.
