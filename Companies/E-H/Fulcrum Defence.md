# Fulcrum Defence

> Veteran-owned UK supplier of bespoke equipment carriage, packaging and protection solutions for defence and emergency services.

## What they do

Fulcrum Defence is a veteran-owned UK firm that project-manages the design and delivery of bespoke equipment carriage, packaging and protection solutions for the military and emergency services. Its focus is protecting and transporting sensitive or mission-critical equipment in demanding operational environments, with attention to ergonomics and rapid access. It works through a network of partners and suppliers to deliver custom solutions to defence standards, including codification and NSN compliance.

## Products and solutions

- **Communications and walk-on fit cases** - carriage systems for communications equipment.
- **Unmanned systems cases** - protective carriage for unmanned platforms.
- **Weapons and armoury cases** - cases for weapons, ammunition, ancillaries and mobile armouries.
- **Aircrew carriage** - flight helmet and flight bag carriage solutions.
- **Lima Pack** - dual carriage system, alongside bespoke pouches and mission-specific systems.
