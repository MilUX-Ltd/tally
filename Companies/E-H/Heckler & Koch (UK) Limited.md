# Heckler & Koch (UK) Limited

> UK subsidiary of Heckler & Koch GmbH, supplying the company's small arms portfolio to the MoD and UK Police Forces from its base in Nottingham.

## What they do

Heckler & Koch GmbH is a German manufacturer of small arms including handguns, rifles, submachine guns and grenade launchers, headquartered in Oberndorf am Neckar. The UK subsidiary, based in Nottingham, provides the full HK product portfolio to the Ministry of Defence and UK Police Forces. HK is a leading small arms supplier to NATO and EU member states.

## Products and solutions

- **Handguns** - service pistols for military and law enforcement.
- **Rifles** - assault rifles and designated marksman rifles for military use.
- **Submachine guns** - compact submachine guns for specialist units and law enforcement.
- **Grenade launchers** - underbarrel and standalone grenade launchers for infantry use.
