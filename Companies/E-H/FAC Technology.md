# FAC Technology

> London deep-tech R&D company developing advanced composite materials and adhesive systems for military aviation, aerospace and space applications.

## What they do

FAC Technology (trading name of Fergusson's Advanced Composite Technology Limited) is a specialist research and development company working at the molecular level of advanced composite materials. Founded by Dr Alexander Fergusson, an aerospace composites researcher from Imperial College London, where he holds Honorary Lecturer and Academic Visitor status, the company develops high-performance composite materials, structural adhesives and potting compounds with documented application in civil and military aviation, satellite manufacturing and space systems. FAC Technology holds Civil Aviation Authority Alternative Design Organisation approval (UK.AP1002) and has achieved ETSO C90d certification for its composite air-cargo pallet. The company participated in the Advanced Propulsion Centre Technology Developer Accelerator Programme (TDAP), raising technology readiness from TRL 3 to TRL 8.

## Products and solutions

- **Deep Air Carbon (DAC) composite materials** - structural composite material system in use in civil and military aviation applications; forms the basis of the ETSO C90d-approved composite air-cargo pallet (PMC type).
- **Fire-resistant composites** - flame penetration resistant materials with a burn rate of 0 mm/min, relevant to aircraft and military vehicle applications.
- **Advanced structural adhesives** - developed for satellite manufacturing, confirmed for use in critical space applications with a leading satellite manufacturer.
- **Motor resins** - high-thermal-performance materials for electric motor applications including UAV and propulsion systems.
- **Potting compounds** - specialist encapsulation compounds for aerospace and space applications.
