# Forcys

> Maritime defence company delivering integrated underwater sensing, communications and payload solutions for navies and defence organisations worldwide.

## What they do

Forcys integrates, delivers and supports customer-shaped solutions for underwater and maritime defence operations, drawing on 50 years of combined domain expertise across its constituent businesses. The company brings together technologies covering sonar, optical systems, navigation, communications and command and control into payloads for unmanned underwater vehicles (UUVs), remotely operated vehicles (ROVs) and surface platforms. It serves navies, government defence agencies, and critical national infrastructure protection programmes across the UK, US, Australia and allied nations.

Forcys is part of the Covelya Group. In February 2026, Wavefront Systems formally merged with Chelsea Technologies and Forcys, with all three operating under the Forcys brand.

## Products and solutions

- **Sentinel IDS** - intruder detection sonar capable of detecting, tracking and classifying divers and UUVs at ranges up to 1,000m and 1,500m respectively; described as the world's most deployed underwater intruder detection sonar.
- **Solstice MAS** - multi-aperture sonar for high-resolution seabed mapping on AUV platforms.
- **Vigilant FLS** - forward-looking sonar for obstacle avoidance and navigation.
- **Sentry IMS** - intruder monitoring system for protected area surveillance.
- **Equinox** - remotely operated towed vehicle for mine countermeasures operations.
- **ROV and UUV payloads** - integrated sensor suites for underwater identification, ISR and environmental assessment missions.
- **Secure diver communications** - encrypted acoustic communications for diver and subsurface operations.
- **Tactical modules** - containerised expeditionary solutions for rapid deployment.
