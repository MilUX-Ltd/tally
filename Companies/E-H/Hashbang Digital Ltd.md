# Hashbang Digital Ltd

> UK software engineering company delivering operationally focused digital systems for defence, national security, and government.

## What they do

Hashbang Digital builds secure, mission-critical software for defence, national security, and government customers. The company specialises in AI and advanced analytics, cloud transformation, and data solutions, with a focus on edge-deployable and sovereign platforms. Registered in England and Wales (No. 12504402), based in London.

## Products and solutions

- **AI and Advanced Analytics** - LLM-based and agentic AI applications, retrieval augmented generation (RAG), and local AI deployments that keep sensitive data within customer networks.
- **Cloud Transformation** - public, private, and hybrid cloud migration, security, compliance monitoring, and FinOps.
- **Data Solutions** - end-to-end data management including ETL pipelines, data lakes, and Zero Trust data security; big data and Spark workloads.
