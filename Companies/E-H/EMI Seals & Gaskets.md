# EMI Seals & Gaskets Ltd

> UK manufacturer of custom sealing, shielding and thermal components for defence, aerospace, nuclear and space applications.

## What they do

EMI Seals & Gaskets Ltd designs and manufactures custom sealing and EMI/RF shielding components, including seals, gaskets, O-rings, bonded washers, thermal management products, foams, extrusions and mouldings. The company supplies the defence, aerospace, nuclear, space and industrial sectors, and holds a long-standing position in the UK defence supply chain with products embedded in named platforms including the BOXER Armoured Fighting Vehicle, Type 26 Frigate and nuclear submarine programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **EMI/RF shielding components** - custom shielded gaskets and seals for electromagnetic compatibility in defence and aerospace platforms.
- **Sealing solutions** - O-rings, bonded washers, moulded seals and extrusions for aerospace, nuclear and defence applications.
- **Thermal management products** - thermal interface materials and associated components for use in demanding environments.
