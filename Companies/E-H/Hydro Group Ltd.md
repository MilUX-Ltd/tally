# Hydro Group Ltd

> Global subsea connectivity and engineering specialist with nearly four decades of experience in cable and connector systems, now part of the US Trexon/Amphenol group.

## What they do

Hydro Group designs, manufactures and tests complete subsea electrical and optical interconnect packages for the oil and gas, marine renewable energy and defence industries, from its Aberdeen headquarters and facilities in the USA and Singapore. The company describes itself as a vertically integrated design house, controlling design, manufacture and testing in-house for cables, connectors, penetrators and glands used in the harshest underwater environments. It states a defence and nuclear heritage dating to the early 1990s, supplying prime defence and nuclear-related contractors including the Ministry of Defence, and describes itself as part of the supply chain supporting the Royal Navy's Astute-class submarine programme.

## Products and solutions

- **Subsea cables** - purpose-built electrical, fibre optic and hybrid cables for power distribution, data transmission and control systems.
- **Connectors** - electrical and optical connection systems for data, power and signal transmission in underwater and high-pressure environments.
- **Penetrators** - submarine hull and bulkhead penetrators, including a fibre optic manned penetrator for submersibles.
- **Glands** - subsea glands, splice blocks and terminations providing sealing and electrical connections.
- **ROV umbilicals and illuminated LED tethers** for remotely operated vehicles.
- **Cable assemblies and terminations**, designed, manufactured and pressure-tested in-house.

The company states its defence-related project experience includes active towed sonar, bow and dipping sonar, ROV minehunter systems, subsea acoustic signature and acoustic buoy systems, tethered communications and mast systems, non-penetrative mast optical communications, flank array sonar, mine countermeasure systems, and acoustic hull recognition. Hydro Group supplies the connectivity and interconnect hardware for these systems; it does not itself state that it manufactures the sonar or sensor payloads.

## Recent news

- **2025-11-06** - Amphenol Corporation completed its acquisition of Trexon (Hydro Group's parent) for approximately $1 billion, bringing Hydro Group under Amphenol's Harsh Environment Solutions segment. [source](https://investors.amphenol.com/news-and-events/news-details/2025/Amphenol-Corporation-Closes-Trexon-Acquisition/default.aspx)
- **2025-05-21** - Trexon acquired Aberdeen-based Greco Developments Ltd, expanding Trexon's subsea portfolio alongside its existing Hydro Group and CRE Marine businesses. [source](https://www.hydrogroup-uk.com/pdf/Greco-Press-Release-05.21.25-Final.pdf)
- **2025-02-06** - Hydro Group published a feature on its vertically integrated design, manufacturing and testing model for subsea cables, connectors, penetrators and glands, citing both defence and renewable energy applications. [source](https://www.trexon.com/news-and-events/hydro-group-leading-the-way-in-vertically-integrated-design-solutions-for-subsea-cables-connectors-penetrators-and-glands)

_Hydro Group was acquired by Ohio-based TPC Wire & Cable (an Audax Private Equity portfolio company) in 2021, later brought together with other specialty interconnect businesses under the Trexon brand; Trexon itself was acquired by Amphenol Corporation (US) in November 2025. Hydro Group Ltd remains a UK-registered company (Companies House SC132331) but is foreign (US) owned via this chain._
