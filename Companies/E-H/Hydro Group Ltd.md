# Hydro Group Ltd

> Global subsea connectivity and engineering specialist with nearly four decades of experience in cable and connector systems.

## What they do

Hydro Group designs, manufactures and supplies subsea cables, connectors, and umbilicals for defence, oil and gas, and oceanographic markets. The company is headquartered in Aberdeen and is recognised as a leader in subsea connectivity and engineering innovation.
