# Hawkeye
> US commercial GEOINT company operating a constellation of RF-sensing microsatellites to detect, characterise and geolocate radio frequency signals for defence, intelligence and maritime domain awareness.
## What they do
HawkEye 360 operates a commercial satellite constellation of around 30 microsatellites flying in formation clusters at 400 to 600 km altitude, using trilateration to precisely geolocate RF signal sources globally. Primary use cases are maritime domain awareness, dark vessel tracking, illicit activity detection and SIGINT-led tipping and cueing of electro-optical and SAR collection. In 2019 the US National Reconnaissance Office awarded HawkEye 360 a contract to integrate its commercial RF data into geospatial intelligence architecture. The 2024 acquisition of RF Solutions from Maxar Intelligence expanded spectrum scanning capability.
## Products and solutions
- **RF geolocation** - Satellite-derived detection and geolocation of radio frequency emitters globally.
- **Vessel Custody ID** - Maritime tracking feature designed for national security applications.
