# Hawkeye

> US commercial GEOINT company operating a constellation of RF-sensing microsatellites to detect, characterise and geolocate radio frequency signals for defence, intelligence and maritime domain awareness.

## What they do

HawkEye 360 operates a commercial satellite constellation of around 30 microsatellites flying in formation clusters at 400 to 600 km altitude, using trilateration to precisely geolocate RF signal sources globally. Primary use cases are maritime domain awareness, dark vessel tracking, illicit activity detection and SIGINT-led tipping and cueing of electro-optical and SAR collection. In 2019 the US National Reconnaissance Office awarded HawkEye 360 a contract to integrate its commercial RF data into geospatial intelligence architecture. The 2024 acquisition of RF Solutions from Maxar Intelligence expanded spectrum scanning capability. The company completed its initial public offering in May 2026.

## Products and solutions

- **RF geolocation** - satellite-derived detection and geolocation of radio frequency emitters globally, using clustered formation-flying satellites for triangulation accuracy.
- **Vessel Custody ID** - a maritime tracking feature that uses artificial intelligence to assign unique tracking identifiers to high-interest vessels, maintaining custody across multiple satellite collections and scoring the confidence of each identification without dependence on self-reported vessel data.
- **Spectrum mapping and monitoring** - RF spectrum scanning and monitoring capability, expanded through the 2024 acquisition of RF Solutions from Maxar Intelligence.
