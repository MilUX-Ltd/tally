# Harland & Wolff

> Shipbuilding, ship repair and heavy fabrication group operating four UK yards, now owned by Spain's Navantia.

## What they do

Harland & Wolff is a shipbuilding and fabrication business operating yards at Belfast (Northern Ireland), Appledore (Devon), and Methil and Arnish (Scotland). The Belfast and Appledore yards focus on naval and commercial shipbuilding and ship repair; the Scottish yards specialise in energy-sector fabrication. The business was acquired by Navantia UK, the British arm of the Spanish state-owned shipbuilder Navantia, in January 2025, which retained more than 1,000 jobs across the four sites.

## Products and solutions

- **Naval shipbuilding** - construction of vessels for UK defence programmes, including work on the Fleet Solid Support (FSS) ships for the Royal Fleet Auxiliary at Belfast and Appledore.
- **Ship repair and conversion** - dry-dock and repair services across the yards.
- **Heavy fabrication** - structures for the energy and offshore sectors, primarily at the Methil and Arnish yards.
