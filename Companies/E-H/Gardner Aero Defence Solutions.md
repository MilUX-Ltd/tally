# Gardner Aero Defence Solutions Ltd

> Aerospace and defence consultancy specialising in disruptive air vehicle concepts, strategic development, and business improvement for the military aviation sector.

## What they do

Gardner Aero Defence Solutions (GADS) provides consultancy services to the military aerospace and defence sectors, drawing on 30 years of practitioner experience. Services cover concept innovation for new air vehicle product concepts, management services for programme and project delivery, and business venture support for new market entry and collaborative development. GADS also maintains a cooperation with ARMYTEC of Israel, pooling capability in military products and support solutions for allied nations.

## Products and solutions

- **Management services** - Business simplification, programme and project management, business process redesign, risk assessment, and strategy review for aerospace and defence clients.
- **Concept innovation** - Technology surveys and appraisal, feasibility assessment, concept realisation, and regulatory support for new air vehicle programmes.
- **Business ventures** - Market assessment, collaborative venture formation, due diligence, and joint venture support for international defence business development.
