# GridSmart Ltd

> Exeter-based supplier of secure end-to-end tracking, sensing and switching software and hardware for military, law enforcement and intelligence customers.

## What they do

GridSmart supplies and supports secure tracking, sensing and switching solutions to law enforcement, military and intelligence agencies. Its Auspex platform is the common operating environment across the GridSmart product range, built to NCSC guidelines with security ingrained at the code level and OWASP best-practice development standards applied throughout. The company is staffed and operated by ex-military and police operators and provides first, second and third-line support for all software and hardware. Server solutions can be deployed on-site or as a secure cloud solution. GridSmart holds Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 9001 and ISO 27001 certifications.

## Products and solutions

- **Auspex platform** - secure software and hardware tracking platform for covert and overt tracking of subjects, operators and equipment; deployable on-site or as a secure cloud solution.
- **Tracking hardware** - devices sourced from a trusted supply chain and supplied with unlimited data for the duration of contracts.
- **Tailored hardware and software** - bespoke solutions developed and manufactured to meet individual customer operational requirements.
