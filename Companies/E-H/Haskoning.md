# Haskoning

> Global engineering and consultancy firm with a specialist practice in naval base design, shipbuilding infrastructure, simulation, and defence engineering.

## What they do

Haskoning (operating globally as Royal HaskoningDHV) integrates over 140 years of design and engineering expertise with digital technologies including modelling, simulation, and software solutions. The firm is active in naval base and shipyard lifecycle projects and holds a portfolio of defence consultancy, maritime infrastructure, and simulation and modelling work. Its UK office is based in Peterborough.

## Products and solutions

- **Naval base and shipyard design** - master planning, infrastructure design, and lifecycle consultancy for naval facilities and shipbuilding yards.
- **Simulation and modelling** - modelling and simulation services applied to defence engineering and infrastructure problems.
- **Defence consultancy** - strategic and technical advisory across the full defence consultancy spectrum per the ADS directory.
- **Maritime infrastructure** - engineering design and consultancy for port, harbour, and maritime facility development with defence application.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
