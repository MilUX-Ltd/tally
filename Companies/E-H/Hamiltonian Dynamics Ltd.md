# Hamiltonian Dynamics Ltd

> UK defence and cyber security software company delivering mission-critical systems for government, national security and law enforcement.

## What they do

Hamiltonian Dynamics builds secure, high-performance software for the defence, national security and law enforcement sectors, with a secure-by-design engineering approach applied from the outset. The company works across discovery, architecture, development, test and transition to operation, and delivers via early and incremental agile methods. It collaborates with government and private sector partners across command and control, data analytics and data collection solutions.

## Products and solutions

- **STAR (Speech Text Audio Recognition)** - a bespoke speech-to-text capability within the company's Data Analytics Framework (DAF).
- **Data Analytics Framework (DAF)** - a suite of bespoke analytics capabilities developed for government and defence customers.
