# Frankenburg Technologies Ltd

> Estonian defence technology startup building affordable, mass-manufacturable guided interceptor missile systems for counter-UAS and short-range air defence, with a UK registered entity and investment in UK R&D.

## What they do

Frankenburg Technologies is a European defence technology company founded in 2024 and headquartered in Tallinn, Estonia, with a UK entity registered in London. The company designs and manufactures low-cost guided interceptor missiles intended for mass production and rapid replenishment, addressing what it describes as a cost and scale gap in modern air defence. Frankenburg's Mark I missile is designed to intercept Class III UAS targets and other aerial threats. The company has operations in Estonia, Latvia (including a missile assembly site in Riga), Lithuania, the UK, and Ukraine, and has committed to investing in UK research and development. Partners include Babcock, Airbus, Milrem Robotics, and Colt CZ Group. The UK entity's SIC codes include defence activities (84220).

## Products and solutions

- **Mark I Guided Interceptor Missile** - The company's first product; described as the smallest and lowest-cost guided interceptor missile to have completed a live-fire intercept. Engineered for mass production using commercially available components and integrated across land, air, and maritime platforms.
