# Fieldfisher

> International law firm advising the defence, aerospace and space sectors, with active programmes across F-35, satellite, and defence technology.

## What they do

Fieldfisher LLP is a multinational law firm providing legal services across commercial, regulatory, transactional, and technology matters. Its headline sectors include technology, financial services, energy and natural resources, and life sciences. Within those it runs specialist practices in satellite and space, international trade and sanctions, intellectual property, defence supply chain, and real estate. The firm is an ADS Group member and has a documented track record advising defence primes, MOD joint ventures, and national-security-focused space companies.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence programme legal advisory** - commercial, supply chain, IP, real estate, health and safety and regulatory advice on major defence programmes, including the F-35 global maintenance, repair, overhaul and upgrade programme for the BAE Systems / Northrop Grumman / MOD joint venture Sealand Support Services Limited.
- **Space and satellite law** - advising on satellite launches, regulatory matters, and national security space projects; acted for SatVu on the HotSat-2 launch (SpaceX Transporter-16, March 2026), a satellite carrying mid-wave infrared sensors for national security applications.
- **International trade and sanctions** - regulatory advice relevant to defence exports, dual-use goods, and Russia sanctions.
- **Defence technology procurement analysis** - published analysis on MOD's Defence AI Centre (DAIC) AI Model Arena and technology procurement processes.
