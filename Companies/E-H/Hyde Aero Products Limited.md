# Hyde Aero Products Limited

> Aerospace precision manufacturer producing detailed parts and integrated structural components, with over 50 years of design and manufacturing experience.

## What they do

Hyde Aero Products designs, manufactures, and surface-finishes detailed parts and integrated structural components for aerospace customers globally. Capabilities span CNC machining (5- and 6-axis), additive manufacturing, welding, forming, finishing, and assembly, covering both long-term strategic supply and rapid-response AOG requirements.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ITAR, NADCAP (CP, Heat Treatment, NDT, Welding), Boeing D1-4426, Cyber Essentials Plus, SC21 Bronze
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
