# Faltec Europe

> Sunderland precision plastics manufacturer supplying ground systems, aerospace defence and specialist equipment programmes; UK subsidiary of Tokyo-listed Faltec Co. Ltd.

## What they do

Faltec Europe is a UK precision manufacturer based at the International Advanced Manufacturing Park (IAMP) in Sunderland, producing injection-moulded, co-extruded and assembled components for automotive, defence and aerospace customers. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of Faltec Co. Ltd, listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange and headquartered in Kawasaki, Japan. Its primary business is automotive Tier 1 supply, but it operates a dedicated defence sector offering covering ground systems, aerospace defence components and specialist mission equipment. Faltec Europe is JOSCAR-registered, holds ADS Group and Make UK Defence memberships, and has signed the Armed Forces Covenant. The company employs approximately 450 people at its Sunderland site.

## Products and solutions

- **Ground systems components** - internal and external vehicle system parts, structural and housing components, and lightweight shielding for military ground platforms.
- **Aerospace defence components** - structural and interior elements for defence aviation applications.
- **Specialist mission equipment parts** - bespoke injection-moulded components manufactured to customer specification for mission-specific requirements.
- **In-house manufacturing capabilities** - injection moulding (350 to 2,300 tonne presses), co-extrusion, assembly and automation, surface treatment, toolroom operations and precision testing.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR (registered)
- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials; IATF 16949 (automotive quality, encompasses ISO 9001, BSI certified); ISO 14001 (LRQA certified); ISO 45001; Armed Forces Covenant signatory; FSQS registered
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Make UK Defence; NE RDSC (North East Reserve and Defence Support Community)
