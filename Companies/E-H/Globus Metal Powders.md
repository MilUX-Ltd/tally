# Globus Metal Powders Ltd

> UK manufacturer and atomiser of high-integrity metal powders for aerospace, defence and other mission-critical applications, using vacuum inert gas atomisation technology.

## What they do

Globus Metal Powders produces high-purity, process-optimised metal powders for powder metallurgy Hot Isostatic Pressing (PM-HIP), additive manufacturing, thermal spraying and related advanced consolidation processes. The company manufactures at its Middlesbrough facility using VIGA (vacuum inert gas atomisation) technology and undertakes in-house alloying to achieve controlled chemistry, ultra-low oxygen content and precise particle morphology. Customers use Globus powders to produce mission-critical components including turbine parts, airframe structures, naval hardware and submarine components.

## Products and solutions

- **Nickel superalloy powders** - including GMP 625, GMP 718, GMP 939 and GMP Alloy X for aerospace turbine and structural applications.
- **Martensitic and precipitation-hardening steel powders** - including GMP 300M, GMP M300, GMP 13-8Mo, GMP 15-5PH and GMP 17-4PH for high-strength defence components.
- **Cobalt-chromium alloy (GMP F75)** - for wear-resistant and biomedical applications.
- **Nickel-aluminium bronze (GMP 02-833)** - for marine and naval defence hardware.
- **Toll atomising and powder recycling services** - contract atomisation and re-certification of customer-supplied materials.
