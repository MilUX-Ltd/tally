# EXFO Europe Ltd

> UK and European subsidiary of EXFO Inc, a Canadian test and measurement company with a dedicated defence and homeland security division covering fibre optic network testing, tactical communications intelligence and counter-UAV systems.

## What they do

EXFO Europe Ltd is the UK-registered subsidiary of EXFO Inc, headquartered in Quebec City, Canada, with approximately 1,500 employees globally. The UK entity, based in Chandler's Ford, Eastleigh, operates across two distinct capability areas: fibre optic and network test and measurement equipment serving defence laboratories and supply chains, and a dedicated Homeland Security Division offering tactical communications intelligence systems. EXFO's defence test and measurement products are used to verify fibre connectivity from the lab to live deployed environments across all branches of armed forces; the Homeland Security Division products serve elite police forces, military special forces and government security agencies. The company exhibited at Security and Policing 2026, the NPSA's closed government-facing exhibition at Farnborough.

## Products and solutions

- **Fibre optic test and measurement** - OTDRs, fibre inspection scopes, field certifiers and lab instruments for verifying fibre infrastructure in defence and aerospace environments; includes the FIP-500 fibre inspection probe and MaxTester 945 Fiber Certifier.
- **5G-to-the-battlefield network testing** - tools for validating mobile network performance in deployed military environments.
- **IMSI catchers with direction-finding** - portable, vehicular, fixed-site and airborne systems for cellular interception and geolocation.
- **Mobile device interrogation and network analysis** - equipment for mobile phone interrogation, cellular and WiFi interception, and mobile service blocking.
- **Counter-UAV measures** - systems described in the Homeland Security portfolio targeting small UAS threats.
