# High Temperature Material Systems Ltd HTMS

> Materials startup developing Ceramic Matrix Composites for aerospace, defence and high-temperature applications.

## What they do

HTMS develops bespoke Ceramic Matrix Composite (CMC) materials, specialising in novel matrix chemistries that yield improved performance and durability at extreme temperatures. Founded in 2021 to address the UK's lack of sovereign CMC capability, the company produces a CMC prepreg that processes similarly to polymer prepreg but sustains long-term use up to 1,000°C. It works with defence and automotive Tier 1 suppliers and OEMs on applications including thermal protection systems, heat shields and exhaust components.

## Products and solutions

- **Ignishield** - CMC material using basalt fibre, designed for thermal protection and fire-resistant applications.
- **ThermaLite** - CMC material using alumina fibre for high-temperature structural use.
- **Carbonite X** - CMC material using carbon fibre for performance-critical applications.
