# First Light Fusion

> Oxford University spinout commercialising hypervelocity amplifier technology for defence, space, and fusion research customers.

## What they do

First Light Fusion developed inertial confinement fusion technology using projectile-based and pulsed-power approaches. Following a strategic pivot in 2025, the company now operates two commercial lines: licensing its amplifier technology to other fusion ventures, and selling and deploying its experimental infrastructure to defence, space, and research customers. Their VIPER velocity amplifier and supporting facilities are explicitly positioned for warhead assurance, armour and materials qualification, and micro-meteoroid and orbital debris testing.

## Products and solutions

- **VIPER velocity amplifier** - a device that boosts projectile velocity beyond 10 km/s; first commercial sale delivered to Texas A&M HVIL in June 2026. Stated applications include hypervelocity impact testing, orbital debris simulation, and warhead assurance.
- **Two-stage gas gun** - the UK's largest, used for hypervelocity launch and impact testing.
- **Pulsed power facility** - Europe's largest pulsed power facility, used for high-energy physics, propulsion research, and extreme-environment testing relevant to defence materials science.
