# Global Marine Systems Ltd

> UK-based subsea cable installation and maintenance company, part of Global Marine Group, acquired by Keppel Infrastructure Fund (Singapore) in 2025; the only UK-domiciled operator laying and maintaining international submarine communications cables.

## What they do

Global Marine Systems provides installation, maintenance and repair of submarine fibre optic cables to the telecommunications, oil and gas, and deep-sea research sectors. The company operates a fleet of cable ships and trenching assets and holds maintenance agreements covering approximately 33% of the world's subsea fibre optic cable, including systems across the North and South Atlantic, English Channel, North Sea, Caribbean Sea and South East Pacific. As the sole UK-domiciled cable ship operator, it holds sovereign relevance to UK critical national infrastructure. The wider Global Marine Group, headquartered in Chelmsford, Essex, also includes OceanIQ (subsea cable data, route engineering and permitting) and SubConnect (subsea housing and interconnectors).

The Group was acquired by Keppel Infrastructure Fund (Singapore) and Keppel Infrastructure Trust, with the transaction completing in stages through 2025. The operating entity and vessels remain UK-domiciled; the group HQ is now Singapore.

## Products and solutions

- **Subsea cable installation** - planning and installation of submarine fibre optic cable systems using a fleet of six dynamically positioned cable ships.
- **Subsea cable maintenance and repair** - emergency and scheduled repair under long-term maintenance agreements covering Atlantic, Channel and North Sea systems.
- **OceanIQ data and route engineering** - subsea cable data records covering 2.8 million km of cable (97% of global fibre), survey, route engineering and permitting services.
- **SubConnect** - design, testing and supply of subsea housing, interconnectors and cable jointing services.
- **Staffing and asset hire** - specialist offshore personnel and vessel asset hire.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; ISO 14001; ISO 45001
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Atlantic Cable Maintenance and Repair Agreement (ACMA)
