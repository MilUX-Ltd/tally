# HeliOperations

> A UK helicopter aviation services company providing search and rescue aircrew training, synthetic simulator training, and operational management services to military and government clients from its base at Portland, Dorset.

## What they do

HeliOperations is a British helicopter company based at The HeliPort on Osprey Quay, Portland, Dorset, the site of the former RNAS Portland (HMS Osprey). The company delivers SAR aircrew and groundcrew training, both live flying from its Portland heliport and synthetic training at its simulator facility at RNAS Culdrose. It also supplies aircraft, technical support, and operational management services to government operators and military customers. HeliOperations holds active contracts with the UK Ministry of Defence and the German Federal Navy.

## Products and solutions

- **SAR aircrew training** - Live helicopter pilot and crew training including night-vision (NVIS) and SAR-specific programmes, delivered from Portland and overseas.
- **Synthetic simulator training** - Bespoke simulated helicopter pilot training packages using a Sea King simulator at the Culdrose facility, including NVG scenarios.
- **Fleet helicopter support** - Aircraft provision, technical support, maintenance, pilots, and crew under contracted support arrangements for military customers.
- **Operational and regulatory support** - Aviation regulatory assistance and operational management services for government and commercial operators.
