# HumaniFAI Ltd

> Human factors and AI consultancy specialising in ethical, trust-centred AI deployment for defence and security applications.

## What they do

HumaniFAI provides research and consultancy services integrating human factors, cognitive science, cyber security, and ethical AI design to support safe and accountable AI deployment in defence and security organisations. The company covers C2 systems, counter-terrorism intelligence, simulation and modelling, security systems integration, and training services across military, emergency services, and government customers.
