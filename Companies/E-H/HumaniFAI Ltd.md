# HumaniFAI Ltd

> Human factors and AI research consultancy examining how AI-enabled systems affect people, teams and organisations in high-stakes environments.

## What they do

HumaniFAI is a research and consultancy organisation specialising in human-centred AI from a human factors perspective. The company supports organisations seeking to understand how AI-enabled systems interact with people, teams and organisations in high-stakes environments, focusing on how AI affects decision making, workload, trust, accountability and system resilience when deployed in real operational contexts. HumaniFAI states it does not build AI systems; instead it provides independent, research-led insight into the human implications of AI, drawing on human factors, cognitive science and socio-technical systems thinking, for defence, security, healthcare, cybersecurity, transport and critical national infrastructure customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Human-centred AI research and consultancy** - independent assessment of how AI systems affect human decision-making, workload, trust and system resilience across the system lifecycle, from concept through deployment, use, failure and recovery.
- **Assurance and governance support** - human factors evidence to inform AI safety, assurance and procurement decisions.

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; ISO 27001:2022; Cyber Essentials; Cyber Essentials Plus; Def Stan 05-135
