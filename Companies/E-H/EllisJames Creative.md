# EllisJames Creative

> Defence and aerospace creative agency specialising in brand, bid design, and stakeholder communications.

## What they do

EllisJames Creative is a Bristol-based creative consultancy serving organisations in the aerospace and defence sector. It provides brand strategy, bid and proposal design, 3D modelling and animation, web design, copywriting, and events support to capture, business development, and communications teams inside defence primes and their supply chain. The company was incorporated in 2014 and positions itself specifically around the MOD and UK Government audience engagement cycle.

## Products and solutions

- **Bid and proposal design** - visual design and layout support for capture and bid teams across the defence and aerospace sector.
- **Brand strategy and identity** - brand development, messaging frameworks, and design systems for defence-sector clients.
- **3D modelling and animation** - technical visualisation for platforms, systems, and concepts.
- **Stakeholder communications** - engagement campaigns, presentation design, and exhibition support targeting MOD and UK Government audiences.
