# ECS Special Projects

> Designer and manufacturer of maritime and sub-sea explosive ordnance disposal systems, with products in service with the Royal Navy.

## What they do

ECS Special Projects designs, manufactures, and supplies maritime EOD systems for military and commercial UXO clearance operations. Its flagship Cobra MDS (Mine Disposal System) is a modular sub-sea EOD system in service with the UK Royal Navy and used commercially on offshore wind farm and pipeline projects. The company also manufactures non-lethal security systems and inert training mine targets. It trades as Cobra MDS.

Engineering design is ISO 9001 certified. The company works through a network of strategic partners including Atlas Elektronik GmbH, Ocean Modules, and VideoRay for system integration.

## Products and solutions

- **Cobra MDS** - modular sub-sea mine disposal system; in service with the UK Royal Navy for mine investigation and neutralisation; used commercially on North Sea wind farm and pipeline UXO clearance projects.
- **Viper MDS** - twin-shot disruptor integrated onto the VideoRay Defender ROV for military and commercial EOD operations.
- **Cobra MASTT** - command and control system for Cobra MDS.
- **MX series (MX-50, MX-100, MX-150)** - self-fill, transportable IED and UXO mine disposal tools rated to 300 m depth.
- **LX range** - self-fill low-order deflagrators and disruptors for controlled IED and UXO neutralisation.
- **VAL (Vessel Arrest Launcher)** - pneumatic non-lethal vessel arrest system; deployed by Marine Security Services at the London 2012 Olympics and at the 2013 G8 Summit.
- **Inert mine targets** - replica training mines for Royal Navy validation and training.
