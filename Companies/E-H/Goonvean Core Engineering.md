# Goonvean Core Engineering

> Devon-based precision CNC machining and CMM inspection company serving aerospace and defence manufacturing customers.

## What they do

Goonvean Core Engineering operates from its facility in Cullompton, Devon, providing CNC machining and coordinate measuring machine (CMM) inspection services. The company holds 5-axis machining capability and in-house design, serving aerospace and defence supply chains. As an ADS member, the company is positioned within the UK defence and aerospace manufacturing base.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC machining** - 5-axis precision machining for complex components.
- **CMM inspection** - coordinate measuring machine inspection for precision verification and quality assurance.
- **In-house design** - design support alongside machining and inspection services.
