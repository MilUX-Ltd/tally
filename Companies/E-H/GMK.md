# GMK Ltd

> UK importer and distributor of firearms, ammunition and associated equipment to the shooting sports market, law enforcement and the British military, operating as part of the Beretta Holding Group.

## What they do

GMK is the UK's principal importer of shotguns, rifles and rifle ammunition from Beretta group brands, distributing to the recreational shooting market, law enforcement and the Ministry of Defence. The company holds the Beretta Gallery in St James's, London, and has supplied firearms and associated equipment to British Army and UK law enforcement bodies. In 2025 GMK Tactical Products became part of Beretta Defence Technologies (BDT), through which the full range of Beretta group products is offered to UK law enforcement and the British military. Beretta Holding Group holds 60% of the company.

## Products and solutions

- **Beretta shotguns and rifles** - distributed under exclusive UK import arrangements.
- **Sako and Tikka rifles** - precision bolt-action rifles for sporting and professional use.
- **Benelli shotguns** - supplied to the British Army under a won tender.
- **Rifle ammunition** - RWS, Norma and GECO ammunition brands distributed through RUAG Ammotec arrangement.
- **Optics and accessories** - scopes, binoculars, thermal optics, moderators and gun safes.
- **GMK Tactical Products / Beretta Defence Technologies** - law enforcement and military product range from across the Beretta group, from 2025 operating under the BDT umbrella.
