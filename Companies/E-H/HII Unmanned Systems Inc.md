# HII Unmanned Systems Inc

> Designer and manufacturer of autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs) for marine research, commercial, and defence applications.

## What they do

HII Unmanned Systems Inc (trading as Hydroid) produces the REMUS family of AUVs, used across defence, research, and commercial maritime missions. The company operates in the UK from Fareham, Hampshire, and is a subsidiary of Kongsberg Maritime.

## Products and solutions

- **REMUS AUVs** - A range of autonomous underwater vehicles spanning shallow to deep-water operations, used for mine countermeasures, hydrographic survey, and intelligence-gathering missions.
