# HII Unmanned Systems Inc

> Designer and manufacturer of autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs) for marine research, commercial, and defence applications.

## What they do

HII Unmanned Systems Inc (trading as Hydroid) produces the REMUS family of autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs), used across defence, research, and commercial maritime missions. The company operates in the UK from Fareham, Hampshire, and is a subsidiary of HII (Huntington Ingalls Industries), the largest military shipbuilder in the United States, following HII's acquisition of Hydroid in 2020.

## Products and solutions

- **REMUS AUVs** - A range of autonomous underwater vehicles spanning shallow to deep-water operations, used for mine countermeasures, hydrographic survey, and intelligence-gathering missions. Variants include the REMUS 100, 300, 600 and 620.
- **Sea Launcher** - an automated, ship-based launch and recovery system for REMUS vehicles.
- **ROMULUS** - an unmanned surface vessel under development by HII, complementing the REMUS underwater family.
