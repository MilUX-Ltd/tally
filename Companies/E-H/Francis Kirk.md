# Francis Kirk (Socket Screws) Ltd

> Manchester-based distributor and stockist of specialist fasteners, socket products, PPE, and industrial supplies, serving the aerospace and other precision engineering sectors.

## What they do

Francis Kirk is a fifth-generation family business founded in 1868, operating from a 24,000 square foot warehouse and trade counter in Denton, Manchester. The company supplies fasteners, socket screws, bolts, nuts, washers, hand tools, PPE, and industrial consumables. It holds exclusive UK distribution rights for the Philidas self-locking nut range, and is the only UK stockist to carry Philidas, Unbrako, and Holo-Krome fasteners under one roof. Francis Kirk is a member of the North West Aerospace Alliance and supplies the aerospace sector; its ADS Group membership confirms its position within the aerospace, defence, and security supply chain.

## Products and solutions

- **Philidas self-locking nuts** - Anti-vibration all-metal locking nuts used in aerospace, automotive, rail, renewable energy, and defence applications.
- **Socket screws and fasteners** - Socket head caps (DIN 912), countersunk, button, and set screw ranges in metric and imperial, including Unbrako and Holo-Krome branded products.
- **Nord-Lock washers** - Wedge-locking washers for high-vibration environments.
- **PPE and workwear** - Industrial personal protective equipment, high-visibility clothing, and footwear.
- **Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) and Kanban** - Inventory management services for industrial customers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, North West Aerospace Alliance, British and Irish Association of Fastener Distributors (BIAFD)
