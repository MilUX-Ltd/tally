# hpad

> AI system that learns how complex organisations operate from expert interviews, documents, policies, and workflows.

## What they do

hpad builds AI for organisational knowledge capture and process understanding. The system ingests expert interviews, internal documents, policies, and workflows to model how complex organisations operate, with applications across defence and other complex institutional environments. The company is a member of the D3IP community at the Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
