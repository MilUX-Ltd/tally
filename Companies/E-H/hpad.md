# hpad

> US enterprise AI company whose Enterprise AI Platform has been trialled on a UK Army capability management challenge through the D3IP defence innovation network.

## What they do

hpad, Inc. builds an "Enterprise AI Platform" that captures organisational knowledge from documents, standards, and subject matter experts, structures it into a shared enterprise AI model, and uses that model to power role-specific "AI companions" for decision support. The company positions its approach as knowledge-driven and traceable, in contrast to general-purpose AI tools that rely only on large datasets. hpad is headquartered in Wichita, Kansas, and was founded in 2019. Its public case experience spans process safety, industrial automation, banking regulatory compliance, business and technology strategy, and defence capability planning.

hpad's UK defence engagement runs through the D3IP collaboration network at the Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park. In 2026, hpad and Mission Decisions completed the Incubate phase of D3IP's FCCM (Force Coherence Capability Management) initiative, delivered on behalf of Defence BattleLab under UK Defence Innovation (UKDI). The work explored using AI to support the British Army's structured capability management process, tracing a "golden thread" from strategic policy through to capability outputs, and the companies reported that their prototypes reduced the manual effort involved in producing capability artefacts.

## Products and solutions

- **hpad Enterprise AI Platform** - a platform comprising an enterprise knowledge model, a reasoning layer that encodes expert judgement, role-specific AI companions, an integration and data layer, and the "Workbench" environment used by subject matter experts to build, review, and validate the model.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Programmes:** D3IP collaboration network (Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park); completed the FCCM Incubate phase delivered under UK Defence Innovation (UKDI).
