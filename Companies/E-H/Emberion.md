# Emberion

> Cambridge-based developer of colloidal quantum dot infrared sensors and cameras for surveillance, drone-based imaging, and defence ISR; acquired by French electro-optical company Exosens in April 2026.

## What they do

Emberion Limited is a deep-technology sensor company that designs and manufactures shortwave infrared (SWIR) and broadband visible-to-thermal cameras using colloidal quantum dot technology and graphene transducers integrated with proprietary CMOS readout ICs. Founded in 2016 as a spinout from Nokia with R&D operations in Cambridge (UK) and Espoo (Finland), the company targets defence surveillance, portable imaging, and drone-based ISR markets. In April 2026 Emberion was acquired by Exosens (Nasdaq: EXOSENS), a French electro-optical group, to expand Exosens's defence and surveillance imaging portfolio. The Emberion brand and Cambridge operation remain active post-acquisition.

## Products and solutions

- **VS20 Vis-SWIR camera** - 400-2000 nm broadband camera combining visible and shortwave infrared in a single sensor, suited to night vision, surveillance, and laser range finding.
- **Ultra-wide band Vis-SWIR-Thermal sensors** - broad-spectrum sensors spanning visible, SWIR, and thermal bands for multi-domain ISR.
- **Graphene quantum dot transducers** - proprietary sensing element technology underpinning the product range; covered by 25 protected patent families worldwide.
