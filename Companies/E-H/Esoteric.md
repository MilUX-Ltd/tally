# Esoteric

> Counter-espionage and Technical Surveillance Countermeasures (TSCM) specialist serving defence, government and high-profile clients; a wholly-owned subsidiary of Mitie since November 2021.

## What they do

Esoteric is a specialist counter-espionage and TSCM company with over 20 years of experience, founded in 1998 and now operating within Mitie Intelligence Services. The company provides TSCM sweeps (bug detection and electronic surveillance countermeasures), RF-shielded room installation, cyber TSCM, physical security penetration testing, insider threat services and accredited covert surveillance training. Esoteric's engineers are drawn exclusively from government agency or military backgrounds and cleared to the highest government security levels. The company serves defence, transportation, financial, pharmaceutical and government clients.

## Products and solutions

- **TSCM (Technical Surveillance Countermeasures)** - detection and removal of covert listening devices and electronic surveillance threats.
- **TRAICE** - integrated offering spanning TSCM, Risk Assurance, Insider Threat and Counter-Espionage.
- **Cyber TSCM and penetration testing** - technical assessment of cyber and physical security posture.
- **Covert surveillance training** - accredited courses for government and law enforcement personnel.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001 (NSI-approved); ISO 27001 (BSI-certified); Cyber Essentials Plus (IASME-certified); NSI accreditation for electronic sweeping and covert investigations; BS 7858:2019 (all staff)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
