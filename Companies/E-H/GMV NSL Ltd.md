# GMV NSL Ltd

> Nottingham-based UK subsidiary of Spanish technology group GMV, specialising in position, navigation and timing, guidance, navigation and control, and autonomy.

## What they do

GMV NSL is the UK subsidiary of GMV, an international aerospace and defence technology group. The company specialises in position, navigation and timing (PNT) technology including satellite navigation, augmentation systems, alternative PNT, threat detection and sensor fusion. It delivers guidance, navigation and control (GNC) solutions and autonomy technology for mission-critical applications across defence, security and space.

## Products and solutions

- **PNT systems** - satellite navigation, augmentation and alternative PNT solutions incorporating threat detection and sensor fusion for high-integrity applications.
- **GNC** - guidance, navigation and control engineering for platforms requiring precise autonomous operation.
- **Orbit determination and prediction** - services supporting space mission planning and operations.
