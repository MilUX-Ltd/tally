# Electrogear

> Fareham-based electro-mechanical and RF electronics manufacturer serving aerospace, defence, maritime, and energy sectors.

## What they do

Electrogear (Fareham) Ltd is a small engineering business providing electro-mechanical, electrical, electronic, and radio frequency design and manufacturing services. The company covers the full product lifecycle from prototype development through to manufacturing, installation, and commissioning. It serves sectors including aerospace, defence, nuclear, automotive, and maritime. In December 2024, Electrogear entered a strategic collaboration with Barnbrook Systems Ltd to develop opportunities in defence, energy, and hydrogen.

## Products and solutions

- **RF, Electronics, and Electro-Mechanical Design for Manufacture** - custom design and build services across radio frequency, electronic, and electro-mechanical product domains.
- **PCB Assembly and Cable Harness** - board-level assembly through to full cable harness and on-site installation.
- **Prototype and Low-Volume Manufacturing** - rapid prototyping, testing, troubleshooting, and commissioning support.
