# Elasticsearch

> UK subsidiary of Elastic NV, providing search-powered AI and cybersecurity platforms used in defence and government, including the UK Ministry of Defence.

## What they do

Elasticsearch Limited is the UK entity of Elastic NV (NYSE: ESTC), a Dutch-American software company headquartered in San Francisco. Elastic's Search AI Platform is used by defence organisations to unify data across classification domains, accelerate threat detection, and support compliance. The company has published materials aligned with the UK MoD's Defence Data Strategy and is an exhibitor at UK Security and Policing events.

## Products and solutions

- **Elastic Search AI Platform** - unified search and analytics across structured and unstructured data, supporting multi-domain environments including Official, Secret, and above-Secret classifications.
- **Elastic SIEM** - security information and event management tooling used in government and defence cybersecurity operations.
- **Elastic Observability** - platform monitoring and performance management for defence IT and operational systems.
