# HK Systems Ltd

> Systems engineering and project management consultancy serving the defence sector.

## What they do

HK Systems Ltd provides specialist systems engineering and project management services to defence clients, covering the full engineering lifecycle from requirements elicitation through to integration, verification, and validation. The firm also offers obsolescence management and FMEA support.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems Engineering Services** - Requirements management, architecture, design, integration, verification and validation aligned to ISO/IEC 15288 lifecycle processes.
- **Project Management** - Programme and project management support for defence programmes.
