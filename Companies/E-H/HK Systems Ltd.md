# HK Systems Ltd

> Systems engineering and project management consultancy serving the defence sector.

## What they do

HK Systems Ltd provides specialist systems engineering and project management services to defence clients, covering the full engineering lifecycle from requirements elicitation through to integration, verification, and validation. The firm also offers obsolescence management and FMEA support. It is registered in London and states plans to expand its systems engineering services into aerospace, space and automotive sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems Engineering Services** - Requirements elicitation and management, systems engineering lifecycle processes aligned to ISO/IEC 15288, system architecture, design and interfaces, integration, verification and validation, and systems engineering management.
- **Obsolescence Management** - Technical-solution obsolescence management for defence programmes.
- **FMEA** - Failure mode and effects analysis support.
- **Project Management** - Programme and project management support for defence programmes.
