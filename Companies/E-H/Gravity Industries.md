# Gravity Industries

> British developer of the patented Jet Suit for human-vectored flight.

## What they do

Gravity Industries designs and builds the Jet Suit, a wearable human propulsion system enabling vectored-thrust flight and agile manoeuvre. Founded in 2017, the company has moved from demonstration and entertainment toward professional and defence deployment, with applications cited in ship boarding, coast guard operations, special forces mobility, and medical and mountain rescue.

## Products and solutions

- **Jet Suit** - patented wearable jet propulsion system providing human-vectored thrust, demonstrated at speeds in excess of 80 mph.
- **Defence and security applications** - rapid ship boarding, force mobility and casualty-response use cases trialled with military and rescue users.

## Recent news

- **2021-11** - The British Army and Royal Marines trialled the Jet Suit for ship boarding and combat mobility off the UK's southern coast. [source](https://thedefensepost.com/2021/11/05/british-army-jet-suit-combat/)
