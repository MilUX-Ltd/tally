# Hiatus

> Veteran-owned design and communications company building brand identities, websites and UX for defence, dual-use and space organisations.

## What they do

Hiatus is a veteran-owned design practice working across defence, dual-use and space sectors. The company designs brand identities and high-performance, accessible websites for organisations operating in complex and regulated environments, and offers human-centred design and accessibility training, including UX workshops and interface audits, for defence and critical-sector clients. Hiatus is a member of the D3IP community at the Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.

## Products and solutions

- **Brand identity design** - visual identity systems for defence contractors and dual-use organisations, built to hold up across regulatory and operational contexts.
- **Website design and build** - accessible, high-performance websites for defence and aerospace clients, from procurement platforms to internal operations portals.
- **UX training and consulting** - hands-on training and strategic consulting in human-centred design and accessibility for defence and critical-sector teams.
