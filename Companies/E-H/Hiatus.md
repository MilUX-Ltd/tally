# Hiatus

> Veteran-owned British and European design company specialising in branding, web design, content, and UX design for defence and adjacent clients.

## What they do

Hiatus is a veteran-owned design practice offering branding, web design, content, and user experience (UX) design. The company is a member of the D3IP community at the Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
