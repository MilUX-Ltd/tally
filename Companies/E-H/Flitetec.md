# Flitetec

> Aerospace manufacturer and distributor of aircraft interior components and assemblies for civil and military fixed-wing and rotary platforms.

## What they do

Flitetec Limited was established in 1985 and is headquartered in Amersham, Buckinghamshire, with additional operations in Hampshire and south-west France. The company designs, manufactures, repairs, overhauls, sources, and integrates aircraft interior products for both civil and military aircraft, including fixed-wing and helicopter platforms. Flitetec also operates an aerospace distribution service, and holds CAA Part 21G manufacture approval, Part 145 maintenance approval, and EASA Part 145 maintenance approval, with quality management certified to AS9100 D by Lloyds Register.

## Products and solutions

- **Aircraft interior manufacture and repair** - design, manufacture, and overhaul of aircraft interior components including seating products, galley assemblies, lavatory modules, fairings, meal trays, and specialist interior fittings for civil and military platforms.
- **Aerospace distribution** - sourcing and distribution of aviation components and products for defence and commercial aircraft applications across approximately 100 countries.
