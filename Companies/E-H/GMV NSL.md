# GMV NSL

> UK arm of the GMV group specialising in GNSS, space and geospatial systems, formed from Nottingham Scientific Ltd.

## What they do

GMV NSL is the UK subsidiary of the GMV group, created when GMV merged its UK operations with Nottingham Scientific Limited (NSL). It specialises in Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS), positioning, navigation and timing, and geospatial services. The wider GMV group works across space, defence and security, cybersecurity and intelligent transport, supporting government and defence programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **GNSS and PNT services** - positioning, navigation and timing engineering and analysis.
- **Space systems** - support to satellite and ground segment programmes.
- **Geospatial services** - location data products and applications.
