# HS Infra

> UK secure-infrastructure specialist building SCIFs, data centres, structured cabling, and audio-visual systems for MOD, government, and defence industry clients.

## What they do

HS Infra designs and builds secure physical infrastructure for defence and government clients, including Sensitive Compartmented Information Facilities (SCIFs), data centres, and structured cabling for protectively-marked environments. The firm holds JOSCAR registration, SCIDA credentials, ISO 9001, ISO 27001, and is an Armed Forces Covenant signatory. Based in Herefordshire, it serves MOD and wider government across the UK.

## Products and solutions

- **SCIF design and build** - end-to-end design and construction of Sensitive Compartmented Information Facilities to government security standards.
- **Data centre design and build** - bespoke and as-a-service data centre solutions for secure environments.
- **Structured cabling** - design and installation for classified and protectively-marked environments.
- **Technical Surveillance Counter Measures (TSCM)** - detection and removal of surveillance devices in sensitive environments.
- **Audio-visual integration** - AV systems integration into secure facilities.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 27001, SCIDA credentials, Armed Forces Covenant signatory
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered
