# ExTrac

> British AI-driven open-source intelligence and geopolitical risk-analytics company serving defence, security and government.

## What they do

ExTrac is a British OSINT and geopolitical risk-intelligence company. It combines curated data, human subject-matter expertise and domain-specific AI to detect and forecast risk across the physical world, online, and where the two intersect. It ingests high-relevance, hard-to-reach data streams to track violence, map narratives and surface emerging threats, supporting customers in defence, security, government, public safety, executive protection and risk monitoring. The registered entity is ExTrac AI Limited.

## Products and solutions

- **ExTrac platform** - a customisable web application for leaders, decision-makers and analysts, hiding complexity by default while keeping the underlying data fully accessible and auditable.
- **Co-Analyst** - a domain-specific, agentic AI that retrieves data, surfaces insight and generates visualisations and reports in response to analyst requests.
- **Curated data feeds and bespoke reporting** - expert-curated, hard-to-access data streams, with the option for customers to integrate their own data, plus a mobile app and bespoke reporting.
