# Ecolog International UK

> Global provider of integrated base support and life support services to military and government customers in remote and austere environments.

## What they do

Ecolog International delivers the full suite of base and life support services that keep military personnel operational: accommodation, catering, laundry, facilities management, supply chain, modular construction, engineering, waste and environmental services, healthcare, fuel, water supply, and air service. The company was founded in 1999 following identification of unmet support needs during NATO KFOR operations in Kosovo. Defence and government are its primary markets, with confirmed military customers including the UK Ministry of Defence, US Army, Bundeswehr, NATO, French Army, and the United Nations.

The UK entity (incorporated 2017) is the delivery vehicle for UK and European military contracts. The operational base is in Harlow, Essex. Ecolog has signed the UK Armed Forces Covenant and exhibits regularly at DSEI.

## Products and solutions

- **Life support** - integrated accommodation, catering, food service, and laundry for deployed military personnel.
- **Base and facilities management** - full site management services for military installations in remote and austere environments.
- **Supply chain and logistics** - end-to-end supply chain management for operational and contingency deployments.
- **Modular construction and engineering** - design, build, and maintenance of modular infrastructure for forward operating bases.
- **Healthcare services** - medical support and healthcare provision for deployed forces.
- **Environmental services** - waste management, water supply, and environmental compliance in operational settings.
