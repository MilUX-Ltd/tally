# High Value Manufacturing Catapult

> Innovate UK-funded national network of seven industrial innovation centres advancing high-value manufacturing capability, including for defence.

## What they do

The High Value Manufacturing Catapult is established and funded by Innovate UK to enable UK manufacturers to achieve measurable improvements in performance and productivity. It operates through seven specialist research, technology and innovation centres working collaboratively across advanced manufacturing disciplines. The HVM Catapult has a formal alliance with the Ministry of Defence to grow the defence industrial base and develop dual-use manufacturing technologies. Its work encompasses R&D translation, technology scale-up, supply chain development and manufacturing process innovation.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence manufacturing R&D** - applied research and technology translation supporting MoD programmes and the wider defence industrial base.
- **Dual-use technology development** - advanced manufacturing processes with both civilian and defence application, including areas such as AI, quantum technologies and semiconductors.
- **Supply chain development** - programmes to grow and qualify UK defence manufacturing suppliers.
