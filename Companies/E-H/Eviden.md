# Eviden

> Atos Group brand for cybersecurity, vision AI and mission-critical systems, with a defence offering serving European and Five Eyes ministries of defence.

## What they do

Eviden is the Atos Group brand for advanced computing, cybersecurity products, mission-critical systems and vision AI. It builds cyber-secure-by-design systems and electronics, data platforms for equipment availability and personnel readiness, and high-performance and AI computing, positioning around digital sovereignty and mission-critical applications. It runs a defence offering under its Mission-Critical Systems unit and operates a Cyber Defence Centre providing offensive security, red teaming and penetration testing. Eviden is part of Atos SE, which is publicly listed on Euronext Paris.

## Products and solutions

- **Cybersecurity products** - data encryption, digital identity management, post-quantum security, and a Cyber Defence Centre offering penetration testing, red teaming and phishing simulation.
- **Mission-critical systems** - defence, public safety and transport systems, including secure real-time communications.
- **Vision AI** - including ELIT AI, a radar-signals sensor with built-in sovereign AI.
