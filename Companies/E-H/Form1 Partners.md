# Form1 Partners Ltd

> Technology commercialisation partner connecting defence and security scale-ups with established corporates and primes.

## What they do

Form1 Partners is a commercialisation consultancy focused on B2B technology scale-ups and the corporates and primes that seek to work with them. The company has a specific defence and national security practice, staffed by personnel with backgrounds spanning MOD, RAF, Strategic Command, jHub and industry primes. It works with scale-ups to build partnerships with established defence corporates, and with corporates to access and qualify emerging technology from the SME and start-up market.

Form1 was appointed by Leonardo UK as its SME Collaboration Partner to run the Leonardo SME Collaboration Partner Programme, launched in March 2025. The programme identifies and operationalises long-term partnerships between Leonardo's UK business and defence and security SMEs, with the aim of delivering sovereign capability to UK defence and allied forces.

## Products and solutions

- **Corporate partnership advisory** - identifying, structuring and closing technology partnerships between scale-ups and established defence and security corporates.
- **SME programme management** - managing structured SME collaboration programmes on behalf of primes, including sourcing, qualification and relationship management.
- **VC and investor advisory** - supporting venture capital investors with defence sector access and qualification of portfolio company commercial opportunities.
