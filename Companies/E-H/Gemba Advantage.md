# Gemba Advantage

> Software and data engineering business delivering digital solutions for UK government departments and defence clients requiring high security clearance.

## What they do

Gemba Advantage is a software and data engineering company building and maintaining digital solutions for organisations operating in high-security and operationally complex environments. The majority of staff hold high-level security clearance. The company holds multi-year contracts with UK public sector departments and agencies, and serves private sector defence clients including BAE Systems. Gemba was acquired by Actica Consulting in October 2024, forming part of the Actica Group alongside Ntegra; Actica is backed by Sovereign Capital Partners and focuses on technology transformation and cybersecurity for national security clients.

## Products and solutions

- **High-impact software delivery** - custom software products for mission-critical government and defence programmes.
- **Digital transformation services** - advisory and delivery support helping organisations adopt modern technology practices within secure environments.
- **Supercharged product management** - user research, design and product management services applied to complex public sector programmes.
