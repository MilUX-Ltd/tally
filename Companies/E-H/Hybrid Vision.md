# Hybrid Vision

> AI assurance company improving the safety and survivability of fully autonomous systems for UK MoD and AUKUS partners.

## What they do

Hybrid Vision develops OughtAI, a product focused on the assurance and survivability of fully autonomous systems. The company works with UK MoD and within the AUKUS partnership, addressing the assurance challenge for autonomous platforms operating without direct human control.

## Products and solutions

- **OughtAI** - Assurance platform for fully autonomous systems, targeting UK MoD and AUKUS programmes.
