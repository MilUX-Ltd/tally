# Hybrid Vision

> AI assurance company delivering runtime monitoring and safety intervention for robotics and autonomous systems, marketed as OughtAI.

## What they do

Hybrid Vision develops OughtAI, a runtime AI assurance product for robotics and autonomous systems. The company states its hardware and software is platform-agnostic for edge installation on uncrewed air, ground and surface vehicles (UAV, UGV and USV) operating in comms-denied and electronic warfare environments. OughtAI is described as offering real-time AI monitoring and threat detection, with the ability to manoeuvre or execute a hard stop to render an autonomous system safe. The company states its patent-granted technology supports persistent human-on-the-loop control and is designed to help autonomous systems meet UK MOD JSP 936 dependable AI guidance. Hybrid Vision states its patents are granted in the UK, US and Australia, and describes its target markets as defence, aerospace, commercial marine, automotive and mining.

## Products and solutions

- **OughtAI** - runtime AI assurance platform for autonomous systems, providing real-time monitoring, threat detection, and an independent, electronically isolated stop capability intended to be unblockable by software, for UAV, UGV and USV platforms.
