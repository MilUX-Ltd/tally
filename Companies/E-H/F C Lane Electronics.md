# F C Lane Electronics

> West Sussex specialist distributor and value-added assembler of military-grade electrical connectors and interconnect solutions.

## What they do

F C Lane Electronics is a franchised value-added distributor of military and aerospace-specification electrical connectors, established in 1966 and operating from Slinfold in West Sussex. The company holds in-house assembly and testing approval for MIL-DTL-38999 Series connectors under a QPL Category C authorisation from Eaton/Souriau, and its sister company Weald Electronics manufactures connectors to military standards on the same site. FC Lane is a BAE Systems Aircraft Business Units approved supplier (BAE/AG/20198/MAA) and holds a NATO NCAGE code (U0125). The Lodge Group umbrella covers FC Lane, Weald Electronics and Lane Motorsport.

## Products and solutions

- **Military circular connectors** - MIL-DTL-38999 Series I and III, MIL-DTL-26482, MIL-DTL-5015 and MIL-DTL-55302 connectors held in stock and available with full traceability.
- **RF and coaxial connectors and cable assemblies** - HUBER+SUHNER range for military communications and data link applications.
- **Fibre optic connectors and assemblies** - for defence and security performance-critical applications.
- **Custom connector assemblies** - in-house assembly and testing of military circular connectors to MIL-STD-790; specials design and manufacture through Weald Electronics.
- **Backshells and accessories** - circular connector backshells and accessories to AS85049.
