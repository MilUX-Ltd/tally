# Erebus UK

> Specialist covert technical training and bespoke equipment provider for law enforcement and government agencies.

## What they do

Erebus UK operates within the niche of Covert Methods of Entry (CME), Covert Search, Alarm Defeat and Restraint Escape. The company provides operationally focused covert technical training and designs bespoke specialist equipment for select law enforcement and governmental agencies worldwide. Instructors are drawn from operationally experienced government department backgrounds and hold relevant security clearances.
