# Fraunhofer UK Research Ltd

> UK affiliate of the Fraunhofer-Gesellschaft, providing applied photonics research and development to industry across security, space, sensing and quantum technologies.

## What they do

Fraunhofer UK Research Ltd is a not-for-profit company limited by guarantee, established in 2012 in partnership with the University of Strathclyde. It oversees Fraunhofer Research Centres in the UK and provides a central UK point of contact for the wider Fraunhofer global network. Its principal research activity is the Centre for Applied Photonics (Fraunhofer CAP), which performs industry-driven laser and optical systems research for sectors including security and defence, space, healthcare and renewable energy.

## Products and solutions

- **Applied photonics R&D** - industry-contracted research in laser and optical technologies, with applications in security, environmental monitoring, sensing and quantum technologies.
- **Fraunhofer CAP** - the first UK Fraunhofer Centre, based at the University of Strathclyde's Technology and Innovation Centre; focuses on enabling new or improved products and processes for industrial partners.
