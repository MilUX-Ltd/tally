# Efficio

> Procurement and supply chain consultancy serving the UK Ministry of Defence and defence supply chain organisations.

## What they do

Efficio is an independent procurement and supply chain consultancy, operating across 14 offices in Europe, the Middle East, North Africa, and North America. It works with public sector clients including the UK MOD on procurement transformation, commercial capability development, and supply chain resilience. Its public sector practice has more than 150 practitioners.

Defence-specific work includes supporting UK Defence Medical Services with sourcing and delivery of medical equipment to the Armed Forces of Ukraine, advising on MOD commercial capability gaps, and publishing a series of insights for UK Defence leaders on strategy, commercial skills, and SME engagement. Efficio has signed the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Procurement transformation** - advisory and implementation services to restructure procurement functions and improve commercial outcomes.
- **Supply chain resilience** - supply chain risk assessment, mapping, and resilience programmes for defence and government clients.
- **Spend analysis and category management** - data-led category strategies across complex spend portfolios.
- **Sustainable procurement** - net-zero and ESG integration into procurement strategy.
