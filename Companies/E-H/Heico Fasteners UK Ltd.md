# Heico Fasteners UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of the German HEICO Group, supplying engineered fastening solutions including anti-vibration wedge locking washers and precision cold-formed parts for industrial and aerospace applications.

## What they do

Heico Fasteners UK is the British arm of the HEICO Group, a family-owned fastening technology business established in 1900. The company supplies bolt securing elements, pre-tensioning systems, and precision cold-formed and hot-forged parts. Products are used across industrial, aerospace, and infrastructure applications where vibration resistance and bolt security are critical requirements.

## Products and solutions

- **HEICO-LOCK Wedge Locking Washers** - Anti-vibration bolt security washers that prevent loosening under dynamic load.
- **HEICO-TEC Tension Nuts** - Large-bolt tensioning system designed to simplify and speed up pre-tensioning operations.
- **Precision cold-formed and hot-forged parts** - Manufactured to dimensional drawings for customer-specific applications.
