# FLIR Systems

> UK subsidiary of Teledyne FLIR, designing and manufacturing thermal imaging, surveillance sensors, and uncrewed systems for defence, security, and commercial customers.

## What they do

FLIR Systems Limited is the UK-registered subsidiary of Teledyne FLIR LLC, the defence and sensing division of Teledyne Technologies Incorporated (NYSE: TDY). The UK entity was incorporated in 1977 and operates from sites in Kent and Chelmsford. FLIR supplies thermography, night vision, and surveillance equipment to military, law enforcement, and commercial customers, with a defence portfolio spanning thermal imaging for vehicles, aircraft, and dismounted soldiers, autonomous uncrewed aerial systems, and chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear, and explosives (CBRNE) detection. Teledyne completed its acquisition of the parent company, FLIR Systems Inc., in May 2021 for approximately 8.2 billion USD.

## Products and solutions

- **ThermoVision SA90-HD** - upgraded situational awareness vehicle vision system launched at Eurosatory 2026, providing HD thermal imaging for military vehicles.
- **ThermoVision DV55-HD** - HD driver vision system for military vehicles, announced alongside the SA90-HD in June 2026.
- **MilSight LIRC III** - compact vehicle-mounted weapon sight, announced at Eurosatory 2026.
- **SkyRaider and SkyRanger UAS** - tactical uncrewed aerial systems for reconnaissance and persistent surveillance.
- **SkyCarrier** - autonomous UAS launch and recovery platform for deployment from vehicles, vessels, or fixed sites, introduced at DSEI 2025.
- **Black Recon** - autonomous micro-drone system for vehicle-based reconnaissance, weighing under 450 grams; launched June 2026.
- **Handheld and airborne sensors** - thermal imaging and visible-spectrum sensor systems for soldier, maritime, and airborne applications.
- **CBRNE detectors** - chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear, and explosives detection systems.
