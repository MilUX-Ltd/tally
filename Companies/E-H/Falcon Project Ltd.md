# Falcon Project Ltd

> Developer of hybrid airship and unmanned air vehicle technology.

## What they do

The Falcon Project develops hybrid air vehicle and airship technology, combining aerostatic lift with powered flight for heavy-lift and unmanned applications. The company has collaborated with BAE Systems' FalconWorks on airship and UAV development. It is based at Westcott Venture Park in Buckinghamshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Hybrid airship / UAV** - hybrid air vehicle designs for heavy-lift and unmanned roles.
