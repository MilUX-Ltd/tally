# Grainger & Worral Ltd

> Aluminium sand casting and machining specialist serving the defence, aerospace and automotive sectors.

## What they do

Grainger & Worral produces lightweight, high-integrity aluminium sand castings for demanding applications in defence, aerospace and automotive. The company covers design consultancy, prototyping, casting, CNC machining and validation, supporting programmes from early-stage concept through to low-volume production. It operates from Bridgnorth, Shropshire, and is part of the Castings Group.

## Products and solutions

- **Aluminium sand castings** - high-integrity castings for defence and aerospace applications, including powertrain and structural components.
- **CNC machining** - precision machining of cast components.
- **Prototype to production** - end-to-end casting solutions from design through to validated production parts.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** JOSCAR registered; ISO 9001; ISO 14001; ISO/TS (IATF) 16949
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
