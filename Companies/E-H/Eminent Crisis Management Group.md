# Eminent Crisis Management Group Ltd

> Security risk management, investigations and counter-terrorism training provider, delivering to government, legal and private sector clients worldwide.

## What they do

Eminent Crisis Management Group Ltd provides investigation, security risk management, intelligence and advisory services through two operating subsidiaries: Eminent Security Ltd and Eminent Investigations Ltd. The company serves government, legal, industry and private clients internationally, with an international presence in South Africa and the United States. Its training arm delivers Protect Duty and Active Threat courses delivered by former UK Special Forces personnel.

## Products and solutions

- **Security risk management and advisory** - risk assessment and advisory services for government and corporate clients.
- **Investigations** - business intelligence and investigations services; ranked by Chambers and Partners (2022, 2023) as one of the leading UK-wide Business Intelligence and Investigations practices.
- **Counter-terrorism and active threat training** - Protect Duty and Active Threat certified training delivered by former UK Special Forces-trained instructors.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001, Cyber Essentials Plus, CPD-certified training
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Armed Forces Covenant signatory
