# Hypermotive

> UK specialist in electrical systems for electrification, hybrid and hydrogen platforms across defence, automotive, motorsport and aerospace.

## What they do

Hypermotive designs and manufactures high-performance electrical systems for battery-electric, fuel-cell and hydrogen platforms, working since 2016 across automotive, defence, motorsport and aerospace. Its products include low and high voltage wiring harnesses, power distribution units, DC-DC converters and EMC/EMI filters. The company operates a manufacturing facility in Northampton optimised for low to medium volume production. In defence it is active in land vehicle electrification and in subsea applications including uncrewed surface vehicles.

## Products and solutions

- **Wiring harnesses** - low and high voltage harnesses for electrified platforms.
- **Power Distribution Units (PDU)** - power distribution for electric and hybrid systems.
- **DC-DC converters** - voltage conversion for electrified platforms.
- **EMC/EMI filters** - electromagnetic compatibility and interference filtering.
