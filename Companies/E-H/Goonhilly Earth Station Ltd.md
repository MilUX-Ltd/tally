# Goonhilly Earth Station Ltd

> Cornwall-based space technology company providing secure satellite communications, space domain awareness and lunar and deep space communications services.

## What they do

Goonhilly Earth Station Ltd operates from its site on Goonhilly Downs in Cornwall, with additional facilities in the USA. The company provides services across three divisions: Commercial Satcom, Defence and Security, and Lunar and Deep Space Communications. It offers secure and reliable satellite communications, telemetry tracking and command, carrier monitoring, and space domain awareness services. Defence and security customers include Dstl, with whom the company has delivered antenna projects.

## Products and solutions

- **Commercial Satcom** - satellite communications services including uplink and broadcast, satellite internet, gateway operations, LEO services and carrier monitoring.
- **Defence and Security** - secure communications and space domain awareness solutions for government and defence customers.
- **Lunar and Deep Space** - ground station services supporting lunar and deep space missions, including large X/S-Band antenna capability.
- **System integration** - mobile ground stations and antenna integration.
- **Space consultancy** - advisory work and project support for space programmes.
