# ePropelled

> Electric motors, hybrid starter-generators, and power management systems for defence uncrewed aerial, ground, and maritime platforms.

## What they do

ePropelled designs and manufactures high-performance electric motors, hybrid starter-generators, electronic speed controllers, and intelligent power management systems for uncrewed platforms in defence and allied applications. The company holds 36 patents in advanced magnetics, electric drive, and energy management. The UK entity, originally ElectronicaUK Ltd (incorporated 1998 and acquired by ePropelled in 2018), operates from a Global Innovation Centre in Coventry opened in March 2026, targeting production of one million propulsion systems annually by 2027. The US parent is headquartered in Laconia, New Hampshire, with additional R&D and operations in Chennai, India.

## Products and solutions

- **Sparrow Series** - Brushless motors from micro-class (APM 1404) to 7,000W for small tactical UAVs, loitering munitions, and ISR drones.
- **Falcon Series** - Higher-power motors up to 10 kW (iAPM600) for larger uncrewed aerial platforms.
- **Hercules Series** - Hybrid starter-generators up to 14 kW for hybrid-electric propulsion architectures; supports mission ranges over 1,000 km and heavy-fuel (jet-fuel-compatible) configurations for military UAV applications.
- **ePConnect** - Onboard service manager and telemetry platform for real-time monitoring, control, and analytics on uncrewed platforms.
