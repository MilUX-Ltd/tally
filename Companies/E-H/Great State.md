# Great State

> Bristol digital product and service design agency working with UK government and defence clients.

## What they do

Great State designs and builds digital products, platforms and services for public sector organisations including the Ministry of Defence and the Royal Navy. It applies user research, product design and engineering to deliver digital transformation in line with Government Digital Service principles. Its engineering team holds Security Check clearance and works to UK Cabinet Office security compliance requirements.

## Products and solutions

- **MyNavy** - digital platform for Royal Navy personnel, delivered in partnership with the programme team; described by the product owner as a "phenomenal product built in ten weeks".
- **British Army digital** - digital service design and development for army.mod.uk.
- **Government digital services** - research, strategy, product design and engineering for public sector organisations requiring scalable, user-focused systems.
