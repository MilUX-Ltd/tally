# Futronics Limited

> Designer and manufacturer of vehicle-based command and control, mobile data, communications and ANPR enforcement systems for policing and law enforcement.

## What they do

Futronics designs and manufactures vehicle-mounted command and control systems, mobile data terminals, ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition) enforcement systems, optical fingerprint scanners and video monitoring systems. The company serves police forces worldwide and has been a technology partner to the UK Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA) for its Home Office approved ANPR enforcement capability. Products are deployed across emergency services, transport and public safety organisations.

## Products and solutions

- **eTS Touchscreen** - vehicle-mounted touchscreen command and control terminal for operational policing.
- **F-Box** - integrated mobile data and communications platform for emergency vehicles.
- **eMC Range** - modular communications systems for vehicle deployment.
- **DVLA Stingray II** - Home Office approved ANPR enforcement system, deployed for DVLA vehicle excise duty enforcement.
- **eFP / eFR** - optical fingerprint scanner and fingerprint reader units for law enforcement.
