# Galaxkey Limited

> Identity-based data protection platform providing encryption for email, files, messaging, and collaboration, with a specific offering for the defence and Official-Sensitive supply chain.

## What they do

Galaxkey provides a post-quantum ready data protection platform built on a three-layer, identity-based encryption architecture. Organisations retain full control of their own encryption keys. The platform is listed by the NCSC and has a dedicated product configuration for the UK defence and Official-Sensitive supply chain, protecting data that must be shared across contractors and subcontractors beyond secure network boundaries. Clients include KNDS UK (formerly WFEL), the Ministry of Justice, Bradford City Council, UCL, and international financial institutions.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure Emails** - end-to-end encryption for business communications, preventing unauthorised forwarding or sharing.
- **Secure Workspace** - controlled environment for secure collaboration and large file transfer between organisations including defence subcontractors.
- **Secure Files and Digital Rights Management** - persistent protection applied to Official-Sensitive documents regardless of location or recipient system.
- **LockChat** - enterprise secure messaging with identity-based encryption, positioned as a replacement for consumer apps such as WhatsApp in regulated environments.
- **Secure Signatures** - digitally signed contractual and compliance documentation.
- **Secure Shield** - centralised data protection dashboard with e-discovery capability.
