# Force Development
> South Coast engineering and manufacturing SME specialising in bespoke mobile infrastructure, ballistic protection and vehicle modifications for defence and security.
## What they do
Force Development Services designs and manufactures bespoke engineering solutions for defence and security customers from its Fareham base. The product range covers mobile infrastructure, PODS modularity systems, vehicle conversions, ballistic protection of people and assets, vehicle borne intervention equipment, ladder design and manufacture, and specialist textiles. The company positions itself on delivering innovative solutions at pace and participates in the South Central Regional Defence and Security Cluster.
