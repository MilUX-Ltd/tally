# Hawk Information Services Ltd

> Open-source intelligence and strategic consulting specialist serving the aerospace, defence, and security sectors.

## What they do

Hawk Information is a UK-registered OSINT and strategy firm providing intelligence reports, research, analysis, and strategic consulting to the international aerospace, defence, and security industries. It employs an extensive network of associates and subject-matter experts to collect and validate open-source material, and delivers intelligence-led insights and strategic advice to both public and private sector clients.

## Products and solutions

- **NightHawk** - a hyper-personalised aerospace and defence intelligence reporting service using an ROI framework to align output to specific departmental intelligence requirements, drawing on OSINT methodology and structured analytical techniques.
- **Custom research and analysis** - bespoke intelligence and analysis services covering emerging business opportunities, technology foresight, and market developments in aerospace, defence, and security.
- **Strategic intelligence advisory** - country-specific business support, geopolitical analysis, and strategic advice for organisations navigating international markets.
- **Proprietary data services** - access to leading aerospace and defence market intelligence providers and programme forecasts via long-term data partnerships.
- **NightHawk Advantage Consortium** - a collaborative network offering access to international associates, funding and procurement experts, and research project coordinators.
