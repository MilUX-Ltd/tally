# Eagle Ray Robotics Ltd

> Edinburgh startup building cooperative autonomy and distributed sensing for uncrewed subsea systems.

## What they do

Eagle Ray Robotics Ltd is an Edinburgh-based startup, incorporated in 2023, developing technologies that let autonomous systems operate more effectively in the subsea domain. Its work focuses on distributed sensing, cooperative autonomy and intelligent underwater systems supporting defence, offshore energy and environmental monitoring. The company develops a distributed autonomy platform that enables underwater vehicles to operate as a coordinated sensing network, sharing updates while submerged.

## Products and solutions

- **ConstelX (Constellation OS)** - a fleet intelligence operating system that connects heterogeneous autonomous underwater vehicles into cooperative subsea sensing networks, integrating with onboard vehicle software so operators can add cooperative sensing to existing platforms.
