# High Tor Capital Ltd

> Venture fund investing in foundational dual-use technologies with national security and strategic advantage applications.

## What they do

High Tor Capital is a venture capital fund focused on foundational, dual-use technologies that strengthen national security and provide strategic advantage. Its investment thesis covers novel materials, advanced manufacturing and other deep-technology areas with far-reaching defence and civilian application. The fund takes a patient capital approach with active portfolio involvement. It is an appointed representative of Thornbridge Investment Management LLP, which is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority.
