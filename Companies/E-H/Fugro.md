# Fugro GB Limited

> Geo-data solutions company providing subsea survey, maritime domain awareness and critical underwater infrastructure monitoring services to government and defence clients.

## What they do

Fugro is a Dutch-listed global geo-data company with a long-established UK entity (Fugro GB Limited, Wallingford, Oxfordshire, incorporated 1973). The UK operation delivers geophysical, hydrographic and geotechnical survey services and supports subsea domain awareness for national security and critical infrastructure protection. Fugro deploys a fleet of uncrewed surface vessels, subsea robotics and remote operations centres to deliver persistent maritime surveillance and data collection.

The parent company, Fugro NV, is headquartered in Leidschendam, Netherlands, and listed on Euronext Amsterdam. Fugro GB Limited is the primary UK legal entity and ADS member; separate UK entities include Fugro GB (North) Marine Limited (Edinburgh) and Fugro Subsea Services Ltd.

## Products and solutions

- **Uncrewed Surface Vessels (USVs)** - persistent, low-risk maritime surveillance and geophysical data acquisition in complex operational zones.
- **Subsea Robotics and Remote Operations** - inspection, repair and maintenance of critical underwater infrastructure including cables and pipelines.
- **Hydrographic and Geophysical Survey** - data acquisition supporting maritime situational awareness and infrastructure resilience for government and defence clients.
- **Subsea Domain Awareness Platform** - data fusion platform (developed with partners DTACT and Ubotica) combining geo-data, satellite intelligence and other data streams for detection of threats to subsea infrastructure.
