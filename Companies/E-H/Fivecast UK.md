# Fivecast UK

> AI-powered open-source intelligence platform for national security, defence, and law enforcement missions.

## What they do

Fivecast is an Australian-founded digital intelligence company that develops AI-enabled OSINT software for government and enterprise customers across national security, defence, law enforcement, border security, and financial crime prevention. The company was founded in 2017 from collaboration between Australian government agencies and research institutions, and has expanded into the United Kingdom and United States. Fivecast UK Ltd is the UK legal entity (Companies House 13861067), serving UK and European customers from a London-based team as part of the company's Five Eyes market expansion.

## Products and solutions

- **Fivecast ONYX** - OSINT collection and analysis platform for broad discovery, targeted data collection, and near real-time insights across millions of online sources.
- **Fivecast LUNEX** - analyst-curated dataset product that surfaces hidden threat networks and delivers rapid intelligence, integrated into the ONYX platform.
- **Fivecast MATRIX** - large-scale risk assessment solution for security vetting and screening, developed in conjunction with the US Government.
- **Fivecast Discovery** - automated digital footprinting, identity resolution, and information verification capability integrated within ONYX.
