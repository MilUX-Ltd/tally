# Eagle Eye Innovations

> UK-based RPAS training, operations and consultancy company serving commercial and defence customers globally.

## What they do

Eagle Eye Innovations (EEI) specialises in Remotely Piloted Aircraft Systems (RPAS) training, turn-key operational services, security services, independent consultancy, and research and development for commercial and defence customers. Founded in 2013, EEI was the UK's longest-running CAA-approved drone training provider and the first organisation accredited to deliver the CAA Remote Pilot Certificate Level 1. Its team draws heavily on former military aviation personnel.

## Products and solutions

- **EEI Academy** - CAA-accredited RPAS training programme covering regulatory compliance, airspace, and practical operations across all UAS weight classes.
- **Defence and Security Services** - mission-ready training, operational support, and tailored drone solutions for military and government security customers.
- **Turn-key RPAS Operations** - end-to-end drone services including survey, inspection, and persistent security operations for civil and defence clients.
