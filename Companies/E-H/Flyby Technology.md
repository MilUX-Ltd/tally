# Flyby Technology

> York-based developer and manufacturer of armed VTOL UAVs for military and government customers, alongside professional drone training.

## What they do

Flyby Technology designs and manufactures vertical take-off and landing (VTOL) unmanned air systems for military, government and commercial customers. The company's flagship platform is the JACKAL, an all-electric VTOL UAV capable of carrying and firing air-to-surface missiles. Flyby also operates as a Civil Aviation Authority-authorised Recognised Assessment Entity, providing professional beyond-visual-line-of-sight (BVLOS) drone pilot training delivered by former RAF pilots.

## Products and solutions

- **JACKAL** - all-electric VTOL UAV with approximately 80 nautical miles range and up to 65 kg payload capacity; demonstrated firing Thales Lightweight Multirole Missiles (LMMs) from a hover. Flyby holds full UK manufacturing and export rights to the design.
- **Havoc** - larger planned variant with hybrid propulsion for extended range and heavier weapon carriage.
- **Military drone training** - BVLOS pilot training programme written and delivered by ex-RAF qualified weapons instructors, aimed at military and government operators.
- **Military consultancy and design** - bespoke UAV design and capability development for defence customers.
