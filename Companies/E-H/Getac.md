# Getac

> Taiwanese manufacturer of rugged laptops, tablets and mobile computing for defence and field use.

## What they do

Getac is a Taiwanese manufacturer of rugged computing devices, including fully rugged and semi-rugged laptops and tablets, software and mobile video solutions built to military-grade durability standards. The company was established in 1989 as a joint venture with GE Aerospace to provide defence electronics and now serves defence, public safety, utilities, automotive, manufacturing and logistics customers in over 100 countries. Getac Technology Corporation is a subsidiary of Getac Holdings Corporation, listed on the Taiwan Stock Exchange, and is headquartered in Taipei.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged laptops and tablets** - fully and semi-rugged devices certified to MIL-STD-810 and IP ingress standards for field use.
- **5G rugged devices** - rugged hardware for defence, first response and resources frontline operations.
- **Mobile video and software solutions** - field data, video and device-management software for operational deployment.
