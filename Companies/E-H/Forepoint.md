# Forepoint Limited

> Creative consultancy specialising in brand, digital and motion communications for defence, nuclear, energy and other critical industries.

## What they do

Forepoint is a creative consultancy with over 30 years of experience working with critical industries including defence, nuclear, energy, and safety sectors. The company delivers strategic communications programmes covering brand strategy and development, digital and web, and motion and video production. It works with clients at times of significant change, including major transformation programmes, cultural change initiatives, supply chain communications, and public and stakeholder engagement. Forepoint holds Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus certification.

Defence clients include BAE Systems, with whom Forepoint has worked since 2006 as one of eight agencies on BAE Systems' design roster, supporting air, land and sea divisions. The company also works with the Dreadnought Alliance, AWE, and the Nuclear Decommissioning Authority.

## Products and solutions

- **Brand** - brand strategy, brand development, communications, design services and exhibitions for defence and critical industry clients.
- **Digital** - digital strategy, website development, hosting and maintenance, app development and social media.
- **Motion** - video production, 2D and 3D animation, scripting and immersive content for mission-critical communications.
