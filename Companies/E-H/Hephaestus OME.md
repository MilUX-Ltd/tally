# Hephaestus OME

> Specialist consultancy providing expert technical support for the safe management of Ordnance, Munitions and Explosives in MOD and defence contracting.

## What they do

Hephaestus OME provides subject matter expert (SME) support to the MOD and its contractors on the safe management of Ordnance, Munitions and Explosives (OME) across the full lifecycle from manufacture to disposal. The company specialises in Munitions Technical Publications, Technical Data Packs, explosive safety cases, and report authoring. It also provides support at the tender stage and throughout delivery of Defence OME contracts, including interpreting and applying the relevant Defence Standards.

## Products and solutions

- **OME Safety Consulting** - Impartial safety management advice and support to Defence contracting activities involving munitions, explosives and weapon systems.
- **Munitions Technical Publications** - Authoring and review of technical publications for MOD and defence contractors.
- **Technical Data Packs** - Preparation and review of TDPs to Defence Standards requirements.
- **Explosive Safety Cases** - Authoring and review of safety cases for munitions and explosives programmes.
- **Defence Contracting Support** - Expert assistance from tender stage through contract delivery on OME-specific requirements hidden within Defence Standards.
