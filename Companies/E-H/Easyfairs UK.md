# Easyfairs UK

> UK operating entity of the Easyfairs Group, a pan-European events company that organises Advanced Engineering, the UK's leading aerospace, defence and engineering supply-chain exhibition.

## What they do

Easyfairs UK Limited is the British subsidiary of the Brussels-headquartered Easyfairs Group, which runs over 110 market-leading events across 12 countries. In the UK, the principal defence-relevant event is Advanced Engineering, held annually at the NEC Birmingham, which brings together OEMs and engineering supply-chain professionals across aerospace, defence, space, composites, automotive and adjacent sectors. ADS Group is a headline partner for the defence and security strand. For 2026, Make UK and Make UK Defence are launching a joint pavilion at the event.

## Products and solutions

- **Advanced Engineering** - UK's principal gathering of engineering OEMs and supply-chain companies, with a dedicated defence and security strand and an established partnership with ADS Group.
