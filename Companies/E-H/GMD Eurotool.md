# GMD Eurotool Ltd

> Precision engineering and turnkey manufacturing company serving the UK Ministry of Defence, part of the Leviathan Engineering Group, with a track record in warship refit, submarine components and land vehicle parts.

## What they do

GMD Eurotool provides precision engineering services from its Plymouth facility, specialising in turnkey project delivery, CNC and manual machining, welding, fabrication and design-for-manufacture consultancy. The company is a preferred supplier to Babcock International Group under the Leviathan Engineering Group parent framework, and has a history of manufacturing components for warships, submarines, infantry fighting vehicles and supporting major dockyard infrastructure. Defence work includes build-to-print, emergency turnaround and specialist procurement.

## Products and solutions

- **Turnkey defence engineering projects** - end-to-end design, manufacture and delivery of components and assemblies for naval and land platforms.
- **Warship and submarine components** - precision-machined and fabricated parts for refit and maintenance packages.
- **Land vehicle components** - infantry fighting vehicle and support vehicle parts.
- **OEM mechanical spares and reverse engineering** - reproduction of legacy components where original suppliers are unavailable.
- **Special purpose machinery** - custom machinery design and build for defence and industrial customers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, ISO 3834 (welding)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Make UK Defence
