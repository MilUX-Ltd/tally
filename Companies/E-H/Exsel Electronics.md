# Exsel Electronics

> Software-centric systems integrator focused on data-centric security and multi-domain information management for defence and government.

## What they do

Exsel Electronics is a division of the Exsel Group, operating as a standalone legal entity based in Welshpool, Powys. It is a software-centric systems integrator with a specialised focus on information and cyber security, working closely with governments and defence organisations to pioneer interoperable information security systems. Its core domain covers multi-domain communications, data, and system management, with an emphasis on ensuring users have continuous security and accessibility of information across all domains. The approach aligns with the UK National Cyber Security Strategy.

## Products and solutions

- **Need2Know** - a document-level data protection product that safeguards secret, sensitive, or personal information contained within documents before they are shared, providing a single source of truth for information control.
- **Data Centric Security** - a unified security model for governments and organisations that protects data and ensures business continuity across hybrid work environments, cloud computing, and complex regulatory frameworks.
