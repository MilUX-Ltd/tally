# EDM Limited

> Manchester manufacturer of high-performance training simulators and emergency-training equipment for the civil aviation, defence and rail sectors.

## What they do

EDM designs and manufactures training simulators and emergency-training equipment, describing itself as the world's leading supplier of training simulators to the civil aviation and defence markets. Product lines span full-scale cabin crew trainers, maintenance rigs, fire and smoke devices, and defence procedure and maintenance trainers. It is a JOSCAR-registered first-tier supplier and a UK, USA and NATO defence-approved supplier.

## Products and solutions

- **Cabin Emergency Evacuation Trainers (CEETs)** - fuselage-based trainers for cabin crew SOP, SEP and CRM drills, including smoke, fire and evacuation scenarios, in static or motion-based configurations.
- **Door trainers, cabin service trainers and full-size mockups** - civil aviation cabin and crew training devices.
- **Defence procedure trainers, maintenance trainers, ejection seats, simulators and full-size replicas** - synthetic training and maintenance simulators plus ground support equipment.
- **Virtual Slide Trainer** - a recently launched training product.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, AS9100D, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR registered, RISQS verified (rail). Approved supplier to UK, USA and NATO defence, and to Airbus, Boeing, BAE Systems, Rolls-Royce, Martin Baker and Lockheed Martin.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Make UK, North West Aerospace Alliance, BSI, National Training and Simulation Association.
