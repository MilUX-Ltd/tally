# Hellios Information Limited

> Hellios manages the JOSCAR supplier qualification register for the UK Aerospace, Defence and Security sector.

## What they do

Hellios collects, validates and distributes supplier data on behalf of buying organisations in the Aerospace, Defence and Security community. Its primary product is JOSCAR (Joint Supply Chain Accreditation Register), which allows suppliers to submit a single registration to provide pre-qualified data to over 29 buying members, including the Ministry of Defence. Hellios uses that data to help buying organisations identify potential supply chain risks and make informed procurement decisions.

## Products and solutions

- **JOSCAR** - The Aerospace, Defence and Security industry's shared supplier qualification register. Over 5,000 published suppliers provide validated compliance and capability data through a single submission, used by MOD and major primes.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR (operator)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
