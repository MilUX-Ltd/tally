# Griffon Marine

> British designer and manufacturer of military, rescue and commercial hovercraft and marine craft.

## What they do

Griffon designs, builds and supports hovercraft with payloads from around 380kg up to about 150 tonnes, for military, coastguard, rescue and commercial operators. It states over 180 craft operating in more than 40 countries, with customers including the UK Royal Marines, the Canadian Coast Guard and the Colombian Navy. Having rebranded from Griffon Hoverwork to Griffon Marine in 2025, it has broadened from hovercraft into wider marine structures and high-speed boats while keeping hovercraft as its core. It is owned by the Bland Group and holds ISO 9001, and runs a hovercraft type-rating training course accredited by the UK Chamber of Shipping.

## Products and solutions

- **Hovercraft range** - small to large amphibious craft (payloads ~380kg to ~150 tonnes) for military, rescue and commercial roles.
- **Wyvern LCAC** - a Landing Craft Air Cushion class for amphibious military transport, granted Approval in Principle by Lloyd's Register.
- **Marine solutions** - lightweight marine structures and high-speed boats.
- **Support and training** - spares, in-service support, and a Chamber of Shipping accredited type-rating course.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001 (via LRQA)
- **Other:** Armed Forces Covenant signatory; UK Chamber of Shipping accredited training course
