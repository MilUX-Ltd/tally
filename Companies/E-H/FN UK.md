# FN UK

> The UK's only onshore manufacturer of military rifles, machine guns and weapon mounts, supplying the UK Ministry of Defence and law enforcement agencies.

## What they do

FNH UK Limited (trading as FN UK) is the UK-based subsidiary of FN Herstal, part of the FN Browning Group, owned by the Wallonia regional government of Belgium. Operating from a facility in Erith, Kent, FN UK manufactures small-calibre weapons and weapon mounts under licence for the UK MoD and UK law enforcement customers. The company was formerly Manroy Engineering, acquired by FN Herstal in 2017. FN UK holds an in-house design and engineering team and CNC, paint, chrome finishing, and phosphating capabilities.

## Products and solutions

- **.50 M2/HMG** - heavy machine gun manufactured for UK Armed Forces.
- **GPMG/MAG 58** - general-purpose machine gun manufactured for UK Armed Forces.
- **5.56mm rifles** - military assault rifles manufactured in the UK.
- **Weapon mounts** - vehicle and platform weapon mounts designed and manufactured to UK MoD requirements.
