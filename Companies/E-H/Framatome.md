# Framatome Ltd

> UK subsidiary of the French nuclear technology group Framatome, providing engineering, component manufacturing, instrumentation and control, and fuel services to the British nuclear, chemical, process, and defence industries.

## What they do

Framatome Limited is the UK entity of Framatome, an international nuclear technology company majority-owned by EDF. The UK business has operated in the British nuclear industry for over 25 years and employs more than 350 people across sites in Avonmouth (Bristol), Cranfield (Bedfordshire), Hinkley Point C, and Sizewell B. The company provides engineering services, computational software code development, project management, site and outage support, instrumentation and control systems, and component manufacturing. Framatome explicitly lists the British Defence industry among its UK customer sectors. The parent group operates in space and healthcare alongside its core nuclear business.

## Products and solutions

- **Nuclear engineering and services** - Design and service engineering, site outage support, and long-term operations support for existing UK nuclear power plants.
- **Instrumentation and control systems** - I&C solutions for nuclear plant safety and operations.
- **Component manufacturing** - High-quality components for nuclear steam supply systems, including post-weld heat treatment services.
- **Computational software code development** - Software tools for nuclear engineering analysis.
- **Nuclear fuel services** - Fuel design and manufacturing capability; Framatome has announced plans to establish a nuclear fuel fabrication facility in the UK.
