# Eficode UK

> DevSecOps and software lifecycle consultancy with a dedicated defence and aerospace service line and security-cleared staff.

## What they do

Eficode is a European DevOps and software lifecycle consultancy, headquartered in Helsinki with operations across ten countries. The UK entity, headquartered in Southampton and originally trading as Clearvision, was acquired by Eficode in 2022. Eficode provides Atlassian tooling (Jira, Confluence), GitLab, GitHub, DevSecOps, scaled Agile, managed DevOps platforms (Eficode ROOT), ITSM, and AI-assisted software development.

The company has a dedicated "Agile and DevOps in Defence and Aerospace" service line, employs security-cleared staff, and has experience working in air-gapped environments. It is a member of Team Defence Information, the MOD-industry-academia collaboration body, and holds JOSCAR registration alongside ISO 27001 and Cyber Essentials.

## Products and solutions

- **Eficode ROOT** - a managed DevOps toolchain platform; available on G-Cloud.
- **Atlassian services** - implementation, migration, and support for Jira, Confluence, and the Atlassian product suite.
- **GitLab and GitHub services** - advisory, implementation, and managed services; Eficode is an Atlassian Platinum and GitHub partner.
- **DevSecOps for defence and aerospace** - secure software pipeline design, implementation, and operation in regulated and classified environments.
- **Agile and scaled Agile** - coaching, tooling, and transformation for Agile adoption on complex defence programmes.
