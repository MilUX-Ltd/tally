# High Technology Sources Ltd

> Radiological safety, detection and non-destructive testing specialist serving CBRN, security and defence organisations.

## What they do

HTSL Group supplies radioactive isotopes, radiation detection systems, and non-destructive testing equipment across the UK. The company provides end-to-end lifecycle management for ionising radiation, including supply, installation, calibration, maintenance and repair. Its CBRN and security division supports defence, policing and critical infrastructure organisations with tailored radiological compliance and detection services.

## Products and solutions

- **Radiation detection systems** - supply, installation and lifecycle management of equipment for nuclear, industrial, medical and security applications.
- **Accredited calibration services** - calibration and maintenance of radiological instruments to regulatory standards.
- **NDT products** - non-destructive testing equipment supply, including sourcing of radioactive isotopes for industrial NDT.
- **Radiological managed service** - end-to-end managed service covering compliance, safety and regulatory reporting for organisations where ionising radiation is present.
