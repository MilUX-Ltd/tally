# HEO Robotics (UK) LTD

> HEO delivers non-Earth imaging and Space Domain Awareness data using a network of spaceborne sensors.

## What they do

HEO (High Earth Orbit Robotics) provides on-demand, high-frequency imagery of satellites and other resident space objects using a distributed network of spaceborne sensors. Its non-Earth imaging (NEI) data supports Space Domain Awareness (SDA) for defence and intelligence customers, satellite operators, and civil governments. The UK entity is registered in London; the parent company (High Earth Orbit Robotics Pty Ltd) is headquartered in Sydney, Australia.

## Products and solutions

- **HEO Inspect 3.0** - Software platform providing access to non-Earth imaging data and derived insights on space objects.
- **Non-Earth Imaging Cameras** - Hosted payload cameras enabling spacecraft to collect NEI data as part of their mission.
- **Tessera** - A product aimed at giving government and defence customers a sovereign non-Earth imaging capability.
