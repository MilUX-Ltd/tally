# Elendil Alliance Ltd

> UK defence venture builder creating sovereign joint ventures between UK interests and Ukrainian defence technology companies, focused on UAVs, electronic warfare, C-UAS, and autonomy.

## What they do

Elendil Alliance structures and builds UK-incorporated joint ventures with Ukrainian defence technology innovators. The stated model is to identify Ukrainian companies with mature capabilities in unmanned systems, electronic warfare, and autonomy, and commercialise those capabilities for the UK and NATO markets through sovereign joint ventures. The company was incorporated in November 2025 and has participated in UK-Ukraine defence technology forums including the 5th UK-Ukraine Defence Tech Forum at RUSI.

## Focus areas

- UAVs and uncrewed aerial systems
- Electronic warfare and SIGINT
- Counter-UAS
- Unmanned ground vehicles
- Autonomy
