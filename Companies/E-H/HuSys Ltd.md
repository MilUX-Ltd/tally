# HuSys Ltd

> Defence management consultancy providing programme management, systems analysis, and bid support for complex, high-tempo operations.

## What they do

Founded in 2013, HuSys helps clients understand issues around complex, high-tempo operations using evidence-based recommendations to derive pragmatic solutions. Services span bid support, social value, programme and project management (P3M/PMO), concept development (CONOPS, options analysis, OA, URD), and systems analysis, delivered through a network of associates and ex-IPT leaders.
