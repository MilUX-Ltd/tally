# HuSys Ltd

> Defence and human factors consultancy providing programme management, systems analysis, and bid support for complex, high-tempo operations.

## What they do

Founded in 2013, HuSys helps clients understand issues around complex, high-tempo operations using evidence-based recommendations to derive pragmatic solutions. Services span human factors integration (control room and workplace design, usability, manpower planning), training needs analysis, programme and project management (P3M/PMO), bid support, concept development (CONOPS, options analysis, OA, URD), and systems engineering, requirements and design assurance, delivered across the full acquisition lifecycle through a network of associates and ex-IPT leaders. The company states its work spans defence, transport, security and nuclear sectors.
