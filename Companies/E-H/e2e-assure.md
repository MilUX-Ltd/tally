# e2e-assure

> UK-owned managed threat detection and response provider, specialising in 24/7 SOC-as-a-service for defence, government, and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

e2e-assure delivers Security Operations Centre services to defence, government, and critical infrastructure organisations, using exclusively SC-cleared UK-based analysts. The company operates its proprietary CUMULO platform, which connects IT and OT monitoring, AI-driven threat detection, and real-time response under a single sovereign capability. It has over a decade of experience securing defence and government environments and holds JOSCAR registration, Cyber Essentials Plus, and G-Cloud 14 accreditation. BGF backed the company with a Series B investment in February 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **CUMULO** - Sovereign AI-driven SOC platform for IT and OT environments, supporting zero-day threat detection through behaviour-based rules and live threat intelligence, with Standard and Enterprise tiers.
- **Managed Threat Detection and Response (MDR)** - 24/7 SOC-as-a-service with SC-cleared UK analysts, covering endpoint, cloud, identity, and OT telemetry.
- **OT Security Monitoring** - Passive network monitoring for SCADA, ICS, and industrial control systems, delivering compliance support for NIS2, CAF, and IEC 62443.
- **Vulnerability Scanning and Monitoring** - Continuous vulnerability management integrated with the SOC service.
