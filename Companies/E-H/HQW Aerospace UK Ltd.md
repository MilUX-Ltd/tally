# HQW Aerospace (UK) Ltd

> Precision bearing manufacturer supplying super-precision ball bearings for aerospace and defence applications under the Barden brand.

## What they do

HQW Aerospace (UK) Ltd, trading as Barden Bearings, designs and manufactures super-precision ball bearings for critical aerospace and defence applications, including APU generators, primary actuator motors, cabin air systems, and military gyroscopes. The company is the UK entity of the HQW Precision group and operates from its Plymouth facility serving global aerospace and defence customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Barden super-precision ball bearings** - High-tolerance bearings for aerospace actuators, gyroscopes, APU generators, and other safety-critical rotating assemblies.
