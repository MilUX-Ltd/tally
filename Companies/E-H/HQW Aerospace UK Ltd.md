# HQW Aerospace (UK) Ltd

> Precision bearing manufacturer supplying super-precision ball bearings for aerospace and defence applications under the Barden brand.

## What they do

HQW Aerospace (UK) Ltd, trading as Barden Bearings, designs and manufactures super-precision ball bearings for critical aerospace and defence applications, including APU generators, primary actuator motors, cabin air systems, and military gyroscopes. The Plymouth facility previously operated under Schaeffler ownership before being sold to HQW Precision GmbH in 2019, a transaction that secured the site and its manufacturing jobs. The company is the UK entity of the German HQW Precision group and continues to operate from its Plymouth facility serving global aerospace and defence customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Barden super-precision ball bearings** - High-tolerance bearings for aerospace actuators, gyroscopes, APU generators, and other safety-critical rotating assemblies.
