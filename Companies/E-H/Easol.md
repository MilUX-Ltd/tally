# Easol

> Innovation-as-a-service partner helping defence and government organisations turn early-stage ideas into market-ready products and services.

## What they do

Easol is a Gosport-based innovation consultancy working across a spectrum of government and private-sector projects. The company uses a proprietary methodology called HeadStarts to move clients from strategy and early-stage concepts through to functional, deployable outcomes. It has worked as a supplier to the Accelerated Capability Environment (ACE) on multiple commissions, including market analysis and technology evaluation work for policing applications. Approximately half its project mix is government and defence.

## Products and solutions

- **HeadStarts methodology** - structured process for moving innovation concepts through feasibility, market analysis and deployment planning.
- **Bullfrog** - a breakout product in development: automated composite non-destructive testing (NDT) inspection and analysis using thermography and AI, targeting aerospace and defence manufacturing applications.
