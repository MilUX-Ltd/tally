# Harquebus

> Intelligence and security risk management consultancy serving governments, multinationals, NGOs, and private clients.

## What they do

Harquebus draws on a team of ex-military and civilian intelligence officers with backgrounds in counter-IED, counter-terrorism, counter-insurgency, and counter-intelligence. The firm provides intelligence and security services to governments, multinationals, NGOs, and family offices, operating globally. Its focus is on enabling clients to make informed decisions in complex environments, from routine operations through to crisis.

## Products and solutions

- **Integrated Crisis Management** - combining operational crisis management expertise with strategic communications to prepare clients for and support them through crises.
- **Security Risk and Resilience Management** - threat assessment, due diligence, dispute resolution, investigations, insider threat mitigation, and M&A target assessments.
- **Training and Capacity Enhancement** - restructuring and skills development for UK and allied government security organisations (military, civil, and police) to address existing and emerging threats.
- **Counter-Terrorism and Intelligence Services** - specialist advisory and training services across the full counter-terrorism and intelligence spectrum.
- **Surveillance Services** - operational surveillance support.
- **Transport Security Services** - transport security advisory.
