# Hunter Simulation Limited

> Specialist consultancy providing technical and engineering services for civil and military flight simulation programmes.

## What they do

Hunter Simulation delivers project engineering management, requirements definition, systems design, software and hardware design, systems integration, and qualification support for flight simulation programmes. The company also offers proprietary modelling for hose-and-drogue air-to-air refuelling simulation, including high-fidelity hose and drogue dynamics.

## Products and solutions

- **Simulated systems development** - technical engineering support to develop new simulated systems where a customer lacks in-house skills or headcount.
- **Hunter Hose & Drogue Model** - proprietary model realistically simulating hose and drogue behaviour in real time for air-to-air refuelling simulation.
- **Pilot simulator services** - current TRE and TRI-rated pilots for simulator acceptance and testing support.
