# FEL Avionics

> Specialist stockist, repairer and support provider for legacy defence radar systems, holding over 16,300 registered NATO/NSN part numbers.

## What they do

FEL Avionics is a subsidiary of UKC Group, operating as a stockist and distributor of discontinued piece parts and line-replaceable units for legacy defence radar systems. The company provides repair and overhaul services, onsite fault diagnosis, technician training, and a travelling wave tube test facility. Where components are globally unavailable, the team reverse-engineers and manufactures small batches of critical parts.

## Products and solutions

- **Legacy radar spares** - stock supply of discontinued components and LRUs for BAE/Plessey Radar Systems (S511, Martello, Watchman, AR-51, AR325, AR327), naval radar (994, 996), ground radar (AR3D), and US Army radars AN/TPS-70 and AN/TPS-43; also GEC Marconi, Racal Decca, and Marine Avionics lines.
- **Repair and overhaul** - fault-finding, repair, and full overhaul by technicians trained at Plessey, BAE, Marconi, and AMS.
- **TWT test facility** - Travelling Wave Tube testing capability for radar sub-assembly verification.
- **Reverse engineering and batch manufacture** - small-batch manufacture of globally unavailable critical components.
- **Lease and lend programme** - partnership plan for specialised sub-assemblies to support continuity of operations.
- **Technician training** - courses at all levels for covered radar systems.
