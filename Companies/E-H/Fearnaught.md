# Fearnaught

> Tri-service defence consultancy of retired senior military officers advising defence companies on UK MOD engagement and procurement.

## What they do

Fearnaught is a Hampshire-based consultancy comprising retired senior British military officers drawn from across the three services. It supports defence companies seeking to develop and market their capabilities to the UK Ministry of Defence, providing insight into MOD procurement processes, acquisition pathways and customer relationships. The company is registered with ADS Group under its Defence Consultancy Services capability category and is based in Odiham, Hampshire, close to RAF Odiham.
