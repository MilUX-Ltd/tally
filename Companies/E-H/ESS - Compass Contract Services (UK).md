# ESS - Compass Contract Services (UK)

> Defence and government soft facilities management provider, delivering catering, cleaning and support services to over 250 UK military establishments.

## What they do

ESS is the defence, government and offshore division of Compass Group UK and Ireland. It provides catering, cleaning and a broad range of soft facilities management services to the UK Armed Forces, government agencies, and critical national infrastructure sites. ESS has served the UK defence estate for over 40 years and supports Army, Royal Navy, Royal Marines, RAF and Strategic Command establishments, as well as the Defence Training Estate and a number of private finance initiative sites.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence catering** - food service operations across more than 250 military establishments, delivering over 16.5 million meals annually including range packed meals and operational ration support.
- **Soft FM services** - cleaning, hotel services, waste management, laundry, tailoring, stores and warehousing, grounds maintenance and helicopter landing support.
- **Government services** - catering and facilities management for police, government agencies and US Air Force bases in the UK.
