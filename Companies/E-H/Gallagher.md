# Gallagher Security Europe Limited

> Physical and cyber security systems manufacturer providing high-security access control, perimeter protection, and NPSA-compliant products to government, defence, and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Gallagher designs, manufactures, and supplies security systems covering access control, perimeter protection, intruder alarms, and integrated cybersecurity. The company's UK high-security team delivers solutions to Five Eyes government and critical national infrastructure sites. The High Security Controller 7000 holds compliance awards from the UK National Protective Security Authority (NPSA) under both the Automatic Access Control Systems (AACS) and Cyber Assurance of Physical Security Systems (CAPSS) standards. The UK European headquarters is in Warwick; global headquarters is in Hamilton, New Zealand.

## Products and solutions

- **High Security Controller 7000** - NPSA and CAPSS compliant access control hardware with FIPS-approved microprocessor, authenticated and encrypted reader communications, designed for UK government and Five Eyes high-security sites.
- **Command Centre** - integrated security management software platform covering access control, perimeter, video, and alarm management.
- **Perimeter Security Hardware** - fence controllers, gate hardware, and sensors for critical infrastructure and defence site perimeter protection.
- **PIV Software** - identity credential management for high-security government environments.
- **AccessNow and OneLink** - cloud and networked access control solutions for scalable deployment.
