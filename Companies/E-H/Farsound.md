# Farsound Aviation

> Specialist distributor and supply chain solutions provider for the aero engine MRO market.

## What they do

Farsound Aviation is a UK-headquartered procurement and supply chain company serving the aero engine maintenance, repair and overhaul (MRO) sector. It sources, manages and distributes high-volume consumable hardware components for engine overhaul programmes, including kitting, direct line feed and vending provision. The company operates a purpose-built 58,000 sq ft automated warehouse in Brentwood, Essex, with AI-driven robotic picking. It serves MRO organisations globally, some of which support military aviation programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Consumable hardware supply** - procurement and distribution of high-volume aero engine hardware components for MRO operations.
- **Kitting and line-feed services** - pre-kitted component sets and direct-to-line delivery for engine overhaul facilities.
- **Inventory management** - vendor-managed inventory and vending solutions for MRO customers.
- **Global distribution network** - facilities in the UK, USA (San Antonio), Spain (Madrid), Canada, Singapore and Japan.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN 9120B (aerospace distributor quality management); ISO 9001:2015; ISO 14001; UK CAA AC00-56 (stores organisation approval); ASA-100 (Aviation Suppliers Association)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Midlands Aerospace Alliance
