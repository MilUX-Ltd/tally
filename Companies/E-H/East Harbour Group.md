# East Harbour Group

> Specialist supplier of chemical intermediates and energetics raw materials to defence and high-hazard manufacturers.

## What they do

East Harbour Group supplies raw materials to manufacturers in the energetics and high-hazard industries, including the civil and defence sectors. The company distributes chemical intermediates used in the production of explosives, propellants and pyrotechnics, alongside a broader range of industrial and speciality chemicals. It operates from Colchester, Essex, and has additional operations in India, with logistics arrangements certified to UKWA, BIFA and FTA standards.

## Products and solutions

- **Energetics raw materials** - supply of materials including PETN (pentaerythritol tetranitrate) and ammonium perchlorate (primary oxidiser in solid rocket motors and composite propellants) to manufacturers in the defence and civil sectors.
- **Chemical intermediates** - distribution of speciality and industrial chemicals including propellant stabilisers such as ethyl centralite, and process chemicals for energetics manufacturing.
