# Explosive Learning Solutions

> Defence and government training organisation specialising in training needs analysis, e-learning development, end-point assessment, and ordnance, munitions and explosives education.

## What they do

Explosive Learning Solutions (ELS) is an independent learning and development (L&D) accredited training organisation, consultancy company, and End Point Assessment Organisation (EPAO). Since 2005 it has delivered Training Needs Analysis, training design, digital and blended e-learning development, and training delivery to the UK MOD (Defence Academy and Defence Equipment and Support), UK National Government, and major defence organisations. ELS applies the Defence Systems Approach to Training (DSAT) across its work and is a formally recognised Vocational Education and Training (VET) provider, as well as a company member of the Institute of Explosive Engineers (IExpE) supporting Ordnance, Munitions and Explosives (OME) contracts.

## Products and solutions

- **Training Needs Analysis and design** - bespoke TNA, training design, and digital content for blended, virtual, and e-learning programmes aligned to DSAT best practice.
- **End Point Assessment (EPA)** - multi-year EPA contracts across defence, aviation, supply chain, NDT, and data disciplines at Levels 2 through 6, including OME Technician and Intelligence Analyst standards.
- **P3M and procurement training** - commercial off-the-shelf and bespoke training in Projects, Programmes and Portfolios methodologies and defence acquisition skills, delivered at Harwell Science and Innovation Campus or at customer sites.
- **CBRN and counter-terror consultancy** - specialist advisory and training services in CBRN, covert and counter-terror, hostile environment, and bomb disposal domains.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus, SC21 Bronze
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Institute of Explosive Engineers (IExpE); approved training organisation for City and Guilds, ILM, APMG International, PeopleCert, APM, Skills for Justice, and CIPS
