# Greycoat Global Ltd

> London-based cyber security consultancy and training provider with roots in MOD and government intelligence and communications roles.

## What they do

Greycoat Global was formed in 2017 by two founders who bring together over 50 years of combined intelligence, communications and cyber experience gained across the Ministry of Defence and other government departments. The company delivers cyber security consultancy, digital upskilling, open-source intelligence (OSINT) training, and capacity-building programmes for government and commercial clients. It has delivered bespoke cyber security training to government departments, including work that was subsequently made mandatory for all staff. Training can be delivered to audiences of any size, from individual sessions to large groups.

## Products and solutions

- **Consultancy** - expert guidance on cyber security posture and organisational risk.
- **Cyber security training** - needs-led training covering cyber awareness, digital hygiene and incident response for organisations including government departments.
- **OSINT training** - application of open-source intelligence as both an offensive and defensive tool.
- **Hermes Secure Communications Platform** - secure communications capability described on the company website.
- **Capacity building** - programmes to reduce insider threat and upskill workforces in digital and cyber domains.
