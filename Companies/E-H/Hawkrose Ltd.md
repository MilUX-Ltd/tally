# Hawkrose Ltd

> Software technology company that develops and deploys decision support solutions for the UK public and private sector, including military asset transition planning.

## What they do

Hawkrose develops and deploys tailored software to help organisations solve complex problems, primarily in the UK public sector and among its suppliers. The company uses a multi-disciplinary team covering data scientists, software developers, financial and operational analysts, and domain specialists. Its work spans decision support, process digitisation, machine learning, and advanced analytics; published applications include military asset transition planning, estates rationalisation, and carbon reduction strategies.

## Products and solutions

- **Optimisation tools** - custom software algorithms that identify high-performing options within complex, high-dimensional operational contexts.
- **Process digitisation** - software that embeds accountability, traceability, and efficiency into legacy processes by digitising operational and organisational workflows.
- **Machine learning models** - algorithms that expose hidden patterns within operational datasets, support fast-time simulation scaling, and search large solution spaces.
- **Advanced analytics and digital twins** - tools that emulate organisational and system dynamics to help decision makers assess the impact of process change against strategic goals.
- **Gamification events** - structured decision-making exercises designed to support senior leaders working through complex tactical and strategic choices.
