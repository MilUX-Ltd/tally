# Finch Consulting

> Engineering risk management consultancy serving the defence and aerospace sectors.

## What they do

Finch Consulting provides specialist engineering and health and safety risk management services. Their offer spans asset management, health and safety management, and process safety management, delivered through consulting, expert witness work, and training. Defence and aerospace are named sectors on their website, with staff drawn from military aviation and defence HSE backgrounds.

## Products and solutions

- **Asset Management consulting** - engineering risk and maintenance strategy services for complex asset-intensive programmes.
- **Health and Safety Management** - safety management systems and regulatory compliance advisory for defence and high-hazard industries.
- **Process Safety Management** - process hazard analysis and risk reduction for defence and industrial clients.
- **Expert witness services** - technical evidence and dispute support in engineering and safety matters.
