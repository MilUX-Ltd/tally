# Hughes Network Systems Ltd

> Satellite broadband and network solutions provider with a UK entity serving European defence and government customers.

## What they do

Hughes Network Systems is a global provider of satellite broadband and managed network services, operating in Europe through Hughes Network Systems Europe Limited from Milton Keynes, as part of Hughes Network Systems, LLC (Germantown, US), an EchoStar company. The European business delivers satellite communications infrastructure to government, defence and enterprise customers, describing itself as developing, installing, managing and maintaining networks and classified terminals and systems for defence and security applications. In 2024 Hughes Europe launched a dedicated Military, Defence & Government business unit, delivered in collaboration with Telespazio using Eutelsat Konnect VHTS satellite capacity, targeting government communications ministries, military operators and blue-light services across Europe.

## Products and solutions

- **JUPITER System** - high-throughput satellite system, including flyaway kits and manpack terminals.
- **HM System terminals** - software-defined satellite modems (including the HM400) using an Enhanced Scramble Code Multiple Access waveform across L-, Ku-, Ka-, Mil Ka and X-bands.
- **Software-Defined Core Module** - small form-factor modem building block for fixed, land, aero, maritime and portable terminals and gateways.
- **Smart Network Edge** - software for orchestrating multi-orbit (GEO/MEO/LEO) and 5G network connectivity, alongside a Network Management System for defence customers.
