# Hughes Network Systems Ltd

> Satellite broadband and network solutions provider with a UK entity serving European defence and government customers.

## What they do

Hughes Network Systems is a global provider of satellite broadband and managed network services, operating through its European subsidiary from Milton Keynes. The company delivers satellite communications infrastructure to government, defence, and enterprise customers, including VSAT terminals, network management systems, and broadband-over-satellite services.
