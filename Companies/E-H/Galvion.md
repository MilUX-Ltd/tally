# Galvion UK Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of ballistic helmet systems, soldier power and data management solutions, and future dismounted soldier technologies.

## What they do

Galvion designs and delivers integrated protective head systems and intelligent power and data solutions for military and tactical teams. The company holds a significant position on the UK MOD VIRTUS programme, having supplied over 200,000 helmet systems since contract award in 2015. Galvion's UK entity (Companies House 09713398) supports its British military customer base, with manufacturing for UK and European orders fulfilled in partnership with NP Aerospace. The parent company is headquartered in Montreal, Canada.

## Products and solutions

- **Batlskin helmet systems** - Modular, ballistic protective helmets including the Caiman, Hellbender, and Caiman TL variants, used by UK, US, Canadian, and NATO forces.
- **Nerv Centr power and data systems** - Soldier-worn power management, battery charging, and data distribution systems including the Squad Power Manager (SPM) and MAX-8 Mission Adaptive Charging Station.
- **CORTEX smart helmet integration platform** - Digitally enabled headborne integration system stemming from UK DSTL Future Integrated Dismounted Soldier Vision (FIDSV) programme work.
- **E.D.G headborne power and data system** - Headborne power and data distribution and management for the integrated soldier.
