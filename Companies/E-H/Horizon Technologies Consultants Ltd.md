# Horizon Technologies Consultants Ltd

> Airborne and space-based SIGINT specialist producing satellite monitoring systems for government ISR operations worldwide.

## What they do

Horizon Technologies develops and manufactures the FlyingFish and BlackFish families of passive satellite communication monitoring systems, enabling governments to intercept SATCOM, L-band and maritime radar signals from airborne, ground, uncrewed and space-based platforms. The company states its systems are deployed on more than 60 NATO and non-NATO aircraft, and describes contributions to counter-piracy, counter-terrorism, border security, anti-poaching and search-and-rescue operations, including migrant rescue efforts in the Mediterranean. Horizon (operating as Horizon Space Technologies for its space programme) is prime contractor for the AMBER programme, a planned constellation of CubeSats delivering space-based maritime domain awareness data to the UK's National Maritime Information Centre.

## Products and solutions

- **FlyingFish** - airborne SATCOM and L-band monitoring system for passive SIGINT collection, configurable for manned aircraft, ground vehicles, uncrewed aircraft and fixed-wing platforms.
- **BlackFish** - tri-band passive L-band SIGINT system, described as the latest evolution of the FlyingFish line, also detecting and characterising maritime radar emissions.
- **AMBER** - space-based maritime SIGINT constellation programme, demodulating satellite-phone and radar signal content to provide vessel identification and geolocation data.
