# Horizon Technologies Consultants Ltd

> Airborne SIGINT specialist producing satellite monitoring systems for government ISR operations worldwide.

## What they do

Horizon Technologies develops and manufactures the FlyingFish family of airborne satellite communication monitoring systems, enabling governments to passively intercept SATCOM signals from tactical airborne platforms. Their products are deployed by NATO and non-NATO nations in support of counter-narcotics, counter-terrorism, border security, and anti-poaching operations.

## Products and solutions

- **FlyingFish** - UK dual-use COTS airborne SATCOM monitoring system for passive SIGINT collection from manned and unmanned platforms.
