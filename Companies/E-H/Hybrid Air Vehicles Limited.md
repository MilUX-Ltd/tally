# Hybrid Air Vehicles Limited

> Developer of the Airlander hybrid aircraft platform, targeting logistics, communications, surveillance and low-emissions passenger transport.

## What they do

Hybrid Air Vehicles (HAV) designed and manufactures Airlander, a large hybrid aircraft that combines lighter-than-air and aerodynamic lift. Airlander 10 carries up to 100 passengers or 10 tonnes of freight and supports use cases including surveillance and communications alongside logistics and tourism, with a stated pathway to zero-emissions aviation by 2030.

## Products and solutions

- **Airlander 10** - Large hybrid aircraft platform capable of carrying 100 passengers or 10 tonnes of freight; supports ISR, logistics, and communications missions.
