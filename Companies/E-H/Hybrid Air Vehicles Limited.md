# Hybrid Air Vehicles Limited

> Developer of the Airlander hybrid aircraft platform, targeting logistics, communications, surveillance and low-emissions passenger transport.

## What they do

Hybrid Air Vehicles (HAV) designs and manufactures Airlander, a large hybrid aircraft that combines lighter-than-air and aerodynamic lift. Airlander 10 carries up to 100 passengers or 10 tonnes of freight and supports use cases including intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance, and beyond-line-of-sight communications relay, alongside logistics and tourism, with a stated pathway to zero-emissions aviation by 2030. The company states Airlander can operate crewed for up to five days aloft, or uncrewed for over two weeks, carrying a three-tonne payload of sensors and communications equipment.

## Products and solutions

- **Airlander 10** - large hybrid aircraft platform capable of carrying 100 passengers or 10 tonnes of freight; supports ISR, logistics and communications relay missions in crewed or uncrewed configurations.
- **Communications and surveillance payload** - on-board sensors with data collection, processing and dissemination, described by the company as able to act as an airborne intelligence, command and communications centre.
