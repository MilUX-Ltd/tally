# Hamble Aerostructures Ltd

> UK-based designer and manufacturer of advanced composite, metallic and hybrid aerostructures for civil and military aircraft programmes, operating as part of the Aernnova group.

## What they do

Hamble Aerostructures Limited (HAL) is headquartered in Hamble-le-Rice, Hampshire, and has operated as an aerostructures manufacturer since 1936. The business became part of the Spanish aerospace group Aernnova following its acquisition from GE Aviation in 2020. HAL designs and manufactures major aircraft structures in composite, metallic, acrylic and hybrid materials, and holds design authority for a number of structural components. Its customer base includes Airbus, Boeing, BAE Systems and Bombardier. HAL also produces military role equipment and canopy systems for defence programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Composite aerostructures** - design and manufacture of primary and secondary composite structures for civil and military aircraft, including trailing-edge wing components for the Airbus A350.
- **Metallic aerostructures** - machined and sheet-formed metallic structures using in-house treatment and finishing capabilities.
- **Military role equipment and canopy systems** - specialist structures and transparencies for military aircraft programmes.
- **End-to-end design and build** - in-house design authority, manufacturing (composite, metallic machining and sheet forming), surface treatments and transparencies.
