# Fenner Precision Polymers

> Manufacturer of engineered polymer products including aerospace-grade ducting and coated fabrics, operating as a Michelin Group subsidiary with its UK manufacturing base in Lincoln.

## What they do

Fenner Precision Polymers manufactures engineered polymer components across three global product families: industrial motion (timing and power transmission belts), high-tech coated fabrics, and air and fluid handling products. The Lincoln, UK site holds AS9100 Rev D accreditation for aerospace and defence manufacturing, covering ducting under the James Dawson brand and specialist coated fabrics and textiles under the Critical Thermal Composites brand. Fenner joined ADS Group in August 2025 to target growth in the aerospace, defence, and space markets. The company is wholly owned by Michelin Group, which acquired Fenner plc in May 2018.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace ducting** (James Dawson brand) - air and fluid handling products for aerospace and defence applications, manufactured at Lincoln under AS9100 Rev D.
- **Critical thermal composites** (Critical Thermal Composites brand) - specialist coated fabrics and textiles for thermal management in aerospace and defence environments, manufactured at Lincoln under AS9100 Rev D.
- **Industrial motion products** (Fenner Precision brand) - timing belts and power transmission components for wider industrial markets.
