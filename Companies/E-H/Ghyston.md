# Ghyston

> Bristol-based bespoke software development agency delivering business-critical software solutions, with security-cleared work in the defence sector.

## What they do

Ghyston is a Bristol-based bespoke software development and consultancy agency founded in 2012. The company delivers end-to-end software solutions covering consultancy, AI enablement, development, and long-term support. It is technology-agnostic, selecting the appropriate stack for each client's requirements. Ghyston holds ISO 27001 and Cyber Essentials certifications and works with security-cleared personnel for defence sector projects. The company is a Crown Commercial Service supplier.

## Products and solutions

- **Bespoke software development** - custom software designed around client requirements, from concept through to deployment and ongoing support.
- **Software consultancy** - advisory and process consultancy for organisations developing or improving software-based systems.
- **AI enablement** - integration of AI capabilities into client software and workflows.
- **Software support** - enterprise helpdesk and long-term support services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001, Cyber Essentials
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
