# Geodesy

> GIS consultancy providing geospatial systems, mapping applications and data integration for government agencies and the built environment.

## What they do

Geodesy is a San Francisco-based GIS consultancy providing needs analysis, implementation, systems integration and automation for government and built-environment clients. Its stated client base is local, state and federal agencies in the United States, including cities, counties and federal bodies. Public information does not evidence a UK presence or a direct UK or allied defence contract; the entry is carried in Tally for its geospatial intelligence (GEOINT) tradecraft relevance rather than a demonstrated defence customer base.

## Products and solutions

- **Encompass** - enterprise GIS web application giving local government staff and the public map, data and analytics access; successor to a long-running desktop product line.
- **Dox** - spatially indexed document management system linking stored files to GIS map features.
- **GIS consulting and systems integration** - needs analysis, data modelling, and integration between GIS and other enterprise systems, typically built on Esri ArcGIS alongside open mapping tools (Google Maps, OpenLayers, Leaflet).
- **3D mapping applications** - browser-based 3D city and building models for planning and analysis use cases, built on Esri ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Online.
