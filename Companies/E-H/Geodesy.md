# Geodesy
> Specialist GIS solutions provider serving government agencies, with defence relevance via geospatial intelligence workloads.
## What they do
Geodesy provides Geographic Information System solutions for local, state and federal government agencies and the built environment. The company has a UK and US presence. Defence relevance is via geospatial intelligence (GEOINT) tradecraft and proximity to workloads including the UK Defence Geographic Centre. Public information on specific defence contracts is limited.
