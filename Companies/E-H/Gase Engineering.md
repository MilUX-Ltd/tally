# Gase Engineering

> Family-owned precision engineering manufacturer supplying machined parts, sheet metal fabrication and sub-assemblies to aerospace, defence, nuclear and telecommunications industries.

## What they do

Gase Engineering is a family-owned and operated precision engineering company founded in 1964, with heritage derived from Strand Electric (Covent Garden), MOD Woolwich Arsenal and Vickers of Crayford. It operates four factories in Dartford, Kent, with a workforce of around 60. The company supplies manufactured parts to aerospace, defence, nuclear and telecommunications customers, offering capabilities from single prototypes through to high-volume batch production.

## Products and solutions

- **5-Axis CNC Milling** - High-precision machining of complex aerospace and defence components.
- **CNC Mill/Turn** - High-precision combined mill/turn operations for complex parts.
- **Precision Sheet Metal Work** - Fine-limit sheet metal fabrication, a core heritage capability.
- **Waterjet Cutting** - Specialised in-house waterjet cutting processes.
- **Final Assembly** - On-site assembly of components requiring machining, fabrication and welding.
