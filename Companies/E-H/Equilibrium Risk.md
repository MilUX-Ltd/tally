# Equilibrium Risk

> Specialist security and risk management, including close protection and SIA-standard training.

## What they do

Equilibrium Risk is a specialist security and risk management company providing protective services to individuals and organisations. Services include close protection, door supervision and static site guarding, delivered by a team with combined military experience. It also runs training courses meeting Security Industry Authority (SIA) standards.

## Products and solutions

- **Close protection** - protective security operations in the UK and worldwide.
- **Guarding** - door supervision and static site guarding.
- **Training** - SIA-standard security training courses.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** SIA-compliant; training accredited by Pearson Edexcel.
