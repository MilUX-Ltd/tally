# Enable Strategic Consulting Ltd

> Defence and public sector bid consultancy, specialising in capture management, Position-to-Win analysis and bid writing for suppliers to MOD and government.

## What they do

Enable Strategic Consulting Ltd provides business-to-government growth advisory services focused on the UK defence and public sector. The company works with suppliers seeking to win MOD and government contracts, offering capture management, Position-to-Win analysis, bid writing and bid team capability development. It has also published analysis on the impact of the Procurement Act on contract award evaluation and post-award processes.

## Products and solutions

- **Capture management and bid advisory** - structured support across the capture and bid lifecycle for defence and public sector suppliers, including SMEs.
- **Position-to-Win analysis** - competitive positioning analysis to improve bid win probability.
- **Bid team development** - training and process optimisation for in-house bid functions, including AI-assisted bid delivery.
