# Esparta Applied Intelligence

> Manchester-based technology partner delivering cloud platforms and applied intelligence solutions for UK defence and security.

## What they do

Esparta engineers secure, scalable cloud platforms that underpin mission-critical operations for UK defence and security organisations. It delivers applied intelligence and big data solutions to support more informed decision-making, and embeds with client teams to build lasting engineering capability. The company uses AWS as its primary cloud platform.

## Products and solutions

- **Cloud Platforms** - secure, scalable AWS-based cloud infrastructure for defence and security mission-critical operations.
- **Applied Intelligence Solutions** - big data and data science solutions enabling UK defence and security customers to extract insight from complex datasets.
