# Eurofins E&E UK

> UK network of test and compliance laboratories providing military EMC, environmental simulation and product certification to defence, aerospace and commercial customers.

## What they do

Eurofins E&E UK is an end-to-end testing, certification and compliance provider covering electromagnetic compatibility (EMC), electrical safety, environmental simulation and radio equipment testing. Its UK network of UKAS-accredited laboratories, headquartered in York, serves military and commercial product programmes across land, sea and air domains. The network includes the Eurofins York brand (formerly York EMC Services) and laboratory sites in Devon, Southampton, London, Bristol, Castleford and Grangemouth. For projects requiring security clearance, the York site handles those enquiries. Eurofins E&E UK is part of the Eurofins Scientific group, headquartered in Luxembourg.

## Products and solutions

- **Military EMC testing** - UKAS-accredited testing to DEF STAN 59-411 and MIL-STD-461 (variants D through G) for land, sea and air platforms.
- **Environmental simulation** - extreme environmental testing (vibration, shock, temperature, humidity, ingress) for military and defence applications.
- **EMC training** - a four-day EMC for Military course covering DEF STAN 59-411, MIL-STD-461 and related standards.
- **Fibre optic testing** - testing to MIL-PRF-28876, MIL-PRF-85045 and MIL-DLT-83526.
- **Electromagnetic shielding effectiveness** - testing for materials and enclosures up to 40 GHz, including comb generator emitters.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** UKAS accreditation covering DEF STAN 59-411, DEF STAN 59-41, MIL-STD-461.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
