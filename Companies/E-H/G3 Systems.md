# G3 Systems

> Portland-based provider of deployable and fixed military infrastructure, engineering and support services for austere environments.

## What they do

G3 Systems designs and produces deployable infrastructure solutions and delivers engineering, service and support to defence and government customers operating in austere locations. Its work spans deployable and fixed infrastructure and systems, outsourced fire and rescue services, and through-life support. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of US-headquartered Intrepid Global Solutions and operates from Portland, England.

## Products and solutions

- **Deployable facilities** - military shelters, containerised laboratories and field operating sites for expeditionary use.
- **Deployable logistics infrastructure** - flatracks, containers and associated equipment supplied to defence ministries.
- **Engineering and support services** - through-life support and outsourced fire and rescue services in austere environments.
