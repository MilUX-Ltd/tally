# Harwin

> Portsmouth-based manufacturer of high-reliability electronic connectors for defence, aerospace, space, and other demanding applications.

## What they do

Harwin manufactures high-reliability electronic connectors designed to withstand high levels of shock and vibration and extreme temperatures. The company holds EN 9100 / AS9100D certification and supplies connector products for defence, aviation, space, UAV, industrial, and medical markets. Manufacturing is based at its Portsmouth headquarters, with offices in the USA, Germany, France, and Singapore, and a worldwide distribution network.

## Products and solutions

- **High-Reliability Signal Connectors** - PCB-to-PCB and cable-to-PCB connectors rated for harsh environments including defence platforms.
- **Power Connectors** - high-reliability power connectors for demanding applications.
- **Coax Connectors** - coaxial connector products for RF and signal applications.
- **EMC / Board Level Shielding** - EMI and RFI shielding solutions for PCB-level protection.
- **Cable Assemblies** - custom high-reliability cable assemblies configurable for specific applications.
