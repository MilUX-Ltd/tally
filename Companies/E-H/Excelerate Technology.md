# Excelerate Technology Ltd

> Welsh systems integrator providing hybrid satellite and cellular communications, deployable connectivity and situational awareness solutions to military, government and emergency services customers.

## What they do

Excelerate Technology Ltd is a Cardiff-based communications and situational awareness systems integrator with over 20 years of experience serving military, first responder and government markets. The company designs and supplies hybrid connectivity systems that bond satellite, 4G and 5G links to provide resilient, on-the-move communications independent of terrestrial network availability. Excelerate positions itself as a niche and agnostic integrator, supporting deployable command and control, vehicle-mounted and portable communications platforms. Defence is an explicit named vertical on their public website.

## Products and solutions

- **Hybrid communications systems** - vehicle-mounted and portable platforms bonding satellite, 4G and 5G for high-throughput, resilient military connectivity.
- **Hybrid Edge Appliance** - 4G/5G/satellite bonding device for deployable private secure networks.
- **Linx Hub** - portable Wi-Fi hotspot for field connectivity.
- **Video streaming and situational awareness** - live video and data solutions for command and control in deployed environments.
- **Deployable command support units** - communications-equipped command vehicles and incident command units, built to customer specification for emergency services and military end-users.
