# GEM Cable Solutions

> UK manufacturer of bespoke defence-standard cable assemblies, wiring harnesses, looms and panel builds for military and aerospace applications.

## What they do

GEM Cable Solutions is a UK manufacturer of bespoke cable assemblies, control boxes, wiring harnesses, looms, panels and fibre optic products. The company has over 30 years of experience and manufactures entirely in the UK from facilities in Hertfordshire, including within a Class 8 clean room. It serves the defence, aerospace, marine, robotics, nuclear, rail, broadcast, motorsport and satellite sectors, with defence forming a dedicated product line.

## Products and solutions

- **Military cable assemblies and wiring harnesses** - custom-built to DEF STAN and Mil-Spec requirements, covering power, signal, data and multicore configurations for military vehicles, radar, avionics and naval equipment.
- **Mil-Spec wiring systems** - components manufactured to MIL-DTL and MIL-C specifications with full traceability and certificates of conformity.
- **Sealed heatshrink and military connector systems** - components for harsh-environment applications subject to shock, vibration, moisture and extreme temperatures.
- **Fibre optic products and total network solutions** - bespoke fibre assemblies for communications and sensor systems.
