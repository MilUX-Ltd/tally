# Freightline Carriers Ltd

> Specialist logistics provider serving the aerospace and defence sectors, offering time-critical road and air freight, customs clearance and warehousing across Europe.

## What they do

Freightline Carriers is a UK-based logistics specialist with more than 50 years of heritage in freight forwarding. The company operates 24/7 with a multilingual team and access to over 1,000 vehicles across Europe. It holds a dedicated aerospace and defence practice focused on transporting sensitive, high-value components and equipment, and supporting manufacturing, maintenance, repair and overhaul supply chains.

## Products and solutions

- **Urgent, time-critical logistics** - 24/7 road and air freight with 15-minute response and two-hour collection commitment; used in aerospace and defence supply chains to prevent production line stoppages or aircraft groundings.
- **Air freight and air charter** - global transport of aerospace and defence cargo including helicopters, private jets and large payload aircraft.
- **Customs clearance** - in-house agents handling cross-border documentation for defence materiel.
- **Warehousing and distribution** - European network of storage facilities with integrated warehouse management systems for high-value stock.
