# HyImpulse UK Ltd

> German-founded rocket launch company with a UK presence in Scotland, developing launch vehicles for small satellites and sub-orbital sounding rockets.

## What they do

HyImpulse develops hybrid-propulsion rocket launch systems targeting two markets: affordable, reliable access to space for small satellites, and sub-orbital sounding rockets capable of Mach 5 with an 800 km downrange. The company operates across Scotland and Germany.
