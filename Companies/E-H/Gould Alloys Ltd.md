# Gould Alloys Ltd

> Supplier of high-integrity ferrous and non-ferrous metals and components to the aerospace, defence and power generation sectors.

## What they do

Gould Alloys supplies high-integrity metals and components for critical applications across aerospace, defence, automotive and power generation. Operating as part of the Aero Metals Alliance (AMA), the company holds access to large stocks of commercial and aerospace-grade aluminium, stainless steel, copper, brass and bronze in all semi-finished forms. It operates from a purpose-built facility in Derbyshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace and defence metals** - aluminium alloys, stainless steel, copper, brass and bronze in bar, sheet, tube and other semi-finished forms to aerospace and commercial specifications.
- **Component supply** - end-to-end supply chain solutions including processing capabilities.
