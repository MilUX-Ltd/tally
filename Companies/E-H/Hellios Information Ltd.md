# Hellios Information Ltd

> Operator of community-based supplier assurance schemes, including JOSCAR for the defence, aerospace and security sector.

## What they do

Hellios operates community-based platforms that collect, validate and share supplier data on behalf of buying organisations, simplifying compliance and reducing supply chain risk. Its JOSCAR scheme is the supplier assurance system used across the defence, aerospace and security sector, with buyers including the Ministry of Defence and major primes. It also runs schemes for financial services (FSQS) and energy (ESSCAR), plus sustainability and carbon reporting tools.

## Products and solutions

- **JOSCAR** - supplier assurance and pre-qualification for defence, aerospace and security.
- **JOSCAR Zero** - carbon and sustainability reporting for the JOSCAR community.
- **Pooled audit and SME portal** - shared assurance services for suppliers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO/IEC 27001, Cyber Essentials Plus.
