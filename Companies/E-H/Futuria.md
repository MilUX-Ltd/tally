# Futuria Ltd

> UK agentic AI platform provider targeting defence, government, and enterprise environments.

## What they do

Futuria builds and deploys multi-agent AI teams designed to accelerate decision-making and process execution in high-assurance enterprise environments. The platform is positioned for secure, proprietary environments where accuracy, full audit trails, and human oversight are required. Defence is one of several named industry verticals alongside government, critical infrastructure, and manufacturing.

## Products and solutions

- **Futuria Adaptive Work Platform** - multi-agent AI orchestration platform designed for secure enterprise and government environments, with support for defence use cases including decision velocity, operational coordination, and intelligence synthesis.
