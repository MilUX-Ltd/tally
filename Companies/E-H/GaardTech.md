# GaardTech

> Australian maker of robotic enemy-vehicle targets and live-fire training systems for armed forces.

## What they do

GaardTech designs and manufactures robotic enemy-vehicle targets and live-fire training systems for armies. Its catalogue of 3D Robotic Enemy Vehicles replicates a range of enemy platforms with thermal, electronic and radar signatures, and the mobile targets can be operated remotely or autonomously across terrain. The company is based in Brisbane, Queensland, and exports to several allied armed forces.

## Products and solutions

- **3D Robotic Enemy Vehicles** - mobile robotic targets replicating tanks, armoured personnel carriers and other platforms with multi-spectral signatures for live-fire training.
- **Flatpack robotic targets** - deployable, recyclable robotic targets and decoys.
- **Doctrinally correct enemy formations** - target groupings representing enemy battle groups for collective live-fire training.

## Recent news

- **2025** - Awarded a contract to supply the British Army with 32 3D Robotic Enemy Vehicles for live-fire training, to be built in Brisbane. [source](https://www.defenceconnect.com.au/land/7479-gaardtech-awarded-british-army-contract)
