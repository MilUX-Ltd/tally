# Entrust Security Solutions UK

> UK operating subsidiary of Entrust Corporation, with roots in the Datacard physical credential issuance business, supplying secure card and document issuance systems, encryption hardware and identity infrastructure to government and defence-adjacent customers.

## What they do

Entrust Security Solutions UK Ltd is a UK-registered operating subsidiary of Entrust Corporation, a US-headquartered digital security company. The entity traces its lineage to Datacard Corporation (founded 1969), which was acquired by Entrust Inc. to form Entrust Datacard in 2013 and rebranded as Entrust in 2020; the UK entity was renamed to its current form in 2021. It sits within the Entrust portfolio alongside Entrust (Europe) Ltd, which holds the European software and services activity. The SIC code is 46520 (wholesale of electronic and telecommunications equipment). The registered office is at 9 Devonshire Square, London.

## Products and solutions

- **Card and credential issuance systems** - hardware and software platforms for printing and personalising government identity cards, military credentials, smart cards and passports. Rooted in the Datacard card-printing heritage.
- **nShield Hardware Security Modules** - FIPS 140-3 Level 3 and Common Criteria EAL4+ certified cryptographic devices for key management and PKI, shared across the Entrust group following the nCipher acquisition from Thales in 2019.
- **ePassport and national identity document infrastructure** - secure credential issuance platforms used by governments for travel documents and national ID schemes.
- **PKI and encryption** - certificate lifecycle management and encryption infrastructure.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022 (Entrust Corporation organisation-wide); nShield HSMs certified to FIPS 140-3 Level 3 and Common Criteria EAL4+.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
