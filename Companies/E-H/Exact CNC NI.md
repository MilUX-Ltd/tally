# Exact CNC (NI) Ltd

> Northern Ireland precision CNC machining and finishing company supplying the aerospace, space and defence supply chain.

## What they do

Exact CNC (NI) Ltd is a precision engineering subcontractor based in Newry, operating primarily in the aerospace, space and defence sectors. From a 72,000 sq ft facility at Craigmore Mill Industrial Estate, the company provides CNC milling and turning across 2D and 3D configurations from 3-axis to 9-axis, covering composites, plastics, tooling board and metals. It also offers anodising, chemical coating, powder coating, CMM inspection and 3D scanning, and metal fabrication and assembly. Named customers include Collins Aerospace, Thales UK and Thompson Aero Seating.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision CNC milling and turning** - multi-axis machining (3- to 9-axis) of aerospace and defence components in metal and composite materials.
- **Cerakote ceramic coating** - ceramic-polymer finishing used on military small arms, weapon accessories and tactical equipment; Exact Group is a certified applicator.
- **Surface treatment and finishing** - anodising, chemical coating and powder coating to aerospace supply chain standards.
- **CMM inspection and 3D scanning** - dimensional verification and quality control against AS9100 Rev D requirements.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100 Rev D; ISO 9001; ISO 13485; SC21 Silver (first company in Ireland to achieve Silver on first engagement; reaccredited July 2024 for the fourth consecutive year); SC21 Operational Excellence; EFQM 5 Star (2023); Cyber Essentials Plus (achieved June 2026).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
