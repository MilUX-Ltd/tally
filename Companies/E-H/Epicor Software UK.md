# Epicor Software UK

> Enterprise resource planning and manufacturing management software supplier to the aerospace and defence supply chain.

## What they do

Epicor sells ERP and manufacturing management software with a named aerospace and defence vertical. Their core platform, Epicor Kinetic, is a cloud ERP system with compliance capabilities covering ITAR export control, CMMC 2.0 cybersecurity readiness, DCAA financial accountability, DFARS and DCMA requirements, and AS9100 quality management workflows including first article inspection, non-conformance tracking, and CAPA. The UK subsidiary in Bracknell serves as the sales, support, and professional services presence for the UK market.

## Products and solutions

- **Epicor Kinetic** - Cloud ERP platform with an aerospace and defence module covering ITAR, CMMC 2.0, AS9100, shop floor data collection, finite capacity scheduling, and defence supply chain management.
- **Epicor Prism** - Agentic AI capability for manufacturing operations, announced at Epicor Insights 2025.
- **Epicor Grow AI** - Predictive machine learning for manufacturing, announced 2025.
