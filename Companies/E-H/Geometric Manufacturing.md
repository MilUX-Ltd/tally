# Geometric Manufacturing

> Tewkesbury-based CNC machining specialist with a long history of supplying defence primes and mid-tier suppliers with electronic warfare enclosures, ECM housings, and precision components.

## What they do

Geometric Manufacturing has been producing precision CNC-machined components for the defence and aerospace industries since 1986. The company's core competence is the machining of enclosures and components used in electronic warfare systems, ECM equipment, and cyber encryption products. It also machines parts for fighting vehicles and aircraft cabin interiors. Customers include HMG and their prime contractors, as well as mid-tier defence suppliers. The company operates across two manufacturing sites with 24-hour working, and maintains air-gapped IT infrastructure and staff vetting.

## Products and solutions

- **Electronic warfare enclosures** - machined housings and enclosures for EW systems and ECM equipment, to Def Stan and MIL-SPEC standards.
- **Cyber encryption component machining** - precision components for secure communications and encryption equipment.
- **Vehicle and platform assemblies** - complex assemblies for man-pack, land vehicle installations, and maritime and air platform components.
- **CNC machined parts** - CAM-programmed precision parts from a network of UK and overseas suppliers, with in-house sub-contract finishing including plating and painting.
