# Electropak

> Preston-based precision engineering and busbar manufacturer with AS9100 certification and BAE Systems approval, supplying aerospace and defence programmes including Eurofighter, Tornado, Hawk, and F-35.

## What they do

Electropak Limited is a precision engineering and busbar manufacturer established in 1986 and based in Preston, Lancashire. The company holds AS9100 Rev D and ISO 9001 certifications and is approved by BAE Systems. It provides CNC machining, sheet metal fabrication, busbar manufacture, aerospace engraving, painting, and surface finishing services to the aerospace, defence, energy, medical, and rail sectors. Electropak is a subsidiary of UKC Group and exhibited at DSEI 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision CNC Machining** - close-tolerance machined components for aerospace and defence platforms.
- **Busbar Manufacture** - custom busbar assemblies for aerospace, defence, and energy applications.
- **Aerospace Engraving** - engraving of labels, panels, and tools for aircraft including Eurofighter, Tornado, Hawk, and F-35 Joint Strike Fighter.
- **Defence Painting and Surface Finishing** - AS9100-certified powder coating and painting for defence and aerospace components.
- **Sheet Metal Fabrication** - bespoke fabricated structures and assemblies for defence and aerospace programmes.
