# Adams Electronics

> Designer and manufacturer of specialised hand-held metal detection equipment.

## What they do

Adams Electronics was established in 1965 and designs and manufactures hand-held metal detection equipment in the UK. Its products are aimed at police, security and law enforcement roles. The company's products conform to international standards including CE marking and IEC standards.

## Products and solutions

- **Hand-held metal detectors** - portable detection equipment designed and manufactured in the UK for police and security applications.
