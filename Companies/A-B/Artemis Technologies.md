# Artemis Technologies

> Belfast maritime design and applied-technologies company developing electric hydrofoil vessels, including patrol and coastal-security variants.

## What they do

Artemis Technologies is a high-performance maritime design and applied-technologies company based in Belfast, spun out from the Artemis Racing America's Cup team in 2017 to commercialise its hydrofoiling and autonomy intellectual property. Its patented eFoiler system combines a high-power-density electric drivetrain with an autonomously controlled hydrofoil, lifting the hull clear of the water at speed to cut drag. The company also provides engineering services and develops maritime simulators. A Belfast Maritime Consortium led by Artemis won a UK government grant to develop the technology.

## Products and solutions

- **eFoiler system** - electric drivetrain integrated with an autonomously controlled hydrofoil.
- **EF-12 Patrol** - electric hydrofoil vessel for coastal security, with a top speed of 30 knots and an optional diesel hybrid range extender.
- **EF-12 Pilot** - electric hydrofoil pilot boat for transferring ships' pilots.
- **Maritime simulators** - simulation tools for high-performance vessel design and operation.
