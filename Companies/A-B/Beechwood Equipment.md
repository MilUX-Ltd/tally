# Beechwood Equipment

> Importer and distributor of defence and emergency services equipment, including electro optics, UAVs, ballistic protection and specialist clothing.

## What they do

Beechwood Equipment is an importer and distributor of equipment for the defence and emergency services sectors. Founded in 1987 and based in Guildford, Surrey, the company supplies and supports soldier systems, specialist law enforcement products and rescue equipment. Areas of expertise include electro optics, optics, UAVs and UGVs, ballistic protection and specialist clothing. It is an approved MoD supplier and a Police National Framework supplier.

## Products and solutions

- **Optics and electro optics** - optical sights, electro-optics and through-life support.
- **Drones and robotics** - UAV and UGV systems from a range of partner brands.
- **Clothing and protection** - ballistic protection and specialist clothing.
- **Tactical and duty equipment** - tactical and duty kit for military and law enforcement users.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** various MoD, Police and commercial framework contracts.
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015; Cyber Essentials; Armed Forces Covenant signatory.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
