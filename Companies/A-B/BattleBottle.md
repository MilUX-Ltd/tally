# BattleBottle
> AI-powered drone warfare simulator combining a consumer mobile game and a browser-based tactical advisor for defence customers, underpinned by a shared simulation engine and crowdsourced AI data layer.

## What they do
BattleBottle is a UK defence-tech startup building AI-powered simulation tools for drone warfare. The company runs two surfaces off one simulation engine: a mobile consumer game (B2C) where players make tactical decisions using an in-game AI advisor, and a browser-based tactical advisor for defence customers (B2G) that models force composition, deployment and engagement trade-offs across configurable scenarios. Every game session generates labelled training data that feeds back into the AI model. Platforms and maps reference real systems in service, including Switchblade, RQ-11 Raven and Anduril Anvil. The positioning aligns to UK Strategic Defence Review 2025 priorities on human-machine teaming.

## Products and solutions
- **BattleBottle (B2C)** - Mobile drone combat game generating crowdsourced AI training data through player tactical decisions.
- **BattleBottle Tactical Advisor (B2G)** - Browser-based decision support tool for defence customers, comparing force composition and engagement outcomes across three operational modes.
