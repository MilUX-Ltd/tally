# AXYZ Automation UK

> Supplier of CNC routers, knife cutting systems and waterjet machines for sectors including aerospace, defence, security and space.

## What they do

AXYZ Automation (U.K.) Limited supplies CNC cutting equipment, working alongside the WARDJET waterjet brand under the same group. The company makes configurable CNC routers and knife systems for processing aluminium, composites, plastics and metals, with motion control, high-speed cutting and options such as automatic tool changers. Per its ADS profile, AXYZ and WARDJET serve aerospace, defence, security and space alongside other engineering sectors. The UK operation is based in Telford and forms part of an international CNC cutting business.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC routers** - configurable router systems for cutting aluminium, composites, plastics and metals.
- **Knife cutting systems** - automated knife systems for sheet and roll materials.
- **WARDJET waterjet systems** - waterjet cutting machines with custom configurations for a range of materials.
