# Brownell

> Designer and manufacturer of humidity, moisture and pressure-control products for sensitive electrical and electronic equipment.

## What they do

Brownell designs and manufactures humidity, moisture and pressure-control products that protect sensitive electrical and electronic equipment from condensation, humidity and pressure effects. It has operated since 1952 and serves sectors from defence and security to power generation and distribution, with a stated focus on the aerospace and defence sectors. The company is based at Park Royal in London.

## Products and solutions

- **Desiccants** - desiccant bags, container desiccants, refills and packaging and preservation solutions, including Envirogel silica gel.
- **Humidity indicators** - relative-humidity and condensation indicators.
- **Breathers and valves** - pressure relief valves, immersion-proof breathers, transformer breathers and tank-vent dryers.
- **VMAP** - Versatile Moisture Adsorbent Polymer, a mouldable or machinable adsorbent material developed for electronics, aerospace, defence and optics applications.
