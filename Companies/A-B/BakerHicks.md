# BakerHicks

> Multi-disciplinary design, engineering and project delivery consultancy with a dedicated defence sector practice.

## What they do

BakerHicks is a design, engineering and project delivery consultancy working across infrastructure, life sciences, industrial and public sector markets. It operates a defined defence business unit providing integrated design and engineering services for regulated and secure environments. The firm traces its origins to IDC Group, founded in 1957, and is part of the listed Morgan Sindall Group.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence design and engineering** - integrated design, project management and compliance services for secure and regulated defence environments.
- **Digital asset and configuration management** - asset and configuration record management, laser scan-to-BIM and AR/VR modelling for defence infrastructure.
- **Continuous at Sea Deterrent support** - work supporting the UK submarine-based nuclear deterrent programme.
