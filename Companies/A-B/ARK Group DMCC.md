# ARK Group DMCC

> Social enterprise delivering stabilisation, security and capacity-building programmes in fragile and conflict-affected states.

## What they do

ARK Group is a social enterprise working at the intersection of governance, security and community resilience, delivering stabilisation, development and capacity-building programmes in fragile and conflict-affected states. Its work covers research and analysis, programme design and management, capacity development, and monitoring and evaluation for governments, donors and local partners, with delivery from Haiti and Lebanon to Syria, Yemen and Nigeria. ARK is headquartered in Dubai; the UK entity (registered as a foreign company, FC035638) supports UK-based staff and client compliance. ARK is an ADS Group member, listing defence consultancy, counter-terrorism and intelligence, security consultancy, surveillance and tactical training among its capabilities.

## Products and solutions

- **Stabilisation and capacity building** - programme design and delivery in fragile and conflict-affected states.
- **Defence and security consultancy** - defence consultancy, counter-terrorism and intelligence, and security consultancy services.
- **Training** - covert and counter-terror, security, systems and tactical training.
- **Research, monitoring and evaluation** - research and analysis and programme monitoring and evaluation.
