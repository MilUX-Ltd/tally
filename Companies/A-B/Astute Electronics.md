# Astute Electronics

> Franchised high-reliability electronic component distribution and supply-chain management for the defence and aerospace markets.

## What they do

Astute Electronics, trading as Astute Group, distributes franchised electronic components and provides global procurement, component engineering, managed supply chains and anti-counterfeit testing. The company traces its work with the defence industry to 1989 and supports defence, aerospace and other mission-critical sectors. It is headquartered in the UK with offices across five continents.

## Products and solutions

- **Franchised component distribution** - connectors, semiconductors, passives, power and electromechanical lines from manufacturers serving defence and aerospace.
- **Supply-chain management** - cradle-to-grave component support across the product life cycle, including obsolescence management.
- **Counterfeit detection** - component testing through AS6171-certified laboratories.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS6171-certified component test laboratories; ISO 9001.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
