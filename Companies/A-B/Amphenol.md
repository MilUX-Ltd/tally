# Amphenol

> UK manufacturer of high-performance interconnect systems and connectors for military aircraft, naval, ground, space and soldier-system applications.

## What they do

Amphenol Limited designs, manufactures and supplies connectors and cable assemblies for harsh-environment defence and aerospace applications. Its products cover circular and rectangular connectors, hermetic and fibre-optic connectors, cable assemblies and EMI/EMP filter connectors. The UK business is based in Whitstable, Kent, and is part of the US-listed Amphenol Corporation.

## Products and solutions

- **MIL-DTL-38999 circular connectors** - standard military circular interconnect range.
- **Rhino and Raptor series** - high-power and high-voltage connectors for vehicle and electric-flight applications.
- **Databus connectors** - MIL-STD-1553B data transmission interconnect.
- **Fibre-optic connectors and cable assemblies** - high-speed data interconnect for airborne and ground systems.
