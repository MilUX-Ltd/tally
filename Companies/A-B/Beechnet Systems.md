# Beechnet Systems

> Sub-system design consultancy providing electronics, FPGA and software design for defence and commercial clients.

## What they do

Beechnet Systems is a sub-system design consultancy that provides electronics design, FPGA design and software design. It works with clients ranging from large defence companies to small commercial firms, supporting work from initial concept through prototype to full-scale production. The company is based in Silsoe, Bedfordshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Electronics and FPGA design** - design of experimental electronic sub-systems.
- **Software design** - software development supporting sub-system work.
- **Prototype to production** - support from early prototype through to production.
