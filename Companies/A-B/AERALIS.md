# AERALIS

> UK developer of a modular family of jet aircraft built around reconfigurable common airframes.

## What they do

AERALIS designs a family of modular jet aircraft built around a common core fuselage with interchangeable wing and engine modules, intended to be reconfigured to cover roles from basic and advanced jet training through to aerobatics, display and light combat. The company opened its first site in Bristol and has assembled an industry supply base around the programme.

## Products and solutions

- **AERALIS Advanced Jet Trainer (AJT)** - the initial variant of the modular family, an advanced jet trainer reconfigurable across multiple training and light-combat roles through common modules.

## Recent news

- **2026-05-14** - AERALIS entered administration after its main financial backer withdrew funding, with administrators citing sustained cash-flow pressure linked to delays to the UK Defence Investment Plan.
