# Alton Valley

> Designer and manager of mission-critical security, network and application systems for demanding UK environments.

## What they do

Alton Valley designs, builds and manages security, network and application systems for defence and critical infrastructure. Founded in Derby in 2009, the company provides end-to-end technology from physical security to cloud. It reports a decade-long partnership with a UK engineering and manufacturing client and holds security-vetted staff.

## Products and solutions

- **Network installations** - network infrastructure for secure and classified environments, from survey to handover.
- **Security and access control** - access control, CCTV and visitor management for high-security facilities.
- **Application development** - web applications, workflow automation and system integrations.
- **Managed services and support** - monitoring, incident response and ongoing support across infrastructure.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
