# Blue Bear Systems Research

> Developer of uncrewed systems and autonomy for land, air and maritime, now part of Saab.

## What they do

Blue Bear develops uncrewed systems and autonomy across land, air and maritime domains, with experience in fixed-wing, rotary and lighter-than-air vehicles. The company has delivered UK and international firsts in unmanned flight, including the deconfliction of a large UAV swarm in 2018. It was acquired by Saab UK in August 2023 and operates as a centre for rapid concept development within the Saab group; the wider Saab parent is headquartered in Sweden.

## Products and solutions

- **Smart Connect** - inter-swarm autonomy device that tasks multiple uncrewed systems for collaborative multi-domain missions.
- **Centurion Mission System** - mission system technology used in swarming drone operations.
- **Autonomy and open architecture** - certifiable autonomy and open architecture work underpinning the RAF's 216 Squadron swarming drones.
