# Allen-Vanguard

> Develops electronic countermeasure systems to defeat radio-controlled improvised explosive devices and small unmanned aerial systems.

## What they do

Allen-Vanguard designs and manufactures electronic countermeasure (ECM) equipment for defeating radio-controlled improvised explosive devices (RCIEDs) and the threat from small drones. The company supports bomb disposal technicians, armed forces, and protective security teams operating in high-threat environments. It is based in Tewkesbury, Gloucestershire, and is owned by US private equity firm Versa Capital Management, which acquired the business in 2009.

## Products and solutions

- **EQUINOX NG** - vehicle-based electronic countermeasure system built around electronic warfare hardware standards.
- **SCORPION** - man-portable electronic countermeasure system for protecting personnel from RCIEDs and unmanned aerial systems.
