# BMT

> An international engineering, science and technology consultancy best known for naval design and maritime engineering.

## What they do

BMT Group is a multidisciplinary engineering, science and technology consultancy serving the defence and security, critical infrastructure, commercial shipping and environment sectors. It is a maritime-orientated high-end design house and technical consulting firm, and describes itself as the world's largest independent naval design consultancy. The company employs around 1,350 professionals across offices in the Americas, Asia Pacific and Europe.

## Products and solutions

- **Maritime design and naval architecture** - concept-to-detailed design services for advanced and specialised vessels.
- **Defence design support** - contributions to programmes including the Queen Elizabeth class aircraft carriers, Tide class tankers, Type 31 frigates and the Fleet Solid Support programme.
- **Risk and contract management** - engineering, design support and risk management consultancy.
