# ACLAS Technics

> Aviation service provider offering repair, overhaul and inspection of flight controls and structural components.

## What they do

ACLAS Technics is an aviation service provider offering repair, overhaul and inspection of flight controls, leading edges and structural components, with work focused on ATR, Boeing and Airbus aircraft and serving airlines, OEMs and lessors. The company is based in Livingston, Scotland, and is a signatory to the UK Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Component repair and overhaul** - repair, overhaul and inspection of flight controls, leading edges and structural components.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR
- **Accreditations:** EASA / FAA / TCCA Part 145 component repair organisation
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
