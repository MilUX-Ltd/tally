# APH10

> Software supply chain security company helping organisations identify and manage risks from complex and potentially compromised software supply chains.

## What they do

APH10 is a UK software supply chain security company, registered as APH10 Limited. The company addresses the growing threat of software supply chain attacks, giving organisations the tooling and advice to manage complex dependency chains and identify vulnerabilities before they are exploited. Its work centres on Software Bill of Materials (SBOM), helping organisations understand the components of their software and the risks attached to them. APH10 operates within the D3IP community at the Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.

## Products and solutions

- **SBOMLens** - product for managing and reviewing Software Bills of Materials.
- **SBOM consulting** - developing an SBOM programme to understand software components and associated risks.
- **Assessments and audits** - reviews of secure development and Secure-by-Design processes, using the OWASP SAMM framework to assess development process maturity.
- **Training** - training in the use and benefits of SBOMs, secure software development and the use of open source software.
- **Design and implementation review** - review of software designs and implementations for security vulnerabilities, with additional cyber security services such as training, audit and penetration testing offered through partners.
