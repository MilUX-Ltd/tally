# APH10
> Software supply chain security platform helping organisations identify and manage risks from complex and potentially compromised software supply chains.

## What they do
APH10 is a UK software supply chain security company. The platform addresses the growing threat of software supply chain attacks, giving organisations the tooling to manage complex dependency chains and identify vulnerabilities before they are exploited. APH10 operates within the D3IP community at the Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
