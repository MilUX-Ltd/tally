# Aquark Technologies

> Southampton quantum-technology company building miniaturised, deployable cold-atom hardware.

## What they do

Aquark Technologies, a spin-out associated with the University of Southampton, develops miniaturised cold-atom quantum hardware for sensing and timing. Its technology laser-cools atoms to near absolute zero, packaged small enough to be portable and deployable. The company targets accuracy, precision and stability for critical operations across defence and industry.

## Products and solutions

- **AQlock** - described by the company as the first commercially available cold-atom atomic clock designed and built in the UK.
- **Cold-atom quantum sensing hardware** - deployable cold-atom systems for navigation, timing and sensing applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Programmes:** awarded an Innovate UK contract to develop a high-performance cold atom clock.
