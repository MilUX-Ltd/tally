# Bruntons Aero Products

> Manufacturer of machined components and mechanical cable assemblies for the aerospace supply chain, including flight control cables, helicopter winch cables and runway arrester pendants.

## What they do

Bruntons Aero Products is a specialised manufacturer of machined components and mechanical cable assemblies, supplying direct to all levels of the aerospace supply chain across prototype, serial production and spares. It is a supplier of control cables and lanyards for the aviation industry and also supports the vintage aircraft market with streamline wires. The company operates from a facility in Musselburgh, near Edinburgh.

## Products and solutions

- **Aircraft control cables** - mechanical control cables from 1mm to 10mm diameter, flight control system cables, release mechanism cables and retaining cords.
- **Helicopter winch cables** - cable assemblies for helicopter winch systems.
- **Runway arrester pendants** - cross runway arrester ropes and pendants from 22mm to 32mm diameter for military and naval airfield arresting systems.
- **Machined components and lanyards** - machined parts, lanyards and streamline wires.
