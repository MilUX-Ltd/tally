# Booz Allen Hamilton International (UK)

> Management and technology consulting for defence, intelligence and government.

## What they do

Booz Allen Hamilton provides management and technology consulting in defence, intelligence and civil markets. Booz Allen Hamilton International (U.K.) Ltd is the UK entity of the US firm, registered in Huntingdon and engaged in management consultancy. The wider firm works in areas including digital, data, cyber and analytics for government and defence customers.
