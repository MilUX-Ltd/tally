# Aerossurance

> Aberdeen-based aviation consultancy helping organisations procure and assure aviation services across civil and military operations.

## What they do

Aerossurance is an aviation consultancy founded in 2014 and based in Aberdeen, Scotland, supporting customers globally. It helps organisations procure aviation services by identifying requirements, developing technical specifications, conducting market analyses, preparing tenders and draft contracts, and evaluating bids. It helps organisations assure aviation services through reviews, audits, investigations and supplier contract management. The company has worldwide experience across fixed wing, rotary wing and UAS/RPAS operations, onshore and offshore, civil and military.

## Products and solutions

- **Procurement support** - aviation requirements definition, technical specifications, tendering and contract evaluation.
- **Assurance** - aviation reviews, audits, accident investigation and supplier contract management.
- **Specialist advisory** - airworthiness, certification, safety and risk management, expert witness work, and support to civil and military regulators.
