# AirTanker Services

> Operator of the Royal Air Force's Voyager air-to-air refuelling and transport fleet.

## What they do

AirTanker Services operates the RAF's fleet of Airbus A330 Multi-Role Tanker Transport aircraft, in RAF service known as Voyager. Voyager is a dedicated air-to-air refuelling platform that also operates in air transport and aeromedical evacuation roles. In 2008 the AirTanker consortium signed a 27-year contract with the Ministry of Defence to deliver and support 14 MRTT aircraft under the Future Strategic Tanker Aircraft (FSTA) programme. AirTanker's responsibilities span aircraft delivery, crew training, maintenance and dispatch.

## Products and solutions

- **Voyager fleet operation** - operation and support of 14 A330 MRTT aircraft for the RAF.
- **Military flying** - delivery of air-to-air refuelling and air transport capability.
- **Maintenance** - end-to-end aircraft maintenance.
- **Training** - aircrew and ground crew training.
- **Civil flying and leasing** - non-core aircraft made available to the civil leasing market.

Nine aircraft form a core fleet for the RAF; the five non-core aircraft are available to the RAF and can also be offered to other parties, including civil leasing.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** Future Strategic Tanker Aircraft (FSTA) PFI contract with the MoD
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
