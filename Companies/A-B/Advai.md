# Advai

> UK deep-tech specialist in AI assurance, testing AI and machine-learning systems for robustness, safety and security.

## What they do

Advai is a UK deep-tech company focused on AI safety and security. It tests and evaluates artificial intelligence and machine-learning systems, including large language models and computer vision, so customers can assure them for deployment in business-critical or regulated environments. Its tooling stress-tests AI to find reliable operating boundaries and build early warning systems for natural or adversarial failure. From defence origins, the company now also serves regulated commercial sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Advai platform** - testing, compliance assurance and continuous monitoring across the AI lifecycle, covering performance, security, safety, robustness, auditability and stability, producing go-live thresholds and evidence packs for deployment sign-off.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Programmes:** funded through the Defence and Security Accelerator (DASA) from 2020 across multiple projects.
