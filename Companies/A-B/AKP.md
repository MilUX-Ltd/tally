# AKP

> Sub-contract precision engineering company supplying machined components to aerospace and defence.

## What they do

AKP is a sub-contract manufacturer of precision component parts, offering CNC milling, turning and multi-axis machining for component sizes from around 1mm to 150mm. It serves sectors including aerospace, defence, subsea, oil and gas, medical, motorsport and marine. For aerospace it supplies precision components for applications such as avionic connectors, systems and actuation, and commercial aircraft seating.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC milling and turning** - precision machined components to tight tolerances.
- **Multi-axis and sliding head machining** - complex component manufacture.
- **Manual machining** - additional machining services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015, AS9100D
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Made in Britain
