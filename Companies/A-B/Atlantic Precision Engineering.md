# Atlantic Precision Engineering

> Precision machining and assembly supplier producing close-toleranced components for the aerospace and defence industry.

## What they do

Atlantic Precision Engineering manufactures high-precision, close-toleranced machined components and assemblies in metals, plastics and composite materials for the aerospace and defence industry. It provides CNC machining, including 5-axis work, and mechanical and bonded assembly, holding approvals from major aerospace and defence contractors.

## Products and solutions

- **5-axis CNC machining** - machining of metallic, plastic and composite components on palletised 5-axis cells.
- **CNC milling and turning** - high-speed milling and turning.
- **Plastics and composites machining** - a dedicated CNC cell for plastic and composite parts.
- **Assembly** - mechanical and bonded assembly of components and sub-assemblies, including Airbus assembly approval.
