# AYR Orbis

> Logistics and operational support provider for civil protection and defence, including unmanned systems for ISTAR, cargo and ground support.

## What they do

AYR Orbis Limited provides logistics and operational support across humanitarian, peacekeeping, disaster relief and contingency operations, working with governments, NGOs, UN agencies and multinational organisations. The company states it has worked across 47 countries and has expanded into unmanned technologies for civil protection and defence. Its work spans uncrewed systems for Intelligence, Surveillance, Target Acquisition and Reconnaissance (ISTAR), heavy-lift cargo transport, and unmanned ground vehicle support, supported by in-house research and development and verification and validation activity. The company has previously traded as AYR Aviation, AYR Group and AYR Logistics.

## Products and solutions

- **Integrated logistics support** - turnkey logistics and operational support for missions in austere environments.
- **Unmanned ISTAR** - uncrewed systems for intelligence, surveillance, target acquisition and reconnaissance.
- **Unmanned cargo and ground support** - heavy-lift cargo UAS and all-weather, all-terrain unmanned ground vehicle support.
- **UAS training and consultancy** - certification pathways, integrated training and instruction for VLOS and BVLOS operations.
