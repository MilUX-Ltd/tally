# Advanced Logistics for Aerospace (UK) Limited

> UK arm of ALA Group, an Italy-headquartered supply chain integrator and distributor to the aerospace and defence sector.

## What they do

Advanced Logistics for Aerospace (UK) Limited is the UK entity of ALA Group (A.L.A. S.p.A.), an aerospace and defence supply chain integrator headquartered in Naples, Italy, with offices and operations across Italy, France, Germany, the United Kingdom, Israel and North America. The group describes itself as a single-source partner for the management and distribution of products, services and engineering solutions to aerospace and defence manufacturers, covering both production and aftermarket phases of programmes. The UK company is registered in Esher, Surrey, and was incorporated in 1988 under the former name Specialist Technologies Ltd.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace and defence supply chain integration** - distribution and management of hardware, components and engineered products for aerospace and defence OEMs and operators.
- **Production and aftermarket logistics** - supply chain services spanning production support, spare parts distribution, inventory management and in-service support.
- **Defence-sector product supply** - per the ADS member listing, product manufacturing and supply across defence-relevant categories including bomb disposal and explosive search equipment, CBRN and decontamination, asset tracking, replenishment-at-sea, search and rescue, and weapons storage.
