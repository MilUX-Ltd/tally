# Advanced Tactical Resources Limited

> Specialist protection and risk management provider delivering training, security services and covert ISR equipment to defence, law enforcement and government.

## What they do

ATR provides specialist protection and risk management services, focused on training solutions, security strategies, services and specialist equipment. Its Military and Government Division supplies capabilities to defence, law enforcement and government agencies, including support to sensitive ISR and technical surveillance operations. The company also delivers regulated security qualifications, close protection, conflict management and first aid training.

## Products and solutions

- **Covert ISR equipment** - concealment systems, cameras, lenses and imaging components, GPS tracking, covert audio and video recording platforms, sensor and trigger technologies, transmission and specialist communications, and custom power solutions for extended deployments.
- **Technical surveillance training** - hands-on programmes covering technical surveillance operator digital fabrication, mimicry and concealment, and technical operations, tailored to ISR and low-visibility missions.
- **Technical services** - bespoke hide fabrication, technical system integration, product development and engineering support, and consultancy for ISR and covert operational requirements.
- **Security and resilience training** - HEAT, CONDO, close protection, physical intervention, conflict management, trauma first aid and regulated qualifications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001
- **Memberships:** ADS Group. Signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant.
