# ARX Robotics UK Ltd

> UK arm of European defence-technology firm ARX Robotics, building software-defined uncrewed ground vehicles.

## What they do

ARX Robotics develops software-defined uncrewed ground vehicles (UGVs) and the operating software that runs them. ARX Robotics UK Ltd is the UK entity, incorporated in 2025, established to manufacture autonomous ground systems in the United Kingdom with a London headquarters and a production facility in south-west England. The wider group describes its platforms as modular and mission-ready, designed to integrate with existing military systems.

## Products and solutions

- **GEREON uncrewed ground vehicle** - modular UGV manufactured in the UK, configurable for reconnaissance, logistics support, route clearance, casualty evacuation and force protection, and able to carry intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance payloads.
- **Mithra OS** - the company's operating software for managing and integrating uncrewed systems.
