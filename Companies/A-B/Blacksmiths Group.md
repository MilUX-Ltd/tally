# Blacksmiths Group

> Defence and security specialists providing consultancy and technology for insider risk, threats and cyber security.

## What they do

Blacksmiths is a team of physical and digital security specialists based in the UK and operating globally. The company provides consultancy and develops technology to help clients manage security challenges and defend against complex threats. Its teams hold skills acquired in UK intelligence, military and industry.

## Products and solutions

- **Threats** - assessment of threat actors, intent and capability, vulnerability and risk.
- **Insider risk** - insider threat, behaviours and cultures, and personnel security.
- **Cyber** - operational technology security, technical security and penetration testing.
- **Security management** - risk management, security governance and leadership advisory.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
