# AAG DS

> Aircraft maintenance, repair and overhaul provider for the Royal Air Force A400M Atlas fleet, trading as AAG Defence Services.

## What they do

AAG Defence Services is a Military Aviation Authority Part 145 Approved Maintenance Organisation based at RAF Brize Norton. It works in partnership with Airbus to supply maintenance, repair and overhaul services, including heavy and line maintenance, to the Royal Air Force fleet of A400M Atlas aircraft. The team operates from a three-bay facility designed for A400M maintenance. AAG Defence Services is a subsidiary of Atlantic Aviation Group, headquartered at Shannon in Ireland.

## Products and solutions

- **A400M MRO** - heavy and line maintenance for the Royal Air Force A400M Atlas fleet at RAF Brize Norton.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Military Aviation Authority Part 145 Approved Maintenance Organisation.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
