# Belcan Engineering Services UK

> UK arm of Belcan, a global engineering and technical services supplier to aerospace, defence and space.

## What they do

Belcan supplies design, software, manufacturing, supply chain, information technology and digital engineering services to the aerospace, defence, space and other markets. The UK entity, Belcan Engineering Services UK Limited, is registered in Scotland and operates from sites including Derby and Filton, Bristol. The group is headquartered in the United States and was acquired by Cognizant in August 2024.

## Products and solutions

- **Engineering design** - full-lifecycle design, component engineering and systems integration.
- **Digital engineering** - embedded software, mechanical, electrical and systems engineering services.
- **MRO support** - maintenance, repair and overhaul services.
