# AVPE Systems

> Manufacturer of complex machined components and sub-assemblies for civil and defence aerospace.

## What they do

AVPE Systems, based in Bitton near Bristol, supplies complex machined components and sub-assemblies to aerospace OEM and Tier 1 customers across civil, defence and aftermarket sectors. It specialises in airframe and landing gear parts and the machining of castings, forgings and 3D printed parts, supplying flight-critical parts for aircraft including the A320, A350 and A220, as well as helicopters and military aircraft. AVPE Systems is part of Broadway Group, formed in 2021 to bring together Broadway Engineering and AVPE Systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Complex machining** - CNC milling, turning, grinding and thread rolling.
- **Sub-assembly and supply chain management** - for serial production and MRO programmes.
