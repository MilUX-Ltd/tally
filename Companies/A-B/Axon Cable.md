# Axon' Cable Limited

> UK subsidiary of the Axon' Cable group, a vertically integrated manufacturer of wire, cable, connectors and cable assemblies for defence, aerospace and space.

## What they do

Axon' Cable is a vertically integrated manufacturer of high-performance interconnect solutions for defence, military aerospace and space applications, marking 60 years of the group in 2025. The group is headquartered in Montmirail, France, with a UK presence through its Rosyth site in Fife. The UK operation employs over 50 people and carries out prototyping, engineering development and small to medium scale manufacturing. Group strengths include fluoropolymer insulated and flat cable, complex Micro and Nano-D harnessing, RF and EMI shielding and Mil-1553B databus looms.

## Products and solutions

- **Cables and wire** - fluoropolymer insulated and flat cable constructions.
- **Connectors and harnesses** - complex Micro-D and Nano-D harnessing and cable assemblies.
- **Interconnect for mission-critical systems** - RF and EMI shielding and Mil-1553B databus looms, from polymer extrusion to clean-room assembly.
