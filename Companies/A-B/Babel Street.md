# Babel Street

> Risk intelligence software for defence, intelligence, law enforcement and regulated industries.

## What they do

Babel Street, founded in 2009, provides AI-driven risk intelligence used by defence, intelligence, law enforcement and regulated organisations. Its platform ingests records across languages, domains and regions to support identity and vendor vetting, situational awareness and threat intelligence, with entity resolution, risk scoring, event detection and network mapping. The company is headquartered in the Washington, D.C. area, with offices including London, Canberra and Ottawa; its UK presence operates as Babel Street Limited. The UK entity is registered under the former Basis Technology Ltd name, the multilingual text analytics and digital forensics business Babel Street acquired.

## Products and solutions

- **Babel Street Insights** - threat intelligence, identity and risk analysis across multilingual open and licensed data.
- **Identity and vendor risk intelligence** - vetting of identities and vendor networks.
- **Strategic threat intelligence** - situational awareness and threat detection for government and defence.
- **Rosette text analytics** - multilingual entity extraction, name matching and translation for identity resolution.
