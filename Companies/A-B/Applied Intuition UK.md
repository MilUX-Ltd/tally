# Applied Intuition UK

> UK subsidiary of Applied Intuition, providing autonomy software and simulation tooling for defence platforms across air, land, sea and space.

## What they do

Applied Intuition UK is the UK arm of Applied Intuition, a vehicle intelligence company headquartered in Mountain View, California. The UK subsidiary was established to deliver autonomy management and development systems for existing and next-generation defence platforms. It describes itself as a sovereign UK subsidiary working across air, space, land and sea domains.

## Products and solutions

- **Axion** - mission-critical toolchain for delivering and operating collaborative autonomy in contested environments, including the Axion Sim simulation product.
- **Acuity** - autonomy software deployed directly onto the vehicle.
