# ATL Turbine Services

> Independent gas turbine component repair, overhaul and coating specialist serving civil aerospace, defence aerospace, marine and industrial turbine sectors.

## What they do

ATL Turbine Services is one of the UK's independent gas turbine component repair organisations, specialising in the refurbishment and repair of hot-section gas turbine components and the application of high-temperature coatings. It delivers bespoke engineering and repair solutions across the industrial, civil aerospace, defence aerospace and marine turbine sectors, operating around the clock. The company describes itself as a specialist in late-lifecycle turbines.

## Products and solutions

- **Component repair and overhaul** - repair of rotary and static hot-section gas turbine parts in super-alloy materials to OEM specification.
- **Coatings** - thermal spray, gas-phase aluminizing and high-temperature protective coatings; investment in an Oerlikon Metco Surface Two thermal spray system.
- **Defence aerospace MRO** - repair of the RB199 engine (Panavia Tornado) and support for the EJ200 engine (Eurofighter Typhoon).
- **Defence marine** - support for the Rolls-Royce WR-21 gas turbine powering the Royal Navy's Type 45 destroyers.
- **Laboratory, NDT and engineering services** - assessment, non-destructive testing, ultrasonic cleaning and laser powder fusion.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** EASA/FAA Part 145, AS 9110C, ISO 9001, NADCAP (NDT); Rolls-Royce approved special processes (SABRe 3).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
