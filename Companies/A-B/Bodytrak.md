# Inova Design Solutions Ltd (trading as Bodytrak)

> In-ear wearable that provides real-time physiological monitoring, including core body temperature and heart rate, for workplace and operational safety.

## What they do

Bodytrak is a wearable safety solution built around an in-ear earpiece with multiple sensors that capture real-time physiological data, analysed by AI on the device and in the cloud. The ear's proximity to the hypothalamus allows the device to measure core body temperature and heart rate, with reported mean absolute error of 0.18C against the gastro-intestinal pill at elevated temperatures. The company markets the solution across industrial, emergency services, defence and scientific or research sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Bodytrak 1** - in-ear wearable for real-time physiological monitoring.
- **Bodytrak 1 IS** - intrinsically safe variant for hazardous environments.
- **Smart Safety Solution** - platform covering heat stress monitoring, fatigue monitoring, lone worker safety, musculoskeletal disorder prevention, geolocation and geofencing, alerts and automated reporting.
