# Antillion

> Defence and security product and services company designing deployable, ruggedised edge-computing systems.

## What they do

Antillion is a product and services company based in Bristol, focused on the defence and security sectors. It designs edge-computing solutions that bring data-centre performance to defence, first-responder and security teams operating in challenging environments. The company has in-house mechanical and software design and build capability and integrates its systems into customer platforms. Antillion has over a decade of experience supporting NATO and UK MOD programmes, with UK offices in Bristol, Aylesbury and Tring and a US presence in Herndon, Virginia.

## Products and solutions

- **PACE FRONTIER** - purpose-built rugged edge compute platform for tactical field use, with a modular, shock-resistant design for secure high-performance computing in extreme environments.
- **PACE AIR** - edge computing platform for high-performance data processing tailored to specific operational contexts.
- **Deployable ruggedised systems** - tactical cloud and edge orchestration capabilities, including Azure Stack HCI deployments and automation.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Partnerships:** systems integrator for Everfox cross-domain and high-assurance cyber security capabilities.
