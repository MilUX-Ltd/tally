# boxxe Limited

> UK IT hardware, software, and managed services provider with over 30 years of active MOD engagement and a dedicated defence solutions practice.

## What they do

boxxe supplies IT hardware, software licensing, cybersecurity, and managed services to UK public sector and corporate customers. Its defence practice covers Microsoft Enterprise Licensing, secure cloud and virtualisation infrastructure, ruggedised device procurement and configuration, and encryption solutions for classified and operational environments. The company operates from a MOD-accredited facility and holds Microsoft Large Authorised Reseller status.

## Products and solutions

- **Microsoft Enterprise Licensing and Licence Management Platform** - manages and optimises Microsoft EA compliance and spend for MOD environments; underpinned the £291 million MOD Microsoft agreement (2022-2025).
- **Secure data encryption** - Stonewood Flagstone Enhanced Data Encryption deployed on Royal Navy submarine laptops at HMNB Clyde and HMNB Devonport.
- **Ruggedised device procurement** - configured portable devices and software for the British Army's Multiple Launch Rocket System Electronic Data Library programme.
- **Cloud and virtualisation infrastructure** - supply and support for OPNET, CAMTES, and related defence programme environments.
