# Arc Additive

> Scottish supplier of large-scale wire-arc additive manufacturing (WAAM) for metal parts, including aerospace and defence applications.

## What they do

Arc Additive industrialises large-scale metal 3D printing using wire-arc additive manufacturing (WAAM). The company runs a factory in Livingston, West Lothian, and prints in superalloys and critical metals including titanium, Inconels, stainless steels and aluminium. Its stated focus is certified metal part production that reduces lead times and reliance on global supply chains in sectors including aerospace and defence.

## Products and solutions

- **WAAM part production** - large-scale directed-energy-deposition (DED) wire-arc additive manufacture of metal components.
- **AML3D ARCEMY distribution** - appointed in 2025 as a non-exclusive UK and Europe distributor for AML3D's ARCEMY wire-arc metal additive manufacturing systems.
