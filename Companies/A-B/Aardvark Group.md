# The Aardvark Group

> Designer and manufacturer of mine clearance systems and protected mobility vehicles.

## What they do

The Aardvark Group has been designing and manufacturing mine clearance systems since 1982, with in-house design and testing capabilities. Its work covers protected mobility and the clearance of explosive ordnance. Aardvark machines have been used by armed forces, the United Nations, non-governmental organisations and charities for humanitarian mine clearance in Europe, Africa, the Middle East and the Far East. The company is based in Dumfries, Scotland.

## Products and solutions

- **AMCS mine flail system** - armoured flail vehicle for landmine clearance, in production since 1984 and used in more than 30 countries.
