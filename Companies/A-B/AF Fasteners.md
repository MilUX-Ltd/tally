# AF Fasteners

> Precision fastener manufacturer for the aerospace and defence industries, based in Glossop, Derbyshire.

## What they do

AF Fasteners is a fastener manufacturer dedicated to the global aerospace industry, with over 50 years of experience in high-performance fastener manufacturing. The company manufactures precision systems in house and is approved to supply leading aerospace companies. It offers over 2,500 products, including licensed production of the Phillips Screw Company drive systems range. AF Fasteners is part of the FMT Group.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace fasteners** - in-house manufactured precision fastener systems, including alloy steel bolts.
- **Licensed drive systems** - production of the Phillips Screw Company range.
- **CNC components** - precision machined components and PTFE fasteners.
