# Bifrost Defence

> Copenhagen-based defence venture studio that co-founds and invests in hardware-focused defence technology companies.

## What they do

Bifrost Defence is a venture studio operating within the Bifrost Studios ecosystem, founded in 2025. Rather than following a traditional venture-capital model, it actively co-founds and builds companies from the ground up, providing operational teams, go-to-market strategy, legal, finance and fundraising support. It partners with founders, engineers, military personnel and researchers to turn early-stage ideas into scalable, field-tested defence products. Stated areas of interest include unmanned ground vehicles, unmanned surface vehicles, rocketry, infrastructure, energy, communications and defence AI.

## Products and solutions

- **Venture studio model** - co-founds and builds hardware-focused, mission-critical defence technology companies, with comprehensive operational and investment support.
- **Portfolio** - portfolio ventures include Thor's Haven, which provides towable, combat-proven infrastructure deployed in Ukraine as trauma care centres, command posts or drone manufacturing facilities.
