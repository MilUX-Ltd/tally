# Aragorn Systems

> Resilience solutions for defence and security clients, focused on infrastructure security and resilience decision-making.

## What they do

Aragorn Systems is a UK registered, veteran-owned small and medium enterprise providing end-to-end resilience solutions for defence and security clients operating in conventional, asymmetric and multi-domain environments. Its focus is on offensive, protective security and resilience decision-making involving infrastructure, helping clients understand, exploit and protect critical system vulnerabilities. The company is recognised under the UK Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Infrastructure system-of-systems analysis** - data capture, analysis and visualisation of different infrastructure types to support offensive and protective security decision-making, including digitisation.
