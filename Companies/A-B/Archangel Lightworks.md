# Archangel Lightworks Ltd

> Laser communications systems linking satellites and ground networks.

## What they do

Archangel Lightworks develops free-space optical (laser) communication systems that carry high-volume, secure data between space and the ground. The company positions optical links as an alternative to radio frequency connectivity, aimed at cloud providers, network operators and governments.

## Products and solutions

- **TERRA-M OGS** - a deployable optical ground station providing secure, high-volume connectivity to and from space.
