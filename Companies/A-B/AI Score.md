# AI Score

> AI governance and risk management platform for enterprises managing regulated AI workloads.

## What they do

AI Score is a London-based startup providing a modular, cloud-native platform that gives enterprises visibility and control across their AI estate. It connects third-party SaaS products, models, risks and outcomes into a single view, delivering real-time insight, intelligent scoring and automated safeguards. The platform is described by the company as a control plane for enterprise AI governance, aligning AI usage with policy and covering visibility, compliance, performance analytics and risk management in one layer. The platform is not defence-specific but is relevant to defence buyers managing regulated AI workloads. AI Score was built within GALLOS Technologies, a venture studio for security, defence and resilience companies, and its advisory board includes a former GCHQ director. The company secured around 864,000 euros (roughly 1 million US dollars) in pre-seed funding, announced in November 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **AI Score platform** - model-agnostic AI governance dashboard covering risk scoring, compliance monitoring and automated safeguards across an organisation's AI estate.
