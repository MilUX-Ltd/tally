# AI Score
> AI governance and risk management platform for enterprises managing regulated AI workloads.
## What they do
AI Score is a London-based startup providing a modular, cloud-native platform that gives enterprises visibility and control across their AI estate. It connects third-party SaaS products, models, risks and outcomes into a single view, delivering real-time insight, intelligent scoring and automated safeguards. The platform is not defence-specific but is relevant to defence buyers managing regulated AI workloads. The company secured around 864k euros in pre-seed funding in November 2025.
## Products and solutions
- **AI Score platform** - Model-agnostic AI governance dashboard covering risk scoring, compliance monitoring and automated safeguards across an organisation's AI estate.
