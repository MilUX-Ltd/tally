# ASL (Aerospace Logistics)

> Supplier, source and repairer of military aircraft, vehicle and marine spare parts and components.

## What they do

Aerospace Logistics Ltd (ASL) supplies, sources and repairs equipment and components to keep platforms operational, with through-life support. It specialises in military defence products, with expertise in UK and US rotary and fixed-wing platforms, ground support and maintenance bay equipment. ASL works with overseas air forces, navies and defence organisations, including NATO and the UK MOD.

## Products and solutions

- **Air** - supply, stocking and repair of aerospace equipment and components for platform serviceability.
- **Sea** - fleet equipment maintenance for the marine industry to end of life.
- **Land** - repair of military vehicle, power generation and mass transit equipment.
- **Repair and overhaul** - service, maintenance, repair, overhaul and manufacture across product ranges.
