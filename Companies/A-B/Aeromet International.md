# Aeromet International

> Supplier of aluminium and magnesium castings and finished cast parts for the aerospace and defence industries.

## What they do

Aeromet International is a supplier of aluminium and magnesium castings and fully finished, assembly-ready cast parts for aerospace and defence customers. It has over 40 years of casting experience and operates three UK sites. It supplies aerospace and defence OEMs and supply-chain companies including Airbus, Boeing, Rolls-Royce, Safran and Embraer.

## Products and solutions

- **Sand castings** - aluminium and magnesium sand castings.
- **Investment castings** - aluminium investment castings by conventional and SOPHIA processes.
- **SOPHIA high-property investment castings** - a high-property casting process.
- **A20X** - an aluminium casting alloy developed by Aeromet.
- Cast components include avionic chassis and housings, escape doors, aero-engine heat exchangers, air intakes, structural castings and transmission castings.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Nadcap (reported as the first foundry to achieve Nadcap Metallic Materials Manufacturing accreditation); ISO 9001
