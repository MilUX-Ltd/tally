# Axis Electronics Limited

> Electronic manufacturing services provider specialising in high-reliability, high-mix electronics for the defence and aerospace sectors.

## What they do

Axis Electronics was formed in 1995 as a UK contract electronic manufacturing services (EMS) provider. It specialises in the design, manufacture and testing of high-technology, high-mix, low-to-medium volume electronic products for defence and aerospace, alongside security, automotive, oil and gas and industrial customers. The Bedford facility is MOD approved for handling protected information and applies cyber security controls aligned to the Defence Cyber Protection Partnership. Axis was acquired by Cicor Group in November 2021 and operates within Cicor's group of EMS companies.

## Products and solutions

- **Contract electronics manufacturing** - high-reliability, complex PCBA and system assembly and test.
- **Design and test services** - electronics design, manufacture and testing to defence and aerospace workmanship standards (IPC-A-610 Class 3, J-STD-001 Class 3).

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR.
- **Accreditations:** AS9100 (including ISO 9001), ISO 27001, Cyber Essentials Plus; SC21 award winner; MOD approved for handling protected information.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Space UK.
