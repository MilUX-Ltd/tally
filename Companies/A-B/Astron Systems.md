# Astron Systems

> Developer of fully reusable small-lift launch vehicles for responsive access to orbit.

## What they do

Astron Systems is developing fully reusable small launch vehicles to provide low-cost, responsive access to orbit for small payloads. Its work includes long-lifetime rocket engines and reusable upper stages aimed at cutting the cost of small launch. The company works with defence partners on responsive space capabilities, including an agreement to launch a spacecraft with responsive UK-based tasking, and is based in Didcot, Oxfordshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Aurora** - a fully reusable small launch vehicle designed to sharply reduce small-rocket operating costs, with reusable engines and upper stage.
