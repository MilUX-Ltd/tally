# Butser Rubber

> Custom rubber moulding manufacturer supplying components for the aerospace, defence and industrial sectors.

## What they do

Butser Rubber manufactures custom rubber mouldings, O rings, gaskets and rubber-bonded components from its site in Liss, Hampshire. It has supplied the aerospace and defence sectors for over 30 years, including direct supply to the UK Ministry of Defence and private defence companies in the UK, the United States and Europe. Components are produced to commercial, aerospace and defence specifications including DTD, AMS, Def Stan and BS.

## Products and solutions

- **Custom rubber mouldings** - bespoke moulded rubber and silicone components.
- **Gaskets and O rings** - in-house gasket manufacture using rubbers, silicones and sponge materials.
- **Rubber-bonded components** - rubber-to-metal bonded parts.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** components manufactured to Defence Standards (Def Stan), DTD, AMS and BS specifications.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
