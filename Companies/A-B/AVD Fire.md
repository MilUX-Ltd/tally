# AVD Fire

> UK manufacturer of lithium-ion battery fire containment and suppression products.

## What they do

AVD Fire develops and produces active and passive safety products to control and extinguish lithium-ion battery fires. The company uses Aqueous Vermiculite Dispersion (AVD), an extinguishing agent that cools, contains and extinguishes lithium battery fires. It is a subsidiary of Goodwin PLC. Its products are used across applications including electric vehicles, energy storage, consumer electronics, government facilities and defence systems.

## Products and solutions

- **AVD lithium battery fire extinguishers** - portable extinguishers using the Aqueous Vermiculite Dispersion agent.
- **Fixed suppression systems** - installed systems for lithium-ion battery fire containment.
- **Fire blankets** - specialist blankets for lithium battery fires.
