# Blackdot Solutions

> UK software company behind Videris, an open source intelligence platform for investigators and analysts.

## What they do

Blackdot Solutions develops Videris, an investigations platform that helps teams collect, analyse and visualise open source data. The company began developing Videris in 2014 to help organisations use internet data in the fight against crime. Its customers include government agencies, law enforcement and financial institutions.

## Products and solutions

- **Videris** - OSINT investigations platform that brings collection, analysis and reporting into one place, searching corporate registries, publicly available social media, and surface, deep and dark web sources.
- **Videris Automate** - adds AI capabilities to automate investigations and screening processes.
