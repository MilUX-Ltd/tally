# Airnesco Group

> Designer and manufacturer of pneumatic barrel cleaning systems for military weapon platforms.

## What they do

Airnesco designs and manufactures specialist compressed air products for bore cleaning applications, supplying air tools and associated equipment to industrial and military markets. The company's weapon care equipment cleans rifled and smooth barrels, mortars and launchers from 12.7mm upwards. From its head office in Kent it works with agents, distributors and licensees internationally.

## Products and solutions

- **Barrel Cleaning System (BCS)** - pneumatically powered system for cleaning smooth and rifled barrels, mortars and launchers of calibre 12.7mm and above.
- **Small Gauge BCS** - for weapon platforms with a calibre between 12.7mm and 40mm.
- **Large Gauge BCS** - for weapon platforms with a calibre between 57mm and 203mm, with larger sizes available.

The Airnesco BCS entered UK MoD trials in the late 1990s and was accepted into service with the Royal Navy and British Army by 2000. It is also used by other armed forces internationally.
