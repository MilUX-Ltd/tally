# ABP Technologies

> UK manufacturer of EOD disruptors, remotely operated vehicles, and maritime systems, with products in continuous MOD service since 1966.

## What they do

ABP Technologies designs, manufactures, and provides in-service support for explosive ordnance disposal equipment, remotely operated ground vehicles, and maritime platform systems. Founded as a primary MOD contractor in 1966 and trading from Poole, Dorset, the company has sustained that customer relationship for over fifty years. Products are used by British bomb disposal teams, UK police, emergency services, and international security agencies. The company also provides bespoke engineering design and prototyping, and manages Contractor Logistics Support and Contracting for Availability arrangements, including obsolescence management and in-service modification kits.

## Products and solutions

- **Needle / Needle Plus Disruptor** - precision EOD disruptor for smaller IEDs; in active service with UK MOD.
- **Pigstick / Pigstick Kit** - thin-walled IED disruption system; over thirty years in MOD service.
- **Hotrod / Hotrod Lightweight Disruptor** - EOD disruptor for heavier-walled packages and suitcases.
- **ABL2000 Recoilless Disruptor and De-armer Kit** - designed for medium IEDs and conventional munitions disposal.
- **EOAB Kit** - Explosive Ordnance Assault Breaching kit.
- **ROV/UGV platforms** - ground-based remotely operated vehicles for EOD, military, and security applications.
- **Maritime systems** - platform management consoles, water-cooled cabinets, combat management equipment, and communication systems for surface ships and submarines.
