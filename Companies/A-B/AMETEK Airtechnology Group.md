# AMETEK Airtechnology Group

> Designer and manufacturer of thermal management and motion control products for aerospace and defence platforms.

## What they do

AMETEK Airtechnology Group is a design-to-manufacture business specialising in custom, build-to-specification thermal management and motion control products for the aerospace, defence, oil and gas, and rail industries. It develops and manufactures high-integrity equipment and systems across multiple specialist facilities. It is part of AMETEK Inc., a US-listed corporation.

## Products and solutions

- **Thermal management** - active and passive cooling, vapour cycle systems, environmental control and air-conditioning, engine and gearbox cooling, heaters and fan heaters.
- **Motion control** - high-performance motors, generators, alternators, electric and direct-drive fans, and advanced electro-mechanical actuators.
- **Sensors** - angular speed and position sensors.
