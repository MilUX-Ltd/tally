# Agilent Technologies LDA UK

> UK arm of Agilent's Raman spectroscopy business, formerly Cobalt Light Systems, making security and material-identification instruments.

## What they do

Agilent Technologies LDA UK is the UK entity behind the former Cobalt Light Systems, a Harwell spin-out acquired by Agilent Technologies in 2017. It develops and manufactures Raman spectroscopy instruments that identify chemicals and materials through opaque barriers and sealed containers. Applications span aviation security screening, hazardous-material and chemical-warfare-agent identification, and pharmaceutical quality control. The parent, Agilent Technologies, is a US-listed company headquartered in Santa Clara, California; this UK entity is registered in Cheadle, Cheshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Insight200M** - liquid explosive detection system (LEDS) for aviation security, screening common container types for liquid explosives.
- **Resolve** - handheld Raman analyser for rapid identification of explosives, narcotics, toxic industrial chemicals and chemical warfare agents through sealed, opaque containers.
