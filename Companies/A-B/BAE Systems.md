# BAE Systems

> Britain's largest defence company, supplying air, land and naval platforms, electronics, cyber and security services worldwide.

## What they do

BAE Systems plc is a British multinational aerospace, defence and security company headquartered in London. It is the largest defence contractor in Europe. Its products and services span air, land and naval forces, alongside advanced electronics, security, information technology and support services. The company employs around 93,500 people worldwide.

## Products and solutions

- **Type 26 frigates** - City Class anti-submarine warfare frigates built in Glasgow for the Royal Navy and export customers.
- **Combat aircraft** - including the Eurofighter Typhoon and the Tempest future combat air programme.
- **Combat vehicles and munitions** - armoured vehicles, artillery and ammunition for land forces.
- **Electronic systems and cyber** - avionics, electronic warfare, and cyber and intelligence services.
