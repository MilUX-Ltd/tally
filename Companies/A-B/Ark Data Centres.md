# Ark Data Centres

> UK operator of secure colocation data centres serving government, defence and enterprise customers.

## What they do

Ark Data Centres operates secure, energy-efficient colocation campuses across the UK, with sites including Corsham in Wiltshire and Longcross in Surrey. It is the joint venture partner with the Cabinet Office in Crown Hosting Data Centres, which hosts public sector workloads. Ark facilities used for government and defence work are built to physical security and ISO 27001 information security standards. Ark is an ADS Group member.

## Products and solutions

- **Colocation** - secure rack and hall colocation across UK campuses.
- **Crown Hosting** - joint venture with the Cabinet Office providing colocation to the UK public sector.
- **High-density and AI-ready capacity** - campus expansions configured for higher-density and AI workloads.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001
