# AVRT

> Free-roam wireless VR training system for military, law enforcement, and emergency services, requiring no controllers and supporting up to sixteen concurrent users.

## What they do

AVRT develops fully immersive, wireless free-roam VR training systems. Trainees use natural movement and real-world replica weapons with no handheld controllers, in shared virtual environments accommodating up to sixteen concurrent users. The system is instructor-configurable for scenarios including lethal and less-lethal force, CBRN, FIBUA, vehicle interdiction, counter-terrorism, and counter-drone (CUAS/C-sUAS). AVRT is a product line of KIM Software Solutions Limited, which holds supply relationships with Home Office agencies including UK Border Force, HMRC, and the NCA, as well as the Metropolitan Police Service.

## Products and solutions

- **AVRT Military System v3** - large-area free-roam VR for Army collective training; demonstrated at 400 sq m with 12 concurrent soldiers; supported by the UK Export Support Team.
- **AVRT Law Enforcement System** - scenario-based VR for police Taser and use-of-force training.
- **Counter-drone (CUAS/C-sUAS) training** - counter-UAS scenario training within the AVRT free-roam VR system.
