# Brigantes Consulting Ltd

> Specialist tactical and adventure-training equipment procurement and distribution for defence.

## What they do

Brigantes is an on-the-man tactical apparel and equipment procurement specialist, supplying soldier systems to NATO military and elite police units. The company was conceived to bridge the gap between the outdoor and defence industries. It develops product ranges through alliances with specialist brands and distributes equipment across operational theatres.

## Products and solutions

- **Tactical apparel and equipment** - on-the-man soldier systems and procurement for military and elite police units.
- **Exclusive distribution** - UK and European distributor for OR (Outdoor Research) tactical products.
- **Summit Tactical** - the company's outdoor and tactical equipment retail operation, based in Exeter, serving military, veterans and blue-light personnel.
- **Winter warfare equipment** - supply of snowshoes and crampons for winter warfare training, including Snowmotion M22 snowshoes and Grivel G12 crampons.
