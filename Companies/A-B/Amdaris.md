# Amdaris
> Global technology company delivering bespoke software development, AI and data engineering services to UK defence and security customers.
## What they do
Amdaris delivers digital transformation through bespoke software solutions tailored to complex business needs. Core services cover software development via security-vetted extended teams, technical and product consultancy, and application support, with specialist capabilities in AI, data engineering, cloud and DevOps, product design (UX/UI) and product management. The firm is headquartered in Bristol and is a member of ADS Group.
## Products and solutions
- **Extended development teams** - Security-vetted engineers augmenting customer delivery capability.
- **Digital transformation consultancy** - Technical, business and product advisory.
- **Application support** - Ongoing support for deployed software products.
