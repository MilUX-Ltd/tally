# Anschuetz UK

> UK naval navigation subsidiary of the German Anschütz group, supporting and integrating Royal Navy bridge and navigation systems.

## What they do

Anschuetz UK provides in-service support to Warship Integrated Navigation and Bridge Systems in the Royal Navy, and integrates navigation and surveillance systems in the UK. It is based at Portsdown Technology Park overlooking Portsmouth Harbour, positioned for waterfront response to ships at Portsmouth Naval Base and deploying engineers UK-wide. It is the UK entity of the Anschütz group, headquartered in Kiel, Germany, and ultimately owned by DMB Holding following the group's separation from Raytheon in 2023.

## Products and solutions

- **Warship Integrated Navigation and Bridge Systems** - design, integration and in-service support for Royal Navy warships including Type 45, Type 26 and Type 31.
- **SYNAPSIS NAVAL** - integrated navigation system being installed on the Type 26 (HMS Glasgow).
- **SMARTBLUE** - modular surveillance system for coastal surveillance and waterway management.
- **Gyrocompasses and bridge systems** - parent group navigation portfolio including ECDIS and steering control.
