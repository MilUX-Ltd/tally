# BSE Engineering Group

> Systems engineering and project governance consultancy supporting defence, aerospace and infrastructure programmes.

## What they do

BSE Engineering Group provides systems engineering, verification and validation, and project governance services. It positions itself as the bridge between consultancy and project ownership, taking ownership of complex engineering projects to support on-time delivery, working across defence, aerospace and infrastructure. The company was incorporated in September 2024 and is registered in Leighton Buzzard, Bedfordshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems engineering and design** - system architecture, compartmentalisation strategies and scalable design evolution across the project lifecycle.
- **Verification and validation** - V&V services applying a V-Model approach to confirm functional and environmental compliance against requirements.
- **Project governance** - full project lifecycle oversight and governance intended to mitigate risk and support on-time delivery.
