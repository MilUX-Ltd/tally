# BSE Engineering Group

> Systems engineering and project governance consultancy supporting defence, aerospace and infrastructure programmes.

## What they do

BSE Engineering Group provides systems engineering, verification and validation, and project governance services. It positions itself as taking ownership of complex engineering projects to support on-time delivery, working across defence, aerospace and infrastructure. The company was incorporated in September 2024 and is registered in Leighton Buzzard, Bedfordshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems engineering** - engineering support across the project lifecycle.
- **Verification and validation** - V&V services for complex systems.
- **Project governance** - programme governance and assurance.
