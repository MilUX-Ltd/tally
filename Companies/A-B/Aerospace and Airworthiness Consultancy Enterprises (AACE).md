# Aerospace & Airworthiness Consultancy Enterprises (AACE)

> Independent consultancy in system safety engineering and airworthiness for defence.

## What they do

AACE is a family-owned, independent SME providing technical and engineering advice across the land, sea and air domains to the Ministry of Defence and the wider defence enterprise. It specialises in system safety engineering, airworthiness assessments, logistics and operational assurance, with personnel experienced as operators, maintainers and regulators. The company has experience spanning the design, build, operation and in-service support of systems.

## Products and solutions

- **System safety engineering** - safety assessment and assurance across defence platforms.
- **Airworthiness and integrity management** - independent airworthiness assessment and certification support across military and civil environments.
- **Compliance audits and technical evaluations** - including policy development and RPAS airspace integration.
