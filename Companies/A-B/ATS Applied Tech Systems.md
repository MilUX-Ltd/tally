# ATS Applied Tech Systems

> System integrator delivering smart manufacturing, automation and quality software, with a dedicated aerospace and defence practice.

## What they do

ATS Applied Tech Systems is the UK operating entity of the ATS Global group, an independent solution provider for digital transformation in manufacturing. It provides products, solutions and services across the automation pyramid, covering control, execution and information layers. The group dates to 1986 and supports manufacturers across aerospace, defence and other sectors with automation, IT/OT integration, manufacturing execution and quality management.

The UK company is based in Nuneaton, Warwickshire; the ATS Global group is headquartered in Haarlem, Netherlands, with offices across multiple countries.

## Products and solutions

- **Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES/MOM)** - execution-layer manufacturing software and operations management.
- **Automation and IT** - industrial automation and IT/OT convergence, including equipment connectivity.
- **Smart manufacturing and Industry 4.0** - IoT, location-awareness devices and predictive data analytics.
- **PLM and quality management** - product lifecycle management integration and quality management systems.
- **Supply chain management** - advanced planning and scheduling.
- **Aerospace and defence practice** - digital transformation solutions for aerospace and defence OEMs and tier suppliers.
