# Advanced Systems Understanding (ASU)

> Employee-owned, veteran-led consultancy specialising in enterprise and systems architecture for defence and the wider public sector.

## What they do

Advanced Systems Understanding (ASU) is an employee-owned, veteran-led consultancy operating at the intersection of systems thinking, strategy and transformation. It works with clients across defence, healthcare, transport and beyond, specialising in enterprise, business and solution architecture and in system-of-systems engineering. Its work analyses systems, processes and delivery models to help organisations evolve with clarity.

## Products and solutions

- **Enterprise and business architecture** - architecture-as-a-service consultancy across strategic domains of expertise.
- **System-of-systems engineering** - tailored engineering and enterprise architecture consultancy for complex defence and public sector programmes.
