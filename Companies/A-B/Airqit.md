# Airqit
> UK UAS and adjacent disciplines engineering company delivering solutions to defence and public safety organisations, with capability in electronics design, RF and mechatronics.

## What they do
Airqit is a UK-based engineering company focused on unmanned aerial systems and adjacent technical disciplines. The company serves defence and public safety customers, drawing on capability in UAS, electronics design, RF engineering and mechatronics. The founder also established Evolve Dynamics, the platform behind the Foxe UAS.
