# Airqit

> UK engineering company working on unmanned aerial systems and adjacent disciplines for defence and public safety customers.

## What they do

Airqit is a UK engineering company incorporated in June 2025, registered in London. It describes its focus as unmanned aerial systems and adjacent technical disciplines, serving defence and public safety customers, with capability spanning electronics design, RF engineering and mechatronics. The company's founder also established Evolve Dynamics, which develops the Foxe and Wolfe uncrewed aerial systems; those platforms are Evolve Dynamics products, not confirmed as Airqit's own.
