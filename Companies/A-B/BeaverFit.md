# BeaverFit

> UK manufacturer of containerised gym systems, outdoor fitness equipment, and special operations training infrastructure for military and defence customers worldwide.

## What they do

BeaverFit has been a leading supplier of container gyms and outdoor fitness solutions to the US and UK militaries since 2010. In 2015 the company launched BeaverFit Special Operations Equipment (SOE) as a dedicated sister capability, supplying bespoke special operations training infrastructure. Customers include the UK MOD, UK Special Forces, Royal Marines, NATO, US Armed Forces, and the Foreign Commonwealth Office. The company has delivered over 350 container gyms and more than 30 SOE projects.

## Products and solutions

- **FOB-Series Container Gyms** - fully functional containerised multi-modality fitness facilities deployable to forward operating bases.
- **BeaverFit SOE Training Towers** - purpose-built special operations training structures.
- **Live Fire Shoot Houses** - modular live-fire training facilities.
- **Urban Climbing Features** - tactical urban environment training infrastructure.
- **Air Delivery Training Stations** - parachute and air insertion training platforms.
- **Outdoor Fitness Equipment** - rigs, racks, and functional training installations for permanent military bases.
