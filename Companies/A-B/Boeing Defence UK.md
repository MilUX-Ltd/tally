# Boeing Defence UK

> UK defence arm of Boeing, supporting Ministry of Defence aircraft and equipment programmes.

## What they do

Boeing Defence UK provides support for Boeing products across UK and US military programmes. It delivers technology, products, services and capabilities to the Ministry of Defence, including in-service support, maintenance, logistics and training. The company operates across sites in the UK, with a presence near MOD Abbey Wood in Bristol and at locations co-located with customers.

## Products and solutions

- **In-service support** - maintenance, technical services and logistical support for UK military rotary and fixed-wing platforms.
- **Training** - aircrew, ground and maintainer training for supported platforms.
