# By Light Technologies

> UK arm of a US defence technology firm providing cyber training, cyber ranges and simulation services.

## What they do

By Light Technologies trains cyber operators, runs custom cyber ranges and provides protection for systems vulnerable to cyber attack, alongside modernised simulation and training services. The UK business, By Light Technologies Limited, was incorporated in February 2024 to support UK sovereign defence requirements, including the Ministry of Defence's training and cyber needs. The parent company is headquartered in McLean, Virginia, United States.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber operator training** - training programmes for cyber operators.
- **Cyber ranges** - custom cyber range environments.
- **Simulation and training** - modernised simulation and training delivery.
