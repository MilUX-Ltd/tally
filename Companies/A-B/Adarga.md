# Adarga

> A British AI software company building information intelligence and decision-advantage tools for defence, security and resilience.

## What they do

Adarga develops artificial intelligence software that helps defence, national security and commercial organisations process and analyse large volumes of structured and unstructured data. Its platform fuses multimodal data from sources such as global news, reports and open-source intelligence to surface decision-ready insight. The company also offers a practitioner-led intelligence service, pairing analysts with its tooling to deliver mission-relevant analysis.

## Products and solutions

- **Catalyst** - multidomain data fusion platform underpinning Adarga's AI-driven solutions, combining deep-learning AI with symbolic reasoning for explainable analysis.
- **Augur** - near real-time alerting and intelligence tool that detects, translates and scores alerts from open-source intelligence and publicly available information; evolved from the earlier Vantage application.
- **Intelligence services** - practitioner-led analysis delivered through a Global Intelligence Centre and embedded analysts.
