# BI Engineering

> Family-owned UK manufacturer of fabrications and assemblies for the defence and off-highway vehicle sectors.

## What they do

B. I. Engineering, the trading name of Bozeat Industrial Limited, has supplied manufactured parts and assemblies to the defence and commercial on- and off-highway sectors since 1975. It offers design, manufacture, supply and fitment of light, heavy and armoured fabrications, and specialises in vehicle engine cooling and heat transfer. It works with OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers, and houses its operations across 185,000 square feet of manufacturing, R&D, bonded stores and distribution facilities in Wellingborough.

## Products and solutions

- **Armoured and heavy fabrications** - light, heavy and armoured fabrications for military and off-highway vehicles.
- **Vehicle engine cooling and heat transfer** - radiators, heat transfer and repair.
- **Military vehicle seating** - in-house upholstery for military vehicle seating.
- **Machining and finishing** - precision machine shop, press shop, paint, phosphating and hot-tin dipping.
