# Arcani Systems

> UK developer of passive acoustic sensing technology for counter-UAS and ground threat detection, selected into the NATO DIANA 2026 Challenge Programme.

## What they do

Arcani Systems develops the HARK (Hostile Acoustic Reconnaissance Kit) sensor system: a passive distributed acoustic array with integrated edge processing for real-time detection, classification, and localisation of aerial and ground-based threats. The system generates no emissions, making it resistant to electronic warfare, and integrates with C2 platforms including ATAK and Lattice. Arcani positions the technology for deployment at fixed sites, forward operating bases, and on vehicles.

## Products and solutions

- **HARK (Hostile Acoustic Reconnaissance Kit)** - passive microphone array with edge processing providing sub-second detection of drones and ground threats; outputs location, azimuth, height, drone model, speed, and direction of travel; deployable as distributed arrays over ultra-low-bandwidth mesh networks.
