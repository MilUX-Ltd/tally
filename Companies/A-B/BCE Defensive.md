# BCE Defensive

> UK manufacturer of ballistic combat equipment and personal protection for military use.

## What they do

BCE Defensive (Ballistic Combat Equipment) is a UK company specialising in the design and manufacture of ballistic and protective equipment for military applications. It emphasises UK manufacturing, design innovation, adaptive and ergonomic features, and the use of advanced materials in its combat protective gear. BCE Defensive Limited is registered in Plymouth and was incorporated in October 2022.

## Products and solutions

- **NG-Serpa** - a military neck protection system designed to guard against fragmentation and shrapnel in combat. The ballistic neck-gaiter has been tested for thermal loading, breathing allowance and general ergonomics.
