# AmSafe Bridport

> Engineered textile manufacturer producing safety, restraint and protection products for the aerospace and defence markets.

## What they do

AmSafe Bridport designs and manufactures engineered textile solutions for aerospace and defence, covering airframe restraints, barrier systems, cargo handling, air cargo covers and vehicle armour protection. It takes products from conceptual design through analysis, testing and delivery, with accreditation from the CAA, FAA and government bodies. The company is headquartered in Bridport, Dorset, and is part of the US-listed TransDigm Group, which acquired the AmSafe business in 2012.

## Products and solutions

- **Cargo restraint** - pallet nets and cargo securement systems, including the Pallet Net Zero range.
- **Cabin safety** - installed physical secondary barrier systems for aircraft.
- **Fire protection** - fire containment products.
- **Defence** - troop and vehicle protection solutions, including RPG protection armour and airframe restraints.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials; CAA and FAA approvals.
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered.
