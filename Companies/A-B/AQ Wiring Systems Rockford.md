# AQ Wiring Systems Rockford

> Manufacturer of cable assemblies, wiring harnesses and system-level electrical equipment for the aerospace, defence and industrial markets.

## What they do

AQ Wiring Systems Rockford designs, manufactures, tests and delivers system-level electrical equipment, from wiring harnesses and electro-mechanical sub-assemblies through to complete systems. It describes itself as the UK's largest independent provider of wiring, interconnect and system solutions for the aerospace, defence and industrial markets. The business has operated since 1978 and runs sites at Rendlesham, Worksop and Salisbury. It was formerly Rockford Components Limited and was acquired by the Swedish AQ Group AB in July 2024.

## Products and solutions

- **Cable assemblies and wiring harnesses** - including hermetically sealed and over-braided harnesses, over-moulded connectors and high-speed USB and ethernet.
- **System-level electricals** - looms, box builds, control panels, racks and electro-mechanical assemblies.
- **Lifecycle services** - design, rapid prototyping, on-site installation, production, logistics, spares and obsolescence management.
