# Amfax

> Test engineering company designing and manufacturing automated test equipment and related hardware for the aerospace and defence sectors.

## What they do

Amfax designs, builds and supports automated test equipment (ATE) and test engineering solutions, alongside electronic, electrical and mechanical engineering services. It provides bespoke cable, harness and wiring loom manufacture and test fixtures. The company is a National Instruments system integrator partner, with LabVIEW and TestStand at the core of its software work.

## Products and solutions

- **Automated test equipment (ATE)** - design and build of PXI-based and custom test systems.
- **Test fixtures, cables and harnesses** - bespoke interconnect and wiring loom manufacture.
- **PCB assembly inspection** - inspection systems supplied through the Amfax A3DI brand.
- **Test software** - LabVIEW and TestStand development, plus the SAM test executive.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100 Rev D; Cyber Essentials; ISO 9001.
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Midlands Aerospace Alliance.
