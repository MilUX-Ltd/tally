# Acuity Risk Management

> Provider of the STREAM cyber governance, risk and compliance software platform.

## What they do

Acuity Risk Management provides cyber governance, risk and compliance (GRC) software that helps organisations identify, assess and manage cyber security, IT and operational risks. Its clients operate in markets including government, defence, broadcasting, utilities, manufacturing and healthcare. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of Acuity RM Group plc, whose shares trade on AIM under the ticker ACRM.

## Products and solutions

- **STREAM** - cyber GRC platform that collects and analyses data to present cyber risk in business terms, available as STREAM Cloud for rapid deployment and STREAM Classic for enterprise-scale modelling.
- **Vendor Management Hub** - supplier and vendor risk management software.
