# Aleph Insights

> Analytical and data science consultancy serving defence, security and intelligence customers.

## What they do

Aleph Insights is an analytical consultancy and data science company founded in 2014 by analysts from the UK Ministry of Defence. It develops analytical, data-driven and AI-based solutions for problems where complexity is high and data quality is low. Since founding it has delivered projects across defence, security, intelligence, insurance and cyber security.

## Products and solutions

- **Data science R&D** - applied research and concept prototyping of analytical and data tools.
- **Analysis and forecasting** - scenario development, probabilistic assessment and decision support.
- **Technology roadmapping and dataset creation** - structured analytical support for technical and policy decisions.
