# Boyce Precision Engineering

> Precision CNC machining of high-specification components for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Boyce Precision Engineering is a family-founded business established in 2006 in Portadown, Northern Ireland, specialising in precision machined components for the aerospace, defence, pharmaceutical, textile, motorsport and food sectors. It machines components in aluminium, hard metals and engineered polymers, and supplies jigs, fixtures and sub-assemblies. The company became employee-owned in May 2024 through an Employee Ownership Trust covering its 27 staff.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision CNC components** - high-specification machined parts in aluminium, hard metals and engineered polymers for aerospace and defence.
- **Jigs, fixtures and sub-assemblies** - tooling and assembled components.
