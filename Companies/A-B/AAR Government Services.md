# AAR Government Services

> Aviation maintenance, repair, overhaul and supply chain services for commercial, defence and government aircraft fleets.

## What they do

AAR is an independent provider of aviation services to the commercial aerospace and government and defence sectors worldwide. It provides maintenance and supply chain programmes, parts distribution, and shelter and sustainment systems to airlines, original equipment manufacturers and governments. The company is headquartered in the Chicago area and operates across multiple countries, including a presence in the United Kingdom. AAR is listed on the New York Stock Exchange.

## Products and solutions

- **MRO services** - airframe maintenance, repair and overhaul for commercial, defence and government fleets.
- **Supply chain and parts** - parts distribution and supply chain programmes for aircraft operators.
- **Shelter and sustainment systems** - mobility and sustainment products for government and defence customers.
