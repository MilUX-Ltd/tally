# Alloyed

> Designs advanced metal alloys and additive manufacturing solutions for aerospace, space, and high-technology applications.

## What they do

Alloyed Ltd, formerly OxMet Technologies, is a University of Oxford spin-out that develops proprietary metal alloys and end-to-end metal additive manufacturing for aerospace and other demanding sectors. Its Alloys-by-Design platform uses predictive modelling to design alloys that balance properties such as high-temperature strength and printability. The company is based in Yarnton, Oxfordshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Alloys-by-Design (ABD)** - computational platform for designing metal alloys for additive manufacturing.
- **Additive manufacturing** - production of components for air and space applications, including jet-engine parts and satellite components.
