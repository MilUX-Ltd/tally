# Aluminium Special Products

> Supplier of bespoke aluminium raw materials and machined components, with aerospace and defence as a focus.

## What they do

Aluminium Special Products (ASP) supplies raw materials and customer-designed components in aluminium, serving aerospace, defence and automotive customers. Material forms cover extrusion, tube, wire, bar, strip, sheet and plate, supported by in-house precision cutting, drilling, tapping, punching, notching, pressing, CNC machining and surface finishing.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace and defence components** - including ejector seat guide rails, rocket motor bodies, aircraft tie rods, wing struts and seat assemblies for platforms such as the C-17, F-35, Eurofighter Typhoon and UH-60M Black Hawk.
- **Raw material supply** - tube, special extrusions, bar, sheet and plate.
- **Product manufacture** - precision cut-to-length and machined components.
