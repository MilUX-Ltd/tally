# Altair Engineering

> Simulation, high performance computing and AI software, with applications in defence and aerospace.

## What they do

Altair provides software and cloud solutions in simulation, high performance computing, data analytics and artificial intelligence. Its defence and aerospace work covers computer-aided engineering, digital twin technology, maintenance and reliability analysis, and radar and radio coverage analysis. Altair Engineering Ltd is the UK entity, with an office in Royal Leamington Spa, Warwickshire. The group is headquartered in Troy, Michigan, United States, and was acquired by Siemens in 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **Simulation and CAE software** - design, analysis and optimisation tools used across aerospace and defence engineering.
- **HPC and data analytics** - high performance computing and data science tools, including digital twin and predictive maintenance applications.
