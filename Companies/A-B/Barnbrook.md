# Barnbrook Systems Limited

> UK aerospace, defence, maritime and rail engineering firm designing and manufacturing electro-mechanical switching and safety-critical systems, with MRO services and IIoT asset-monitoring technology.

## What they do

Barnbrook designs, manufactures and supports control systems, relays and contactors, and pressure transducers and systems for the aerospace, aviation, defence, maritime, rail and commercial sectors. It provides Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul (MRO) to extend the life of older defence assets such as fighter aircraft and warships, and is building Parts Manufacturer Approval (PMA) capability. It also develops IIoT remote-sensing technology for real-time asset monitoring. The company is based in Fareham, Hampshire, with offices in Virginia, USA, and Prague, Czech Republic.

## Products and solutions

- **Relays, contactors and circuit breakers** - electromechanical and solid-state.
- **Pressure transducers and systems, power controllers, engine control units and solenoids.**
- **Actuators and test sets** - flight and fire-suppression actuators and bespoke test sets.
- **Rail braking controller systems.**
- **Intelligent in-flight refuelling switch** - uses BlueCube sensing to detect pressure changes and cut pumps to prevent spills.
- **BlueCube** - an IIoT remote-sensing and tracking system monitoring temperature, pressure, humidity, GPS location, current and movement across assets such as trains, boats and aircraft.
- **E:BAG** - a lithium-ion battery fire-suppression bag.
- **MRO and PMA services** for aerospace and defence.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials, AS9100D (EN 9100:2018), ISO 9001, CAA Part 21-G, CAA Part 145, FAA FAR 145, Gulfstream UK145 approval, SIL2 (IEC 61508-2:2010).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Farnborough Aerospace Consortium (founding member), International Institute of Obsolescence Management, Make UK Defence. Armed Forces Covenant signatory.
