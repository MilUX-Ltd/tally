# Augur

> AI-powered sensor intelligence platform that turns existing camera and IoT infrastructure into real-time threat detection and situational awareness for critical sites and grey-zone threat scenarios.

## What they do

Augur connects to cameras and sensors already installed at transport hubs, energy infrastructure, stadiums, and other sensitive sites. Machine learning models detect anomalous behaviours, link activity across multiple locations, and reconstruct incident timelines in seconds. The platform operates on anonymised behavioural and movement patterns without facial recognition, and is framed around grey-zone threats including sabotage and sub-threshold adversarial activity against critical infrastructure.

## Products and solutions

- **Sensor Intelligence Platform** - AI-driven threat detection and situational awareness across existing multi-site sensor networks; no facial recognition.
