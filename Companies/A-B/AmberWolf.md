# AmberWolf

> Cyber security consultancy providing threat-led red teaming and penetration testing.

## What they do

AmberWolf provides technical security services and risk management consultancy through attack simulation. The company specialises in threat-led red teaming and penetration testing, with experience of frameworks including the Bank of England's CBEST scheme and the government GBEST scheme. It is incorporated in Harrogate, North Yorkshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Red teaming** - threat-led adversary simulation, including CBEST and GBEST engagements.
- **Penetration testing** - technical security testing across organisations.
- **Risk management consultancy** - advisory on security and resilience.
