# Actica Consulting

> Independent, UK-owned technology and management consultancy supporting public sector, defence and national security programmes.

## What they do

Actica Consulting is an independent, UK-owned technology and management consultancy based at Surrey Research Park, Guildford. It supports complex, high-security and mission-critical digital transformation programmes across the UK public sector, bringing architecture, data, cyber, transformation and engineering specialisms. The company has experience in defence, police, central and local government, and specialises in supporting the acquisition of ICT solutions, working across both waterfall and agile delivery methodologies.

## Products and solutions

- **Architecture and engineering consultancy** - enterprise and solution architecture support across defence and government programmes from inception to closedown.
- **Cyber security consultancy** - NCSC-assured services in risk management and security architecture.
- **Data and transformation services** - advisory support for data and digital transformation programmes.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** NCSC Assured Cyber Security Consultancy (Risk Management and Security Architecture).
