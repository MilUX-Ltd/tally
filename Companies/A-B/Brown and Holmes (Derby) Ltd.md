# Brown and Holmes (Derby) Ltd

> Electrical and mechanical engineering for defence, nuclear, naval and aerospace.

## What they do

Brown and Holmes (Derby) provides electrical and mechanical engineering for high-integrity applications across the defence, nuclear, naval and aerospace sectors. Capabilities span control panels, PCB assembly, wiring harnesses, systems integration and through-life support. The business traces an engineering heritage supporting UK defence back to 1939.

## Products and solutions

- **Control panels and wiring harnesses** - build and assembly for safety-critical systems.
- **PCB assembly** - electronic assembly for high-integrity applications.
- **Systems integration and lifecycle support** - integration and through-life support for defence and industrial platforms.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 14001, ISO 45001, AS9100; JOSCAR registered.
