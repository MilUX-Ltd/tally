# Albacom

> Designer and manufacturer of high-voltage power supplies and power electronics for defence and industrial applications.

## What they do

Albacom is a Dundee-based engineering company supplying high-voltage power supplies and power electronics, with complete engineering services covering design, machining, assembly and testing. The company supplies products into military applications in international defence markets and also serves aerospace and industrial sectors. The name combines "Alba", an old word for Scotland, with "com" for the company's microwave background.

## Products and solutions

- **High-voltage power supplies** - engineered for defence and industrial use.
- **Power electronics** - bespoke power electronics products.
- **Engineering services** - mechanical design, sub-contract assembly, CNC machining, lifting and load testing, and vibration and environmental testing.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** IPC certified (IPC-A-610 and related soldering, wiring and inspection standards); BSI
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Scottish Engineering; Lifting Equipment Engineers Association (LEEA)
