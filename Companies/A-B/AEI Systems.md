# AEI Systems

> Designer and manufacturer of medium-calibre cannon and weapon systems for air, land and naval platforms.

## What they do

AEI Systems is a UK defence company specialising in the design, development, test, manufacture, validation and installation of medium-calibre weapons and weapon systems. It has engineering heritage dating to 1961 and works in revolver cannon technology across air, land and naval domains. The company is based in Ascot. Since 2023 it has been a subsidiary of the Turkish defence group Samsun Yurt Savunma (SYS), which acquired a majority stake.

## Products and solutions

- **Medium-calibre cannon** - 20mm, 25mm and 30mm cannon for integration into fast jets, armoured vehicles and naval platforms.
