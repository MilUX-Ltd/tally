# Aerospace Metal Finishers

> Surface treatment and non-destructive testing provider for the aerospace and defence sectors.

## What they do

Aerospace Metal Finishers, based in Ballymena, provides non-destructive testing and surface treatments for the aerospace and defence sectors. The company was established in 2002 and is part of the Dontaur Group. It holds prime contractor and OEM approvals including Bombardier, Boeing, Airbus, Safran Aircelle, Moog, GE Aerospace, GKN Aerospace and Thales Air Defence.

## Products and solutions

- **Chemical processing** - anodising (hard, tartaric sulphuric, chromic acid, sulphuric acid, boric/sulphuric), passivation, conversion coating, titanium pickle and etch.
- **Non-destructive testing (NDT)** - magnetic flaw detection, fluorescent penetrant inspection, hardness and conductivity testing.
- **Blasting and paint** - dry abrasive blasting, dry film lubricant, fuel tank coating, anti-fret and protective paint finishes.
- **In-house laboratory** - salt spray corrosion testing, coating thickness and adhesion testing, chemical analysis.
