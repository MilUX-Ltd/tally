# Arke

> Wiltshire consultancy providing cost engineering, decision support and analysis to UK defence and the wider public sector.

## What they do

Arke is an independent SME consultancy based in Westbury, Wiltshire, working with UK and international public and private sector organisations. It built its reputation in defence cost engineering and evidence-based decision support, and has expanded into business analysis, programme and project management, modelling and simulation, and technology and futures work. The company supports clients across government departments and agencies operating in highly regulated sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Cost engineering and decision support** - independent cost analysis and evidence-based advice for complex defence and technology decisions.
- **Modelling and simulation** - analytical modelling to support strategic options and capability choices.
- **Business analysis and P3M** - portfolio, programme and project management support.
