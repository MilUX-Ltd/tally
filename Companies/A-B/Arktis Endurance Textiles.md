# Arktis Endurance Textiles

> Exeter manufacturer of military and police clothing, tactical vests and load-carrying equipment.

## What they do

Arktis Endurance Textiles manufactures and supplies clothing and equipment for military, police and emergency services from its factory in Exeter, Devon. The Arktis brand was created in 1985 by a former Royal Marine to improve on standard-issue combat uniform. In 2017 the brand was acquired by the directors of County Antrim textiles firm Francis Dinsmore Ltd, who formed Arktis Endurance Textiles Ltd to continue trading from the original Exeter site. The company designs camouflage patterns in-house and counts special forces among its customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Smocks and jackets** - field and combat outer garments.
- **Tactical vests and chest rigs** - load-carrying equipment and MOLLE systems.
- **Trousers, bags and webbing** - clothing and equipment for military and police use.
