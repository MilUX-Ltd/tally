# Astra Terra

> London consultancy in positioning, navigation and timing, space situational awareness and earth observation.

## What they do

Astra Terra works with industry, governments and non-governmental organisations on positioning, navigation and timing (PNT), space applications, geospatial science and earth observation, and security and resilience. Its PNT work covers high-accuracy, high-integrity positioning in urban environments. Its space work covers space safety, satellite tracking, space situational awareness and space traffic management.

## Products and solutions

- **PNT solutions** - high-accuracy positioning for smart mobility, cities and space-based applications.
- **Space situational awareness** - satellite tracking, space safety and space traffic management.
- **Geospatial and earth observation** - geospatial science and asset management.
- **Security and resilience** - resilience architecture assessment and cyber security.
