# Alford Technologies

> Designs and manufactures explosive charge systems for explosive ordnance disposal, counter-IED and breaching.

## What they do

Alford Technologies designs and manufactures user-filled, patented explosive charge container systems used by explosive ordnance disposal (EOD) and counter-IED organisations in military, government and commercial sectors worldwide. Founded in 1985 by Dr Sidney Alford, it supplies explosive solutions for breaching, unexploded ordnance, conventional munitions disposal and improvised explosive device disposal. It also runs Alford Training, delivering counter-IED, EOD and breaching courses at ranges in the South West of England.

## Products and solutions

- **Vulcan** - shaped charge used for bomb disposal and deliberate disruption of unexploded ordnance.
- **Krakatoa** - cutting and shaped charge for demolition and disposal tasks.
- **Pluton** - maritime EOD kit for low-order disposal of underwater unexploded ordnance.
- **REDS** - ROV EOD Deployment System for remote underwater disposal.
