# Bruhn NewTech

> Specialist developer of CBRN knowledge management and warning-and-reporting software for defence and civil authorities.

## What they do

Bruhn NewTech develops chemical, biological, radiological and nuclear (CBRN) knowledge management software, sensor connectivity software, and battle management and command-and-control integration. Its products are used by defence forces and civil authorities in more than 30 countries. The group is headquartered in Søborg, Denmark, and the UK entity is a long-established subsidiary based in Salisbury, Wiltshire. The wider group is majority-owned by Nordic private equity firm Adelis Equity Partners following a 2024 acquisition.

## Products and solutions

- **CBRN-Analysis** - off-the-shelf CBRN information management, hazard prediction and warning and reporting application.
- **CBRNE-Frontline** - companion CBRN warning-and-reporting and hazard-prediction application.
- **SafeVita** - hazard monitoring and reaction planning for non-CBRN specialists.
- **SCIM** - sensor connectivity software that integrates multiple sensor types into a single display and standard CBRN messaging.
- **BRaCIS** - biological, radiological and chemical warning-and-reporting software for which Bruhn NewTech is the OEM to the UK MoD.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group, CBRN-UK, NATO ATP-45(F) standards committee.
