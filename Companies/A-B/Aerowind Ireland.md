# Aerowind Ireland

> Maintenance, repair and overhaul of aviation rotating equipment, based in County Clare, Ireland.

## What they do

Aerowind Ireland Ltd, founded in 1999 and based in Scariff, County Clare, provides maintenance, repair and overhaul (MRO) of aviation rotating equipment including motors and sub-components such as armatures, field coils, rotors and stators, for OEMs and first tier suppliers. Services span electric motor rewinding, repair, surface treatments and manufacturing assembly, with testing and quality assurance on components such as cabin air compressors and nitrogen generating systems. Aerowind is an ADS member; its head office is in Ireland.

## Products and solutions

- **MRO** - repair and overhaul of aviation electric motors, armatures, field coils, rotors and stators.
- **Manufacturing and assembly** - build to print or design and development, plus surface treatments.
- **Test and quality assurance** - testing of critical components including cabin air compressors and nitrogen generating systems.
