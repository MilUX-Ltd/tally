# Aptilium

> Precision engineering and technical services across the automotive, aerospace, marine and defence sectors.

## What they do

Aptilium provides precision-engineered systems and engineering services from concept to turnkey delivery. The company has its roots in Formula 1 and high-performance engineering, and manages the project lifecycle across engineering, logistics, supply chain, quality assurance and low-volume production. It works across the automotive, aerospace, marine and defence sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Engineering and turnkey delivery** - design, prototyping, testing and low-volume production of precision-engineered and electromechanical systems.
