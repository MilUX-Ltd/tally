# Ascent Aerospace

> Provider of tooling, assembly systems and factory automation for the aerospace, defence and space industries.

## What they do

Ascent Aerospace supplies production tooling and automated assembly systems for aircraft manufacturing, serving commercial aerospace, defence, military and space customers. It produces mould and assembly tooling and works with airframers from process engineering through to build and installation. The company is part of the wider AIP Aerospace group, backed by American Industrial Partners.

## Products and solutions

- **Tooling** - mould and assembly tooling, including layup moulds, masters, and trimming and drilling fixtures.
- **Automation and assembly systems** - factory automation and assembly line integration for airframe production.
- **Additive manufacturing** - large-scale additive production of tooling components.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100, ISO 9001.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
