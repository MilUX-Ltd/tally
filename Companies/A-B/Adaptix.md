# Adaptix

> Developer of 3D X-ray imaging technology with non-destructive testing for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Adaptix develops flat-panel 3D X-ray imaging technology that produces 3D images at doses and costs comparable to traditional 2D X-ray. The technology is applied across medical, veterinary and industrial sectors. Through its AdaptixNDE line, the company provides non-destructive testing capability relevant to aerospace and defence manufacturing and maintenance.

## Products and solutions

- **AdaptixNDE** - fast-acquisition, deployable, low-flux 3D X-ray capability for inspecting dry-preform composites, supporting manufacturing inspection, deployable maintenance inspection and through-life digital twins.
- **3D X-ray imaging platform** - flat-panel source technology generating 3D images at low dose and cost.
