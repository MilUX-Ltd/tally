# Bikar Aerospace Metals

> UK supplier of non-ferrous semi-finished metals for aerospace and defence applications.

## What they do

Bikar Aerospace Metals Ltd is the UK arm of the German BIKAR group, supplying semi-finished non-ferrous metals from a Bristol base. It stocks and processes aluminium, copper, brass and bronze, including aerospace aluminium, supplied as plates, sheets, bars, profiles, tubes, rings and precision-cut blanks. UK infrastructure includes two offices, a production facility and an additional warehouse, supporting processing, lead times and supply for aerospace applications. The parent group is headquartered in Bad Berleburg, Germany.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace aluminium** - aluminium grades for aerospace applications.
- **Non-ferrous metals** - copper, brass and bronze in plate, sheet, bar, profile, tube and ring form.
- **Precision-cut components** - precision blanks and cut-to-size parts.
