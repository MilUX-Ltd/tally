# Archangel Imaging

> Developer of AI-powered edge sensing and autonomy for unmanned systems operating in challenging outdoor environments.

## What they do

Archangel Imaging, trading as Archangel Autonomy, develops artificial intelligence solutions for autonomous drones and unmanned systems that operate in demanding outdoor conditions. Its technology supports threat prevention, detection and response, and enables human-machine teaming for military personnel, first responders, law enforcement and rangers. The company is based in Oxford, with an additional presence in the United States.

## Products and solutions

- **Argonaut** - an edge AI camera and drone copilot that detects intruders and threats while minimising power draw and electromagnetic signature.
- **Xnaut** - a navigation and situational awareness system for small unmanned aircraft that provides accurate navigation over long ranges without reliance on vulnerable external infrastructure.
- **Skybase** - AI processing of satellite imagery to extract intelligence.
