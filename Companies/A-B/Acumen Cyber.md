# Acumen Cyber

> Managed security services provider running a 24/7 CREST-accredited Security Operations Centre.

## What they do

Acumen Cyber is a UK-based managed security services provider delivering enterprise-grade cyber defence to organisations of varying sizes. The company operates a 24/7 CREST-accredited Security Operations Centre, with each customer assigned a named SOC client lead. Its work includes threat monitoring, detection and response, drawing on technology partners including CrowdStrike, Elastic, Fortinet and Barracuda.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed SOC** - 24/7 CREST-accredited Security Operations Centre delivering monitoring, detection and incident response.
- **Threat exposure management** - continuous validation of defensive controls using adversary emulation mapped to MITRE ATT&CK.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 27001:2022, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus, CREST-accredited SOC.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
