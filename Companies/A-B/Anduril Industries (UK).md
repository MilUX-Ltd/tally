# Anduril Industries (UK)

> The UK arm of US defence technology company Anduril Industries, developing autonomous systems, drones and surveillance technology.

## What they do

Anduril Industries UK Ltd is the British subsidiary of Anduril Industries, a US defence technology company that develops drones, autonomous weapons systems, surveillance technology and robotics. The UK operation houses engineering and business development teams and supports Anduril's work with UK and European customers, including the Ministry of Defence. The company has stated plans to establish a UK manufacturing and research and development site to act as its European headquarters.

## Products and solutions

- **Autonomous platforms** - drones and autonomous systems for defence applications, including work under the British Army's Project NYX.
- **Surveillance systems** - autonomous sentry and surveillance towers.
