# Avantis Performance Ltd

> Defence-focused consultancy in digital transformation, capability delivery and workforce scaling for secure and public sector organisations.

## What they do

Avantis Performance is a consultancy supporting digital transformation, capability delivery and workforce scaling for secure and public sector organisations. Its specialisms include requirements management, cost modelling, and project and programme controls and management. The team has combined experience in the defence sector working with digital technology and engineering organisations, SMEs, large technology firms and the public sector.

## Products and solutions

- **Project and programme controls** - controls, transformation and project management for defence and secure-sector programmes.
- **Requirements management and cost modelling** - support across the procurement cycle.
- **Test and user trials** - capability delivery support.
