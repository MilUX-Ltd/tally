# ALTEN

> Engineering and IT services consultancy supporting aerospace, space and defence clients in the UK.

## What they do

ALTEN provides engineering and technology consulting services across aerospace, defence, security and naval, automotive, energy, rail and IT. Its UK operation works with manufacturers and equipment suppliers on engineering, industrial methods, integrated logistics support and digital transformation. The UK business is part of the ALTEN Group, headquartered in France, with several hundred engineers across the UK.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace engineering** - aerostructures, cabin, system design (avionics, electrics, landing gear, propulsion), software development, testing, and certification and airworthiness processes.
- **Defence, security and naval engineering** - engineering and technology consulting for defence and naval programmes.
- **Engineering and IT services** - mechanical and materials, embedded and application software, cybersecurity, data management, artificial intelligence, systems engineering, quality assurance and manufacturing support.
