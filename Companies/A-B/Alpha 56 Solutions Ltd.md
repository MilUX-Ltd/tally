# Alpha 56 Solutions Ltd

> Veteran-owned tactical communications and TAK integration specialist delivering deployable C2 architectures, MANET radio integration, and specialist training to UK defence and government.

## What they do

Alpha 56 Solutions delivers tactical communications systems, end-to-end system integration, and specialist training built around the Android Tactical Assault Kit (ATAK) ecosystem. The company serves dismounted, mounted, and headquarters roles, providing complete deployable communication architectures combining MANET mesh radios, SATCOM, LTE connectivity, drone integration, and ruggedised compute hardware. Alpha 56 describes itself as the UK lead TAK training subject-matter expert and a single point of supply for tactical and strategic communications, from system integration through to through-life support.

## Products and solutions

- **Dismounted Tactical Systems** - body-worn and OEM radio modules for dismounted personnel supporting live video, ISR feeds, and C2 data on the move.
- **Mounted and HQ Tactical Systems** - deployable C2 systems integrating LTE, TAK, and existing communications infrastructure for vehicles and command posts.
- **Silvus MANET Radios** - official UK supplier and integrator of Silvus StreamCaster radios into TAK-centric architectures.
- **ATAK and TAK Training** - specialist training in the Android and Windows Tactical Assault Kit ecosystem.
- **SATCOM Integration** - agnostic SATCOM and radio connectivity for global coverage at the tactical edge.
- **INVISIO Tactical Audio** - reseller and integrator of INVISIO tactical headsets and hearing protection.
