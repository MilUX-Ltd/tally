# Ark Integrated Solutions

> Northern Ireland systems integrator providing engineered solutions to the security and defence sectors.

## What they do

Ark Integrated Solutions provides engineered solutions to the security and defence sectors, with a main focus on personnel protection systems in custodial environments. It delivers and supports passive cyber security monitoring systems for IP and legacy serial networks across OT and IT, and provides IoT edge monitoring to a secure platform for environmental monitoring and location services. System integration forms a large part of its delivery, working with end clients on bespoke systems. The company is based in Ballynahinch, Northern Ireland, and is an ADS Group member.

## Products and solutions

- **Personnel protection systems** - protection systems for custodial and secure environments.
- **Passive cyber security monitoring** - monitoring for IP and legacy serial networks across OT and IT.
- **IoT edge monitoring** - edge devices feeding a secure platform for environmental monitoring and location services.
- **Systems integration** - bespoke integration of security and building systems.
