# ATL Europe

> Aircraft design and certification, including modifications and repairs for military and civil aircraft.

## What they do

ATL Europe is an aircraft design and certification provider. It holds EASA Part 21 Subpart J Design Organisation Approval and UK Military Aviation Authority DAOS approval, with a scope covering aircraft structural, cabin interior, electrical and avionics design. Its design approval certification extends to military aircraft and airborne equipment, and to modifications and repairs for fixed and rotary wing aircraft. The company is registered at Bournemouth Airport, Christchurch, Dorset, and is part of the KDC Group following an acquisition in late 2022.

## Products and solutions

- **Design modifications** - changes to aircraft, systems and equipment in avionic, hydro-mechanical and structural fields, including TCAS, EGPWS and RVSM installations.
- **Certification and approvals** - management of certification processes under EASA Part 21J, UK MOD DAOS and ISO 9001:2015, with a catalogue of ready-to-implement DOA solutions and Supplemental Type Certificates.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** EASA Part 21 Subpart J Design Organisation Approval; UK MAA DAOS; ISO 9001:2015.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
