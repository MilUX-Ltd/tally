# A2i

> Defence technology platform that aggregates SME and startup capabilities to deliver integrated solutions to governments and military customers across Europe and NATO.

## What they do

A2i (Allied Adaptive Industries) is a defence technology company that sources technologies from the SME and startup ecosystem and bundles them into end-to-end solutions for government and military customers, taking on delivery responsibility. The company operates a procurement marketplace and offers subscription services for digital procurement tools and support. It is backed by Notion Capital and 1991 VC, and was founded by a former Royal Marines officer.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence Procurement Marketplace** - platform connecting startups, SMEs, and primes to government procurement opportunities.
- **Integrated Capability Delivery** - bundled defence products and services from the SME ecosystem, delivered under government contracts.
- **Subscription Support Services** - tiered subscriptions providing digital procurement tools and advisory support.
