# Advanex Europe

> Precision engineering company manufacturing springs, wire forms, deep drawn parts and metal inserts for aerospace and other sectors.

## What they do

Advanex Europe is the European arm of the Advanex Group, a precision engineering business. It designs and manufactures springs (tension, compression and torsion), wire forms, precision pressings and stampings, deep drawn parts, coil thread inserts and metal inserts for composite materials. The company supplies aerospace OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers across civil and defence programmes, using processes including CNC coiling, deep drawing, micro-forming and multi-slide technology. It operates from sites at Southwell and Bilborough in Nottinghamshire and a facility in Prague, Czech Republic, as part of the wider Advanex Group, which is headquartered in Japan.

## Products and solutions

- **Springs and wire forms** - tension, compression and torsion springs and custom wire forms.
- **Deep drawn components** - lightweight, high-strength parts for aerospace systems.
- **Coil thread inserts** - tangless coil thread inserts (KATO).
- **Insert mouldings** - precision metal inserts for composite materials.
