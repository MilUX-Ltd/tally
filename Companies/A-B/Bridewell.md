# Bridewell

> Cyber security services provider for critical national infrastructure and regulated sectors.

## What they do

Bridewell is a UK-headquartered cyber security services company protecting business functions across IT, OT and cloud. It delivers end-to-end consulting and 24x7 managed detection and response to organisations operating across critical national infrastructure and other regulated industries. Founded in 2013, it is one of the UK's larger independent cyber security services providers, headquartered in Reading with offices across the UK and USA.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed detection and response (MDR)** - 24x7 security monitoring and response.
- **Penetration testing** - including NCSC CHECK-accredited testing.
- **Cyber threat intelligence** - threat monitoring and intelligence services.
- **Security consultancy** - risk management, audit and review, and security architecture.
- **Cyber incident response** - including NCSC-assured Cyber Incident Response and Cyber Incident Exercising.
- **Data privacy** - compliance-driven advisory.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** CREST (member company); NCSC-assured services including CHECK Penetration Testing, Cyber Incident Response (CIR), Cyber Incident Exercising (CIE), and consultancy in risk management, audit and review, and security architecture; ISO 27001, ISO 27701, ISO 9001.
