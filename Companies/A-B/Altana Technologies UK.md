# Altana Technologies UK

> AI-driven supply chain and value chain intelligence, including defence supply chain resilience.

## What they do

Altana applies artificial intelligence to supply chain data to map supplier and distribution networks. Its platform surfaces companies' legal status, ownership connections, sanctions exposure, supply chain behaviours and overall health. Altana Technologies UK Ltd is the UK entity, registered in London; the group is headquartered in New York, United States. The company is a signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Value Chain Management System** - a network platform that maps extended supplier and distribution chains and powers risk and resilience workflows across them.
