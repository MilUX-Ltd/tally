# Beagle Technology Group

> Designs and manufactures metallic and composite parts, structures and finished components for the aerospace, defence and marine markets.

## What they do

Beagle Technology Group designs and manufactures metallic and composite parts, structures and finished components for the aerospace, defence and marine markets. Metallic, composite, machining and design capabilities are offered in-house from its facility in Christchurch, Dorset. The company traces its origins to Beagle Aircraft and now supplies engineering and manufacturing services across several sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Metallic assemblies and machining** - manufacture of metallic parts and structures.
- **Composite structures** - design and manufacture of composite parts and components.
- **Design and engineering** - in-house design capability supporting build to print and turnkey work.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, AS9100, with supporting NADCAP accreditations.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
