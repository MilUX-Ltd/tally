# Boeing Distribution Services

> Aerospace and defence parts distribution and supply chain management.

## What they do

Boeing Distribution Services distributes aircraft parts and consumables to civil and defence customers in the UK and globally. It carries products from over 250 aviation manufacturers, covering airframe parts, electrical components, hardware, avionics, instruments, and ground support and test equipment. It also provides supply chain management solutions for aerospace and defence customers. The UK operation runs a distribution site at Burgess Hill, West Sussex.

## Products and solutions

- **Parts distribution** - airframe parts, chemicals and consumables, electrical components, hardware, avionics and instruments.
- **Supply chain management** - inventory and distribution solutions for aerospace and defence customers.
