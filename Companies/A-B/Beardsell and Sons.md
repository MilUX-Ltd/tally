# Beardsell & Sons

> Supplier of force protection engineering, battlefield fortifications and hardened structures, trading as B+SL.

## What they do

Beardsell & Sons, trading as B+SL, supplies force protection engineering, battlefield fortifications and hardened structures. The company applies existing and new technology to protective and infrastructure products for the defence sector. It is based at Glasson Industrial Estate, Maryport, Cumbria.

## Products and solutions

- **Universal Battle Trench (UBT)** - hardened fortification supplied as designed or tailored to user requirements, including protection of remote weapon stations and battlefield infrastructure.
- **OG-SPB** - a product within the B+SL portfolio.
- **GapX** - a gap-crossing concept developed for Dstl.
