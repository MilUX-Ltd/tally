# Avon Protection

> Manufacturer of respiratory protection, CBRN and ballistic protection equipment for military and first responders.

## What they do

Avon Protection develops and manufactures respiratory protection system technology and related protective equipment. It has supplied protection solutions to the UK Ministry of Defence and allied forces for many decades and is a supplier of CBRN respiratory equipment to the United States Department of Defense. Its product range covers respirators, CBRN protective wear, escape devices, self-contained breathing apparatus, powered air purifying respirators, thermal imaging and underwater systems. The business is part of Avon Technologies plc, listed on the London Stock Exchange, with its operation based in Melksham, Wiltshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Air purifying respirators** - full face masks including the FM50, FM53, FM54, M50, M53A1, C50 and PC50.
- **CBRN protective wear** - the EXOSKIN range of protective clothing.
- **Self-contained breathing apparatus** - the ST50, ST53 and ST54 systems.
- **Powered air purifying respirators (PAPR)** - including CS-PAPR and MP-PAPR units.
- **Thermal imaging** - the Mi-TIC range of thermal imaging cameras.
- **Underwater systems** - the MCM100 underwater rebreather.
