# Babcock International Group

> A UK aerospace, defence and nuclear engineering services company, supporting the Royal Navy, the wider armed forces and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Babcock International Group plc is a British aerospace, defence and nuclear engineering services company based in London. Although it holds civil contracts, its main business is with public bodies, particularly the UK Ministry of Defence. The company is listed on the London Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the FTSE 100, operating across the UK and overseas in Africa, North America, South America, Europe and Australasia.

## Products and solutions

- **Submarine and naval base support** - sustainment, refit and infrastructure services for the Royal Navy's fleet and nuclear submarines, including work at Devonport.
- **Arrowhead frigate** - the Type 31 / Arrowhead 140 frigate design, built for the Royal Navy and offered for export.
- **Defence training and equipment support** - through-life support, training and engineering services for land, air and naval platforms.
- **Nuclear engineering** - decommissioning and submarine dismantling services.
