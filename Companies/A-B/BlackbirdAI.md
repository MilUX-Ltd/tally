# BlackbirdAI
> AI-driven narrative intelligence platform that identifies disinformation, bot networks and threat actors affecting national security, government and commercial organisations.

## What they do
BLACKBIRD.AI protects organisations from narrative attacks that cause financial, operational and reputational harm. The platform ingests open-source data, identifies key narratives and the actors and networks driving them, detects anomalous bot behaviour scaling them, and maps the communities they connect. The company was founded by a team spanning AI, threat intelligence, journalism and national security. Customer use cases cover strategic communications, brand protection and national security.

## Products and solutions
- **Constellation** - AI narrative intelligence platform providing real-time identification of disinformation campaigns, bot networks, deepfakes and coordinated inauthentic behaviour across open-source channels.
