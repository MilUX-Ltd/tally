# BlackbirdAI

> AI-driven narrative intelligence platform that identifies disinformation, bot networks and threat actors affecting national security, government and commercial organisations.

## What they do

BLACKBIRD.AI protects organisations from narrative attacks that cause financial, operational and reputational harm. The platform ingests open-source data, identifies key narratives and the actors and networks driving them, detects anomalous bot behaviour scaling them, and maps the communities they connect. The company was founded by a team spanning AI, threat intelligence, journalism and national security. Customer use cases cover strategic communications, brand protection and national security, with customers including national security organisations, NATO and major enterprises.

## Products and solutions

- **Constellation** - AI narrative intelligence platform providing real-time identification of disinformation campaigns, bot networks, deepfakes and coordinated inauthentic behaviour across open-source channels.
- **Compass Context** - harmful narrative detection across text and media content.
- **Compass Vision** - detection of deepfake and AI-generated images.
- **RAV3N Watch** - curated narrative research and analyst expertise service.
- **RAV3N Recon** - influencer and threat actor investigation tool.
- **RAV3N Agent** - embedded AI analyst for Constellation workflows.
- **Constellation API** - programmatic integration layer for narrative intelligence data.
