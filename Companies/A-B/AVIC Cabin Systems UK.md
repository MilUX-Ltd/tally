# AVIC Cabin Systems UK

> Designer and manufacturer of aircraft cabin interiors for commercial and military aircraft.

## What they do

AVIC Cabin Systems UK (trading as ACS UK) designs, manufactures, certifies and maintains cabin interiors for major airlines on Airbus and Boeing aircraft. The UK operation is based in Bournemouth and continues programmes previously delivered as AIM Altitude. AIM Altitude has worked on military aircraft programmes including the Eurofighter Typhoon and the Merlin helicopter, and has been a supplier to the Ministry of Defence and BAE Systems. The UK business is part of the AVIC Cabin Systems group, which is owned by Aviation Industry Corporation of China (AVIC), headquartered in Beijing.

## Products and solutions

- **Cabin interiors** - galleys, stowages, bars, lounges, feature panels and premium cabin interior products.
- **Composites** - advanced materials, complex mouldings and decorative finishes with in-house testing capability.
