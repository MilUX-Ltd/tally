# Broder Aerospace

> Distributor and supplier of materials and equipment for air, land and marine commercial and defence requirements.

## What they do

Broder Aerospace supplies and distributes materials and equipment into air, land and marine markets, covering both commercial and defence requirements. Its defence-facing range spans ground support equipment, hose and ducting, onboard power solutions, LED lighting, steel and alloys, and military HVAC systems, alongside managed MRO services and procurement support. Broder Aerospace is the defence and aerospace arm of Broder Metals Group Ltd; it was set up as a separately registered subsidiary in 2020 and is based in Sheffield.

## Products and solutions

- **Ground support equipment** - powered GSE systems for aircraft and platform support.
- **Onboard power and lighting** - onboard power solutions and LED illumination for air, land and marine platforms.
- **Military HVAC** - heating, ventilation and air conditioning systems for military tents and shelters.
- **MRO and procurement** - maintenance, repair and overhaul services and procurement support.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, AS9120.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, West of England Aerospace Forum, North West Aerospace Alliance.
