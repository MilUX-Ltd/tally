# Air Tanker
> Operator of the UK's Future Strategic Tanker Aircraft capability, providing air-to-air refuelling and strategic transport for the RAF using the Airbus A330 MRTT Voyager.

## What they do
AirTanker delivers the RAF's Future Strategic Tanker Aircraft (FSTA) capability under a long-term private finance contract. The company owns, maintains and operates a fleet of 14 Airbus A330 MRTT Voyager aircraft from RAF Brize Norton, providing air-to-air refuelling and strategic transport. AirTanker is owned by a consortium including Airbus, Rolls-Royce, Babcock International, Thales and Equitix Investment Management.

## Products and solutions
- **Voyager A330 MRTT** - Multirole tanker and transport aircraft operated on behalf of the RAF; the platform replaced the VC10 and reached full tanker capability in 2013.
