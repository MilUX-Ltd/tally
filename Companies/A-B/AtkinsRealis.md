# AtkinsRéalis

> Engineering and project-management services across UK defence programmes, including nuclear propulsion and naval support.

## What they do

AtkinsRéalis is a professional services and project-management company. Its UK defence work spans nuclear propulsion, safety assurance, systems engineering, programme delivery, cyber resilience and reactor substantiation. The UK entity, AtkinsRéalis UK Limited, is registered at Epsom, Surrey; the wider group is headquartered in Montreal, Canada following SNC-Lavalin's 2017 acquisition of Atkins and the group's renaming in 2023.

## Products and solutions

- **Nuclear propulsion support** - specialist engineering and design support to Rolls-Royce Submarines, including a fissile design partner role.
- **Defence engineering delivery** - an Engineering Delivery Partner to the Ministry of Defence's Defence Equipment & Support organisation, supporting programmes across land, maritime and air.
- **Naval base and dockyard services** - support to Babcock at Devonport, HMNB Clyde and Rosyth.
