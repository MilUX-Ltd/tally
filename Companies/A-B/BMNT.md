# BMNT

> Defence and national-security innovation consultancy and early-stage tech accelerator.

## What they do

BMNT is an innovation company for governments and their partners that uses startup methods to create new capabilities for defence and national security. The UK arm supports companies, from pre-seed and seed to enterprise, in entering the UK, US and Australian defence and national-security markets. It operates internationally with offices including Silicon Valley, Austin, Washington, London, Cheltenham and Canberra.

## Products and solutions

- **Commercial readiness and regulatory compliance** - support for companies preparing to enter defence and national-security markets.
- **Market validation** - assessment and validation of dual-use and deep-tech propositions.
- **Navigation and enablement** - guidance through procurement and entry into defence ecosystems.
- **H4X Labs** - a deep-tech, dual-use accelerator for defence and national security.
- **Hacking for the Ministry of Defence (H4MoD)** - the UK university programme based on the Hacking for Defense (H4D) model, launched in January 2019.
