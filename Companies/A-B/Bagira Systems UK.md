# Bagira Systems UK

> UK arm of an Israeli training and simulation company supplying live, virtual and constructive military training systems.

## What they do

Bagira Systems designs, develops and supports training and simulation systems for military forces, covering live, virtual and constructive training. The company states it trains soldiers across more than 40 sites worldwide. Bagira Systems UK Ltd is the UK entity, registered in London in 2023; the wider business is headquartered in Israel. In 2023 Bagira won a UK Ministry of Defence contract for its Magnet live tactical engagement simulation system, delivered to the British Army with UK partner Babcock International.

## Products and solutions

- **Magnet live training system** - force-on-force tactical engagement simulation using real weapons without blank ammunition.
- **JTAC and joint fires training** - simulation for joint terminal attack controller and joint fires training.
- **UAV mission simulator** - simulation for unmanned aerial vehicle mission training.
- **Infantry skills trainer** - simulator for individual and small-team infantry skills.
- **Howitzer and artillery crew trainers** - crew training simulators for artillery systems.
- **CBRN simulation** - training simulation for chemical, biological, radiological and nuclear scenarios.
