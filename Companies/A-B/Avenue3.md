# Avenue3

> A Leeds-based digital and data consultancy specialising in open data standards for healthcare and public-sector providers.

## What they do

Avenue3 describes itself as a technology partner for government and public-sector providers, specialising in the adoption of open data standards and open data solutions. It builds solutions that keep health data independent of the applications that use it, on the principle that separating data from software improves flexibility, reduces cost and supports long-term access and interoperability. Its defence relevance is specific: it holds a UK Ministry of Defence contract to deliver a deployable electronic health record for military clinicians.

## Products and solutions

- **Strategy** - advice to shape digital strategies around open data.
- **Discovery** - stakeholder engagement, identifying system challenges and producing technical roadmaps for complex projects.
- **Architecture** - specialists designing and implementing scalable open data system integrations.
- **Engineering** - openEHR engineers building scalable open-data solutions for data management and healthcare delivery.
- **Project Mercury** - a secure, deployable electronic health record system for UK Defence Medical Services, supporting offline data access and sharing across devices.
