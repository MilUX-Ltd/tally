# Barum & Dewar

> UK manufacturer of protective cases, transit containers and packaging solutions for the defence, aerospace and space sectors.

## What they do

Barum & Dewar designs and manufactures complete packaging solutions for the defence, space, aerospace, subsea and industrial sectors. The company offers in-house technical design (3D CAD), foam engineering and the manufacture of bespoke shipping containers, handling equipment and protective cases, alongside off-the-shelf transit cases and 19" racks. It has supplied the UK MoD, NATO and defence primes for over 20 years.

## Products and solutions

- **Bespoke shipping containers** - engineered containers for payloads from 5kg to 5000kg.
- **Foam engineering** - in-house foam facility producing custom protective inserts.
- **19" racks** - COTS, COTS+ and bespoke rack systems, including half-19" formats.
- **Protective cases** - aluminium, plastic and flight cases, including distribution of Zarges, Peli, SKB, Thermodyne and Hardigg.
- **Sewn products** - rugged fabric covers and tarpaulins.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered.
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; Cyber Essentials; Cyber Essentials Plus.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; West of England Aerospace Forum.

## Ownership

Acquired by Macfarlane Group in October 2023. Operates within Macfarlane Packaging while retaining the Barum & Dewar brand and legal entity. The company is registered in Scotland (Glasgow) with its manufacturing operation in Barnstaple, Devon.
