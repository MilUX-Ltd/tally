# BAR Associates

> Defence and security sector business development consultancy helping SMEs with high-value technologies and services grow within UK and US markets.

## What they do

BAR Associates provides strategic and commercial advisory services to companies seeking to grow within the UK defence and security sectors. Services include strategy development, business development support, operations management, and business coaching. The firm has a US presence to support UK-US bilateral defence and security programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Strategy Development** - market entry and growth strategy for defence and security SMEs.
- **Sales Development** - pipeline building and business development support for the defence market.
- **Business Coaching** - leadership and commercial coaching for founders and senior teams.
