# BAR Associates

> Defence and security sector business development consultancy helping SMEs with high-value technologies and services grow within UK and US markets.

## What they do

BAR Associates provides strategic and commercial advisory services to companies seeking to grow within the UK defence and security sectors. Services include strategic planning, operations management, digital technology, sales development, and business coaching. The firm describes its approach as "Appropriate, Affordable, Achievable" and states it works with UK defence and security companies with the appetite and ambition for growth, including serving personnel, reservists, veterans and military families.

## Products and solutions

- **Strategic Planning** - market entry and growth strategy for defence and security SMEs.
- **Operations Management** - support restructuring operating models and business processes.
- **Digital Technology** - advisory support on digital capability as part of growth planning.
- **Sales Development** - pipeline building and business development support for the defence market.
- **Business Coaching** - leadership and commercial coaching for founders and senior teams.

Published case studies on the company website describe engagements with Clearswift (strategic planning to support growth in the UK defence market) and Varitech Systems (business model and go-to-market work supporting entry into the defence and security market).
