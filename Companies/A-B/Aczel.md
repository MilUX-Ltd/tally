# Aczel

> Management consultancy serving the aerospace, defence and security sectors.

## What they do

Aczel is a management consultancy whose teams are drawn from experienced aerospace, defence and security industry practitioners. Its services focus on operational strategy, business winning and bid support, programme delivery, innovation management, supply chain and manufacturing optimisation, and regulatory compliance. In defence it supports clients across the maritime, air, land, space and cyber domains and across the procurement and operations chain.

## Products and solutions

- **Operational strategy** - translating business strategy into operations.
- **Business winning** - support on competitive bids and new proposals.
- **Programme delivery** - planning and executing complex defence programmes, including recovery of troubled programmes.
- **Supply chain and manufacturing** - optimisation of internal and external operations.
