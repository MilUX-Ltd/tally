# Black Space Technology

> UK military medical software SME specialising in combat telemedicine and digital battlefield casualty care.

## What they do

Black Space Technology is a privately funded UK SME, formed in 2018, that develops scalable, future-proofed mobile telemedicine and telehealth solutions for the digitalisation of the medical battlespace. Its solutions are software focused, hardware agnostic and configurable to local service needs including language localisation. They are used internationally by intergovernmental, non-governmental and military customers deployed on land, sea and air.

## Products and solutions

- **Rapid Triage-EPR** - a multi-language electronic patient record with wireless vital-sign monitoring and telemedicine capability for prehospital care and remote environments.
- **Mass Casualty Incident management system** - a configurable Android digital platform for civil and military contingency medical needs, developed with input from the UK Ministry of Defence and RAID (a French National Police tactical unit).

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** Crown Commercial Service supplier; JOSCAR registered.
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 13485 (medical software), IASME IoT Gold, NHS Data Security and Protection Toolkit, Cyber Essentials Plus; products carry full medical accreditation and GDPR compliance.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
