# Agentycs

> UK sovereign agentic AI platform for defence, intelligence, government and enterprise.

## What they do

Agentycs is a UK company building agentic AI that operates across high-side and low-side networks, deployed from a UK sovereign cloud or at the tactical edge. The platform fuses structured, unstructured and sensor data in an AI-native lakehouse and supports hybrid search, natural-language analytics and agentic reasoning. It is designed to run in air-gapped, disconnected and comms-degraded environments without reliance on US hyperscalers or foreign infrastructure.

## Products and solutions

- **Agentycs Platform** - end-to-end AI operating platform spanning a data and knowledge fabric (Atom), inference orchestration (Anima), mission applications (Agent2) and deployment runtime and edge (Apex).
- **Agentycs-in-a-Box (AIB)** - a rugged, self-contained, man-portable AI appliance preloaded with models, lakehouse and agentic stack, able to run offline and reach initial operating capability rapidly.
