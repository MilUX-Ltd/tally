# Akkodis UK

> Engineering and digital consultancy serving the aerospace and defence sector as part of the global Akkodis Group (Adecco).

## What they do

Akkodis UK is the British entity of Akkodis, a Swiss-headquartered engineering and technology consultancy owned by the Adecco Group. In the UK, the company provides engineering design, systems integration, and digital transformation services to defence and aerospace customers, working with OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers. It holds Gold status under the MoD's Employer Recognition Scheme and is a member of ADS.

## Products and solutions

- **Engineering Consulting** - multi-disciplinary engineering support across defence and aerospace programmes, covering systems design, digital engineering, and product lifecycle management.
- **Digital Transformation** - integration of engineering and digital toolchains to accelerate product development from concept to through-life support.
- **Workforce Solutions** - specialist engineering talent deployed to defence and aerospace prime contractors.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** MoD Employer Recognition Scheme Gold Award (2024).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
