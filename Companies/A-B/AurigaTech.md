# AurigaTech

> Developer of cognitive agentic AI for autonomous defence systems.

## What they do

AurigaTech develops muBrain, a cognitive agentic AI platform that enables drones, vehicles, robots and maritime systems to perceive, decide and act in GPS-denied and contested environments. It targets defence autonomy, counter-drone, border surveillance and robotics applications. Incorporated in 2025, it is based in Bicester, Oxfordshire.

## Products and solutions

- **muBrain** - cognitive agentic AI platform for autonomous systems operating without remote piloting or communications links.
- **Fleet management** - cloud-based management for autonomous fleets.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Standards:** platform aligned with DO-178C, MIL-STD-882E and SAE ARP 4754A.
