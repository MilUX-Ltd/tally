# BiP Solutions

> Public sector procurement intelligence and e-sourcing platform; official publisher of MOD contract information for over 16 years.

## What they do

BiP Solutions has operated at the interface of public and private sector procurement for more than 35 years, making public sector contracting more transparent, competitive, and efficient. The company is the official publisher of MOD contract information and operates Defence Contracts International (DCI), the primary source of MOD and international defence contract intelligence. Its Delta eSourcing platform is used by over 500 public sector organisations and approximately 100,000 active suppliers.

## Products and solutions

- **Delta eSourcing** - UK-compliant e-procurement platform including Tender Manager, Supplier Manager, Contract Manager, eAuctions, and Buyer Portal.
- **Defence Contracts International (DCI)** - premium intelligence service covering MOD and international defence contracts.
- **Defence Contracts Bulletin (MOD DCB)** - official MOD contract publication.
- **Supply2Gov** - tender alert service for sole traders and micro businesses.
- **CompeteFor** - supply chain matching tool.
