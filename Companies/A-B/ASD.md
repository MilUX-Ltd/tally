# ASD

> Metal stockholder and processing supplier to high-integrity sectors including defence.

## What they do

ASD is a UK metal stockholder and processing supplier serving high-integrity sectors such as defence, marine and nuclear. It sources and supplies a range of alloys to project specifications and provides in-house processing. The company is part of the MOD supply chain and is a member of the Hierros Anon Group.

## Products and solutions

- **Metal products** - carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminium products, Alucore panels, bi-metallic products and armour plates.
- **Processing** - laser and water jet cutting, plasma and oxy-fuel profiling, press brake bending, and precision sawing and drilling.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015, NHSS3B, UKCA and CE (BS EN 1090-1 and EN 1090-2), Fit For Nuclear.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
