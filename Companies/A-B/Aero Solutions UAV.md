# Aero Solutions UAV Ltd

> UK developer of fixed-wing unmanned aerial systems for defence applications.

## What they do

Aero Solutions UAV Ltd designs, develops and deploys unmanned aerial systems. The company was incorporated in August 2023 and is registered in Hertfordshire. It works with partners across the UK engineering supply chain on military UAV platforms.

## Products and solutions

- **ASX-1** - a fixed-wing UAV platform described as modular and adaptable to customer-specific requirements, with a maximum take-off weight of 35 kg, payload capacity up to 20 kg and endurance up to 3 hours.
- **HAMMER** - a one-way attack drone developed in partnership with Edinburgh Drone Company, carrying a warhead of around 2.5 kg (extendable), with range options of about 22 km standard or up to 50 km with an extended-range kit. Catapult launched.
