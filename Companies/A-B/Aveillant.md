# Aveillant

> Developer of 3D Holographic Radar for drone detection and air surveillance.

## What they do

Aveillant is a radar technology company that pioneered 3D Holographic Radar. Unlike mechanically or electronically scanned systems, Holographic Radar transmits a wide-angle pulse and forms multiple simultaneous receive beams, maintaining a continuous picture of the full airspace volume. The company is based in Cambridge and was acquired by the Thales Group in November 2017, so it now operates as part of the French-headquartered Thales. The Companies House entity remains active.

## Products and solutions

- **Gamekeeper** - Holographic Radar system to detect, track and classify drones, including micro systems, providing 3D tracks and classification to discriminate drones from birds with detection at long range.
