# Applied Space Solutions

> Aerospace engineering and systems company focused on space domain awareness, space situational awareness and space related analytics.

## What they do

Applied Space Solutions is a privately owned aerospace engineering, system design and implementation company established in 2017 to carry out applied research in aerospace and defence. It focuses on space related analytics, applications and services, drawing on experience in the design, manufacture, launch and operation of space based systems. The company advocates for the responsible and sustainable use of space.

## Products and solutions

- **Space domain awareness** - astrodynamics, deep learning and object classification research.
- **Flight dynamics software** - software for space object detection and tracking.
- **Space traffic management** - capabilities supporting space situational awareness.
