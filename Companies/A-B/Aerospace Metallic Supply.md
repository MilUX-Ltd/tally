# Aerospace Metallic Supply

> Distributor and processor of metallic materials with supply chain services for the aerospace and defence markets.

## What they do

Aerospace Metallic Supply Limited, trading as Aero Metallic, supplies aerospace and commercial materials and supply chain services to the aerospace, defence and specialist engineering markets globally. The company describes itself as a processor and distributor in the aerospace and defence industry, offering supply chain management covering cost reduction, demand and inventory management, and tailored supply and processing.

## Products and solutions

- **Material supply** - aerospace and commercial metallic materials for aerospace and defence customers.
- **Processing** - plate cutting, bar cutting, close tolerance guillotining and poly-coating in house, with sheet routing, laser cutting, precision grinding, water jet cutting, heat treatment, first stage machining and non-destructive testing via partners.
- **Supply chain management** - inventory management and tailored supply and processing packages.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
