# Arista Networks UK

> UK arm of the US networking firm Arista, supplying high-performance data centre and cloud networking platforms.

## What they do

Arista Networks builds data-driven networking for data centre, cloud and campus environments, based on its Extensible Operating System (EOS). Arista Networks UK Ltd is the company's UK-registered entity, incorporated in 2009 and based in London. The parent group serves cloud providers, enterprises and government and intelligence customers, and is an ADS Group member through its UK entity.

## Products and solutions

- **EOS (Extensible Operating System)** - the single network operating system across Arista's switching and routing platforms.
- **Data centre and cloud switching** - high-throughput Ethernet switches for data centre and AI infrastructure.
- **R Series routing platforms** - routed backbone and data centre platforms, including the R4 Series announced in 2025.
- **CloudVision** - network management, telemetry and automation.
