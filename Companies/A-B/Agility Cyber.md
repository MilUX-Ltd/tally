# Agility Cyber

> Independent UK cyber security consultancy providing security assessment and assurance services.

## What they do

Agility Cyber is an independent cyber security consultancy offering security assessment, assurance and training services. It carries out assessments across physical security, servers and wider infrastructure, and positions itself as vendor-independent, delivering all work with a UK-based team. The company is named as a supplier to UK central government departments, the wider public sector and charities.

## Products and solutions

- **Security assessment services** - physical security, server and infrastructure security assessments to evaluate and help remediate risk.
- **Cyber security training** - training delivered by certified specialists.
