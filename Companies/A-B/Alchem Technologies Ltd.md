# Alchem Technologies Ltd

> Data integration platform provider enabling selective, on-demand data sharing between trusted partners in national security, defence, and critical infrastructure.

## What they do

Alchem Technologies builds a data integration platform that allows organisations to establish collaborative programmes, verify data quality, and create a single source of truth across partner networks. The platform uses a mesh architecture enabling selective data sharing between trusted partners, with operational data remaining in situ with each data owner. The company positions itself as a dual-use capability with applications across defence, border and maritime security, and critical national infrastructure.

## Products and solutions

- **Data Integration Platform** - mesh architecture enabling selective data sharing between trusted partners while preserving data governance and chain of custody with each owning organisation.
- **AI Scaling** - funnels partner data into AI models to improve training quality and output at scale.
- **Data Quality and Lineage** - verifies data quality and maintains certified data attributes and chain of custody across collaborative programmes.
