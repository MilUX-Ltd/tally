# Alchem Technologies Ltd

> Data integration platform provider enabling selective, on-demand data sharing between trusted partners in national security, defence, and critical infrastructure.

## What they do

Alchem Technologies is a data and systems integrator providing technical capabilities in the national security, defence and critical infrastructure sectors. It builds a data integration platform that allows organisations to establish collaborative programmes, verify data quality, and create a single source of truth across partner networks. The platform uses a mesh architecture enabling selective data sharing between trusted partners, with operational data remaining in situ with each data owner. The company positions its mesh architecture as extending beyond defence and border and maritime security into critical national infrastructure monitoring.

## Products and solutions

- **Data Integration Platform** - mesh architecture enabling selective data sharing between trusted partners while preserving data governance and chain of custody with each owning organisation.
- **AI Scaling** - funnels partner data into AI models to improve training quality and output at scale.
- **Data Quality and Lineage** - verifies data quality and maintains certified data attributes and chain of custody across collaborative programmes.
