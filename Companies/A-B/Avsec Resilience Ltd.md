# Avsec Resilience Ltd

> Independent consultancy and training provider focused on operational resilience in aviation security.

## What they do

Avsec Resilience provides consulting, training and development services that strengthen operational resilience in aviation security. It offers strategic advisory work, bespoke courses and workshops, human capability development and custom course design. The company describes its approach as independent and human-centred, and is based in Henley-on-Thames.

## Products and solutions

- **Advisory and consulting** - strategic and organisational resilience advice tailored for aviation security.
- **Training courses and workshops** - programmes covering security awareness, stakeholder engagement, behavioural change and policy development.
