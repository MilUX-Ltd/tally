# AMS Global Solutions

> Defence support services provider specialising in maintenance, repair and overhaul of military land systems and equipment.

## What they do

AMS provides defence support services for military land systems and equipment, including maintenance, repair, overhaul and modernisation programmes, logistics and end-to-end supply chain. It works for governments across Europe, Asia, Africa and the Middle East. The company is also referred to as AMS Integrated Solutions and operates from offices in Exeter, Dubai and Florida.

## Products and solutions

- **Maintenance, Repair & Overhaul (MRO)** - through-life equipment support and modernisation programmes.
- **Field Support Representatives (FSR)** - frontline technical support working alongside armed forces personnel.
- **Technical Training Services** - structured training programmes for equipment sustainment.
- **Supply Chain Services** - sourcing, procurement and logistics, including spare parts and defence vehicle procurement.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015; ISO 14001:2015; ISO 45001:2018.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
