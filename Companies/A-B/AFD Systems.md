# AFD Systems

> Advanced engineering and manufacturing business serving the aerospace and defence sectors, based at Blackpool Airport.

## What they do

AFD Systems is an advanced engineering and manufacturing business serving the aerospace and defence sectors, with additional work in space, nuclear, rail, medical and motorsport. Formerly Airframe Designs, it rebranded to AFD Systems in 2025. Since launching in 2009 it has completed over 600 projects across air, land and sea platforms. The company is headquartered at Blackpool Airport's Enterprise Zone and employs over 30 specialists.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems integration** and **airframe engineering** for new design, modification and repair.
- **Cabin interior engineering** and **reverse engineering**.
- **Additive manufacturing**, **innovative tooling solutions** and **high integrity test rigs**.
- **Metrology and 3D scanning**.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR, Exostar.
- **Accreditations:** AS9100, ISO 14001, ISO 27001, Cyber Essentials.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Composites UK, North West Aerospace Alliance.
