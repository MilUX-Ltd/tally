# Broder Metals Group

> Independent stockist and distributor of high-specification stainless steels, nickel alloys and super alloys.

## What they do

Broder Metals Group is an independent stockist and distributor of high-specification stainless steels, nickel alloys and super alloys, supplying UK, European and worldwide markets. It serves oil and gas, petrochemical, power generation, marine and subsea, motorsport, nuclear and aerospace customers. Its defence and aerospace supply runs through its Broder Aerospace subsidiary. The business has traded since 2003 and is based in Sheffield.

## Products and solutions

- **Stainless steels** - high-specification stainless grades in round bar and other forms.
- **Nickel and super alloys** - nickel alloys and super alloys for demanding applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, AS9120, ISO 19443.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, British Stainless Steel Association, North West Aerospace Alliance, Motorsport Industry Association.
