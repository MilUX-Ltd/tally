# ANSYS UK

> UK subsidiary of Ansys, the engineering simulation software company whose tools are used across aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Ansys develops engineering simulation software that predicts how product designs behave in real-world environments, spanning structures, fluids, electromagnetics and multiphysics. ANSYS UK Limited is the Sheffield-registered UK entity of Ansys Inc, headquartered in Canonsburg, Pennsylvania. Ansys was acquired by Synopsys in July 2025 and is now a wholly owned subsidiary of that US company. Its simulation tools are used across aerospace and defence, supported by a dedicated public defence industry programme.

## Products and solutions

- **Ansys Mechanical** - structural finite element analysis.
- **Ansys Fluent** - computational fluid dynamics for fluid flow, heat transfer and reactions.
- **Ansys HFSS** - 3D electromagnetic simulation used for antenna design in aerospace and defence.
- **Ansys LS-DYNA** - explicit multiphysics for crash and impact, used in aerospace and military structural analysis.
