# Araldo

> Defence consultancy providing capability strategy and customer engagement for the defence market.

## What they do

Araldo is a defence consultancy practice specialising in defence customer engagement and the delivery of change. It supports businesses in developing strategy for the defence market in the UK and internationally, and supports government in maximising value for money in the management of defence capability through planning frameworks, analysis, options and implementation plans. The practice has worked on UK protected mobility vehicles and vehicle and individual armour systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Capability and market strategy** - business and capability strategy development for the defence market.
- **Customer engagement** - engaging the defence customer on behalf of businesses to build relationships and traction for products and services.
