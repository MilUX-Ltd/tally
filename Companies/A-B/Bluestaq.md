# Bluestaq

> Data management software for defence, space and national security, including the Unified Data Library.

## What they do

Bluestaq builds data management platforms for defence, space and national security customers. Its Unified Data Library aggregates and shares operational data across organisations, and its Global Data Marketplace supports data-driven collaboration across the United States, United Kingdom and Australia. Bluestaq Ltd is the UK subsidiary of Bluestaq LLC, incorporated in February 2025 and based in London.

## Products and solutions

- **Unified Data Library (UDL)** - a platform that ingests, stores and shares space and operational domain data for allied defence and intelligence users.
- **Global Data Marketplace** - a service for sharing data across allied defence, space, security and intelligence sectors.
