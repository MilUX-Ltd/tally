# Bitwise

> Safety-critical and secure software engineering for defence, aerospace and other regulated sectors.

## What they do

Bitwise provides software development, testing and software safety management for systems where assurance, safety and performance matter. It has worked in safety-critical software engineering for over 35 years and employs more than 80 staff across offices in Scotland and the Netherlands. Its Dunfermline headquarters provides secure labs and infrastructure audited and approved by defence sector customers. Defence work has covered surveillance, data monitoring, radio, video, secure communications and encryption.

## Products and solutions

- **Development** - software development across secure environments including radar and military communications.
- **Testing** - independent verification and validation.
- **Software safety management** - software safety cases and safety management to standards such as IEC 61508 and DEF STAN 00-56.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 27001:2022, ISO 13485, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus. Experience with IEC 61508, DO-178B/C and DEF STAN 00-56.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
