# APEL

> Manufacturer of precision fasteners and machined components for the aerospace and defence industry.

## What they do

APEL Limited, established in 1965, is a manufacturer and supplier of precision fasteners and machined parts to the aerospace and defence industry. It supplies a worldwide customer base from its facility in Wilmslow, Cheshire. Its stated customers include BAE Systems, Airbus, Spirit AeroSystems, Bombardier and Beechcraft.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision aerospace fasteners** - bolts, nuts and machined fastening components for airframe and aircraft applications.
- **Precision machined parts** - CNC-turned and milled components for aerospace and defence platforms, produced on sliding-head lathes and machining centres with CMM inspection.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Nadcap; BSI-certified quality management.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
