# ABD Solutions

> Retrofit vehicle autonomy specialist applying driving-robotics technology to defence and industrial fleets.

## What they do

ABD Solutions is a subsidiary of UK-listed AB Dynamics plc, formed to apply the group's proven vehicle automation technology to industrial and defence applications. It specialises in after-market autonomy that can be retrofitted to existing vehicles, using physical driving robotics that control steering, clutch, brake, throttle and gearshift rather than relying on CAN-based integration. This allows almost any vehicle with a steering wheel and pedals, including those with mechanical linkages and manual transmissions, to be automated. Target sectors include defence, mining, construction, materials handling and agriculture.

## Products and solutions

- **Indigo Drive** - a software ecosystem and robotic actuation system that enables defence and industrial vehicles to be automated quickly and cost-effectively. Features include driverless or remote vehicle control, precise path following, task planning, drive-by-wire and robotic actuation, redundant architecture, secure communications and pedestrian detection.
