# Blue Mountain Training

> Security, hostile-environment and close-protection training, including CONDO courses for personnel supporting Ministry of Defence operations.

## What they do

Blue Mountain Training, part of Blue Mountain Group, delivers risk-management training and security services through training, security and advisory work. Its instructors are drawn from former military, policing and other security backgrounds. The training offer includes hostile-environment and first-aid training, close-protection courses, and CONDO courses for personnel working in operational areas in support of the Ministry of Defence.

## Products and solutions

- **CONDO courses** - training for personnel working within operational areas in support of the Ministry of Defence.
- **Hostile Environment and First Aid Training (HEFAT)** - preparation for working safely in high-risk environments.
- **Close protection training** - SIA-licensed close-protection and related courses.
