# AVoptics

> UK supplier of engineered fibre-optic, photonic, electrical and electronic solutions for harsh aerospace, defence and space environments.

## What they do

AVoptics (A V Optics Limited) provides engineered photonic solutions for harsh environments and supplies aerospace and defence primes. Its capability spans production, with operators accredited to internationally recognised aerospace fibre-optic standards, and a consultancy division of scientists, engineers and technicians experienced in optics and photonics for harsh environments. The company is based in Yeovil, Somerset, with a research and development facility in Chepstow, Wales.

## Products and solutions

- **Free Space Optics (FSO/FSOC)** - laser-based optical communications, including the "Galahad" modulated retro-reflector system for covert, secure, low size, weight and power links.
- **Cable assemblies** - build-to-print or full design service.
- **Test equipment and components** - inspection, hand tools, curing, polishing, cleaning, components and ready-made patchcord assemblies, sold via an online shop.
- **Services** - research and development, fibre-optic training to internationally recognised standards, design, calibration, on-site services, in-field repair and test, and an environmental testing facility.
