# Allied
> European defence procurement and production platform connecting engineers, manufacturers and technology partners across Europe and Ukraine to source and integrate defence systems at speed.

## What they do
Allied (Allied Adaptive Industries, trading as A2I) is a UK defence procurement and production platform. The company connects capable engineers, manufacturers and technology partners across Europe and Ukraine to get defence systems, including drones, air defence and software, into the field quickly. The model is integration-led rather than end-to-end product development, sourcing and combining third-party technologies. Allied is backed by Notion Capital and 1991 VC, and was co-founded by a former Royal Marines officer.
