# Allied
> European defence procurement and production platform connecting engineers, manufacturers and technology partners across Europe and Ukraine to source and integrate defence systems at speed.

## What they do

Allied (Allied Adaptive Industries, trading as A2I) is a UK defence procurement and production platform. The company describes itself as building infrastructure that connects capable engineers, manufacturers and technology partners across Europe and Ukraine to get their systems into the field, on a peer-to-peer partner model rather than a traditional prime-subcontractor hierarchy. It frames the problem it addresses as one of production capacity rather than a lack of technology. The model is integration-led rather than end-to-end product development: Allied sources and combines third-party systems, including drones, air defence systems and software, and bundles them with support services into contract solutions for government customers. Allied is backed by Notion Capital and 1991 VC, and was co-founded by a former Royal Marines officer.

## Products and solutions

- **Arena** - a marketplace platform for defence technology systems, sub-systems, components and related professional services, connecting suppliers with government and industry buyers.
- **AlliedOS** - a platform layer supporting the marketplace's digital procurement tooling.
- Access to the marketplace is free for participating companies and governments; Allied's stated business model takes a transaction fee on sales made through the platform, alongside tiered subscriptions for support services and digital procurement tools.
