# ACUA Ocean

> UK developer of long-endurance uncrewed surface vessels for maritime security, defence and offshore operations.

## What they do

ACUA Ocean designs and builds uncrewed surface vessels (USVs) for ocean monitoring, data collection and maritime security. The company, based at Turnchapel Wharf in Plymouth, focuses on long-endurance platforms built around stable hull designs intended to operate in high sea states for extended periods.

## Products and solutions

- **Pioneer USV** - a medium uncrewed surface vessel built on a SWATH hull for stability and endurance, used for tasks including surface ISR, sonar deployment and towed-array operation. ACUA states Pioneer was certified under the MCA Workboat Code 3.
- **Medium USV design (MROS)** - a larger long-endurance uncrewed surface vessel concept with naval and civilian applications, developed under UK government-backed work.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Pioneer certified under the MCA Workboat Code 3.
- **Memberships:** Society of Maritime Industries.
