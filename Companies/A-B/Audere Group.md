# Audere Group Limited

> London intelligence-led security, commercial intelligence and investigations group serving government, defence and corporate clients.

## What they do

Audere provides intelligence-led security, commercial intelligence, investigations and risk advisory services, with origins in foreign direct investment, reconstruction and security work in conflict-affected regions. The group comprises Audere International, which specialises in commercial intelligence and investigations, and Audere Securus, an intelligence-led security company. It has a global footprint across North America, the Asia-Pacific region, the Middle East, Central and Eastern Europe and Ukraine, and has taken part in UK defence industry trade missions.

## Products and solutions

- **Audere International** - commercial intelligence, due diligence and investigations.
- **Audere Securus** - intelligence-led security services, including operations expanded in Ukraine.
