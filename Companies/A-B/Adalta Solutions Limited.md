# Adalta Solutions Limited
> Defence-focused consulting and delivery partner that curates and leads specialist teams to deliver high-assurance engineering outcomes.

## What they do
Adalta Solutions is a UK defence consulting and delivery firm. The company assembles specialist teams to deliver high-assurance engineering work for defence customers. It operates within the D3IP community at the Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
