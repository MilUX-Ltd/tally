# Adalta Solutions Limited

> IT, systems engineering and security recruitment and delivery specialist serving the aerospace, defence, space and energy sectors.

## What they do

Adalta Solutions is a UK consultancy providing IT, systems engineering and security recruitment and project delivery, working with a number of defence primes as an approved supplier to BAE Systems. Its stated areas of specialisation include system architecture and model-based systems engineering, digital twin, EM/RF, zero trust cyber security, AI/ML simulation, verification, validation and accreditation (VV&A), and computational fluid dynamics and multiphysics. The company is registered in England as Adalta Solutions Limited (company number 07031508), previously named Adalta Recruitment Solutions Limited until February 2025, with a registered office in Cheadle Hulme, Cheshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems engineering and security recruitment** - permanent, contract and statement-of-work project delivery covering IT, systems engineering and security roles.
- **Specialist consultancy** - project management, business architecture and analysis, enterprise and solution architecture, and operational analysis and systems modelling for defence and aerospace customers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials.
- **Memberships:** North West Aerospace Alliance; company approval with BAE Systems.
