# Atlantic Plus

> Defence cyber advisory, consultancy and integration firm supporting UK Government and Defence in the cyber domain.

## What they do

Atlantic+ was founded in 2019 and provides specialist knowledge, skills and experience aimed at helping UK Government and Defence realise their ambition in the cyber domain. It positions itself as an advisory and integration firm, providing domain-specialist advisors and coordinating technology to deliver operational outcomes.

## Products and solutions

- **Domain-specialist advisors** - strategic insight, operational guidance, technical expertise and hands-on support.
- **Technology coordination and integration** - applying and integrating technology to deliver operational outcomes.
