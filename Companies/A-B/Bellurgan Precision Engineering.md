# Bellurgan Precision Engineering

> Irish precision engineering firm machining high-tolerance components and electromechanical assemblies for aerospace, medical and electronics OEMs.

## What they do

Bellurgan Precision Engineering is a contract manufacturer of precision turned and milled components and electromechanical assemblies, based in Dundalk, Co. Louth. Operating since 1978, it supplies global OEMs across medical device, aerospace and electronics sectors. The company has organised into three sector-specific entities for medical, aerospace and electronics work, with multi-axis milling and turning capability.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision machined components** - high-tolerance turned and milled parts for aerospace and other regulated sectors.
- **Electromechanical assemblies** - assembled subsystems for OEM customers.
