# Brookhouse Aerospace

> Build-to-print manufacturer of composite and metallic aerostructures for civil and defence aerospace.

## What they do

Brookhouse Aerospace is a build-to-print aerostructures and engineering specialist serving civil and defence aerospace. It operates three business units covering metallics and fabrication, composites, and surface treatments, producing composite structures, metallic detail parts, surface treatments and integrated assemblies across two sites in Lancashire. The company traces back to 1985 and operated under the Kaman and Brookhouse Composites names before a 2021 management buyout re-established it as an independent UK business.

## Products and solutions

- **Composite structures** - monolithic and sandwich parts in carbon fibre, glass and Aramid, autoclave-cured in clean room facilities.
- **Metallics and fabrication** - sheet metal fabrications and machined metal parts, including high-volume CNC machining.
- **Surface treatments** - surface treatment processes offered to external customers and used in-house.
- **Defence end products** - aerostructures, fuselage and engine panels, missile structures and UAV components.
