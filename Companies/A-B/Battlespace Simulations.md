# Battlespace Simulations, Inc.

> Military modelling, simulation and analysis software for multidomain combat training and rehearsal.

## What they do

Battlespace Simulations, Inc. (BSI) provides modelling, simulation and analysis software focused on multidomain combat simulation in near-peer threat environments, simulating the entire battlespace including non-visible effects. Its software is used by militaries and companies internationally for joint fires training, wargaming, mission planning and rehearsal, electronic warfare training, operational research and after-action review. In February 2025 BSI was acquired by FAAC Incorporated, a division of Arotech Corporation.

## Products and solutions

- **MACE (Modern Air Combat Environment)** - computer-generated forces engine used as the simulation engine behind multiple combat system simulations worldwide.
- **ARMOR (Augmented Reality Mission Observation & Rehearsal)** - real-time 3D visualisation of the battlespace, with terrain that can be built anywhere in the world within MACE.
