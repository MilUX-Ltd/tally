# Bellstone Labs UK

> UK subsidiary of the Bellstone Group, an energetics and armoured vehicle manufacturer, seeking UK partners in equipment, materials and expertise.

## What they do

Bellstone Labs is the UK subsidiary of the Bellstone Group, described by ADS as a Ukrainian manufacturer of energetics and infantry fighting vehicles and armoured personnel carriers (IFV/APC). In the UK the company states it is seeking to partner with producers of equipment, suppliers of materials and experts in those fields. The UK entity is registered in London.
