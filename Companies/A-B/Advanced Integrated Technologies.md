# Advanced Integrated Technologies

> UK electromechanical systems integrator designing and manufacturing UAS airframes, counter-drone platforms, radomes, and composite assemblies for defence and aerospace customers.

## What they do

Advanced Integrated Technologies (trading as AiT) is a Cambridgeshire-based manufacturer incorporated in February 2024. The company takes full responsibility for the design, build, integration, test, and in-service support of complex composite components and electromechanical assemblies, reducing the engineering and integration burden on defence customers. Core focus areas are unmanned air, ground, and maritime vehicles; counter-UAS platforms; and antenna and radome manufacture. AiT operates a clean room facility and a 3.5m autoclave for composite production, and references a design centre in South Africa for airframe manufacturing.

## Products and solutions

- **Fixed-wing and VTOL UAS airframes** - lightweight, cost-effective designs with custom flight control systems, hot-swappable payload bays, and EO/IR and passive RF payload integration.
- **Counter-UAS platform** - integrated C-UAS system built around distributed sensing and electronic engagement; described in company blogs as a next-generation mission system.
- **Antennas and radomes** - radar systems and sensor assemblies for defence applications.
- **Unmanned maritime and ground vehicles** - UUV, USV, and UGV platforms.
- **Ballistic and blast-protected land vehicles** - composite-based protective structures.
- **Composite design, prototyping, and manufacture** - full-cycle service including CFD, FEA, environmental test, and First Article Inspection.
