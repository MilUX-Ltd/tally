# Aero Metals Alliance UK

> Group of metal suppliers providing materials and supply chain management to aerospace and defence customers.

## What they do

Aero Metals Alliance (AMA) provides materials and supply chain management services to aerospace and defence companies. It brings together metal suppliers (Gould Alloys, Service Centres Aero, Sunshine Metals, PASU Progressive Alloy and Wolften) to offer stockholding, distribution and metal processing through service centres across the Americas, Europe and Asia Pacific. AMA acts as a single point of contact for OEMs and sub-contractors sourcing aeronautical metals, processing and related services.

## Products and solutions

- **Metal supply** - ferrous and nonferrous metals including titanium, nickel, stainless and re-melted alloys, tungsten, molybdenum and tantalum.
- **Processing capabilities** - metal processing to British, American and German specifications and customer-specific grades.
- **Centralised purchasing and supply chain services** - aggregated demand across a single supply chain.
