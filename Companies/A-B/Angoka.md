# ANGOKA

> Belfast cyber-security company securing machine-to-machine communications for connected systems and smart mobility.

## What they do

ANGOKA is an IoT and machine-to-machine security company protecting communications for smart cities, smart mobility and connected industrial systems. Headquartered in Belfast, it has held offices in London and The Hague. Its patented approach secures the device and its identity rather than the network, safeguarding the integrity and provenance of machine-to-machine data even across untrusted networks.

## Products and solutions

- **Device identity and M2M security** - solutions that create trusted connections between machines, protecting communication integrity and data provenance for connected and autonomous systems.
