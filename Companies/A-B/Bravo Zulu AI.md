# Bravo Zulu AI

> AI governance, training, and AI Management System provider for UK defence organisations and regulated sectors, drawing on high-reliability organisation and defence aviation safety heritage.

## What they do

Bravo Zulu AI provides practical AI training, advisory services, and AI Management Systems for MOD organisations, defence primes, and SMEs. Its methodology applies high-reliability organisation and defence aviation safety thinking to AI governance and adoption. The company trained over 600 UK MOD leaders and managers in 2024-25 and delivers more than 25 courses per year.

## Products and solutions

- **AI Management System (AIMS)** - lightweight ISO 42001-aligned AI governance baseline for defence and regulated environments, covering policy, risk, and compliance.
- **AI Training Workshops** - group and one-to-one sessions on safe AI use, team playbooks, and adoption habits for MOD and defence prime teams.
- **Advisory and Consultancy** - tool selection, AI governance support, process mapping, and automation roadmapping for defence and government customers.
