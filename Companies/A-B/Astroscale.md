# Astroscale

> In-orbit servicing and active debris removal, delivered from the company's UK and European base at Harwell.

## What they do

Astroscale develops in-orbit servicing solutions, including active debris removal, end-of-life services, life extension and in-situ space situational awareness. Astroscale Ltd is the UK and European subsidiary of Astroscale Holdings Inc., which is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan. The UK operation runs offices, satellite servicer manufacturing facilities and a Mission Control Centre at the Zeus building on Harwell Science and Innovation Campus, Oxfordshire.

## Products and solutions

- **COSMIC** - the Cleaning Outer Space Mission through Innovative Capture spacecraft, a UK-led active debris removal mission to remove two inactive UK satellites from orbit.
- **ELSA-M** - a commercial end-of-life service designed to remove prepared satellites from orbit.
- **Docking plates** - hardware fitted to client satellites to enable later capture and removal.
