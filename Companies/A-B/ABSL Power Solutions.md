# ABSL Power Solutions

> Designer and manufacturer of lithium-ion and other batteries and power solutions for defence, space and aerospace.

## What they do

ABSL Power Solutions researches, designs and manufactures rechargeable and non-rechargeable batteries, charging solutions, lithium-ion cells and control electronics. It serves defence, space, aerospace, marine and industrial markets. ABSL builds batteries used in military radios and portable communication systems, and in unmanned aerial, undersea and ground vehicles. The UK operation is based at Culham Science Centre in Oxfordshire. ABSL has its roots in AEA Technology and was acquired by the US company EnerSys in 2011; the ABSL brand now sits within EnerSys, which is listed on the New York Stock Exchange.

## Products and solutions

- **ABSL lithium-ion batteries** - battery packs for military vehicles, aircraft, submarines, underwater vehicles, missiles and weapons.
- **Soldier and communications power** - batteries for military radios and portable communication systems.
- **Space batteries** - batteries supplied for satellite and space mission systems.
