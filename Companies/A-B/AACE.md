# AACE

> Independent UK defence engineering consultancy providing embedded technical experts across air, land, and maritime domains throughout the CADMID lifecycle.

## What they do

AACE (Aerospace & Airworthiness Consultancy Enterprises Limited) is a Bristol-based SME founded in 1997 by a former RAF officer. The company provides independent engineering consultancy to MOD and the wider Defence Enterprise, with staff drawn predominantly from military backgrounds. Work spans five core service lines: air safety and engineering, land safety and engineering, maritime safety and engineering, integrated logistics support, and technical publications.

## Products and solutions

- **Air Safety and Engineering** - airworthiness (Type and Continuing), Release to Service, Ship Air Integration, MRP 145 compliance, Human Factors investigation, and RPAS airspace integration.
- **Land Safety and Engineering** - safety case development, hazard analysis, risk management to Def Stan 00-056/00-051, POSMS/POEMS, legislative exemptions, and mid-life upgrade support.
- **Maritime Safety and Engineering** - safety cases for submarines and surface ships, ASEMS frameworks, SIL 2 functional safety documentation, naval certification, and combat systems safety.
- **Integrated Logistics Support** - full CADMID lifecycle ILS programme management, supportability analysis, support solution design, and business case authoring aligned to Def Stan 00-600 and the DLF.
- **Technical Publications** - APs, AESPs, BRs, Illustrated Parts Catalogues, and maintenance manuals aligned to DefStan 00-601 Part 4.
