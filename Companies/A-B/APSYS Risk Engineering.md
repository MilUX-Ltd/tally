# APSYS Risk Engineering

> Risk management and safety engineering consultancy, an Airbus Group subsidiary, supporting aerospace and defence certification.

## What they do

APSYS is an engineering services company headquartered in Paris and a subsidiary of the Airbus Group, with operations including the UK. Its public self-description covers systems safety and certification, process assurance (DO178C and DO254), reliability, availability and maintainability (RAMS), integrated logistic support, and safety management systems. It supports certification for aircraft manufacturers and equipment suppliers across civil (FAA, EASA) and military environments.

The UK entity is registered at Companies House under company number 07541925; that registration is now recorded under the name Airbus Protect Limited, while the ADS membership and prior public profile use the APSYS Risk Engineering name. The UK operation has worked from Bristol and Aberdeen.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems safety and certification** - safety analysis and certification support for aerospace and defence systems.
- **Process assurance** - DO178C and DO254 compliance support.
- **RAMS and ILS** - reliability, availability and maintainability analysis and integrated logistic support.
