# Abbattis Risk Management

> International security and risk management consultancy serving defence, critical national infrastructure and other sectors.

## What they do

Abbattis Risk Management has provided security and risk management consultancy since 2007. Its work includes security risk and design consulting, business continuity and crisis management, due diligence and analysis, close protection and maritime security, information and cyber security, and technical surveillance countermeasures. It states experience in critical national infrastructure, defence and national security, including military basecamp and defence infrastructure design and the safe storage of ammunition. The company operates globally, is registered in Switzerland and the United Arab Emirates, and has a UK-registered entity. It is listed by ADS under counter-terrorism and intelligence services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group. Staff are listed on the UK Register of Security Engineers and Specialists (RSES).
