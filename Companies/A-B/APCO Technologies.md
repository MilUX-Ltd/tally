# APCO Technologies

> Manufacturer of mechanical and electromechanical equipment for the space, energy and naval sectors.

## What they do

APCO Technologies designs, manufactures, integrates and tests mechanical and electromechanical equipment for the space, energy and naval markets. Its work spans feasibility studies, design, manufacturing, integration, testing and on-site services. It is a family company founded in 1992 and headquartered at Aigle in Switzerland.

## Products and solutions

- **Space flight equipment** - flight hardware for institutional and commercial satellites.
- **Launcher equipment** - flight and ground segment components, including work on the Ariane 6 launcher.
- **Mechanical Ground Support Equipment (MGSE)** - ground handling equipment for space programmes.
- **Naval equipment** - electromechanical equipment for the naval sector.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** EN 9100 (Aerospace and Defence), ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 27001, ISO 45001, ISO 19443, CEFRI E.
