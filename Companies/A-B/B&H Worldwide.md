# B&H Worldwide

> Specialist aviation and aerospace logistics provider, including bespoke defence logistics solutions.

## What they do

B&H Worldwide was established in the United Kingdom in 1988 and provides logistics services dedicated to the aviation and aerospace industry. It handles time-critical and routine shipments of aerospace components for airlines, MROs, stockists, leasing companies and OEMs, including aircraft engines, avionics, rotables and consumables. The company operates from hubs in locations including London, Birmingham, Amsterdam, Frankfurt, Dubai, Singapore, Hong Kong and Australia, with 24/7 AOG support. Among its sector services it offers bespoke defence logistics covering routine, critical and AOG, out-of-gauge and dangerous goods cargo. In March 2026 an affiliate of Sun European Partners completed an investment in the company.

## Products and solutions

- **AOG and critical logistics** - 24/7 aircraft-on-ground and time-critical freight services.
- **Defence logistics** - tailored defence logistics for routine, critical, AOG, out-of-gauge and dangerous goods cargo.
- **Warehousing and inventory** - forward stocking locations and inventory management for aerospace parts.
- **Engine transport and storage** - movement and storage of aircraft engines.
- **Trade compliance and customs clearance** - customs and trade compliance support across markets.
