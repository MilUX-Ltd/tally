# Archer Aviation UK

> UK subsidiary of US electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) developer Archer Aviation, set up to support an engineering hub and the company's UK defence work.

## What they do

Archer Aviation UK Limited is the UK arm of Archer Aviation, a US-headquartered developer of eVTOL aircraft listed on the New York Stock Exchange (ACHR). The UK entity was incorporated in December 2025 to support an engineering hub in South West England, building a local engineering team for Archer's UK and international work. Archer's defence activity in the UK centres on eVTOL aircraft development and hybrid propulsion.

## Products and solutions

- **eVTOL aircraft** - electric vertical take-off and landing aircraft development, with a dual-use strategy spanning civil and defence applications.
- **Project NYX teaming** - part of a teaming agreement with Anduril UK and GKN Aerospace supporting the British Army's Project NYX and the Ministry of Defence's Land Autonomous Collaborative Platform programme.
