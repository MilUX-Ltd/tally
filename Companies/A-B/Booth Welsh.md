# Booth Welsh

> Integrated engineering services for the nuclear, defence and process sectors.

## What they do

Booth Welsh provides integrated engineering services for highly regulated environments, including nuclear and defence. Its disciplines cover electrical, control and instrumentation, process engineering, mechanical and piping, civil and structural, automation, systems integration and digital transformation. The company has over 30 years of experience and works with government bodies and Tier 1 contractors, with staff trained for security-cleared environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Engineering services** - electrical, control and instrumentation, process, mechanical, civil and structural engineering.
- **Automation and systems integration** - control systems integration and digital transformation for regulated industries.
