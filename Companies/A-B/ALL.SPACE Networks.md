# ALL.SPACE Networks

> Develops multi-orbit, multi-network satellite communications terminals for defence and government.

## What they do

ALL.SPACE develops satellite communications terminals built around a single antenna that connects simultaneously to multiple satellites across different orbits (LEO, MEO, GEO, HEO) and networks. The company markets the result as resilient, jam-resistant communications-on-the-move across land, sea and air, with a dedicated government and defence line of business. It was founded in 2013 as Isotropic Systems and renamed ALL.SPACE in 2022. The company has raised approximately 160 million US dollars, with backers including Seraphim Space, Boeing, AE Industrial Partners, Promus Ventures and BOKA Group.

## Products and solutions

- **Hydra 2** - dual-link, multi-orbit Ka-band mobility terminal.
- **Hydra 4** - quad-link terminal connecting military Ka, commercial Ka, Ku and L-band simultaneously.
- **Hydra MAX** - dual-beam, full-duplex satellite communications terminal for on-the-move connectivity.
