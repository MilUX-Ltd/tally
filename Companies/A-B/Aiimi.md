# Aiimi

> Data, AI and digital consultancy delivering secure information management platforms and advisory services to government, defence, and enterprise clients.

## What they do

Aiimi provides data engineering, data science, AI implementation, and digital consultancy services to organisations across government, defence, intelligence, and the private sector. The company helps clients govern, classify, and unlock value from enterprise data at scale. Its proprietary software platform extends these services with a productised capability for secure, AI-powered workplace information management.

## Products and solutions

- **Aiimi Insight Engine** - An AI-powered enterprise search and data management platform that automates discovery, enrichment, classification, and compliance across large data estates.
- **Workplace AI** - A secure data management platform designed for regulated and sensitive environments, enabling organisations to adopt AI on their own data with appropriate governance controls.
