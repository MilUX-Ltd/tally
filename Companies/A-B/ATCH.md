# ATCH

> UK telecoms security and national security consultancy delivering lawful intercept, 5G architecture design, secure persona services, and emergency service network engineering to government buyers.

## What they do

ATCH operates at the intersection of telecommunications security and national security, offering services from 5G architecture design to communications data retention and secure web browsing for covert use cases. Its services are primarily aimed at government, law enforcement, and national security buyers, procured through G-Cloud frameworks. The company has links to the UK Defence BattleLab in Dorset.

## Products and solutions

- **5G Architecture and Security Design** - transformation from legacy to modern telecoms architectures for secure environments.
- **Emergency Service Network Design and Engineering** - design support for critical public-safety communications infrastructure.
- **Communications Data Collection, Retention and Analysis** - lawful intercept and data retention solutions for government and law enforcement.
- **Secure Web Browsing and Persona Service** - secure browsing and social media access from a trusted environment for intelligence and special operations contexts.
