# Bertin Exensor

> Designs and manufactures networked Unattended Ground Sensor (UGS) systems for military and security surveillance, with its production site in the UK.

## What they do

Bertin Exensor designs, develops and manufactures networked Unattended Ground Sensor (UGS) systems used for force protection, long-range surveillance, counter-mobility and asset protection. Founded in 1987, the company has its headquarters and main R&D centre in Lund, Sweden, a communications and network lab in Kalmar, and its production and manufacturing site near Basingstoke, UK. Since 2017 it has been a subsidiary of the French group Bertin Technologies. The UK entity, Bertin Exensor Limited, handles production, post-design services, product R&D and sales, including support to UK MOD contracts.

## Products and solutions

- **Flexnet** - wireless UGS platform combining seismic, acoustic, magnetic and thermal imaging sensors to detect, classify and report targets.
- **UGS sensors** - the Mini Mk3, Scout Mk3, Surveyor, PIR and MicroRadar ground sensors.
- **Flexnet Commander** - command and control software for the sensor network.
- **Gateways and modems** - networking hardware linking sensors to the C2 system.
