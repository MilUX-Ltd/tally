# Alpha Crucis Solutions

> Defence and security consultancy supporting business development, bid management and capability acquisition.

## What they do

Alpha Crucis Solutions is a defence and security consultancy. It works on business development, bid management and support, operational analysis, product pivots and transformation at programme and project level. The team draws on operational experience across air, land and maritime, and on knowledge of the UK MOD capability and acquisition process from concept through to in-service support.
