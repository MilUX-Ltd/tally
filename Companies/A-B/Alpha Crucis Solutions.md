# Alpha Crucis Solutions

> Defence and security consultancy supporting business development, bid management and capability acquisition.

## What they do

Alpha Crucis Solutions is a defence and security consultancy, registered in Bristol and incorporated in March 2019. It delivers business development, bid management and support, technological and operational analysis, product pivots, and transformation at programme and project level. The company draws on operational experience across air, land and maritime, and on knowledge of the UK MOD capability and acquisition process from concept through to in-service support, combining direct service in the UK Armed Forces with contracting experience providing SME support, advice, training and delivery of specialist projects in the defence sector.

## Products and solutions

- **Business development and bid support** - support to defence and security firms on business development, bid management and bid writing.
- **Operational and technological analysis** - analysis at programme and project level to inform capability and product decisions.
- **Transformation support** - support for product pivots and organisational transformation in defence and security programmes.
