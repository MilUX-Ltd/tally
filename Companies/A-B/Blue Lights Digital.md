# Blue Lights Digital

> Digital investigation and intelligence capability building for law enforcement, intelligence agencies and military clients.

## What they do

Blue Lights Digital (BLD) builds digital investigation and intelligence capability for law enforcement, intelligence agencies and militaries, combining consultancy, training and a portfolio of digital products. The company works in the digital investigation, intelligence and communications data fields, and routinely supports UK law enforcement and military partners. It offers an end-to-end service spanning capability assessment, technology and training design, and continuous reporting.

## Products and solutions

- **Digital investigation training** - courses covering digital forensics, communications data, open source intelligence, social media analysis and RF and WiFi propagation, delivered to law enforcement and military clients.
- **Investigation platforms and products** - a portfolio of digital tools supporting complex investigations.
- **Discovery consultancy** - tailored assessments of a client's capacity building needs.
