# Advent IM

> Independent information security and physical security consultancy supporting governance, risk and compliance, including for the defence sector.

## What they do

Advent IM is an independent information security and physical security consultancy supporting governance, risk and compliance (GRC). It provides consultancy, training, outsourced solutions and testing across information and cyber security, physical security, business continuity, and data protection. In defence it supports adoption of the Secure by Design (SbD) process and works to frameworks including the Cyber Assessment Framework (CAF), JSP 440, ISO 27001 and the NIST Cybersecurity Framework. Its consultants are security vetted.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure by Design (SbD) support** - assurance of MOD capabilities in line with policy.
- **Defence Cyber Certification (DCC)** - supplier cyber assurance across the defence supply chain.
- **Physical security reviews and red teaming** - benchmarking and testing of physical security measures.
- **Training Academy** - SIRO, IAO, data protection and security awareness training.
- **Outsourced security solutions** - contingent security roles supplied on demand.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** G-Cloud; Digital Outcomes and Specialists; Cyber Security Services 3 (CCS)
- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001; ISO 9001; Cyber Essentials
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Make UK Defence; JOSCAR
