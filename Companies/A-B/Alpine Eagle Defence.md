# Alpine Eagle Defence

> Airborne counter-unmanned aircraft systems for defence and security.

## What they do

Alpine Eagle builds airborne counter-drone systems for defence and security applications. Its system integrates multi-sensor detection, fire control and strike into a single networked capability. The company was founded in 2023 in Germany and announced expansion into the UK in July 2025, with plans for a UK base covering business development, manufacturing and research. Alpine Eagle Defence Ltd is the UK-registered entity; the group is headquartered in Germany.

## Products and solutions

- **Sentinel** - an airborne, air-to-air counter-UAS system combining airborne radar, EO/IR sensors and interceptor capability in a closed sensor-to-shooter loop, designed to integrate with battle management systems and ground-based effectors.
