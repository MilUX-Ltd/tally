# Avantix

> Defence management and transformation consultancy supporting primes and manufacturers with programme management, agile transformation, and operating model design.

## What they do

Avantix works with defence contractors and manufacturers to embed new ways of working, improve organisational resilience, and support strategic direction. Case studies cover programme transformation for complex systems integrators, agile transformation for defence manufacturers, and operations and safety management transformation under enhanced regulatory scrutiny. Roles require UK security clearance eligibility.

## Products and solutions

- **Programme Transformation** - programme management and transformation for complex defence systems programmes.
- **Agile Transformation** - SAFe Lite and Enterprise SAFe implementation for defence manufacturing environments.
- **Operations Management Transformation** - operating model design and safety management for regulated defence manufacturers.
- **Business Analysis** - analysis for in-service date and delivery challenges.
