# Bowmill Engineering

> Sub-contract precision machining and surface finishing for the aerospace, defence and marine sectors.

## What they do

Bowmill Engineering, part of the Bowmill Group, provides sub-contract machining, sub-assemblies and surface finishing treatments for the aerospace, defence and marine sectors. Established in 1970, it operates from sites in Poole, Dorset and Tewkesbury, Gloucestershire. The company manufactures components to sub-micron tolerances, from small valve assemblies to flying control assemblies and landing gear kits, with non-destructive testing and metal treatments available in-house through Bowmill Metal Treatments Ltd.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision machined components** - multi-disciplined machined products for aerospace, defence and marine customers.
- **Assemblies** - sub-assemblies and final assembly, including flying control assemblies and landing gear kits.
- **Surface finishing and testing** - metal treatment processing and non-destructive testing.
