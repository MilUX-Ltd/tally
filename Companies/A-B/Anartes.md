# Anartes

> UK defence procurement and supply specialist providing COTS and bespoke hardware, including FPV drone systems, to the MOD, Armed Forces, police, and government agencies.

## What they do

Anartes is a Section 5 certified UK defence supplier providing specialist procurement, supply chain, and distribution services to the MOD, British Army, police, and government agencies. They source and supply commercial off-the-shelf and bespoke hardware, operating secure UK-based storage and distribution. The company also partners with manufacturers to bring emerging technologies including unmanned ground vehicles to the UK defence market.

## Products and solutions

- **FPV Drone Kits** - supply of 5-inch, 8-inch, and 10-inch quadcopter FPV drone systems, batteries, and goggles for British Army strike pilot training.
- **Defence Procurement Services** - specialist sourcing for MOD, law enforcement, and government customers.
- **Unmanned Systems** - supply of unmanned ground vehicles via partnership with XRC Robotics.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Section 5 Firearms Act certification; Armed Forces Covenant signatory
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
