# AeroGlow International

> Supplier of emergency escape and survivability lighting systems for military armoured vehicles.

## What they do

AeroGlow International, based in Salisbury, supplies survivability systems for military armoured fighting vehicles. The company has worked in soldier safety and escape lighting for around two decades. Its systems are in service on military platforms internationally.

## Products and solutions

- **HaLO** - emergency escape lighting for armoured vehicles, designed to aid crew egress.
- **VELS (Vehicle Escape Lighting System)** - the UK military designation for the escape lighting product.
- **EELS (Emergency Egress Lighting System)** - the US military designation for the product.
- Related capabilities include attitude warning, blast recording, handheld IR light detectors and bespoke capacitor packs.
