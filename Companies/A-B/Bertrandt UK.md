# Bertrandt UK

> UK entity of the German Bertrandt engineering group, which provides engineering and development services across automotive, aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Bertrandt UK Limited is the UK entity of the Bertrandt Group, a German engineering and development services company wholly owned by Bertrandt AG. The UK operation has a long-standing automotive engineering presence, including work as a Ford Tier 1 supplier from its Dunton site. At group level Bertrandt provides engineering and consultancy to the aerospace and defence sector, covering the lifecycle of military aircraft systems from analysis and design through development, verification and in-service support, plus structural design and safety-critical software development to standards such as RTCA DO-178C.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace and defence engineering services** - system development and integration, structural design and physical engineering for military aircraft platforms.
- **Safety-critical software engineering** - development to aerospace and functional-safety standards including DO-178C and IEC 61508.
