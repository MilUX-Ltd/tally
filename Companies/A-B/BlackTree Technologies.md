# BlackTree Technologies

> Supplier of tactical communications solutions to the UK Ministry of Defence.

## What they do

BlackTree Technologies provides tactical communications equipment to the UK MOD. Its work includes the Dismounted Data System, an AI-capable equipment package of radios, headsets, display tablets, cables, batteries, pouches and antennas designed to speed delivery of reconnaissance and intelligence data to soldiers. The company is registered in England with operations in Worcester and the West Midlands.

## Products and solutions

- **Dismounted Data System (DDS)** - integrated soldier system of AI-capable radios, headsets, tablets, batteries and accessories delivering reconnaissance and intelligence data at the dismounted level.
