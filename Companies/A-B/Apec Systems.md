# Apec Systems

> UK designer and manufacturer of bespoke covert camera and video surveillance systems for police, government and military customers.

## What they do

Apec Systems is an independently owned UK designer and manufacturer of bespoke camera and covert video surveillance solutions. It has operated in the government, law enforcement and security sectors for over 15 years, supplying short, medium and long-range surveillance camera systems to police, government agencies and military customers globally. The company emphasises VIP protection and operational surveillance.

## Products and solutions

- **Covert video surveillance systems** - bespoke camera solutions for short, medium and long-range surveillance.
- **Surveillance vehicle conversions** - whole-vehicle platform integration for mobile surveillance.
- **Deployed observation systems** - off-vehicle stand-off observation and intelligence gathering.
- **Camera technologies** - low-light, pan-and-tilt, thermal and infrared imaging, and CCTV/ANPR cameras with control systems.
