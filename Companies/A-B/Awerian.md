# Awerian Limited

> Technology consultancy in the homeland security and defence space, providing research, development and technology demonstrators for the UK and its allies.

## What they do

Awerian is a TTP Group company based near Cambridge, formed from an internal group within TTP plc that had worked in defence and related areas for over a decade. It provides research and development services intended to extend the ability of the UK and its allies to defend themselves. Its work runs from ideas generation through rapid design and implementation of technology demonstrators to complete product design and transfer to manufacture.

## Products and solutions

- **Science and technology** - applied research addressing defence and security problems.
- **Cyber** - cyber capability development.
- **Artificial intelligence and machine learning** - AI/ML applied to defence and security.
- **Autonomous systems** - design and development of autonomous capability.
- **Electromagnetics** - electromagnetics engineering, complemented by bespoke designs for technology demonstrators and special products.
