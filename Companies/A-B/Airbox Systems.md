# Airbox Systems

> UK developer of situational awareness and mapping software for defence, aviation and emergency services.

## What they do

Airbox Systems is an independent UK company founded in 2008 that develops software to help users visualise their environment and share operational data. Its tools cover planning, execution and after-action review, run across iOS, Android and Windows on laptops, tablets and phones, and can operate offline with stored maps and charts. The software is used by law enforcement, military, special forces, search and rescue, fire and medical emergency services.

## Products and solutions

- **ACANS** - Aviation Command and Aircraft Navigation System, situational awareness software for aircrews.
- **MOSAIC** - Mission Optimised Situational Awareness Information Collaboration, situational awareness software for ground users.
