# ADDEV Materials UK Limited

> Aerospace and defence materials specialist: chemicals, coatings, adhesives, films, tapes and custom packaging, with a UK operation in Hampshire.

## What they do

ADDEV Materials is a supplier of high-performance materials for the aerospace and defence industries, covering engineered chemical solutions, advanced converting and custom packaging. The group designs custom value-added materials for aircraft manufacturers, equipment suppliers, airlines, MRO providers, operators and defence integrators. Its UK presence was established in 2019 through the acquisition of the Graytone Group, including Pexa and PSG, and the UK entity (formerly Graytone Limited) operates from Bordon, Hampshire. Headquarters of the wider group are in Lyon, France.

## Products and solutions

- **Adhesives, sealants and surface treatments** - aerospace-specification adhesives, sealants, cleaners, solvents and surface preparation fluids.
- **Paints and coatings** - primers, topcoats, conformal coatings, corrosion inhibiting compounds (CICs) and specialty coatings; distribution of brands including AkzoNobel, PPG, Henkel and Sherwin-Williams.
- **Tapes, foams, films and composites** - technical films, tapes, foams, laminates, composite fabrics, prepregs and resins.
- **Converting and engineered components** - die-cut adhesives, foams, films, insulation, gaskets and bonding components.
- **Custom packaging (Andpak)** - repackaging of aerospace chemicals into aerosols, cartridges, syringes, tubes, pouches and bespoke formats.
- **Zip-Chem** - OEM-approved corrosion inhibitors, cleaners and lubricants for airlines, MROs and military operators.

The group serves commercial aviation, business aviation, MRO, space, air defence and land defence markets.
