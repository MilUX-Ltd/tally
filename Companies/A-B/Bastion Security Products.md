# Bastion Security Products

> UK manufacturer of modular, demountable secure room and armoured walling systems for government and defence use.

## What they do

Bastion specialises in modular, reusable secure room and partitioning systems. Its armoured walling and enclosure products provide burglar, bullet and blast resistance, along with resistance to forced entry and surreptitious attack. The bolt-together systems can divide an existing space, create five- or six-sided secure rooms of almost any size, or reinforce weak walls, and are demountable and relocatable.

## Products and solutions

- **BastionWALL A.C.E.** - CPNI-approved walling for UK Government use; meets the MOD physical security standard for highest-level secure containment.
- **BastionWALL4** - LPS 1175 SR4 rated walling system.
- **BastionWALL5** - LPS 1175 SR5 rated walling system.
- **Multi-Threat modular room and walling system** - upgradable on site as threat levels change; holds LPS 1175 Issue 8 certifications D5 to G5 with a 120-minute fire integrity rating.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; Cyber Essentials; Cyber Essentials Plus; CPNI approval; MOD physical security standard compliance; LPS 1175 product certifications (SR4, SR5, Issue 8 D5 to G5).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Made in Britain; Secured by Design.

## Ownership

Acquired by BeaverFit in September 2025, folding Bastion's modular walling into BeaverFit's force-protection and deployable-infrastructure offering. UK design and manufacturing retained.
