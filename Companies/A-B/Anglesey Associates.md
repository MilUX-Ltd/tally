# Anglesey Associates

> Defence and space consultancy linking niche suppliers to prime contractors and public sector clients in the UK and allied nations.

## What they do

Anglesey Associates provides consultancy and market research linking niche suppliers in the defence and space sectors to prime contractors and public sector clients in the UK and allied or NATO countries. It helps defence and space businesses enter new markets and pursue opportunities with the UK MOD and prime contractors. The team is drawn from former UK military aviators and defence procurement specialists, several ex-MOD or Dstl.

## Products and solutions

- **Analysis and research** - market intelligence, policy briefing and capability development including technology roadmaps and TRL/MRL work.
- **Product development** - storyboarding, bespoke collateral and innovation bids, with stated experience of DASA and Innovate UK submissions.
- **On-premises services** - joint project teams (P3M), tender support for MOD and other public bodies, and red teaming.
- **Bespoke software tools** - data analytics for supply chain analysis and live logistics tracking.
