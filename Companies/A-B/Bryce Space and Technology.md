# Bryce Space and Technology

> Technology and analytics consultancy serving the space, national security and aerospace and defence sectors.

## What they do

BryceTech provides analysis, forecasting and advisory work on the space economy, covering civil, commercial and national security space programmes. It offers market, investment, strategic and technology analyses, along with acquisition and programme support. The firm has UK operations registered as Bryce Space and Technology Limited, with the wider group headquartered in Alexandria, Virginia, United States.

## Products and solutions

- **Space economy analysis** - data-driven market, investment and technology analysis of the global space sector.
- **Aerospace and Defense practice** - a dedicated division supporting national security and defence customers.
- **Biosecurity and Emerging Threats practice** - analysis and advisory work on emerging national security threats.
