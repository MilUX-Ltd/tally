# Bentley Systems (UK)

> UK entity of Bentley Systems, a US infrastructure engineering software company whose tools are used for the design, construction and operation of large built assets.

## What they do

Bentley Systems (UK) Limited is the UK arm of Bentley Systems, Incorporated, a US-headquartered, listed infrastructure engineering software company. Its software is used to design, engineer, build and operate constructed assets including roads, railways, bridges, buildings, plants and utility networks. The UK entity holds a Crown Commercial Service framework agreement covering cloud software, and its products are used across UK government and defence estate and infrastructure programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **ProjectWise** - collaboration and common data environment for engineering project delivery.
- **AssetWise** - asset lifecycle information management.
- **iTwin** - digital twin platform for infrastructure assets.
- **OpenGround** - geotechnical data management.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** Crown Commercial Service (G-Cloud and CCS cloud software agreement)
- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus; UK MOD Cyber Defence and Risk (CyDR) accreditation cited by the company
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
