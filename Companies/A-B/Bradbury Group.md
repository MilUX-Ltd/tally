# Bradbury Group

> Manufacturer of physical security doorsets, grilles and steel security products, with products certified for use across UK government and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Bradbury Group is a UK manufacturer of physical security solutions, operating from a head office in Lincolnshire. It manufactures a portfolio of third-party accredited doorsets for security, fire, flood and acoustics, serving sectors including architecture and construction, utilities and defence. The business operates through four divisions: Bradbury Security, Extendor, Martin Roberts and Newton Security Doors.

## Products and solutions

- **Security doorsets** - steel security doors rated SR2 to SR4, plus fire, flood-defence and acoustic doorsets.
- **Security grilles and cages** - including LPS 1175 SR3 certified grilles, certified and CPNI-approved for use by UK government departments and critical national infrastructure.
- **Architectural and communal doors** - steel and aluminium entrance doors via the Martin Roberts and Newton divisions.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, LPS 1175 (LPCB), Secured by Design, CPNI approval for SR3 grilles.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
