# ATL Transformers

> Designer and manufacturer of small to medium magnetics and wound products, including for military applications.

## What they do

ATL Transformers designs and manufactures small to medium magnetics. The company was established as a family business in Greater Manchester by the Haide family in the early 1970s and is registered at Middleton, Manchester. Its products serve rail signalling, underground, marine, military, power generation and machine-building sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Transformers** - single and three-phase isolation transformers and specialist wound products up to 900kVA.
- **Reactors and inductors** - AC reactors, DC chokes and air-cored inductors.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015 (assessed by BSI); RISQS qualified; Achilles Link-up approved supplier to the railways.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
