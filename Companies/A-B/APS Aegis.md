# APS Aegis

> Specialist provider of high security perimeter protection, access control, detection and monitoring systems.

## What they do

APS Aegis supplies high security perimeter protection, access control, detection and monitoring systems for sensitive and critical sites. It offers land and marine protection, detection systems ranging from land-based stations to drone detection, access control with biometric integration, and full turnkey security solutions including installation and maintenance.

## Products and solutions

- **Anti-ram products** - tested and certified (K4, K12 and British Standards) rising road blockers, automatic bollards and a Tyre Killer Barrier System to protect against vehicular attack.
- **Detection systems** - passive and discreet sensor systems including land-based stations and drone detection, for ports, harbours, pipelines and other critical applications.
- **Access control** - centrally logged personnel and visitor control with biometric recognition integration.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; Achilles (UVDB, FPAL, Source Select); CHAS; Construction Line; Safe Contractor
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; United Nations supplier registration
