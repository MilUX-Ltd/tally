# Apache iX Limited

> Bristol-born P3M and technical services consultancy delivering change and secure cloud solutions to government and defence clients.

## What they do

Apache iX is a Bristol-born consultancy with a UK-wide team providing specialist P3M and technical services, including the design and development of secure cloud-based solutions, to government and industry clients across defence and adjacent sectors. The firm positions itself around helping clients cut through complexity to find a clear path to success, delivering tailored solutions across the change lifecycle. Its strapline is "Straightforward thinking for a complex world".

## Products and solutions

- **Strategy** - strategic advisory across the change lifecycle.
- **P3M** - portfolio, programme and project management services.
- **Business Analysis & Systems Engineering** - requirements, analysis and systems engineering support.
- **DevSecOps** - design and development of secure cloud-based solutions.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Professional bodies (team):** Team members hold APM, CMI, IET and SAFe certifications.
