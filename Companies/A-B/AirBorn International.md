# AirBorn International

> UK manufacturer of cable assemblies, looms and connectors for aerospace, defence and space.

## What they do

AirBorn International is the UK arm of AirBorn, a manufacturer founded in 1958 that began producing electronic connectors for the military and aerospace industries. The Edenbridge, Kent facility manufactures cable assemblies, looms and connectors for aerospace (civil and military), space, defence, geophysics and medical markets, with experience across telecoms, transportation and maritime environments. The wider AirBorn group operates manufacturing in the USA and the UK, with operations in Europe and Asia.

## Products and solutions

- **Cable assemblies and looms** - custom interconnect assemblies for aerospace, defence and space applications.
- **Connectors** - electronic connectors for military, aerospace and harsh-environment use.
