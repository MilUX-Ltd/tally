# Axillium Research Ltd

> Independent research and innovation agency that designs and manages collaborative R&D programmes across aerospace, automotive and defence.

## What they do

Axillium is an independent research and innovation agency working with businesses, public bodies and universities to deliver R&D projects, programmes and clusters. It brings people and technology together in collaborative research programmes that are complex and innovative, and supports companies in delivering technology innovation. It works across the aerospace, automotive and defence sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Collaborative R&D programmes** - design and management of multi-partner research programmes.
- **Innovation advisory and grant funding support** - support for companies accessing R&D funding and structuring innovation work.
