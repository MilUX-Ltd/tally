# Angloco

> British manufacturer of fire fighting and rescue vehicles, including airport crash tenders and vehicles for the UK Ministry of Defence.

## What they do

Angloco designs and manufactures fire fighting and rescue vehicles, with distribution and after-sales service worldwide. Its vehicles and equipment serve petro-chemical, industrial, airport, municipal, rescue and defence applications, with customers in over 50 countries. Most vehicles are custom-built to individual customer requirements.

## Products and solutions

- **Fire fighting vehicles** - aerial appliances, airport crash and rescue tenders, industrial and pumping appliances, tankers and special vehicles.
- **Fire boats** - 14m to 23m fire fighting and rescue boats.
- **Equipment** - Angloco Mist-Tech high-pressure misting systems and distributed rescue, foam and thermal imaging products.
- **Services** - commissioning, training, service, maintenance, repair and spare parts.
