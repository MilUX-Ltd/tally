# Ankasa

> UK systems engineering and technical assurance SME specialising in tactical communications and C4I programmes for the British Army and MOD.

## What they do

Ankasa provides independent systems engineering and technical assurance services to MOD teams and industry partners on complex defence communications programmes. Core specialism is tactical information systems, with sustained delivery on the British Army's Bowman/BCIP programme, Skynet 5, and current programmes including Future C4I and Land Deployable C4. Services include programme technical assurance, acceptance, governance, vendor-independent process development, and knowledge transfer.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems Engineering and Technical Assurance** - programme-level technical assurance and acceptance support for large-scale MOD communications programmes.
- **Tactical Network Expertise** - specialist knowledge of tactical routing, bearers and radios, network modelling, and battlefield management applications.
- **Programme and Bid Governance** - agile programme management, single-source contract support, and industry engagement.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR (registered September 2020); Aurora framework (registered November 2020)
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001 (certified January 2022); Cyber Essentials Plus (achieved November 2024)
