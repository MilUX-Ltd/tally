# Ambrey Baker Industrial Solutions

> Specialist industrial construction and hard facilities management provider serving the UK defence sector.

## What they do

Ambrey Baker is a turnkey industrial solutions and construction company working across food, pharmaceutical processing, advanced manufacturing, cold and chill storage, and the defence sector. For defence it delivers integrated capital projects and hard facilities management for secure, mission-critical environments, working with MOD stakeholders, defence contractors and estate managers. The company is based in Sleaford, Lincolnshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure design and build** - capital projects for secure and mission-critical defence facilities.
- **Hard facilities management** - asset lifecycle upgrades and maintenance for defence estates.
- **Refurbishment and remediation** - complex refurbishments, dilapidations and remedial works.
- **Surveys and compliance** - building condition, structural, hazardous materials and other technical surveys.
