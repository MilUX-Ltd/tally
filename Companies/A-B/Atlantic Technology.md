# Atlantic Technology

> UK electronics design house developing operational security and Tech Ops products for the law enforcement, national security and defence communities.

## What they do

Atlantic Technology specialises in the development of operational security products, ranging from high-performance network data processing to small, low-power data recorders. It provides commissioned product development services covering design, prototyping, manufacturing and support to the international law enforcement, national security and defence communities. It also develops hardware-enforced cyber security products.

## Products and solutions

- **Tech Ops electronics** - small form-factor, low-power equipment, including covert data recorders and a modular data record and egress product family.
- **Network processing and SDR** - high-speed network data processing and Software Defined Radio for data transfer in harsh RF environments.
- **Hardware-enforced cyber security products.**
- **Development services** - PCB design and manufacture, FPGA development, embedded software, mechanical design and in-service support.
