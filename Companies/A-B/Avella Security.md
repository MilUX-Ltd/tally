# Avella Security

> Managed security service provider delivering cyber and physical security to government and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Avella Security is a boutique managed security service provider working with central government and clients designated as Operators of Essential Services or part of the UK's critical national infrastructure. It holds multiple National Cyber Security Centre accreditations and delivers cyber and physical security services. The company is registered in London.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber security consultancy** - NCSC Assured Cyber Security Consultancy across Audit and Review, Risk Management and Security Architecture.
- **Cyber incident exercising** - NCSC Assured Service Provider of Cyber Incident Exercising.
- **Cyber Resilience Audit** - independent cyber audits for buyers on behalf of Cyber Oversight Bodies.
- **Security testing** - CHECK-approved penetration testing under the NCSC scheme.
- **Physical security services** - physical security to complement cyber services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus; NCSC Assured Cyber Security Consultancy; CHECK-approved.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Chartered Institute of Information Security.
