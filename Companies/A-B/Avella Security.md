# Avella Security

> Managed security service provider delivering cyber and physical security assessment services to government, the energy and utilities sectors, the NHS, defence, legal institutions and commercial enterprises.

## What they do

Avella is a boutique managed security service provider (MSSP) led by a team of security experts, including former commercial CISOs, ex-special-forces and intelligence operatives, and senior advisors to central government. Its experience, built through work with government bodies, the energy and utilities sectors, the NHS, defence, legal institutions and commercial enterprises, gives Avella insight into the specific threats its clients face. The company is registered in London.

## Products and solutions

- **Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) services** - accredited under the PQC ACSC pilot, covering discovery, migration planning and advice.
- **AI security practice** - expert advice and solutions for AI adoption, guiding mitigation of AI-specific risks from governance through deployment and ongoing resilience.
- **Cyber security consultancy** - NCSC Assured Cyber Security Consultancy across Audit and Review, Risk Management and Security Architecture.
- **Cyber incident exercising** - NCSC Assured Service Provider of Cyber Incident Exercising.
- **Cyber Resilience Audit** - NCSC Assured Service Provider of Cyber Resilience Audit.
- **Security testing** - CHECK-approved penetration testing under the NCSC scheme.
- **CISO and CIO expertise** - advisory built on a foundation of trust, experience and proven success across a range of industries.
- **Physical security services** - physical security to complement cyber services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus, DCC Level 0; NCSC Assured Cyber Security Consultancy; CHECK-approved.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Chartered Institute of Information Security.
