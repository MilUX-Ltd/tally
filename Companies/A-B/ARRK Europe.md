# ARRK Europe

> UK product development and manufacturing specialist providing prototyping, tooling and composites to aerospace, defence and other sectors.

## What they do

ARRK Europe provides product development and manufacturing services from sites at Gloucester and Nuneaton, with capabilities spanning additive manufacturing, vacuum casting, CNC machining, composites, precision engineering, tooling and injection moulding. Its Nuneaton technical centre supplies engineering, tooling and composite solutions to aerospace, automotive, motorsport and defence customers. ARRK Europe Limited is the UK operating company within the wider ARRK group, whose ultimate parent is ARRK Corporation of Japan.

## Products and solutions

- **Prototyping and rapid tooling** - 3D printing, vacuum casting, mock-ups and low-volume manufacturing.
- **Composites, engineering and manufacturing** - composite components and precision-engineered parts for aerospace and defence supply chains.
- **CNC machining and tooling** - machining, moulding and finishing services.
