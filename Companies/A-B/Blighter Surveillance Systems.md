# Blighter Surveillance Systems

> Designer and manufacturer of ground-based electronic-scanning radars for surveillance across land, coastline and air.

## What they do

Blighter designs and manufactures ground-based radars for surveillance of moving objects on the ground, coastline and in the air. The company delivers integrated multi-sensor packages comprising e-scan radars, cameras, thermal imagers, trackers and software. Its radars are deployed in 35 countries along borders, at coastal facilities, military bases and critical national infrastructure.

## Products and solutions

- **Blighter e-scan radars** - solid-state radars combining Passive Electronically Scanned Array technology with Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave and Doppler processing.
- **BlighterNexus** - software platform for sensor integration, sensor fusion and command-and-control (C2).
- **Multi-domain surveillance solutions** - ground surveillance, aerial threat detection and integrated sensor packages.
