# Allan Webb

> Supportability engineering and through-life support consultancy for defence and other regulated sectors.

## What they do

Allan Webb is a supportability engineering and asset-management consultancy. It provides support engineering, technical documentation, obsolescence management, NATO codification and integrated logistic support to reduce whole-life cost and increase operational availability. The company works across defence, aerospace, nuclear, rail, industrial and renewables, with many staff drawn from former Ministry of Defence inventory management roles. It operates from sites in Stonehouse, Gloucestershire and Barrow-in-Furness.

## Products and solutions

- **DiamondOM** - obsolescence management service for long-life equipment programmes.
- **Integrated logistic support** - ILS and integrated product support across the equipment lifecycle.
- **Technical documentation** - technical publications, illustration and S-Series standards support.
- **NATO codification** - codification and supply chain data services.
