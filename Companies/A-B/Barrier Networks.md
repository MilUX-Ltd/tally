# Barrier Networks

> Scottish managed security service provider delivering cyber defence, penetration testing and incident response, including to government and MoD customers.

## What they do

Barrier Networks is a Glasgow-based managed security service provider and value-added reseller. It provides defensive cyber operations, vulnerability management, penetration testing, governance and compliance, operational technology security and managed security operations. The company lists HMG and MoD among the sectors it serves and is certified under the NCSC Cyber Incident Response scheme.

## Products and solutions

- **Barrier Blue** - defensive cyber operations, threat detection and response.
- **Barrier Red** - offensive security and penetration testing.
- **Barrier OT** - operational technology and critical national infrastructure security.
- **Cyber Incident Response** - 24/7 managed incident response, certified under the NCSC CIR scheme.
- **Managed SOC** - managed security operations centre services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** G-Cloud supplier.
- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001; ISO 9001; Cyber Essentials Plus; IASME Gold; CREST (penetration testing and SOC); NCSC Cyber Incident Response certified.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
