# Arqit

> London quantum-safe encryption company providing symmetric key agreement technology for government, defence and telecoms.

## What they do

Arqit develops symmetric key agreement (SKA) technology for quantum-safe encryption, serving telecoms, government and defence customers. Its software lets a device download a lightweight agent that generates encryption keys locally with any number of other devices, avoiding the size, weight and power limitations of hardware encryption. The parent company, Arqit Quantum Inc, is registered in the UK and listed on NASDAQ under the ticker ARQQ.

## Products and solutions

- **SKA-Platform** - software platform for quantum-safe symmetric key agreement across devices and cloud.
- **SKA Edge Controller (SKA-EC)** - ruggedised, quantum-safe communications platform for forward-deployed operations, supporting secure command and control in contested and disconnected environments.
- **Encryption Intelligence (EI)** - automated cryptographic discovery to support post-quantum migration.
