# Accrofab

> Manufacturer of fabricated and machined components for the aerospace, defence and power generation sectors.

## What they do

Accrofab manufactures complex, high-precision machined and fabricated components, with experience in hard alloys and exotic materials. Capabilities include laser cutting, fabrication, welding, superplastic forming and heat treatment, supporting new product introduction, volume production and legacy platforms. The company operates UK facilities including sites in Alcester and Derby, the latter established in 1977.

## Products and solutions

- **Fabricated and machined components** - precision parts for commercial and military engine programmes and airframe platforms.
- **End-to-end manufacturing** - laser cutting, welding, superplastic forming and heat treatment under one supplier.
