# AdaCore

> Supplier of development and verification tools for safety- and security-critical software.

## What they do

AdaCore develops tools and provides support for the production of safety-critical and security-critical software. Its technologies are used by aerospace, defence and rail contractors for high-integrity systems including tactical command and control, missile and artillery launcher systems, and missile defence. The group is headquartered in New York, with the UK entity AdaCore Ltd (registered office in Yeovil) operating as a centre of excellence for safety- and security-critical software tools, opened in 2019.

## Products and solutions

- **GNAT Pro** - Ada/SPARK development toolchain for high-integrity software, with support for C/C++ and Rust.
- **SPARK Pro** - formal verification toolset for software with stringent security requirements, based on a formally verifiable subset of Ada.
- **QGen** - model-based code generator and static verifier for a safe subset of Simulink and Stateflow models.
