# Alpha Data Parallel Systems

> Supplier of FPGA-based reconfigurable computing boards and embedded systems for defence, aerospace and space.

## What they do

Alpha Data was founded in 1993 in Edinburgh and supplies commercial-off-the-shelf (COTS) reconfigurable computing platforms and support software. It works across digital signal processing, imaging, communications, military and aerospace, and high performance computing. The company also produces ruggedised and radiation-resilient components, including FPGA boards aimed at the space and NewSpace market.

## Products and solutions

- **FPGA computing boards** - reconfigurable hardware for embedded and high performance computing applications.
- **FPGA Full System Stacks** - integrated hardware and software platforms, developed in partnership with Missing Link Electronics, for defence and aerospace customers.
