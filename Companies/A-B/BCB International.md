# BCB International

> Cardiff-based manufacturer of survival, protective and mission-support equipment for the British Army and NATO forces.

## What they do

BCB International designs, manufactures and supplies survival, protective and mission-support equipment. It serves the British Army and the defence, security, government, humanitarian and maritime sectors worldwide. Many of its core products are standard issue to NATO troops, and over 200 of its products hold NATO approvals. The brand markets a heritage dating to 1854; the current legal entity was incorporated in 1979.

## Products and solutions

- **Soldier survivability and sustainment** - individual first aid kits, trauma pouches, haemostatic dressings, stretchers, survival medical kits, FireDragon fuel and stoves, water purification and shelter systems.
- **ATMIS multispectral camouflage** - signature management across UV, visual, NIR, SWIR, TIR and radar, including patrol cloaks and vehicle concealment nets.
- **Blast Boxers** - ballistic protective undergarments for groin and femoral protection.
- **Marine survival** - life-raft survival packs, marine pyrotechnics, water rations and self-inflating maritime body armour.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group.

## Ownership

Acquired by Wescom Group in January 2025 and integrated into Wescom Defence in August 2025 around four capability pillars: prime contracting support, operational infrastructure, soldier survivability and sustainability, and multispectral camouflage. The operating facility remains in Cardiff.
