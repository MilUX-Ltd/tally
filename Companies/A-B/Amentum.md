# Amentum

> A global engineering and project delivery company supporting UK defence, nuclear and energy programmes from its Warrington base.

## What they do

Amentum is a global engineering, project management and solutions integration company. Its UK arm is headquartered in Warrington and employs more than 6,000 people, providing programme, project and engineering services to major UK defence, nuclear and energy organisations. Its UK defence work includes safety and technology services for the Royal Navy's nuclear submarines, management of the UK military training estate for the Defence Infrastructure Organisation, and engineering support for the Atomic Weapons Establishment. The parent company is headquartered in Chantilly, Virginia, USA.

## Products and solutions

- **Submarine safety and technology services** - supporting the Royal Navy submarines that deliver Operation Relentless.
- **Hypersonic capability development** - systems engineering, mission planning, test design and safety systems under the UK's Hypersonic Technologies and Capability Development Framework.
- **Nuclear engineering and decommissioning** - design, safety case and project management at Sellafield and other UK nuclear sites.
- **Training estate management** - maintenance of the UK military training estate.
