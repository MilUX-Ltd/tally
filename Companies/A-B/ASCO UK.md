# ASCO UK

> Logistics, materials management and supply chain services for energy, defence and other critical industries.

## What they do

ASCO advises and operates logistics and materials management for customers across energy, utilities, metals and defence. Its services cover supply base operations, warehousing, freight forwarding, project cargo and customs handling. The company runs a network of control centres, fleet and facilities across multiple UK and international sites.

## Products and solutions

- **Supply base operations** - operation and development of supply bases for high-consequence operations.
- **Materials management and warehousing** - inventory and warehousing services for high-value builds.
- **Global freight services** - specialist cargo transport, freight forwarding, project cargo and customs handling.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR.
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, Cyber Essentials.
