# Border Holdings (Britpart)

> Independent wholesaler of parts and accessories for Land Rover vehicles, trading as Britpart.

## What they do

Border Holdings (U.K.) Ltd trades as Britpart, an independent wholesaler of parts and accessories for Land Rover and Range Rover vehicles. The company supplies customers worldwide, ranging from government agencies to trade outlets, across a catalogue of over 30,000 part numbers. Its range covers classic Series and Defender models through to current vehicles, relevant to military and ex-military Land Rover fleets still in service.

## Products and solutions

- **Britpart Land Rover parts** - own-label, OEM and genuine parts and accessories for Land Rover and Range Rover models, including Series and Defender vehicles used in military roles.
