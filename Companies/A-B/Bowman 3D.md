# Bowman 3D

> Production-volume additive manufacturing division of Bowman International Ltd, serving aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Bowman 3D is the additive manufacturing division of Bowman International Ltd, a UK bearing and precision component manufacturer based in Abingdon. The division uses powder bed fusion 3D printing, including HP Multi Jet Fusion, to produce components in volumes from single prototypes to tens of thousands of parts. It works with aerospace and defence customers supplying production parts as well as gauges, jigs, fixtures and placeholder components.

## Products and solutions

- **Additive manufactured components** - 3D printed parts in engineering-grade materials produced to be traceable, accurate and verifiable, for aerospace and defence applications.
- **Tooling and fixtures** - gauges, jigs, fixtures and placeholder components for aerospace OEMs.
