# BSL Express Spares

> Supplier and manufacturer of obsolete electronic and electromechanical components for defence and aerospace systems.

## What they do

BSL Express Spares sources, stocks, manufactures and supplies obsolete components, units and sub-assemblies used to keep electronic systems operational. It specialises in radar and communication spares for defence and commercial aircraft, and supplies parts for naval, land, communications, navigation, guidance, surface and submarine systems. The company is part of the UKC Group and is registered in Latchingdon, Essex.

## Products and solutions

- **Obsolete component supply** - sourcing and stock of legacy electronic and electromechanical parts.
- **Radar and communications spares** - parts for defence and civilian radar, communications and navigation networks.
- **NATO/NSN parts** - registered supplier and manufacturer of NATO stock-numbered parts.
