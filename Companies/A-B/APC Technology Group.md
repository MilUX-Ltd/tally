# APC Technology Group

> Specialist distributor and systems integrator of electronic components and sub-systems for the defence, avionics and aerospace sectors.

## What they do

APC Technology Group is a UK and Ireland distributor and specialist for over 100 global electronic-technology manufacturers, providing design-in, specification and sourcing of specialist electronic components, control technologies and connectivity products. Its main focus, accounting for the majority of its sales, is the defence, avionics and aerospace sectors, where mission-critical reliability is required. It supports defence manufacturers and military primes with high-reliability and MIL-STD technology for naval, land and avionic applications. It is part of Certify Holdings.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence-grade computing** - rugged laptops, tablets and displays, high-performance computing and MIL-STD VPX solutions for C4IS and military networking, built to SWaP-C constraints.
- **RF and microwave** - RF sensors, military-spec connectors and sub-systems for secure communications, navigation, electronic warfare and radar.
- **Mission-critical components** - analogue and mixed-signal ICs for flight control, navigation and engine management; rigid-flex PCBs; power and protection solutions.
- **Component sourcing and testing** - anti-counterfeit and electrical testing to AS6171 for UK defence and aerospace customers.
- **SCIF** - turnkey design and installation of Secure Compartmented Information Facilities.
