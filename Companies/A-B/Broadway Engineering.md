# Broadway Engineering

> Precision-machining manufacturer of high-complexity components for civil and defence aero-engine and airframe applications, and the founding business of the Broadway Group.

## What they do

Broadway Engineering manufactures high-complexity, tight-tolerance machined components for aero-engine, power-generation and airframe applications, serving UK and international civil and defence aerospace customers. It is the founding business of the Broadway Group, an aerospace manufacturing group that also includes Broadway AVPE and Broadway GET, offering machining through to finishing and inspection. The company is based in Bristol and dates its founding to 1938.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision machining** - grinding, milling and turning of structural components and assemblies for aero-engines and airframes.
- **Inspection and treatment** - non-destructive testing, pressure testing, heat treatment and surface treatments delivered across the group.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN 9100 (aerospace quality, incorporating ISO 9001); Cyber Essentials Plus.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; West of England Aerospace Forum; Midlands Aerospace Alliance.
