# Airbus Defence and Space

> The defence and space division of Airbus, designing and building military satellites, secure communications and space systems in the UK.

## What they do

Airbus Defence and Space is the military and space systems division of Airbus, formed in 2014 from the restructuring of EADS. In the UK it designs, manufactures and operates satellites and space systems, with major sites at Stevenage and Portsmouth and further capability in Newport, Guildford, Newcastle and Chippenham. Stevenage is a centre for satellite design, manufacture and operations; Portsmouth houses the largest clean room complex in the UK and develops satellite payloads.

## Products and solutions

- **Skynet** - military satellite communications systems for the UK Ministry of Defence, including the Skynet 6A satellite built in the UK.
- **Oberon** - two synthetic aperture radar satellites providing space-based intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance for the UK MOD.
- **Space systems** - telecommunications, Earth observation, scientific and meteorology satellites and spacecraft propulsion.
