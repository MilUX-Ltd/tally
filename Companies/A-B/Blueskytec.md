# Blueskytec

> Cyber security hardware that protects industrial control systems and operational technology at the device level.

## What they do

Blueskytec develops hardware-based cyber security for cyber-physical systems, driving protection down to the control-systems level. Its devices are designed for industrial control systems, operational technology, IoT and transport, and for safety-critical use in marine, industrial, high-voltage and nuclear environments, including space, reactor rooms and underwater marine settings. The company is based in Bath and describes its products as designed, built, manufactured and distributed in Great Britain.

## Products and solutions

- **KeySpace encryption technology** - hardware encryption establishing and defending the integrity of communications between edge systems, designed to counter advanced persistent threats and zero-day attacks.
- **Cyber-physical protection hardware** - devices for ICS, OT, IoT and transport across marine, land, air and space applications, designed to exceed IEC 62443 SL4.
