# Analytics Engines

> Belfast data and AI company, with a defence and security capability embedding AI into hardware platforms for edge analytics.

## What they do

Analytics Engines helps organisations use data and artificial intelligence across AI strategy, data science, data engineering and delivery enablement. It works as a data services consultancy rather than a single-product vendor. Alongside commercial verticals it has a defence and security thread, embedding AI directly into hardware platforms for analytics at the point of collection.

## Products and solutions

- **Panoramic AI** - named AI offering on the company's site.
- **Edge analytics for defence** - AI embedded in hardware platforms for real-time sensor data processing, anomaly detection and classification in austere or contested environments, keeping data secure and sovereign.
- **Service lines** - data science, data engineering, AI strategy and delivery enablement.
