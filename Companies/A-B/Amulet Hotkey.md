# Amulet Hotkey

> Designer and manufacturer of secure remote computing and infrastructure for government, defence and security organisations.

## What they do

Amulet Hotkey designs, manufactures and integrates secure remote computing solutions for government, defence and financial-services customers. Its infrastructure centralises compute in the data centre and delivers it to thin and zero clients and remote workstations, keeping data off endpoint devices. Products are engineered and manufactured in the UK and tested for use across land, air and sea in Five Eyes nations. The company was previously named Amulet Electronics Limited.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure thin and zero clients** - endpoint devices designed to keep data within the data centre, including the MagNOS zero client.
- **CoreStation remote workstations** - dedicated high-performance remote workstation platforms (RX360, RX660).
- **DX1500 secure client** - thin client designed for security and reliability.
