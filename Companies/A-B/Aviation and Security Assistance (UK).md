# Aviation and Security Assistance (UK)

> Provider of security, aviation and travel services to corporate, private and government clients.

## What they do

Aviation and Security Assistance (UK) Ltd is the UK entity of the ASA Group, established in 1999 and headquartered in Manila with permanent offices across Hong Kong, Thailand, Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, the Philippines, the UK and the US. The group describes itself as an Asia Pacific operations specialist, delivering tailor-made security, aviation and travel services to corporate, private and government clients, and extends its reach into Africa, the Middle East, Europe and the Americas through partner arrangements. The UK entity is a signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant and states that it recognises the value that serving personnel, reservists, veterans and military families bring to the business.

## Products and solutions

- **Aviation services** - ground handling and permits, air charter, air cargo, and slots.
- **Security services** - executive protection, secure transportation, maritime security, evacuation, bodyguard services, and technical surveillance counter measures (TSCM).
- **Consulting and investigations** - risk assessment, due diligence, investigations, crisis management and response planning.
- **Travel and concierge services** - fast-track immigration, meet-and-greet, and luxury concierge services.
- **Training** - security and travel-related training courses.
