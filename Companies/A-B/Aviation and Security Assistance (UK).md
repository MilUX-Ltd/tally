# Aviation and Security Assistance (UK)

> Provider of security, aviation and travel services to corporate, private and government clients.

## What they do

Aviation and Security Assistance (UK) Ltd is part of the ASA Group, established in 1999, which operates from registered offices in seven countries. The group delivers security, aviation and travel services to corporate, private and government clients, with a core specialism in the Asia Pacific region. The UK entity is a signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Aviation services** - ground handling and permits, air charter, and slots.
- **Security services** - executive protection, secure transportation, maritime security, and technical surveillance counter measures (TSCM).
- **Consulting and investigations** - risk assessment, due diligence, investigations, crisis management and response planning.
- **Training** - security and travel-related training courses.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Other:** Armed Forces Covenant signatory.
