# Auterion UK Ltd

> UK entity of Auterion, a developer of operating-system software for autonomous drones and robotic systems used in defence.

## What they do

Auterion develops a common operating system and software applications for autonomous systems, focused on the defence and enterprise sectors. AuterionOS coordinates drones across air, land and sea, and the company's hardware modules integrate autopilot, mission computing, AI autonomy, secure communications and edge computing. Auterion UK Ltd is the company's UK-registered entity; the wider group is headquartered in Arlington, Virginia, with a software hub in Munich and origins in Zurich, where it was founded in 2017.

## Products and solutions

- **AuterionOS** - an operating system for coordinated, large-scale autonomous drone operations across air, land and sea.
- **Skynode X** - an integrated module combining autopilot, mission computer, AI autonomy, secure communications and edge computing, designed to operate under GPS and video jamming.
- **Nemyx** - a defence system for turning autonomous drones into coordinated forces.
