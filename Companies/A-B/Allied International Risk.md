# Allied International Risk

> Provides protective security services and security training to defence, government, and private-sector clients.

## What they do

Allied International Risk Limited (AIRL) delivers protective services and security training to clients across defence, government, and private industry. Its work includes close protection and hostile-environment training. The company is a signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant and is based in High Wycombe.

## Products and solutions

- **Security training** - protective security and hostile-environment training courses.
- **Protective services** - close protection and risk management for clients in defence, government, and private sectors.

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
