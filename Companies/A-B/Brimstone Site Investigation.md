# Brimstone Site Investigation

> Unexploded ordnance risk-mitigation and explosive ordnance disposal specialist serving construction, infrastructure and brownfield development in the UK and internationally.

## What they do

Brimstone is a UXO (unexploded ordnance) risk-mitigation specialist for the construction, infrastructure and brownfield-development sectors. It describes itself as the only UK Government and MoD-assured contractor for explosive ordnance clearance and conventional munitions disposal. The team is drawn from explosive ordnance disposal personnel recruited from the British Army, Royal Navy and Royal Air Force.

## Products and solutions

- **UXO risk assessment and consultancy** - qualitative and quantitative desktop risk assessments, training, and a watching brief during site works.
- **UXO survey** - intrusive and non-intrusive land and marine surveys, including pushcart, towed and drone-borne magnetometry and geophysical survey.
- **Explosive ordnance disposal** - licensed EOD and conventional munitions disposal, delivered internationally.
- **Remnant** - a live UXO intelligence platform monitoring and visualising reported UXO incidents.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group; listed by the Geneva International Centre for Humanitarian Demining.
