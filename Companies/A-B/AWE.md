# AWE plc

> UK Ministry of Defence body responsible for the design, manufacture and in-service support of the warheads for the UK's nuclear deterrent.

## What they do

AWE designs, manufactures and supports the nuclear warheads for the UK's continuous at-sea deterrent, and applies its specialist expertise to wider national security work. Its activity spans the warhead life cycle, from concept, assessment and design, through component manufacture and assembly, to in-service support and eventual decommissioning and disposal. The organisation is owned by the Ministry of Defence and operates as a government body rather than a commercial supplier.

## Products and solutions

- **Warhead design and manufacture** - design, production and assembly of warhead components for the UK's nuclear deterrent.
- **In-service support** - maintenance and assurance of the warhead stockpile across its life cycle.
- **National security science and technology** - specialist research applied to wider defence and security challenges.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Regulated by the Office for Nuclear Regulation across its Aldermaston and Burghfield sites.
