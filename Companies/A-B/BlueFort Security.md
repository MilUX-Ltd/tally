# BlueFort Security

> Independent cyber security solutions partner serving the UK defence sector and other regulated industries.

## What they do

BlueFort Security is a UK cyber security solutions provider working across defence and other sectors including finance, public sector and healthcare. It operates as an independent Security Solutions Partner, using a framework of continuous discovery, validation and control to help clients assess, consolidate and optimise their security environments. For defence clients, BlueFort focuses on protecting mission-critical systems, information and operations from cyber threats.

## Products and solutions

- **External Attack Surface Management** - identification and management of an organisation's exposed assets.
- **Continuous Threat Exposure Management (CTEM)** - ongoing assessment of cyber exposure.
- **Identity and Zero Trust security** - cloud and identity security, including zero-trust identity access management.
- **Optimised SIEM and cloud security posture management** - monitoring and cloud security services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 27001, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
