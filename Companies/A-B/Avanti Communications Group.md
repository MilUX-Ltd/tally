# Avanti Communications Group plc

> UK satellite operator providing Ka-band connectivity to government, defence, enterprise and carrier customers.

## What they do

Avanti Communications is a satellite operator selling wholesale satellite broadband and connectivity to internet service providers, mobile network operators, enterprises, governments and other satellite operators. It operates a fleet of high throughput satellites alongside an international fibre network connecting its gateways and data centres. Its government and defence services cover remote bases, border security and disaster recovery using secure Ka-band communications.

## Products and solutions

- **HYLAS satellite fleet** - high throughput Ka-band satellites (HYLAS 1 and HYLAS 2) providing broadband connectivity across EMEA.
- **ARTEMIS** - a multiband satellite within the Avanti fleet.
- **Government and defence connectivity** - secure, resilient Ka-band services for military bases, outposts, border security and disaster recovery.
