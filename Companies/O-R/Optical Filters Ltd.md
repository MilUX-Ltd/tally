# Optical Filters Ltd

> Oxfordshire manufacturer of optical display enhancements and EMI/RFI shielding for avionics, automotive and defence.

## What they do

Optical Filters designs and manufactures advanced optical enhancements for display performance, including cover glass, display filters, optical bonding and touchscreen integration. The company also produces RFI/EMI shielding using its proprietary MicroMesh and EmiClare technologies. It serves the avionics, automotive and defence markets.

## Products and solutions

- **Display filters and enhancements** - contrast enhancement, anti-reflection and ruggedised cover lenses.
- **EMI/RFI shielding** - MicroMesh and EmiClare shielding for electromagnetic protection of displays.
- **Optical bonding and lamination** - optical bonding and touchscreen integration services.
