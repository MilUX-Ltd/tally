# Rohde & Schwarz

> Independent technology group supplying RF test and measurement, military radiocommunications, electronic warfare, and cybersecurity products to defence and government customers worldwide.

## What they do

Rohde & Schwarz is an independent, privately owned technology group, founded in Munich in 1933, that develops, produces and markets communications, information and security products for industry and government. Its Aerospace, Defense and Security division designs security, surveillance and intelligence solutions for national security and defence customers, alongside separate test and measurement, broadcast and networks and cybersecurity divisions. The company describes itself as one of Europe's largest providers of network and cybersecurity equipment, applying a "security by design" approach to mission-critical and classified-level communications. Its UK entity, Rohde & Schwarz U.K. Limited, has operated in the UK for over 45 years.

## Products and solutions

- **SOVERON radio family** - secure tactical VHF/UHF and strategic HF software-defined radios for armed forces, based on the Software Communications Architecture.
- **R&S M3AR** - multiband, multimode, multirole airborne radio fitted to jets, helicopters and uncrewed aircraft, with over 10,000 units sold.
- **SATURN waveform support** - support for the NATO-designated secure, interoperable SATURN waveform, deployed across thousands of radios.
- **NAVICS** - integrated navigation and communication system, in service on Royal Navy Type 26 frigates.
- **NEMACS** - directional, ultra-secure multi-link system connecting air, land, sea and space assets using directional SATCOM links designed to reduce RF signature and resist jamming and interception, unveiled in 2026.
- **Radiomonitoring and direction-finding** - transportable monitoring stations, direction-finding antennas and systems for civil and military spectrum monitoring.
- **Network encryption and zero-trust security** - high-speed network encryption and endpoint security products, with some product lines certified by Germany's BSI for classified official-use information.
