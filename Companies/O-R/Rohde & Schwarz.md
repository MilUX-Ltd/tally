# Rohde & Schwarz

> Independent technology group supplying RF test and measurement, military radiocommunications, and cybersecurity products to defence and government customers worldwide.

## What they do

Rohde & Schwarz develops, produces, and markets communications, information, and security products for industry and government. Its defence portfolio spans military radiocommunications, air traffic control, electronic warfare support, and government cybersecurity, alongside its market-leading position in wireless communications and RF test and measurement.

## Products and solutions

- **Military radiocommunications** - tactical and strategic radio systems for armed forces.
- **RF test and measurement** - industry-leading test equipment for wireless and broadcast applications.
- **Cybersecurity** - network security and encryption products for government and critical infrastructure.
- **Air traffic control** - communications and surveillance systems for civil and military ATC.
