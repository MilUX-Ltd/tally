# Ploughshare
> MOD-owned commercialisation vehicle that turns crown-owned intellectual property from DSTL, AWE and other government labs into licensed products and spinouts.

## What they do
Ploughshare is wholly owned by the UK Ministry of Defence and operates as the commercial licensing arm for government-developed IP. It negotiates licence agreements and creates spinout companies across four portfolios: defence and security, digital science, health and wellbeing, and climate and environment. The organisation works directly with R&D teams inside DSTL, AWE and other MOD science and technology establishments.
