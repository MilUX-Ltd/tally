# Project-Athena
> UK micro-SME developing modular, mission-configurable uncrewed aerial systems and payload integration for defence and security.

## What they do
Project-Athena designs and builds modular UAS platforms with a focus on mission-configurability and payload integration for defence and security customers. The company operates within the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park. Its approach to modular design allows payloads and mission systems to be swapped without redesigning the airframe.
