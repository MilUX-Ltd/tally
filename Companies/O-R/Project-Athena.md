# Project-Athena
> UK micro-SME developing modular, mission-configurable uncrewed aerial systems and payload integration for defence and security.

## What they do
Project-Athena designs and builds modular UAS platforms with a focus on mission-configurability and payload integration for defence and security customers. The company operates within the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park. Its approach to modular design allows payloads and mission systems to be swapped without redesigning the airframe.

Separately, a company trading as Project Athena Ltd is listed as a member of the Electech Innovation Cluster (Lancashire and Cumbria), described there as a specialist in UAV defence and countermeasure systems, with a supplier relationship to Edgar Brothers on the British Army's Fused Target Locator (FTL) programme. It is not confirmed from primary sourcing whether this is the same entity as the Dorset-based Project-Athena above, or a separate company with a similar name; flagged here for a future verification pass rather than merged into the page body above.
