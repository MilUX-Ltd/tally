# Orbit Fab Ltd

> In-orbit propellant supply and refuelling infrastructure company extending the lifespan and flexibility of spacecraft.

## What they do

Orbit Fab is building orbital refuelling infrastructure to provide a ubiquitous supply of propellant for government and commercial in-orbit assets, ending the single-use paradigm for spacecraft. Their UK team operates from the Harwell Science and Innovation Campus and leads missions focused on space sustainability.

## Products and solutions

- **RAFTI (Rapidly Attachable Fluid Transfer Interface)** - an open-licence, industry-standard docking and refuelling valve supporting multiple storable propellants, approved by the US Space Force as an accepted refuelling interface for military satellites.
- **GRIP** - the active-side docking and refuelling nozzle that mates with RAFTI.
- **RAVEN shuttle and NEST depot** - refuelling shuttle and propellant depot expanding the in-space refuelling network.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Other:** RAFTI's design follows CONFERS (Consortium for Execution of Rendezvous and Servicing Operations) best-practice standards.
