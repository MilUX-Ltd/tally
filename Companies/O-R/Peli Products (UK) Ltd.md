# Peli Products (UK) Ltd

> UK arm of Peli Products, manufacturing watertight, crushproof protective cases for military and hostile-environment applications.

## What they do

Peli Products (UK) manufactures the Peli Protector, Air, Storm, and Hardigg case ranges, designed for military, emergency services, and hostile-environment use. Many models carry Defstan 81-41 certification and US NATO stock numbers. The company offers a full customisation service including military packaging design.

## Products and solutions

- **Protector Cases** - watertight, crushproof, dustproof hard cases for equipment protection in field conditions.
- **Storm Cases** - lightweight, high-strength protective cases for transport of sensitive equipment.
- **Hardigg Cases** - heavy-duty transit and storage cases for military and industrial applications.
- **Military Packaging Design** - custom case configuration and foam fitting for specific equipment loads.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
