# PGI Protection Group International Ltd

> Intelligence and cybersecurity advisory firm supporting governments, corporations, and NGOs against digital threats.

## What they do

PGI provides intelligence and cybersecurity advisory services to nations and organisations across more than 90 languages, covering threat mitigation, regulatory compliance, counter-terrorism intelligence, cyber forensics, and security training. The company has worked with SMEs, large corporations, NGOs, and government clients worldwide.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber intelligence advisory** - Proactive threat mitigation and emerging digital threat analysis for government and enterprise clients.
- **Counter-terrorism intelligence services** - Intelligence support and advisory for government and national security customers.
- **Cyber forensics** - Digital and computer forensics services.
- **Security training** - Training in security and simulation for government and corporate clients.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 27001:2022, Cyber Essentials Plus, IASME Cyber Essentials, Defence Cyber Certification (DCC) Lv0-Lv3
