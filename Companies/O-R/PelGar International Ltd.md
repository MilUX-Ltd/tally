# PelGar International Ltd

> British manufacturer of CBRN countermeasures, rodenticides, insecticides, and repellents for public health pest control and chemical warfare defence.

## What they do

PelGar manufactures CBRN countermeasures against chemical warfare attack alongside a range of rodenticides, insecticides, and repellents for public health and agricultural pest control. The company maintains an active R&D programme drawing on market intelligence and technical feedback.

## Products and solutions

- **CBRN Countermeasures** - products providing protection against chemical warfare agents.
- **Insect Vector Control** - insecticides and repellents targeting public health pest species.
- **Rodenticides** - rodent control products for agricultural and public health applications.
