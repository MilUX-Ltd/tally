# Oxford Dynamics Limited

> UK sovereign AI defence company building multi-agent AI and robotics for sensing, sense-making and action at the tactical edge.

## What they do

Oxford Dynamics describes itself as a sovereign AI defence company building a unified system for sensing, sense-making and action, with AI and robotics at the edge and humans in control. It engineers autonomous systems, robotics and multi-agent AI for defence forces, with systems trained on real-world scenarios and engineered for regulated, contested domains. The company is based at Harwell Campus in Oxfordshire.

## Products and solutions

- **AVIS (A Very Intelligent System)** - flagship multi-agent AI engine; a modular, containerised data-interrogation and analytics platform providing explainable insights and command-and-control from tactical edge to command level in bandwidth-denied environments.
- **SR-1** - multi-sensor robot for drone detection, fusing EO/IR, RF, LiDAR and radar for passive scouting and AI-directed investigation.
- **STRIDER** - autonomous ground robotic platform for hazardous (CBRN, ordnance, logistics) environments with hot-swap payloads and beyond-line-of-sight tele-operation.
