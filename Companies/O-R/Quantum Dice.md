# Quantum Dice

> Oxford quantum company building certified quantum random number generators for cryptographic security.

## What they do

Quantum Dice is a University of Oxford photonics spinout developing self-certifying quantum random number generators (QRNGs) and photonics-based probabilistic computing hardware. Its DISC-certified QRNGs provide high-quality entropy for cryptographic security and the transition to post-quantum security. The company has partnered with Thales on QRNG-backed hardware security modules.

## Products and solutions

- **DISC QRNG** - self-certifying quantum random number generators for cryptographic key generation.
- **PCIe QRNG** - high-speed quantum entropy source.
- **ORBIT PPU** - photonics-powered probabilistic processing unit for optimisation and AI.
