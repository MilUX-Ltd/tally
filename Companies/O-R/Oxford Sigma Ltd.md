# Oxford Sigma Ltd

> Advanced materials engineering company serving fusion, nuclear, and defence applications.

## What they do

Oxford Sigma is an advanced materials company focused on fusion, nuclear, and defence systems. Founded in 2019, it develops and validates materials for extreme environments, applying a "right material, right place" approach that is agnostic to fusion confinement method (magnetic, inertial, or hybrid). Its work spans fusion-agnostic materials engineering, component design and prototyping, modelling and simulation (including neutronics and tritium migration analysis), irradiation experimental planning, and regulatory and standards alignment. The company has since expanded from its fusion origins into advanced fission and defence applications, and holds an in-house manufacturing capability for trial production and scale-up of specialist materials.

## Products and solutions

- **Structural and functional materials substantiation** - qualification and substantiation of fusion and fission core materials against extreme thermal, mechanical, and radiation conditions.
- **In-vessel component design** - design and concept development of plasma-facing structures, blankets, and magnets for fusion power plant developers.
- **Breeder and multiplier materials** - lithium ceramic materials development for tritium breeding, including the UKAEA-funded VICE (Validation in Ceramic Experiments) programme.
- **Plasma diagnostics** - development of diagnostic devices for fusion power plants, including enhanced mirrors and erosion sensors.
- **Materials manufacturing services** - in-house pilot batch and small-scale production, process scale-up, and controlled handling of sensitive materials (wet chemistry, powder processing, hydrogen handling), commissioned in 2026.
- **Defence materials solutions** - materials for shielding, propulsion, and containment in extreme thermal, mechanical, and radiation environments, including participation in the UK's Hypersonic Technologies & Capability Development Framework.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** UKAEA Embedded Engineering Resources Framework (Tier 1 contractor); UK Hypersonic Technologies & Capability Development Framework
- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials; ISO 9001; UK Defence Employer Recognition Scheme (Silver); Armed Forces Covenant signatory
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Fusion Industry Association (affiliate); The Fusion Cluster; Fusion Industry Taskforce; contributor to ASME Boiler & Pressure Vessel Code Section III Division 4 (Fusion Energy Devices)
