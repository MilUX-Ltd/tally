# Oxford Sigma Ltd

> Advanced materials engineering company serving fusion, nuclear, and defence applications.

## What they do

Oxford Sigma develops and deploys advanced materials technologies for extreme environment applications, with a track record in fusion energy. Its capabilities span advanced materials engineering, innovation and R&D, engineering substantiation, and testing, standardisation, and validation under extreme thermal, mechanical, and radiation conditions.
