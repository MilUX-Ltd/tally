# Ryobi Aluminium Casting UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of the global Ryobi Group, manufacturing high-pressure die cast aluminium components primarily for automotive OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers.

## What they do

Established in 1990 and based in Carrickfergus, Northern Ireland, Ryobi Aluminium Casting UK produces complex high-pressure die cast aluminium components, principally for the automotive sector, supporting lightweighting and emissions reduction goals. The company runs 24 die casting machines and over 50 CNC machining centres, employs around 550 people, and operates as part of the wider Ryobi Group, founded as a die casting company in Japan in 1943 with facilities across Japan, China, the USA, Mexico, Thailand and Europe.

## Products and solutions

- **High-pressure die cast aluminium components** - complex automotive components supplied to OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers including Ford, Volkswagen, Skoda, Peugeot Citroen, Jaguar Land Rover and Magna.
