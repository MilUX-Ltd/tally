# Ryobi Aluminium Casting UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of the global Ryobi Group, manufacturing high-pressure die cast aluminium components for aerospace and automotive OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers.

## What they do

Established in 1990 and based in Carrickfergus, Northern Ireland, Ryobi Aluminium Casting UK produces complex high-pressure die cast aluminium components for major OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers, supporting lightweighting and emissions reduction goals. The company employs over 500 people and operates as part of the wider Ryobi Group.
