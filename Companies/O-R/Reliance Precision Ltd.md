# Reliance Precision Ltd

> Manufacturer of customised precision electro-mechanical components and assemblies for high-performance applications.

## What they do

Reliance Precision designs and manufactures precision electro-mechanical components and sub-assemblies to demanding specifications for high-performance sectors including aerospace and defence. The company operates from its facility in Huddersfield, Yorkshire.
