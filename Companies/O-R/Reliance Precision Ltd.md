# Reliance Precision Ltd

> Manufacturer of customised precision electro-mechanical components and assemblies for high-performance applications.

## What they do

Reliance Precision designs, develops, prototypes, manufactures, assembles and tests precision electro-mechanical components and sub-assemblies to demanding specifications for high-performance sectors including space, defence and industrial applications. The company operates from its facility at Rowley Mills, Huddersfield, with a second manufacturing site in Ireland.

## Products and solutions

- **Reli-a-Flex flexible shaft couplings** - precision motion transmission components.
- **Linear and space actuators** - actuation mechanisms supplied for space platforms, including the Eurostar Neo geostationary telecommunications satellite platform.
- **Precision gear manufacturing** - custom gear production for high-reliability applications.
- **Clean-room assembly** - laminar flow clean-room facilities for sensitive sub-system assembly.
