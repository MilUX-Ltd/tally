# Prolinx

> UK-owned SME providing secure, sovereign hosting and managed IT services across all government security classifications for defence and the public sector.

## What they do

Prolinx is a UK-owned and based SME, established in 1997, delivering secure hosting and managed services covering all government security classifications. It operates a secure multi-tier data centre with a 24/7 service desk, a Network Operations Centre and a Security Operations Centre, accredited to government security standards. Its stated customer base includes the Ministry of Defence, Defence Digital, the British Council and the higher-education sector.

## Products and solutions

- **Citadel** - UK-sovereign, fully managed end-user collaboration service that lets organisations handle protectively marked data securely while using tools such as Microsoft 365 and CAD.
- **Citadel Secure** - fully managed secure service assured to the OFFICIAL-SENSITIVE classification, with 24/7 support and a scalable consumption model.
- **Citadel Red** - fully managed secure service for higher security classifications, in use within specific areas of the MOD and national security agencies across multiple classifications.
- **Prolinx Assured Cloud (PAC)** - assured cloud and collaborative working environment listed on the G-Cloud Digital Marketplace.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001 (Quality Management); ISO/IEC 20000-1 (IT Service Management); ISO/IEC 27001 (Information Security Management).
- **Memberships:** techUK; Armed Forces Covenant signatory.
