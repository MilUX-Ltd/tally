# Protronix Limited

> Engineering services company designing, developing, and manufacturing custom electronic systems for military and high-reliability applications.

## What they do

Founded in 1994 and based in Bellshill, Scotland, Protronix designs and manufactures custom electronic equipment and integrated systems for military, medical, automotive, and industrial customers. The company specialises in system design and integration, delivering solutions that meet high-reliability and defence-grade requirements.
