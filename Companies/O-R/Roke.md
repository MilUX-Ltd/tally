# Roke

> British defence and national-security technology and engineering company specialising in sensors, communications, electromagnetic warfare, cyber, AI and autonomy, part of Chemring Group.

## What they do

Roke integrates physical and digital systems to solve complex challenges across communications, sensors, cyber security, artificial intelligence and autonomy. It operates in the defence, national security and geospatial intelligence sectors for public and private clients, delivering advanced research and development, services and technologies. It is also a recognised crypto test house for UK Government.

## Products and solutions

- **CORTEXA GUARDIAN** - modular, mast-mounted system combining RADAR and PTZ sensor inputs for autonomous detect, track and identify, enhancing counter-UAS.
- **EM-Vis Deceive** - electromagnetic attack system.
- **EM-Vis Resolve** - electromagnetic warfare system for interception and location of radio communications signals.
- **Pattern of Life** - cyber-range solution providing realistic user emulation to test security teams and optimise cyber ranges.
- **Tactical wideband EW sensor** - adaptive beamforming and direction finding for electromagnetic awareness in contested environments.
- **Dismounted EW sensor** - field-proven find, fix and intercept against peer threats.
- **Acoustic sensor system** - detection and geolocation of artillery fire.
