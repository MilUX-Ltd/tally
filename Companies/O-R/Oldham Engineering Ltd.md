# Oldham Engineering Ltd

> Defence and nuclear sector manufacturer offering coded fabrication, CNC machining, complex assembly and testing from Greater Manchester.

## What they do

Oldham Engineering is a preferred supplier to the defence and nuclear industries, trading for over 160 years and operating across 80,000 square feet at its Oldham site plus a second site in Sheffield. The company offers in-house coded fabrication, medium-heavy CNC machining, and complex assembly and testing, specialising in high-integrity assemblies, bespoke and batch complex fabricated and machined components, and turnkey project management. Beyond defence and nuclear, the company also serves the oil and gas, subsea, aerospace, and rail sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Coded fabrication and welding** - fabrication to AWS D1.1, BS EN ISO 15614, ANSI, and NORSOK codes.
- **Medium-heavy CNC machining** - bespoke and batch complex machined components.
- **Complex assembly and testing** - high-integrity assemblies with turnkey project management.
- **Nuclear engineering services** - bespoke fabrications, decommissioning engineering, heavy machining, and welding for the nuclear sector.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001 (certificate FM24503, UKAS-accredited via BSI)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; associate member of the Nuclear AMRC; signatory to the Nuclear Decommissioning Authority supply chain charter

## Recent news

- **2025** - Oldham Engineering acquired by ASDAM (Australian Sovereign Defence and Advanced Manufacturing Group), a CPE Capital portfolio company; a full exit for the Dunigan family, owners since 1993, with existing management continuing. [source](https://www.corbettkeeling.com)
