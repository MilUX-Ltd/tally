# Oldham Engineering Ltd

> Defence and nuclear sector manufacturer offering coded fabrication, CNC machining, complex assembly and testing from Greater Manchester.

## What they do

Oldham Engineering is a preferred supplier to the defence and nuclear industries, operating across 80,000 square feet with in-house coded fabrication, medium-heavy CNC machining, and complex assembly and testing. The company specialises in high-integrity assemblies, bespoke and batch complex fabricated and machined components, and turnkey project management.
