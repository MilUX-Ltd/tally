# QinetiQ Training & Simulation Ltd

> Provider of simulation and analysis-driven decision support and training systems for military and national security customers.

## What they do

QinetiQ Training & Simulation Ltd (QTSL) develops and delivers decision training systems from the tactical to theatre level, supporting the Contemporary Operating Environment for military and National Security and Resilience markets. Their systems are in use at the UK Defence Academy and international staff colleges.

## Products and solutions

- **JOCASTS** - Theatre-level joint operations command and staff training system, used by the UK Defence Academy and International Staff Colleges.
- **CONTACT range** - Products covering brigade, urban, resilience, education, and analysis training requirements.
