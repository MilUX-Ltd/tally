# PusulaIT Ltd

> Software engineering and technology consultancy serving defence, security and public sector clients.

## What they do

PusulaIT specialises in secure software development, cloud-native solutions and identity and access management for defence and security clients. The company works across AWS, Azure and Kubernetes environments, delivering system integration alongside cloud and security engineering.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure software development** - bespoke application development for defence and security clients.
- **Identity and access management** - IDAM services for government and defence customers.
- **Cloud-native engineering** - solution design and delivery across AWS, Azure and Kubernetes.
