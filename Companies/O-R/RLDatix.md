# RLDatix

> Global provider of workforce planning and risk management solutions for the defence and maritime sectors.

## What they do

RLDatix's Government and Commercial business unit delivers workforce solutions to global defence and maritime organisations, helping partners ensure they can respond to a growing list of global threats. It states its software gives organisations real-time visibility of the skills, qualifications and experience of their people, and unifies workforce planning, compliance and performance in a single system. Named partners include the Royal Fleet Auxiliary, NATO and the Australian Defence Force.

## Products and solutions

- **OneView** - force readiness and resource management platform unifying workforce planning, compliance and performance tracking, used by the Royal Fleet Auxiliary, NATO Special Forces and the Australian Defence Force.
