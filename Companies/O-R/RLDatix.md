# RLDatix

> Global provider of workforce planning and risk management solutions for the defence and maritime sectors.

## What they do

RLDatix's Government and Commercial business unit delivers workforce solutions to global defence and maritime organisations, helping partners ensure they can respond to a growing list of global threats. Named partners include the Royal Fleet Auxiliary, NATO, and the Australian Defence Force.
