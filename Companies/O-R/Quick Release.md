# Quick Release

> Product and engineering data management consultancy with a dedicated defence practice serving aerospace, space and defence programmes.

## What they do

Quick Release provides product and engineering data management services, working alongside major manufacturers, OEMs and tier suppliers to build capability, accelerate development programmes and improve engineering team efficiency. Recognised as a leading consultancy for automotive, the company is increasingly partnering with space, defence and aerospace organisations, and has a dedicated defence practice supporting these programmes with data and programme management expertise.

## Products and solutions

- **Master Product Data** - engineering change management, product data management (PDM) and product lifecycle management (PLM) services to improve engineering data quality and flow from design through to in-service upgrades and maintenance.
- **Prototype Build and Launch Operations** - support for new product introduction (NPI) processes, tooling and infrastructure to support volume ramp-up, including for defence manufacturers.
- **Production, Service and Maintenance** - engineering support to increase repair flowline throughput, delivered to jet engine OEMs among other customers.
- **Operational Performance Improvement and Enterprise Lifecycle Transformation** - process development, deployment and business consultancy services spanning aerospace, defence, space, automotive, medical devices, rail and robotics sectors.
