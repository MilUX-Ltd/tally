# Parmley Technologies Ltd

> North East manufacturer of electronic control panels, PCBs and harnesses for military vehicles and harsh-environment industrial applications.

## What they do

Parmley Technologies designs and manufactures electronic control products for military vehicles and other industrial applications operating in harsh environments, from its facility on the Tyne Tunnel Trading Estate, North Shields. The company states it has over 20 years of experience in the field and has more than 100 different types of control panels installed on military vehicles in the UK, North America and other European countries, including platforms such as Terrier, Challenger II-based Trojan/Titan vehicles, Stryker, the Assault Breacher Vehicle, the Joint Assault Bridge, Patria vehicles and RUAG platforms. It also supplies industrial products for harsh environments, including for underwater platforms and subsea remotely operated vehicles (ROVs).

## Products and solutions

- **Electronic control panels** - control panels for military vehicles, manufactured in-house.
- **Power distribution units** - electronic power distribution for vehicle and platform systems.
- **Hydraulic and pneumatic control systems** - control systems for vehicle and industrial applications.
- **Joystick consoles** - operator control consoles for vehicle systems.
- **PCBs and harnesses** - in-house design, manufacture and repair capability.
- **Industrial and subsea products** - control products for harsh-environment industrial use, including underwater platforms and ROVs.
