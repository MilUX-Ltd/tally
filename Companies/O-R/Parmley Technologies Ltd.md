# Parmley Technologies Ltd

> North East manufacturer supplying components, platform structures, platform systems, and PPE to the defence sector.

## What they do

Parmley Technologies manufactures and supplies a broad range of defence-related products from its Tyne Tunnel Trading Estate facility, including components, platform structures, platform systems, propulsion elements, and PPE including webbing. The company covers construction and infrastructure supply alongside equipment and technology manufacture.
