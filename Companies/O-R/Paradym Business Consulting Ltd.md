# Paradym Business Consulting Ltd

> Management consultancy focused on strategic and operational challenges in the defence and security sectors.

## What they do

Paradym Business Consulting is a management consultancy that draws on more than 20 years of experience in the defence and security sectors to support organisations addressing complex strategic and operational issues. It operates an owner-practitioner model, supplemented by a network of associates, and states its approach is tailored to each client's specific needs rather than a standard methodology. The company describes its focus as delivering systemic change proven effective in regulated environments, combining transformational change expertise with operational experience.

## Products and solutions

- **Strategic and operational consultancy** - support for organisations in the defence and security sectors facing complex challenges, delivered through a bespoke, client-specific approach.
- **Transformational change support** - guidance on systemic change in regulated environments, drawing on the owner's and associates' defence and security sector experience.
