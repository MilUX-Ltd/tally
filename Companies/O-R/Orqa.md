# Orqa

> Croatian manufacturer of FPV drone goggles, components and complete unmanned aerial systems.

## What they do

Orqa is a Croatian drone-tech company headquartered in Osijek that designs and manufactures first-person view (FPV) equipment and complete unmanned aerial systems. It began with the FPV.One goggles for the consumer and prosumer market and has expanded into mission-ready aerial robotics for defence and security use. Development and production are based in Osijek, with products marketed as made in Europe.

## Products and solutions

- **FPV.One goggles** - FPV goggle systems for piloting drones, including the FPV.One Pilot and OLED variants.
- **FPV MRM-2 Interceptor** - an FPV interceptor drone reported to be in use by the Croatian military.
- **FPV components and systems** - NDAA-compliant, Chinese-component-free FPV drones produced at the Osijek facility.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Manufacturing:** states an annual production capacity of around 280,000 NDAA-compliant FPV drones.
