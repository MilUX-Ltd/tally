# Oxford Quantum Circuits Ltd

> UK quantum computing company delivering quantum-computing-as-a-service via superconducting circuits.

## What they do

Oxford Quantum Circuits (OQC) is Europe's first quantum-computing-as-a-service (QCaaS) provider and the only company deploying quantum systems into commercial data centres. Its core technology uses a patented 3-D superconducting circuit architecture designed to scale whilst maintaining speed, with a sovereign and secure-by-design offering.
