# Range403
> UK micro-SME delivering cyber training and Capture the Flag events for the defence and security sector.

## What they do
Range403 describes itself as a cyber training platform built to meet the cybersecurity training needs of educational institutions, corporations, and defence and government organisations. It positions its approach around hands-on, real-world scenarios rather than theoretical learning, with challenges it says are designed by professionals from the relevant field. Its first public event was an Easter-themed Capture the Flag challenge launched in March 2026. The platform is offered in both cloud-based and on-premise deployments.

## Products and solutions

- **Educational track** - curriculum-aligned challenges intended to complement academic coursework in cybersecurity, computer science and information technology programmes.
- **Enterprise track** - customisable training programmes addressing an organisation's specific technology stack and threat model.
- **Specialised track** - advanced scenarios for defence, government and intelligence personnel.
- **Capture the Flag / tournament hosting** - a tournament platform with live scoreboards, automated challenge deployment, leaderboards and team-based challenge modes.
