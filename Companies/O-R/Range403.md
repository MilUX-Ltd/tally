# Range403
> UK micro-SME delivering cyber training and Capture the Flag events for the defence and security sector.

## What they do
Range403 is an early-stage UK venture building cyber talent through custom training events and Capture the Flag challenges. Its first event was an Easter-themed CTF launched in March 2026. The company describes its purpose as growing and educating cyber talent through tailored challenge and educational packages, with a focus on the defence and security market.
