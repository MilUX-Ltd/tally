# PRP Optoelectronics Limited

> Designer and manufacturer of high-performance LED-based displays and systems for defence and professional applications.

## What they do

PRP Optoelectronics specialises in the design and manufacture of LED displays, monolithic LED chips, hybrids and related systems for military and civil avionics. The company offers complete in-house design, development and manufacturing capability for custom LED solutions.

## Products and solutions

- **LED Displays** - Custom LED display units for cockpit and professional applications.
- **Monolithic LED Chips** - Chip-level components for integration into hybrid and system assemblies.
- **Avionics Lighting Systems** - Specialist LED assemblies for military and civil aviation platforms.
