# Oxford Space Systems

> Deployable antennas and structures for satellites and defence space missions.

## What they do

Oxford Space Systems, based at Harwell Campus in Oxfordshire, designs and manufactures deployable antennas and structures for spacecraft. Its wrapped rib antenna technology enables high-resolution synthetic aperture radar (SAR) on smaller, lower-cost satellite platforms. The company has been supported by the UK Defence and Security Accelerator (DASA) and the Defence Science and Technology Laboratory (Dstl), and was selected to supply the antenna for the Airbus-led Oberon SAR demonstrator.

## Products and solutions

- **Wrapped rib deployable antenna** - compact deployable antenna for synthetic aperture radar.
- **Deployable structures and antennas** - helical antennas and deployable booms and structures for satellites.
