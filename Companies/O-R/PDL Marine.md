# PDL Marine

> Portsmouth marine engineering firm building aluminium and steel landing craft and providing vessel fabrication, repair and refit for commercial, defence and specialist marine sectors.

## What they do

PDL Marine designs and builds aluminium and steel landing craft for commercial, harbour, aquaculture and defence-sector applications, supporting personnel transfer, cargo movement, beach landing and marine logistics. Alongside new build, it provides structural fabrication, coded welding, ship repair, refit and project-based engineering support. It operates from Portsmouth (Trafalgar Wharf) and Devonport, serving the South Coast and wider UK.

## Products and solutions

- **Landing craft** - aluminium and steel landing craft for commercial, harbour, aquaculture and defence use.
- **New vessel build** - workboats and specialist craft.
- **Marine fabrication** - structural steel and aluminium work, coded welding.
- **Ship repair and refit** - maintenance, refit and project-based marine engineering support.
