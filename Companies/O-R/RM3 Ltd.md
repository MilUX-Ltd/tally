# RM3 Ltd

> Bespoke training equipment and software solutions for the military, policing, and security services, led by experienced ex-military and police professionals.

## What they do

RM3 Ltd designs bespoke training packages including fabricated Method of Entry training apparatus and remote immersive app-based Learning Management Systems for military, policing, security services, and commercial clients. The company is led by ex-military and police professionals with decades of operational and instructional experience. RM3 Ltd is registered in England and Wales (company number 14671887), with a registered office in Hereford.

## Products and solutions

- **Method of Entry training apparatus** - bespoke fabricated equipment for Method of Entry training.
- **Learning Management System** - remote, immersive app-based learning platform.
- **Instructor-based training modules** - practical, instructor-led training delivery.
- **Train-the-Trainer (T3) programmes** - programmes to develop in-house instructor capability for customer organisations.
