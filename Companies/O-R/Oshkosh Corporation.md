# Oshkosh Corporation

> US-headquartered defence and industrial manufacturer producing specialist vehicles, components and weapons systems for military and government customers worldwide.

## What they do

Oshkosh Corporation designs and manufactures purpose-built vehicles, defence systems and components for military and government operators through its Oshkosh Defense business. Its defence portfolio includes tactical wheeled vehicles, protected mobility platforms and associated sustainment services, delivered to the US military and allied foreign governments. Oshkosh Defense maintains a UK branch office in Bristol, registered at Bristol Business Park, alongside its global manufacturing base in the United States.

## Products and solutions

- **JLTV / eJLTV (Joint Light Tactical Vehicle)** - light tactical vehicle family with the TAK-4i intelligent independent suspension system, scalable protection levels and integrated plug-and-play C4ISR provision; a hybrid-electric variant (eJLTV) has also been shown publicly. Used by the US military and allied forces including Lithuania, Slovenia, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Mongolia, Belgium, Slovakia, Brazil, Romania, Israel and the Netherlands.
- **Family of Medium Tactical Vehicles (FMTV) A2** - medium tactical wheeled vehicles supplied to the US Army.
- **Family of Heavy Tactical Vehicles (FHTV) / Palletized Load System (PLS) A2** - heavy tactical vehicles and load-handling systems supplied under US Army contract.
- **ROGUE-Fires / L-MAV** - remotely operated and autonomous multi-mission ground vehicle platforms built on Oshkosh's tactical vehicle base, developed for the US Marine Corps and demonstrated with third-party mission payloads.
