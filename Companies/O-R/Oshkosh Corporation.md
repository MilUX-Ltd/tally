# Oshkosh Corporation

> US-headquartered defence and industrial manufacturer producing specialist vehicles, components and weapons systems for military and government customers worldwide.

## What they do

Oshkosh Corporation designs and manufactures purpose-built vehicles, defence systems and components for military and government operators. Its defence portfolio includes tactical wheeled vehicles, protected mobility platforms and associated systems, with manufacturing and programme management functions based in the UK at Bristol.
