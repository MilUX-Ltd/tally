# PQShield Ltd

> Post-quantum cryptography specialist delivering hardware and software security upgrades for the global technology supply chain.

## What they do

PQShield develops post-quantum cryptography (PQC) solutions, stating it contributed to the first international PQC NIST standards and was the first cybersecurity company to develop quantum-safe cryptography on chips, in applications and in the cloud. Its products address the security threat posed by quantum computing to existing encryption, serving markets that include semiconductors and manufacturing, identity and payments, military and aerospace, system integrators, automotive, industrial IoT, and network and telecommunications.

## Products and solutions

- **PQMicroLib-Core** - ultra-small, baremetal post-quantum cryptography library for embedded and memory-constrained devices, with a footprint as low as 5KB.
- **PQCryptoLib-Core** - FIPS 140-3 CMVP certified, CAVP compliant general-purpose cryptographic library supporting classical, post-quantum and hybrid algorithms on Linux, Windows and Mac.
- **PQCryptoLib-SDK** - PQCryptoLib-Core packaged with an OpenSSL 3.x provider to help transition OpenSSL-based applications to post-quantum algorithms including ML-DSA, Falcon and ML-KEM.
- **PQPlatform-CoPro** - FIPS 140-3 CAVP compliant hardware cryptographic coprocessor adding post-quantum capability to existing security subsystems, with side-channel protection.
- **PQPlatform-TrustSys** - PQC-focused Root-of-Trust solution supporting NIST-standardised ML-KEM and ML-DSA alongside traditional ECC and RSA, with fault-tolerance and side-channel countermeasures.
- **PQPerform-Flare / PQPerform-Inferno / PQPerform-Flex** - hardware-accelerated post-quantum cryptography products for high-throughput, low-latency use cases including TLS and network HSMs.
