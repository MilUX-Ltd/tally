# PQShield Ltd

> Post-quantum cryptography specialist delivering hardware and software security upgrades for the global technology supply chain.

## What they do

PQShield develops post-quantum cryptography solutions, contributing to new global standards and delivering real-world hardware and software upgrades. Their work addresses the security threat posed by quantum computing to existing encryption across defence, government and commercial technology systems.
