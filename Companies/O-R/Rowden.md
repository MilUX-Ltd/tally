# Rowden

> Engineering company building sensing and information systems for constrained, degraded, and edge environments.

## What they do

Rowden Technologies designs and manufactures systems that enable data sensing, processing, and sharing in conditions of limited connectivity. Its products include edge device management solutions and the Dismounted Data System (DDS), fielded with the British Army. It also delivers mission systems programmes for the UK Government, combining commercial technology with manufacturing and engineering depth.

## Products and solutions

- **Dismounted Data System (DDS)** - sensing and data-sharing system fielded with the British Army.
- **Edge device management** - products for managing devices in degraded and disconnected environments.
- **Mission systems** - bespoke capability delivery for UK Government programmes.
