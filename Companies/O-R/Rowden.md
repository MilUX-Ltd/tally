# Rowden

> Engineering company building sensing and information systems for constrained, degraded, and edge environments.

## What they do

Rowden Technologies designs and builds sensing, networking and edge-compute information systems for constrained, degraded and contested environments, where communications are unreliable and decisions are time-critical. Its stated customers include the UK Ministry of Defence and emergency services. Its products include edge device management solutions and the Dismounted Data System (DDS), fielded with the British Army as part of the ASGARD programme, alongside the Mimic RF decoy system for land forces. It also delivers mission systems programmes for the UK Government, combining commercial technology with manufacturing and engineering depth, and describes a design philosophy of "walk-up operation" so systems can be used by non-specialists across a wider force.

## Products and solutions

- **Dismounted Data System (DDS)** - sensing and data-sharing system fielded with the British Army under the ASGARD programme.
- **Mimic** - compact, tactical RF decoy system for land forces, designed for large-scale deployment with a browser-based interface intended to bring most soldiers to proficiency in under two hours.
- **Edge device management** - deployment and management of machine learning models and mixed fleets of networked devices in degraded and disconnected environments.
- **Mission systems** - bespoke capability delivery for UK Government programmes.
