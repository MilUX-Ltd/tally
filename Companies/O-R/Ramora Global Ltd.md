# Ramora Global Ltd

> EOD and explosive disposal specialist providing bomb disposal, site remediation, EOD search, and training services to defence and emergency services customers.

## What they do

Ramora UK delivers explosives and security-related services to commercial and government sectors, including governments, corporations, military and law enforcement agencies internationally. Its offer spans reactive 24-hour bomb disposal through to counter-terrorist search and disposal techniques, backed by a risk management service running from initial threat assessment to strategic management policy. The company also operates a Hazardous Energetic Materials Unit (HEMU), a dedicated 24/7 callout capability for emergency services and government agencies.

## Products and solutions

- **Land Ordnance Disposal** - UK-wide 24/7 EOD response to unexploded ordnance incidents, using response vehicles licensed under carriage of dangerous goods legislation.
- **Maritime Ordnance Disposal** - a proprietary Remote Explosive Ordnance Disposal System (REODS) built for maritime EOD, removing unexploded ordnance and explosive remnants of war from pipelines, platforms and open seabed to depths in excess of 1,000 metres.
- **Site Remediation and FFEH Certification** - decontamination of former military or explosives-manufacturing sites, culminating in Free From Explosives Hazard certification for redevelopment or sale.
- **Explosive substances disposal** - assessment and disposal of chemicals with an explosive nature.
- **Consultancy and risk mitigation** - explosive consultancy delivered by the same explosives officers who carry out disposal work.
- **Training** - certified education and training packages delivered by former military personnel, from explosives awareness through to military and security forces training; facilities include a full-size search house, ship, aeroplane and search vehicle, a recompression chamber and dive tank, a lecture theatre and outdoor training grounds. EOD and IEDD training packages are delivered only to serving police or military personnel.
- **Hazardous Energetic Materials Unit (HEMU)** - a complimentary 24/7 callout service for registered emergency services and government agencies, covering chemical assessment and disposal, ordnance and explosive substance disposal, search and locate for hazardous materials, remote systems, portable chemical spectrometry and thermal imagery.
- **Equipment distribution** - UK distributor for MED-ENG and ExploSim equipment, and explosive storage solutions.
