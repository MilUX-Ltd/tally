# Ramora Global Ltd

> EOD and explosive disposal specialist providing bomb disposal, site remediation, EOD search, and training services to defence and emergency services customers.

## What they do

Ramora Global delivers explosive ordnance disposal services including 24/7 reactive bomb disposal, site remediation, and EOD search, alongside training for defence and emergency services organisations. The company positions itself as a specialist capability provider with close-support experience across both military and civil contexts.
