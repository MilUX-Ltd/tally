# PLEXSYS Interface Products UK Ltd

> Defence simulation and training company delivering Live, Virtual, and Constructive (LVC) training readiness solutions to military customers.

## What they do

Founded in 1986, PLEXSYS is a modelling and simulation software company with over 30 years of experience delivering LVC training solutions. It designs and integrates simulation environments for communications, command and control, and tactical training, with customers across multiple nations.

## Products and solutions

- **LVC Training Solutions** - Integrated live, virtual, and constructive environments that connect real forces, simulators, and constructive entities for joint training exercises.
- **Communications Simulation** - Software tools replicating radio and C2 networks within simulation environments for realistic training fidelity.
