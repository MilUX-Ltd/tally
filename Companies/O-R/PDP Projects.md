# PDP Projects

> UK security consultancy providing physical, information and personnel security services, including rapidly deployable secure infrastructure, to government and commercial clients.

## What they do

PDP Projects Ltd is a UK security consultancy that has worked with clients including the FCDO, MOD and wider HMG, designing, building and maintaining secure working environments in numerous countries. The company states it covers human, physical and cyber security, offering a security review and risk assessment process followed by design, procurement and implementation of security measures tailored to each client's requirements. It also describes rapidly deployable, modular secure infrastructure built on its experience of HMG and MOD operational and security requirements.

## Products and solutions

- **Security reviews and risk assessment** - audits, testing, monitoring and penetration testing of existing security arrangements.
- **Secure facility design and construction** - doors, windows, gates, barriers, panic rooms, armoured vehicles, ballistic and blast protection.
- **Deployable secure infrastructure** - rapidly deployable, modular secure facilities for government and commercial clients.
- **Security systems procurement and integration** - access control, biometric authentication, CCTV and intrusion detection, sourced and integrated on a vendor-agnostic basis.
- **Security training** - programmes covering personal safety, security awareness and information security.
