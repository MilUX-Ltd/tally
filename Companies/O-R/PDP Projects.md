# PDP Projects
> UK supplier of mobile, scalable and accredited deployable platforms used as C3I nodes in operational and warfighting environments.
## What they do
PDP Projects supplies deployable command, control, communications and intelligence (C3I) infrastructure for defence customers. Products include deployable HQs and Remote Operation Centres designed for operational environments and conventional warfighting. Platforms are accredited and scalable.
