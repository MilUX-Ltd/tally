# Real-Time Innovations (RTI)

> Connext data-distribution software underpinning real-time, autonomous and mission-critical defence systems.

## What they do

RTI is a software framework company for intelligent physical systems. Its Connext product suite is a real-time data streaming and connectivity platform built on the Data Distribution Service (DDS) standard, used to integrate distributed, autonomous and mission-critical applications. RTI describes itself as the world's largest DDS supplier, with software embedded across hundreds of major defence programmes and autonomous-vehicle deployments.

## Products and solutions

- **Connext Professional** - software framework for building distributed real-time and physical AI systems on the DDS standard.
- **Connext product suite** - data connectivity, real-time data streaming and integration tooling for autonomous systems, command and control, and digital twins.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Standards:** built on the Object Management Group (OMG) Data Distribution Service (DDS) standard.
