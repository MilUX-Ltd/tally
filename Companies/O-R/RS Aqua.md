# RS Aqua

> Portsmouth supplier of ocean-technology sensors, instruments and monitoring systems for marine science, aquaculture, defence and maritime industries.

## What they do

RS Aqua supplies and integrates ocean-technology sensors, instruments and monitoring systems, and has done so for over 40 years. Its range covers waves, currents and weather, oceanographic and submersible instruments, acoustic telemetry and underwater noise monitoring, aquaculture intelligence, autonomous and robotic platforms, and satellite telemetry. It acts as UK partner or distributor for manufacturers including Innovasea, Nortek and SEABER, and offers rental, project support, technical support and training. It is JOSCAR registered and holds ISO 9001. Since November 2024 it has operated as an autonomous business within the General Oceans group.

## Products and solutions

- **Marlin** - real-time acoustic monitoring, including underwater-noise compliance for offshore wind.
- **Acoustic telemetry and monitoring** - tagging, tracking and passive acoustic monitoring, including the Orca sensor.
- **Waves, currents and weather** - surface and submersible measurement systems.
- **Oceanographic instruments** - submersible measurement and sampling instruments.
- **Autonomy** - autonomous, robotic and uncrewed platforms.
- **Satellite telemetry and asset tracking.**
