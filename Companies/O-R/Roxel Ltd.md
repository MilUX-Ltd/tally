# Roxel Ltd

> Franco-British missile propulsion specialist designing and manufacturing rocket and missile motors for European missile programmes.

## What they do

Roxel designs and manufactures propulsion systems for most existing European missiles. Key technologies include Thrust Vector Control, Insensitive Munitions, Dual Pulse systems, and smokeless propellants. Advanced manufacturing capabilities include additive manufacturing and Resonant Acoustic Mixing, with next-generation propellant development supported by the UK MoD and French DGA.

## Products and solutions

- **Missile and rocket motors** - propulsion systems for the European missile inventory.
- **Thrust Vector Control** - precision flight control through propulsion.
- **Insensitive Munitions** - reduced-sensitivity propellant formulations meeting modern safety standards.
- **Dual Pulse systems** - two-stage propulsion for extended-range applications.
