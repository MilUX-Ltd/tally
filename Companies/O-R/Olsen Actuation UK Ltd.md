# Olsen Actuation UK Ltd

> Precision actuator and motion control manufacturer serving aerospace, defence, and other demanding platform environments.

## What they do

Olsen Actuation UK Ltd, trading as Olsen Actuators & Drives, designs and manufactures actuator and motion control solutions for challenging mechanical applications across aerospace, defence, and industrial sectors. The company supplies components and subcomponents for platform systems including flight control, hydraulic and pneumatic systems, landing gear, propeller control, and helm/steering systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Actuators and drives** - Precision actuation solutions for aerospace and defence platform systems.
- **Motion control components** - Electrical, electronic, and mechanical subcomponents for platform integration.
