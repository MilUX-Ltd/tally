# Rigetti Computing UK

> UK operation of quantum computing company Rigetti, which built and hosts a superconducting quantum computer in Abingdon, Oxfordshire.

## What they do

Rigetti Computing is a full-stack quantum computing company that designs and builds superconducting quantum processors and delivers access through its Quantum Cloud Services platform. Its UK operation led a government-backed consortium to build the UK's first commercially available quantum computer, a 32-qubit system hosted in Abingdon, Oxfordshire. The parent company is listed on Nasdaq. Quantum computing has significant applications for government, defence and secure computing.

## Products and solutions

- **Superconducting quantum computers** - Aspen-series quantum processing units.
- **Quantum Cloud Services (QCS)** - cloud access to quantum hardware for UK partners.
