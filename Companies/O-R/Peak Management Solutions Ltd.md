# Peak Management Solutions Ltd

> Management consultancy specialising in AI, system integration, and system improvement for defence and government security.

## What they do

Peak Management Solutions delivers consulting services across three areas: AI (machine learning, predictive analytics, and advanced data processing), system integration, and system improvement. Their work is tailored specifically to defence and government security projects.

## Products and solutions

Peak Management Solutions describes its offer as three consultancy service lines rather than named products:

- **AI advisory** - machine learning, predictive analytics, and advanced data processing applied to decision-making, threat detection, and operational efficiency for defence and government security clients.
- **System integration** - methodologies for interoperability and secure data exchange across complex defence and government systems.
- **System improvement** - assessment of existing systems followed by reliability, security, and scalability improvement work.
