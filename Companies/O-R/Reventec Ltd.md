# Reventec Ltd

> Custom programmable sensor designer and manufacturer serving unmanned systems, military and motorsport sectors from Wiltshire.

## What they do

Reventec is a mechanical and electronic design consultancy, established in 2013, specialising in sensor products for motorsport, defence, aerospace and industrial sectors. It designs and manufactures electronic and mechanical sensors, including capacitive liquid level, non-contact position and programmable temperature sensors for demanding environments. The company states it has significant experience designing sensors for military UAV platforms, and its products are in widespread use across unmanned aerial systems and military platforms, as well as motorsport and automotive applications. Reventec supplies sensors that integrate into unmanned aircraft systems; it does not manufacture UAS platforms or payloads itself.

## Products and solutions

- **LSM800** - mil-spec capacitive UAV fuel and oil level sensor with a threaded two-bolt mount, custom-built up to 450mm.
- **LSM500 range (including LSM500-38999 and LSM500-CONTOUR)** - mil-spec liquid level sensors, with a variant featuring an integrated MIL-DTL-38999 connector for plug-and-play installation.
- **LS845** - threaded liquid level sensor for fuel and oil in high-temperature, harsh-environment applications.
- **DroneCAN protocol option** - added to the capacitive liquid level sensor range; the company states it is the first capacitive liquid-level sensor manufacturer to offer DroneCAN compatibility on new orders, enabling direct integration into UAV flight controller systems.
- **Non-contact TMR position sensors** - measure through non-ferrous material and across air gaps, for ruggedised applications.
- **Programmable temperature sensors** - resistive or ratiometric voltage output, for long-term reliability in harsh and extreme-temperature environments.
