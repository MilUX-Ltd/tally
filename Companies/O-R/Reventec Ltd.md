# Reventec Ltd

> Custom programmable sensor designer and manufacturer serving unmanned systems, military and motorsport sectors from Wiltshire.

## What they do

Reventec designs and manufactures electronic and mechanical sensors, including liquid level, temperature and position sensors for demanding environments. Products are in widespread use across unmanned aerial systems and military platforms, as well as motorsport and automotive applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Liquid level sensors** - Custom programmable sensors for fuel and fluid management in unmanned and military systems.
- **Position sensors** - Precision position measurement for UAS and ruggedised applications.
- **Temperature sensors** - Bespoke temperature monitoring for harsh environment platforms.
