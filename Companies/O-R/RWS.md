# RWS

> Global language services and content management company with a dedicated government, aerospace and defence division.

## What they do

RWS (formerly SDL Government, Aerospace and Defence) provides professional language services and multilingual content management solutions to over 50 governments globally, including defence, intelligence and law enforcement clients. Its technical documentation capability covers S1000D and military and commercial aerospace standards, including software for creating, editing, managing and publishing mission-critical maintenance information for vehicles, vessels and aircraft. RWS also provides data services, including data science, data migration, and AI training-data annotation and labelling through its TrainAI offering.

## Products and solutions

- **Language Services** - Professional translation and localisation for defence and government clients.
- **Technical Documentation** - S1000D-compliant documentation systems for military and aerospace platforms.
- **Multilingual Content Management** - Platforms for managing mission-critical maintenance and operational information.
- **Data Services** - Data science, analytics and visualisation, and data migration between legacy and modern systems.
- **TrainAI** - Data collection, annotation and labelling of AI training data (text, audio, image, video) via a global network of vetted annotators.
