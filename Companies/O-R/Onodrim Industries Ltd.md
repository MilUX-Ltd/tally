# Onodrim Industries Ltd

> Defence industrial platform company advancing manufacturing, multi-domain sensing, and networked systems for European and allied forces.

## What they do

Onodrim Industries is building a defence industrial platform aimed at high-priority national security challenges, including border protection and resilient supply chains for mission-critical systems. The company is developing capabilities across defence manufacturing, multi-domain sensing, and networked systems to strengthen deterrence, improve operational readiness, and enable interoperability among European and allied forces. The company is UK-incorporated, describes itself as European in outlook, and was at seed stage as of early 2026 with no named products yet published.
