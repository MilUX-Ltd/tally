# Onodrim Industries Ltd

> Defence industrial platform company advancing manufacturing, multi-domain sensing, and networked systems for European and allied forces.

## What they do

Onodrim Industries is building a defence industrial platform aimed at high-priority national security challenges, including border protection and resilient supply chains for mission-critical systems. The company is developing capabilities across defence manufacturing, multi-domain sensing, and networked systems to strengthen deterrence and improve operational readiness.
