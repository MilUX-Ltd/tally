# ODU UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of German connector manufacturer ODU, supplying components and equipment for defence and aerospace applications.

## What they do

ODU UK is the British subsidiary of ODU GmbH, a specialist in electrical connector and contact solutions. The company supplies components and equipment technologies to defence, aerospace and industrial customers from its facility in Runcorn, Cheshire.
