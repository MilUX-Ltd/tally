# ODU UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of German connector manufacturer ODU, supplying components and equipment for defence and aerospace applications.

## What they do

ODU UK is the British subsidiary of ODU GmbH, a specialist in electrical connector and contact solutions. The company supplies components and equipment technologies to defence, aerospace and industrial customers from its facility in Runcorn, Cheshire.

## Products and solutions

- **ODU AMC** - break-away military connector rated for 5,000-plus mating cycles, described by ODU for next-generation military applications.
- **ODU AMC NP** - a standardised connector used on Dismounted Soldier Systems, covering tactical soldier batteries, power distribution units, conformable wearable batteries and C4ISR peripherals.
- **ODU MEDI-SNAP** - lightweight, plastic-housed push-pull and break-away connector, up to 75 percent lighter than metal equivalents, used across medical, test and industrial applications.
- ODU states it has supplied more than 100,000 miniature push-pull helmet connectors for the UK's Bowman military communications programme since 2002.
