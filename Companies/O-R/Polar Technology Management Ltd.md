# Polar Technology Management Ltd

> Oxfordshire engineering group delivering advanced composite materials and metallic fabrications for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Polar Technology provides technology-based solutions around advanced composite materials and metallic fabrications. It operates through two brands, Lentus Composites for composite design and manufacture, and SST Technology for precision fabrications and thermal management systems. The group serves aerospace and defence markets, including lightweight, high-performance UAV structures.

## Products and solutions

- **Composites** - filament winding, moulded composites and lightweight structures (Lentus Composites).
- **Fabrications and thermal management** - precision fabrications and thermal management systems (SST Technology).
- **Engineering services** - additive manufacturing and engineering support.
