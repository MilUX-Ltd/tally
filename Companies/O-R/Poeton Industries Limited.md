# Poeton Industries Limited

> Surface treatment and coating specialist with over 120 years of experience, serving aerospace and defence with ITAR-approved, NADCAP-accredited processes.

## What they do

Poeton provides advanced surface treatment solutions including electroplating, ceramic coatings, corrosion inhibition treatments, and specialist finishing for components operating in demanding conditions. It holds NADCAP accreditation across chemical processing, coating, and non-destructive testing, and carries ITAR approval for defence programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Apticote Coatings** - Proprietary range of advanced surface treatments including ceramic, tungsten carbide, and PTFE-based coatings for aerospace and defence components.
- **Electroplating and Finishing** - Industry-standard electroplating processes including chromium, non-chromium alternatives, and conductive coatings.
- **NDT Services** - Non-destructive testing capability accredited under NADCAP NDT.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ITAR Approval, NADCAP CP, NADCAP CT, NADCAP NDT
