# Primetake

> British manufacturer of specialist pyrotechnics, ammunition, and less-lethal munitions for defence and law enforcement.

## What they do

Primetake has manufactured specialist pyrotechnics and ammunition for military and law enforcement clients since 1991. The company works with customers to design, develop, and manufacture custom products covering method-of-entry ammunition, anti-drone cartridges, EOD disruptor cartridges, less-lethal rounds, distraction devices, signal cartridges, smoke grenades, and ceremonial saluting cartridges.

## Products and solutions

- **Method of Entry Ammunition** - specialist breaching rounds for military and law enforcement.
- **Anti-Drone Cartridges** - counter-UAS projectile solutions.
- **EOD Disruptor Cartridges** - explosive ordnance disposal tools for bomb disposal operators.
- **Less-Lethal Ammunition** - PAVA irritants, kinetic effectors, and crowd control munitions.
- **Distraction Devices** - stun grenades and distraction devices for tactical use.
- **Signal Cartridges and Smoke Grenades** - pyrotechnic signalling and obscurant products.
