# PDL Solutions (Europe) Limited

> Advanced engineering analysis consultancy serving the nuclear, defence, and offshore energy sectors.

## What they do

PDL Solutions (Europe) Limited is an engineering analysis consultancy based in Hexham, incorporated in 2001. It provides advanced engineering consultancy services to clients in safety-critical, mission-critical industries, working predominantly across defence, nuclear, oil and gas, transport, renewable energy and water. The company describes its core skill sets as finite element analysis (FEA), computational fluid dynamics (CFD), global dynamic analysis, process optimisation, structural calculations, engineering design, pipe stress analysis, and linear elastic fracture mechanics, with clients across Europe, North America and Asia.

## Products and solutions

- **Finite Element Analysis (FEA)** - structural and stress analysis for design validation.
- **Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)** - fluid and thermal modelling for complex engineering problems.
- **Global Dynamic Analysis** - dynamic response analysis for structures and systems.
- **Pipe Stress Analysis** - analysis using CAESAR II for piping systems in safety-critical plant.
- **Process Optimisation and Structural Calculations** - engineering design support for asset validation and in-field problem-solving.
