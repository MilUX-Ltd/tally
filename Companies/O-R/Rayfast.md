# Rayfast

> Swindon supplier of heat-shrink tubing and cable protection products for aerospace, defence and industrial markets.

## What they do

Rayfast (IS-Rayfast) supplies and converts heat-shrink tubing, wiring accessories, connectors and cable protection products, including Raychem-type materials, for demanding environments. It serves aerospace, defence, military, rail and industrial customers, offering single-wall, dual-wall, heavy-duty and fabric heat-shrink products. The company is part of a wider group and operates an online catalogue and quotation service.

## Products and solutions

- **Heat-shrink tubing** - single-wall, dual-wall, heavy-duty and fabric variants.
- **Cable protection and wiring accessories** - connectors, boots and identification products.

- **Accreditations:** ISO certified (BSI).
