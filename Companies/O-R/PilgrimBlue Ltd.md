# PilgrimBlue Ltd

> Project delivery consultancy providing project management, procurement and contract management services to the defence sector.

## What they do

PilgrimBlue offers flexible project delivery services including project management, procurement, and contract management, with a stated focus on removing the delay and noise typical in defence-sector programmes. The company is based in Co. Durham and operates across the North East of England.
