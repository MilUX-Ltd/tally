# Parker Meggitt

> British aerospace and defence components and sub-systems business, part of Parker Hannifin's Aerospace Group.

## What they do

Parker Meggitt (formerly Meggitt plc) is a British aerospace, defence and energy components company based in Coventry. Founded in 1948, it became part of the Parker Aerospace Group of US motion and control company Parker Hannifin in September 2022. The business supplies products across the aerospace, defence and energy markets and employs more than 9,000 people across over 40 manufacturing facilities and regional offices worldwide.

## Products and solutions

- **Landing systems** - landing gear, wheels and braking systems.
- **Fire and safety systems** - detection and suppression for aircraft and platforms.
- **Sensing systems** - sensors and condition-monitoring products.
- **Fuel systems** - fuel measurement and management.
- **Polymer seals and composites** - polymer seals and composite components.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
