# Perfect Bore Manufacturing Limited

> Precision bore manufacturing specialist serving aerospace and defence OEMs.

## What they do

Perfect Bore manufactures bores in components to extreme tolerances, including sub-micro geometrical tolerances, for major OEMs in the aerospace and defence sectors. Their work supports programmes including the A400M, A350, and B787 Dreamliner for customers such as Airbus, BAE Systems, Boeing, Moog, Goodrich Actuation Systems, and Rolls-Royce.
