# Palentec Ltd

> AI technology company developing traceable and auditable software for safety-critical defence and aviation applications.

## What they do

Palentec creates AI technologies and supplies them as fully traceable, auditable software applications for safety-critical tasks. Its ADS-listed capabilities include simulation and modelling, data analysis, software optimisation, software modelling, TEMPEST, and training services covering engineering, flight, manufacture, simulation, and systems.
