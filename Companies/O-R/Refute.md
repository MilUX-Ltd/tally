# Refute
> UK AI company detecting and countering disinformation by tracing and scoring narratives and adversarial behaviours across interconnected data at scale.

## What they do
Refute applies a quantitative approach to counter-disinformation, fusing, tracing and scoring narratives and behaviours across connected data sources and using generative AI to automate detection and response. The platform is designed to operate at the speed of the adversary, targeting narrative-based attacks on organisations and governments. Co-founders Tom Garnett and Vlad Galu bring experience in counter-terrorism data analysis, cyber attack response and internet identity trust systems.

Refute raised PS5M in seed funding led by Amadeus Capital Partners, with participation from Playfair, Episode 1, Osney Capital and the National Security Strategic Investment Fund (NSSIF).
