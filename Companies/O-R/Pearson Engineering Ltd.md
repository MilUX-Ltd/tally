# Pearson Engineering Ltd

> Manufacturer of interchangeable front-end equipment and attachments for armoured vehicles, covering mobility, counter-mobility, and survivability operations.

## What they do

Pearson Engineering designs and manufactures breaching, obstacle-crossing, explosive ordnance clearance, and protection systems that attach to armoured vehicles. Their Armstrong Works facility also delivers manufacturing services to UK and overseas defence programmes, with a focus on increasing UK industrial content.

## Products and solutions

- **Minefield Breaching Systems** - interchangeable front-end attachments for armoured vehicle minefield and obstacle clearance.
- **Explosive Ordnance Clearance Equipment** - systems for EOD and route-clearance operations.
- **Obstacle Creation and Crossing** - engineering solutions for counter-mobility and gap-crossing tasks.
- **Autonomy Integration** - adding intelligence and autonomy to mobility, counter-mobility, and survivability platforms.
- **Armstrong Works Manufacturing** - defence manufacturing services supporting UK and overseas programme content.
