# Precision Technic Defence

> Danish-headquartered supplier of tactical equipment, integrated systems and through-life services to armed forces and national security agencies.

## What they do

Precision Technic Defence Group is a defence supplier headquartered in Svenstrup, Denmark, tracing its origins to 1985. It develops, tests and supplies turn-key solutions to armed forces, combining components from selected sub-suppliers into interoperable systems and running them through testing before delivery. The group has companies in Denmark, Germany, Sweden, France, Poland, the USA, Australia and the UK, and its subsidiary Precision Services provides maintenance, integration and through-life support. A UK arm, Precision Technic Defence Limited, is incorporated in England (Companies House 08255248) and is an ADS Group member.

## Products and solutions

- **Integrated tactical systems** - interoperable systems and solutions assembled from market-leading manufacturers for military and national security use.
- **Vehicle preparation and modification** - preparation and modification of Mercedes-Benz G-Class vehicles for the Danish Armed Forces, as sole subcontractor to Ejner Hessel.
- **Precision Services** - technical maintenance, integration and through-life support for products and systems.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group (via Precision Technic Defence Limited, UK); FAD (Forsvars- og Aerospaceindustrien i Danmark) member.
