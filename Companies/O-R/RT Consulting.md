# RT Consulting
> UK SME providing digital transformation, security assurance and AI services to MOD and other public sector clients across multiple defence procurement frameworks.

## What they do
RT Consulting (legal name Real-Time Consultants Ltd) delivers across three service areas: digital transformation covering full project and live service lifecycle management; integrated security assurance spanning cyber, information assurance, physical identity access management and crypto security; and AI services including process optimisation, digital twin and shadow AI mitigation. The firm is a long-standing supply chain member of the Programme Delivery Partner (PDP-S), providing PPM, project controls and operational support to MOD.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR accredited; Crown Commercial Services registered supplier; Engineering Delivery Partner (EDP) Aurora Provider Network; ASTRID; Digital and IT Professional Services (DIPS); Programme Delivery Partner (PDP-S); Next Generation Assurance Programme (NGAP); Commercial Delivery Partner (CDP).
