# RED Scientific Reply
> Hampshire research and analytical consultancy providing operational analysis, systems engineering and wargaming to UK defence, including the CIRSIUM digital wargaming product.

## What they do

RED Scientific (legal entity RED Scientific Reply Limited, formerly RED Scientific Limited) is a research and niche analytical consultancy serving UK government, its agencies and industry. Its work spans operational analysis, systems engineering, integrated logistics support, training and documentation, and compliance across safety, security and information assurance. It maintains government classified premises at Alton, Hampshire, and provides on-site expert support to customers across the UK.

## Products and solutions

- **CIRSIUM** - a playable mathematical model of military engagements, combining software, combat algorithms and supporting data, developed for the British Army Land Warfare Centre and NATO's Allied Rapid Reaction Corps to support operational-level course-of-action and analytical wargaming. Demonstrated at the Defence Academy Industry Fair in November 2025.
- **RAW (RED Analytical Wargame)** - operational analytical wargaming tool developed with the British Army Land Warfare Centre, used by HQ Allied Rapid Reaction Corps.
- **Data Systems** - bespoke digital solutions built on Oracle, Oracle APEX and MS SQL Server, including MODNET-hosted capability.
- **Dashboard Intelligence (RED-Vi)** - open-source-intelligence harvesting, parsing and fusion with interactive dashboard visualisation.
