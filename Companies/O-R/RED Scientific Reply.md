# RED Scientific Reply
> Hampshire research and analytical consultancy providing operational analysis, systems engineering and wargaming to UK defence, including the CIRSIUM digital wargaming product.

## What they do

RED Scientific (legal entity RED Scientific Reply Limited, formerly RED Scientific Limited) is a research and niche analytical consultancy serving UK government, its agencies and industry. Its work spans operational analysis, systems engineering, integrated logistics support, training and documentation, and compliance across safety, security and information assurance. It maintains government classified premises at Alton, Hampshire, and provides on-site expert support to customers across the UK.

## Products and solutions

- **CIRSIUM** - digital wargaming product, demonstrated at the Defence Academy Industry Fair in November 2025.
