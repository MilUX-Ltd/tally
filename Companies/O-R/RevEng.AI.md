# RevEng.AI

> Foundational AI platform for reverse engineering compiled software, supporting malware detection and software supply chain assurance for defence and national security.

## What they do

RevEng.AI builds AI models that enable Defence and National Security users to detect novel malware, identify previously unknown software vulnerabilities, and assure complex software supply chains and device firmware. Its foundational model, BinNet, analyses compiled software, including closed-source and third-party binaries and firmware, to determine what is actually inside them without needing source code access. The platform operates at speed and scale, reducing the cost of binary analysis for government and defence customers, and the company states BinNet has been trained alongside allied government cyber units and commercial security teams. RevEng.AI is a graduate of the NCSC for Startups programme.

## Products and solutions

- **BinNet** - foundational AI model for binary-level software analysis, turning compiled binaries back into readable, source-like output and identifying functional behaviour rather than relying on signature matching.
