# RevEng.AI

> Foundational AI platform for reverse engineering compiled software, supporting malware detection and software supply chain assurance for defence and national security.

## What they do

RevEng.AI builds AI models that enable Defence and National Security users to detect novel malware, identify previously unknown software vulnerabilities, and assure complex software supply chains and device firmware. The platform operates at speed and scale, reducing the cost of binary analysis for government and defence customers.
