# Propak Sheet Metal Ltd

> Precision sheet metal fabrication and assembly manufacturer serving aerospace, space and defence, holding AS9100 and SC21 Silver accreditation.

## What they do

Propak Sheet Metal has been manufacturing precision sheet metal components and assemblies since 1977, operating from Stevenage, Hertfordshire. The company holds AS/EN/JISQ 9100 (Aviation, Space and Defence quality standard) and is a JOSCAR-registered Tier 1 supplier, producing components and platform-level assemblies for defence and aerospace customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision Sheet Metal Fabrication** - Complex sheet metal components manufactured to aerospace quality standards with full traceability.
- **Platform System Assemblies** - Avionics, electrical/power, communications, and engine control assemblies.
- **Counter-Drone and Radar Systems** - Component and assembly production for counter-UAS and radar programmes.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR Registered, SC21 OE, SC21 Bronze, Distribution or Limited Approval
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
