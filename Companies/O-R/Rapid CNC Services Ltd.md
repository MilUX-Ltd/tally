# Rapid CNC Services Ltd

> Precision engineering company specialising in 5-axis CNC machining and high-tolerance component manufacture for aerospace and defence supply chains.

## What they do

Rapid CNC Services provides prototype and small-batch precision machined components with fast lead times, working across aluminium alloys, steel alloys (including Inconel), titanium, and engineering plastics. The company operates Mazak 5-axis CNC centres and offers over 50 years of combined machining experience across aerospace, defence, automotive, and oil and gas.

## Products and solutions

- **5-axis CNC machining** - High-tolerance milling, turning, boring, and tapping on a range of metals and plastics.
- **Prototype and small-batch production** - Fast-turnaround manufacture for development programmes.
