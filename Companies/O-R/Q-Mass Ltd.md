# Q-Mass Ltd

> Precision machining and manufacturing specialist serving aerospace, defence and weapons programmes.

## What they do

Q-Mass provides precision CNC machining, EDM, gun-drilling, honing and CMM inspection services to the aerospace and defence sectors from a 57,000 sq ft facility in East Kilbride. The company manufactures mechanical components, sub-assemblies and weapon-related parts including carriage and mounts, propulsion components and landing gear elements.

## Products and solutions

- **5-Axis CNC Turning and Milling** - Precision machined components to tight tolerances for aerospace and defence applications.
- **EDM Spark and Wire Erosion** - Electrical discharge machining for complex geometries and hard materials.
- **Gun-Drilling and Honing** - Deep-hole drilling and bore finishing for propulsion and weapon components.
