# Panoptech Ltd

> Secure IT infrastructure and digital services company serving defence and aerospace customers.

## What they do

Panoptech, founded in 2000, delivers secure IT infrastructure, cyber security, data analysis, video and surveillance systems, secure telecoms, and bespoke software into high-security environments, with a focus on communications, command and control, and surveillance capabilities for defence and aerospace organisations. Its software team develops applications that combine multiple datasets into a single data-rich picture of a geographical area or ongoing operation. The company holds ISO 27001:2022 and Cyber Essentials Plus accreditations and is a G-Cloud supplier.

## Products and solutions

- **Video and surveillance systems** - camera technology, video management and storage, delivered as a Premier Partner for Milestone VMS and Husky, lead UK reseller for ProHawk Video AI and VEGA Redundancy & Migration Solutions, and a gold AXIS partner.
- **Secure telecoms** - mobile SIM, fixed line, wireless and internet connectivity delivered into secure customer environments, including SIP phone systems.
- **IT infrastructure and networking** - secure network design, delivery and long-term support for high-security environments.
- **Software development** - bespoke mobile and web applications, including data-fusion tools that combine multiple datasets into a single operational picture.
- **Security audits** - system security surveys and audits to assess and reduce exposure to intrusion.
