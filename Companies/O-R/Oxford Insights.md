# Oxford Insights

> AI and digital strategy consultancy working with governments on emerging technology adoption.

## What they do

Oxford Insights works with governments to harness emerging technology to achieve their strategic and operational goals. It specialises in artificial intelligence, digital transformation, and digital strategy, with a focus on human-centred design, user research, and evaluation. Founded in 2016, the company describes its thematic expertise as covering big data and AI, and has also analysed quantum technologies, digital twins, and the immersive economy.

## Products and solutions

- **Government AI Readiness Index (GARI)** - an annual comparative assessment of governments' capacity to harness AI for public benefit, now in its 8th edition. The 2025 edition ranks 195 countries across 69 indicators, 14 dimensions and six pillars (Policy Capacity, Governance, AI Infrastructure, Public Sector Adoption, Development and Diffusion, Resilience), and is used as a benchmark by national governments and international bodies including the G20.
- **Service delivery** - business analysis, user research, service design, and delivery management to develop public services.
- **Evaluations** - human-centred evaluation combining theories of change, qualitative interviews, and quantitative impact measurement.
- **Policy advice** - advisory work supporting governments in developing, implementing, and evaluating AI and digital policy.
