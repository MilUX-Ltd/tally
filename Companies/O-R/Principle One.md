# Principle One

> Technology consultancy delivering systems engineering, data science, and AI services to UK defence, law enforcement, and national security.

## What they do

Founded in 2018, Principle One is a London-based technology consultancy of around 100 staff working with UK law enforcement, national security, health, and defence. The company delivers systems engineering, technical and solution architecture, data science, AI adoption, design and development services, synthetic data services, and experimentation and innovation support against UK mission outcomes.

## Products and solutions

- **Synthetic data capability** - development of realistic synthetic data to support police analyst capability development, training and experimentation.
- **National data management capability** - multi-disciplinary support for a national data management capability for UK law enforcement.
