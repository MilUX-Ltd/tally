# Para Tech Coating UK Ltd

> UK specialist in high-reliability coating and protection services for PCBs and electronic assemblies in the aerospace and defence supply chain.

## What they do

Para Tech Coating provides Parylene coating, conformal coating application and removal, adhesive dispensing, and related services for electronic assemblies, supporting customers from prototype through to production. The company has over 24 years of experience serving the aerospace and defence supply chain with controlled, traceable processes.

## Products and solutions

- **Parylene Coating** - Application to customer specification for high-reliability electronic assemblies.
- **Conformal Coating** - Application, removal, and controlled masking and demasking services.
- **Adhesive Dispensing** - Including staking and potting for electronic assembly protection.
