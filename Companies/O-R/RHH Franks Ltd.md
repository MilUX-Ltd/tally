# RHH Franks Ltd

> Hampshire-based precision engineering manufacturer producing CNC-machined components and assemblies for aerospace and defence, with capabilities spanning fabrication, welding, brazing and non-destructive testing.

## What they do

RHH Franks is an end-to-end provider of precision engineered components and assemblies, serving aerospace and defence customers from its New Milton facility. Capabilities include 4- and 5-axis CNC machining, sheet metal fabrication, small bore tube extrusion, TIG welding, torch and dip brazing, silver soldering, heat treatment of aluminium alloys, non-destructive testing and pressure testing.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC Machined Components** - 4- and 5-axis CNC machined parts and subassemblies to aerospace tolerances.
- **Sheet Metal Fabrication** - Sheet metal parts including forming, blanking, press and stretch operations.
- **Welded and Brazed Assemblies** - TIG welding, torch and dip brazing, and silver soldering for structural and fluid-carrying assemblies.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001:2015, NADCAP (Chemical Processing, Heat Treatment, NDT, Welding), SC21 OE, SC21 Bronze
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
