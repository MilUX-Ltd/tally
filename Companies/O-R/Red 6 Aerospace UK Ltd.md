# Red 6 Aerospace UK Ltd

> Develops augmented reality flight training systems for military pilots, addressing the NATO pilot shortage and readiness crisis.

## What they do

Red 6 has developed the Advanced Tactical Augmented Reality System (ATARS), which overlays synthetic adversaries and training scenarios onto the real-world environment as seen from a cockpit. The system is described by the company as delivering live, virtual and constructive training, projecting synthetic threats, adversary aircraft and friendly assets directly into a pilot's view during real flight, and is designed to make military flight training more authentic, scalable and cost-effective without the constraints of live aircraft availability.

## Products and solutions

- **ATARS (Advanced Tactical Augmented Reality System)** - airborne augmented reality platform projecting synthetic threats and training assets into the live cockpit environment.
- **EVE Headset (Enhanced Visual Environment)** - proprietary augmented reality headset designed for use outdoors in daylight and at flight speed.
- **ARCADE (Augmented Reality Command and Analytic Data Environment)** - post-mission debrief and analytics tool built on ATARS flight data.
