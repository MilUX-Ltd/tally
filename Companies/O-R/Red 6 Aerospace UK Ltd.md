# Red 6 Aerospace UK Ltd

> Develops augmented reality flight training systems for military pilots, addressing the NATO pilot shortage and readiness crisis.

## What they do

Red 6 has developed the Advanced Tactical Augmented Reality System (ATARS), which overlays synthetic adversaries and training scenarios onto the real-world environment as seen from a cockpit. The system is designed to make military flight training more authentic, scalable and cost-effective without the constraints of live aircraft availability.

## Products and solutions

- **ATARS (Advanced Tactical Augmented Reality System)** - Airborne augmented reality platform projecting synthetic threats and training assets into the live cockpit environment.
