# Prisma Worldwide Ltd

> UK agent and distributor of tactical gear for the military, police, first responder and special forces sectors, and sole UK distributor of TASMANIAN TIGER.

## What they do

Prisma Worldwide is a UK based agent and distributor supplying tactical products to military, police, first responder and special forces customers. Established in 2016, the company describes itself as specialising in high quality tactical gear and products for professional users, covering one-off projects as well as long-term supply requirements. It states it has access to tactical gear, ballistic plates, portable solar panels, hearing protection and bespoke manufacturing.

Prisma Worldwide is the UK's sole distributor for TASMANIAN TIGER, a German-designed brand of tactical equipment used by military, police and medical sectors internationally. TASMANIAN TIGER products are developed, in the brand's own words, to the specific operational needs and requirements of military and police end users, with a focus on quality and functionality.

## Products and solutions

- **TASMANIAN TIGER** - backpacks, plate carriers, tactical bags, pouches and clothing for military, police and special forces use; Prisma Worldwide is the sole UK distributor.
- **Tatonka** - German outdoor gear and heavy duty backpacks; Prisma Worldwide directs enquiries to relevant retailers.
- **Dynamic Ear Company** - hearing protection products allowing professional operators to protect hearing while retaining situational awareness.
- **RAMP GLOBALTEC** - performance buckles and fasteners for tactical and other applications.
- **Power Traveller** - portable power and solar charging solutions.
