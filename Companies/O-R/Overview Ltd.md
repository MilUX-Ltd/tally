# Overview Ltd
> Innovators in sensor systems and sensor pointing technologies, differentiated by agility, low latency and direct-drive design.
## What they do
Overview Ltd develops, supplies and supports advanced sensor positioning technologies and specialist sensor systems. Products are built around agility, low latency and direct-drive principles. The company serves defence and adjacent markets and is a member of both the ADS Group and the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
