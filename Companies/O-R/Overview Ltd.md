# Overview Ltd

> Innovators in sensor systems and sensor pointing technologies, differentiated by agility, low latency and direct-drive design.

## What they do

Overview Ltd designs, develops and manufactures Integrated Servo Drives, sensor pointing solutions and remote autonomous sensor systems. The company states it is a leader in integrated servo motors and their application into mechatronic sensor positioning solutions, serving the Defence, Security, Energy, Broadcast/Pro AV and Environmental markets. Overview says it has invested more than 20% of revenue in UK-based R&D every year since 1988, and is a member of both the ADS Group and the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.

## Products and solutions

- **Servotorq I and Servotorq II** - integrated servo motor technology underlying Overview's drive products; Servotorq II delivers torque up to 4Nm and speeds up to 1000°/s, with direct-drive accuracy and repeatability to 0.0055°.
- **iBLDC positioning mechanism** - direct-drive pan-tilt-zoom (PTZ) mechanism built around Overview's Servotorq drives, aimed at applications needing agile, silent and ultra-smooth sensor pointing.
- **Product families (Titan, Leto, Miranda, Atlas, Artemis, Helios, Lelantos, Hydra, AdvISE, PodView)** - Overview's named ranges of integrated servo drives and sensor positioning products, applied across surveillance, camera pointing and remote sensor platforms.
- **Case study application** - Overview states its Atlas-derived Servotorq technology was used by a compact surveillance radar manufacturer as the motion platform within a third-party counter-drone solution.
