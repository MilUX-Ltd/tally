# Renishaw plc

> UK-listed precision engineering company specialising in metrology, additive manufacturing and healthcare technologies, with applications across defence.

## What they do

Renishaw is a manufacturer of high-precision metrology and healthcare technology. Its products and systems improve manufacturing efficiency and product quality and are used across aerospace, automotive, semiconductor, electronics, medical and defence sectors. The company designs, develops and manufactures in-house, with operations spanning around 36 countries, and is listed on the London Stock Exchange (ticker RSW) as a constituent of the FTSE 250.

## Products and solutions

- **Touch-trigger and scanning probes** - the founding product, sensors for coordinate measuring machines used for precision inspection of machined parts including defence components.
- **Coordinate measuring machine (CMM) probes, software and retrofits** - measurement and inspection systems for high-precision quality control.
- **Machine tool probes** - on-machine measurement and tool setting for CNC machining.
- **Position encoders** - linear, rotary, angle and magnetic encoders for high-accuracy motion control in robotics, semiconductors and precision machining, with relevance to guidance, optics and targeting systems.
- **Metal additive manufacturing (RenAM series)** - metal 3D-printing systems using multi-laser powder-bed fusion, marketed for defence applications including lightweight, complex metal parts.
- **Equator gauging system** - flexible, thermally insensitive shop-floor gauging with intelligent process control for automated CNC manufacturing.
