# RAINCLOUD Group
> US-based business intelligence and networking platform for the global defence technology sector, providing a curated invitation-only digital ecosystem for companies, capability discovery and market intelligence.

## What they do
RAINCLOUD Group builds invitation-only B2B platforms for high-stakes industries, with defence as its primary vertical under the RAINCLOUD DEFENSE brand. The platform combines a vetted member directory, a product showcase (the Digital Defense Show), an opportunities board, private organisational workspaces (Pavilions), and weekly intelligence outputs for its reported 6,000-plus member companies across 100-plus countries. Platform infrastructure is provided by Hivebrite. The group is incorporated in the US, UK and UAE and is expanding into a second vertical, RAINCLOUD UKRAINA, for Ukraine reconstruction business.

## Products and solutions
- **RAINCLOUD DEFENSE** - invitation-only defence technology ecosystem and business intelligence platform.
- **Digital Defense Show** - 24/7 virtual catalogue of defence and dual-use products and capabilities.
- **Private Pavilions** - tailored member community spaces for organisations within the network.
- **Weekly Forecast** - proprietary defence-industry intelligence outputs published to members.
