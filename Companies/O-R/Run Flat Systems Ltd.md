# Run Flat Systems Ltd

> Developer and manufacturer of patented runflat insert systems for military and defence vehicles.

## What they do

Incorporated in 2007 as an R&D company, Run Flat Systems has designed and patented a modern runflat insert for single-piece drop-centre wheels, enabling all-tyres-down control and drivability across all terrain types. The system includes patented Active Beadlock technology and is compatible with Central Tyre Inflation (CTI) systems, supporting low-pressure tyre operations for military vehicles.

## Products and solutions

- **Runflat Insert System** - Patented insert enabling continued vehicle mobility on deflated tyres across all terrain.
- **Active Beadlock** - Patented technology preventing tyre bead separation during low-pressure operation.
