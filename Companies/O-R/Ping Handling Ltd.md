# Ping Handling Ltd

> Bespoke cargo and aircraft ground handling operator based at Belfast International Airport.

## What they do

Ping Handling, trading as PING Group, provides professional cargo and aircraft handling services at Belfast International Airport, with a second site at Edinburgh Airport, offering a bespoke service with added-value features for its customers. The company serves the aerospace, defence and logistics supply chain, stating it provides engineering, supply chain, and technical services to the aerospace and defence industry.

## Products and solutions

- **Aircraft and cargo handling** - Ramp technical handling, pallet build and breakdown, de-icing and anti-icing, flight manifesting, fuel management, and full flight dispatch.
- **Warehouse handling** - Internal Transit Shed Facility, authorised consignee and consignor status, and CAA-approved dangerous goods handling.
- **Transport and courier services** - Multi-modal UK and EU delivery, temperature-controlled transport, and worldwide express parcel handling.
- **Charter services** - Urgent, pharma, medical and aircraft-parts charter brokerage.
