# Ping Handling Ltd

> Bespoke cargo and aircraft ground handling operator based at Belfast International Airport.

## What they do

Ping Handling provides professional cargo and aircraft handling services at Belfast International Airport, offering a high-profile bespoke service with added-value features for its customers. The company serves the aerospace and logistics sector from its base at the airport's cargo terminal.
