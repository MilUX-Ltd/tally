# RC Fornax PLC

> Veteran-owned SME delivering managed service teams and outsourced work packages across defence engineering, project management, software, simulation, and TEMPEST services.

## What they do

RC Fornax specialises in rapid team formation and managed service delivery, providing on-site teams or outsourced work packages that sit outside IR35 constraints. Capability spans aerospace, CBRN, maritime, space, and software disciplines, with specific expertise in data analysis, software optimisation, modelling, and TEMPEST.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed service delivery** - On-site teams or outsourced work packages structured for IR35 compliance.
- **Engineering and systems consultancy** - Aerospace, maritime, and defence engineering support.
- **Software and digital services** - Data analysis, software optimisation, modelling, and TEMPEST-qualified work.
- **Simulation and modelling** - Simulation design and delivery for defence customers.
- **Training** - Engineering, simulation, software, and systems training.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus
