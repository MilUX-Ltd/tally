# PATCH
> Waterproof wearable device providing off-grid, AES-256 encrypted mesh connectivity for tactical teams with no infrastructure requirement.
## What they do
PATCH, manufactured by Roper Solutions, Inc. (Austin, TX), creates zero-infrastructure mesh networks for situational awareness and secure messaging when cellular, Wi-Fi, satellite or conventional radio are unavailable. The system uses the SnapMesh protocol, generates one-time-use AES-256 keys at network initialisation and wipes them on shutdown. Designed with no operating system, no open-source code and no persistent data storage; independently tested as jamming-resistant outside 277 yards. NDAA compliant and ATAK-compatible. Represented in the UK by Benjia.
## Products and solutions
- **PATCH device** - Wearable encrypted mesh node; AES-256, BLE Secure Connections for smartphone pairing, automatic location and activity monitoring across the network.
