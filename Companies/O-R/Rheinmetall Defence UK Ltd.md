# Rheinmetall Defence UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of Rheinmetall AG, a German defence and automotive prime with capabilities spanning armoured vehicles, weapons systems, air defence and ammunition.

## What they do

Rheinmetall Defence UK is the British entity of Rheinmetall AG, one of Europe's largest defence primes. The group's defence activities cover armoured vehicles and survivability systems, large-calibre weapons and ammunition, air defence systems, and sensors and electronics for land and air platforms.
