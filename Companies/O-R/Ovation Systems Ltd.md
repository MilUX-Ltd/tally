# Ovation Systems Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of rugged, high-performance video surveillance products for military, police and government law enforcement agencies worldwide.

## What they do

Ovation Systems designs, develops and manufactures its own range of rugged video recording and surveillance products for covert vehicle and body-worn deployments. The company states it has supplied the world's law enforcement, military and government agencies with video and surveillance products for over twenty years, with all products designed, developed and manufactured in the UK. Products are supplied to military, police and government law enforcement customers and include digital video recorders and streamers with SD, HD, WiFi and 4G connectivity.

## Products and solutions

- **FlashBack-2 and FlashBack-3** - miniature, rugged and compact digital video recorders for covert, body-worn and mobile recording; FlashBack-3 uses H.264 video compression, offering over an hour of record time per GB.
- **AfterBurner and AfterBurner-2** - evidential DVD and digital video recorders designed to meet police evidential standards.
- **AfterBurner-IP** - 4-channel IP video recorder.
- **ViewLock-II and Micro-ViewLock-II** - video encryption systems using line-cut-and-rotate encryption with programmable encryption keys; Micro-ViewLock-II is a miniature version for ultra-covert and body-worn use.
- **Digital video recorders and streamers** - compact, rugged flash-memory SD and HD recorders for covert vehicle and body-worn use, with WiFi and 4G connectivity options.
