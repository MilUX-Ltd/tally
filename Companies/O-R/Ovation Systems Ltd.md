# Ovation Systems Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of rugged, high-performance video surveillance products for military, police and government law enforcement agencies worldwide.

## What they do

Ovation Systems designs and develops its own range of rugged video recording and surveillance products for covert vehicle and body-worn deployments. Products are supplied to military, police and government law enforcement customers and include digital video recorders and streamers with SD, HD, WiFi and 4G connectivity.

## Products and solutions

- **Digital video recorders and streamers** - compact, rugged flash-memory SD and HD recorders for covert vehicle and body-worn use.
- **Surveillance systems** - integrated video surveillance solutions for military and law enforcement platforms.
