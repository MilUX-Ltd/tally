# Pandect Precision Components Ltd

> Specialist designer and manufacturer of custom slip ring assemblies for the defence and aerospace sectors.

## What they do

Pandect has designed and manufactured custom slip ring assemblies (SRAs) for passing data and power across rotating interfaces since 1964, with the defence and aerospace markets as its primary customers. Its products integrate microwave rotary joints, fibre optic rotary joints, and hydraulic or pneumatic rotary joints depending on application requirements.

## Products and solutions

- **Slip Ring Assemblies** - Custom assemblies for long-term defence and aerospace programmes, supporting data, power, RF, and optical transmission across rotating interfaces.
- **Microwave Rotary Joints** - Integrated into SRAs for radar and satellite-on-the-move applications.
- **Fibre Optic Rotary Joints** - Integrated into SRAs for very high data rate requirements.
