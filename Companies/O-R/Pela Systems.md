# Pela Systems

> Manufacturer of the patented PELAmesh sensor integration and fusion range, providing detection, identification and monitoring for CBRNe and hazardous environments.

## What they do

Pela Systems designs and builds the PELAmesh range, a portable, sensor-agnostic detector integration network aimed at Chemical, Biological, Radiological, Nuclear and explosives (CBRNe) and hazmat situations. PELAmesh networks detectors from multiple manufacturers into a single visual display, giving operators real-time, positionally mapped data from legacy and current-generation sensors in harsh environments. The company states that it designs and builds all hardware and software in the UK, and serves defence and CBRN forces, critical national infrastructure and transport hub operators, first responders, and conservation and agriculture customers. Pela Systems is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.

## Products and solutions

- **PELAmesh** - patented, portable, sensor-agnostic detector integration and fusion network; ruggedised to exceed IP69 and STD-810H; encrypted data transmission from sensors to a Commander unit metres or many miles away.
- **PELAmesh Commander** - field-deployable hardware for storing, processing and re-transmitting sensor data to specialists at distance.
- **PELAcan** - field-deployable sensor node component of the PELAmesh range.
- **Scene Assessment** - situational awareness tools for hazardous incident response.
- **Tactical Communication** - communication capability supporting detection and response operations.
