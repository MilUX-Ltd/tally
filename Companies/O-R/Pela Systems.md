# Pela Systems
> Manufacturers of the patented PELAmesh sensor integration range, specialising in data acquisition from legacy and current sensors in harsh environments.
## What they do
Pela Systems manufactures the PELAmesh range of sensor integration products, enabling data acquisition from both legacy and current-generation sensors operating in harsh environments. A member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
