# Premier Hytemp Ltd

> Manufacturer and supplier of engineered metal solutions across aerospace and defence supply chains.

## What they do

Premier Hytemp, headquartered in Kirknewton, Scotland and incorporated in 1985, manufactures and manages engineered metal materials and components across the aerospace, defence, oil and gas, and nuclear supply chains. It states its services span material management, first-stage machining, heat treatment, cladding, testing and finished machining, supplying low alloy steel, stainless steel, nickel alloy and bar stock. The company describes a four-decade reputation for engineered solutions to complex material requirements and for meeting challenging lead times. Premier Hytemp was acquired by Souter Investments in partnership with the existing management team in November 2023.

## Products and solutions

- **Material supply** - low alloy steel, stainless steel, nickel alloy and bar stock for aerospace, defence, oil and gas, and nuclear applications.
- **Processing services** - first-stage machining, heat treatment, cladding, proof machining, final machining and testing.
