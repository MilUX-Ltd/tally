# Rochester Electronics Ltd

> Authorised semiconductor distributor and licensed manufacturer specialising in end-of-life and active devices for defence and aerospace applications.

## What they do

Rochester Electronics describes itself as the world's largest continuous source of semiconductors, authorised by more than 70 leading manufacturers to distribute and manufacture devices no longer produced by the original maker. Founded in 1981 in Massachusetts, USA, with its UK operation registered as an overseas company establishment, the company states its mission is to provide the semiconductor industry with an authorised, guaranteed supply source for long-lifecycle applications, holding more than 15 billion devices across 200,000-plus part numbers.

## Products and solutions

- **Authorised distribution** - 100% authorised, traceable distribution of active and end-of-life semiconductors.
- **Licensed manufacturing** - full manufacturing, including design, wafer processing, assembly, test and reliability, for turnkey re-manufacture of obsolete devices; over 20,000 device types produced, with capacity for more than 70,000.
- **Die bank** - wafer back-grinding, precision dicing, automated optical inspection and packaging, holding more than 12 billion die in stock.
- **Wafer bank** - wafer preservation in ISO-certified clean environments.
- **Obsolescence management** - replication of die, chip and package designs for devices no longer available.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; AS9120; QML (MIL-PRF-38535); QSLD (Qualified Suppliers List of Distributors).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group (UK); ADS Toulouse (France, joined 2025).
