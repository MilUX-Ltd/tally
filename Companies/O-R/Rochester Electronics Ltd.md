# Rochester Electronics Ltd

> Authorised semiconductor distributor and licensed manufacturer specialising in end-of-life and active devices for defence and aerospace applications.

## What they do

Rochester Electronics is the world's largest continuous source of semiconductors, authorised by over 70 leading manufacturers, holding more than 15 billion devices across 200,000+ part numbers. It also operates as a licensed semiconductor manufacturer, producing over 20,000 device types to support long-lifecycle programmes where original devices are no longer available.
