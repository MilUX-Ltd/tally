# QRT Power Ltd

> Engineering and technology solutions provider delivering mission-critical services in defence, energy, and infrastructure, including UAV technologies and turnkey project delivery.

## What they do

QRT Power delivers full life-cycle engineering projects under single-point accountability, from concept design through operational commissioning, in high-risk and complex environments. Core capabilities include UAV technologies for advanced surveying and ISR, alongside engineering services for government, industrial, and defence clients.

## Products and solutions

- **UAV Surveying** - drone technology equipped with specialised sensors for safe, high-resolution surveys of hazardous or inaccessible areas, supporting unexploded ordnance detection and infrastructure damage assessment.
