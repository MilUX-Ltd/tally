# Occam Industries
> Early-stage London-based autonomy company building under the tagline "Autonomy Simplified".
## What they do
Occam Industries is a post-stealth autonomy start-up founded in 2025 and based in London. The company's public positioning is values-led and sits firmly in the Western defence-tech and European-sovereignty bracket, with explicit orientation towards Ukraine. Its stated product is a retrofit computer-vision autopilot for FPV drones, installed as software on existing, mass-manufactured drone airframes without hardware or supply-chain changes.

## Products and solutions

- **OccamX** - a retrofit autopilot using onboard computer vision for perception, target tracking and navigation, intended to reduce reliance on continuous manual control, GPS, or external datalinks in contested or GPS-denied environments.
