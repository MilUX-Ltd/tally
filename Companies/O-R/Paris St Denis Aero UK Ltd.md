# Paris St Denis Aero UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of the PSD Group, supplying high-performance aerospace metals with AS9100 and AS9120 accreditation.

## What they do

Paris St Denis Aero UK Ltd supplies precision aerospace metals including stainless steel, titanium, aluminium, copper, nickel, and cobalt to the aerospace and defence supply chain. Operating from Bolton, the UK entity combines the PSD Group's global reach with local expertise, supported by AS9100 and AS9120 quality management accreditations.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace Metals Supply** - Stainless steel, titanium, aluminium, copper, nickel, and cobalt to aerospace and defence specifications.
- **Value-Added Services** - Integrated stock management, precision materials processing, and supply chain support.

- **Accreditations:** AS9100, AS9120
