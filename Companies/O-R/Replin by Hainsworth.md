# Replin by Hainsworth

> Yorkshire-based manufacturer of precision interior fabrics for the aviation sector.

## What they do

Replin by Hainsworth designs and manufactures interior fabrics for aviation clients, covering seating, wall coverings, curtains, and blinds. Founded in 1945, the brand marks 80 years of aviation textile supply to major international airlines. Since a 2015 merger with A.W. Hainsworth, a Yorkshire wool mill trading since 1783, Replin combines wool and modern fibre expertise with bespoke design, weaving and finishing at its Pudsey mill to produce durable, specification-compliant aviation interior textiles.

## Products and solutions

- **Aviation seating fabrics** - abrasion-resistant, tightly woven textiles for passenger, pilot and crew seating.
- **Aviation curtain fabric** - flame-retardant, colour-fast fabric for cabin curtains.
- **Vertical surface fabric (WNN and WNN Nature)** - high-traffic cabin wall covering, including an aramid and undyed wool blend.
- **Eclipse and Eclipse Steadfast ranges** - wool and aramid (Nomex) blends for premium seating pods, including undyed alpaca and aramid blends.
- **Steadfast Collection** - sustainable, undyed-fibre fabric range for cabin interiors.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018. Fabrics stated to meet FAR/CS 25.853 aviation flammability requirements, tested in an in-house UKAS-accredited laboratory.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Green Cabin Alliance.
