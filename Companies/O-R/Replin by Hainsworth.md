# Replin by Hainsworth

> Yorkshire-based manufacturer of precision interior fabrics for the aviation sector.

## What they do

Replin by Hainsworth designs and manufactures interior fabrics for aviation clients, covering seating, wall coverings, curtains, and blinds. The company combines wool and modern fibre expertise with bespoke design and precision engineering to produce durable, specification-compliant aviation interior textiles.
