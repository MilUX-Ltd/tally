# Overwatch Aerospace

> Independent British defence company designing and manufacturing unmanned aerial systems and precision strike munitions.

## What they do

Overwatch is an independent British defence business that designs, engineers and manufactures unmanned aerial systems and precision strike solutions for kinetic and non-kinetic operations. It develops pack-portable UAS and loitering munitions, and works with energetic OEMs on precision strike technology. The company forms part of Overwatch Group.

## Products and solutions

- **Unmanned aerial systems** - pack-portable UAS and VTOL platforms, including work with the PteroDynamics Transwing design.
- **Loitering munitions** - precision strike systems with kinetic payloads.
- **Energetics** - precision strike and energetic solutions with OEM partners.
