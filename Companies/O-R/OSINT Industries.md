# OSINT Industries

> Automated, scalable open-source intelligence platform serving law enforcement, government and OSINT practitioners across more than 1,500 sources.

## What they do

OSINT Industries provides an automated OSINT platform that checks emails, phone numbers and usernames in real time across more than 1,500 sources to identify linked accounts on social and messaging platforms. Customer base includes more than 5,000 law enforcement departments worldwide. Searches are not retained post-query. The company also delivers tailored OSINT training and bespoke tooling for clients with specific operational needs.

## Products and solutions

- **OSINT platform** - real-time enrichment of email, phone number and username selectors across more than 1,500 public sources, with no retention of search data.
- **API access** - integration of real-time OSINT data into a customer's existing platforms, including bulk data integration.
- **Training programmes** - modular, sector-specific OSINT training delivered live online or in person, including for law enforcement, government, non-profit and journalism sectors.
- **Free platform access schemes** - access for law enforcement, government, non-profit and journalism sector users.
