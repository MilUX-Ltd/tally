# Radio Design Ltd

> UK sovereign manufacturer of RF and microwave sub-system solutions for the defence market, covering filters, countermeasures, and counter-UAS applications.

## What they do

Radio Design Ltd specialises in the design and manufacture of RF and microwave sub-systems, including analogue and digital circuit design, embedded systems, and mechanical design. The company supplies components and sub-systems for communications, radar, electronic countermeasures, and counter-drone applications.

## Products and solutions

- **RF/Microwave filters and circuits** - Custom filter and sub-system design for defence electronics.
- **Counter-UAS systems** - Counter-drone and counter-UAV capability.
- **Electronic countermeasures** - Jamming, scramblers, and electronic effectors.
- **Box build and PCB assembly** - IPC 610 Class 2 manufacture of circuit boards and flexible circuits.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR Registered
