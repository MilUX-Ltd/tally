# PAC Group

> Northern Ireland engineering firm providing process automation, robotics, and composite manufacturing solutions for industrial and defence sectors.

## What they do

PAC Group designs, manufactures, and commissions automated machinery and manufacturing systems for industrial and commercial clients across Northern Ireland and the UK. It operates through service divisions including PAC Automation, PAC Composites and PAC Calibrations. Capabilities include process automation, robotics, cobots and autonomous mobile robots, remote monitoring, and composites manufacturing (resin transfer infusion, preformers, hot drape formers). Customers span aerospace, automotive, marine and defence-adjacent manufacturing.

## Products and solutions

- **PAC Automation** - design, build and integration of bespoke automated machinery, robotics, cobots and Industry 4.0 manufacturing systems.
- **Preformers and hot drape formers** - large-format composite forming equipment for aerospace and automotive composite layup, including work for the Aerospace Innovation Centre in Prestwick.
- **Resin transfer infusion equipment** - composite manufacturing systems for aerospace, automotive and marine applications.
- **PAC Calibrations** - instrumentation and calibration services for industrial process control.
