# PAC Group

> Northern Ireland engineering firm providing process automation, robotics, and composite manufacturing solutions for industrial and defence sectors.

## What they do

PAC Group designs, manufactures, and commissions automated machinery and manufacturing systems for industrial and commercial clients across Northern Ireland and the UK. Capabilities include process automation, robotics and cobots, composites manufacturing (resin transfer infusion, preformers, hot drape formers), and design and manufacture of high-specification equipment.
