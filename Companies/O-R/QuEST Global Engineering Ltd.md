# QuEST Global Engineering Ltd

> Global product engineering and lifecycle services company serving aerospace and defence OEMs and tier suppliers.

## What they do

QuEST Global partners with leading OEMs and technology providers to deliver engineering services across mechanical design, manufacturing technologies, embedded electronics, and software engineering. The company operates across aerospace and defence, combining product engineering, digital and domain expertise to accelerate development programmes and reduce costs for strategic clients. It has supported aerospace customers in the UK since 2004, from its Derby office, and describes its aerospace and defence offer as spanning the full product lifecycle, from design through aftermarket, for mechanical and embedded parts and systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Product engineering services** - mechanical design, manufacturing technologies and embedded electronics for aerospace and defence OEMs and tier suppliers.
- **Digital engineering** - engineering analysis and lifecycle services intended to support faster development and lower costs.
