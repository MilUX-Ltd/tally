# Omni Executive Ltd

> Defence, national security, intelligence, and critical infrastructure consultancy delivering solutions in complex and demanding environments.

## What they do

Omni Executive Ltd is the UK arm of the Omni Executive group, launched to extend the group's defence and national security capabilities into the United Kingdom. The group provides solutions focused on defence, national security, intelligence, and critical infrastructure, working to further the national interest. Its stated approach centres on trusted, proven delivery in demanding environments, protecting individuals, organisations, and governments from traditional and non-traditional risks. Group-wide, the business states it draws on more than 400 professionals across consulting, vetting, security, and aerospace, including veterans, former senior police, and intelligence specialists.

## Products and solutions

- **Personnel vetting and psychological suitability assessment** - vetting services assessed against recognised protective security and personnel standards.
- **Risk consultancy** - risk assessment, threat and vulnerability evaluation, and treatment planning.
- **Training** - hostile environment awareness, personal safety, situational awareness, trauma first aid, counter-elicitation, and resilience and leadership training.
- **Intelligence and professional services** - support across the intelligence capability lifecycle.
