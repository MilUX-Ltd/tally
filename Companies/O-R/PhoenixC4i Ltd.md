# PhoenixC4i Ltd

> Technology R&D consultancy providing independent advice and solutions for defence C4i challenges in the UK and internationally.

## What they do

PhoenixC4i is an independent consultancy addressing technical challenges within government, defence, security and commercial markets, delivering technology-based research and development in the command, control, communications, computers, and intelligence (C4i) domain. The company states it develops next-generation technologies across the electromagnetic, optical and digital domains, aiming at low-cost, deployable systems that maintain operational advantage.

## Products and solutions

- **Concept development and rapid prototyping** - Early-stage technical solutioning for defence and government clients.
- **Systems design, integration and testing** - Including independent test, validation and verification.
- **RF, antenna and communications engineering** - Wireless and tactical communications design work.
- **Information assurance and network resilience** - Network protection and hardening, and access control.
- **Applied research and analysis, field trials and environmental testing** - Supporting capability development through practical trials.
