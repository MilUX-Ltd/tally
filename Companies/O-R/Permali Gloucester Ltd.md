# Permali Gloucester Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of high-performance composite and polymeric materials for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Permali designs and manufactures lightweight ballistic and mine protection solutions, including composite vehicle structures and cargo systems for aircraft, with protection levels to STANAG 4569 Level 4 and above. The company is also a licensed applicator of Battlejacket spray-on fuel tank protection for armoured vehicles and aircraft.

## Products and solutions

- **Ballistic and mine protection panels** - Lightweight composite armour for armoured fighting vehicles, certified to STANAG 4569 Level 4 and higher.
- **Aircraft cargo and ballistic systems** - Lightweight structural and protective cargo systems for fixed and rotary-wing platforms.
- **Battlejacket fuel tank protection** - Licensed spray-on protection for aircraft and vehicle fuel systems.
