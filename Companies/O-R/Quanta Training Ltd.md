# Quanta Training Ltd

> Training design, delivery, and consultancy business with a dedicated Defence division serving the UK MOD and armed forces.

## What they do

Quanta Training provides training needs analysis, training design, training delivery, and consultancy across agile, cyber, IT service management (ITSM/ITIL), project and programme management, technical IT, and leadership development. Its Defence division has worked with a range of MOD departments for over a decade, providing security-cleared trainers and MOD-compliant equipment and course software, and supports the Forces Resettlement scheme through Enhanced Learning Credits (ELC).

## Products and solutions

- **Defence training programmes** - bespoke, MOD-compliant training delivered by security-cleared trainers, aligned to purchasing frameworks and departmental objectives.
- **Training Needs Analysis (TNA)** - a structured process to identify skills gaps, evaluate existing skills, and target training budget against defined objectives.
- **Cyber training** - courses spanning cyber best practice (CISM, CISSP), EC-Council (CEH, CSCU), CompTIA (CySA+, PenTest+), and general cyber security awareness.
- **Forces Resettlement (ELC) courses** - training funded through Enhanced Learning Credits for serving and former armed forces personnel.
- **Civil Service Learning delivery** - Quanta is an approved learning specialist under the Civil Service Learning framework, delivering courses used across government departments including the MOD.
