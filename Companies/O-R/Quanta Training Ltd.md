# Quanta Training Ltd

> Training design, delivery, and consultancy business with 20 years of experience serving the UK defence industry and MOD.

## What they do

Quanta Training provides training needs analysis, training design, training delivery, and consultancy across technical IT, communications systems, project and programme management, IT service management, and leadership development. They hold JSP822 DSAT accreditation and work with customers across the tri-service and defence primes.
