# Prevail Partners

> UK defence and security consultancy offering intelligence, operational support and defence technologies, with a focus on border security and maritime domain awareness.

## What they do

Prevail Partners is a UK defence and security consultancy incorporated in 2018, with a registered office in Poole, England. It provides operational support, intelligence and defence capabilities to government, law-enforcement and commercial clients, with a focus on border security and maritime domain awareness. Its services span intelligence fusion, risk management, training, financial investigations, and cyber operations and security, and it has moved into defence technology through partnerships in unmanned systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Security and risk management** - operational support, due diligence, asset recovery and risk management for complex environments.
- **Border security and maritime intelligence** - border security and maritime domain awareness services.
- **Intelligence services** - human, satellite, imagery, open-source and signals intelligence, plus financial forensics and bulk data analytics.
- **Raybird (via Skyeton Prevail Solutions)** - long-endurance unmanned aerial system offered through a joint venture with Ukrainian UAV company Skyeton.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Joint ventures:** Skyeton Prevail Solutions Ltd, a joint venture with Skyeton to deliver the Raybird UAS in the UK.
