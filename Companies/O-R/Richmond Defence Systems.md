# Richmond Defence Systems

> Norfolk-based manufacturer of explosive ordnance disposal equipment, supplying EOD operators across military, policing, security and NGO customers on five continents.

## What they do

Richmond Defence Systems (RDS) designs and manufactures equipment for explosive ordnance disposal operators, focused on defeating improvised explosive devices and unexploded ordnance. Customers include military and policing forces, presidential protection and security teams, airport authorities and NGOs worldwide.
