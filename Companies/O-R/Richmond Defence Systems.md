# Richmond Defence Systems

> Norfolk-based manufacturer of explosive ordnance disposal equipment, supplying EOD operators across military, policing, security and NGO customers on five continents.

## What they do

Richmond Defence Systems (RDS) designs and manufactures equipment for explosive ordnance disposal operators, focused on defeating improvised explosive devices and unexploded ordnance. Customers include military and policing forces, presidential protection and security teams, airport authorities and NGOs worldwide. The company states its Norfolk facilities include a 30-metre indoor ballistic test range, CNC machining, 3D printing and cartridge loading and electro-mechanical assembly.

## Products and solutions

- **EOD de-armer and disruptor equipment** - equipment to aid EOD operators in de-arming and disrupting IEDs and unexploded ordnance.
- **Search and detection equipment** - specialist equipment to aid location and identification of UXO and IEDs.
- **ROV integration systems** - systems for mounting EOD disruptors to third-party remotely operated vehicles.
- **Aerial disruptor systems** - disruptor-equipped drone for use in ground-hostile environments.
