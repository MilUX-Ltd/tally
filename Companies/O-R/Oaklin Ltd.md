# Oaklin Ltd

> Strategy, programme delivery and digital transformation consultancy serving the aerospace and defence sectors alongside other industries.

## What they do

Oaklin helps business leaders solve complex problems and deliver lasting change through strategy, programme management and digital transformation. Their sector experience includes aerospace, defence, energy, engineering, financial services and telecommunications. On the Aerospace and Defence sector page, Oaklin states its consultants hold security clearances and bring experience across defence, aerospace and national security work.

## Products and solutions

- **Strategy and commercial advisory** - support in shaping strategy, commercial decisions and innovation direction.
- **Digital and cloud transformation** - digital transformation and cloud transformation programme delivery.
- **Data and AI** - services covering data governance and risk mapping for AI use, scalable data solutions, and embedding analytics into client organisations.
- **Programme and project delivery** - programme management, business improvement, and organisational and people change.
- **M&A integration** - support for mergers, acquisitions and inorganic growth, including integration.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
