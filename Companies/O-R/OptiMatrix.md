# OptiMatrix
> Independent defence and aerospace consultancy specialising in procurement, supply chain, commercial, and operations management. JOSCAR registered.
## What they do
OptiMatrix provides independent consulting services to the defence, aerospace and security sector across procurement, supply chain, commercial and operations management. Work extends to programme management, project management and bid management. The firm has supported both industry clients (from SMEs to international primes) and government agencies, including MOD transformation programmes and complex procurement environments. JOSCAR registered since 2020; customers using JOSCAR include BAE Systems, MOD, Babcock, Leonardo, QinetiQ, NATS, Meggitt, MBDA, General Dynamics, Rolls-Royce, Roke and Leidos.

## Products and solutions

- **Commercial strategy development and execution** - shaping how equipment and services are sourced, acquired and supported through life.
- **Bid and proposal development** - support for industry clients preparing defence and aerospace tenders.
- **Market engagement, sourcing and tendering** - support across procurement and supply chain optimisation.
- **Operational design and restructure** - support for organisational and operational change programmes.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR Registered
- **Memberships:** ADS Group (Defence SME Committee); Defence Industry Joint Council (DIJC) Communications forum; UK Defence and Security Export (UKDSE) Faculty.
