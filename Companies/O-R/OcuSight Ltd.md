# OcuSight Ltd

> Developer of passive surveillance camera systems with AI-driven image processing for situational awareness applications.

## What they do

OcuSight designs passive surveillance cameras and systems integrating advanced image processing and AI algorithms. Their capability set covers IP-based sensors, edge computing, target tracking, motion detection and classification, aimed at delivering enhanced situational awareness. The company is based at the OpTIC Technology Centre in St Asaph, North Wales, and describes its team as bringing over 100 person-years of experience in electro-optical systems for defence and paramilitary applications.

## Products and solutions

- **PARS** - a stabilised imaging and positioner product incorporating a Long-Wave InfraRed (LWIR) camera housed to provide a second axis of motion without external mechanisms, a single connector for camera and stabiliser functions, and an integrated IMU for motion tracking and control. Positioned as cost-effective stabilised imaging for uncrewed ground and surface platforms and one-way mission systems.
