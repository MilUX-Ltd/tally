# OcuSight Ltd

> Developer of passive surveillance camera systems with AI-driven image processing for situational awareness applications.

## What they do

OcuSight designs passive surveillance cameras and systems integrating advanced image processing and AI algorithms. Their capability set covers IP-based sensors, edge computing, target tracking, motion detection and classification, aimed at delivering enhanced situational awareness.
