# Research Innovations

> Non-traditional defence software company bringing commercial technology to national security problems in the UK, US and Canada.

## What they do

Research Innovations Inc (RII) develops Joint All-Domain C2, tactical C2, cyber, AI, and Information Operations products used across the joint force from tactical edge to strategic level. The company has a consistent track record of transitioning R&D into operational use across UK, US and Canadian customers.
