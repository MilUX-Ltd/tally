# Research Innovations

> Non-traditional defence software company bringing commercial technology to national security problems in the UK, US and Canada.

## What they do

Research Innovations Inc (RII) develops Joint All-Domain C2, tactical C2, cyber, AI, and Information Operations products used across the joint force from tactical edge to strategic level. The company has worked with the UK Ministry of Defence since 2017, developing joint and multi-domain C2 capabilities in support of UK Defence's data strategy, with its London team working alongside the British Army's Information Activities and Operations programme and UK Strategic Command. RII has a consistent track record of transitioning R&D into operational use across UK, US and Canadian customers, with offices in Alexandria (Virginia), Melbourne and St Petersburg (Florida), San Antonio (Texas) and London.

## Products and solutions

- **PRIISM (Decision Advantage Platform)** - flagship Combined Joint All-Domain Command and Control product, combining data integration, data security and AI-driven analytics for coalition and joint decision-making.
- **PRIISM Accelerated Planning** - described as compressing a 24-month planning cycle to 24 days.
- **PRIISM Joint Targeting Platform** - compresses the sensor-to-decision timeline across domains and coalition networks.
- **PRIISM Information Advantage** - sensing, sense-making and acting on authoritative and open-source data across the information environment.
- **PRIISM MAX** - variant architecture supporting multi-national data sharing and configurable network boundaries for coalition targeting.
