# Ocean Infinity Group (UK) Ltd

> Large-scale marine robotics company operating fleets of uncrewed surface and underwater vehicles for ocean data and maritime defence.

## What they do

Ocean Infinity deploys fleets of marine robots, operated remotely from onshore control centres via satellite, to capture ocean and seabed data and deliver maritime solutions. The company describes itself as a pioneer of large-scale marine robotic operations, working across survey, offshore energy and defence markets. Operations are run from control centres in Southampton and Austin.

## Products and solutions

- **Armada fleet** - robotic and optionally-crewed surface vessels (78-metre and 86-metre classes) operated remotely from shore.
- **Infinity fleet** - autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs) for deep-ocean data acquisition.
- **NeedleFish USV** - 14-metre dual-hull uncrewed surface vessel for patrol, surveillance, mapping and survey.
