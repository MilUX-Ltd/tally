# riskHive Technical Services Ltd

> Risk analytical software, enterprise risk management installations, and subject matter expert consultancy for defence, security, and aerospace.

## What they do

riskHive provides risk analytical software and ERM installations alongside experienced risk consultants into the defence, security, and aerospace sectors. Founded in 1999, the company has delivered integrated and agile risk management software applications and services for over two decades. Clients include DE&S (CAAS and Project Controls), DIO, Main Building, AWE, Airbus, BAE Systems, and UK intelligence agencies.

## Products and solutions

- **riskHive Enterprise Risk Manager** - secure, browser-based risk database supporting organisational risk management.
- **Arrisca Risk Analyser** - tool for assurance, risk-adjustment and stochastic analysis of cost and schedule models built in Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Project.
- **Independent Cost Analysis Service (iCAS)** - service developed with UK MoD and industry over twenty years, providing assurance and optimisation of cost, schedule and performance models.
