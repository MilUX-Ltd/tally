# Ontic Engineering & Manufacturing Ltd

> Global aerospace OEM specialising in the acquisition, manufacture, and support of legacy and hard-to-source aerospace product lines.

## What they do

Ontic is a global aerospace OEM with over 1,300 employees across seven sites and four time zones, including its Cheltenham and Bishops Cleeve sites in the UK. The company acquires product lines divested by leading aviation OEMs, manufacturing and supporting those components over the long term so the original manufacturers can focus on new development. Its portfolio spans electronics, electromechanical systems, hydraulics, pneumatics, and structural components, including fuel measurement and gauging systems, avionics, and cockpit instruments and controls, across more than 200 licensed product lines. Platforms supported include Airbus and Boeing commercial airliners, Sikorsky and Leonardo helicopters, and fighter aircraft including the F-16. Ontic is owned by CVC Capital Partners, following acquisition from BBA Aviation in 2019 and a recapitalisation with new minority investors in 2024.

## Products and solutions

- **Legacy aerospace product line acquisition** - acquiring and sustaining product lines divested by original aviation OEMs.
- **Electromechanical and fuel systems** - fuel measurement and gauging equipment, including systems transferred from Airbus for the A320 family.
- **Avionics and cockpit instruments** - cockpit instruments and controls for commercial and military platforms.
- **MRO services** - maintenance, repair and overhaul support for legacy aerospace components.
