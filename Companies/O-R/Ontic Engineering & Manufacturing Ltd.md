# Ontic Engineering & Manufacturing Ltd

> Global aerospace OEM specialising in the acquisition, manufacture, and support of legacy and hard-to-source aerospace product lines.

## What they do

Ontic is a global aerospace OEM with over 1,300 employees across seven sites and four time zones. The company acquires product lines divested by leading aviation OEMs, manufacturing and supporting those components over the long term so the original manufacturers can focus on new development.
