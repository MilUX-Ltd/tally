# Quell

> Scottish defence technology company developing AI-powered passive threat detection systems for counter-drone and national security applications.

## What they do

Quell develops advanced machine learning systems for national security, centred on its proprietary Deep Truth® AI platform. Its SkySpyke® product is a passive 360-degree threat detection system designed to counter drone and other unmanned threats, with land and maritime variants engineered for contested environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Deep Truth®** - Proprietary AI and machine learning platform underpinning Quell's threat detection systems; capable of real-time AI model generation in rapidly changing conditions.
- **SkySpyke®** - Passive 360-degree counter-UAS system with land and maritime variants; detects, classifies, tracks and neutralises aerial and surface threats.
