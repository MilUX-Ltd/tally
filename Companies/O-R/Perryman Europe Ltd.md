# Perryman Europe Ltd

> European arm of a global titanium alloy and mill products supplier serving aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Perryman Company supplies titanium alloys and titanium mill products globally, with bar production capability ranging from 457 mm down to 0.2 mm diameter. The company melts titanium ingots and is a leading supplier of titanium additive manufacturing wire for aerospace, medical, and infrastructure markets.
