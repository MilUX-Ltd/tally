# Piran Advanced Composites Ltd

> Advanced composite components and structures manufacturer serving aerospace, defence and marine customers.

## What they do

Piran Advanced Composites Ltd, part of the Piran Technology group, designs and manufactures advanced composite components and structures using autoclave, oven and press curing processes alongside multiple 5-axis CNC machining centres. The company operates four autoclaves ranging from 1 metre to 3 metres in diameter, four Class 8 cleanrooms, and automated ply-cutting capability at its site at Aerohub, Newquay Cornwall Airport. For the defence and military sector specifically, the company states its expertise includes lightweighting military vehicles and components, and manufacturing armour and ballistics protection and fairings for defence and military vehicles.

## Products and solutions

- **Military vehicle components** - lightweighted composite components and armour/ballistics protection for military vehicles.
- **Defence UAV structures** - composite airframe and structural components for defence uncrewed aerial vehicles.
- **Fighter jet components** - composite parts for fast jet applications.
- **Submarine components** - composite antennas and radar-related components for submarine platforms.
- **Missile and rocketry components** - composite parts for missiles and rocketry applications.
