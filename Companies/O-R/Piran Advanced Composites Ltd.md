# Piran Advanced Composites Ltd

> Advanced composite components and structures manufacturer serving aerospace, defence and marine customers.

## What they do

Piran Advanced Composites Ltd, part of the Piran Technology group, designs and manufactures advanced composite components and structures using autoclave, oven and press curing processes alongside multiple 5-axis CNC machining centres. The company operates four autoclaves ranging from 1 metre to 3 metres in diameter, four Class 8 cleanrooms, and automated ply-cutting capability at its site at Aerohub, Newquay Cornwall Airport.
