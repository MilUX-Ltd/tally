# Ruddy Nice Ltd

> Defence consultancy with expertise in military training and simulation, and in exhibitions and events.

## What they do

Ruddy Nice provides advisory and delivery services across military training and simulation, defence consultancy, and defence-sector exhibitions and events management. Founded in 2011, the company is based in Salisbury, a hub for UK defence activity, and works across marketing, business development and event delivery for aerospace and defence clients.

## Products and solutions

- **Serious Games, Simulation and Wargaming Hub** - a facility in Salisbury bringing together serious games, simulation and wargaming development and innovation talent.
- **DSET (Defence, Simulation, Education and Training)** - an event run by Ruddy Nice for the military training and simulation community.
- **Defence media and business development consultancy** - marketing strategy, press and social media campaigns, website design, and business development support for aerospace and defence clients, including start-ups entering the sector.
- **Exhibitions and events delivery** - end-to-end event management including booth design and build for defence-sector exhibitions.
