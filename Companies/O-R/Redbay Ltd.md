# Redbay Ltd

> Cyber security consultancy helping organisations understand and mitigate their cyber risks.

## What they do

Redbay is a digital resilience consultancy spanning two service lines, cyber security and digital delivery. It works with organisations of all sizes to assess cyber risk and deliver tailored security capabilities and mitigations, with a focus on protecting operational output and revenue through practical, risk-proportionate cyber security measures.

## Products and solutions

- **Vulnerability management** - identifying and managing vulnerabilities to strengthen compliance and system resilience.
- **Security programme development** - security controls, governance frameworks, incident response plans and technical risk identification.
- **Secure by design** - shaping architecture decisions and defining controls ahead of development, applying NIST risk management and security control frameworks, and assessing in-flight projects against secure-by-design principles.
