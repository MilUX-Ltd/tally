# OxCyber CIC

> Not-for-profit cyber security community for individuals, SMEs and organisations across Oxfordshire and the Thames Valley.

## What they do

OxCyber is a community interest company that acts as a hub for the cyber technology ecosystem of Oxfordshire and the Thames Valley. It supports growth and development through industry connections, networking events and workshops, mentorship and training, and knowledge sharing on emerging cyber threats.

## Products and solutions

- **Community and networking** - events and workshops connecting cyber security professionals and SMEs.
- **Talent development** - mentorship and training initiatives.
