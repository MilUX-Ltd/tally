# RunX Technology Inc.

> Deterministic FPGA and SoC solutions for defence, aerospace and industrial applications.

## What they do

RunX Technology is a deep-tech company based in Ankara, Türkiye, specialising in deterministic FPGA and SoC solutions for mission-critical defence, aerospace and industrial use. It provides end-to-end FPGA engineering: system architecture, RTL design, IP core development, verification, system integration and hardware optimisation, with a focus on high-speed communications, radar signal processing, real-time video processing and embedded AI. It is an Altera Solution Acceleration Partner and a global partner of Lattice Semiconductor.

## Products and solutions

- **FPGA and SoC design** - deterministic FPGA and SoC solutions for mission-critical applications.
- **IP cores** - production-ready IP cores for video processing and high-speed interfaces.
- **Radar and signal processing** - real-time radar signal processing and data acquisition.
- **Embedded AI and vision** - FPGA-accelerated edge AI and computer vision systems.
- **High-speed communications** - Ethernet, PCIe, DDR and JESD204-based communication architectures.
- **Consulting** - RTL design, verification, integration and hardware optimisation.
