# PhillipsManley Ltd

> Independent mechanical, systems, and manufacturing engineering consultancy delivering asset lifecycle support for defence and industry.

## What they do

PhillipsManley provides asset management and lifecycle engineering services, including maintenance strategy development, maintenance scheduling, spares optimisation, obsolescence management, and reliability engineering. The consultancy applies engineering data analysis and visualisation to improve the performance, reliability, and cost-effectiveness of physical assets and manufacturing operations across defence and other sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Asset lifecycle engineering** - Maintenance strategy, scheduling, spares optimisation, and obsolescence management across the asset life.
- **Reliability engineering** - Data-driven reliability analysis and improvement for defence and industrial platforms.
