# Robosys Automation

> UK developer of AI-powered maritime autonomy and smart-shipping software for crewed, lean-crewed and uncrewed vessels.

## What they do

Robosys develops AI-powered maritime autonomy and smart-shipping software for crewed, lean-crewed and autonomous vessels. Its flagship VOYAGER AI software can transform a new or existing vessel into a fully autonomous uncrewed surface vessel capable of independent navigation, autonomous collision avoidance, obstacle and anti-grounding detection, and dynamic route optimisation. The company is UK-headquartered in Hamble, Southampton, with regional offices in the USA, Canada and India.

## Products and solutions

- **VOYAGER AI** - flagship maritime autonomy software suite delivering remote pilotage and advanced autonomous navigation, converting new or existing vessels into autonomous uncrewed surface vessels with collision avoidance, obstacle and anti-grounding detection and route optimisation.
- **VOYAGER AI Autonomous Navigation System (ANS)** - the autonomous navigation product within the VOYAGER AI line.
- **VOYAGER AI Simulator** - maritime simulation environment for the VOYAGER AI ANS, launched at Oceanology International 2026.
- **Subsea AI-supported navigation and vessel control** - a newer subsea product line announced following a contract for a fleet of advanced swimmer delivery vehicles.
