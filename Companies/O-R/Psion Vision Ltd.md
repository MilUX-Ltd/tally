# Psion Vision Ltd
> Hampshire-based software developer building smart eyewear and mixed-reality applications for defence and industrial users, sponsored by the UK Space Agency to develop the technology for the defence market.

## What they do
Psion Vision develops software for smart eyewear, providing hands-free displays, voice and head-movement control, and real-time data, video and voice communication for personnel working in demanding operational environments. The company moved into the smart eyewear market in 2018 after UK Space Agency sponsorship to develop the technology for defence applications, building on more than 30 years of prior work supporting the UK armed forces alongside the Ministry of Defence, Defence Equipment & Support (DE&S) and the Defence Science and Technology Laboratory (DSTL). It is based at the Solent Enterprise Zone, Daedalus Park, Lee-on-the-Solent, on the former RNAS Lee-on-Solent (HMS Daedalus) site.

## Products and solutions

- **Smart eyewear software** - hands-free, voice- and head-movement-controlled software for smart eyewear devices, designed to work with standard hard hats, bump caps and safety glasses, with a high-resolution micro-display and support for real-time video and voice communication with remote experts.
- **NOMAD (Naval Operations Mobile Augmented Display)** - developed with OSI Maritime Systems, a secure smart eyewear solution for Navy personnel to remotely access bridge and platform information systems and communicate via live video feed. Deployable on surface, sub-surface and soft boats, with NOMAD Support (engineering and logistics) and NOMAD Bridge (command and control) variants; described as fielded for the British Army and around 26 navies.
- **Structural integrity management software** - an earlier product line (from around 2015, developed over roughly 8 years with DSTL support) providing condition- and risk-based maintenance assessment for armoured fighting vehicles, using 3D modelling to assess structural integrity in place of fixed-schedule maintenance.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
