# Psion Vision Ltd
> UK Space Agency-sponsored technology company developing mixed reality solutions, including smart eyewear, for professional and defence applications.

## What they do
Psion Vision develops mixed reality technology and smart eyewear systems for professional users, with defence applications in situational awareness and field operations. The company is supported by the UK Space Agency and is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park. Its work sits at the intersection of wearable hardware and augmented reality software for demanding operational environments.
