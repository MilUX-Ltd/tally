# Qdot Technology Ltd

> Oxfordshire aerothermal technology company providing thermal management for defence and aerospace platforms.

## What they do

Qdot Technology develops next-generation aerothermal technologies, including binder-jet additively manufactured heat exchangers, heat sinks and cooling systems. Founded in 2020 as a spinout from the University of Oxford's Thermofluids Institute, it serves defence, aerospace, fusion energy and other high-power sectors. Its defence work supports thermal management for high-power radar, directed energy systems and next-generation propulsion across land, sea and air platforms.

## Products and solutions

- **Heat exchangers** - additively manufactured, high-effectiveness units for compact cooling.
- **Battery and fuel cell cooling** - thermal management for hybrid and electric propulsion.
- **Radar and directed energy cooling** - thermal solutions for high-power mission systems.
