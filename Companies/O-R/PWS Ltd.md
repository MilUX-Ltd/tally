# PWS Ltd

> Specialist sheet metal fabricator and machinist serving the aerospace, defence and associated industries.

## What they do

PWS manufactures sheet metal assemblies, structural fabrications and precision components using TIG welding and prismatic machining processes. The company works across a wide range of alloys including aluminium-lithium, Inconel, titanium and stainless steel, producing fine-limit, high-quality parts to customer specification.

## Products and solutions

- **Sheet Metal Assemblies** - Fine-limit assemblies in a range of aerospace and defence-grade alloys.
- **Structural Fabrications** - Large structural fabrications for aerospace and defence platforms.
- **Prismatic Machining** - Precision machined components and sub-assemblies to customer specification.
