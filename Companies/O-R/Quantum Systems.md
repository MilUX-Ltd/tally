# Quantum-Systems GmbH

> German deep-tech company developing and manufacturing electric VTOL fixed-wing uncrewed aerial systems for ISR and survey, with a strong defence focus.

## What they do

Quantum Systems is a European deep-tech company that develops, designs and manufactures small uncrewed aerial systems (UAS), specialising in electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) fixed-wing drones. It builds its core technologies in-house, spanning aircraft platforms, mission software and AI-driven aerial-intelligence analytics. Founded for commercial and survey applications, it has grown into a dual-use company serving commercial, security and military customers globally. It is headquartered in Gilching, Bavaria, near Munich.

## Products and solutions

- **Vector** - tactical eVTOL fixed-wing ISR drone sold only to governments; selected by the Australian Defence Force under LAND 129 Phase 4B.
- **Twister** - short-range, backpackable ISR sUAS designed to operate in GNSS-denied and electronically contested environments; selected as the Bundeswehr ALADIN successor.
- **Reliant** - long-range VTOL fixed-wing UAS with a heavy-fuel engine delivering over 10 hours of persistent ISR.
- **Trinity Pro** - eVTOL fixed-wing mapping and survey drone supporting RGB, oblique, multispectral and LiDAR payloads.
