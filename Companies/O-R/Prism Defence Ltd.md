# Prism Defence Ltd

> Specialist in ship-air integration, delivering software and instrumentation for embarked aviation management and Ship Helicopter Operating Limits trials.

## What they do

Prism Defence develops software solutions and flight and ship test instrumentation to support the full embarked aviation continuum, working with embarked aviation specialists alongside aeronautical and software engineers. The company conducts Ship Helicopter Operating Limits (SHOL) development through at-sea trials using its own instrumentation suite and is focused on the digital transformation of complex operations management aboard naval vessels.

## Products and solutions

- **Embarked aviation management software** - software supporting the full cycle of aviation operations at sea.
- **SHOL trial instrumentation** - flight and ship test instrumentation used to establish operating limits for ship-helicopter combinations.
