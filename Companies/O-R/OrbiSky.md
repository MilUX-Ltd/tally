# OrbiSky
> Builds secure, assured Edge AI and autonomy for defence, space and critical infrastructure, combining cybersecurity with runtime monitoring.
## What they do
OrbiSky develops Edge AI and autonomy solutions for defence, space and critical infrastructure environments, integrating cybersecurity with runtime monitoring to deliver assured autonomous systems. The company's public description now centres on in-orbit manufacturing mission assurance: turning process telemetry, provenance records, anomaly detection and acceptance metrics into structured evidence for hosted payload providers, orbital manufacturing teams, mission assurance teams and in-space assembly and manufacturing (ISAM) operators. A member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.

## Products and solutions

- **Mission evidence platform** - converts runtime telemetry, logs, control actions and calibration context into structured, reviewable evidence packs for in-orbit manufacturing operations.
- **Anomaly-aware verification** - flags missing, delayed, corrupted or off-nominal data with traceability to the affected mission claims or decisions.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus.
- **Other:** UK Space Agency-funded studies under the Sustainability and In-Space Assembly and Manufacturing (ISAM) and Unlocking Space programmes; member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.

## Recent news

- **2026-02-09** - Awarded UK Space Agency funding for the "SkyYield" study, designing a payload for processing ZBLAN fluoride glass in microgravity for optical fibre manufacturing. [source](https://www.gov.uk/government/news/new-studies-for-manufacturing-advanced-materials-in-orbit)
