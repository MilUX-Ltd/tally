# OrbiSky Ltd

> London-based space technology startup building assurance, verification and evidence infrastructure for autonomous in-orbit operations.

> Note: civil/commercial space company with dual-use potential. No confirmed defence contract, DASA funding or NATO DIANA participation found in public sources as of 2026-06-29. Included pending review of defence relevance.

## What they do

OrbiSky is a space technology company focused on secure autonomy, verification and assurance for space operations. It describes itself as building the evidence layer for autonomous in-orbit operations, turning mission and process telemetry into structured, auditable evidence packs for in-space manufacturing and in-orbit servicing, assembly and manufacturing (ISAM).

## Products and solutions

- **Mission evidence infrastructure** - converts process telemetry, logs and control actions into reviewable evidence packs.
- **Anomaly-aware verification** - flags missing, delayed or off-nominal data with traceability to affected claims and decisions.
- **SkyYield** - study to manufacture and process ZBLAN fluoride-glass optical fibre in microgravity, including process controls and verification.

## Recent news

- **2026-02-09** - Awarded £295,000 by the UK Space Agency for the SkyYield study into processing and verifying ZBLAN fluoride glass in microgravity. [source](https://www.gov.uk/government/news/new-studies-for-manufacturing-advanced-materials-in-orbit)
