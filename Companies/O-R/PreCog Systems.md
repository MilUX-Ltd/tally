# PreCog Systems

> UK data analytics company providing anomaly detection and movement-pattern intelligence for national security, law enforcement and border control.

## What they do

PreCog Systems is a London-based software company that analyses structured and unstructured data to detect anomalies and infer movement and behaviour patterns. Its platform collects signals passively from devices searching for Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connections, producing real-time and historical intelligence without connecting to those devices. It positions its work for national security, law enforcement, border control, airport security and counter-terrorism, and states its software is designed to comply with privacy measures such as GDPR.

## Products and solutions

- **PreCog analytics platform** - anomaly detection across datasets with passive data collection, real-time and historical intelligence reporting, and the ability to infer global movement patterns at a scale ranging from small deployments to nationwide systems.
