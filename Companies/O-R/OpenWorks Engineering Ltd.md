# OpenWorks Engineering Ltd

> Counter-drone and autonomous optical detection and tracking technology for defence and security.

## What they do

OpenWorks Engineering develops counter-drone capture systems and autonomous optical detection, tracking and classification solutions, describing itself as a provider of counter-UAS, air defence and surveillance technology. Its stated aim is to protect the territory and airspace of NATO countries and allies, and to protect high-profile assets, facilities, the public and VIPs from aerial threats. Products are deployable on static, mobile and unmanned platforms. The company states its specialist capability includes dynamic positioners, EO/IR optical units, sensor fusion including with radar, and a common interface enabling integration of third-party detectors, classifiers, interceptors and effectors.

## Products and solutions

- **SkyWall Patrol** - handheld pneumatic launcher that physically captures a hostile drone in a net-carrying smart projectile, using an onboard targeting system; projectiles are reusable and deployed operationally for VIP and event protection.
- **Vision Flex** - modular electro-optical (HD daylight) and cooled thermal (MWIR) camera system for static, vehicle-mounted or rapid tripod deployment, with built-in AI modules for classification, tracking and false-alarm reduction, and integration with third-party command and control systems.
- **Vision Pace** - stabilised, direct-drive optical tracking and targeting system for on-the-move use on vehicles and vessels, built through integration with third-party gimbal and radar sensors for cueing kinetic and non-kinetic effectors.
- **Vision Guard** - portable, rapidly deployable autonomous surveillance system combining daylight panoramic imaging with configurable active and passive sensors, including radar and acoustic options, for long-range detection, tracking and identification of small unmanned aircraft.
