# OpenWorks Engineering Ltd

> Counter-drone and autonomous optical detection and tracking technology for defence and security.

## What they do

OpenWorks Engineering develops drone capture systems and autonomous optical detection, tracking, and classification solutions. Their products are designed to protect high-profile assets, facilities, the public, and VIPs from aerial threats, and are deployable on static, mobile, and unmanned platforms.

## Products and solutions

- **SkyWall** - pneumatic counter-drone capture system that physically nets and retrieves hostile UAS.
- **Autonomous optical detection and tracking systems** - high-performance surveillance and classification solutions for static and mobile deployment.
