# Quantamatic

> High-volume precision turned components manufacturer serving aerospace, defence, and advanced engineering sectors.

## What they do

Quantamatic produces millions of precision turned parts per week using Escomatic and Star CNC machinery, covering diameters from 0.80 mm to 20 mm. The company operates to PPAP and FAIR standards with full traceability and supply chain compliance for regulated defence and aerospace applications.

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials, JOSCAR Registered
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
