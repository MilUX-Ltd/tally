# Quantamatic

> High-volume precision turned components manufacturer serving aerospace, defence, and advanced engineering sectors.

## What they do

Quantamatic produces millions of precision turned parts per week using Escomatic and Star CNC machinery, covering diameters from 0.80 mm to 20 mm. Established in 1989, the company describes itself as the fastest-growing UK producer of quality small turned parts, exporting to the EEC, USA and Far East. The company operates to PPAP and FAIR standards with full traceability and supply chain compliance for regulated defence and aerospace applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision turned components** - small diameter parts from 0.80 mm to 20 mm, produced in ferrous, non-ferrous and exotic materials from coil and bar stock, in round, square, hexagon, rectangle and custom profiles.
- **Escomatic and Star CNC machining** - fully automated Swiss coil-fed Escomatic and Japanese Star CNC lathes supporting front and back threading, drilling, milling, slotting, crimping, knurling and indexing.
- **Priority CAM sampling service** - rapid "Right First Time" sample production to speed up product development.
- **Quality-approved manufacturing** - production and inspection supported by computerised control systems, optical projectors, toolmakers' microscopes and digital measuring equipment calibrated by an independent accredited laboratory.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015 (BSI certificate FM13395), ISO/TS 16949 (PPAP) component documentation on request, Cyber Essentials, JOSCAR Registered
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
