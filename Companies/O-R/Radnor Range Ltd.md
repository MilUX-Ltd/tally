# Radnor Range Ltd

> Privately owned multi-disciplinary range complex in Wales specialising in testing and evaluation of weapons, ordnance, munitions, and explosives.

## What they do

Radnor Range operates a test and evaluation facility with a 50 kg HT 1 limit, supporting work on energetic materials, ballistics, current weapons systems, and armoured vehicle survivability. The company also undertakes manufacture and prototyping, staffed by personnel with military and defence industry backgrounds.
