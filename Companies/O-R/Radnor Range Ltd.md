# Radnor Range Ltd

> Privately owned multi-disciplinary range complex in Wales specialising in testing and evaluation of weapons, ordnance, munitions, and explosives.

## What they do

Radnor Range is a UK MOD-accredited independent test house for the test and evaluation of weapons, ordnance, munitions and explosives, operating from its own range complex near Presteigne. It conducts instrumented testing of armoured vehicles and armoured materials to international standards, Insensitive Munitions trials, UN Series testing, and weapons and vehicle-mounted weapon proofing, supported by its own explosive manufacturing, processing and pressing facilities. The company also supports drone and counter-drone flying activity, and is staffed by personnel with military and defence industry backgrounds.

## Products and solutions

- **Armoured vehicle testing** - instrumented survivability and ballistic testing of civilian and military armoured vehicles and structures.
- **Ballistic and weapons testing** - weapon systems and materials testing, including a Burst Fire Weapon System.
- **Explosives trials** - explosive composition trials, Insensitive Munitions trials, and UN Series testing for transport and hazard classification of explosives.
- **Blast trials** - blast trials for national infrastructure, including 3D digital image correlation blast modelling.
- **Munitions surveillance and disposal** - munitions surveillance, breakdown and remote sectioning of explosives, weapons and all-up munitions, and explosive disposal.
- **UAS trials** - drone and counter-drone flying trials.
- **Range training** - MOD-approved range for live fire and tactical training, and demolition training.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered
- **Accreditations:** UKAS 17025:2017 testing laboratory accreditation, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, Cyber Essentials Plus; UK MOD Defence Ordnance Safety Group approved independent test house; DSTL-approved range facility
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
