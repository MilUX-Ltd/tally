# Plextek Services Limited

> Advanced engineering and technology consultancy specialising in sensors, RF, communications, and defence electronics from concept through to manufacturing support.

## What they do

Plextek works with defence, security, and commercial clients to turn complex engineering challenges into products and solutions. Its multidisciplinary teams cover systems engineering, RF and antenna design, sensor development, biometrics and imagery, simulation, and space, providing capability from early concept through to prototype and manufacturing support.

## Products and solutions

- **RF and Antenna Systems** - Design and development of RF sensors, radar, and antenna systems for defence and security applications.
- **Sensor and Imaging Solutions** - Advanced sensing technologies including biometrics and imagery for security and ISR roles.
- **Engineering Consultancy** - Systems engineering, design, prototyping, and research services across aerospace, defence, and space sectors.
