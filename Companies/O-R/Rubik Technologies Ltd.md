# Rubik Technologies Ltd

> UK and Ireland systems integrator delivering digital transformation for manufacturing and engineering firms through Manufacturing Execution Systems.

## What they do

Rubik Technologies partners with Tulip MES to digitise production environments in aerospace, space and complex regulated manufacturing. Its work covers real-time process data capture, paper-free workflows, traceability for complex assemblies, root-cause analysis tooling and first-time-right quality improvement programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Tulip MES integration** - manufacturing execution system deployment for real-time process data capture, paper-free shop-floor workflows and traceability across complex, regulated assemblies.
- **Data platform** - a data backbone integrating production, design, supply chain and finance systems, supporting predictive analytics, AI-driven optimisation and dashboards tracking metrics such as OEE, work in progress, lead times and carbon footprint.
- **Root-cause analysis and quality tooling** - software supporting faster root-cause analysis, reduced rework and first-time-right quality, aimed at aerospace and space manufacturing.
