# Rubik Technologies Ltd

> UK and Ireland systems integrator delivering digital transformation for manufacturing and engineering firms through Manufacturing Execution Systems.

## What they do

Rubik Technologies partners with Tulip MES to digitise production environments in aerospace, space and complex regulated manufacturing. Its work covers real-time process data capture, paper-free workflows, traceability for complex assemblies, root-cause analysis tooling and first-time-right quality improvement programmes.
