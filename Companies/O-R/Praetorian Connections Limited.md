# Praetorian Connections Limited

> Open source intelligence provider specialising in in-language collection and in-depth analysis.

## What they do

Praetorian Connections is a Bristol-registered open source intelligence (OSINT) provider founded by former military intelligence officers. It states its teams combine specialist tools and evolving technologies with broad linguistic capability and deep cultural and regional expertise, including in hard-to-access areas, to collect, filter and analyse large volumes of data into verified, actionable intelligence. The company describes itself as operating a multi-source digital collection platform, with analysts collating, validating and fusing data from a broad spectrum of sources. Its work supports clients on market access research, due diligence and situational awareness, and it states it draws staff with backgrounds across government agencies, the commercial intelligence sector and academia.

## Products and solutions

- **OSINT research and investigations** - in-language, multi-source open source collection and analysis, delivered as structured data for direct ingestion into client systems or as deep-dive reporting.
- **Emerging technology reporting** - identification of new equipment and technologies within the geopolitical and security space, and how they relate to other systems.
- **Verified events reporting** - primary-source reporting from hard-to-access information environments using linguist analysts.
- **Geo-political indicators and warning** - multi-source monitoring for trends towards conflict or instability, including supply chain and manufacturing disruption analysis.
- **Supply chain and sanctions compliance investigations** - research into corporate structures and relationships to assess export control and sanctions risk.
- **Training** - instruction in OSINT methods and contextualised reporting.
