# Planet Labs UK 2 Ltd

> Earth observation company operating the world's largest constellation of imaging satellites, delivering daily global imagery.

## What they do

Planet designs, builds, and operates a large fleet of imaging satellites that capture daily imagery of the entire Earth's landmass. It provides mission-critical geospatial data and analytics to government, defence, and commercial customers through its online platform.

## Products and solutions

- **PlanetScope** - Daily 3–5 m resolution imagery of the entire Earth from a constellation of small satellites.
- **SkySat** - High-resolution tasking satellite providing sub-metre imagery and video for time-sensitive intelligence requirements.
- **Planet Platform** - Cloud-based geospatial analytics platform for accessing, processing, and analysing satellite data.
