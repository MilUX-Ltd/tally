# Resonate Testing Limited

> UKAS-accredited environmental, vibration, shock and fire testing laboratory serving aerospace, space and defence customers.

## What they do

Resonate Testing is a wholly owned subsidiary of The Nacelle Group, operating a purpose-built test facility in Newry, Northern Ireland, established in 2015. The company's core disciplines are vibration and shock testing, fire and flammability testing, and environmental and climatic testing, and it states it operates the only commercial facility on the island of Ireland able to perform combined temperature-and-vibration testing up to 54kN. It serves aerospace, space, automotive, energy, electrical and mechanical, medical technology, defence, humanitarian aid, and logistics and transport sectors, with public emphasis on propulsion-system fire testing for large civil aircraft and environmental, vibration and shock qualification for space launch vehicles and satellite hardware.

## Products and solutions

- **Vibration testing** - random, sine and combined vibration-and-temperature testing up to 54kN.
- **Shock testing** - Shock Response Spectrum methodology.
- **Environmental and climatic testing** - temperature, humidity and altitude testing.
- **Fire and flammability testing** - including propulsion-system fire testing for large civil aircraft; stated to be the only commercial aircraft-interiors and components fire test lab on the island of Ireland.
- **Thermal vacuum (TVAC) testing** - launched 2026, stated to be the largest and most capable TVAC chamber on the island of Ireland, for satellite and spacecraft qualification.
- **Battery testing** - stated to be the first battery testing facility on the island of Ireland, with an explosive and fireproof chamber and abuse test rigs for eBike, EV, consumer electronics and space applications.
- Testing performed to standards including RTCA DO-160, MIL-STD-810, DEF STAN 00-35 and EN 60068.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** UKAS accreditation to ISO/IEC 17025 (vibration, environmental, fire and flammability testing), ISO 9001:2015, Cyber Essentials.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, NI Space Ready Programme cohort; listed on the STFC-run UK Space Facilities directory.
