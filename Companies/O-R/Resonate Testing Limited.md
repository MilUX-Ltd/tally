# Resonate Testing Limited

> Environmental, vibration and fire testing laboratory serving aerospace and defence customers.

## What they do

Resonate Testing is a wholly owned subsidiary of The Nacelle Group, operating a 6,000 sq ft purpose-built test facility in Newry, Northern Ireland. The company focuses on three core services: vibration testing, environmental testing, and fire testing, with a fast-turnaround operating model.
