# Reliance Cyber Ltd

> Specialist managed cyber security services provider with over 20 years of experience protecting organisations across multiple sectors including defence.

## What they do

Reliance Cyber delivers managed cyber security services tailored to each client's risk profile, operating across sectors including defence, local government and the NHS. The firm builds solutions using in-house teams, its own tooling and technology partnerships, combining threat intelligence with bespoke security strategies in a partnership model with client organisations. It is certified under the NCSC Cyber Incident Response scheme and states it is one of a small number of managed security service providers partnered with Google Cloud Security.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed SASE** - secure access service edge delivered in partnership with Cato Networks, aimed at zero-trust deployment for mid-market clients.
- **Managed detection and threat intelligence** - managed security services incorporating Google Threat Intelligence.
- **Identity and email security** - identity security and email and cloud security services.
- **Incident response** - response services assured under the NCSC Cyber Incident Response scheme.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** NCSC Cyber Incident Response (CIR) scheme
