# Roke Manor Research Limited

> UK science and engineering innovator combining physical and digital systems for defence, national security, and Fortune 500 clients.

## What they do

Roke has over 60 years of heritage in defence and applies that depth across computer vision, AI, autonomous systems, command and control, ISR, maritime, space consultancy, and software and digital services. The company states it has more than 600 engineers combining deep knowledge of sensors, communications, cyber and AI, and describes its ISR work as building solutions "from sensor to decision" across a Sense-Decide-Effect continuum. It operates in defence, national security and geospatial intelligence for public and private clients, and is part of Chemring Group. Note: this entry and the separate "Roke" page describe the same legal entity, Roke Manor Research Limited.

## Products and solutions

- **CORTEXA GUARDIAN** - modular, mast-mounted counter-drone system combining radar and PTZ sensors, underpinned by Roke's RapidEO automated threat detection software for a fused air picture.
- **Dragonfly** - passive, vision-based system enabling uncrewed aircraft to land on moving platforms such as ships at sea without dependency on GPS or RF beacons, designed for contested environments.
- **Autonomous shipping navigation** - autonomous navigation and planning technology for uncrewed vessels, with trials demonstrating extended continuous operation while maintaining COLREGS compliance.
- **AI inspection** - AI-powered inspection systems for aircraft and other platforms.
- **Project ZODIAC** - British Army contract to mature Land ISTAR capability.
- **Project STORM** - research and development lead role at the UK Missile Defence Centre's Science and Technology Oriented Research and development in Missile programme, covering counter-proliferation, deterrence and missile defence including hypersonic threats.
