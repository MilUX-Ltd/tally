# Roke Manor Research Limited

> UK science and engineering innovator combining physical and digital systems for defence, national security, and Fortune 500 clients.

## What they do

Roke has over 60 years of heritage in defence and applies that depth across computer vision, AI, autonomous systems, C2, counter-terrorism, ISR, maritime, space consultancy, and software and digital services. Its work spans prototype development through to capability integration, serving UK government and commercial customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Autonomous systems** - computer vision and control systems including autonomous drone landing at sea.
- **AI inspection** - AI-powered inspection systems for aircraft and other platforms.
- **C2 services** - command and control capability for defence and national security programmes.
- **Counter-terrorism and intelligence** - data and analytical capabilities for security agencies.
- **Simulation and modelling** - synthetic environments and modelling tools.
