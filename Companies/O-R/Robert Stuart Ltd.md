# Robert Stuart Ltd

> Metal finishing and surface treatment manufacturer serving the aerospace and defence supply chain from a single site in Harlow, Essex.

## What they do

Founded in 1945 and still in the same family ownership, Robert Stuart has specialised in aerospace work since its inception, with early customers including De Havilland, Hawker Siddeley, Handley Page, Rolls-Royce, British Aircraft Corporation and Westland Helicopters. The company states it offers the largest range of metal finishing services on a single site in the UK, and has carried its aerospace-derived skills into the satellite, motorsport and medical industries. It holds approvals to work for prime aerospace and defence manufacturers.

## Products and solutions

- **Anodising** - chromic, sulphuric, hard, colour-dyed and titanium anodising.
- **Chemical conversion coatings** - including Alocrom, Alodine 1200S and Iridite processes, plus chromating of magnesium.
- **Plating** - hard chromium, nickel and zinc-nickel plating.
- **Passivation and phosphate conversion coating** - manganese and zinc phosphate processes.
- **Painting and paint systems** - epoxy, polyurethane and water-based primers and top coats, bond primers and bespoke finishes.
- **Dry film lubricants** - cetyl alcohol treatments.
- **NDT and inspection** - conductivity, hardness and magnetic flaw testing, penetrant flaw detection, salt spray and humidity testing, and X-ray fluorescence.
- **Specialist masking** - selective masking processes for treatment of specific component areas.
