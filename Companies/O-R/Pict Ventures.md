# Pict Ventures

> Technology research and development company building applied machine-learning solutions to real-world problems.

## What they do

Pict Ventures is a research and technology company based in Edinburgh, working across the UK and internationally to solve complex, real-world problems using applied machine learning. The company states its work spans a multi-domain machine-learning software platform, alongside a design methodology developed for security and defence applications that combines machine learning, additive manufacturing and single-board computing.

## Products and solutions

- **Deep Truth** - A multi-domain machine-learning software platform described by the company as sitting at the heart of its products.
- **Defence Plasticity** - A design methodology developed for security and defence use, combining machine learning, additive manufacturing and single-board computer hardware.
