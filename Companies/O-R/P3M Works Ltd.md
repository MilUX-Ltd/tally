# P3M Works Ltd

> Consultancy specialising in digital transformation and cyber security for public and private sector organisations.

## What they do

P3M Works is a Cheltenham-based cyber resilience consultancy delivering complex digital transformation and cyber security programmes for defence, government, and critical national infrastructure clients, as well as private sector and SME organisations. It covers defence, space, and information technology consultancy services, with capabilities declared to ADS Group spanning cyber security and systems integrity, data analysis, digital and computer forensics, software optimisation, and TEMPEST. The company also supports major project and programme delivery (assurance, governance, and delivery recovery) and helps clients select and operationalise third-party cyber security technology.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber resilience services** - ongoing, practical cyber security support from initial assessment through to embedded long-term partnership, including penetration testing and awareness campaigns.
- **Major projects** - structured programme and project delivery, assurance, and governance support for defence, government, and critical national infrastructure clients, including delivery recovery on troubled programmes and alignment to NIST CSF, Secure by Design, and MOD JSP 440/453.
- **Technology partners** - selection, procurement, deployment, and ongoing optimisation of third-party cyber security platforms across identity and access, endpoint protection, and resilience and assurance tooling, working with vendors including Akamai, CyberArk, and Sophos.
- **Bug bounty and adversarial testing** - delivered an in-person bug bounty capability for a defence sector client, establishing a new adversarial testing methodology.
