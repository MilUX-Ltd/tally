# RHE Southern Limited

> Specialist supplier of aircraft jacks and aviation lifting equipment, serving governments, airlines, MROs and operators worldwide.

## What they do

RHE Southern supplies, tests, services and repairs aircraft jacks and associated lifting equipment, with over 30 years of experience in the sector. Customers include government operators, airlines, MROs, simulation providers and military operators; the product range covers jacks, slings, chains, hydraulic lifting equipment, fall arrest systems, staging, platforms and a range of aircraft-specific support stands.
