# RHE Southern Limited

> Specialist supplier of aircraft jacks and aviation lifting equipment, serving governments, airlines, MROs and operators worldwide.

## What they do

RHE Southern supplies, tests, services and repairs aircraft jacks and associated lifting equipment, with over 30 years of experience in the sector. Customers include government operators, airlines, MROs, simulation providers and military operators; the product range covers jacks, slings, chains, hydraulic lifting equipment, fall arrest systems, staging, platforms and a range of aircraft-specific support stands. The company states its equipment is approved by operators and manufacturers including Boeing, Thales, Marshall Aerospace, ST Aerospace, TUI, easyJet, Ryanair and West Atlantic.

## Products and solutions

- **Flyaway Aircraft Jack Kit** - portable jacking kit for wheel-change operations on Boeing 737-100 to -800 series and Airbus aircraft.
- **Oleo Change Trolley** - purpose-built trolley for oleo servicing.
- **Cowl Removal Trolley** - support equipment for engine cowl removal.
- **Shoring and stress jacks** - built to Structural Repair Manual dimensions for specific aircraft types.
