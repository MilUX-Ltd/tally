# Ray Systems Ltd
> UK startup developing bio-inspired, low-signature autonomous underwater vehicles for defence and dual-use persistent monitoring and secure operations.

## What they do
Ray Systems designs and builds autonomous underwater vehicles (UUVs) inspired by biological movement principles, targeting low-signature profiles for defence and dual-use applications. The company positions itself as a "systems in the water" business, focused on persistent exploration, real-time monitoring, and secure operations in denied environments. In December 2025 it announced a development agreement with US-listed Beam Global to design and deliver custom battery systems for its platforms.

## Products and solutions
- **RayDrive** - autonomous underwater vehicle; Mk2 completed ground-up redesign in 86 days and shipped for customer testing.
