# Phoenix Contact Ltd

> UK subsidiary of a global OEM of low-voltage electrical and automation components for defence and industrial applications.

## What they do

Phoenix Contact Ltd is the UK subsidiary of Phoenix Contact GmbH & Co. KG, established in 1985 to service the UK market and now offering engineering services and support alongside sales and marketing. The UK operation is based in Telford, Shropshire. The wider group manufactures low-voltage electrical components, connectivity products, and industrial automation systems used in defence and other sectors, with a product range supporting remote monitoring, diagnostics, and cloud-connected applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Terminal blocks and connectors** - Circular, data, energy, PV, and heavy-duty industrial connectors, plus PCB connectors and cabling.
- **Industrial Ethernet switches** - FL SWITCH product families, including switches meeting IEC 61850-3 and IEEE 1613 for critical infrastructure, and switches certified to IEC 62443-4-2 for industrial network security.
- **PLCnext platform** - Automation and edge computing platform.
- **Power supplies, surge protection and UPS** - Including relays and industrial security components.
