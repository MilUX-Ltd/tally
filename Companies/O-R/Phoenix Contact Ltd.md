# Phoenix Contact Ltd

> UK subsidiary of a global OEM of low-voltage electrical and automation components for defence and industrial applications.

## What they do

Phoenix Contact manufactures low-voltage electrical components, connectivity products, and industrial automation systems used in defence and other sectors. The company's product range supports remote monitoring, diagnostics, and cloud-connected applications, addressing the digitalisation requirements of defence platforms and infrastructure.
