# OPSWAT Ltd

> Cybersecurity platform protecting critical infrastructure through malware prevention and secure data transfer.

## What they do

OPSWAT provides threat prevention and process creation tools focused on eliminating malware and zero-day attacks at the point of data transfer and device access. Its products are deployed to protect critical infrastructure environments across government and defence. The UK entity, OPSWAT Ltd, is registered in London; the global company was founded in 2002.

## Products and solutions

- **MetaDefender** - multi-scanning across multiple anti-malware engines, Deep CDR (content disarm and reconstruction), sandbox and dynamic analysis, and vulnerability and SBOM detection.
- **MetaDefender Kiosk** - scans and sanitises removable media at the perimeter, including the K3001 model.
- **MetaDefender Drive** - verifies device and endpoint security before connection, used in supply chain security contexts.
- **MetaDefender NetWall** - data diodes and secure gateways for one-way OT data transfer across trust boundaries.
- **MetaDefender Aether** - combines sandboxing, machine-learning scoring and threat hunting for perimeter security.
- **Cross-Domain Solutions** - combines data diodes with threat prevention for defence, intelligence and critical infrastructure customers.
- **MetaAccess** - device compliance and network access control for secure endpoint management.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO/IEC 27001:2013; Common Criteria EAL2+ (MetaDefender Core and Kiosk, certified by the Canadian Centre for Cyber Security); FIPS 140-3 validated cryptographic modules.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
