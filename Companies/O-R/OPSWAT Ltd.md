# OPSWAT Ltd

> Cybersecurity platform protecting critical infrastructure through malware prevention and secure data transfer.

## What they do

OPSWAT provides threat prevention and process creation tools focused on eliminating malware and zero-day attacks at the point of data transfer and device access. Their products are deployed to protect critical infrastructure environments across government and defence.

## Products and solutions

- **MetaDefender** - multi-scanning and deep content disarm and reconstruction (CDR) platform for secure file transfer.
- **MetaAccess** - device compliance and network access control for secure endpoint management.
