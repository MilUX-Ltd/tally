# Renaissance Strategic Advisors Ltd

> Strategy, market assessment, and M&A due diligence consultancy focused on aerospace, defence, and government services.

## What they do

Renaissance Strategic Advisors supports premier firms in the aerospace, defence, and government services sector on their most complex strategic issues, including market entry, competitive positioning, and mergers and acquisitions due diligence. The firm draws on a multidisciplinary team with backgrounds spanning military service, defence primes, investment banking, equity research, and government policy. The UK entity is based in London.
