# Renaissance Strategic Advisors Ltd

> Strategy, market assessment, and M&A due diligence consultancy focused on aerospace, defence, and government services.

## What they do

Renaissance Strategic Advisors (RSA) advises corporate and investor clients across aerospace, defence, space, intelligence and government services on growth strategy and mergers and acquisitions. The firm states more than 20 years and 1,000-plus engagements of sector experience, coupling strategy work with M&A and due diligence, and tracks emerging technology areas including AI and machine learning, autonomy and robotics, hypersonics, directed energy and quantum computing. The firm draws on a multidisciplinary team with backgrounds spanning military service, defence primes, investment banking, equity research, and government policy. The UK entity is based in London.

## Products and solutions

- **Strategy consulting** - organic and inorganic growth and value-creation strategy for aerospace, defence and government-services clients.
- **M&A advisory** - full lifecycle support from strategy through due diligence, financial advisory and post-merger integration.
- **Investment and commercial due diligence** - cites more than 350 due diligence assignments and 1,000-plus diligence projects over 20 years.
- **Market assessment and sector analytics** - covering commercial aerospace and general aviation, space, federal IT and government services, defence systems and technology, and disruptive technologies.
