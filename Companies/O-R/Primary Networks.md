# Primary Networks

> Designer and manufacturer of rugged computing and power control products for naval and marine applications.

## What they do

Primary Networks (trading name of Primary Network Products (UK) Ltd, incorporated 2005) designs, manufactures, tests and installs specialised computing and power control products for the Royal Navy and the marine shipping sector. Its trading history extends back further through an ICL "Network Products" division, later part of Stordata plc, before a management buyout in 2001. Products are built to operate in harsh environments and meet defence standards including DEF STAN, MIL-STD and Tempest, with units produced to customers' exact requirements and designed for longevity in the field. Design and manufacture take place in the UK.

## Products and solutions

- **Louvar ZX series rugged naval computers** - a family of rugged computers (including the ZX3, ZX3-D, ZX4i, ZX34, ZX12, ZX5, ZX5-A, ZX6, ZX7 and ZX8 variants) built for naval platforms, EMC tested to EN55022:2010, with DEF STAN, MIL-STD and Tempest variants available for systems handling NATO Secret information; stackable or rackmount, with a fibre upgrade option.
- **G-WIN rugged panel PCs** - a range of rugged panel PCs from 8.4 to 19 inches, rated IP65/IP67 and tested to MIL-STD-810, IEC 60068-2-27 and EN50155.
- **5 KVA power tray with isolation (PT2-N series)** - a DEF STAN compliant power tray providing 115V to 230V isolation for shipboard use.
- **Comms rack filter tray (CR2-N series)** - a filter tray compliant with DEF STAN 59-411 for communications racks.
- **Custom naval server racks** - shock-tested server racks built to customer specification.
- **Secure KVM switches and cabling** - secure KVM switching and cabling integration, including Common Criteria Protection Profile 3.0 compliant units and low-smoke zero-halogen (LSZH) cabling.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Lloyd's Register approval, Investors in People (held for over nine years). Products tested to DEF STAN 59-41 and 59-411, MIL-STD-461 and MIL-STD-810, and Tempest requirements.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Society of Maritime Industries, Institute of Directors, London Chamber of Commerce.
