# Primary Networks

> Designer and manufacturer of rugged computing and power control products for naval and marine applications.

## What they do

Primary Networks designs, manufactures, tests, and installs specialised computing and power control products for the Royal Navy and marine shipping sector. Products are built to operate in harsh environments and meet defence standards including DefStan, MIL-STD, and Tempest, with all units produced to customers' exact requirements and designed for longevity in the field.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged computing systems** - bespoke computers built to shock, vibration, and EMC standards for naval platforms.
- **Power control products** - custom power control units for maritime defence and commercial shipping.
