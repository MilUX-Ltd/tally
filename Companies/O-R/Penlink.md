# Penlink

> Internet intelligence investigation platform providing digital intelligence capabilities for law enforcement, national security, and defence.

## What they do

Penlink delivers an end-to-end digital investigations and threat monitoring platform covering web discovery, visual link analysis, dynamic reporting, real-time alerting, and explainable AI. Their tools are used for digital evidence gathering and intelligence analysis in law enforcement and national security contexts.

## Products and solutions

- **Internet Intelligence Platform** - web discovery, link analysis, and dynamic reporting for digital investigations.
- **Real-Time Alerting** - monitoring and alerting against defined threat indicators.
- **Digital Evidence Capabilities** - tools for gathering and presenting digital evidence to evidential standards.
