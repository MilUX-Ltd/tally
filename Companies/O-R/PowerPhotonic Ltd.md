# PowerPhotonic Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of precision micro-optics for high-power and high-accuracy laser applications.

## What they do

PowerPhotonic, founded in 2004 and based in Dalgety Bay, Scotland, designs and manufactures precision freeform micro-optics using a proprietary laser micro-machining process. Its components, made from UV-fused silica, are produced with sub-micron surface accuracy and are stated to be highly efficient and damage-tolerant, able to operate across the UV, visible and near-infrared bands. The company states its optics serve high-power laser material processing, directed energy, laser communications and quantum technology applications, alongside telecommunications and ophthalmology uses. It also offers rapid prototyping and custom optics design services for OEMs and research institutions.

## Products and solutions

- **Micro-lens arrays** - standard and custom arrays for precision optical systems.
- **Beam shapers** - freeform and structured beam-shaping optics in UV-fused silica for high-power laser applications.
- **Collimators and phase correctors** - optical components for laser beam conditioning.
- **Refractive diffusers** - custom diffusers for high-power laser systems.
- **LightForge** - rapid micro-optics fabrication and prototyping service.
