# PowerPhotonic Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of precision micro-optics for high-power and high-accuracy laser applications.

## What they do

PowerPhotonic uses a proprietary wafer-scale laser machining process to manufacture precision micro-optics in UV-fused silica, including micro-lens arrays, beam shapers and freeform surfaces. Their optics are suited to demanding, high-accuracy applications and high-power laser handling.

## Products and solutions

- **Micro-lens arrays** - standard and custom arrays for precision optical systems.
- **Beam shapers** - freeform and structured beam-shaping optics in UV-fused silica.
