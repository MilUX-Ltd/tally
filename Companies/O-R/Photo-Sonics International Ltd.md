# Photo-Sonics International Ltd

> Flight test instrumentation specialist providing image acquisition, recording and data processing equipment to European flight test engineers.

## What they do

Photo-Sonics International Ltd (PSIL) has supported flight test engineers across Europe since 1973, supplying and supporting equipment for acquisition, recording and processing of images and data. Based in Thame, Oxfordshire, the company holds niche expertise in flight test instrumentation and specialised applications engineering tailored to each customer's requirements.
