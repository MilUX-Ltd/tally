# Photo-Sonics International Ltd

> Flight test instrumentation specialist providing image acquisition, recording and data processing equipment to European flight test engineers.

## What they do

Photo-Sonics International Ltd (PSIL) has supported flight test engineers across Europe since 1973, supplying and supporting equipment for acquisition, recording and processing of images and data. Based in Thame, Oxfordshire, the company holds niche expertise in flight test instrumentation and specialised applications engineering tailored to each customer's requirements. The company's history runs from high-speed and HUD cameras for military aircraft through to its current focus on flight test and aerospace imaging systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Flight test instrumentation** - Image and data acquisition, recording and processing equipment for flight test engineers.
- **Optical tracking systems** - Including the ARTIS/MITS Fly-Out Optical Tracking System, delivered to White Sands Missile Range.
- **Airborne video and telemetry systems** - Airborne video systems, ground and short-range wireless telemetry, airborne Ethernet switches and routers.
- **Space launch instrumentation** - Instrumentation supporting launch vehicle test and data acquisition, via a long-standing relationship with Safran Data Systems.
- **Motion tracking and data analysis software** - TrackImage and TrackReport, resold in the UK under a partnership with ORME Signals & Images.
