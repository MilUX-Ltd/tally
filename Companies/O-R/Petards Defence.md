# Petards Defence

> UK defence engineering business supplying in-service support contracts and proprietary products to the MOD and international customers.

## What they do

Petards Defence, part of Petards Group Plc and founded in 1952, holds in-service support contracts (ISSCs) with the UK MOD covering C-IED training kits, aircraft counter measure dispensing systems, secure and emergency radio systems, and electronic warfare training aids. The company also supplies its own products internationally, including arming assurance devices for aircraft payloads, AFV engine management systems, and a range of test and diagnostic equipment.

## Products and solutions

- **C-IED training kits** - Systems for counter-improvised explosive device training.
- **Counter Measure Dispensing Systems (CMDS)** - Aircraft self-protection dispensing systems.
- **Secure and emergency radio systems** - Communications equipment for defence platforms.
- **Electronic warfare training aids** - EW simulation and training devices.
- **Arming assurance devices** - Payload arming assurance systems for aircraft.
- **AFV engine management systems** - Engine monitoring and management for armoured fighting vehicles.
