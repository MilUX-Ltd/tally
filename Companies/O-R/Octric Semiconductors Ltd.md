# Octric Semiconductors Ltd

> UK manufacturer of compound semiconductors for defence and telecommunications applications, including critical military platforms.

## What they do

Octric develops and manufactures compound semiconductor products used in defence and telecommunications, from a site that has operated continuously since 1991 under successive owners. The company is described as the only secure UK facility with the skills and capability to manufacture semiconductors used in critical military platforms, including the Typhoon fighter jet. The UK Ministry of Defence acquired the Newton Aycliffe facility in September 2024 to secure the defence semiconductor supply chain, and has committed to a 10-year investment plan for the site.

## Products and solutions

- **Gallium arsenide (GaAs) semiconductors** - established production for RF and microwave components used in radar and other high-frequency, high-power applications.
- **Gallium nitride (GaN) and indium phosphide (InP)** - platforms the site states it is expanding production into, alongside its established GaAs line.
