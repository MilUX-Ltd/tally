# Octric Semiconductors Ltd

> UK manufacturer of compound semiconductors for defence and telecommunications applications, including critical military platforms.

## What they do

Octric develops and manufactures compound semiconductor products used in defence and telecommunications. The company is described as the only secure UK facility with the skills and capability to manufacture semiconductors used in critical military platforms, including the Typhoon fighter jet.
