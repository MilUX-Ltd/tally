# Riskaware Ltd

> Bristol-based decision support software company turning scientific insight into actionable intelligence for defence, security and environmental risk challenges.

## What they do

Riskaware develops methods and tools that convert scientific understanding into practical decision support capability for government, defence and industry, work it describes as turning "scientific insight into decision advantage". Its product lines span CBRN and hazardous-material situational awareness, cyber-attack modelling, counter-drone detection, biosurveillance, and marine environmental monitoring. In August 2025 Riskaware was acquired by Evallic, a European defence technology group, joining an integrated CBRNe platform alongside Bruhn Newtech and Argon Electronics.

## Products and solutions

- **UrbanAware** - CBRN and hazardous-material situational awareness and decision support platform for emergency response and training.
- **CyberAware Predict** - processes intrusion-detection and threat-intelligence feeds to model and predict adversary behaviour in real time.
- **CyberAware Resilience** - models the impact of cyber-attacks on missions and network assets using CVSS-based analytics.
- **SpaceAware** - risk modelling and decision support for degraded space assets.
- **BioAware** - biosurveillance data fusion and forecasting for disease outbreak detection.
- **MarineAware** - cloud-based oil spill response platform combining satellite oil detection, AIS vessel tracking and dispersion modelling.
- **EDITTS** - counter-small-drone prototype using machine learning (neural networks and particle filters) for detection and classification, developed under a DASA "Countering Small Drones" call.
