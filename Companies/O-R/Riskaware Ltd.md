# Riskaware Ltd

> Bristol-based incident modelling and situational awareness software company, delivering decision support solutions to government, defence and commercial organisations for over 20 years.

## What they do

Riskaware develops scalable incident modelling platform solutions that provide situational awareness and critical decision support across environmental, human and security challenges. The company works with global government departments and science-led R&D partners, offering actionable intelligence on complex, difficult-domain problems.

## Products and solutions

- **Incident Modelling Platform** - Scalable platform providing situational awareness and decision support for environmental, security and human risk scenarios.
