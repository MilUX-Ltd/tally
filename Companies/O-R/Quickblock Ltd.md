# Quickblock Ltd

> Scottish manufacturer of the QUICKBLOCK® rapid-assembly building system, with a dedicated defence variant providing ballistic and blast protection for force protection and operational infrastructure.

## What they do

Quickblock produces a flat-pack, rapid-assembly building system requiring no skilled labour or tools, enabling structures to be built within minutes. The system serves operational infrastructure and force protection engineering applications, with a QUICKBLOCK® Defence variant that offers ballistic and blast protection through in-fill materials.

## Products and solutions

- **QUICKBLOCK®** - Standard rapid-assembly modular building block; hollow structural form, flat-pack delivery.
- **QUICKBLOCK® Defence** - Military-grade variant with ballistic and blast protection capability for force protection and operational infrastructure.
