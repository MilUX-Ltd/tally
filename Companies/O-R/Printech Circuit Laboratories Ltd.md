# Printech Circuit Laboratories Ltd

> Chelmsford manufacturer of specialist printed circuits, antennas and RF/microwave circuitry for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Printech Circuit Laboratories designs, manufactures and tests specialist circuitry and circuit-based components. Its work includes antennas, flexible and rigid hybrid boards, PTFE softboard and metal-backed circuitry for aerospace, defence, medical, motorsport and RF and microwave communication applications. The company has more than 40 years of operation.

## Products and solutions

- **Antennas** - high-quality antenna manufacture.
- **Specialist PCBs** - flexible, rigid hybrid, PTFE softboard and metal-backed circuitry.
- **RF and microwave circuitry** - bespoke premium circuit components.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015, UL certified, Cyber Essentials certified, JOSCAR+ registered.
