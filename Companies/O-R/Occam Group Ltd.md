# Occam Group Ltd
> Integrated assurance consultancy delivering safety, security and human factors services across high-hazard and high-security defence environments.
## What they do
Occam Group provides turnkey assurance services covering safety, environmental management, human factors, safety-critical systems, security, fire safety and hazardous materials. The firm works across programme lifecycle in high-hazard, high-security environments including radiological, explosive and CBRN domains.
