# Occam Group Ltd
> Integrated assurance consultancy delivering safety, security and human factors services across high-hazard and high-security defence environments.
## What they do
Occam Group provides turnkey assurance services covering safety, environmental management, human factors, safety-critical systems, security, fire safety and hazardous materials. The firm works across the programme lifecycle in high-hazard, high-security environments including radiological, explosive and CBRN domains, serving maritime, land, air, infrastructure, weapons and C4ISTAR markets in defence, alongside renewables and space outside defence. Occam Group states it has launched a dedicated security practice alongside its established safety and human factors assurance work.
## Products and solutions
- **Safety and environmental assurance** - safety management, environmental management, materials assurance and independent technical evaluation across the programme lifecycle.
- **Human factors** - insight and assurance of complex sociotechnical systems and human-based safety claims, accredited as a CIEHF Registered Consultancy.
- **Security practice** - governance, risk and compliance; security operations covering threat intelligence, incident response planning and vulnerability management; and assurance and testing covering security audits, penetration testing and red/purple teaming.
- **Fire engineering** - fire safety assurance services.
## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR (fully registered).
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2015, Cyber Essentials Plus, CIEHF Registered Consultancy.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Affiliate Organisation of the Safety and Reliability Society (SaRS); Armed Forces Covenant signatory (Silver Defence Employer Recognition Scheme).
