# OSL Technology

> Drone detection and counter-drone systems integrator providing situational intelligence solutions for defence, government and security customers.

## What they do

Founded in 2010, OSL Technology supplies and integrates drone detection and counter-drone systems designed to create safer spaces in complex three-dimensional environments. The company delivers complete solutions covering perception, protection and prevention against drone and other aerial threats, and provides systems integration, training and ongoing technical support.

## Products and solutions

- **Drone detection systems** - sensor and software solutions for detecting and tracking UAS in contested airspace.
- **Counter-drone systems** - integrated counter-UAS solutions for defence and security applications.
- **Situational intelligence platform** - command and control software providing real-time visibility across 3D operational environments.
