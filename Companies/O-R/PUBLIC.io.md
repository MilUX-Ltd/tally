# PUBLIC.io
> UK GovTech firm delivering defence innovation training, accelerator programmes and procurement advisory for central government and MOD.

## What they do
PUBLIC works with central government and defence to design digital services and accelerate adoption of emerging technology. In defence, it has run the Percy Hobart Fellowship since 2020, a 12-week innovation programme for over 110 Royal Navy, Royal Air Force and US Navy personnel. It also delivers the DIU-funded "Innovation in Defence" pan-defence training programmes between January and June each year, and partnered with AWS on its first UK defence accelerator.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
- **Partnerships:** Defence Innovation Unit (DIU) delivery partner; Percy Hobart Fellowship delivery partner.
