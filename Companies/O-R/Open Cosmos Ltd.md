# Open Cosmos Ltd

> End-to-end space mission provider offering bespoke mission services, shared constellation infrastructure, and downstream data analytics.

## What they do

Open Cosmos delivers space missions across three service lines: Open Orbit (bespoke mission services), Open Constellation (shared standard infrastructure), and Data Cosmos (downstream data analytics). The company is based at the Harwell Campus in Oxfordshire, a principal UK space cluster.

## Products and solutions

- **Open Orbit** - Bespoke end-to-end space mission design and delivery.
- **Open Constellation** - Mutualised satellite infrastructure for shared access.
- **Data Cosmos** - Earth observation and space-derived data analytics services, combining satellite, drone and sensor data with vector layers on open geospatial standards, with data visualisation tools and AI-based imagery analytics delivered through third-party analytic partners.
