# Onebrief

> Military planning software platform designed to accelerate staff decision cycles and enable collaboration across classification levels.

## What they do

Onebrief is a software platform built for military staff operations, live on NIPR, SIPR, and JWICS networks. It is designed to drive faster decision cycles and enable what the company describes as superhuman military staffs through improved planning and collaboration tooling.
