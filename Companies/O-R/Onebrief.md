# Onebrief

> Military planning software platform designed to accelerate staff decision cycles and enable collaboration across classification levels.

## What they do

Onebrief is a software platform built for military staff operations, live on NIPR, SIPR, JWICS, and coalition networks, and accredited to Impact Level 5 (IL5). It is designed to drive faster decision cycles and enable what the company describes as superhuman military staffs through improved planning and collaboration tooling. The company states it is the planning layer within the US Army's Next Generation Command and Control (NGC2) prototype and has been used in the 4th Infantry Division's IVY STING experimentation series.

## Products and solutions

- **Collaborative planning** - real-time, cross-echelon plan editing and synchronisation, with course-of-action development support.
- **AI-assisted staff work** - agentic AI, built on Anthropic's Claude, trained on open-source material, military doctrine, and historical orders and staff outputs, supporting contextual planning, automated intelligence processing, multi-domain coordination, and course-of-action drafting.
- **Operational data visualisation** - chart-building and relationship visualisation of the operational picture for staff planning.
