# Racal Acoustics

> Specialist manufacturer of military hearing protection and acoustic communications equipment, serving armed forces in over 80 countries.

## What they do

Racal Acoustics designs and manufactures hearing protection and acoustic solutions for military, paramilitary, first responder and aviation markets across land, sea and air use cases. Products are co-developed with frontline personnel to meet operational communications and survivability requirements. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of INVISIO Group.

## Products and solutions

- **RA5001 RAPTOR** - over-the-ear headset combining passive and active noise reduction for speech intelligibility, designed for helmet compatibility.
- **RA4000 Magna** - digital headset providing hearing protection, communications intelligibility and situational awareness.
- **RA5100** - tactical communication headset for hybrid vehicle and dismounted missions.
- **RA315** - communication headset for main battle tanks, armoured fighting vehicles and other tracked high-noise vehicles.
- **Vehicle intercom-compatible headsets** - variants supporting live and switched microphone and single or dual push-to-talk, compatible with common vehicle intercom and radio systems.
