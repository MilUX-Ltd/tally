# Racal Acoustics

> Specialist manufacturer of military hearing protection and acoustic communications equipment, serving armed forces in over 80 countries.

## What they do

Racal Acoustics designs and manufactures hearing protection and acoustic solutions for military and defence applications. Products are co-developed with frontline personnel to meet operational communications and survivability requirements.
