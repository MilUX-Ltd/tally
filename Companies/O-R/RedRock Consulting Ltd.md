# RedRock Consulting Ltd

> Technology and digital professional services consultancy serving public sector and defence clients.

## What they do

RedRock Consulting delivers strategy, technology, and digital transformation services to government, retail, technology and financial services clients across the public, private and third sectors. Founded in 2005 and based in Bristol, the firm works across eight capability lines: agile and programme delivery, cloud and infrastructure services, cyber security and organisational resilience, data, AI and analytics, software development, strategy and advisory, transformation and change, and user-centred design and research. The firm holds JOSCAR registration and Cyber Essentials Plus certification.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber security and organisational resilience** - includes penetration testing, security operations centre monitoring, incident response, identity and access management, and vulnerability management, delivered as consulting services.
- **Data, AI and analytics** - data analytics, data engineering and visualisation services, alongside applied machine learning, natural language processing and generative AI work.
- **Identity and access management** - a named case study describes an IdAM solution covering role-based access control, privileged access management and public key infrastructure for a central government agency.
