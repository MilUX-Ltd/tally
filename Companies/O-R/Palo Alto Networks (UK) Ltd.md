# Palo Alto Networks (UK) Ltd

> Global cybersecurity company, US-headquartered, with a UK entity serving defence and government customers.

## What they do

Palo Alto Networks delivers cybersecurity platforms combining AI, analytics, and automation to protect digital infrastructure at scale. The company serves defence and government organisations as part of a broader enterprise and public-sector portfolio. Its UK entity is assured by the National Cyber Security Centre for Cyber Incident Response (CIR) at both Standard and Enhanced levels, and has extended its Prisma SASE and Cortex XSIAM offerings to UK public-sector customers including the Ministry of Justice.

## Products and solutions

- **Strata** - network security platform covering firewalls and secure network infrastructure.
- **Prisma Cloud / Prisma SASE** - cloud security and secure access service edge platform.
- **Cortex XDR / XSIAM** - AI-enabled security operations platform consolidating detection, investigation and response.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** NCSC Cyber Incident Response (CIR) assured service provider, Standard and Enhanced levels.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
