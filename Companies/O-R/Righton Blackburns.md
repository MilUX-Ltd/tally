# Righton Blackburns

> West Midlands-based distributor and supply chain integrator of ferrous and non-ferrous raw materials to the defence, aerospace, nuclear, oil and gas and marine industries.

## What they do

Righton Blackburns is a UK market leader in supplying high-integrity ferrous and non-ferrous raw materials to safety-critical industries including defence, nuclear, oil and gas and marine. Its Righton Aerospace division provides supply chain management and integration, value-added delivery and technical knowledge for aerospace and defence programmes.
