# Pall Aerospace

> Filtration and separation technology manufacturer supplying aerospace and defence platforms, with its European base in Portsmouth.

## What they do

Pall Aerospace has supplied filtration and separation technology to aerospace and defence platforms since 1946, with filters protecting hydraulic, fuel, lubrication and air systems to extend equipment life. Its filtration products are qualified across major civil and military aircraft programmes. Pall Europe, based in Portsmouth, forms part of Pall Corporation, a subsidiary of Danaher Corporation (US). ADS-listed capabilities cover components, platform structures, platform systems, propulsion, PPE and safety, construction and infrastructure, and petroleum materials.

## Products and solutions

- **Hydraulic filtration** - filters protecting aircraft hydraulic systems.
- **Fuel filtration** - filtration solutions for aircraft fuel systems.
- **Engine and lubrication filtration** - filtration for aerospace engine and lubrication systems.
- **MRO services** - repair and overhaul of hydro-mechanical components and accessories covering fuel, lubrication, hydraulics, pneumatics and engine inlet structures, through repair stations in the UK and US.
