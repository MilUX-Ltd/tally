# Rhea Space Activity UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of US-based Rhea Space Activity Inc, applying advanced technology to security challenges in GPS-denied navigation and secure communications.

## What they do

Rhea Space Activity conducts R&D and government-funded project work in systems, sensors, and electronics for autonomous navigation and guidance where GPS or GNSS is unavailable or unreliable. The company also undertakes research in sensors and systems for advanced and secure communications, including optical communications, for defence and national security customers.
