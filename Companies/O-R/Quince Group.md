# Quince Group

> Oxfordshire consultancy specialising in insider risk and personnel security.

## What they do

Quince Group is a specialist security consultancy focused on the human dimension of security, helping organisations understand, identify and mitigate insider risk. It provides risk assessments, board-level briefings, personnel security programme design, training and tabletop exercises. Its work addresses threats from insiders across organisations, including government departments.

## Products and solutions

- **Insider risk assessments** - discovery and evaluation of personnel security exposure.
- **Awareness and training** - board briefings, masterclasses and tabletop exercises.
- **Programme design** - insider risk and personnel security strategy development.
