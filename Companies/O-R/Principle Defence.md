# Principle Defence
> UK cyber security, data protection and information assurance consultancy and training provider, founded by a British Army veteran.

## What they do
Principle Defence delivers advisory and consulting engagements on cyber security, data protection and information assurance for both private and public sector clients, including multi-billion-pound programmes. The firm also runs security and privacy training, including SME-targeted webinar series. Founder Jim Wright holds L-SIRA (NCSC) qualifications and is a Fellow of Information Privacy (IAPP), and has co-authored a BCS-published title on cybercrime.
