# Principle Defence
> UK information security, data protection and risk management consultancy and training provider, founded by a British Army veteran.

## What they do
Principle Defence delivers advisory and consulting engagements on information security, data protection and risk management for private and public sector clients, including multi-billion-pound programmes and central and local government. Services include Virtual Chief Information Security Officer (vCISO), Data Protection Officer (DPO), security strategy, threat intelligence, and open source intelligence. The firm also runs security and privacy training and certification courses. The company describes itself as veteran-owned and holds the Armed Forces Covenant Silver Award.

## Products and solutions
- **Virtual CISO (vCISO)** - strategic information security leadership and risk management support, provided as an extension of a client's team.
- **Data Protection Officer (DPO)** - certified DPO service supporting compliance with data protection regulation.
- **Security strategy** - risk assessment and development of tailored security strategies aligned to business objectives.
- **Threat intelligence** - threat monitoring and detection, vulnerability assessment, threat analysis and reporting, incident response support, and a continuous threat intelligence feed.
- **Open source intelligence (OSINT)** - OSINT services delivered as a distinct service line.
- **Cyber Essentials and IASME Cyber Assurance support** - assessment and certification support services.
- **Training and certification** - security, cyber, risk and privacy training and certification courses, delivered by instructors registered with BCS, APMG, TQUK and The Open Group.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials, ISO 27001, ISO 9001
- **Memberships/partnerships:** BSI partner, Incensu registered, ECRC Trusted Partner
