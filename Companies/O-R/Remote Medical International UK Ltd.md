# Remote Medical International UK Ltd

> Provider of bespoke medical, security and HSE support for organisations operating in remote, high-risk environments, including defence.

## What they do

Remote Medical International (RMI) describes itself as a bespoke solutions partner, helping clients manage risk through medical, security and health, safety and environmental (HSE) services wherever they operate, from offshore platforms to conflict zones. The company works across oil and gas, renewable energy, mining, corporate travel, construction, maritime and defence, and states it has provided solutions to US and UK government agencies since 2014 as both prime contractor and subcontractor. The UK entity operates from a base in Fareham, Hampshire.

## Products and solutions

- **On-site medical support** - dedicated medical providers and HSE reporting for client operations.
- **Occupational health** - fit-for-duty assessments, medical surveillance and drug testing.
- **Medical assistance** - 24/7 emergency helpline, medical emergency response plans and medical evacuation.
- **Travel medic staffing** - rapid deployment of paramedics and emergency medical technicians.
- **Mobile medical units** - configurable clinical care units for remote deployments.
- **Intelligence services** - country risk assessments and 24/7 monitoring.
- **Security assistance** - 24/7 crisis response and a global intelligence portal.
- **Government and humanitarian response** - base support services, including clinic support delivered under a LOGCAP contract.
