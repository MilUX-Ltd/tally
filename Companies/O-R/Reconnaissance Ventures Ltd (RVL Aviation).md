# Reconnaissance Ventures Ltd (RVL Aviation)

> Multi-capability specialist airline providing airborne survey, surveillance and aerial dispersant application services.

## What they do

RVL Aviation is a specialist charter airline and aerial services operator based at East Midlands Airport, part of RVL Group. It operates a fleet of aircraft in niche aviation markets rather than scheduled passenger service, including airborne survey, intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance, and aerial dispersant application. The company holds long-running contracts with the Environment Agency and the Maritime and Coastguard Agency covering aerial surveillance, search and rescue support, and dispersant spraying.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerial surveillance and survey** - manned airborne platforms flown on contract for government and civil customers requiring persistent surveillance and reconnaissance.
- **Aerial dispersant application** - spraying of pollution dispersants over oil spills, delivered under contract to the Maritime and Coastguard Agency.
- **Ad hoc charter** - passenger and cargo charter flights alongside the specialist aviation services.
