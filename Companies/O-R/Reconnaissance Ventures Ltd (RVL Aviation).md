# Reconnaissance Ventures Ltd (RVL Aviation)

> Multi-capability specialist airline providing airborne survey, surveillance and aerial dispersant application services.

## What they do

RVL Aviation operates a fleet of aircraft in specialist markets including airborne survey, intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance, and aerial dispersant operations. The company serves defence, government and civil customers requiring persistent airborne capabilities across multiple mission types.
