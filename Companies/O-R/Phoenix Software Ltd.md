# Phoenix Software Ltd

> IT solutions and managed services provider specialising in digital transformation for the defence sector.

## What they do

Phoenix Software delivers IT solutions and managed services for defence organisations, covering cloud and hybrid infrastructure, artificial intelligence, data management, security, and collaboration tooling. The company positions itself as a strategic partner for defence customers seeking to advance digital capabilities and operational outcomes.

## Products and solutions

- **Cloud and hybrid infrastructure services** - Design, deployment, and management of cloud and on-premise IT environments for defence.
- **AI and data management** - Data management, analytics, and artificial intelligence solutions tailored to defence sector requirements.
- **Managed security services** - Cyber security and systems integrity services for defence customers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 27001:2022, ISO 14001, ISO 22301, Cyber Essentials Plus
