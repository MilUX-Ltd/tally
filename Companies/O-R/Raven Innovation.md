# Raven Innovation
> UK defence-sector consultancy helping technology SMEs enter and grow in the defence market.

## What they do
Raven Innovation provides consultancy services to technology-focused SMEs seeking to enter or expand in the defence sector. It reviews a client's portfolio and advises on strategy and objectives, business model, technology roadmaps, and current research and development projects, alongside market-ready products, with the stated aim of helping clients overcome barriers to entry into the defence sector for government or commercial business-to-business opportunities. The firm also develops product-specific use cases addressing unresolved military challenges to increase the probability of return on R&D investment, and offers talks, training events and senior leadership mentoring.

## Products and solutions

- **Strategy and positioning advice** - portfolio review, business model definition and technology roadmap development for SMEs entering the defence market.
- **Stakeholder mapping and use-case development** - identifying military use cases and stakeholders to de-risk R&D investment decisions.
- **Training and mentoring** - talks, training events and senior leadership mentoring for client organisations.
