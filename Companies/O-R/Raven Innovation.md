# Raven Innovation
> UK defence-sector consultancy helping technology SMEs enter and grow in the defence market, founded and led by Jez Holmes OBE.

## What they do
Raven Innovation provides consultancy services to technology-focused SMEs seeking to enter or expand in the defence sector. Service lines cover strategy, business model definition, technology roadmap development, stakeholder mapping and use-case development. The firm draws on its founders' backgrounds in UK capability acquisition and the defence sector to advise clients on positioning for MOD and allied customers.
