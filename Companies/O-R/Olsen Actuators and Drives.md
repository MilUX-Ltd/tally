# Olsen Actuators and Drives

> Specialists in the design and supply of electric roller screw actuators for defence, aerospace and space.

## What they do

Olsen Actuators and Drives designs and supplies electric roller screw actuators for the defence, aerospace and space sectors, alongside wider motion control components for flight control, hydraulic and pneumatic systems, landing gear, propeller control, and helm/steering systems. The company is based at the Sci-Tech Daresbury Innovation Centre in Cheshire and is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park. It trades as "Olsen Actuators and Drives" under the registered company Olsen Actuation UK Ltd.

## Products and solutions

- **Electric roller screw actuators** - UK supplier of servo-electric roller screw actuator technology for aerospace, defence and space applications.
- **Primary flight control actuation** - actuation solutions for manned aircraft flight control systems.
- **Test rig actuation** - actuation for dynamic, static, and endurance test rigs used in defence prime contractor work.
