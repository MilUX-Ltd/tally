# Olsen Actuators and Drives
> Specialists in the design and supply of electric roller screw actuators for defence, aerospace and space.
## What they do
Olsen Actuators and Drives designs and supplies electric roller screw actuators for the defence, aerospace and space sectors. The company is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
