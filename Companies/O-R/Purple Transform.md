# Purple Transform

> Developer of the SIYTE agentic AI platform that turns cameras, sensors and systems into an operational intelligence and threat detection layer.

## What they do

Purple Transform develops SIYTE, an operational intelligence platform that uses agentic AI to turn CCTV cameras, sensors and connected systems into a live monitoring and response capability. The platform detects risks, predicts disruptions and coordinates responses across critical infrastructure, transport, healthcare and logistics. It exhibits in the security and policing market.

## Products and solutions

- **SIYTE platform** - agentic AI operational intelligence and threat detection across cameras, sensors and systems.
