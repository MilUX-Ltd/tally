# Plaswire Ltd

> Northern Ireland manufacturer processing co-polymers for aircraft interiors and developing circular-economy solutions for difficult-to-recycle aerospace polymers.

## What they do

Plaswire processes co-polymers used in aircraft interior applications and works on developing new processes for difficult-to-recycle polymers. The company provides collection services, secure material destruction, and returns recovered materials to the supply chain within a cradle-to-cradle model.
