# Revector

> IMSI intelligence specialist providing hardware and software solutions to detect and monitor security threats across Wi-Fi and mobile networks.

## What they do

Revector creates and deploys bespoke hardware and software solutions using advanced IMSI intelligence, enabling organisations to identify and respond to security threats. The technology supports passive and active monitoring for suspicious and criminal behaviour at airports, border crossings, port access points, critical infrastructure, and private facilities, with counter-terrorism and national security applications.
