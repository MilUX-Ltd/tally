# Revector

> IMSI intelligence specialist providing hardware and software solutions to detect and monitor security threats across Wi-Fi and mobile networks.

## What they do

Revector develops cell-site simulator and mobile phone surveillance hardware and software for security, intelligence and public safety applications. Its core technology, the Revector Detector, detects broadcasting IMSIs within range and locates the associated device; a Wi-Fi variant detects devices seeking or interacting with Wi-Fi networks to identify activity around locations of interest. The company describes its own work as "IMSI intelligence" and "Wi-Fi intelligence"; trade press covering the same product line has also framed it as unmanned SIGINT. Publicly stated applications include critical infrastructure and government building protection, border control including UAV-borne monitoring of land and maritime borders, law enforcement and counter-terrorism, and search and rescue.

## Products and solutions

- **Detector MPS (Multi-Platform System)** - portable, covert four-channel IMSI catcher, carried in a backpack or retrofitted into vehicles, helicopters or aircraft.
- **Detector UAV Payload** - low-SWaP active payload module for integration into drone platforms, detecting and tracking 2G/3G/4G devices via dual software-defined radio, recording IMSI, IMEI, TMSI, GPS location and timestamp data.
- **IMSI intelligence devices for search and rescue** - a suite including a UAV payload, a personal-carry version, and a vehicle-mounted version, enabling location of individuals via their mobile devices and two-way contact even without local network coverage.
- **Wi-Fi Catcher** - combined IMSI and Wi-Fi capture device for UAV integration.
