# RLC Engineering Group

> Precision aerospace manufacturing company producing rotatives, aerofoils, and airframe components for the global aerospace and defence industry.

## What they do

RLC has operated in the aerospace industry since 1955 and is a privately owned business with facilities in the United Kingdom, Isle of Man, and Singapore. The group continually invests in capability and technology across its operating entities: RLC Ronaldsway, RLC Langford, RLC (UK) Callender, RLC Singapore, and RLC (UK) Global Point.
