# RLC Engineering Group

> Precision aerospace manufacturing company producing rotatives, aerofoils, and airframe components for the global aerospace and defence industry.

## What they do

RLC has operated in the aerospace industry since 1955 and is a privately owned business with facilities in the United Kingdom, Isle of Man, and Singapore. It states annual sales over £120 million and more than 1,000 employees. The group serves both civil and military aviation customers and continually invests in capability and technology across its operating entities: RLC Ronaldsway, RLC Langford, RLC (UK) Callender, RLC Singapore, and RLC (UK) Global Point.

## Products and solutions

- **Engine components** - rotatives including IBR, blisk and fan blade parts.
- **Aerofoils** - precision aerofoil manufacturing.
- **Airframes** - airframe structural components.
- **Actuation assemblies** - actuation components and assemblies for aerospace platforms.
- **Ejection seat components** - manufactured components for ejection seat systems.
- **Product lifecycle support** - through-life manufacturing and support services.
