# Produmax Limited

> Yorkshire-based precision engineering company producing flight control components and assemblies for global aerospace manufacturers.

## What they do

Produmax is a precision engineering specialist focused on flight control components and assemblies, with around 80% of output exported to some of the world's largest aerospace manufacturers. The company has invested in advanced automated machinery and a purpose-built manufacturing facility to re-shore work previously carried out in lower-cost economies, and won the 2017 Sharing in Growth (SiG) award for best aerospace supplier.
