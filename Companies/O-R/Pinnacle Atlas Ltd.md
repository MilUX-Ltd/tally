# Pinnacle Atlas Ltd

> Defence and security consultancy specialising in Intelligence, Surveillance, Target Acquisition and Reconnaissance (ISTAR).

## What they do

Pinnacle Atlas brings over 30 years of industry and operational experience to the ISTAR domain, supporting customers in making better decisions, converting those decisions to actions, and delivering sustainable outcomes. Based in Yorkshire, the company was formed specifically to serve defence and security customers requiring ISTAR expertise, and states it has delivered over 100 projects to date.

## Products and solutions

- **Solution provisioning** - Product support, server hosting, airtime and equipment provision, and deployed service technicians for maintenance and repair.
- **Product development** - Combining engineering depth with operational experience to deliver hardware and software programmes.
- **Systems integration** - Integrating sensors, embedded systems and secure communications into compact, scalable solutions supporting ISTAR and multi-domain missions.
- **Training** - Programmes covering deployment and use of complex systems and situational-awareness tools.
