# RSL Project Solutions Ltd

> Independent defence project management and technical consultancy based at Old Sarum, Salisbury.

## What they do

RSL Project Solutions Ltd is a privately owned, independent defence project management and technical consultancy based at The Portway Centre, Old Sarum, Salisbury. It provides consulting, engineering, project and programme management services to UK defence primes, engineering SMEs and government agencies, operating through in-house staff and a network of associates.

## Products and solutions

- **Consultancy** - support for project management and engineering challenges, including active end-to-end management of client projects.
- **Interim Professionals** - interim technical and project management capability drawn from in-house staff and a network of qualified associates.
- **Independent project review** - independent health checks of third-party projects.
- **Bespoke PM systems** - development of project management systems tailored to client needs.
- **Training** - project management training courses and staff assessment and development.
