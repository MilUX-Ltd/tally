# RSL Project Solutions Ltd

> Independent defence project management and technical consultancy based at Old Sarum, Salisbury.

## What they do

RSL provides project and programme management consultancy to the defence, aerospace and related high-technology sectors. Services include interim technical and project management capability, independent project health checks, bespoke PM system development, and logistics and distribution support.
