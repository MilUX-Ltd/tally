# Parallel Track Ltd

> Defence and aerospace business development consultancy specialising in sales strategy, bid management, and market communications.

## What they do

Parallel Track provides consulting services helping defence and aerospace companies win contracts and grow in complex markets. Its work spans sales strategy and pipeline development, bid and capture support including proposal development and red/blue team reviews, and marketing communications tailored to defence procurement audiences.
