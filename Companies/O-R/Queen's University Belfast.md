# Queen's University Belfast

> Research-intensive university with active aerospace, defence and advanced manufacturing research activity.

## What they do

Queen's University Belfast is a broadly based, research-driven university with strong interdisciplinary research spanning engineering, aerospace, materials, and advanced manufacturing. It operates through research clusters and interdisciplinary forums, with priority placed on nationally and internationally recognised research excellence.
