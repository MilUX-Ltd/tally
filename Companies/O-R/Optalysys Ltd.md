# Optalysys Ltd

> Silicon photonics company enabling Fully Homomorphic Encryption for secure computation at scale.

## What they do

Optalysys uses advanced silicon photonics to enable Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE), allowing data to be processed without decryption. This addresses technical, regulatory, and commercial barriers to data sharing across defence, intelligence, and sensitive government environments.

## Products and solutions

- **FHE accelerator** - photonic co-processor enabling encrypted computation at performance levels not achievable in conventional silicon.
- **LightLocker Node** - dedicated FHE-acceleration hardware appliance supporting confidential computation use cases such as private data sharing and post-quantum secure processing.
