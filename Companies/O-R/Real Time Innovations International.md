# Real Time Innovations International

> Software framework company specialising in real-time data connectivity for autonomous and intelligent distributed systems.

## What they do

Real-Time Innovations (RTI) is the leading vendor of products compliant with the Object Management Group Data Distribution Service (DDS) standard. Their Connext platform connects AI algorithms to real-time networks of devices, underpinning autonomous system development across defence, aerospace and government programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Connext DDS** - Data Distribution Service middleware connecting distributed sensors, systems and AI algorithms in real time.
