# Real Time Innovations International

> Software framework company specialising in real-time data connectivity for autonomous and intelligent distributed systems.

## What they do

Real-Time Innovations (RTI) is the leading vendor of products compliant with the Object Management Group Data Distribution Service (DDS) standard. Its Connext product suite provides a connectivity framework supporting over 20 defence industry standards, connecting sensors, actuators and AI algorithms in real time across transports including tactical data links, RF, satellite and underwater sensor networks. RTI states its technology underpins mission-critical systems in defence, aerospace and government programmes, and describes itself as chairing the OSD architecture for unmanned ground control station standards and contributing to avionics open-systems standards. The UK entity is registered as an overseas company establishment, with its registered UK office in Bury St Edmunds.

## Products and solutions

- **Connext DDS / Connext Professional** - Data Distribution Service middleware connecting distributed sensors, systems and AI algorithms in real time.
- **Connext Drive** - a communication framework for automotive and electric vehicle architectures, from individual controllers to zonal and central controllers.
- **Connext AI** - a suite of LLM-powered tools intended to help design, code and debug real-time systems, built on RTI's own documentation and code repositories.
- **Admin Console and System Designer** - development tools for visualising running DDS applications and graphically designing and configuring Connext systems.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** states involvement in Modular Open Systems Approach (MOSA), FACE, SOSA, UCS, UMAA and VAULTIS data strategy principles; DO-178 certified variant referenced for airworthy uncrewed aircraft systems use.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
