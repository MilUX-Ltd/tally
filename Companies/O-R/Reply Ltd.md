# Reply Ltd

> Digital and technology consultancy with a specialism in National Critical Infrastructure solutions.

## What they do

Reply designs and implements solutions built on digital media, new communication channels, and emerging technology capabilities. The company builds and operates a number of National Critical Infrastructure solutions for UK clients. In the UK, defence, intelligence and security work is delivered through named operating companies within the wider Reply group: Shield Reply for defence, intelligence and security, Spike Reply for cyber security, and Airwalk Reply, Glue Reply and Solirius for UK public-sector digital transformation.

## Products and solutions

- **Shield Reply** - defence, intelligence and security practice, delivering cloud services on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure including Oracle Roving Edge Devices for disconnected and degraded environments.
- **Spike Reply** - cyber security operating company offering penetration testing (including CREST OVS web application assessment), a 24/7 cyber security operations centre, identity governance and data security, security governance, risk and compliance, and OT/IIoT cyber security integration.
- **Airwalk Reply and Glue Reply** - UK public-sector technology transformation consultancies, delivering complex technology transformation programmes for government departments.
- **Solirius** - UK government digital transformation consultancy covering agile delivery, AI and data management, with publicly named clients including HMCTS, FCDO Services, the Department for Education and BDUK.
- **Cluster Reply, Cortex Reply and Valorem Reply** - data analytics and cloud-native modernisation capability for UK public-sector customers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
