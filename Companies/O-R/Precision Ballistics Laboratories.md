# Precision Ballistics Laboratories

> Ballistic, blast and impact test provider serving the defence, security, automotive and critical infrastructure sectors.

## What they do

Precision Ballistics Laboratories provides ballistic, impact and PPE testing services from a facility at Shrivenham, Oxfordshire. Capabilities include ballistic testing of body, vehicle and transparent armour, fragmentation and STANAG 4569 testing, blast and IED testing, and PPE, helmet and stab testing. It also supports high-speed video and scientific instrumentation work.

## Products and solutions

- **Ballistic testing** - body armour, vehicle armour, transparent armour and fragment testing up to 14.5mm armour-piercing projectiles.
- **Blast and IED testing** - blast, IED and blunt trauma assessment.
- **PPE and protection testing** - helmet, stab and PPE testing.
