# Radiocoms Systems Ltd

> Provider of defence and security communication solutions, designing and deploying mission-critical voice, video, and data systems for military forces worldwide.

## What they do

Radiocoms Systems Ltd designs, commissions, and deploys COTS and customised communication systems for defence and security customers, drawing on multivendor relationships to deliver integrated solutions. It operates as a Tier 1 contractor in partnership with Defence Digital and TacSys Service Executive teams, designing modular, scalable voice, video and data systems for MOD customers worldwide, secured to government classification standards. The company provides system engineering, training, and after-sales support alongside its deployment services.

## Products and solutions

- **Land Mobile Radio** - P25, TETRA and DMR systems, including partnerships with Motorola Solutions, Hytera, Tait, Kenwood and Entel.
- **LTE and PoC devices** - push-to-talk-over-cellular devices including Motorola WAVE PTX and Entel e-PoC.
- **Satellite communications** - satellite communication devices for deployed and remote operations.
- **Deployable networks** - rapidly deployable communication systems establishing LTE voice and data networks, including Wi-Fi and tactical deployable infrastructure.
- **Body-worn video and CCTV** - body-worn cameras (Axon, Hytera, Motorola Solutions, Reveal) and video analytics (Avigilon).
- **Secure voice and data** - end-to-end encryption at the application or device layer for classified voice and data traffic.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
