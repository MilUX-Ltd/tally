# Radiocoms Systems Ltd

> Provider of defence and security communication solutions, designing and deploying mission-critical voice, video, and data systems for military forces worldwide.

## What they do

Radiocoms Systems Ltd designs, commissions, and deploys COTS and customised communication systems for defence and security customers, drawing on multivendor relationships to deliver integrated solutions. The company provides system engineering, training, and after-sales support alongside its deployment services.
