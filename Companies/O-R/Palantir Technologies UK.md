# Palantir Technologies UK, Ltd.

> UK operation of the US data and AI software company Palantir Technologies, serving defence, intelligence and government with its Gotham, Foundry and AIP platforms.

## What they do

Palantir builds software platforms that integrate large, varied data sources and apply analytics and AI to support decision-making in government and commercial organisations. Its flagship platforms are Gotham (government and intelligence), Foundry (enterprise data operations), AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform) and Apollo (continuous software delivery). The UK entity delivers these into UK defence, health and government. The US parent is headquartered in Denver, Colorado, and the UK office is in London.

## Products and solutions

- **Gotham** - built for government and intelligence; pattern identification across data sources for intelligence analysis, battlefield command and targeting.
- **Foundry** - core data operations platform for data integration, ontology, analytics and workflow development.
- **AIP** - generative-AI layer connecting large language models securely into the platforms, with tooling to build agents and automations.
- **Apollo** - continuous delivery platform managing the infrastructure hosting Foundry and AIP.
