# QCC Global LTD

> Provider of Technical Surveillance Counter Measures (TSCM) services and equipment to governments, armed forces, and law enforcement agencies worldwide.

## What they do

Founded in 1999, QCC Global supplies bespoke TSCM services and equipment to clients in 102 countries, including government departments, armed forces, and law enforcement agencies. Expert engineers operate state-of-the-art detection equipment to identify and neutralise electronic surveillance threats.

## Products and solutions

- **TSCM Services** - Counter-surveillance sweeps and threat assessments delivered by specialist engineers.
- **TSCM Equipment** - Detection hardware designed and supplied for government and military end-users.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 27001:2022, ISO/IEC 17025, ISO 41000, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
