# QCC Global LTD

> Provider of Technical Surveillance Counter Measures (TSCM) services and equipment to governments, armed forces, and law enforcement agencies worldwide.

## What they do

Founded in 1999, QCC Global describes itself as the world's largest TSCM and cyber forensics company, supplying bespoke TSCM services and equipment to clients in over 105 countries, including government departments, armed forces, and law enforcement agencies. Expert engineers operate proprietary detection equipment to identify and neutralise electronic surveillance threats, backed by digital forensics and mobile phone forensics laboratories.

## Products and solutions

- **TSCM / Bug Sweeping** - inspections and live monitoring to detect audio or video eavesdropping on buildings, vehicles and aircraft.
- **Cyber TSCM** - inspections to identify eavesdropping attacks on IT systems, data centres and cyber networks.
- **Digital Forensics** - imaging and analysis of computers, drones and other digital media for government, police and corporate investigators, including expert witness testimony.
- **Mobile Phone Forensics** - acquisition and analysis of mobile phones and tablets for investigators.
- **Penetration Testing and Red Teaming** - cyber, physical and social engineering testing with vulnerability and remediation reporting.
- **Secure Communications** - solutions for secure voice, messaging and video conferencing, plus secure rooms and containers.
- **Protective Security** - measures including counterintelligence window film to reduce eavesdropping and intrusion risk.
- **TSCM Training** - training for government TSCM teams on inspection of buildings, vehicles, aircraft and watercraft.
- **TSCM Equipment** - detection hardware including the SearchLight Plus cellular device detection system and the QCC Sentinel Wi-Fi/Bluetooth detection system, alongside RF spectrum analysers, near-field RF detectors, non-linear junction detectors, thermal imaging cameras and forensic lighting.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR

- **Memberships:** ADS Group, ASIS International, TSCMi, DISA (Defense Industrial Security Association)
