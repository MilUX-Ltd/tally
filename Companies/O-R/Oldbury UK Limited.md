# Oldbury UK Limited

> Defence and industrial manufacturer supplying components, platform structures, platform systems and weapons from Wolverhampton.

## What they do

Oldbury UK is a manufacturing organisation supplying a wide range of defence products including components, platform structures, platform systems, platform interiors, platform integration, and weapons. The ADS directory also lists PPE, safety equipment and construction and infrastructure in their capability set. The company traces its origins to 1861 and now operates as the specialist engineering and defence products division of Boughton Engineering Ltd, from a facility in Wolverhampton with overhead craneage, fabrication and fitting bays, a dedicated paint shop and axle shop, and in-house CAD design engineering.

## Products and solutions

- **Specialist and bespoke trailers** - design, development, manufacture, integration and supply of specialist and bespoke mobility and transportable systems, including Urgent Operational Requirement (UOR) solutions, from lightweight through to maximum-weight special types.
- **Hook-loaders and skip-loaders** - carrier platform systems for defence and industrial use.
- **Theatre support equipment** - enclosed generator packages, high-performance air-conditioning units, solar shields, environmental protection systems and shelters, and high free-volume sand and dust filtration packages.
