# Prestwick Aerosystems

> Aerostructures manufacturer at Glasgow Prestwick Airport, now part of the Airbus Group following Airbus's December 2025 acquisition of Spirit AeroSystems' Prestwick site.

## What they do

Prestwick Aerosystems designs and manufactures aerostructure components for commercial and defence aircraft programmes from its facility at Glasgow Prestwick Airport. The site's principal work is the manufacture of wing leading edges and associated aerostructure assemblies, using composite and aluminium manufacturing processes, for the A320 and A350 families among other Airbus programmes. Airbus completed its acquisition of this and other former Spirit AeroSystems sites (including Belfast, Kinston, Saint-Nazaire and Casablanca) on 8 December 2025, as part of the wider split of Spirit AeroSystems between Airbus and Boeing; the Prestwick site became an Airbus affiliate under the Prestwick Aerosystems name. As part of Airbus, the site draws on the group's wider engineering, research and industrialisation capability.

## Products and solutions

- **Wing leading edges and aerostructure assemblies** - composite and metallic structures for Airbus commercial and defence aircraft programmes, including the A320 and A350 families.
