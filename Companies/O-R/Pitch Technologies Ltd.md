# Pitch Technologies Ltd

> Simulation interoperability specialist providing HLA-based and cloud-hosted simulation products and services, now trading as part of BAE Systems OneArc.

## What they do

Pitch Technologies is focused on open standards-based simulation interoperability, including HLA-Evolved (IEEE 1516-2010, and the newer IEEE 1516-2025 "HLA 4") and DIS (IEEE 1278), plus cloud-hosted simulation environments. The company has a strong R&D orientation and supplies products, methodologies and services to defence and government customers. Its UK address is in London; the parent company was headquartered in Sweden. From 12 November 2025 the legal entities within the Pitch Technologies group began a rename process to BAE Systems OneArc, following BAE Systems' earlier acquisition of Pitch Technologies AB; the company states this is a name change only, with no corporate or legal restructuring and no novation of existing contracts.

## Products and solutions

- **Pitch pRTI** - the core Run-Time Infrastructure (RTI) product of the Pitch Infrastructure family, supporting all HLA standards including HLA 4, with data distribution, time synchronisation and dynamic ownership transfer between simulation models.
- **Pitch Commander** - enables monitoring and control of distributed simulation systems running across multiple computers, including discovery and restart of failed simulations.
- **Pitch Booster** - facilitates running distributed simulations across wide area networks, increasing throughput and fault tolerance while traversing firewalls with certificate-based encryption.
- **Pitch CDS Gateway** - supports secure cross-domain simulation, connecting different security domains while controlling data release based on policy.
- **Pitch DIS Adapter** - enables interoperability between HLA-based systems and existing DIS (IEEE 1278) systems.

Named public use cases for the Pitch Infrastructure product family include large-scale military exercises, distributed synthetic training linking into NATO's Distributed Synthetic Training environment, and live, virtual and constructive (LVC) simulation.
