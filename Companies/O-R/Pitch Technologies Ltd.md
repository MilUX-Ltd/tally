# Pitch Technologies Ltd

> Simulation interoperability specialist providing HLA-based and cloud-hosted simulation products and services.

## What they do

Pitch Technologies is focused on open standards-based simulation interoperability, including HLA-Evolved (IEEE 1516-2010) and cloud-hosted simulation environments. The company has a strong R&D orientation and supplies products, methodologies and services to defence and government customers. Its UK address is in London; the parent company is headquartered in Sweden.
