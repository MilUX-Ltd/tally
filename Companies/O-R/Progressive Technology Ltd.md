# Progressive Technology Ltd

> Subcontract CNC machining specialist producing complex precision components for aerospace and defence customers.

## What they do

Progressive Technology has operated as a precision subcontract machining house for over 40 years, based in Newbury, Berkshire. The company manufactures complex components to short lead times using 5-axis CNC milling, turning, and additive manufacturing, serving aerospace, defence, and high-reliability engineering customers.

## Products and solutions

- **5-Axis CNC Machining** - Complex precision components machined to tight tolerances with short lead times.
- **Additive Manufacturing** - 3D printing and Direct Metal Laser Sintering for prototypes and production parts.
- **Finishing Services** - Electroplating, plating, and surface finishing applied in-house.
