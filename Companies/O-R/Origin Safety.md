# Origin Safety

> Independent safety engineering consultancy providing consultancy and auditing services for safety-critical systems in defence and civil sectors.

## What they do

Origin Safety supports the design and operation of safety-critical systems, covering projects from initial conception through to system certification. Their portfolio includes submarines, satellites, and other novel and complex engineering programmes across both defence and civil sectors, including aviation, nuclear, naval, rail, automotive and land. Safety engineers are described as Chartered and TÜV-certified Functional Safety Engineers.

## Products and solutions

- **Safety case development and management** - including Hazard Log development and safety requirement derivation.
- **Hazard identification and risk assessment** - including Failure Modes Effects and Criticality Analysis, and Fault Tree and Event Tree Analysis.
- **Functional and software safety** - assessment against international and regulatory safety standards.
- **Availability, reliability and maintainability analysis** - and cost-benefit analysis to support ALARP assessments.
- **Safety auditing** - including in-service safety performance monitoring and end-of-life or decommissioning safety cases.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered.
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001 (Management of Safety Engineering and Consultancy Services); Cyber Essentials.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
