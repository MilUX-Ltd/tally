# Origin Safety

> Independent safety engineering consultancy providing consultancy and auditing services for safety-critical systems in defence and civil sectors.

## What they do

Origin Safety supports the design and operation of safety-critical systems, covering projects from initial conception through to system certification. Their portfolio includes submarines, satellites, and other novel and complex engineering programmes across both defence and civil sectors.
