# Pinpoint Project Management Ltd

> Defence, security and intelligence consultancy providing business analysis, change management and project management to the public sector.

## What they do

Established in 2012, Pinpoint Project Management augments SME and prime contractors delivering consultancy to the public sector, with a primary focus on Defence, Security and Intelligence. Core services span business analysis, operating model development, process mapping, requirements engineering, strategy development and business case support for change and transformation programmes. The company describes its military lineage as the basis of its drive to see that its work delivers business value.

## Products and solutions

- **Business analysis and requirements engineering** - investigating and documenting business problems, modelling business systems, and requirements engineering for change and transformation programmes.
- **Operating model and process design** - operating model development and process mapping for public sector change initiatives.
- **Strategy and business case support** - scoping, optioneering, business case support, strategy development, and portfolio planning and delivery.
