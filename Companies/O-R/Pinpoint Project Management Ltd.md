# Pinpoint Project Management Ltd

> Defence, security and intelligence consultancy providing business analysis, change management and project management to the public sector.

## What they do

Established in 2012, Pinpoint Project Management augments SME and prime contractors delivering consultancy to the public sector, with a primary focus on Defence, Security and Intelligence. Core services span business analysis, operating model development, process mapping, requirements engineering, strategy development and business case support for change and transformation programmes.
