# PentenAmio

> Provider of secure mobile communications, AI-enabled cyber defence, and electronic deception technologies for defence and security.

## What they do

PentenAmio delivers secure mobile communications, AI-enabled cyber defence, and electronic deception capabilities to UK, Australian, and allied defence and security customers. The company employs around 300 people and operates across engineering design, manufacturing, and security consultancy.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure Mobile Communications** - protected communications systems for defence and security operations.
- **AI-Enabled Cyber Defence** - machine-intelligence-driven tools for cyber threat detection and response.
- **Electronic Deception Technologies** - systems supporting electronic warfare and deception operations.
