# Praetori

> European developer of sovereign "Continuous Control Infrastructure", an agentic AI platform for defence, intelligence and other mission-critical operations.

## What they do

Praetori builds what it calls Continuous Control Infrastructure, positioned as a single, continuously adapting environment that connects information, data and intelligence through to planning, decisions and execution. The company states its platform is built for Europe's operational independence, developed and governed in Europe, deployable across cloud, on-premise and edge environments, and designed on the principle that AI expands human capability rather than replacing human decision-making authority. Praetori describes its infrastructure as interoperable with existing systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Continuous Control Infrastructure** - a platform connecting signals, intelligence, planning and coordinated action into one adapting environment, applied across four stated domains:
  - **Defence and national security** - mission planning, intelligence fusion and multi-domain coordination for armed forces, first responders and security operators.
  - **Intelligence** - multi-source fusion, entity and relationship analysis, and intelligence-to-action workflows.
  - **Critical infrastructure** - risk monitoring, incident coordination and continuity planning for energy, transport, communications and water operators.
  - **Logistics and complex field operations** - field coordination, route and scenario planning, and disruption management.
