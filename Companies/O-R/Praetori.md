# Praetori
> EU-based developer of agentic AI systems for NATO and EU defence and intelligence customers, including a next-generation C2 platform for real-time battlefield management.

## What they do
Praetori builds agentic AI for defence and intelligence, positioning its core product as a next-generation operating system for NATO-integrated command and control. The platform targets real-time battlefield management, multi-domain coordination and decision-tempo restoration at the tactical and operational levels. Design principles emphasise sovereignty by design and an operator-first user model.
