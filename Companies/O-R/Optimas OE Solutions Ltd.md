# Optimas OE Solutions Ltd

> Tech-enabled manufacturer and distributor of precision fasteners and engineered components for defence and mission-critical industries.

## What they do

Optimas supplies precision components, engineered fasteners, and integrated supply chain solutions to global OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers in defence and other mission-critical sectors. The company provides fully customised inventory management and manufacturing programmes designed to support operational readiness and reduce production downtime. The UK operation is based in Gloucestershire, part of a group headquartered in Illinois, United States.

## Products and solutions

- **Engineered fasteners** - custom-engineered fastening solutions built to application-specific tolerances and performance requirements.
- **Standard fasteners and C-Class components** - industrial screws, nuts, bolts, washers and other high-volume production parts.
- **OptiSpec** - bill-of-materials management tool aimed at improving traceability and supply chain visibility for defence manufacturers.
- **OptiTech managed inventory systems** - customised vendor-managed inventory programmes automating fastener replenishment.
