# Optimas OE Solutions Ltd

> Tech-enabled manufacturer and distributor of precision fasteners and engineered components for defence and mission-critical industries.

## What they do

Optimas supplies precision components, engineered fasteners, and integrated supply chain solutions to global OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers in defence and other mission-critical sectors. The company provides fully customised inventory management and manufacturing programmes designed to support operational readiness and reduce production downtime.
