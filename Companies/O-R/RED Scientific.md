# RED Scientific
> Independent British research and analytical consultancy serving UK government and defence agencies, including the CIRSIUM digital wargaming product.

## What they do
RED Scientific (legal name RED Scientific Reply Limited, acquired by Reply and renamed January 2026) provides operational analysis, systems engineering, integrated logistics support, training and documentation, safety and security compliance, and manpower provision to the UK government and MOD. The firm maintains government-classified premises at Alton, Hampshire, and delivers on-site expert support across the UK. It has been a TacCIS Framework participant and holds a long-standing presence at the Defence Academy.

## Products and solutions
- **CIRSIUM** - a playable mathematical model of military engagements, combining software, combat algorithms and supporting data, developed for the British Army Land Warfare Centre and NATO's Allied Rapid Reaction Corps to support operational-level course-of-action and analytical wargaming. Demonstrated at the Defence Academy Industry Fair, November 2025.
- **RAW (RED Analytical Wargame)** - operational analytical wargaming tool developed with the British Army Land Warfare Centre, used by HQ Allied Rapid Reaction Corps.
- **Data Systems** - bespoke digital solutions built on Oracle, Oracle APEX and MS SQL Server, including MODNET-hosted capability.
- **Dashboard Intelligence (RED-Vi)** - open-source-intelligence harvesting, parsing and fusion with interactive dashboard visualisation.
