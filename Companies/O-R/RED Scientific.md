# RED Scientific
> Independent British research and analytical consultancy serving UK government and defence agencies, including the CIRSIUM digital wargaming product.

## What they do
RED Scientific (legal name RED Scientific Reply Limited, acquired by Reply and renamed January 2026) provides operational analysis, systems engineering, integrated logistics support, training and documentation, safety and security compliance, and manpower provision to the UK government and MOD. The firm maintains government-classified premises at Alton, Hampshire, and delivers on-site expert support across the UK. It has been a TacCIS Framework participant and holds a long-standing presence at the Defence Academy.

## Products and solutions
- **CIRSIUM** - digital wargaming product demonstrated at the Defence Academy Industry Fair, November 2025.
