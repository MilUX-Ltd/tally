# Optimise Engineering Ltd

> Systems engineering consultancy supporting complex defence acquisition, design, test, and maintenance programmes.

## What they do

Optimise Engineering helps defence and government organisations respond to the challenges of increasingly complex system design, acquisition, testing, and maintenance. They provide engineering consultancy, systems integration, software services, and engineering training.
