# Optimise Engineering Ltd

> Systems engineering consultancy supporting complex defence acquisition, design, test, and maintenance programmes.

## What they do

Optimise Engineering helps defence and government organisations respond to the challenges of increasingly complex system design, acquisition, testing, and maintenance. They provide engineering consultancy, systems integration, software services, and engineering training, with a core specialism in IBM Engineering Lifecycle Management tooling (DOORS, DOORS Next, Rhapsody, Engineering Workflow Management and Engineering Test Management). Sectors served include aerospace, defence, automotive, energy, infrastructure, medical devices, rail and utilities.

## Products and solutions

- **IBM Engineering Lifecycle Management training** - instructor-led courses on DOORS, DOORS Next, Rhapsody, Engineering Workflow Management and Engineering Test Management, delivered on-site, in Lincoln, or live online.
- **Introduction to Systems Engineering with Requirements Writing** - foundational course on lifecycle principles, requirements traceability and verification methods.
- **INCOSE Systems Engineering Professional exam preparation** - structured, handbook-aligned exam preparation course.
- **Optimise3P** - managed and cloud-hosting service for IBM Engineering Lifecycle Management, covering licensing, hosting infrastructure, training and support.
