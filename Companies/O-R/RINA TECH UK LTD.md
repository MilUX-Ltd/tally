# RINA TECH UK LTD

> UK arm of the Italian RINA group, providing testing, inspection, certification and consulting services across space and defence, electromagnetic compatibility, cyber security, safety and training.

## What they do

RINA TECH UK delivers specialist technical services across the defence supply chain, working directly for governments, space agencies, prime contractors, equipment manufacturers and SMEs. Core service areas include system and integration engineering, electromagnetic compatibility, ICT and software engineering, safety and cyber security, training and learning solutions, and decision support through analytical modelling. The wider RINA group's Space and Defence business covers the full system lifecycle, including specification, design, integration, modelling and simulation, prototyping, testing and validation, for combat systems, radar, surveillance systems, satellite communications, earth observation and navigation.

## Products and solutions

- **System and Integration Engineering** - full system lifecycle support from specification through integration, prototyping, testing and validation for combat systems, radar and surveillance systems.
- **EMC Testing and Integration Engineering** - electromagnetic compatibility and systems integration for defence platforms.
- **Safety, Security and Cyber Security** - safety case support and cyber security services across defence programmes.
- **Training and Learning Solutions** - training and learning support for defence customers.
- **Decision Support Solutions** - analytical modelling and simulation-based decision support for government and defence customers.
