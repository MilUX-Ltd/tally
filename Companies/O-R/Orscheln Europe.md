# Orscheln Europe

> Designer and manufacturer of mechanical and electronic control products for aerospace, defence, commercial vehicle and marine markets.

## What they do

Orscheln Products designs, manufactures and supplies control products across aerospace, defence, commercial vehicle and marine sectors. The Rugby, England site is the group's European hub, serving OEM and aftermarket customers and managing a network of F.A.S.T.® distributors across Europe. The wider Orscheln group is headquartered in Moberly, Missouri, USA, where it manufactures aircraft controls and holds an FAA PMA-approved quality management system.

## Products and solutions

- **Mechanical control cables** - push-pull, tension and ball-bearing control cable assemblies for aerospace, defence, commercial vehicle and marine applications.
- **Aerospace control cables and cable assemblies** - cable-based control systems for commercial aviation, general aviation, light jet, helicopter and military aircraft, plus bonding straps, RAT release and hold-open struts.
- **Electronic gear selectors and actuators** - electronic control actuation for commercial vehicle and defence applications, including shift solutions for AT/AMT transmissions on military wheeled and tracked vehicles.
- **Electronic throttle controls** - electronic foot throttle and pedal box systems for defence and commercial vehicle markets.
- **Fluid level indicators** - dipstick assemblies and fluid level monitoring for military and commercial vehicle platforms.
- **Park-brake systems** - park-brake cable and lever systems for defence and commercial vehicle applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, AS9100D, ISO 14001, FAA PMA-approved quality management system (group-wide).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
