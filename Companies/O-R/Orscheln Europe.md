# Orscheln Europe

> Designer and manufacturer of mechanical and electronic control products for aerospace, defence, commercial vehicle and marine markets.

## What they do

Orscheln Products designs, manufactures and supplies control products across aerospace, defence, commercial vehicle and marine sectors. The product range includes mechanical control cables, lever controls, electronic gear selectors and actuators, electronic throttle controls, fluid level indicators and hard line tubes.

## Products and solutions

- **Mechanical control cables** - flexible control cable assemblies for aerospace and defence platforms.
- **Electronic gear selectors and actuators** - electronic control actuation for commercial vehicle and defence applications.
- **Electronic throttle controls** - throttle control systems for defence and commercial vehicle markets.
