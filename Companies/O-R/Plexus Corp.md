# Plexus Corp.

> Global electronics manufacturing services provider serving aerospace and defence among other regulated industries.

## What they do

Plexus is a global electronics manufacturing services (EMS) company that designs, manufactures and services highly complex products for customers in demanding regulatory environments. It works across the full product lifecycle, from design and development through supply chain, new product introduction, manufacturing and aftermarket services. Its markets include aerospace and defence, healthcare, industrial and semiconductor capital equipment, delivered from around 26 facilities across the Americas, Asia-Pacific and EMEA.

## Products and solutions

- **Design and Development** - product development and engineering, from individual circuit boards to fully tested line replaceable units.
- **Manufacturing and Supply Chain Solutions** - new product introduction and volume manufacturing of complex electronic products.
- **Aftermarket Services** - certified repair-station services including diagnostics, failure analysis, repair and refurbishment.
- **Aerospace and Defense market sector** - dedicated offering covering aerospace, defence, space and security, including a European defence capability for export-compliant manufacturing and engineering.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100; ITAR and EAR compliance; ISO 9001; ISO 14001.
