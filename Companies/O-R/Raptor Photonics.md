# Raptor Photonics

> Northern Ireland manufacturer of high-performance, low-light digital cameras and sensor cores for surveillance, aerospace, space and scientific markets.

## What they do

Raptor Photonics designs, manufactures and sells high-performance, low-light-level digital cameras and camera core engines for surveillance, aerospace and space, scientific and industrial OEM markets. It supplies both off-the-shelf and custom solutions using CCD, EMCCD, CMOS and InGaAs sensors across UV, visible and SWIR wavebands, with cameras built to military specifications and optimised for size, weight and power in harsh environments. Its ISTAR camera systems are deployed on electro-optic platforms from the sea-bed to space, including gimbals and turrets on air, sea and land platforms and payloads on low Earth orbit missions.

## Products and solutions

- **Eagle** - deep-cooled scientific CCD cameras.
- **Hawk** - compact, rugged CMOS cameras for electro-optic integration.
- **Owl** - SWIR and Vis-SWIR InGaAs cameras for night vision and surveillance.
- **Ninox** - cooled SWIR and Vis-SWIR InGaAs cameras.
- **Kestrel 640 eSWIR** - extended-SWIR camera with IRNova T2SL sensor, Stirling-cooled to 170K.
- **Eagle XV / Eagle XO** - X-ray cameras.
- **OEM solutions** - block cores for gimbals and EO turrets, flight-ready camera cores for space, hyperspectral and spectroscopy cores.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015; IPC-A-610 Class 3; Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials PLUS. Cameras designed to MIL-STD-810F and MIL-STD-704F.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Northern Ireland Photonics (NI-PIC).
