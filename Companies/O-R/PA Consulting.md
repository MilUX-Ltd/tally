# PA Consulting

> Global consulting and innovation firm with an established defence and security practice, headquartered in London.

## What they do

PA Consulting is a consulting and innovation firm of around 4,000 strategists, innovators, designers, consultants, scientists, engineers and technologists, with expertise across eight industries including defence and security. Its defence and security practice works with the armed forces, government agencies and police on capability delivery, programmes and transformation. The firm operates from offices across the UK, US, Ireland, the Nordics and the Netherlands.

## Products and solutions

- **Public sector defence** - strategy, capability delivery and transformation for the Ministry of Defence and the armed forces.
- **Aerospace, defence and security industries** - performance, workforce and next-generation technology work for primes and suppliers.
- **Space** - innovation across the space domain, including work with the UK Space Agency.
- **National and homeland security** - critical programmes strengthening national security and public safety.
- **Cyber security** - including CREST-accredited penetration testing.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** MOD Digital and IT Professional Services (DIPS) framework, via the Digital Allies consortium.
- **Accreditations:** CREST member.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, techUK.
