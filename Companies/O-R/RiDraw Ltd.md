# RiDraw Ltd

> Infrastructure intelligence consultancy specialising in spatial intelligence, environmental assessment and operational insight across defence digital, aviation, transport and utilities sectors.

## What they do

RiDraw works at the intersection of infrastructure and change, delivering spatial intelligence, environmental assessment and structured operational insight to operators, investors and regulators. In defence, the firm covers Defence Digital, offering consultancy services spanning commercial strategy, planning, procurement and compliance, as well as simulation and modelling and systems integration.
