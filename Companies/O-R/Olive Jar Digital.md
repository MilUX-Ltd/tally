# Olive Jar Digital

> Award-winning UK digital consultancy delivering managed digital services and expert talent to MOD and central government.

## What they do

Olive Jar Digital is a UK digital services and consultancy firm focused on MOD and central government work. The business provides managed digital delivery and expert talent augmentation, primarily through the Digital Outcomes & Specialists (DOS) and Crown Commercial Service (CCS) frameworks. It operates as a partner-led delivery organisation, regularly teaming with specialist SMEs on MOD, MoJ, DHSC and SFO opportunities.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed digital delivery** - end-to-end delivery for government and defence programmes across the full service lifecycle.
- **Expert talent** - specialist digital professionals placed into client teams across discovery, alpha, beta and live phases.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** Digital Outcomes & Specialists (DOS), Crown Commercial Service (CCS)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
