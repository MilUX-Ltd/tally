# Portsmouth Aviation Ltd

> Integrated defence manufacturer delivering concept-to-delivery design, manufacture, and test services for aerospace, armour, and platform structures from a single site in Portsmouth.

## What they do

Portsmouth Aviation operates from a single integrated facility in Portsmouth, covering initial concept and design through to final manufacture and testing in-house. Its product scope includes platform structures, armour and blast protection, fuselage subcomponents, propulsion components, CBRN equipment, and replenishment-at-sea systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Platform Structures and Armour** - Manufacture of fuselage subcomponents, wing components, and armoured vehicle bodies including blast protection systems.
- **CBRNe and Decontamination Equipment** - Design and manufacture of CBRN decontamination and nuclear engineering equipment.
- **Propulsion Components** - Engine and gearbox components for aerospace and defence platforms.
- **Ground Support Equipment** - Platform assembly and ground support solutions.
