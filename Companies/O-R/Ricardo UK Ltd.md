# Ricardo UK Ltd

> Global engineering and environmental consultancy with over a century of expertise in propulsion systems, clean energy and sustainable mobility, including work on future aircraft propulsion for defence applications.

## What they do

Ricardo plc is an engineering, environmental and strategic consulting company and specialist niche manufacturer. It works across defence, automotive, aerospace, rail and energy sectors, developing propulsion systems for future aircraft and providing sustainability and performance engineering to government and defence customers. Ricardo states it has supported the UK Ministry of Defence and the wider defence industry for over 100 years, and is a long-standing key supplier to the Defence Equipment and Support Marine Engine Group, providing specialist technical services in power plant selection and operation.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence engineering services** - power plant, propulsion and systems engineering support to UK MOD and defence primes, including work on future aircraft propulsion (fixed wing, rotary, eVTOL and UAV platforms).
- **Land systems engineering** - vehicle engineering consultancy supporting UK MOD and defence industry programmes.
