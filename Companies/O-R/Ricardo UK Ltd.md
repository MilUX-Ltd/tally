# Ricardo UK Ltd

> Global engineering and environmental consultancy with over a century of expertise in propulsion systems, clean energy and sustainable mobility, including work on future aircraft propulsion for defence applications.

## What they do

Ricardo plc is an engineering, environmental and strategic consulting company and specialist niche manufacturer. It works across defence, automotive, aerospace, rail and energy sectors, developing propulsion systems for future aircraft and providing sustainability and performance engineering to government and defence customers.
