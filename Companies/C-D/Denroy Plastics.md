# Denroy Plastics Limited

> Northern Ireland injection moulding manufacturer supplying precision plastic components for civil and military aerospace and defence programmes.

## What they do

Denroy Plastics Limited is a privately owned injection moulding manufacturer based in Bangor, County Down, founded in 1972 by the Rainey family. The company operates from two factories covering 7,500 square metres and supplies precision moulded components to civil and military aerospace, defence, automotive and other sectors. Customers include Airbus, BAE Systems, Boeing, Bombardier and Thales. Denroy took over all injection moulding from British Aerospace during a restructuring in 1995 and has since supplied major programmes including Airbus A320, Hawk, Tornado and the Airbus A220 (formerly Bombardier C-Series). The company specialises in lightweighting materials including PEEK and PPS.

## Products and solutions

- **Injection Moulded Structural Components** - precision thermoplastic parts for airframe, wing, centre wing-box and fuel-tank applications, replacing machined aluminium and titanium brackets to reduce weight.
- **Thermoplastic Composites** - advanced moulded components for aerospace and defence using high-performance polymers.
- **Custom Mouldings** - design, tooling and manufacture of moulded components across aerospace, defence and adjacent sectors.
