# CES Advanced Composites UK

> UK arm of CES, designing and manufacturing advanced composite parts and ballistic protection for defence and aerospace platforms.

## What they do

CES Advanced Composites designs and manufactures advanced composite parts and assemblies, and ballistic protection solutions for land, naval and aerospace platforms. Services include composite tool manufacturing, structural design, material characterisation and non-destructive testing.

CES Advanced Composites UK Ltd is the UK entity of the wider CES group, whose main operations are based in Turkey. The UK facility was set up as an R&D and manufacturing site for advanced armour and composite systems, with the aim of supporting MoD and OEM programmes in-country and serving UK and European customers. The company has a facility in Coventry.

## Products and solutions

- **Ballistic armour** - helmets, vests, inserts, shields and EOD search suits for personnel protection.
- **Composite defence systems** - radar and sonar domes, land vehicle composite parts, missile parts and reflective antennas.
- **Engineering services** - composite design, tooling and testing for aerospace and defence applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100 and ISO 9001 (since 2013, group level); NADCAP Non-Destructive Inspection (2018) and NADCAP Composites (2019).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
