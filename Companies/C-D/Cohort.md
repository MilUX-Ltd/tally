# Cohort

> AIM-listed parent group of independent UK and European defence technology businesses spanning land, maritime and air markets.

## What they do

Cohort is the AIM-listed parent of a group of defence technology businesses providing services and products to UK, German, Portuguese, Australian and international customers in defence and related markets. The group model combines the responsiveness of smaller independent businesses with the stability and market access of a listed group. It reports in two segments: Communications and Intelligence, and Sensors and Effectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Chess Dynamics** - surveillance, tracking and fire-control systems for defence and security.
- **EID** - communications systems for naval and military customers (Portugal).
- **ELAC SONAR** - sonar systems and underwater communications for naval customers (Germany).
- **EM Solutions** - satellite communications terminals for navy and maritime customers (Australia); acquired January 2025.
- **MASS** - data technology for defence and security, focused on electronic warfare, digital services and training support.
- **MCL (Marlborough Communications)** - electronic and surveillance technology for UK end users including the MOD.
- **SEA** - technology-based products for defence and transport markets, plus research and training services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** Listed on the London Stock Exchange AIM market (ticker CHRT); complies with AIM Rule 26 and benchmarks governance against the QCA code.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
