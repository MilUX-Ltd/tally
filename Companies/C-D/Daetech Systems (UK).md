# Daetech Systems (UK)

> Manchester-based designer and manufacturer of bespoke digital forensics, evidence retrieval, and field intelligence gathering systems for law enforcement, military, counter-terrorism, and government agencies.

## What they do

Daetech Systems (UK) Limited has manufactured custom intelligence and digital forensics systems since 2000, serving law enforcement, military, state security, counter-terrorism, homeland security, and government agencies in the UK and internationally. The company also holds the exclusive global distributor and hardware integration partnership for Cognitech Inc, the forensic image analysis software developer, providing sales, support, and training on the full Cognitech product range. International deployments include Sweden, the Netherlands, Germany, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Jordan, Morocco, Algeria, Turkey, Lebanon, Mauritius, and India.

## Products and solutions

- **MIDAS (Mobile Image and Data Acquisition System)** - field-deployable, off-grid capable platform for on-scene CCTV retrieval, drive cloning, and multimedia evidence processing; integrated battery system; used by law enforcement, counter-terrorism, and military agencies.
- **DARS (Digital Analysis and Retrieval System)** - video processing and photogrammetry workstations for agencies handling large volumes of digital multimedia evidence; hundreds of systems deployed in the UK and internationally.
- **Cognitech forensic video products** - forensic video enhancement, analysis, and photogrammetry software distributed and integrated exclusively by Daetech for UK and international agencies.
- **Bespoke remote and off-grid computing solutions** - custom-built systems to agency operational and environmental specifications, including austere and remote environments.
- **Training** - digital forensic investigator courses for agency and military personnel.
