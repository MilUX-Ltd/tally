# DIEM Analytics

> UK defence analytics consultancy specialising in decision support, simulation, and capability investment analysis.

## What they do

DIEM Analytics helps defence stakeholders from tactical to strategic level make better decisions about capability investment and operational planning. Their method combines visual modelling of the problem space with structured stakeholder interviews, producing simulation tools, decision aids, and evidence bases for evaluating courses of action. The company is entirely defence-focused; its client and domain list covers Dstl, Raytheon UK, Roke Manor Research, and Cohort plc subsidiaries, across the UK MOD and prime contractor community.

## Products and solutions

- **Decision Support Modelling** - Visual modelling of problem spaces combined with structured small-group stakeholder interviews; produces evidence bases for option analysis.
- **Simulation and Synthetic Environments** - Custom simulation tools for operational planning and capability development; domains include Close Combat, Air Defence, End-to-End Targeting, and Joint Logistics.
- **Data Science and Transformation** - AI and machine learning applied to defence analytical problems; modelling and synthetic environment generation.
