# Custodian360
> UK-based managed security services provider running a human-led SOC for SMBs, with a focus on Microsoft 365 environments.
## What they do
Custodian360 is a Berkshire-based MSSP offering 24/7 SOC-as-a-Service with sub-60 minute triage, incident response, security awareness training, and Cyber Essentials and ISO 27001 compliance support. The company serves small and mid-sized organisations and reports 97 percent customer retention since founding in 2016 and zero successful ransomware incidents among customers.
## Products and solutions
- **SOC-as-a-Service** - 24/7 human-led security operations centre with sub-60 minute triage and incident response.
- **Compliance support** - Cyber Essentials, ISO 27001 assessment and certification support.
- **Security awareness training** - Staff training and phishing simulation.

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials, ISO 27001
