# Concurrent Technologies

> A British designer and manufacturer of high-performance embedded computer products for critical defence and industrial applications.

## What they do

Concurrent Technologies Plc develops and manufactures system-orientated solutions and high-performance computer products, based primarily on Intel processor technology, for use in critical embedded applications. Its products are used by integrators across the defence, security, aerospace, telecommunications, transportation, medical and industrial markets. The company is based in Colchester and is listed on the London Stock Exchange under the ticker CNC.

## Products and solutions

- **VPX and OpenVPX boards and systems** - 3U VPX processor boards and rugged box-level systems, including SOSA-aligned products.
- **Single board computers** - embedded processor boards for mission computing, sensor processing and edge analytics.
- **VME boards** - modern VME processor cards for legacy programme sustainment.
