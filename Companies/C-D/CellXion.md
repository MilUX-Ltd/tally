# CellXion

> UK supplier of cellular intelligence, geo-location and bespoke wireless communications systems for law enforcement, military and government users.

## What they do

CellXion is a privately owned UK company specialising in cellular intelligence-gathering and geo-location tools for law enforcement and military users. It designs and supplies bespoke cellular and wireless communications solutions for UK and international government agencies, with systems spanning 2G, 3G, 4G and 5G networks. The operational base is in Caterham, Surrey, and the company also has a US subsidiary.

## Products and solutions

- **Cellular intelligence and geo-location** - cellular intelligence gathering and geo-location systems for law enforcement and military.
- **Modular cellular solutions** - systems covering 2G, 3G, 4G and 5G.
- **QMTS** - a multi-role communications VPN platform that simultaneously uses the data networks of up to four mobile network operators in one location.
