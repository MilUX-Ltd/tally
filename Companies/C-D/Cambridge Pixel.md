# Cambridge Pixel Ltd

> Employee-owned UK developer of modular radar, camera and sensor processing software, SDKs and interface hardware for defence, maritime and security integrators.

## What they do

Cambridge Pixel develops modular radar, camera and sensor processing software, SDKs and interface hardware that let systems integrators and developers add radar capability to their systems with lower technical risk. It is a supplier of software-based radar tracking and scan-conversion solutions through its SPx software library and HPx hardware range, supporting over 80 makes and models of modern and legacy radar. The employee-owned company is based near Cambridge and serves customers in over 50 countries.

## Products and solutions

- **SPx** - modular software library and SDK for radar, camera and sensor processing.
- **SPx Server V2** - radar tracking software; a 2026 update added proximity detection, HPx-700 support, and TAK and SAPIENT track-output formats.
- **HPx** - radar input and interface hardware card range.
- **HPx-700** - ARM-based edge Radar Input and Signal Processor that digitises analogue radars without a host PC.
- **VSD-C2** - command-and-control display software bridging COTS and bespoke C2 systems.
- Radar scan conversion and display, primary radar acquisition and target tracking, multi-sensor integration, and BlackTalon counter-UAS ecosystem integration.
