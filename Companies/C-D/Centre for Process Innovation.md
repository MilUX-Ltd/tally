# Centre for Process Innovation

> UK technology and innovation centre accelerating development, prototyping, and scale-up of advanced materials, printable electronics, biologics, and formulation technologies, with aerospace and defence as a named served sector.

## What they do

CPI is a not-for-profit technology innovation centre and founding member of the High Value Manufacturing Catapult. It operates five technology platforms: industrial biotechnology, printable and flexible electronics, formulation, biologics, and energy storage. Aerospace and defence is an explicit market sector. The HVM Catapult signed a formal alliance with the Ministry of Defence in September 2025 under the Defence Industrial Strategy, covering advanced materials, AI, and robotics.

## Products and solutions

- **Printable and flexible electronics** - Printed sensor arrays, NFC energy-harvesting devices, and photonic components with defence, security, and aerospace applications.
- **Advanced composites and carbon fibre** - Partnership with the National Composites Centre (announced March 2026) targeting sovereign carbon fibre capability for aerospace, defence, and clean energy.
- **CPI Enterprises portfolio** - Venture arm holding stakes in defence-adjacent companies including Graphene Composites (STANAG 4569/AEP-55 certified armour) and Q5D Technologies (automated wire harness manufacturing; US Army contract).

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** HVM Catapult member (formal MOD alliance, September 2025)
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; ISO 14001; ISO 13485:2016; MHRA Manufacturer's Licence; FDA registered
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; PhotonHub Europe (UK node)
