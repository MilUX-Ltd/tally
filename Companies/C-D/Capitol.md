# Capitol
> Model-agnostic, agentic AI platform that helps organisations produce decision-quality intelligence from their own data.
## What they do
Capitol AI is a Washington DC-based enterprise AI company with a London office serving UK and European customers. The platform is model-agnostic and agentic, designed to help organisations make sense of their own data and produce well-crafted documents, reports and summaries, with every output traceable to source material and each step logged for a complete audit trail. Customer sectors include defence, consulting, media, healthcare, policy and finance. The UK push, led from a London office opened in early 2026, is positioned around regulated, high-trust customers.

## Products and solutions
- **Capitol AI platform** - Agentic, model-agnostic decision-quality intelligence platform that turns structured and unstructured data into reports, briefs and other decision-grade artefacts, run inside the customer's own infrastructure with no data egress.
