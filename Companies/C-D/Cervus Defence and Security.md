# Cervus Defence and Security

> Veteran-owned defence SME providing data analytics for military training, experimentation and decision support.

## What they do

Cervus is a data analytics company serving the defence and security market. Founded in 2013 by three Army veterans, it works in training analytics and experimentation, turning data from live, virtual and constructive simulation into analysis that supports force development and decision-making. The company is recognised as a veteran-owned, SC-cleared SME.

Cervus is based at Porton Science Park, Porton Down, Wiltshire, and employs around 15 people. Its work has included instrumented live fire tactical training experiments for the British Army.

## Products and solutions

- **Hive** - military training analytics platform that ingests live, virtual and constructive simulation data and presents dashboards for analysis and optimisation.
- **XCALIBR** - app-based system providing real-time marksmanship data to soldiers, commanders and instructors on weapon handling and accuracy.
- **Wargames and synthetic environments** - custom digital wargames built for experimentation, concept development and capability assessment.
