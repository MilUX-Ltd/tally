# DTD Consult Ltd

> Defence and security consultancy advising manufacturers, technology firms and government on finding, winning and delivering defence work.

## What they do

DTD Consult is a defence and security consultancy based in Sandwich, Kent. It was originally founded in Germany in 2015 and is registered in England and Wales (company number 15325445). It advises defence manufacturers, technology companies, SMEs and government organisations on defence procurement, market entry, strategic partnerships, capture management and business growth, and states experience across the UK Ministry of Defence, Home Office, and US, EU and NATO engagement. Alongside the defence work it offers general business, strategic and succession consultancy, and supports companies looking to start or expand in Germany.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence business development** - capture management, proposal and contract support, market entry and strategic partnerships for defence and security clients.
- **Government and MOD engagement** - advice and representation across UK MOD, Home Office, US, EU and NATO procurement.
- **Apprenticeship end-point assessment** - EPA for Non-Destructive Testing (L2, L3, L6), Project Controls (L3) and Intelligence Analyst (L4) apprentices.
- **Business consultancy** - strategic, succession and growth planning, including support for companies entering or expanding in Germany.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Programmes:** Mentor on the UK government's Help to Grow programme; recognised by UK Trade & Investment (British Consulate General, Düsseldorf) as a business consultant for companies starting or expanding in Germany.
