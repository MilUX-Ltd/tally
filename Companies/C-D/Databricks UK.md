# Databricks UK Ltd

> UK and EMEA subsidiary of Databricks, Inc., providing a unified data lakehouse and AI platform to government, defence, and enterprise customers.

## What they do

Databricks UK Ltd (registered as Databricks U.K. Limited) is the British subsidiary of Databricks, Inc., founded in 2013 in San Francisco by the original creators of Apache Spark. The company offers a cloud-native Data Intelligence Platform combining data engineering, machine learning, SQL analytics, and generative AI on a unified lakehouse architecture, running natively on AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud. More than 20,000 organisations globally use the platform, including over 50% of the FTSE 100. The UK entity serves as the EMEA hub; a March 2026 commitment of over $850m to UK expansion includes quadrupling the London office footprint and doubling UK and Ireland headcount from 500 to over 1,000. Databricks holds ADS Group membership and G-Cloud 14 listings, and the platform has achieved US DoD Impact Level 5 authorisation on AWS GovCloud, indicating security credentials relevant to defence-grade workloads.

## Products and solutions

- **Data Intelligence Platform** - unified data lakehouse for data engineering, SQL analytics, machine learning, and AI at scale; runs on AWS, Azure, and GCP.
- **Databricks SQL** - SQL analytics and business intelligence on the lakehouse.
- **MLflow and AutoML** - open-source machine learning lifecycle management and automated model training.
- **Unity Catalog** - data governance, lineage, and access control across the platform.
- **Data Intelligence for Cyber Defense** - threat detection, SIEM augmentation, and security analytics capability; announced June 2025.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** G-Cloud 14 (Crown Commercial Service) -- two service listings (Data Intelligence Platform; cloud support).
- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus (Azure Databricks); ISO/IEC 27001:2022; ISO 27017; ISO 27018; ISO 27701; SOC 2 Type II; US DoD IL5 provisional authorisation (AWS GovCloud, November 2024); FedRAMP High Agency ATO (AWS GovCloud, February 2025).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
