# Doza Consulting

> Leeds-based technology consultancy delivering software engineering and digital services, with experience working for the MoD.

## What they do

Doza Consulting is a technology consultancy based in Leeds, incorporated in March 2023 by former colleagues who previously worked at a global technology consultancy. The company specialises in software engineering, full-stack development, cloud engineering, data, mobile applications and delivery leadership. It has delivered work for the NHS, the Ministry of Defence, the Department for Transport, and private sector clients.

## Products and solutions

- **Software engineering** - full-stack development across the application stack from infrastructure to frontend.
- **Cloud engineering** - cloud architecture, migration and platform services.
- **Data and analytics** - data engineering, data platform and analytics capability.
- **Delivery leadership** - programme and delivery management for technology programmes.
