# Cablescan

> Manufacturer of high-performance electrical cable assemblies, wiring harnesses and control panels for military and aerospace platforms.

## What they do

Cablescan manufactures and develops electrical cable assemblies, wiring harnesses, cables and control panels for military and aerospace customers. It holds onsite engineering and prototyping capability, supporting development through to volume manufacture, and maintains a large onsite stockholding of ground-vehicle material to respond to UK Urgent Operational Requirement (UoR) demands. The business was formed in 1994; the current legal entity was registered in 2001, and Cablescan was acquired by Amphenol Corporation in 2019.

## Products and solutions

- **Cable assemblies and wiring harnesses** - design, manufacture and test for military aerospace, naval, land vehicle and UAV applications.
- **Control panels** - manufacture for defence and industrial platforms.
- **Repair, overhaul and modification** - in-service support for existing assemblies.
- **Laser wire marking, automatic testing and machine overbraiding** - supporting manufacturing capabilities.

Stated market sectors include military aerospace, naval, military land vehicles, unmanned aerial vehicles, communications, test equipment and security.
