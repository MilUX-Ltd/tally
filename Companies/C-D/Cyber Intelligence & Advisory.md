# Cyber Intelligence & Advisory Ltd

> Cyber security advisory firm serving government, military and commercial clients; founded 2024 by practitioners with two decades of sector experience.

## What they do

Cyber Intelligence & Advisory Ltd partners with commercial and government organisations to deliver strategic and tactical cyber security solutions. The team delivers security transformation across SecOps, DevSecOps, Identity and Access Management, Cloud and Edge disciplines. Client sectors include telecoms, banking, manufacturing, government and military. The company is an Armed Forces Covenant signatory.

## Products and solutions

- **Fractional CISO and Board Advisory** - Part-time or interim senior cyber leadership.
- **Threat Modelling Workshops** - Structured analysis of an organisation's threat landscape.
- **Security Operations (SecOps)** - Design and improvement of security operations functions.
- **DevSecOps / Application Security** - Integration of security into software development pipelines.
- **End User Awareness Training** - Staff cyber security education programmes.
