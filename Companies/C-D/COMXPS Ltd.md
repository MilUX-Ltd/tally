# COMXPS Ltd

> Independent management consultancy specialising in programme and project management, enterprise architecture, and change management for defence and government clients.

## What they do

COMXPS delivers programme management, PMO, business analysis, SIAM, service design and transition, bid management, enterprise architecture, and commercial and procurement management. Clients confirmed on the Digital Marketplace include the Ministry of Defence, the Australian Department of Defence, and NATO. The firm operates across public, private, and voluntary sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Programme and Project Management** - Agile and Waterfall delivery for complex government and defence programmes.
- **Enterprise Architecture** - Architecture consultancy and advisory for large-scale transformation.
- **Bid Management** - Bid support and commercial management for public sector procurement.
- **PMO and Portfolio Management** - Portfolio-level oversight and governance.
