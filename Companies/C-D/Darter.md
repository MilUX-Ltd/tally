# Darter Ltd

> UK data and digital consultancy operating exclusively in defence, national security, and critical infrastructure.

## What they do

Darter is a Southampton-based consultancy whose stated scope is strengthening UK security, resilience, and prosperity through data strategy and digital transformation. Service lines include data strategy, cloud platform implementation, digital transformation advisory, and AI and data governance programmes. The company works with defence clients including RAF Digital and operates as a partner SME within the Mars Secure Advisors consortium for defence and aerospace data transformation. Darter holds two G-Cloud 14 listings on the Crown Commercial Service digital marketplace.

## Products and solutions

- **Data strategy and advisory** - data maturity assessment and roadmap development for defence organisations.
- **Cloud platform implementation** - secure cloud and data architecture design for defence and national security contexts.
- **Secure digital and data transformation** - structured transformation programmes for engineering and defence organisations; includes experience in secure data architectures for critical infrastructure.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** G-Cloud 14 (Crown Commercial Service) -- two service listings (Cloud Platform Implementation; Secure Digital and Data Transformation for Engineering Organisations).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
