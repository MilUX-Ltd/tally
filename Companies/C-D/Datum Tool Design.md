# Datum Tool Design

> Northern Ireland aerospace tooling specialist designing and manufacturing composite tooling and assembly fixtures for major airframe programmes, including BAE Systems and Airbus.

## What they do

Datum Tool Design is an independent design and manufacturing company based in Lisburn, providing technical design consultancy services and precision tooling to major airframe manufacturers and their supply chains. The company specialises in tooling for composite parts ranging from simple fairings to primary structures such as integrally stiffened wings and fuselage sections. Its work spans assembly fixtures, trimming and drill tooling for both manual and automated operations, and build-to-print manufacturing.

## Products and solutions

- **Composite tooling** - design and manufacture of tooling for composite aerospace structures, from fairings to primary wing and fuselage components.
- **Assembly and ICY fixtures** - precision fixtures for airframe assembly operations.
- **Trimming and drill tooling** - tooling for manual and automated trimming and drilling in high-tolerance aerospace environments.
