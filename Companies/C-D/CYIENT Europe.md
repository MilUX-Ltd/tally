# CYIENT Europe

> UK subsidiary of Cyient Limited, an Indian-headquartered engineering and technology services group providing aerospace and defence engineering, avionics design, aerostructures, and digital services.

## What they do

CYIENT Europe Limited is the UK legal entity of Cyient Limited, incorporated in 1992 (as Infotech Enterprises Europe Ltd) and active with over 1,000 UK staff. The group delivers engineering design, embedded systems, digital aftermarket, and design-led manufacturing for the aerospace and defence sector. The EMEA headquarters is in London (High Holborn); a significant operational presence is maintained in Reading and Cheddar (Somerset), the latter carrying geospatial heritage from the 2016 Blom Aerofilms acquisition.

Cyient Limited is listed on Indian stock exchanges and reported group revenue of approximately $834 million in FY2025 across some 20,800 employees in 24 countries.

## Products and solutions

- **Avionics and embedded systems** - fly-by-wire, actuation, autopilot, collision avoidance, navigation, flight controls, battery management, RF and microwave electronics; systems engineering, design, prototyping, testing, and independent verification and validation.
- **Aerostructures** - metallic and composite design, stress analysis, and value engineering for OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers.
- **Defence electronics** - ITAR-compliant delivery centres; X-band transmit/receive modules for AESA radar (described as ITAR-free); Software Defined Radio for secure military communications.
- **UAV systems** - field-proven ISTAR, artillery target acquisition, battle damage assessment, and persistent surveillance via Cyient Solutions and Systems (a joint venture with BlueBird Aero Systems).
- **Digital aftermarket and MRO** - predictive maintenance, health monitoring, digital technical publications, and asset management.
- **Geospatial services** - aerial survey, mapping, and geospatial data products (Somerset site).
