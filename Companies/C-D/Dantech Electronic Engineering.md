# Dantech Electronic Engineering

> UK designer and manufacturer of electronic power supplies, security hardware, and access-control equipment, serving government and critical infrastructure security integrators.

## What they do

Dantech Electronic Engineering, trading name of Benham (General Engineers) Limited, designs and manufactures electronic security and life-safety products at its facility in Maldon, Essex. Products include power supplies, power management equipment, relay modules, indicators, request-to-exit switches, door interlocks, and equipment-mounting solutions. The company is a named technology partner of Axis Communications and distributes through ADI Global and Norbain. Dantech exhibits at Security and Policing, the Home Office's invitation-only event at Farnborough, confirming engagement with the government security market.

## Products and solutions

- **Power supplies and power management** - a range of 12V and 24V DC and 24V AC power supply units for access control, CCTV, and security systems.
- **Access control ancillaries** - relay modules, door interlocks, request-to-exit switches, and indicator panels.
- **Axis housing and power solutions** - specialist enclosures and power feeds for Axis Communications network door-controller hardware.
- **Bespoke and turnkey engineering** - custom electronic assemblies built to customer specification.
