# Dash-CAE Ltd

> Composite engineering, additive manufacturing and robotics for aerospace, defence, automotive and motorsport.

> [!note] Description note
> Public sources describe Dash-CAE as a composite engineering and manufacturing business rather than a data-acquisition and instrumentation firm. The entry reflects the public self-description.

## What they do

Dash-CAE is a composite engineering and manufacturing company based in Abingdon, Oxfordshire. It combines engineering design and development with composites, robotics and additive manufacturing technology, and works across the automotive, motorsport, aerospace, defence, marine and heritage sectors. The company designs and produces complex, high-strength composite parts, components and tooling.

## Products and solutions

- **Composite engineering and manufacturing** - design and production of carbon fibre and advanced composite parts and components for weight reduction and performance.
- **Additive manufacturing** - 3D-printed parts and tooling, including large-format additive manufacturing (LFAM) for composite tooling produced at speed.
