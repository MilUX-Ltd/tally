# Domo Tactical Communications (DTC)

> UK-based designer and manufacturer of tactical IP mesh communications, software-defined radios, and covert audio and video surveillance solutions for defence, law enforcement, and unmanned systems markets.

## What they do

DTC develops and manufactures mission-critical communications and surveillance technology for demanding operational environments. Its products span tactical IP mesh radio networks, software-defined radios, covert audio and video equipment, and command-and-control software. The company has operated in this space for over 50 years and serves customers in more than 150 countries across military, law enforcement, intelligence, and unmanned vehicle platforms on land, sea, and air.

DTC is owned by Codan Limited, an Australian publicly-listed company, with the UK entity registered in Whiteley, Hampshire.

## Products and solutions

- **NETNode IP Mesh Radios** - multi-role tactical mesh radio nodes for fixed, mobile, and unmanned vehicle installations, supporting real-time video, voice, and data sharing across distributed networks.
- **Software-Defined Radios (SDRs)** - compact transceivers operating across UHF to 5 GHz, carrying DTC's MeshUltra and MeshUltra-X MIMO MANET waveforms; designed for UGV, UAV, and USV integration.
- **Covert Audio and Video Solutions** - surveillance-grade audio and video capture and transmission equipment for intelligence and law enforcement operations.
- **Command and Control Software** - strategic C2 software for managing surveillance and communications networks.
