# David Brown Santasalo UK

> Specialist in mechanical power transmission systems for land and naval defence platforms.

## What they do

David Brown Santasalo designs, manufactures, tests, and provides through-life support for gear systems and drivetrain solutions serving land and naval defence customers. Operating from Huddersfield, the company supplies transmissions for armoured fighting vehicles and propulsion gearboxes for surface ships and submarines, with over 10,000 gear systems delivered globally across defence programmes. The David Brown brand in defence dates to 1860; the Santasalo merger completed in 2016. The company is owned by Stellex Capital Management (US private equity, defence-focused) following acquisition in March 2023.

## Products and solutions

- **TN54E Automatic Transmission** - fully automatic transmission fitted to the Challenger 2 main battle tank and CRAARV recovery vehicle.
- **BOXER MIV Powerpack** - powerpack assembly (engine, transmission, cooling) for the UK Army's BOXER Mechanised Infantry Vehicle, supplied under a 10-year programme with WFEL and KMW.
- **Naval propulsion gearboxes** - propulsion gearboxes for frigates, destroyers, aircraft carriers, and submarines.
