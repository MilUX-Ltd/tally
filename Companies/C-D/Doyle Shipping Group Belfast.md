# Doyle Shipping Group Belfast

> Port services, stevedoring, customs clearance, warehousing, and freight forwarding operating out of Belfast, Larne, and Foyle.

## What they do

Doyle Shipping Group (Belfast) is the Northern Ireland operation of the Doyle Shipping Group (DSG), an Irish family-owned shipping and logistics company founded in 1886. The Belfast entity offers ship agency, stevedoring and heavy lifting, customs clearance, warehousing, and project freight forwarding across Belfast, Larne, and Foyle ports. DSG Belfast played a prominent role in Brexit-related customs and trade matters for the group and carries particular expertise in project freight forwarding for complex cargo movements.

## Services

- **Ship Agency** - port agency and vessel husbandry services at Belfast, Larne, and Foyle.
- **Stevedoring and Heavy Lifting** - cargo handling, loading, and unloading of client vessels.
- **Customs Clearance** - import and export customs management, including post-Brexit NI protocol matters.
- **Warehousing and Storage** - bulk and general warehousing at Belfast port.
- **Project Freight Forwarding** - worldwide logistics for project and abnormal cargo.
