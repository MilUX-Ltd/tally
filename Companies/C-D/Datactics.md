# Datactics

> Data quality and matching software for regulated industries and government, with security-cleared engineers and a track record in national policing and public sector programmes.

## What they do

Datactics develops low-code, self-service data quality and matching software designed for non-technical users across financial services and government. The platform handles data wrangling, classification, quality management, and fuzzy matching at scale. Engineers are security cleared and the company has delivered programmes for highly sensitive government environments.

## Products and solutions

- **DQMatch** - data quality and matching platform for regulated and government data types, configurable without coding.
- **NDQIS (National Data Quality Improvement Service)** - nationwide private cloud system for crime classification and reporting, delivered in partnership with the UK Home Office and national police forces.
- **GovTech solutions** - data quality and automation services tailored to public sector requirements.
