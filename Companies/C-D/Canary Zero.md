# Canary Zero

> UK OPSEC, COMSEC, and INFOSEC consultancy offering operational security, communications security, and information security services to high-profile individuals and private offices.

## What they do

Canary Zero provides security and privacy services across three disciplines: operational security (OPSEC), communications security (COMSEC), and information security (INFOSEC). The company's personnel have backgrounds in former UKIC and British Military operations. Clients are primarily high-profile individuals, families, and private or family offices requiring bespoke security. Services are delivered as a concierge model covering tailored threat modelling, digital footprint auditing, adversary profiling, managed digital identities, secure travel planning, and real-time threat intelligence. The company also holds a registered presence in Dubai.

## Products and solutions

- **OPSEC Services** - Digital footprint auditing, threat modelling, adversary profiling, tailored posting protocols, managed digital identities, and secure travel planning.
- **COMSEC Services** - Securing client communications against interception and unauthorised access.
- **INFOSEC Services** - Safeguarding data against compromise, alteration, or loss.
