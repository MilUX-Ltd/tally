# Decision Analysis Services

> Independent management consultancy applying systems thinking, programme management, and data analysis to defence, energy, government, and critical infrastructure.

## What they do

Decision Analysis Services (DAS) is an independent management consultancy operating across Defence and Security, Energy, Government, Transport, Health, and Critical Minerals. The company supports defence organisations to make resilient acquisition decisions, improve strategic decision-making, and navigate complex programme environments. DAS works with defence system integrators and a range of government departments across the UK, North America, Europe, Japan, South Africa, and Australasia.

## Products and solutions

- **Programme Management and PMO** - structured programme and portfolio management support for defence acquisition and major programmes.
- **Investment Modelling** - quantitative modelling to support complex investment and capability decisions.
- **AI and Data Analytics** - data analysis and decision-support tooling for government and defence clients.
- **Systems Thinking** - complex systems engineering and analysis applied to critical national infrastructure and defence programmes.
