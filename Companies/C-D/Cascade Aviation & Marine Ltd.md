# Cascade Aviation & Marine Ltd

> Tactical equipment supplier for military and professional operators across land, sea and air.

## What they do

Cascade Aviation & Marine supplies specialist tactical equipment to military and professional operators across the land, maritime and air domains. Its range covers situational awareness systems, night vision and thermal imaging, parachute and aerial delivery systems, diving equipment, unmanned aerial vehicles and protective equipment. The company holds a NATO Commercial and Government Entity (NCAGE) code.

## Products and solutions

- **Situational awareness** - ATAK mesh networking and asset tracking.
- **Optronics** - night vision and thermal imaging equipment.
- **Parachute systems** - tactical, cargo delivery and high-altitude oxygen systems.
- **Maritime and diving equipment** - underwater and diving kit.
- **UAS** - networked unmanned aerial vehicles.
- **Protective equipment** - ballistic and fragmentation protection and thermal garments.
