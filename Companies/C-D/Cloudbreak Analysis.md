# Cloudbreak Analysis

> British provider of intelligence analysis and open-source intelligence training and consultancy.

## What they do

Cloudbreak Analysis is a British company specialising in intelligence analysis training and consultancy, delivered in the UK, online and internationally. It builds intelligence analysis capability in public and private sector organisations and delivers online investigation and analysis services. Its team holds current SC and DV security clearances.

## Products and solutions

- **Intelligence analysis training** - a portfolio of courses covering Open Source Intelligence (OSINT), Social Media Intelligence (SOCMINT), Strategic Intelligence Analysis and Structured Analytical Techniques.
- **Tool training** - courses on IBM i2 Analyst's Notebook and IBM iBase.
- **Investigation and consultancy** - online investigation and intelligence analysis services delivered via secure platforms, with research available in multiple languages.
