# Delta Tango

> UK manufacturer of waterproof, military-grade FPV drones and counter-drone payloads.

## What they do

Delta Tango designs and manufactures FPV UAS and associated payloads for defence, public safety, and export markets. Products are IP67-rated waterproof with extended operational ranges, positioned for attack and reconnaissance scenarios. The company exhibits at DroneX in London and markets an Africa export product line alongside its core range.

## Products and solutions

- **10-inch FPV Drone** - Waterproof (IP67), 25 km stated flight range, 5.8 GHz video at 30 W; positioned for attack and defence scenarios.
- **Three-axis Thermal Camera Drone** - Combined thermal infrared and full-HD low-light camera with dimmable LED illuminator.
- **50 W Onboard Jamming System** - Counter-drone payload stated effective to approximately 6 metres; controlled under the Wireless Telegraphy Act 2006 in the UK.
- **Drone-mounted Megaphone** - Stated audio range up to 5 km; marketed at public safety and emergency response.
- **Object Detection Software** - Real-time target tracking and analysis.
