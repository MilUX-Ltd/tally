# Cellebrite DI Ltd.

> Digital intelligence company providing digital forensics and investigation tools to law enforcement, government and enterprise.

## What they do

Cellebrite is a digital intelligence company founded in Israel in 1999 and headquartered in Petah Tikva. It produces tools for the collection, analysis and management of digital data, used by law enforcement, intelligence agencies, military branches, corporate security and investigations. The company is listed on NASDAQ (CLBT) and operates offices across the US, Europe, the UK, Asia Pacific and Latin America. Its largest shareholder is Sun Corporation of Japan.

## Products and solutions

- **Mobile and device forensics** - collection and extraction of data from mobile devices and other digital sources.
- **Investigation and analytics platform** - tools to analyse and manage digital evidence across investigations, extended through recent acquisitions of Corellium (Arm-based mobile virtualisation) and SCG Canada (UAV/drone forensics).
