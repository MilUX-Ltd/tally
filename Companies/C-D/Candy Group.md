# Candy Group

> Advisory, equipment supply and secure logistics firm serving government, law-enforcement and defence customers.

## What they do

Candy Group provides advisory services, security and defence equipment supply, and secure logistics to government, law-enforcement and defence organisations. Its work covers project design, proposal and project management, compliance and ITAR-related advice, and the procurement, licensing and delivery of equipment. The company handles the transport of security-sensitive, hazardous and high-value cargoes.

## Products and solutions

- **Advise** - advisory and project-delivery services for government and defence customers, including compliance and ITAR matters.
- **Equip** - security and defence equipment solutions, including procurement, licensing, delivery and product support across a range of manufacturers, delivered through a US-based team.
- **Supply Solutions and Deliver** - secure logistics and transport of security-sensitive, hazardous and high-value cargoes.
- **Odin Sense** - remote sensor technology integration for awareness in complex environments.
