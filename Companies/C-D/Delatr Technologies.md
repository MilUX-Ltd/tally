# Delatr Technologies

> Quantum-safe encryption for drone command-and-control links.

## What they do

Delatr Technologies develops post-quantum cryptography for military communications, focused on securing drone command-and-control (C2) links across air, land and sea platforms. Its product, Lineage, is designed to be quantum-safe from the outset and to maintain message delivery through jamming, signal loss and bit errors. It works across common data formats including MAVLink and STANAG 4586.

## Products and solutions

- **Lineage** - post-quantum encryption for drone C2 links, adding minimal packet overhead and supporting multiple message and video formats.
