# Chiltern Global

> Supplier of specialist bespoke equipment for the defence, police and security sectors.

## What they do

Chiltern Global supplies specialist equipment for the defence, police and security sectors, providing bespoke manufacturing solutions for service personnel. The company has supplied bespoke products to the defence industry since 2005 and operates from its head office in Chesham, with additional offices in the United Arab Emirates and China.

## Products and solutions

- **Load carriage systems** - bespoke load carriage equipment.
- **Protective clothing and ballistic solutions** - protective clothing and ballistic products.
- **Riot control and survival equipment** - riot control equipment and outdoor survival kit.
- **Camouflage and concealment** - camouflage concealment systems.
