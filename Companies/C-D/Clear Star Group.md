# Clear Star Group

> Organisational transformation and portfolio management consultancy serving UK central government and defence, founded by a team with direct MOD experience.

## What they do

Clear Star Group delivers lean portfolio management, operating model design, agile delivery, leadership development, and transformation programmes for UK public sector and defence clients. The founding team brings direct MOD experience: one co-founder served as Deputy Principal Staff Officer to the Chief of the Defence Staff and as Head of Defence Commitments and Crisis; another spent 20 years in Crown Service across central government and national security. The firm positions as a specialist alternative to large incumbent consultancies.

## Products and solutions

- **Lean Portfolio Management** - Structured approach to aligning investment and delivery capacity to organisational strategy.
- **Operating Model Design** - Service and organisational design for government transformation programmes.
- **Agile Delivery and Training** - ICAgile-accredited training across four certified courses; Kotter Certified Change Leadership.
