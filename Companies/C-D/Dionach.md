# Dionach

> CREST-approved cybersecurity consultancy with a dedicated defence and aerospace practice; now part of the Nomios Group.

## What they do

Dionach is a CREST-approved cybersecurity consultancy with over 25 years of experience delivering assurance, compliance and incident response services. It holds NCSC CHECK company status for penetration testing and serves defence contractors, aerospace manufacturers and government and MoD suppliers, helping them manage cyber risk across IT, OT and embedded environments. In October 2024, Dionach was acquired by Nomios, a European cybersecurity and network-services group. The company retains its UK offices in Oxford and Glasgow.

## Products and solutions

- **Penetration testing** - network, web application and threat-led penetration testing; NCSC CHECK-certified.
- **OT/ICS/SCADA testing** - security assessment of operational technology and industrial control systems in defence and aerospace environments.
- **Red team and adversary simulation** - red team assessments, assumed breach exercises and purple teaming.
- **Compliance services** - ISO 27001, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials, NIST, NIS2 and MoD supplier assurance support.
- **Solas Platform** - proprietary cyber risk management platform.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** CREST (penetration testing, incident response, vulnerability assessment); NCSC CHECK; ISO 27001; ISO 9001; Cyber Essentials Plus; PCI QSA.
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered; Crown Commercial Service supplier.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
