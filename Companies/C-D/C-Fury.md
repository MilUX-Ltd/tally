# C-Fury

> UK manufacturer of hydrofoiling rigid inflatable boats, including the Patrol AWC variant supplied to the Ministry of Defence.

## What they do

C-Fury designs and manufactures twin-hulled hydrofoil RIBs from a facility in Herefordshire. Their hull technology combines a catamaran form, sub-hull air tunnels, and an assister hydrofoil to deliver lift, stability, and fuel efficiency at speed. The Patrol AWC (F70) variant is purpose-built for professional and public-sector maritime operations, with a heavier lay-up, open deck, and Yamaha F40 powerplant capable of over 40 knots. Confirmed professional customers include the UK Ministry of Defence and the Port of London Authority.

## Products and solutions

- **Patrol AWC F70** - Professional patrol hydrofoil RIB for public-sector and maritime operations.
- **Voyager** - Leisure and adventure hydrofoil RIB.
- **Storm** - Leisure and adventure hydrofoil RIB.
- **Yacht** - Leisure and adventure hydrofoil RIB.
