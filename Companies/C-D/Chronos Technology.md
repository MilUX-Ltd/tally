# Chronos Technology

> Specialist supplier and installer of GNSS, timing and synchronisation systems, including for defence and security customers.

## What they do

Chronos Technology designs, installs and supports GNSS (GPS, GLONASS, Galileo and BeiDou) and time synchronisation systems. The company supplies simulators, repeaters, receivers, antennas and infrastructure, and works in positioning, navigation and timing (PNT). Customers span defence and security, telecom, energy, transport, broadcast and finance. Chronos has supplied and installed GNSS repeating and distribution systems for the Ministry of Defence and RAF, including for maintenance hangars, Quick Reaction Alert shelters and military vehicles. The company also works in GPS jamming detection and location.

Chronos transitioned to employee ownership through the Chronos Technology Employee Ownership Trust in 2022. It is based at Lower Lydbrook in the Forest of Dean, Gloucestershire.

## Products and solutions

- **GNSS and GPS systems** - simulators, repeaters, receivers, antennas and infrastructure for GNSS reception and distribution.
- **GNSS repeating and distribution** - indoor GNSS coverage for test facilities, maintenance hangars, QRA shelters and inside land, air and marine vehicles.
- **Timing and synchronisation** - PTP, NTP and SyncE based resilient timing for critical national infrastructure.
- **Interference detection** - GPS jamming detection and location technology.
