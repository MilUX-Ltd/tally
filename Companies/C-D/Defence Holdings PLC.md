# Defence Holdings PLC

> London-listed, software-led defence-technology holding company building and acquiring sovereign defence capability.

## What they do

Defence Holdings PLC is a London-listed, software-led defence-technology holding company building and acquiring sovereign defence and national-security capability for the UK and European markets. It positions itself as a listed, software-led defence platform developing and acquiring AI-enabled sensing, secure communications, autonomous drone systems and cyber-defence across land, sea, air, space and cyber domains. It operates as a platform and holding model and runs a defence technology accelerator, rather than as a single point-solution vendor. The entity was formerly the cash shell Cassell Capital Plc and rebranded to Defence Holdings PLC in May 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **Stated capability pillars** - drone warfare and aggregation, AI agents for defence operations, information and influence warfare, critical infrastructure defence, and a national security pillar.
- **Defence Tech Accelerator** - programme for early-stage defence and national-security startups.
