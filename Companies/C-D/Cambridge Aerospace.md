# Cambridge Aerospace Limited

> UK defence-technology company developing low-cost air and missile defence interceptors to counter drones and missiles.

## What they do

Cambridge Aerospace is a UK defence-technology scale-up founded in 2024, focused on sovereign air and missile defence for the UK and allied customers. Its stated mission is to "eliminate the risk of existential war in Europe", developing scalable, low-cost interceptors against drone and cruise-missile threats. It is also establishing UK solid-rocket-motor manufacturing to underpin its own systems and supply chain.

## Products and solutions

- **Skyhammer** - tube-launched interceptor against slower, lower-value threats such as Shahed-style drones and subsonic missiles. Reported speed around Mach 0.7, range over 30 km, unit cost in the tens of thousands of dollars.
- **Starhammer** - interceptor for faster, higher-value targets.
- **Nightstar** - solid rocket motors, to be produced at a planned Norfolk facility for the company's own use and potential third-party sale.
