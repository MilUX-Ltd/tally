# CHAOS Industries

> US defence-technology company building distributed radar and sensing networks that detect airborne threats faster than traditional radar.

## What they do

CHAOS Industries develops a multi-product portfolio of advanced sensing and detection systems powered by its Coherent Distributed Networks (CDN) technology. The products are aimed at warfighters, commercial air operators and border-protection teams, detecting and tracking drones, missiles and aircraft. The company describes itself as built by US veterans and Silicon Valley engineers, and is classified under Defense and Space Manufacturing.

## Products and solutions

- **Coherent Distributed Networks (CDN)** - flagship technology category. A self-forming network of time-synchronised sensor nodes using two-way time transfer and cooperative data processing, interoperable with command-and-control systems.
- **Vanquish (CH001)** - distributed early-warning radar; expeditionary, low size, weight and power, for early warning and tracking against UAS, missiles and aircraft.
- **Astria (CH002)** - long-range radar for high-precision tracking and extended-range surveillance.
