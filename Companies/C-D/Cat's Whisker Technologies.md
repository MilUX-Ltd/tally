# Cat's Whisker Technologies

> Designs and manufactures technical surveillance equipment for government, military and law-enforcement customers.

## What they do

Cat's Whisker Technologies designs and manufactures technical surveillance equipment for government, homeland security, military and law-enforcement agencies. Its in-house capabilities cover electronics, software and mechanical engineering, alongside UAV consultancy. The company is based in Ashtead, Surrey, and supplies customers and sales partners internationally.

## Products and solutions

- **Technical surveillance equipment** - covert and technical surveillance products for government, military and law-enforcement use.
- **Engineering services** - electronics, software and mechanical engineering.
- **UAV consultancy** - advisory work on unmanned aerial systems.
