# CCPI Europe

> A manufacturer of temperature measurement sensors and a provider of UKAS-accredited calibration for the aerospace and defence industry.

## What they do

CCPI Europe supplies high-accuracy temperature measurement and calibration solutions for the aerospace and defence industry. Its products include thermocouples, RTDs and reference sensors used across heat treatment, forging, composites and materials processing. The company runs a UKAS-accredited calibration laboratory and supports aerospace special-process requirements such as AMS2750 and NADCAP-aligned practices. It is based at Vector 31 Business Park in Sheffield.

## Products and solutions

- **Thermocouples and RTDs** - temperature sensors for heat treatment, forging and materials processing.
- **Aero engine test cables** - thermocouple cabling for engine and aerospace testing.
- **Calibration services** - UKAS-accredited calibration of thermocouples, resistance thermometers and associated instrumentation.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group, North West Aerospace Alliance
