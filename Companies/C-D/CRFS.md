# CRFS Limited

> Cambridge-based manufacturer of RF spectrum monitoring and geolocation systems for military, intelligence, and national security customers.

## What they do

CRFS designs, builds, and deploys hardware and software for detecting, monitoring, and geolocating RF signals across congested and contested electromagnetic environments. The company serves military and EW operators, security and intelligence agencies, system integrators, and national spectrum regulators. In September 2025 CRFS was acquired by Motorola Solutions as part of its purchase of Silvus Technologies.

## Products and solutions

- **RFeye Node** - Portable, rugged RF sensors for real-time 24/7 spectrum monitoring and geolocation, covering frequencies up to 40 GHz; deployed on unmanned systems, integrated vehicles, and fixed installations.
- **RFeye Array** - Direction-finding antenna arrays (100 and 300 series) for signal geolocation.
- **RFeye Guard** - Technical surveillance countermeasures (TSCM) sensor.
- **RFeye Suite (Site / Mission Manager / DeepView)** - Software platform for spectrum analysis, mission management, and advanced signal intelligence analytics.
- **RFeye SenS Portable** - I/Q data recorder for RF signal capture.
