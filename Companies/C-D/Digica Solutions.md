# Digica Solutions

> AI and software engineering company with a dedicated defence practice, covering ISR, autonomous systems, sensor fusion and computer vision.

## What they do

Digica builds AI and software solutions in cloud, IoT and embedded systems for industries including defence, automotive, healthcare and semiconductors. Its defence practice focuses on mission-critical AI that operates with sparse data, covering intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance, autonomous systems, chemical analysis and fault prediction. The company holds offices in the UK, Poland and the USA, and describes itself as owner-led.

## Products and solutions

- **Military-grade DPV detectors** - deep-learning training pipeline capable of detecting and differentiating drones, people and vehicles from petabytes of sensor data.
- **Target tracking and re-identification** - integrated surveillance system combining thermal cameras, visual-light cameras and motion detectors for multi-source target tracking.
- **Automatic payload monitoring** - GPU-accelerated method that detects which equipment payloads (fuel, ammunition) are being loaded onto a UGV.
- **Unified battlefield system** - fuses visual-light and thermal camera feeds, radar, motion detectors and target classifiers into a single surveillance and observation interface.
- **Chemical analysis module** - ML segmentation model trained to detect reacted surfaces indicating chemical contamination on military vehicles, with display via military-grade VR helmet.
