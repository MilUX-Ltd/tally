# Camcode Global

> Provider of item-level asset identification, unique identification marking and traceability for defence using NATO standards.

## What they do

Camcode Global is a defence technology company specialising in item-level traceability and asset identification using NATO standards. As a NATO and Partnership for Peace approved partner, it works to the STANAG 2290 / S2000 framework and unique identification (UID) to support asset tracking across the lifecycle. It was established in 2012 and has offices in the UK, the Netherlands, the USA and Australia.

## Products and solutions

- **UID marking and asset identification** - durable item-level marking and identification meeting standards including CDS-751, DEF STAN 05-132, MIL-STD-130, DEF(AUST) 1000C and DAOD 3038-0, delivered with an integrated mobile laser marking cart solution.
- **Data cleansing** - asset data cleansing services to support traceability.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** NATO and Partnership for Peace approved partner; works to STANAG 2290 / S2000, DEF STAN 05-132 and MIL-STD-130
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
