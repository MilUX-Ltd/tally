# Cherton Secure

> Veteran-owned provider of security risk mitigation services to government, business and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Cherton Secure is a veteran-owned and operated company delivering security risk mitigation solutions to government, business and critical national infrastructure. Services span physical security, threat intelligence, information security and incident response, covering organisations' physical, digital and technical environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Security advisory consultancy** - strategic threat identification and assessment, geopolitical and regional risk analysis, risk monitoring and early warning, and insider threat advisory.
- **Corporate due diligence and investigations** - investigation strategy design and delivery, drawing on open-source intelligence (OSINT) and liaison with law enforcement and government bodies.
- **Organisational risk and resilience** - resilience planning, crisis management frameworks and business continuity and contingency planning.
- **Incident and crisis response** - incident advisory, crisis containment and post-incident review.
