# Cundall Johnston & Partners LLP

> Global multi-disciplinary engineering consultancy with a dedicated defence sector practice serving the UK defence estate.

## What they do

Cundall is an independent multi-disciplinary engineering and design consultancy with over 1,200 engineers and designers operating across 29 locations globally. In defence, the company works directly with the defence authorities, as framework suppliers, and with contractors, delivering projects across the UK defence estate. Work includes net zero carbon pathways, estate masterplanning, accommodation, technical facilities and training facilities. The company's focus within defence is supporting the estate transition to zero carbon, applying circular economy principles and assessing operational and embodied carbon emissions in designs.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence estate engineering** - multi-disciplinary engineering and design across accommodation, technical buildings and training facilities on MOD sites.
- **Net zero carbon pathways** - decarbonisation strategies and design for the defence estate.
- **Estate masterplanning** - masterplanning services for defence authorities and DIO contractors.
