# CoMech Metrology

> Precision calibration and measurement provider serving regulated sectors including defence and aerospace.

## What they do

CoMech Metrology provides verification, calibration and precision measurement services from custom-built laboratories in Derby. It works across rail, aerospace, defence, automotive, manufacturing and other regulated sectors. Services can be delivered by sending equipment to the laboratories or by on-site engineer visits, and the company supplies asset management software for calibration compliance.

## Products and solutions

- **Laboratory calibration** - electrical, mechanical, pressure, dimensional, torque, force and mass calibration delivered in UKAS-accredited laboratories.
- **On-site services** - on-site calibration, hand-arm vibration assessment and PAT testing.
- **Asset management software** - a tracking app and software for calibration records and audit compliance.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** UKAS ISO/IEC 17025:2017 (laboratories); ISO 9001:2015; Cyber Essentials.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
