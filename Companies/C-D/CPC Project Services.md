# CPC Project Services (UK) LLP

> Project and programme management consultancy with a defence and security practice.

## What they do

CPC Project Services provides technical expertise to deliver infrastructure, property and transformational change projects across the UK and Ireland. It runs a dedicated defence and security practice, providing project, programme and portfolio level consultancy advice drawing on more than 30 years of delivery in public and private sectors. The firm acts as a supplier to public and private sector organisations and procurement agencies.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence and security consultancy** - independent project, programme and portfolio advice for defence and security clients.
- **Infrastructure and property project services** - technical advisory across infrastructure, property and transformational change programmes.
