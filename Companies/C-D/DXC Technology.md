# DXC Technology

> IT services and solutions provider with over four decades of UK aerospace and defence experience, delivering mission-critical systems for the MOD and defence industry.

## What they do

DXC Technology provides IT services, consulting, and solutions to defence, aerospace, and public sector organisations. In the UK, the company operates through its subsidiary Enterprise Services Defence and Security UK Limited and is the sole organisation accredited by the Military Aviation Authority to design, modify, and sustain the Advanced Mission Planning Aid (AMPA) system for the Royal Air Force. DXC has a presence of more than 6,500 people across the UK and opened a dedicated Aerospace and Defense Hub in Farnborough in June 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **Advanced Mission Planning Aid (AMPA)** - Ground-based mission planning system providing computerised support for Typhoon, Hercules, Storm Shadow, Hawk TMk1, and Hawk TMk2 aircrew for mission planning, rehearsal, and debriefing.
- **Aerospace and Defense IT Solutions** - AI-powered IT modernisation, cloud transformation, and digital engineering services for defence primes and government customers.
- **Military Training and Simulation** - In partnership with Hadean, development of next-generation training, planning, and data exploitation capabilities for defence.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Military Aviation Authority Design Organisation approval (AMPA)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
