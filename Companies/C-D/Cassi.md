# Cassi

> AI-powered probabilistic forecasting and decision support platform for defence and government.

## What they do

Cassi is a UK AI startup building a probabilistic forecasting and decision support engine for high-stakes environments. The company has a core defence and government focus: its CEO, Dr Keith Dear, is a former RAF Intelligence Officer and former Expert Adviser to the Prime Minister on Science, Technology, Space, and Modernising Defence. Cassi submitted AI forecasting evidence to Parliament's Defence Select Committee and actively engages on autonomous systems, military AI strategy, and UK defence modernisation.

## Products and solutions

- **Cassi superstrategy engine** - Probabilistic decision support platform that forecasts outcomes, quantifies risk, and recommends strategies. Benchmarked as a leading AI forecaster on ForecastBench at time of Parliamentary submission.
