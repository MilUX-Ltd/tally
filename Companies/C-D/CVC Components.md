# CVC Components Limited

> Essex-based obsolescence supply-chain specialist stocking over 100 million hard-to-find legacy, military, avionic, radar, and electronic components.

## What they do

CVC Components, part of the UKC Group, is one of the UK's largest stockists of obsolete, discontinued, and hard-to-find electronic and mechanical components for military, avionic, radar, industrial, and commercial applications. Operating from a purpose-built 45,000 sq ft secure storage facility on a 40-acre site in Latchingdon, Essex, the company supplies government, defence, and commercial customers worldwide. Capabilities extend to sourcing, reverse engineering, testing and analysis services, and small-batch manufacturing when replacement components are globally unavailable. The business traces its origins to Chelmer Valve Co Ltd, established in 1961, and rebranded as CVC Components in 2018.

## Products and solutions

- **Obsolete and legacy component supply** - over 100 million electronic and mechanical components in stock, covering semiconductors, integrated circuits, connectors, resistors, capacitors, transistors, diodes, circuit breakers, synchro motors, and NATO-numbered (NSN) items for military ships, aircraft, land vehicles, and radar systems.
- **Sourcing services** - international network-based sourcing of hard-to-find and obsolete parts not held in stock.
- **Reverse engineering** - engineering and manufacturing of replacement components when originals are globally unavailable.
- **Testing and analysis** - independent component testing and verification services.
