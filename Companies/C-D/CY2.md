# CY2 Ltd

> Cyber security consultancy, training and exercising firm based in Cheltenham, with proprietary exercise management and learning platforms.

## What they do

CY2 delivers cyber security consultancy, training and exercising in hyper-realistic environments, alongside STEM outreach programmes across the UK. The company develops and sells its own software products to support cyber exercise planning and workforce development. Research and development underpins its service offering.

## Products and solutions

- **Exmato** - ISO 22398-aligned exercise management application supporting all exercise types.
- **Arena** - Interoperable learning management system designed for learners, mentors, teams and recruitment.
- **Cyber Security Consultancy** - Penetration testing, forensic investigation and related advisory services.
- **Cyber Training and Exercising** - Design, delivery and continual improvement of cyber exercises.
