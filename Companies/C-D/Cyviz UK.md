# Cyviz UK

> UK subsidiary of Oslo-listed Norwegian technology company Cyviz AS, designing and delivering high-performance control rooms, command-and-control centres, and deployable visualisation systems for defence, government, and enterprise.

## What they do

Cyviz Limited (trading as Cyviz UK Ltd) is the British entity of Cyviz AS, incorporated in Scotland in 2012 and registered at Dalkeith, Midlothian. The parent is listed on Euronext Growth (Oslo, ticker: CYVIZ) and operates across seven countries. Cyviz builds integrated control room and C2 environments combining video wall infrastructure, data visualisation, and a software management platform for remote monitoring and multi-site management. The company is in an active strategic transition from system integrator to software and platform provider.

The company claims over 1,500 control room deployments across 80-plus countries and serves NATO bodies, governments, and major commercial enterprise customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Integrated control room and C2 systems** - turnkey hardware and software environments for joint operation centres, security operation centres, and emergency operation centres; supports Tempest A/B certifiable and Common Criteria EAL4 certifiable architectures.
- **Deployable and containerised command centres** - mobile, ruggedised C2 capability for expeditionary and field use; demonstrated 24-hour deployment during a NATO-aligned Dutch military exercise (June 2025).
- **Software Management Platform** - remote monitoring, management, and multi-site control of AV and visualisation infrastructure; the core platform-as-product offering.
- **Design and professional services** - site design, systems integration, and ongoing managed support.
