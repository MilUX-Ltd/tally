# C2 Risk Ltd

> UK risk and compliance software company providing a platform for third-party, vendor and cyber risk management.

## What they do

C2 Risk provides a risk management platform for organisations to assess, identify and mitigate risks across supplier, compliance, privacy and cyber programmes. The company draws on experience across the defence, public and private sectors and works with government and public sector organisations as well as commercial clients.

## Products and solutions

- **RiskStore** - online platform for managing risk, vendor and third-party assessments, and compliance in one place.
