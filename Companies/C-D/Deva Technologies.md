# Deva Technologies

> North Wales engineering design company specialising in safety-critical electronics, motor control and electromechanical actuation for aerospace and defence applications.

## What they do

Deva Technologies Limited is an independent engineering design and manufacturing company established in 1993 and based in Wrexham, North Wales. The company designs, develops and manufactures bespoke electromechanical actuators, motor control electronics, electronic sub-assemblies and contactless position sensors for flight control surfaces and secondary actuation systems. It serves first and second tier customers across UK, European and international aerospace and defence markets (civil and military, land, sea and air). The team provides DO-254 electronics and DO-178 safety-critical software design and verification, as well as pre-qualification EMI/EMC screening services.

## Products and solutions

- **Electromechanical actuators** - bespoke actuators for flight control surfaces and secondary actuation systems, including UAV and drone flight surface actuation systems, qualified for harsh environments.
- **Motor control and drive systems** - precision embedded servo motor drives and power electronics for aerospace and defence platforms.
- **DO-254/DO-178 design and verification services** - safety-critical electronics hardware and software design, documentation and pre-qualification EMI/EMC screening for development and production programmes.
- **Bespoke electronic test equipment** - custom test and interface boxes built to customer requirements; legacy electronics assembly upgrade reviews.
