# C3IA Solutions

> Cyber security consultancy and NCSC Assured service provider serving UK defence, government and commercial customers.

## What they do

C3IA designs, delivers and assures critical information and communication systems for customers where high availability and resilience are absolute requirements. It works across UK defence, government and the commercial sector, with particular depth in MoD acquisition processes and Secure by Design. Its stated purpose is "Connecting People. Securing Systems. Protecting Data."

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber security services** - Cyber Essentials certification body, penetration testing, security testing and IT health checks, Security as a Service, cloud security, counter eavesdropping, audit and compliance.
- **Security and risk consultancy** - security management, GovAssure, supply chain assurance, business continuity and risk consultancy.
- **Secure by Design** - support for the MoD Secure by Design approach.
- **Project, programme and portfolio management (P3M)** - delivery and assurance services.
- **IT service management and systems engineering** - design and through-life support of secure systems.
- **Secure communications technologies** - UK Master Distributor for Silvus Technologies StreamCaster MANET radio (via C3IA Technologies); Instant Connect communications interoperability.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** NCSC Assured Cyber Security Service Provider; ISO 27001; ISO 9001; ISO 45001; Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus; JOSCAR registered; Crown Commercial Service supplier; Defence Cyber Certification (DCC) Level 2 accredited Certification Body.
- **Memberships:** techUK; Chartered Institute of Information Security (CIISec); Armed Forces Covenant signatory (Employer Recognition Scheme Gold Award).
