# Delta Mobrey

> UK manufacturer of precision process instrumentation for defence, nuclear, oil and gas, and safety-critical industrial applications.

## What they do

Delta Mobrey designs and manufactures process instrumentation for the measurement and control of fluids and gases, with a heritage in the field spanning over 100 years. The company was formed when Delta Controls Ltd acquired the Mobrey range of instruments from Emerson in 2019; all products are manufactured in the UK at its Camberley, Surrey site. Delta Mobrey has supported UK defence programmes since 1947, supplying instrumentation to land facilities and mobile weapons platforms including naval vessels and military vehicles. The company is JOSCAR accredited.

## Products and solutions

- **Pressure and differential pressure transmitters** - High-accuracy instruments for safety-critical and mission-critical environments including weapons systems and fuel management.
- **Level measurement systems** - Fuel tank measurement and fluid level monitoring for military vehicles, naval vessels and fixed installations.
- **Flow and temperature measurement** - Industrial instrumentation designed for harsh environments with high vibration and electromagnetic interference tolerance.
- **Switches and gauges** - Process monitoring and control devices with proven in-service longevity for defence applications.
