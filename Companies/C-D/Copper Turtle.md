# Copper Turtle

> Organisational transformation and project outcomes consultancy working with the UK defence innovation community.

## What they do

Copper Turtle is a Dorset-based consultancy specialising in organisational transformation for engineering and manufacturing organisations, covering culture, structure and strategy development. It works with customers to define project specifications from the outset and agree measurable outcomes, drawing on a network of partners for specialist expertise. The company is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab on Dorset Innovation Park, and has engaged with Army and NavyX innovation activity through that community.

## Products and solutions

- **Organisational transformation consultancy** - reviews of culture, structure and strategy for engineering and manufacturing organisations, aimed at improving how they adapt to change.
- **Project definition and outcomes** - working with customers to set project specifications and agree measurable outcomes from the outset.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** D3IP community, Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
