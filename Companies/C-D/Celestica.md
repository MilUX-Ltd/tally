# Celestica

> Global electronics manufacturing services provider with an aerospace and defence business and a UK manufacturing site at Telford.

## What they do

Celestica is a global electronics manufacturing services (EMS) company providing design, engineering, electronics manufacturing, hardware platform and supply chain services across the product lifecycle. It serves aerospace and defence, communications, enterprise and cloud, healthtech, industrial, capital equipment and smart energy markets. The business runs two segments: Advanced Technology Solutions, which contains the Aerospace and Defense unit, and Connectivity & Cloud Solutions. The UK arm trades as Celestica Limited (Companies House 03228362) and operates from the Castle Farm Campus in Telford, Shropshire; the ADS membership is held under the parent operating partnership Celestica International LP. The group is headquartered in Toronto and is listed on the New York and Toronto stock exchanges (CLS).

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace and defence manufacturing** - low-to-medium-volume, high-mix printed circuit assembly and box-build assembly for commercial, general aviation and military electronics.
- **System integration and test** - complex system integration, test and upgrade of electronic products.
- **Maintenance, repair and overhaul** - MRO and after-market services for electronic products in aviation and defence applications.
