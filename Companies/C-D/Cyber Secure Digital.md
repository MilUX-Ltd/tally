# Cyber Secure Digital

> Veteran-founded specialist cyber domain consultancy serving UK and allied governments, Critical National Infrastructure providers, and larger consultancies.

## What they do

Cyber Secure Digital provides cost-effective, agile and flexible cyber consultancy to UK and allied government clients, Critical National Infrastructure operators, and prime consultancies engaging specialist associates. The company was founded by veterans and draws on operational cyber experience across security, operations and resilience disciplines.
