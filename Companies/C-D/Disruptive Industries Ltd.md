# Disruptive Industries Ltd

> AI-driven threat intelligence and risk identification platform for defence, national security, and critical infrastructure customers.

## What they do

Disruptive Industries builds the Keystone threat discovery engine, which uses behavioural sequencing, geospatial analysis, and real-time anomaly detection to identify threats across proprietary data streams. Its ODDITY product monitors GPS interference and its ORBAT feed consolidates adversarial intelligence from multiple sources into a near-real-time single feed. The company targets defence and national security customers, positioning Keystone across air, land, sea, space, and cyber domains within a C5ISR framework.

## Products and solutions

- **Keystone** - AI-powered threat discovery engine; behavioural sequencing, geospatial analysis, real-time risk and anomaly detection. Listed on the G-Cloud Digital Marketplace.
- **ODDITY** - GPS interference monitoring and overlay feed; counter-UAS and electronic warfare relevance.
- **ORBAT** - Consolidated adversarial intelligence feed with one-hour refresh cadence; listed on the Digital Marketplace.
- **Curated Alerting** - Actionable tactical threat analysis including threats from illicit online communities.
- **Tailored Reporting** - Bespoke weekly or monthly intelligence reports.
