# CBRNE Ltd

> A specialist consultancy providing CBRNE and explosive ordnance resilience, training and advisory services.

## What they do

CBRNE Ltd provides advice, training and resilience services in the chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear and explosive (CBRNE) field. Its team draws on experience in explosive ordnance disposal (EOD) and CBRNE operations to deliver organisational resilience audits and related support to defence, government and commercial clients. The company is registered in London.
