# Curtiss-Wright Defense Solutions

> US-headquartered defence electronics division of Curtiss-Wright Corporation (NYSE: CW), with UK design and manufacturing facilities specialising in embedded computing, flight recorders, and video management systems.

## What they do

Curtiss-Wright Defense Solutions is a division of Curtiss-Wright Corporation, supplying rugged embedded computing, flight data recording, mission computing, and video management systems for military and commercial aerospace applications. The UK-based business, with facilities at Christchurch (Dorset) and Letchworth (Hertfordshire), designs and manufactures flight recorders, air data computers, mission computers, video recorders, and video management solutions. UK manufacturing capabilities include environmental stress screening and system integration of COTS cards into rugged enclosures, all qualified to AS9100. The Letchworth site has been a centre of excellence for defence and aerospace computing products since 1982.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged embedded computing** - COTS-based processor systems and application-ready computing platforms for unmanned vehicles, helicopters, missiles, and fixed-wing aircraft.
- **Flight data recorders** - airborne flight data and cockpit voice recorders for military and commercial platforms.
- **Video management systems** - video recorders and management solutions for airborne ISR and mission systems.
- **Air data computers** - aircraft air data sensing and processing systems.
- **Data acquisition systems** - high-speed flight test instrumentation including network switches, recorders, and RF transmitters.
