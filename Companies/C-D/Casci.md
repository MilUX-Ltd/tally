# Casci

> Workflow and AI orchestration consultancy with case and task management delivery experience in defence and security.

## What they do

Casci is a UK consultancy specialising in workflow and AI orchestration for regulated organisations, with experience in the defence and security sectors. It designs and delivers case and task management solutions using workflow orchestration, working onshore with security-cleared staff. Its work spans secure-by-design architecture, cloud engineering and workflow orchestration.

## Products and solutions

- **Workflow and AI orchestration** - design and delivery of orchestrated case and task management solutions for regulated organisations.
- **Secure digital platforms** - secure-by-design platform engineering, including cloud engineering and workflow orchestration tooling such as Camunda.
