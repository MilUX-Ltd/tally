# Costain Group PLC

> Infrastructure delivery partner working across UK defence and nuclear energy programmes.

## What they do

Costain is an infrastructure solutions company that designs, manages and builds critical infrastructure. Its defence and nuclear energy sector supports the UK's strategic systems and the service personnel who rely on infrastructure to live, work and train for operations, and the nuclear lifecycle across new build, fuels and decommissioning. As an integrated delivery partner the company provides programme management, constructability and engineering services across defence projects and portfolios.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence delivery partner and construction professional services** - programme management and constructability support across complex inter-related defence programmes.
- **Civil nuclear services** - supply chain integration, front-end design and engineering, and asset optimisation across the nuclear lifecycle.
