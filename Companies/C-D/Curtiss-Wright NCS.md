# Curtiss-Wright Defense Solutions

> Rugged embedded computing and electronic systems for manned and unmanned air, land and naval defence platforms.

## What they do

Curtiss-Wright Defense Solutions is the defence electronics arm of Curtiss-Wright Corporation, a US-listed engineering group headquartered in Davidson, North Carolina. It provides rugged embedded computing modules, electronic systems and networking and communication solutions for manned and unmanned rotorcraft, fixed-wing, ground and naval applications. Its products are based on open-architecture commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) technologies designed to meet size, weight, power and cost (SWaP-C) constraints. The NCS (Networking and Communication Solutions) line provides secure mobile networking and tactical data link processing.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged embedded computing** - single board computers, FPGA processor cards, digital signal processors, I/O and communications boards, and video/graphics cards.
- **Networking and Communication Solutions (NCS)** - secure mobile networks for distributed processing and time-sensitive data sharing, plus tactical data link processing.
- **Computing and electronic systems** - integrated C5ISR and vetronics system solutions ruggedised for harsh land, air and sea environments.
