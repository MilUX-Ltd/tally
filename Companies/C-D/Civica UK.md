# Civica UK

> Public-sector software company whose defence work includes digital transformation and data governance for the British Army and Defence Digital.

## What they do

Civica is a public-sector-focused software company supplying cloud software to government bodies across local and central government, defence, health and care, education, justice and blue-light services. Its portfolio covers workflow and automation, risk and compliance, workforce management, financial management and data analytics. The wider Civica group is owned by Blackstone, which completed its acquisition following the deal announced in late 2023.

## Products and solutions

- **Army Headquarters digital transformation** - a contract of over £5m supporting Army HQ, with architecture teams delivering to the Information Data Authority through to March 2026.
- **Defence Digital data work** - data governance, data management training, production of JSP 441 and a defence data analytics platform since 2019.
- **MoD Enterprise Data Management** - data architecture, governance and quality work under a DAMA framework.
