# DMG MORI UK Limited

> UK subsidiary of the global DMG MORI group, providing high-precision CNC machine tools and manufacturing solutions to the aerospace, defence and space sectors from its Coventry base.

## What they do

DMG MORI UK Limited is the British sales, service and technology centre for DMG MORI, a Japanese-German machine tool manufacturer with operations in 45 countries and approximately 13,500 employees globally. The UK entity is based in Coventry and provides the full DMG MORI product portfolio to British and Irish customers alongside training, maintenance, spare-parts support and application engineering. The global group supplies precision turning, milling, grinding, ultrasonic and additive manufacturing systems, with a dedicated focus on aviation and space, automotive, die and mould, medical, and semiconductor sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC turning and milling centres** - multi-axis turning and milling machines used in aerospace, defence and space component manufacture, including engine housings, landing gear and structural parts.
- **LASERTEC additive manufacturing systems** - hybrid laser deposition welding and 5-axis milling platforms for complex metal components in finished-part quality.
- **Automation and digital integration solutions** - end-to-end automation cells, digital manufacturing software and energy-monitoring tooling under the Machining Transformation (MX) programme.

## Notes

DMG MORI UK Limited (Companies House 01030108, incorporated 1971) is a wholly owned subsidiary of the DMG MORI group, headquartered in Nagoya, Japan. The German entity DMG MORI AG is listed on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange. The UK operation is a sales, service and demonstration centre; manufacturing takes place at the group's overseas production plants.
