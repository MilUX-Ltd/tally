# DMG MORI UK Limited

> UK subsidiary of the global DMG MORI group, supplying high-precision CNC machine tools and manufacturing solutions to the aerospace, defence and space sectors.

## What they do

DMG MORI UK Limited is the British sales, service and technology centre for DMG MORI, a global manufacturer of high-precision machine tools represented in 44 countries. The UK operation is based in Coventry and serves manufacturers requiring precision turning, milling, grinding and additive manufacturing capability across aerospace, defence, automotive and medical sectors. DMG MORI bundles more than 152 years of manufacturing experience under its Machining Transformation (MX) framework, which covers process integration, automation, digital transformation and green transformation.

## Products and solutions

- **Turning and milling centres** - multi-axis CNC machines for complex aerospace and defence components including engine housings, landing gear and structural parts.
- **LASERTEC series** - hybrid additive and subtractive manufacturing machines enabling laser deposition welding combined with 5-axis milling, used for aerospace components requiring finished-part quality.
- **Automation and digitisation solutions** - end-to-end turnkey manufacturing systems covering the full machine life cycle.

## Notes

DMG MORI UK Limited is a wholly owned subsidiary of the DMG MORI group, headquartered in Nagoya, Japan, with the German entity DMG MORI AG listed on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange. The UK company (Companies House 01030108) was incorporated in 1971 and trades under the name DMG MORI UK Ltd. Global group headcount exceeds 13,000.
