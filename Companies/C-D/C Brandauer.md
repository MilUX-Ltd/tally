# C. Brandauer & Co Ltd

> Precision metal stamping and tooling manufacturer serving aerospace, defence and other engineering sectors.

## What they do

Brandauer is a contract precision metal stamping and tooling company based in Birmingham. It works in thin gauge materials, with material thicknesses from roughly 0.1mm to 2mm and tolerances measured in microns. The company supplies the automotive, aerospace, defence, mining and power generation sectors, and lists aerospace and defence among the markets it is targeting under its current ten-year plan.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision metal stamping** - high volume, thin gauge stamped components produced from in-house tooling.
- **Tooling design and manufacture** - modular, in-house tooling solutions.
- **Rapid prototyping** - laser cutting and tool-less precision metal forming to move a design from 2D drawing to formed 3D component.
