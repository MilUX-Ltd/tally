# Cirrus Software Solutions

> Secure software and systems engineering for defence, national security and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Cirrus Software Solutions provides secure software and systems engineering, with a focus on cloud solutions and data engineering. The company works with public and private clients, including government agencies in defence, national security and critical national infrastructure. Services include DevOps, business analysis, systems engineering, software development and cloud security architecture. Cirrus describes over 20 years of government-based secure software development experience across web development, microservice architecture and software delivery management.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure software engineering** - software development and delivery for defence and national security environments.
- **Systems engineering** - systems engineering and architecture, including transition to cloud services.
- **Cloud security architecture** - secure cloud solutions and data engineering.
- **DevOps and delivery** - DevOps, business analysis and software delivery management.
