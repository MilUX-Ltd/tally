# Deep Blue C Technology

> Developer of open-source maritime data analysis and training software for naval defence analysts.

## What they do

Deep Blue C Technology Ltd has produced analysis and training software for naval and maritime personnel since 2000. The company operates an open-source model, making its products free to use and fully documented. It holds accredited research supplier status with DSTL across multiple subject domains.

## Products and solutions

- **Debrief Maritime Analysis Tool** - open-source tool for maritime data analysis and reporting; supports the full analysis process from data loading through to formatted report outputs; in continuous development since 1995 and in use by navies, governmental and non-governmental organisations worldwide.
- **Lessons Management System** - web-based system developed for the Royal Navy for handling exercise lessons capture and management.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001 (since September 2011); DSTL accredited research supplier; FATS/4 and FATS/D&E procurement accreditations
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
