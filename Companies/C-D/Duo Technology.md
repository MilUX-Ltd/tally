# Duo Technology

> UK technology specialist providing secure digital services to the defence and security sectors.

## What they do

Duo Technology delivers secure digital services to clients in the defence and security sectors, with a focus on user experience and data-centric design. Capability areas include defence consultancy, security consultancy, software development and optimisation, unmanned systems, and design and prototyping.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence consultancy** - advisory services to defence and security sector clients.
- **Security consultancy** - specialist security advisory and alarm systems work.
- **Unmanned systems** - listed capability area; specific products not publicly detailed.
- **Software development and optimisation** - bespoke software and digital design for defence and security applications.
