# Commerce Decisions

> Provider of tender evaluation software and procurement support services for major defence and public procurements.

## What they do

Commerce Decisions supplies software and expert services for the evaluation of complex, high-value tenders. It has worked with the UK Ministry of Defence since 2001, and its AWARD evaluation tool is integrated with the MOD Defence Sourcing Portal for the assessment of high-risk or complex bids. Its services team supports buyers from planning through to contract award, including sensitivity analysis, risk and cost scenarios, negotiation support and competitive dialogue.

Commerce Decisions was formerly part of QinetiQ Group. It was acquired in November 2020 by N. Harris Computer Corporation, part of the Canadian-headquartered Constellation Software, so the company is foreign-owned while operating from the UK.

## Products and solutions

- **AWARD** - tender evaluation software for strategic and complex procurements, integrated with the MOD Defence Sourcing Portal.
- **Expert services** - procurement planning, evaluation design, sensitivity analysis, negotiation and debriefing support.
