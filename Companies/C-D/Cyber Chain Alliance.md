# Cyber Chain Alliance Ltd

> Cyber security consultancy and NCSC-assured service provider serving the defence supply chain, public sector and private organisations.

## What they do

Cyber Chain Alliance (CCA) helps organisations manage information risk and strengthen cyber resilience through tailored consultancy, ISO 27001 certification support, managed security solutions and cyber incident and crisis management. The company holds NCSC Assured Service Provider status for Cyber Incident Exercising and operates as a Certification Body for Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus and IASME Assurance. CCA explicitly supports ADS members in protecting assets across the aerospace, defence, security and space supply chains.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber Incident Exercising** - NCSC-assured design and delivery of cyber exercises.
- **ISO 27001 Certification Support** - Consultancy through to certification.
- **Cyber Essentials / CE+ Certification** - Assessment and certification body services.
- **Managed Security Solutions** - Ongoing security management for organisations.
- **Risk Assessments** - Tailored information risk assessments.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** G-Cloud; Cyber Security Services 3; Digital Outcomes
- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus; NCSC Assured Service Provider (Cyber Incident Exercising); IASME Assurance Certification Body
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
