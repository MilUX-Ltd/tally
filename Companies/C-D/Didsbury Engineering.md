# Didsbury Engineering Co. Limited

> Manufacturer of specialist ground support equipment for the defence and civil aerospace sectors, with over 80 years of design and production experience.

## What they do

Didsbury Engineering designs and manufactures lifting and mechanical handling equipment for the defence, civil aerospace and industrial safety markets, operating from Wilmslow, Cheshire. The company works directly with military procurement offices and aircraft manufacturers, supplying ground support equipment for the safe handling of aircraft engines, weapons, personnel and other high-value loads. Products are used worldwide and the company has a service presence in Dallas, Texas, alongside global service centres. Didsbury holds Cyber Essentials certification and is a subsidiary of Lifco AB (a Swedish listed group), as evidenced by the Lifco tax statement on its website.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence Hoist Kits** - bespoke and off-the-shelf kits for engine removal, vehicle ancillary handling and sensitive equipment handling; lightweight, modular and portable for field use.
- **Purge Air Military** - a dedicated purge air system for efficient and safe working within aircraft fuel tanks, developed with Airbus and adapted for military applications.
- **Military Transport Packaging** - bespoke packaging for missiles and armour, designed for secure transport of sensitive defence materiel.
