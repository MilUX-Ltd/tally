# DIS Consulting

> UK defence systems engineering and technical assurance consultancy covering land, air, maritime, and weapons domains.

## What they do

DIS Consulting (Defence and Innovation Systems Consulting) is a Warminster-based technical consultancy providing systems engineering, safety assurance, data science, and quantum technology advisory services to DE&S, UK Front Line Commands, and NATO. The company operates an associate-heavy delivery model, assembling multidisciplinary teams including SQEP consultants and chartership holders. It is independent and supplier-agnostic.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems Engineering** - Complex and safety-critical systems; Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE); validation and verification using digital methods.
- **Safety, Environmental and Material Assurance** - Tailored assurance teams covering legislative, regulatory, and policy responsibilities across all domains.
- **Data Science and Transformation** - Modelling, simulation, synthetic environments, AI and machine learning applied to defence problems.
- **Material Science in Defence** - Material selection, testing and characterisation, modelling, and through-life management.
- **Quantum Technologies** - Advisory on sensing, imaging, PNT, communications, computing, and simulation strategies.
- **Cost Modelling and Analysis** - Concept-phase cost estimating, option modelling, bid evaluation, and negotiation support.
