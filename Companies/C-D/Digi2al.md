# Digi2al Ltd

> UK technology specialist delivering secure digital services, software and digital transformation to defence, security and public-sector clients.

## What they do

Digi2al was founded in 2013 to shape essential public services and deliver digital transformation, drawing on a team with backgrounds in defence, government, technology and the arts. The company focuses on user-centred design and builds tailored software and hardware, working across defence and security, government and public services, health and human performance, and climate and environment sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Service Design and User Experience** - user research and design of public and defence digital services.
- **Cloud Engineering** - cloud-based service development and delivery.
- **Complex Code and Low Code** - custom software development.
- **Cyber Security** - protection of systems, networks and data.
- **OSINT Brokerage and Threat Intelligence** - open-source intelligence and threat intelligence services.
- **Managed Services and Digi2al Labs** - ongoing support and innovation work.
