# Drone Major Ltd

> Independent UAS and counter-UAS consultancy, procurement adviser and marketplace operator, serving defence, government and critical national infrastructure customers.

## What they do

Drone Major Ltd is a London-based specialist incorporated in September 2016, operating as a division of Drone Major Group. It provides strategic consultancy, market intelligence, procurement advisory and test and evaluation services across the drone and counter-drone sector, connecting buyers to suppliers from a vetted global network of over 7,000 suppliers via its marketplace platform. Sectors served include MOD, defence primes, government and critical national infrastructure operators across land, maritime and air domains. In June 2025 it received the first CAA Operational Authority to fly long-range BVLOS drones over active UK critical national infrastructure in non-segregated airspace, in partnership with Network Rail.

## Products and solutions

- **Procurement advisory and supplier matching** - independent advisory connecting buyers to vetted drone and counter-drone suppliers.
- **Drone and counter-UAS consultancy** - strategic consultancy, capability design, rapid prototyping support and project management for UAS and C-UAS programmes.
- **Drone Major marketplace** - buyer/supplier platform covering over 7,000 global drone and autonomous systems suppliers.
- **Digital Tethering** - system for assured BVLOS operations in complex environments; trialled on Network Rail routes for long-range drone navigation and safety.
- **Test and evaluation** - independent test and evaluation services for drone and autonomous systems.
