# Castle Precision Engineering

> Glasgow precision machining company supplying complex components to aerospace and defence customers.

## What they do

Castle Precision Engineering is a high-performance machining supplier of complex parts and assemblies for aerospace and defence customers. It manufactures high-precision gas turbine components for the aerospace and energy sectors, and complex prismatic parts for the defence, space and medical sectors. The company runs a computerised manufacturing facility in Glasgow.

## Products and solutions

- **Aero engine components** - precision machined critical rotating parts for gas turbine engines.
- **Defence components** - machined parts associated with aircraft, radars, countermeasures, missiles, lasers and torpedoes.
- **Prismatic components** - complex prismatic parts for defence, space and medical applications.
