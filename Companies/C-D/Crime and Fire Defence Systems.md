# Crime and Fire Defence Systems Ltd

> Turnkey physical security integrator delivering government-grade perimeter protection and intruder detection for critical national infrastructure sites in the UK and overseas.

## What they do

Crime and Fire Defence Systems designs, constructs, installs, and maintains integrated physical security systems including electrified perimeter fencing, CCTV, intruder detection, access control, and control room monitoring. The company serves government, defence, energy, gas, water, and utility sectors, with solutions engineered to meet NPSA and relevant British Standards. It is part of the Journeo Group. The company signed the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Campost** - Perimeter-mounted integrated security tower combining CCTV, lighting, audio, and detection in a single fence-line unit.
- **Perimeter Intruder Detection (PID)** - Rapid-deployment intruder detection solutions for temporary or high-risk sites.
- **High Security Systems** - Integrated CCTV, access control, intruder detection, and electrified fencing for government and defence sites, including CPNI-compliant configurations.
