# Decision Lab

> AI-powered decision support, digital twins, and drone control systems for defence, national security, and industrial clients.

## What they do

Decision Lab is a technology company specialising in mathematical modelling, optimisation, simulation, data science, and artificial intelligence. For defence and national security clients, the company develops mission-ready decision support systems, secure AI digital twins powered by Agentic AI and Reinforcement Learning, and drone control systems. Clients include Babcock International, Leonardo, Thales, Rolls-Royce, the Defence Science and Technology Laboratory (Dstl), the Royal Navy, and Cranfield University.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure AI Digital Twins** - simulation and digital twin environments powered by Agentic AI and Reinforcement Learning, providing quantitative assurance for mission-critical decisions.
- **Drone Control Systems** - autonomous drone control development using AI and reinforcement learning.
- **Mission-Ready Decision Support Systems** - decision intelligence platforms supporting defence manufacturing, supply chain, and sustainment planning.
- **Munitions and Platform Ramp-Up Analytics** - modelling to de-risk critical production scaling for munitions and defence platforms.
