# Creative Composites

> Northern Ireland composites manufacturer producing glass and carbon fibre reinforced polymer components for automotive, transport, and defence markets, with over 40 years of defence container and packaging supply.

## What they do

Creative Composites designs and manufactures composite components using compression moulding, resin transfer moulding, CNC machining, painting, and assembly at a 100,000 sq.ft facility in Lisburn, Co. Antrim. The company owns the largest compression mould presses in the UK. Primary markets are automotive, transportation (bus, coach, rail), off-highway, and electric vehicles. Defence has been a continuous market for over 40 years, with the company supplying composite containers and packing assemblies for defence applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Composite containers and packing assemblies** - protective transit and storage casings for defence equipment; supplied to the defence sector for over 40 years per ADS member profile.
- **Compression-moulded structural components** - glass and carbon fibre reinforced polymer parts for automotive and transportation OEMs.
