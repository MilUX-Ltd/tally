# Deeptech Legal

> Specialist law firm serving technology companies in cyber, quantum, space, defence, advanced materials and robotics.

## What they do

Deeptech Legal Limited is an SRA-regulated law firm focused on technology companies operating in regulated or security-adjacent sectors. The firm's work spans commercial contracts, export licence applications, NSIA notifications, compliance audits, and fundraising support. It works principally with Series A and beyond companies, though it also supports earlier-stage teams on project and funding matters.

## Products and solutions

- **Retained legal service** - fixed monthly fee covering ongoing commercial contracts, regulatory advice, and ad-hoc queries for technology companies in defence and national security sectors.
- **Project services** - discrete engagements covering export licence applications, ITAR compliance, NSIA notifications, contract template builds, and fundraising support.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
