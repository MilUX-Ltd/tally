# Citation Cyber

> UK cyber-security provider supporting the aerospace and defence supply chain with penetration testing, compliance and security awareness services.

## What they do

Citation Cyber is a UK cyber-security provider offering penetration testing, security consultancy, continuous vulnerability scanning and monitoring, compliance support and security awareness training. Its team operates from Lancaster University's Centre of Excellence in Cyber Security Research. It is a subsidiary of The Citation Group, which acquired the business (then Mitigate Cyber) in 2022.

## Products and solutions

- **Penetration testing** - NCSC-approved CHECK penetration testing for primes and tiered suppliers.
- **Compliance support** - assistance with Defence Cyber Certification (DCC), JOSCAR registration, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus and ISO 27001.
- **Security awareness training** - phishing simulation and NCSC-certified training.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 27001, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus; NCSC-approved CHECK.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; North West Aerospace Alliance.
