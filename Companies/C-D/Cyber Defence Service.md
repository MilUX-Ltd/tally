# Cyber Defence Service Ltd

> UK cyber, radio-frequency and electromagnetic-spectrum firm supporting military, law enforcement and public-sector clients.

## What they do

Cyber Defence Service supports critical organisations across the military, law enforcement, and public and private sectors, including SMEs. It specialises in the cyber, radio-frequency and electromagnetic environments, providing real-time analysis and exposure of what it calls the invisible battlespace. It does this by developing secure platforms and methodologies that connect advanced hardware to secure cloud-based software systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Overt AI** - a wideband, high-fidelity radio-frequency detection and analysis system using machine learning to detect adversary activity, tactics and techniques, providing real-time and recorded overviews of RF activity and geolocation.
- **Service areas** - AI and machine learning, cyber and networks, cyber threat intelligence, data science, information assurance, open-source intelligence, sensors and communications, software, and systems engineering.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus.
- **Memberships:** Stated alumni of the 2021 NCSC Cyber Accelerator and graduate of HMGCC Accelerate; member of the North West Cyber Security Cluster and Greater Manchester Digital Security Hub.
