# Comand AI

> AI-native command-and-control and decision-support platform for defence operations.

## What they do

Comand AI is a Paris-based deep-tech defence software company, founded in 2023, with a presence in London. It develops AI-powered capabilities for command and control of defence and security operations, with outputs designed to be transparent, traceable and contestable, and with a human making every final decision.

## Products and solutions

- **Prevail** - an AI-native command-and-control (C2) platform for planning and decision-making under dynamic, multi-domain conditions. It processes complex battlefield data and simulates courses of action quickly, and provides post-operation analysis tools.

## Recent news

- **2026-06-17** - Raised a EUR 32 million Series A led by Blossom Capital, with strategic investment from Saab and renewed participation from Expeditions, to scale the Prevail platform across NATO markets and into air and maritime domains.
