# Dynatrace

> Observability, AI-powered automation, and security software platform, listed on NYSE, with a UK subsidiary based in Maidenhead and active public sector and defence customers.

## What they do

Dynatrace provides a unified software intelligence platform combining full-stack observability, AI-powered analytics, application performance monitoring, log management, digital experience monitoring, and security. The company serves government and defence organisations seeking to monitor and secure complex, cloud-native and hybrid IT environments. Its UK subsidiary, Dynatrace Limited, is registered in Maidenhead and has supplied platform subscriptions to UK public sector bodies via the G-Cloud framework. Dynatrace's platform can operate in air-gapped architectures, which is relevant to classified defence environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Dynatrace Platform** - Unified observability, AIOps, and security platform with full-stack monitoring across applications, infrastructure, and user experience.
- **Threat Observability** - Security analytics combining runtime vulnerability detection with observability telemetry to support security operations.
- **Government and Public Sector Solutions** - Tailored deployment options for regulated environments, including air-gapped and protected hosting, with compliance enablement for government security standards.
