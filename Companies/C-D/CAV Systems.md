# CAV Systems Ltd

> Aircraft ice protection and drag reduction technology for general aviation, commercial aerospace and defence.

## What they do

CAV Systems designs, certifies and manufactures aircraft ice protection systems and drag reduction technology. Its products are supplied to general aviation, commercial aerospace and defence markets. The company traces its origins to 1942, when the TKS fluid ice protection method was developed for RAF aircraft, and it manufactures from a site in Consett, County Durham.

## Products and solutions

- **TKS Ice Protection System** - fluid-based anti-ice and de-ice system certified for Flight Into Known Icing, installed across many aircraft types.
- **SLD Guard** - ice protection addressing supercooled large droplet icing and associated certification requirements.
- **microLASE laser-perforated surfaces** - laser-perforated panels used for drag reduction.

CAV's technologies have been applied on defence and aerospace platforms including the C-17, Eurofighter and unmanned aircraft.
