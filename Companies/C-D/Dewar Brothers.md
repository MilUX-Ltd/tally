# Dewar Brothers

> Scotland's only commercially MPAS-accredited specialist packaging manufacturer, designing and producing bespoke packaging to MOD standards for defence and aerospace customers.

## What they do

Dewar Brothers Limited is a specialist packaging manufacturer established in 1973 and based in Tillicoultry, Clackmannanshire. The company designs and manufactures corrugated, foam, timber and flight-case packaging for defence, aerospace, medical, industrial, electronic and oil and gas customers. It is the only commercial company in Scotland accredited to the Military Packer Approval Scheme (MPAS), which gives it the facility to design, approve and manufacture packaging to MOD standards. Dewar Brothers is Scotland's sole appointed supplier of SKB cases and the European distributor of Spacecase modular containers, and provides a Kanban/JIT delivery service to key customers.

## Products and solutions

- **MOD-standard defence packaging** - bespoke packaging designed, approved and manufactured to Ministry of Defence standards under MPAS accreditation; covers corrugated, foam, timber and composite formats.
- **Flight cases** - custom-built flight cases with bespoke cut-foam interiors for sensitive defence and aerospace equipment.
- **SKB cases** - Scotland's sole appointed SKB distributor; watertight, dust-proof military-grade protective cases.
- **Spacecase modular containers** - European distributor of rotationally moulded Spacecase modular container systems.
- **Foam engineering** - bespoke foam engineering using CNC router technology for protective equipment interiors.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
