# Drone Defence Services Ltd

> UK counter-UAS company delivering drone detection, tracking, identification and mitigation systems.

## What they do

Drone Defence Services Ltd is a UK counter-UAS company incorporated in 2017, based at the Drone Innovation Centre, West Carr Business Park, Retford. It uses proprietary technologies to detect, track and identify drones across wide areas on land or at sea using multi-sensory technology, and to disrupt drone command and navigation radio transmissions in any weather, day or night. The company states its systems are intended for qualified defence, government and security organisations and are subject to UK export controls.

## Products and solutions

- **AeroStrike** - high-speed kinetic drone interceptor reaching up to 270 km/h, with an intercept envelope of 100-200 km/h, up to 10 minutes endurance and a 13 km operating range; non-explosive and recoverable architecture.
- **AeroTracker** - flight monitoring, tasking and intercept management platform, integrating with radar, RF and EO/IR detection systems.
- **Detection and mitigation systems** - multi-sensory wide-area drone detection, tracking and identification, plus RF disruption of drone command and navigation links.
