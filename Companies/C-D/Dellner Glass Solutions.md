# Dellner Glass Solutions

> UK manufacturer of ballistic and blast-resistant glazing for military vehicles, armoured platforms and critical infrastructure.

## What they do

Dellner Glass Solutions is a specialist glass processor and manufacturer supplying protective glazing to the global transport, security and defence markets. Formed in 2021 through the consolidation of Dellner Romag (Consett, County Durham), Dellner Percy Lane (Tamworth, Staffordshire) and Dellner Wizard (Luton), the business brings over 150 years of combined manufacturing experience. It is a wholly owned subsidiary of Dellner Group, a Swedish privately held industrial group headquartered in Borlänge, Sweden. The company has supplied military glazing for over seven decades and operates its own indoor ballistic range and air cannon for live-fire testing.

## Products and solutions

- **Military vehicle glazing** - Windscreens and vision panels for armoured and military vehicles, tested to STANAG 4569 (ballistic) and DIN 52290 Part 5 (blast); certified from STANAG Level 1 to Level 3.
- **Transparent armour composites** - Ballistic glass for high-risk military installations, government buildings, embassies and VIP protection.
- **Infrastructure security glazing** - Protected windows and partitions for strategic military sites, guard houses and transport security.
- **Cash-in-transit and armoured vehicle glazing** - Specialist glass for security vehicles requiring ballistic protection.
