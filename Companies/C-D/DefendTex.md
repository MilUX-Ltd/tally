# DefendTex

> Australian-owned manufacturer of multi-domain asymmetric warfare solutions, including autonomous drone munitions, armour systems and precision guided weapons.

## What they do

DefendTex is an Australian-owned company with offices in Australia, the United States and the United Kingdom. Incorporated in the UK in 2017, its UK registered office is in London. The UK operating base is in Salcombe Regis, Devon. The company develops cost-effective capabilities designed to increase the strike range, survivability and lethality of existing platforms. Its products are in service with British Army and other Five Eyes partner forces.

## Products and solutions

- **Drone40 (D40)** - 40mm grenade-launched autonomous mini quadcopter; weighs 190g; range of over 12 miles; flight time up to one hour; available with ISR, strike and kinetic payloads; capable of swarm operations and multiple munitions simultaneous impact.
- **Drone60 / Drone81 / Drone155** - extended range and payload variants of the Drone40 family covering 60mm, 81mm and 155mm calibres.
- **Armour systems** - personal protection and vehicle armour solutions.
- **Precision guided weapons** - energetics and propulsion-based precision munitions.
- **Responsive access to space** - small satellite and space access capabilities.
