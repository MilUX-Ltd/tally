# Cogent Technology

> UK contract electronics manufacturer serving military and defence, aerospace, medical, marine, and industrial customers from its Felixstowe facility.

## What they do

Cogent Technology is a contract electronics manufacturer (CEM) with four decades of operating history. It provides PCB assembly, electromechanical assembly, box build, conformal coating, and new product introduction (NPI) through to series production. Military and defence is one of five named specialist verticals. Cogent was acquired by CamdenBoss in January 2024, joining the Heathpatch Group, and continues to operate independently from its Felixstowe site.

## Products and solutions

- **PCB assembly** - Through-hole, surface mount, and mixed-technology assembly to IPC-A-610 standards, including conformal coating for harsh environments.
- **Box build and electromechanical assembly** - Full device build from sub-assembly through to finished product.
- **NPI and series production** - New product introduction support from prototype to volume manufacture.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; ISO 13485; ISO 14001; FDA registered
