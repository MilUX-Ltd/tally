# Druid Software

> Private 4G and 5G cellular core network software provider, with a dedicated defence offering for tactical and welfare communications.

## What they do

Druid Software develops private cellular core network technology, operating in the space since 2000. Its Raemis platform delivers 2G through 5G private network capability for defence, enterprise, healthcare, ports, and utilities customers across more than 35 countries. The company raised $20 million in January 2025 to accelerate growth in defence, shipping, and utilities markets. The UK registered entity, Druid Software NI Limited, is based in Belfast and was incorporated in October 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **Raemis** - A private cellular core network platform supporting 2G, 3G, 4G, and 5G. Deployable on commodity hardware; manages provisioning, reporting, and network management from a single software platform. Configurable for rapid field deployment including vehicular and backpack configurations for mobile patrols.
- **Mesh Networking** - An extension of Raemis allowing multiple patrol deployments to operate as a single network, with dynamic expansion and contraction as units join and leave.
- **Welfare Calling** - A managed mobile basecamp service for defence personnel deployed overseas, providing secure voice and data access on personal devices via SIM provisioning.
