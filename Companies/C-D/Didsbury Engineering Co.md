# Didsbury Engineering Co.

> Manufacturer of lifting, hoisting and mechanical handling equipment for defence, civil aerospace and industrial markets, supplying ground support equipment to defence organisations globally.

## What they do

Didsbury Engineering has designed and manufactured ground support equipment for over 80 years, serving the defence, civil aviation and industrial safety sectors worldwide. The company specialises in lifting and handling devices for aircraft engines, landing gear, APUs, actuators and other high-value components. It supplies equipment directly to defence organisations and is specified by major airframe manufacturers including Airbus, Boeing, Embraer, Fokker, Dassault and Sukhoi. Didsbury Engineering was acquired by Swedish group Lifco AB in March 2023 and sits within Lifco's Systems Solutions business area.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence Hoist Kits** - modular, portable hoist kits for engine changes and component removal in field and maintenance environments.
- **Purge Air Military** - purge air systems for safe working within aircraft fuel tanks, adapted for military applications.
- **Bespoke military transport packaging** - custom packaging for missiles, armour and other sensitive military loads.
- **Ground Support Equipment (GSE)** - off-the-shelf and bespoke kits for aircraft engine removal, vehicle ancillary handling and sensitive equipment movement.
