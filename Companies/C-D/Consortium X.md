# Consortium X Ltd

> Veteran-owned digital transformation and systems engineering consultancy serving the UK defence and intelligence community.

## What they do

Consortium X is a digital transformation and systems engineering consultancy specialising in the UK defence and intelligence community. Its work spans systems engineering, cloud security architecture, data centre design and security upgrade programmes, with experience evaluating major cloud platforms for integration with critical systems. The company is veteran-owned and based in Cardiff, servicing the wider UK.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems engineering** - systems thinking and engineering for complex defence and intelligence programmes.
- **Cloud security architecture** - design of cloud security architectures and evaluation of cloud platforms for critical systems.
- **Data centre design** - architectural design of data centres and management of security upgrade projects.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
