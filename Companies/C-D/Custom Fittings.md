# Custom Fittings Ltd

> West Yorkshire manufacturer of precision hydraulic hose fittings, tube fittings, and adaptors for aerospace, defence, space, and industrial applications.

## What they do

Custom Fittings manufactures and distributes hydraulic hose fittings, tube fittings, pipe fittings, connectors, and adaptors from its AS9100 and ISO 9001 certified facility in Cleckheaton, West Yorkshire. The company supplies the aviation, space, defence, nuclear, oil and gas, and power generation sectors. Its products are used in high-pressure hydraulic systems on aircraft, spacecraft, and defence platforms across land, sea, air, and space applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Flareless fittings** - high-pressure aerospace and defence hydraulic fittings designed for systems subject to rigorous vibration or inconsistent pressures.
- **Beam seal fittings** - high-temperature, high-pressure, anti-vibration, zero-leak metal-to-metal fittings for aerospace, space, and defence fluid delivery systems.
- **Hose and tube assemblies** - bespoke hose connections, pipeline connections, and adaptors for fluid transfer across multiple sectors.
