# CYSIAM

> UK cyber intelligence specialist providing managed detection and response, incident response, and sovereign cyber capacity-building for national security, defence, and public sector clients.

## What they do

CYSIAM is a specialist cyber security company founded in 2018, headquartered in rural Buckinghamshire near Milton Keynes. The team background is military intelligence, law enforcement, and national security; all staff hold HMG security clearances. The company operates a wholly UK-based 24/7/365 Security Operations Centre and delivers managed detection and response underpinned by a proprietary Cyber Threat Intelligence platform informed by collaboration with over 700 global Incident Response Teams. Since 2019 CYSIAM has built sustainable SOC and CIRT capability in over 25 countries, working with national governments and critical infrastructure operators on behalf of UK international development programmes. A private equity firm (undisclosed) acquired the company from Maven VCTs in May 2026.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed Detection and Response (MDR)** - 24/7/365 intelligence-led SOC service with CREST SOC accreditation, built around a proprietary Cyber Threat Intelligence platform.
- **Cyber Incident Response (CIR)** - NCSC-certified Level 2 incident response capability for high-severity events.
- **Cyber Capacity Building** - sovereign SOC and CIRT delivery for national governments and critical infrastructure operators; 25-plus countries since 2019.
- **Penetration Testing and Vulnerability Assessment** - CREST-accredited testing across IT and OT environments.
- **Cyber Incident Exercising** - NCSC Assured Service Provider for tabletop and live exercises.
