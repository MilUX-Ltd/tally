# DarkSaber Labs

> US electronic warfare firm building AI-driven, edge-computing EW systems for dismounted operators.

## What they do

DarkSaber Labs builds electronic warfare systems rooted in agentic AI and edge computing, designed for operators in disconnected, contested, data-heavy environments. It develops modular, full-hardware-stack EW solutions intended to act as a force multiplier across electronic warfare, signals intelligence and cyber operations.

## Products and solutions

- **Yankee One** - an AI-powered sensor fusion system that autonomously interprets electronic signals, imagery and communications to give the operator real-time information. Manpackable with a chest-worn tablet, geared for dismounted operations.

## Recent news

- **2025-07-02** - Janes reports DarkSaber Labs developing the Yankee One EW system for dismounted troops, with talks to test and procure for special operations users. [source](https://www.janes.com/osint-insights/defence-news/c4isr/darksaber-labs-develops-ew-system-for-dismounted-troops)
