# Cosimmetry Ltd

> Behavioural science, AI, modelling and simulation for decision support in defence and security.

## What they do

Cosimmetry combines models and systems mathematics with an understanding of how people work under pressure to build software that supports decision makers in conditions of uncertainty. The tooling and methodology run across three stages: understand, plan and refine, drawing on decision science, red teaming and mathematical modelling such as system dynamics and decision trees. Stated users include military commanders, policy makers, engineers and finance professionals.

## Products and solutions

- **Co\*** - decision support tooling for building shared models and testing options.
- **CoProject** - project decision support within the Co\* family.
- **Wargaming** - methods and tooling to test plans against adversarial systems.
