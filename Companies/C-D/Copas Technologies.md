# Copas Technologies Ltd

> Precision-machined component manufacturer for aerospace and defence supply chains, based in Kilkeel, Northern Ireland.

## What they do

Copas Technologies was founded in 2006 and manufactures precision-machined 3, 4 and 5 axis components for aerospace, defence and technology customers. The company supplies components and support to manufacturers across the UK, the USA and Asia. It works in aluminium, hard metals and plastics, with post-machining finishes including silver plating, bead blasting and anodised coatings.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision machined components** - 3, 4 and 5 axis machined parts for aerospace and defence manufacturers.
- **Post-machining finishing** - silver plate, bead blasting and anodised coatings.
