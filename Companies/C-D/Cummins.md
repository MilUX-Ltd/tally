# Cummins Ltd

> UK subsidiary of Cummins Inc., supplying diesel and alternative-fuel engines, drivetrain and braking systems to the global defence sector.

## What they do

Cummins Ltd is the UK registered entity of Cummins Inc., an American power technology company headquartered in Columbus, Indiana. The company designs, manufactures and supports engines and integrated powertrain solutions for military land vehicles and marine government and defence platforms. UK manufacturing facilities are located in Darlington and Daventry. Cummins Inc. is NYSE-listed and one of the largest independent diesel engine manufacturers in the world.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence diesel engines (60-850hp)** - a range of diesel engines for wheeled and tracked military vehicles, supplied to the UK MoD and other NATO armed forces.
- **Integrated powertrain** - following the 2022 acquisition of Meritor, Cummins offers combined engine, axle, brake, suspension and driveline solutions through Cummins Drivetrain and Braking Systems (CDBS).
- **Marine government and defence** - engine and power systems for naval and coast guard platforms.
