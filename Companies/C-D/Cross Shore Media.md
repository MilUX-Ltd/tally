# Cross Shore Media
> Specialist camera systems for autonomous vehicles across land, sea and air domains.
## What they do
Cross Shore Media manufactures specialty cameras designed for autonomous vehicles operating across land, sea and air environments. The company is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
## Products and solutions
- **Specialty cameras** - Purpose-built camera systems for autonomous vehicles in land, maritime and air roles.
