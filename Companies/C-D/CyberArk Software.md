# CyberArk Software (UK) Ltd

> UK subsidiary of the global identity security specialist, providing privileged access management and identity protection platforms to government and defence customers.

## What they do

CyberArk is a global leader in identity security, specialising in protecting organisations from cyber threats that exploit privileged accounts and credentials. The company pioneered the Privileged Access Management (PAM) category and offers a platform covering both human and machine identities. CyberArk serves UK central government, defence, and critical national infrastructure clients, with its products listed on the G-Cloud Digital Marketplace and the UK Government's approved solutions catalogue. In February 2026 CyberArk was acquired by Palo Alto Networks in a transaction valued at approximately $25 billion; the UK entity continues to operate under the CyberArk brand.

## Products and solutions

- **CyberArk Privilege Cloud** - SaaS privileged access management; listed on G-Cloud for UK public sector procurement.
- **CyberArk Secure Cloud Access** - Secure access controls for cloud infrastructure; available via G-Cloud.
- **CyberArk Identity Security Platform** - Integrated platform securing human and machine identities across enterprise environments, including government and defence deployments.
