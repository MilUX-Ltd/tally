# Denchi Group Limited

> UK-based designer and manufacturer of mission-critical lithium-ion batteries and charging systems for defence, aerospace and adjacent sectors.

## What they do

Denchi Group is a UK-owned battery specialist based in Thurso, Caithness, Scotland. The company designs and manufactures batteries and charging systems for defence and security applications, with origins tracing to the late 1950s and strong links to the UK Ministry of Defence and defence prime contractors. Denchi supplies products for military radios, vision systems, manned military vehicles, unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), unmanned underwater vehicles (UUVs) and unmanned ground vehicles (UGVs). All design and manufacture is carried out within the UK.

## Products and solutions

- **6T Lithium-Ion Battery** - NATO 6T format drop-in replacement for lead-acid batteries in 24V military ground vehicles, covering main battle tanks, armoured personnel carriers and light armoured vehicles; rated for over 4,000 recharge cycles.
- **BB-2590 Smart Battery** - rugged lithium-ion smart battery for military radios and mission-critical portable equipment.
- **UAV/UAS Battery Packs** - specialist cells and packs for commercial and military unmanned aerial systems.
- **UUV and Subsea Battery Systems** - high-reliability battery solutions for underwater unmanned systems.
- **Battery Chargers** - intelligent charging systems designed alongside battery products for defence and security use.
