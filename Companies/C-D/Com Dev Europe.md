# Com Dev Europe

> UK space subsystem manufacturer within Honeywell Aerospace, making space-qualified microwave and RF hardware for satellite payloads.

## What they do

Com Dev Europe designs and manufactures space-qualified hardware: passive microwave equipment, active RF and electronic equipment, ferrite devices, and integrated assemblies for communications, Earth observation and scientific satellites. Its UK sites at Aylesbury and Edinburgh deliver these payload subsystems. The company is part of Honeywell Aerospace following Honeywell's acquisition of COM DEV International in 2016.

## Products and solutions

- **Passive microwave equipment** - integrated multiplexers, filters and ancillary microwave components.
- **Active RF and electronic equipment** for satellite payloads.
- **Ferrite devices and electromechanical RF switches.**
- **Integrated assemblies** for communications, Earth observation and scientific satellite payloads.
