# DTM Global

> Naval simulation and training software company supplying maritime bridge, fire control, communications, and recognition training systems to the Royal Navy and international customers.

## What they do

DTM Global designs and supplies simulation and training software for naval and maritime applications, operating from Ivybridge, Devon. The company holds JOSCAR registration, Cyber Essentials certification, and ISO 9001:2015 UKAS accreditation. Its fire control trainer is installed at HMS Collingwood for Royal Navy small calibre weapon operator training. The company trades as DT Media Ltd t/a DTM Global.

## Products and solutions

- **Fleetman** - A configurable maritime bridge simulation software suite, available in standalone training, bridge training, and classroom training configurations.
- **Fire Control Trainer (FCT)** - A simulation of the MSI Defence Systems small calibre automated gun system, used by Royal Navy personnel at HMS Collingwood.
- **Mobile Fire Control Trainer** - A Peli-case packaged version of the Fire Control Trainer, self-contained and requiring only a power outlet, for remote weapon operator training anywhere. Launched at World Defence Show 2024.
- **Insight Recognition Trainer** - Target recognition and identification training software covering aircraft, land vehicles, and sea vessels. Supports EW, C2, air defence, special forces, and intelligence organisation training, including pre-deployment theatre familiarisation.
- **FleetComms** - A communications simulation product for naval training environments.
- **Flight Deck Officer Trainer** - A virtual reality trainer for flight deck officer roles.
