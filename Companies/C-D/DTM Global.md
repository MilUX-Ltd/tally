# DTM Global Limited

> Devon-based developer of naval simulation and training software, supplying the UK Royal Navy and other military customers with maritime bridge, fire control, recognition, and communications trainers.

## What they do

DTM Global designs and supplies modifiable off-the-shelf (MOTS) and bespoke low-cost maritime and naval simulation and training solutions to civilian and military institutions. The company trades as DT Media Ltd t/a DTM Global. Its products are used at HMS Collingwood and by other UK and international naval organisations, and are built on commercial off-the-shelf PC technology to keep costs accessible.

## Products and solutions

- **Fleetman** - A configurable maritime simulation software suite covering standalone training, bridge team training, and classroom training; used for fleet communications, manoeuvre training, ship handling, and rules of the road.
- **Fire Control Trainer (FCT)** - PC-based procedural trainer simulating the MSI-Defence Systems Automated Small Calibre Gun (DS30M Seahawk A2), with classrooms installed at HMS Collingwood.
- **Mobile Fire Control Trainer** - A compact, Peli-case-housed version of the Fire Control Trainer requiring only a power outlet, designed for remote weapons operators to train anywhere.
- **Insight Recognition Trainer** - Multi-faceted software for platform recognition training and combat identification (CID); supports EW, C2, air defence, special forces, intelligence, and pre-deployment theatre training; covers aircraft, land vehicles, sea platforms, missiles, small arms, and uniforms.
- **FleetComms** - PC-based communications simulation with localised instructor and student positions, supporting multi-channel operation.
- **Flight Deck Officer Trainer** - Virtual reality-capable procedural trainer for flight deck marshalling and deck handling, including emergency procedures and vertical replenishment.
