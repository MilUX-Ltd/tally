# Crowell & Moring UK

> London office of a US-headquartered law firm specialising in aerospace and defence procurement, export control, sanctions and regulatory compliance.

## What they do

Crowell & Moring UK LLP is the London presence of a Washington DC-headquartered law firm. The firm advises leading aerospace and defence companies on government procurement contracts, commercial contracts with partners and suppliers, export control, sanctions, tariffs, supply chain risk and sector-specific regulatory compliance. It works across both sides of the Atlantic, supporting clients seeking to enter or expand in the US and UK markets.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence procurement advisory** - legal advice on government contracts for aerospace and defence primes and suppliers.
- **Export control and sanctions** - regulatory compliance for defence exporters operating across US and UK jurisdictions.
- **Supply chain risk** - legal support on supply chain due diligence, tariffs and partner contracting.
