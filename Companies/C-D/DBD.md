# DBD

> Engineering and advisory consultancy specialising in nuclear defence and civil nuclear programmes.

## What they do

DBD (trading as DBD International) is an independent engineering and advisory consultancy focused on complex, highly regulated environments, with a particular emphasis on nuclear defence and civil nuclear. Services span front-end design, process and systems engineering, commissioning and decommissioning, waste management, safety assurance, and programme and project management. The company is headquartered at Birchwood Park, Warrington, with a further UK office at Westlakes Science Park in West Cumbria and US operations in South Carolina, Washington state, and Idaho. DBD was incorporated in 1995 as Different by Design Limited and renamed in 2009.

## Products and solutions

- **Nuclear defence engineering** - design, safety, and programme support on UK nuclear deterrent infrastructure, including work embedded in the AWE Manufacturing Programme Partnership alongside Mace and BakerHicks.
- **Decommissioning and waste management** - technical consultancy for nuclear site decommissioning and radioactive waste treatment.
- **Safety and assurance** - regulatory assurance, hazard analysis, and nuclear safety case development.
- **Programme management consultancy** - project controls, PMO, and programme delivery support for major nuclear programmes.
