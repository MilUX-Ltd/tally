# Clydesdale Engineering

> West Midlands engineering manufacturer producing components for defence, off-highway and automotive customers.

## What they do

Clydesdale Engineering manufactures componentry for defence, off-highway and automotive customers, from pressings to complex assemblies and fabrications. It operates from a West Midlands facility and employs around 240 people across its divisions, which include Defence Dynamics, Accles & Pollock and Clydesdale Jones, supplying defence, aerospace, automotive and off-highway sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence Dynamics** - anti-vibration and shock isolation mounting solutions for military, marine, aerospace and commercial applications, including a naval mountings range used by the UK MoD and other navies, with shock mounts that reduce explosive shock forces to protect critical equipment.
- **Accles & Pollock** - precision tube and engineered products.
- **Clydesdale Jones** - engineered components and assemblies.
