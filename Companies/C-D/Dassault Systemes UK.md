# Dassault Systemes UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of the French 3DEXPERIENCE software group, providing 3D design, simulation, and digital manufacturing platforms to aerospace, defence, and industrial customers.

## What they do

Dassault Systèmes UK Ltd is the British operating entity of Dassault Systèmes SE (branded as "the 3DEXPERIENCE Company"), founded in 1981 and headquartered in France. The UK subsidiary sells, deploys, and supports the group's platform portfolio into British aerospace, defence, and manufacturing industry. Dassault Systèmes serves 290,000 customers in 140 countries across 11 industries. In the UK it has over 250 employees and a primary office in Warwick, and is a long-standing partner to Airbus, the AMRC at the University of Sheffield, and Cranfield University.

## Products and solutions

- **CATIA** - 3D design and engineering platform; the established standard tool across the Airbus supply chain and used by BAE Systems, Rolls-Royce, and UK defence primes.
- **SIMULIA Aerospace and Defence** - multi-physics simulation covering structural, aerodynamic, aeroacoustic, and electromagnetic analysis for rotorcraft, ground systems, fighter aircraft, and missiles.
- **DELMIA** - digital manufacturing and operations management, including Factory of the Future capability demonstrated at AMRC Cymru.
- **ENOVIA** - product lifecycle and programme management.
- **3DEXPERIENCE platform** - cloud-based integration layer for digital engineering and virtual twin programmes; marketed for defence programme management and sovereign cloud deployment.
- **Sovereign cloud for defence** - a secure, certified cloud collaboration environment for next-generation defence programmes, partnered with Dassault Aviation for FCAS-adjacent work and NATO-aligned customers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015 (design, development, deployment, and support of 3DEXPERIENCE, SIMULIA, and SOLIDWORKS); ISO/IEC 27001:2022 (information security); ISO 27701 (privacy information management); SOC 2 Type II; ASME NQA-1:2022 (nuclear-grade software quality for SIMULIA).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; North West Aerospace Alliance.
