# CMASS NI

> Northern Ireland electronic contract manufacturer providing PCB assembly and product build for sectors including defence and aerospace.

## What they do

CMASS is an electronic contract manufacturer based near Antrim in the Greater Belfast area. It provides electronic manufacturing services including printed circuit board assembly (PCBA), complete product build, assembly and testing, configure-to-order, integration, product upgrade, refurbishment and rework. The company describes serving customers across telecommunications, power, defence, aerospace and connected health.

CMASS was founded in 2010 by former Nortel and Avaya manufacturing staff and operates from an 18,500 square foot factory off the M2 motorway. It carries out high-complexity, low-volume assembly work alongside customised functional, systems and network testing.

## Products and solutions

- **Printed circuit board assembly (PCBA)** - electronic assembly for low-volume, high-complexity production.
- **Product build and integration** - complete product assembly, configure-to-order, integration, upgrade, refurbishment and rework.
- **Test services** - customised functional, systems and network testing.
