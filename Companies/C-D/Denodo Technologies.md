# Denodo Technologies Limited

> Data virtualisation platform vendor with a UK entity serving public sector and defence-adjacent customers through the G-Cloud framework and partnership with UK government-cleared cloud providers.

## What they do

Denodo Technologies develops and licenses a data virtualisation and integration platform that provides a logical abstraction layer across disparate data sources, enabling fast data access without physical replication. The company was founded in 1999 in A Coruña, Spain, and is headquartered in Palo Alto, California, with offices in London and approximately 764 employees globally. Its UK entity, Denodo Technologies Limited, is registered at 5 New Street Square, London. Denodo's platform is used across UK central government departments, and the company has partnered with UKCloudX to deliver data virtualisation capability to defence and government customers operating in secure cloud environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Denodo Platform** - data virtualisation and integration platform for data engineers and analytics teams, enabling unified data access across relational databases, cloud stores, APIs and legacy systems without data movement.
- **Denodo Enterprise Plus** - enterprise-tier licensing available through the UK G-Cloud Digital Marketplace.
- **Data Fabric** - logical data delivery layer with embedded data governance, quality and policy management.
