# Drone Band Ltd

> UK start-up producing a physical, non-kinetic counter-drone capture device for security and defence use in environments where jamming or explosive methods are unsuitable.

## What they do

Drone Band Ltd is a London-based start-up incorporated in January 2023. It has developed the Crown, a 3D-printed, drone-mounted mechanical capture device that intercepts hostile or unauthorised multi-rotor drones mid-flight using a patent-pending physical mechanism with no propellants or pyrotechnics. An interceptor drone deploys the Crown, which entangles the target drone's rotors on contact; the captured drone separates and descends under a parachute for safe recovery and forensic examination. The system is positioned as a terminal layer within wider layered counter-UAS architectures, suited to urban areas, critical infrastructure, prisons, events and sensitive environments.

## Products and solutions

- **The Crown** - 3D-printed mechanical counter-drone capture device; patent pending; fits a range of FPV carrier drones; recovers the captured drone under parachute; no propellants, no pyrotechnics; reusable; combined system weight approximately 500g in the lightest configuration.
