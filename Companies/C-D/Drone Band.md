# Drone Band

> UK developer of physical counter-UAS capture technology for security and defence applications.

## What they do

Drone Band develops counter-UAS (C-UAS) technologies focused on safe physical interdiction of rogue drones. The company's approach centres on lightweight, reusable drone-mounted capture devices designed to intercept multicopter targets and bring them down under controlled parachute descent, minimising collateral risk in urban and sensitive environments. Drone Band holds pending UK, Hong Kong, and PCT published patents for its capture technology.

## Products and solutions

- **Crown** - a 3D-printed FPV drone attachment that physically captures target drones mid-air. Weighing under 100g, it is compatible with a wide range of FPV carrier drones, recovers the target under parachute, and is reusable. Combined system weight (carrier, Crown, and parachute) is approximately 500g in its lightest configuration.
