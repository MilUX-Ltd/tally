# Dorset Software Services Limited

> Poole-based software development and digital consultancy delivering bespoke systems for defence, public sector, and private sector clients since 1987.

## What they do

Dorset Software provides agile software development, system maintenance, and bespoke application delivery to public and private organisations. The company holds ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials, and Cyber Essentials Plus accreditations and is listed on the Crown Commercial Service G-Cloud framework. It publicly lists defence as a sector it supports alongside the NHS and other government bodies, and is an exhibitor at Defence Engage. The company converts complex client requirements into cost-effective software systems, working on-site or remotely across the UK.

## Products and solutions

- **Bespoke software development** - agile delivery of new systems and applications to customer specification for defence and public sector clients.
- **System maintenance and support** - ongoing BAU support and enhancement of existing technology platforms.
- **Digital consultancy** - advisory and delivery services helping organisations plan and execute technology change programmes.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** G-Cloud (Crown Commercial Service)
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
