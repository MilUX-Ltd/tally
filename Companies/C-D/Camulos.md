# Camulos

> Defence and national security company applying data science and artificial intelligence to cyber operations, analytics and cloud architecture.

## What they do

Camulos applies data science, artificial intelligence and human expertise to national security and cyber problems. Its work covers autonomous cyber operations, hybrid cloud architectures for national security partners, and intelligent analytics. The company was incorporated in 2020 and is formed and led by a team with experience in defence, national security and cyber organisations.

## Products and solutions

- **Autonomous cyber operations** - applying AI and data science to cyber operations for national security partners.
- **Hybrid cloud architectures** - cloud architecture work enabling national security partners to deliver new capabilities.
- **Intelligent analytics** - analytics services drawing on data science and AI.
