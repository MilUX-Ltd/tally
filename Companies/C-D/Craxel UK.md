# Craxel UK

> US-founded graph database and AI decision-intelligence software company with a Belfast hub targeting UK and European defence and financial services markets.

## What they do

Craxel UK Ltd is the UK subsidiary of Craxel Inc. (Fairfax, Virginia, USA), incorporated in Belfast in December 2025 with Invest Northern Ireland backing. The parent company, founded in 2007, builds patented high-performance graph database software and AI decision-intelligence platforms for defence, national security, and financial services customers. The Belfast hub, planned to grow to 27 staff by 2028, is the company's primary base for UK and European market development, with a partnership with Queen's University Belfast Centre for Secure Information Technologies (CSIT). The company is an ADS Group member and signed the Armed Forces Covenant in May 2026.

## Products and solutions

- **Black Forest Knowledge Infrastructure** - patented graph database platform delivering O(1) query performance at large scale; supports time-series graph analysis, real-time observability, elastic ingest pipelines, geospatial analytics, edge computing, and IoT data management.
- **Black Forest Reaper** - defensive cyber analytics platform built on Black Forest, enabling real-time threat detection and analysis at scale; assessed "Awardable" on the US DoD CDAO Tradewinds Solutions Marketplace.
