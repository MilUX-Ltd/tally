# DHL Global Forwarding (UK) Limited

> UK entity of Deutsche Post DHL Group providing air, ocean, road and rail freight forwarding with a dedicated public sector and defence logistics capability.

## What they do

DHL Global Forwarding (UK) Limited is the freight forwarding arm of Deutsche Post DHL Group in the United Kingdom, registered at Staines-upon-Thames. The company is an ADS Group member and holds SC21 Bronze and SC21 OE accreditations, reflecting supply chain performance standards relevant to the aerospace and defence sector. DHL Global Forwarding operates dedicated aerospace and aviation logistics services including time-critical AOG solutions, engine transport and hazardous materials handling for defence industry suppliers and government clients. Its public sector practice provides tailored logistics for international governments and defence manufacturers, with stated close collaboration with Western-led Ministries of Defence.

The UK entity previously traded as DHL Logistics (UK) Limited and, before that, as Danzas Limited. The parent group Deutsche Post DHL is headquartered in Bonn, Germany; this is a foreign-headquartered entity with a UK-registered subsidiary.

## Products and solutions

- **Aero AOG** - 24-hour emergency freight for Aircraft on Ground situations, available around the clock.
- **Aero Critical** - next-available-flight service for time-sensitive aerospace and defence parts.
- **Aero Engines** - dedicated transport for high-value, shock-sensitive aircraft engines by air.
- **Public Sector and Defence Logistics** - tailored freight forwarding for governments and defence industry suppliers, covering hazardous materials, out-of-gauge transport, charter services and customs clearance.
