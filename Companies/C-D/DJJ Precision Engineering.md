# DJJ Precision Engineering

> CNC precision engineering manufacturer supplying machined components to defence, aerospace, automotive, motorsport and other sectors from a facility in Pontypool, South Wales.

## What they do

DJJ Precision Engineering Ltd is a 24/7 CNC precision machining and manufacturing company based in Pontypool, South Wales, established in 1976. It produces CNC turned and milled components, broaching, prototypes, assemblies, sub-assemblies and laser-marked parts for over 200 industries including defence, aerospace, automotive, motorsport, medical and rail. The company holds JOSCAR registration as an approved defence supplier and is a Ford Global Tier 1 manufacturer. It has been a signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC machining** - precision CNC turning, milling, broaching, honing, grinding and ID marking from a 10,000 sq ft facility, with 24/7 manufacturing capability.
- **Prototype and batch production** - design and prototype expertise through to full batch production, with CMM inspection, PPAP, ISIR, IMDS and C of C certification.
- **One-stop-shop supply** - surface finishing, treatments, stock management, kanban and global delivery services.
