# CSA Cyber

> Gloucester-based cyber security managed services and consultancy provider, NCSC-approved CHECK company, with a client base including UK government and MoD organisations.

## What they do

Cyber Security Associates Ltd, trading as CSA Cyber, was founded in 2013 by former UK government cyber specialists and operates a 24/7 Security Operations Centre (SOC) in Gloucester. The company provides cyber consultancy, penetration testing, managed detection and response, and incident response services. CSA Cyber holds NCSC CHECK status and serves government organisations including the Ministry of Defence, the Foreign and Commonwealth Office and large UK enterprises. FluidOne invested in CSA in 2020 and CSA acquired SureCloud Cyber Services in March 2024.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed Detection and Response (MDR)** - 24/7 SOC-based monitoring and threat detection using Microsoft Sentinel and SentinelOne.
- **Penetration testing** - NCSC CHECK and CREST-approved application, infrastructure, product and cloud testing.
- **BorderPoint** - CSA's proprietary security incident detection platform providing real-time monitoring and alerting.
- **Incident response** - emergency response, retainer services and tabletop exercises.
- **Cyber Essentials certification** - CE and CE+ assessments as an accredited certification body.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** NCSC CHECK, CREST, ISO 27001, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Certification Body, Microsoft Solutions Partner (Security), Microsoft Advanced Solutions Partner (Cloud Security, Threat Protection)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
