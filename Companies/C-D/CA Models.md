# CA Models Ltd

> Rapid prototyping, additive manufacturing and model making company serving aerospace, defence and other sectors.

## What they do

CA Models is a rapid prototyping and model making business based in Stirling, Scotland, established in 1983. It works across aerospace, automotive, defence, marine, medical, oil and gas and product design. Capabilities include additive manufacturing in titanium, aluminium and stainless steel, SLA, SLS, FDM and DLP printing, five-axis CNC machining, vacuum casting and specialist model making. CA Models is part of the Prototal group following acquisition by Prototal Industries (Sweden); the Stirling operation continues under the CA Models name.

## Products and solutions

- **Additive manufacturing** - metal and polymer 3D printing including titanium, aluminium and stainless steel.
- **Precision CNC machining** - five-axis machining for prototype and production parts.
- **Model making and finishing** - vacuum casting, painting, powder coating, anodising and plating.
