# Daring Futures
> Defence-focused agile coaching and transformation consultancy partnering with aerospace, defence, security and advanced engineering organisations.
## What they do
Daring Futures strengthens delivery capability, uplifts team performance and embeds transformation for organisations in aerospace, defence, security and advanced engineering. The firm operates as a fractional service provider, offering part-time coaching and advisory support without the overhead of full-time hires. ADS Group member.
## Products and solutions
- **Agile coaching** - Team-level and leadership coaching applying Lean/Kanban and agile frameworks.
- **Transformation consultancy** - Fractional delivery and change support across complex programmes.
