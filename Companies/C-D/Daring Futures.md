# Daring Futures

> Defence-focused agile coaching and transformation consultancy partnering with aerospace, defence, security and advanced engineering organisations.

## What they do

Daring Futures is a boutique consultancy, describing itself as offering a blend of research and development with coaching for execution. It partners with organisations in aerospace, defence, security and advanced engineering to strengthen delivery capability, uplift team performance and embed transformation. The firm operates as a fractional service provider, offering high-trust, part-time coaching and advisory support to leaders managing complex change, without the overhead of full-time hires. It states its approaches have been applied across sectors including military, banking, central government and startups.

## Products and solutions

- **Agile coaching** - Team-level and leadership coaching applying Lean/Kanban and agile delivery frameworks.
- **Transformation consultancy** - Fractional delivery and change support across complex programmes, covering research and development, innovation, and lean-agile delivery.
- **Leadership and team coaching** - Coaching support for leaders and teams navigating delivery transformation.
- **Value Stream Management Foundation (VSMF)** - Training course delivered as a DevOps Institute partner.
