# Cooneen Defence

> Design, manufacture and supply of operational and combat clothing for the armed forces.

## What they do

Cooneen Defence, part of the Cooneen Group, designs, manufactures and supplies operational and combat clothing to the UK Ministry of Defence and to police and military authorities. Its range covers items from cargo trousers and berets to parade wear, medical wear, flight suits and marine coveralls, using technical textiles and heat management systems. The company runs managed service centres in Amesbury and Northern Ireland providing logistics, despatch and stock provision.

## Products and solutions

- **Operational and combat clothing** - design, manufacture and high-volume supply of military and police garments.
- **Managed service and logistics** - bulk and single-unit delivery, despatch and emergency stock provision through managed service centres.
