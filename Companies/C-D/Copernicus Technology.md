# Copernicus Technology Ltd

> Through-life engineering, intermittent fault detection and aircrew and maintenance training provider for defence and aerospace, based in Elgin, Scotland.

## What they do

Copernicus Technology Ltd (CTL) was established in 2008 to investigate No Fault Found (NFF) using intermittent fault detection test equipment. It has since expanded into through-life technical engineering services aimed at reducing total ownership costs and improving equipment availability and operational reliability across the aerospace, transport and technology sectors. The company operates in the UK and Australia.

## Products and solutions

- **Intermittent fault detection** - test equipment and data analysis to reduce No Fault Found events and equipment downtime.
- **Training management systems** - DSAT-compliant training management for aircrew and maintenance training across defence capabilities.
- **MPA and ISR support** - specialist training and consultancy support for Maritime Patrol Aircraft and Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance operations.
