# CodaOctopus Martech

> Portland-based designer and manufacturer of electronic and electromechanical systems and test solutions for defence, aerospace and subsea, working as a subcontractor to UK prime defence contractors.

## What they do

Coda Octopus Martech is a designer and manufacturer of electronic and electromechanical systems and special test solutions for defence, aerospace, subsea and science applications. It is based at Osprey Quay, Portland, Dorset. Its business model is the supply of sub-assemblies into larger mission-critical programmes as a subcontractor to prime defence contractors, with scope typically running from concept and design through prototype, manufacture and post-sale support.

The company has operated as a subcontractor to UK and European prime defence contractors since 1988 and holds pre-qualifications used to take part in major defence contract programmes. It is part of Coda Octopus Group, Inc, a US company listed on Nasdaq.

## Products and solutions

- **Bespoke electronic and electromechanical systems** - custom sub-contract design and manufacture of high-integrity devices in small quantities.
- **Special test solutions** - test equipment for high-end defence and subsea applications.
- **Defence sub-assemblies** - sub-components supplied into mission-critical defence programmes run by prime contractors.
