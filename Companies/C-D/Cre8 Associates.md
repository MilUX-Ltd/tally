# Cre8 Associates

> UK micro-SME specialising in EMC engineering, covert and armoured vehicle electrical integration, and specialist electronic products for military, law enforcement, and government customers.

## What they do

Cre8 Associates is an electrical and electronic engineering consultancy and manufacturer operating from Bruntingthorpe Proving Ground, Lutterworth. Core work covers EMC design, filter manufacture, and pre-compliance and compliance testing to Def Stan 59-411 and MIL-STD-461; RF communications including electronic countermeasures system design; and armoured and covert vehicle electrical integration, wiring looms, and power management for government and agency customers worldwide. The company has manufactured and deployed proprietary products including the Start Safe II battery protection system and the LIMS Vehicle Blackout system.

## Products and solutions

- **EMC filters and testing** - design, manufacture, and testing to UK MOD Def Stan 59-411 and US MIL-STD-461 military standards.
- **LIMS Vehicle Blackout** - proprietary system announced 2018; manages electrical load shedding on specialist vehicles.
- **Start Safe II** - battery protection system; 200+ units shipped.
- **Shield Safe** - further battery and protection product; 50+ units deployed as of 2014.
- **Armoured and covert vehicle integration** - bespoke wiring, power management, and data acquisition installations for military and agency vehicles in UK, Middle East, and USA.
