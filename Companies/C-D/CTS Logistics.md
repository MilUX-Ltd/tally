# CTS Logistics Ltd

> Specialist high-consequence logistics provider with over 20 years serving the defence, nuclear and healthcare sectors.

## What they do

CTS Logistics delivers specialist road transportation, global freight forwarding, secure warehousing and site logistics for defence, nuclear and healthcare customers. The company manages all UN dangerous goods classes 1-9, operates a security and safety-enhanced vehicle fleet, and project-manages complex international moves for UK and overseas government departments. It holds Regulated Agent status under IATA.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence logistics** - specialist transportation and warehousing for military consignments, with regular presence at UK and European military sites.
- **Dangerous goods freight** - road, air and sea forwarding covering all UN hazard classes including Section 5 weapons and ammunition.
- **Secure warehousing and screening** - IATA-compliant secure warehousing and cargo screening services.
- **International government freight** - project management of complex international moves for UK and overseas government departments.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** JOSCAR, ITAR Approval, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 27001:2022, ISO 45001, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus; certified under the UK/US Defence Trade Cooperation Treaty Approved Community; Authorised User under the AUKUS Open General Licence.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, IATA, EGADD UK.
