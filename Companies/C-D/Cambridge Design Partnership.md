# Cambridge Design Partnership

> Employee-owned product development and engineering consultancy working across healthcare, consumer and industrial markets, including ruggedised and mission-critical devices.

## What they do

Cambridge Design Partnership (CDP) is an end-to-end product development consultancy that takes products from early insight through prototyping to production. Its interdisciplinary teams of physicists, engineers, designers and human-factors specialists work across three markets: healthcare (drug delivery, life sciences, MedTech), consumer (packaged goods, consumer technology, reduced-risk products) and industrial. The industrial market covers specialised tools and equipment, connected systems and mission-critical devices, including precision mechanisms, ruggedised devices and advanced sensing for sectors such as manufacturing, laboratory science and agritech. The company became fully employee-owned in 2018 through an employee ownership trust, and has offices in Cambridge, UK and Raleigh, North Carolina.

## Products and solutions

- **Product development and engineering services** - end-to-end design and engineering, from applied science and product strategy through electronics and software engineering to manufacturing engineering and industrialisation.
- **Design verification and test engineering** - prototyping and lab testing of new products to prove performance and meet regulatory standards.
- **Ruggedised and mission-critical device development** - precision mechanisms, ruggedised devices and advanced sensing and automation for industrial and mission-critical applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015 (quality management), ISO 13485:2016 (medical device quality management), ISO 27001:2022 (information security management).
