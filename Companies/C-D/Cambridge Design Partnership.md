# Cambridge Design Partnership

> Employee-owned product development and engineering consultancy with a defence and security capability alongside its consumer, healthcare and industrial work.

## What they do

Cambridge Design Partnership (CDP) is an end-to-end product development consultancy that takes products from early insight and ideas through prototyping to production. Its multidisciplinary teams combine science, engineering and design across consumer, healthcare, industrial and defence and security sectors. The company became fully employee-owned in 2018 through an employee ownership trust.

## Products and solutions

- **Product development and engineering services** - design and engineering of complex, high-reliability systems and products for clients, including a dedicated defence and security capability.
