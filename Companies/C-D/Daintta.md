# Daintta

> Data, digital, cyber and national-security consultancy serving government and defence.

## What they do

Daintta is a UK consultancy providing technical and business expertise across data, digital, cyber security and telecommunications for the national security, law enforcement, central government and defence sectors. It works in complex environments to help clients secure and protect the UK and its interests, and to make informed decisions. It maintains offices in London and Cheltenham.

## Products and solutions

- **Data intelligence** - data lifecycle management, data strategy and data science capability development.
- **Cyber security** - cyber strategy, Cyber Security Operations Centre implementation, and risk assessment aligned to government standards.
- **Networks and communications** - telecoms security and network complexity work, including 5G security testing.
- **CyINTer** - AI-supported cyber assessment tool aligned to the NCSC Cyber Assessment Framework.
