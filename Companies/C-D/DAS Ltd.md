# DAS Ltd

> Independent management and technical consultancy providing strategic decision support to defence, government, and critical national infrastructure clients worldwide.

## What they do

Decision Analysis Services Limited (DAS Ltd) is a Basingstoke-based consultancy specialising in systems thinking, programme and portfolio management, investment modelling, and AI-enabled analytics. Defence clients include the UK Ministry of Defence, Australian Department of Defence, US Department of Defense, and New Zealand Defence Force, with domain coverage spanning submarines, air systems, ships, land systems, C4ISTAR, and infrastructure. DAS operates from the UK, Australia, and Poland.

## Products and solutions

- **Intelligent Futures / PreVueAI** - AI-enhanced horizon scanning and strategic foresight service designed for use in highly secure environments, including defence.
- **P3M Advisory** - Programme, portfolio, and project management consulting; accredited P3M3 assessors.
- **Investment Modelling** - HM Treasury Green Book-compliant business case development.
- **Systems Engineering** - Complex systems analysis and decision support for major acquisition programmes.
