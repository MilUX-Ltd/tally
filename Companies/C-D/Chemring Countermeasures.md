# Chemring Countermeasures

> UK manufacturer of expendable countermeasures for air and naval platforms.

## What they do

Chemring Countermeasures is the UK-based manufacturer of expendable countermeasures within the Chemring group. It develops and manufactures flares, chaff and decoys to protect military and civilian aircraft and naval vessels against guided missile threats. The company operates from a site on the edge of Salisbury Plain.

## Products and solutions

- **Aircraft countermeasures** - conventional and advanced flares, chaff and decoys for military and civilian platforms.
- **Naval countermeasures** - infrared and radio frequency decoys, and a trainable naval decoy launcher.

## Group relationship

Chemring Countermeasures Limited is a UK subsidiary of Chemring Group Plc (LSE: CHG).
