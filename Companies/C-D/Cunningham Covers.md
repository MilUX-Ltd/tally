# Cunningham Covers Limited

> Northern Ireland manufacturer of bespoke protective covers for military vehicles, weapons, naval vessels and high-value defence assets.

## What they do

Cunningham Covers designs and manufactures custom textile protective covers for defence and other high-consequence sectors. The company has served the defence market for over 40 years, producing covers for military vehicles, artillery, small arms, battle tanks, ships and submarines. Manufacturing takes place in a 50,000 square foot facility in Maghera, Co Londonderry, with products shipped globally. Cunningham has received the King's Award for Enterprise in recognition of its international trade.

## Products and solutions

- **Military vehicle covers** - moisture-resistant, waterproof and breathable canvas covers designed to protect wheeled and tracked military vehicles operating in harsh terrain and environments.
- **Weapon and ammunition covers** - protective covers for small arms through to artillery and large-calibre weapons systems, designed to prevent corrosion and environmental damage.
- **Naval and submarine covers** - salt-water-resistant covers for naval vessels and submarines, protecting against corrosion.
- **Impact protection** - covers providing impact protection for defence equipment including vehicles, weaponry and ammunition.
