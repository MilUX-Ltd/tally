# Claresys

> Designer and manufacturer of covert video surveillance optics for police, military and government end users.

## What they do

Claresys designs and manufactures patented covert camera optics and opto-electronic systems for law enforcement, security services and armed forces. Several of its underpinning technologies were originally developed by the UK's Defence Science and Technology Laboratory (DSTL) and spun out via Ploughshare. The company is now part of the Danish-headquartered Covidence group, giving it an integrated offering across recording, transmission, control and video analytics.

## Products and solutions

- **Miniature pinhole camera lenses** - covert optics viewing through pinholes of around 0.3mm.
- **Covert pinhole PTZ lenses** - pan, tilt and zoom lenses for C-mount cameras.
- **Telephoto and long-range PTZ lenses** - compact and long-range covert surveillance optics.
