# CrisisCast Ltd

> Immersive security and military training organisation using roleplay actors, casualty simulation, and virtual reality to support defence, emergency services, and government customers.

## What they do

CrisisCast provides scenario-based crisis training using professionally trained roleplay actors, film-grade casualty simulation, and virtual reality platforms. The company works with the military, police, emergency services, hospitals, and government bodies, delivering simulated mass casualty incidents, hostile threat exercises, and pre-deployment cultural immersion for special forces. Specialist actors are trained by behavioural psychologists and rehearsed in criminal and victim behaviour.

## Products and solutions

- **Casualty Simulation (CAS SIM)** - Film-grade moulage makeup, trained amputee actors, and scenario production for battlefield and emergency medical training.
- **Project Tyrion / Human Domain Matrix** - VR prototype training concept developed with the British Army, bringing immersive roleplay into a military virtual reality environment.
- **Pre-deployment VR** - Immersive psychological encounter simulations for special forces, recreating target cultural environments.
- **Crisis Management Exercises** - Scripted live scenarios testing communications, lockdown and evacuation procedures, emergency services response, and media handling.
- **Deepfake Training** - Simulated deepfake and shallowfake scenarios for crisis management exercises and consultancy.
