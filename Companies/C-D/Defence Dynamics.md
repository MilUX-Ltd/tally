# Defence Dynamics

> Manufacturer of anti-vibration and shock isolation mounting solutions for military, marine, aerospace and commercial applications.

## What they do

Defence Dynamics manufactures anti-vibration and shock isolation mounting products for military, naval, aerospace and commercial customers. The company's Admiralty range of naval mountings is the preferred and recommended choice of the UK MoD and is used by navies worldwide. Mounts are manufactured in the UK to defence industry specifications and comply with relevant international standards. The company uses proprietary calculation software to verify mounting selection and predict the response of mounted systems to shock and vibration, providing design guidance alongside its products.

## Products and solutions

- **Admiralty range naval mountings** - shock and anti-vibration mounts for naval vessels; recommended by the UK MoD and in service with multiple navies.
- **Military and aerospace mountings** - anti-vibration and shock isolation solutions for land, air and commercial platform applications.
