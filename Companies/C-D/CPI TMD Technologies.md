# CPI TMD Technologies Limited

> Manufacturer of microwave power sources, high-voltage power supplies and transmitters for radar, electronic warfare and communications.

## What they do

CPI TMD Technologies manufactures microwave power sources, high-voltage power supplies and transmitters for radar, electronic warfare, communications, industrial testing and scientific applications. The business traces its roots to the microwave tube research division of EMI Electronics, established in the early 1940s to develop high power klystrons for the first airborne radars. It is a division of Communications & Power Industries (CPI), which acquired TMD Technologies in July 2021; the UK operation is in Hayes, Middlesex, while the CPI parent is headquartered in the United States.

## Products and solutions

- **Microwave power modules and travelling wave tubes** - power sources for radar, EW and communications systems.
- **High-voltage power supplies** - including rugged units for defence platforms.
- **Rugged and instrumentation amplifiers** - for radar, electronic warfare, communications and EMC testing.
