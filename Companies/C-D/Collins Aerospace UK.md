# Collins Aerospace UK

> UK operations of Collins Aerospace, an RTX business supplying avionics, actuation and mission systems for the global aerospace and defence industry.

## What they do

Collins Aerospace, an RTX business (RTX was formerly Raytheon Technologies), designs and delivers integrated solutions for the global aerospace and defence industry. Its portfolio spans avionics, mission systems, actuation, aerostructures and connected aviation. The UK operations include manufacturing and engineering centres of excellence in the West Midlands and the South West. The UK trading entity is Rockwell Collins UK Limited; the parent RTX Corporation is headquartered in Arlington, Virginia.

## Products and solutions

- **Avionics and flight deck** - navigation, communications, datalink and attitude and heading reference systems.
- **Actuation systems** - Wolverhampton is the global headquarters for actuation manufacturing, including electric thrust reverser actuation systems (elecTRAS).
- **Electronic controls and motor systems** - Solihull centre of excellence.
- **Control, navigation and guidance** - Plymouth, for surface and air applications across defence and aerospace.
- **Aerostructures and nacelle systems** - nacelles, thrust reversers and composite aircraft doors.
