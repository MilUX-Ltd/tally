# Dontaur Engineering Limited

> Northern Ireland precision CNC machining company producing detailed aerospace and defence components and assemblies to AS9100 standard, with customer approvals from Airbus, GE Aviation, and Bombardier.

## What they do

Established in 1976, Dontaur Engineering manufactures quality-assured precision components, assemblies, and kits for the aerospace, defence, and security sectors. The company holds AS9100:2016 and ISO 9001:2015 approval and has maintained SC21 Silver status since 2013. Its core machining capabilities cover 3, 4, and 5-axis milling, multi-axis turning, grinding, thread rolling, and assembly, working in aluminium, steel, titanium, and Inconel. In 2002 Dontaur opened its sister company Aerospace Metal Finishers, providing surface treatment and becoming one of the few one-stop-shops for manufacturing, treating, and assembling aircraft parts in the UK.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision machined components and assemblies** - detailed components, kits, and assemblies manufactured to AS9100 standard for aerospace and defence customers.
- **5-axis machined parts** - complex geometries in titanium, Inconel, and aluminium using DMG/Mori and similar 5-axis centres.
- **Surface treatment and finishing** - via sister company Aerospace Metal Finishers, providing an integrated supply offering for treated and assembled aerostructure parts.
