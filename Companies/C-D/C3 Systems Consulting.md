# C3 Systems Consulting Ltd

> Independent consultancy providing subject matter expertise and project teams to defence and security clients in the UK and overseas.

## What they do

C3 Systems Consulting is an independent company providing subject matter expertise and project teams to defence and security clients across government and industry. The company describes more than 20 years of experience supporting defence and security customers, with work spanning operational analysis, research, development, architecture, implementation, assessment, and the provision of cyber and IT services and solutions.
