# Datalink Electronics

> Contract electronics manufacturer serving aerospace, defence, medical, and industrial sectors, AS9100 certified and based in Loughborough.

## What they do

Datalink Electronics is a UK contract electronics design and manufacturing company established in 1983. The company specialises in low to medium volume, high mix product profiles, with services spanning PCB assembly layout, fast-turnaround new product introduction, cable and box builds, production testing, and final product assembly. It holds AS9100D, ISO 9001, and ISO 13485 certifications, underpinning its work in aerospace and defence.

## Products and solutions

- **PCBA design and manufacture** - PCB layout, component placement, and assembly for high-reliability applications including aerospace and defence.
- **Cable and box builds** - custom cable assemblies and box-build manufacturing for mission-critical environments.
- **New product introduction (NPI)** - fast-turnaround NPI service from concept through to production.
