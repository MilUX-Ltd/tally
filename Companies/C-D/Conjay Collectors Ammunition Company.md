# Conjay Collectors Ammunition Co. Limited

> Defence supplier of armour and ballistics ammunition, explosives and narcotics test kits, and counter-IED equipment, trading in the sector since 1954.

## What they do

Conjay has traded within the defence sector since 1954, initially supplying ammunition to the British MOD and police, then to armour manufacturers and ballistics test ranges. The company supplies explosive test kits, munitions for ballistics and armour trials across international test standards, and counter-IED equipment, and operates a design bureau producing bespoke machines for ballistics research. The wider Conjay group has expanded into security technologies including counter-UAV, secure communications and optics.

## Products and solutions

- **Armour and ballistics ammunition** - munitions supplied for armour and ballistics trials against international test standards including STANAG and NIJ.
- **Explosive test kits** - EXPEN, EXPRAY and DROPEX + kits for confirmation of explosives traces.
- **Narcotic test kits** - drug detection kits.
- **Counter-IED equipment** - equipment and project support for counter-terrorism requirements.
- **Conjay Design Bureau** - bespoke machines for ballistics research, including an automatic bullet puller.
