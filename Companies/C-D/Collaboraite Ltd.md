# Collaboraite Ltd
> Secure, user-centred product design and build leveraging data, analytics and AI for defence operational challenges.
## What they do
Collaboraite Ltd designs and builds secure, user-centred products that use data, analytics and AI capabilities to address operational problems. The company is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park, positioning it firmly within the UK defence innovation ecosystem.
