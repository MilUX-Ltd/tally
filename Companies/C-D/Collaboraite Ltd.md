# Collaboraite Ltd

> Secure, user-centred data and AI products for research, investigation and data analysis, built for law enforcement, government and defence customers.

## What they do

Collaboraite designs and builds secure, user-centred products that use data, analytics and AI to address operational challenges, working through design thinking and agile delivery. Its data and AI toolset, licensed as the Collaboraite capability and accessed through the Collab.hq interface, is built to accelerate research, investigation and data analysis, with a stated focus on internet intelligence and investigation (i3), open-source intelligence (OSINT), and consented or open public data. Collaboraite is part of the Investigo Group, alongside Vestigo Consulting Ltd and Internet Investigation Solutions (IIS), whose Long Arm platform provides the secure hosting environment through which the Collaboraite capability is delivered. The company is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.

## Products and solutions

- **Collaboraite capability** - data and AI products and engines, including data science, machine learning, deep learning and natural language processing (NLP), plus automation and robotic process automation (RPA), accessed via the Collab.hq interface.
- **Data and AI solutions consultancy** - digital, data and analytics discovery and transformation strategy; data fusion and migration via API; data lake, warehousing and virtualisation; data visualisation through licensed (Power BI) and open analytics tools; secure-by-design, GDS-compliant application and product build.
- **Agile and design thinking consultancy** - agile coaching, design thinking and agile transformation training and implementation support, listed separately on G-Cloud.
- Solutions are hosted in UK Police Assured Secure Facilities (PASF) private data centres with disaster recovery provision.
