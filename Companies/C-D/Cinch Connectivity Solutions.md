# Cinch Connectivity Solutions

> Manufacturer of interconnect products and cable assemblies, including mil-spec connectors for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Cinch manufactures connectors, cable assemblies and interconnect products for harsh environment applications across aerospace, defence, industrial and communications markets. Its defence range includes rugged mil-spec connectors and cable assemblies designed, manufactured and tested for military operating conditions. The UK arm operates a fibre-optic facility near London Stansted that holds MIL-DTL-790 status, and the wider business holds FAA-PMA approvals for connectors and harnesses used in commercial and military applications.

Cinch is part of Bel Fuse Inc., a US-listed group that acquired Cinch Connectors in 2010. The Cinch business operates in the UK through two registered companies that are part of the same group: Cinch Connectivity Solutions Limited (company number 02086232), wholly owned by Cinch Connectors Limited (company number 02178707). Both are ADS members. This page covers the single Cinch business; the two entries are the same trading operation. UK manufacturing is at Worksop, England.

## Products and solutions

- **Mil-spec connectors** - rugged connectors and cable assemblies built and tested to military standards for defence platforms.
- **Fibre-optic interconnect** - Mil-Spec D38999 fibre-optic connectors produced at the UK facility holding MIL-DTL-790 status.
- **RF and microwave components** - RF connectors, microwave components and cable assemblies.
- **Specialist connectors** - D-subminiature, filtered D-subminiature, Micro-D and modular connector ranges.
