# Carbon ThreeSixty Ltd

> Advanced composite structures for aerospace, defence, automotive and energy.

## What they do

Carbon ThreeSixty is an innovator in advanced composite structures, providing turnkey solutions across aerospace, defence, automotive and energy. The company specialises in resin transfer moulding (RTM) and novel preforming techniques such as tailored fibre placement (TFP) to produce highly optimised, cost-effective lightweight structures. It was established in 2017 and is based in Chippenham.

## Products and solutions

- **Carbon fibre wheels** - for automotive and defence applications.
- **Rotor blades** - for UAV and eVTOL vehicles.
- **PEMD components** - parts for power electronics, machines and drives applications, including electric motor components.
- **Hydrogen storage solutions** - composite storage structures.
- **TFP and RTM engineering** - design and manufacture services using tailored fibre placement and resin transfer moulding.
