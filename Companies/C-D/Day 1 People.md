# Day 1 People

> Defence and national security programme and bid management consultancy based in Wiltshire.

## What they do

Day 1 People is a small consultancy providing programme management, bid and capture management, and PMO support to clients across defence, national security, and aerospace. The company operates from Chippenham, Wiltshire, in the Defence Digital and MOD Corsham cluster. Its client base spans government departments and prime defence industry contractors. The founding team includes personnel with service backgrounds and defence procurement experience. The company was incorporated in 2017 as EH3 Group Limited and renamed Day 1 People Limited in August 2021.

## Products and solutions

- **Bid and Capture Management** - Pink, Red, and Gold team reviews using Shipley Best Practice methodology, applied to Cat A MOD and defence industry bids.
- **Programme and Portfolio Management (P3M)** - project, programme, and portfolio management support for defence programmes.
- **PMO in a Box** - configurable project management office support packages.
- **Campaign and Capture Management** - market shaping and pipeline development for defence and national security customers.
