# Cavendish Nuclear Limited

> Nuclear services company supporting the UK's defence submarine fleet and civil nuclear sector, a subsidiary of Babcock International Group.

## What they do

Cavendish Nuclear is a wholly owned subsidiary of Babcock International Group and is the UK's largest supplier to the nuclear industry. It provides nuclear engineering, support, decommissioning and waste management services across defence and civil programmes. In defence, the business supports the Royal Navy's nuclear-powered submarine fleet, including through-life support and life extension across submarine classes, and provides support to the Atomic Weapons Establishment and the Submarine Delivery Agency.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence submarine support** - through-life support and life extension for the UK submarine fleet.
- **Submarine decommissioning** - defuelling, decommissioning and dismantling of legacy submarines.
- **Civil nuclear services** - engineering, decommissioning, waste management and new-build support.
