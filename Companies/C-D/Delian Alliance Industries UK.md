# Delian Alliance Industries UK

> AI-first defence technology company building autonomous surveillance and counter-UAS systems for NATO and allied governments.

## What they do

Delian Alliance Industries develops autonomous systems for defence mission-critical applications, including surveillance towers, loitering munitions, electronic warfare and GPS-denied navigation. The company was originally founded in Athens, Greece in 2021 as Lambda Automata, with a UK entity (Delian Alliance Industries UK Ltd) incorporated in August 2024 and registered in St. Albans, Hertfordshire. It serves clients in the United Kingdom and Greece, with plans to expand across Europe.

## Products and solutions

- **LAST (Autonomous Surveillance Tower)** - Portable and static surveillance tower in civilian, marine and multi-domain configurations, fusing and geolocating data from multiple sensors.
- **Jericho** - Portable intelligence, communications and autonomy suite, interoperable with commercial drones and legacy sensor platforms.
- **OSIRIS** - GPS-denied navigation module for operations in contested environments.
- **M4K15** - Electronic warfare system for counter-UAS protection.
