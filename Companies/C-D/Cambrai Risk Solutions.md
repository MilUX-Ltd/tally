# Cambrai Risk Solutions

> UK risk management and protective security consultancy specialising in hostile environments, with a focus on Africa and the Middle East.

## What they do

Cambrai Risk Solutions provides threat and risk analysis, travel risk management, protective services, and security consultancy to private sector clients, NGOs, and government projects operating in high-risk environments. The company is operated by personnel with backgrounds in hostile environment operations. Geographic focus is Africa and the Middle East. Services are structured around pre-deployment threat analysis, real-time travel support, secure transportation, and strategic security advice.

## Products and solutions

- **Threat and Risk Analysis** - Adversary profiling, threat assessment, and tailored risk reporting.
- **Travel Risk Management** - Pre-travel briefings, real-time in-country support, and secure transportation.
- **Protective Services** - Secure movement of goods and assets in high-risk environments.
- **Security Consultancy** - Strategic advice and threat mitigation planning.
- **Hostile Environment Training** - Training for individuals and teams operating in high-risk locations.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015; ISO 31030:2021 (travel risk management); ISO 31000 (risk management principles)
