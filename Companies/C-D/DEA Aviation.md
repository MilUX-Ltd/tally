# DEA Aviation

> Specialist airborne operations provider offering ISR, aerial survey, flight calibration, and maritime surveillance services.

## What they do

DEA Aviation operates a fleet of fixed-wing aircraft from its base at Retford (Gamston) Airport, Nottinghamshire, delivering persistent airborne ISR for European border and security agencies, aerial survey and data acquisition, flight path validation and navigation aid calibration, and maritime surveillance. Over 30% of its workforce are former Armed Forces personnel. The company holds a CAA Air Operator's Certificate, EASA, and FAA approvals, and a Part 145 Maintenance Organisation Approval. In June 2025 it opened an R&D facility in Lincoln within the Greater Lincolnshire Defence and Security Cluster. DEA has secured two tranches of UK Export Finance-backed financing from Santander UK, specifically tied to ISR capability and export growth (2023 and 2025). The company was incorporated in 2006 as Diamond Executive Aviation Limited and renamed in December 2017.

## Products and solutions

- **Airborne ISR** - persistent 24/7/365 surveillance missions for European border agencies; intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance using multi-sensor airborne platforms.
- **Aerial survey and data acquisition** - multi-sensor imaging including a long-running contract with Ordnance Survey to capture national geographic imagery, extended through 2026.
- **Flight calibration** - ground navigation aid calibration covering ILS, VOR, NDB, and related systems.
- **Maritime and border surveillance** - dedicated sensor fits and operational procedures for maritime patrol and border monitoring tasks.
