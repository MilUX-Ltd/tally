# DroneTector Limited

> Glasgow firm building millimetre-wave radar to detect and track small, low-signature drones. Trading as Milliray.

## What they do

DroneTector, now trading as Milliray, builds high-frequency millimetre-wave radar systems designed to detect and track small, low-signature drones that conventional detection systems miss, from single nano or consumer drones to autonomous swarms. It positions cheap consumer drones as a security threat to airports, critical infrastructure, venues, VIP protection and defence, and offers a suite of sensors built around its millimetre-wave radar.

## Products and solutions

- **Millimetre-wave radar** - high-frequency radar purpose-built to detect and classify small and nano drones that other systems struggle with.
- **Sensor suite** - a broader set of sensors for drone detection and tracking.
