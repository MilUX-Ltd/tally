# Centimany

> UK firm providing managed services and tools for finance, business and commercial teams on government, defence and space programmes.

## What they do

Centimany was founded in 2008 to address economic, business and strategic challenges in complex programme acquisition and delivery. It provides managed services to government, defence and space customers. Its stated aim is to develop methods and tools that support finance, business and commercial teams assuring government programmes connected to UK security and prosperity.

The company is based at Broad Town Lodge, near Swindon. Related entities, Centimany Corp and Centimany Group LLC, are registered in Boca Raton, Florida.
