# Daher Aerospace Ltd

> French aerospace group providing integrated logistics, defence equipment, and aircraft manufacturing to aerospace and defence customers, with a UK operating base in the East Midlands.

## What they do

Daher is a family-owned French aerospace and industrial group, active in the UK since the 1960s. Its UK entity operates across integrated supply-chain logistics for aerospace and defence customers (including an active Airbus Defence and Space A400M logistics partnership), specialist packaging and kitting, vendor-managed inventory, MRO support, and export-controlled storage. The wider Daher group manufactures military tactical containers, mobile tactical shelters, and the TBM turboprop aircraft series, and is developing the EyePulse MALE drone demonstrator, with first flight achieved in 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **Military tactical containers** - over 200 types in laminated polyester, thermoformed aluminium, sandwich panel, and 19-inch rack formats; compliant with European military standards for transport and storage of weapons, aircraft components, engines, and communications systems.
- **Mobile tactical shelters** - configured for radar, communications, and operational missions.
- **Integrated logistics support** - supply-chain management, VMI, kitting, MRO support, and mid-life retrofit for aerospace and defence customers; delivered on-site at five customer locations across the UK.
- **EyePulse MALE drone** - medium altitude long endurance drone demonstrator; first flight 2025.
- **TBM turboprop aircraft** - civil and dual-use turboprop series (TBM 910/960 family) manufactured by Daher Aircraft.
