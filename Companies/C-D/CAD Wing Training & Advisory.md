# CAD Wing Training & Advisory

> UK micro-SME providing counter-UAS and tactical UAS training alongside a range of multi-spectral camouflage and concealment products.

## What they do

CAD Wing (trading name of Cadwing Ltd) operates across two verticals: specialist military training and camouflage systems. Training courses cover UAS operations, counter-UAS, SERE, and combat medicine, delivered in the UK and overseas. The camouflage product range addresses thermal, infrared, and visual signature reduction across personal, vehicle, and equipment applications. The company draws on experience of UAS and counter-UAS operations and positions products around lessons from recent conflicts.

## Products and solutions

- **Combat Net Defence (CND) System** - Counter-UAS camouflage and concealment system.
- **Flex 2D MSC Net** - Multi-spectral camouflage netting.
- **Flex Ghillie** - Multi-spectral ghillie camouflage.
- **Responder** - Counter-UAS concealment system.
- **Stargazer** - Arctic multi-spectral camouflage system.
- **BarrelEye** - Multi-spectrum signature reduction product.
- **Huntsman System** - Camouflage and concealment system.
- **SF Patrol Cloak / Sniper Patrol Cloak** - Personal camouflage cloaks.
- **Pilot Pod** - Camouflage product for UAS operators.
- **PDAR** - Camouflage and concealment product.
- **Larva Cloak** - Personal camouflage system.
