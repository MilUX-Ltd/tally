# Contruent

> Capital project management and project controls software for major programmes.

## What they do

Contruent provides cloud-based capital project management software for owners and engineering, procurement and construction firms running large and complex programmes. The platform, formerly known as ARES PRISM, integrates cost, schedule, change, risk and performance management across project portfolios. It is used in capital-intensive sectors including infrastructure, energy, mining and government, and the company states its software is used by defence agencies.

## Products and solutions

- **Contruent Enterprise** - lifecycle cost and schedule management software (formerly ARES PRISM) for capital programmes and megaprojects.
