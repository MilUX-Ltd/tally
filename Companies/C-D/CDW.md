# CDW UK

> Multi-brand IT solutions and services provider with a dedicated defence and public-sector practice.

## What they do

CDW is a technology solutions provider supplying hardware, software, cloud, networking and cyber security to business, government, education and healthcare customers in the UK and internationally. Its UK public-sector business includes a defence team of sector specialists who hold the required security compliance, supporting defence IT procurement and integration. The UK business is part of CDW Corporation, listed on the Nasdaq.

## Products and solutions

- **IT products and procurement** - multi-brand hardware and software supply.
- **Services and integration** - cloud, networking, cyber security and managed services.
- **Defence practice** - a dedicated defence-sector team and security-cleared specialists.
