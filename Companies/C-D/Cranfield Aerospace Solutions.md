# Cranfield Aerospace Solutions

> Aerospace design, modification and propulsion technology company, now also developing hydrogen-electric flight.

## What they do

Cranfield Aerospace Solutions (CAeS) is a UK aerospace company specialising in aircraft design, certification, complex modification and propulsion innovation, with around 30 years of heritage. It holds design organisation approvals for civil and military aircraft and runs programmes in special-mission integration, weapons and defensive-aids integration, and flight test. It is also developing hydrogen-electric, zero-emission propulsion and unmanned platforms.

## Products and solutions

- **Project Fresson** - hydrogen fuel-cell powertrain programme for passenger aircraft.
- **Genesis** - modular, scalable hydrogen fuel-cell propulsion system for aerospace.
- **Stingray (ST-5 / ST-7 / ST-15 / ST-18)** - family of hydrogen-electric, long-endurance, multi-role UAVs.
- **Aircraft modification and integration** - design, certification and integration services for existing civil and military platforms, including special-mission conversions and weapons and defensive-aids integration.
- **Consultancy** - aircraft design, systems integration, testing and analysis, and airworthiness certification.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR.
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; CAA Part 21J Design Organisation Approval; UK MOD MAA DAOS (military design organisation approval).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
