# DMG MORI UK

> UK subsidiary of the global precision machine tool manufacturer DMG MORI, supplying high-precision machining solutions to the aerospace, defence and space sectors.

## What they do

DMG MORI UK Limited is the British sales and service entity of DMG MORI, a Japanese-German machine tool manufacturer with operations in 45 countries and 128 locations worldwide. The UK entity is based in Coventry and provides the full DMG MORI product portfolio alongside training, maintenance and spare-parts support. The global group employs approximately 13,500 people across 18 production plants; the UK operation is a sales, service and demonstration centre rather than a manufacturing site.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC turning and milling machines** - multi-axis turning and milling centres used in aerospace, defence and space component manufacture.
- **Lasertec additive manufacturing systems** - laser deposition welding and hybrid additive/subtractive machining platforms for complex metal parts.
- **Automation and digital integration solutions** - end-to-end automation cells, digital manufacturing (DX) software and Green Transformation (GX) energy-monitoring tooling under the Machining Transformation (MX) programme.
- **Aerospace Excellence Centre support** - feasibility analysis, application technology development and turnkey solutions for aviation and space customers.
