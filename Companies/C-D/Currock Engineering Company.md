# Currock Engineering Company Ltd

> AS9100-approved precision CNC manufacturer supplying metal components to the defence and aerospace sectors from Chelmsford, Essex.

## What they do

Currock engineers and manufactures precision metal components and assemblies for the defence, aerospace, energy, and marine sectors. The company has been part of the UK defence supply chain for over 40 years, producing support weapons parts, missile components, and military communications parts. Services include CNC milling up to 6 axes, CNC turning, painting, clean rooms and assembly, CMM inspection, and project management.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision machined components** - CNC-milled and turned metal parts and assemblies to customer specification, covering support weapons, missile components, and military communications hardware.
- **Clean room assembly** - assembly and inspection services in a controlled environment, qualified to AS9100.
