# Control Risks

> Global risk and strategic consultancy covering political, security and integrity risk.

## What they do

Control Risks is an independent global risk consultancy that helps organisations manage political, integrity and security risks in complex and hostile environments. Its work includes security risk assessment, threat monitoring, open source intelligence and cyber risk services. The firm operates from offices across multiple continents and appears in the ADS members directory under management and consultancy services to the defence and security sector.

## Products and solutions

- **Security risk consulting** - assessment and management of political, security and operational risk.
- **Cyber risk services** - cyber risk assessment, threat monitoring and intelligence.
- **Integrity risk** - due diligence, investigations and compliance support.
