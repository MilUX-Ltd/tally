# Crane Worldwide UK

> Global third-party logistics provider with a dedicated Government and Defense division, handling military cargo, ITAR-controlled materials, and aerospace freight from UK bases.

## What they do

Crane Worldwide UK Ltd is the UK entity of Crane Worldwide Logistics, a privately-owned global third-party logistics provider headquartered in Houston, Texas. The company provides international freight forwarding by air, ocean, road and rail, alongside contract logistics, customs brokerage, project logistics, bonded warehousing, and time-critical Aircraft on Ground (AOG) services. Defence and government logistics is a named vertical, served by a 24/7 government control tower and specialist teams. UK operational bases cover Aberdeen, London Heathrow, Wellesbourne (Midlands), and Manchester Airport. The parent company is owned by Crane Capital Group.

## Products and solutions

- **Government and Defense logistics** - 24/7 control tower, military vehicle spares, weapon systems, arms and munitions, ITAR-controlled materials, oversized military cargo, austere location support, global husbanding and agency services.
- **Aerospace freight** - AOG and Next Flight Out (NFO), aircraft charters, hand-carry courier, oversized structures (wings, engines, flaps), temperature-sensitive avionics, spare parts logistics, ITAR and EAR compliance.
- **International freight forwarding** - air, ocean, road, rail, full and partial charters, project logistics, out-of-gauge cargo.
- **Customs and trade services** - customs brokerage, AEO status at multiple sites, C-TPAT, bonded warehousing.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001:2022 (achieved August 2025), UK Cyber Essentials (achieved August 2025), TAPA Class A (multiple sites), AEO (Authorised Economic Operator, multiple sites), C-TPAT, IATA
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; BIFA (British International Freight Association)
