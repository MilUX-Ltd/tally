# Diegesis Limited

> UK technology consultancy delivering IT integration, legacy system modernisation, GIS, AI and cyber security services to government and defence organisations.

## What they do

Diegesis is a London-based business and technology consultancy founded in 2007, focused on helping organisations manage, modernise and migrate legacy IT systems and databases. The company combines experience in legacy database technologies (Ingres, Informix, Db2) with capability in cloud platforms, AI, GIS and cyber security. Government and defence are stated core markets: the company's website describes it as "trusted for decades to deliver and support systems for government and defence organisations." Partners include IBM, Red Hat, Hexagon and SCC. The company holds Cyber Essentials and IASME accreditations.

## Products and solutions

- **Manage, Modernise, Migrate** - structured approach to extending the life of legacy systems, planning modernisation, and executing migration to cloud platforms including AWS, Azure and GCP.
- **GIS Services** - creation, management, analysis and mapping of geospatial data using Hexagon and other platforms.
- **AI Services** - applied AI and machine learning to extract insight from existing data assets.
- **Cyber Security** - risk management and cyber security advisory for government and public sector clients.
