# Dominion Dynamics
> Canadian defence technology startup building an Arctic autonomy and sensing stack for NATO and allied nations.

## What they do
Dominion Dynamics develops an integrated stack combining Arctic-optimised sensing, autonomous systems and networked platforms. The stated goal is to provide Canada with a domestic defence capability at scale, reducing dependence on large US primes such as Lockheed Martin and Raytheon. The company completed an Arctic deployment of its sensor technology at an early stage and is positioning for Canadian military procurement.

The advisory board includes former Chief of the Defence Staff General Wayne Eyre and former Vice Chief of the Defence Staff Lieutenant-General Mike Rouleau, providing direct access into Canadian defence procurement. Dominion raised CAD 21 million in seed funding led by Georgian Partners, with BCI and Bessemer Venture Partners participating.

## Products and solutions
- **Arctic autonomy stack** - integrated sensing, autonomy and networked platform capability for NATO's Arctic operating environment.
