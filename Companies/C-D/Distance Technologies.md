# Distance Technologies

> Finnish startup developing glasses-free mixed reality displays that turn transparent surfaces into augmented-reality screens.

## What they do

Distance Technologies is a Helsinki-based startup, founded in 2024, developing glasses-free mixed reality technology that transforms transparent surfaces into augmented-reality displays. The system uses AI and eye-tracking to project layered digital information into the user's natural line of sight without headsets or glasses. In defence it is applied to vehicle windshields and heads-up displays, showing real-time 3D tactical data, terrain mapping and AI-driven insights, including in low-visibility conditions such as darkness and smoke. The company initially targeted automotive use to prove low-cost mass production before extending into defence.

## Products and solutions

- **Glasses-free mixed reality display** - turns windshields and transparent surfaces into 3D AR displays driven by AI and eye-tracking, with defence applications in military vehicle heads-up displays.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
