# Cooper Moss Rutland

> Independent planning and project controls consultancy.

## What they do

Cooper Moss Rutland (CMR) is an independent project controls consultancy delivering planning, cost, risk, change, reporting, digital controls and project assurance support. Founded in 2017 and incorporated in the UK and Canada, it works across the infrastructure, building and defence sectors with both commercial and public sector clients. The company states it has worked on multiple projects for the Ministry of Defence, alongside major infrastructure programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Project controls consultancy** - planning, cost, risk, change, reporting and project assurance for major programmes.
- **Digital controls** - data and digital tooling support for project controls delivery.
