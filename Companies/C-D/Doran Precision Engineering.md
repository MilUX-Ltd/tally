# Doran Precision Engineering Limited

> Northern Ireland multi-axis CNC machining company producing precision aerospace, defence, and medical components to AS9100 standard from its Newcastle, County Down facility.

## What they do

Doran Precision Engineering manufactures precision-machined components for aerospace, defence, and medical customers. The company holds AS9100 Rev D and ISO 9001:2015 accreditation and supplies interior and structural parts to a worldwide customer base, including Thompson Aero Seating and Collins Aerospace. Approximately 90% of output serves the aerospace industry. Doran operates 23 3-axis CNC machining centres and a 5-axis UMC machining centre, capable of producing parts up to 2 m by 1 m.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC-machined aerospace components** - precision parts and assemblies for aircraft interiors and structural programmes, manufactured to AS9100 Rev D.
- **Medical device components** - precision-machined components to ISO 13485:2016 for medical device customers.
- **5-axis complex geometry parts** - high-tolerance components machined on a 5-axis UMC centre for aerospace and defence supply chains.
