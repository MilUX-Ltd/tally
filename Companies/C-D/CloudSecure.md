# CloudSecure

> UK provider of secure cloud and application engineering for defence and national security.

## What they do

CloudSecure provides cloud and DevOps engineering services to clients in demanding security environments, with a focus on secure cloud and application development for defence and national security. Its team is drawn from UK national security and defence backgrounds. The company embeds its engineers with clients and designs using secure architectural patterns. It is a signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Application development** - bespoke end-user web and mobile applications.
- **CloudSpace** - secure collaboration environments.
- **DevSecOps engineering** - cloud development tooling and pipelines.
- **Coder deployments** - managed developer environments.
