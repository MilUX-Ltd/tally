# Drumgrange Ltd

> Independent British engineering company specialising in maritime mission systems, C5ISR, and VLF communications for defence.

## What they do

Drumgrange is a family-owned British engineering company established in 1979, operating from sites in Chertsey, Surrey and Portland, Dorset. It designs, develops, integrates, and supports electronic and communication systems for naval and defence customers. The company delivers end-to-end engineering from concept and research through to obsolescence management, with products in service with UK Armed Forces, NATO forces, and foreign militaries.

## Products and solutions

- **Maritime Mission Systems** - Bespoke systems for naval applications including signature management, positioning and navigation, sonar systems and signal processing, and integration services.
- **C5ISR** - Scalable command, control, communications, computers, cyber, intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance solutions. Covers deployable tactical communications, capture solutions, and platform systems integration.
- **VLF Communications** - VLF demodulator and modulator equipment, RED REM processors, and associated broadcast chain infrastructure. Compliant with NATO STANAGs 4724 and 5065. Installed at communication centres, transmitter sites, and submerged platforms.
- **Diamond Intercom and Radio Combiner System** - A radio and intercom integration product.
- **Generic ComSim** - An RF simulator product for training and testing.
- **Acoustic Hailing Devices** - A product family for acoustic communications.
