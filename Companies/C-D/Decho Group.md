# Decho Group

> AI and data transformation consultancy specialising in Palantir software-enabled delivery for defence and security clients.

## What they do

Decho Group is a technology and AI consultancy founded in 2024 by Palantir alumni, specialising in the design and delivery of Palantir software-enabled transformations. The company focuses primarily on defence and security domains, supporting clients across the strategic, operational, and support space. Decho operates as a Strategic Alliance Partner of Palantir Technologies.

## Products and solutions

- **Strategic Programme Shaping** - architecture design and programme definition for defence and security data transformation programmes.
- **Delivery and Operations** - execution and maintenance of operational and business priority workflows built on Palantir software.
- **Enablement and Training** - accompanying capability development for leaders, developers, and operators.
