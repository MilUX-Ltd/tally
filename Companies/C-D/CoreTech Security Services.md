# CoreTech Security Services Limited

> Security-cleared cyber research and development company in Cheltenham, working in vulnerability research and high-assurance software engineering.

## What they do

CoreTech Security Services is a cyber security company based in Cheltenham, specialising in security-cleared research and development. Its multi-disciplinary teams combine vulnerability research with platform research and high-assurance software engineering to deliver capabilities supporting the protection of the UK. The company has around 70 technical consultants and was incorporated in February 2020.

## Products and solutions

- **Vulnerability research** - uncovering hidden weaknesses and demonstrating their real-world impact.
- **High-assurance software engineering** - end-to-end capabilities for security-cleared work.
- **Platform research** - novel platform research alongside vulnerability work.
