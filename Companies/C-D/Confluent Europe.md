# Confluent Europe Ltd

> UK arm of the data-streaming platform company, an ADS member serving the aerospace, defence and security supply chain.

## What they do

Confluent provides data infrastructure focused on data in motion, built around the Apache Kafka streaming platform. Its cloud-native, hybrid and on-premise offerings stream real-time data from multiple sources across an organisation. Confluent Europe Ltd is the UK registered entity of the wider Confluent group, whose parent is headquartered in the United States.

## Products and solutions

- **Confluent Platform** - on-premise and hybrid data-streaming platform built on Apache Kafka.
- **Confluent Cloud** - managed cloud-native data-streaming service.
