# Chess Dynamics

> A systems house designing and manufacturing surveillance, fire control and electro-optical defence systems for land, sea and air.

## What they do

Chess Dynamics designs and manufactures integrated defence systems and electro-optical solutions for land and maritime domains, with capability extending to air defence. Its portfolio covers surveillance, targeting and fire control systems, counter-UAS protection, and real-time video and image processing for electro-optic systems. The company is based in Horsham, with further sites in Plymouth and Wokingham, and employs over 200 people. It is part of Cohort plc, which acquired a majority stake in 2018.

## Products and solutions

- **SeaEagle** - electro-optical and fire control systems for maritime surveillance, target tracking and naval gunfire control.
- **Hawkeye** - modular multi-sensor systems for land-based reconnaissance, surveillance, targeting and ground-based air defence.
- **Counter-UAS (C-UAS)** - drone detection, tracking and defeat systems integrating optics, AI tracking and layered countermeasures.
- **Vision4ce** - AI-powered target detection, classification and tracking built on DEFT tracking and CHARM edge processing.
