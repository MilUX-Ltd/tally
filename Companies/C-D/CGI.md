# CGI Inc.

> Global IT and business consulting firm delivering systems integration and managed services to defence and government, with a large UK arm.

## What they do

CGI is an information technology and business process services firm founded in 1976 and headquartered in Montreal, Canada. It provides advisory services, software solutions, systems integration, secure managed services and training. In the UK it works with the Ministry of Defence and wider government on complex, secure mission-critical information systems spanning back-office, logistics and frontline operations, as well as space domain awareness.

## Products and solutions

- **BOREALIS** - command, control and data processing software for UK Space Domain Awareness, delivered into the National Space Operations Centre to help monitor and protect satellites.
- **Aurora** - existing UK space surveillance capability on which BOREALIS builds.
- **Systems integration and secure managed services** - advisory, software, hosting and training across defence and government programmes.
