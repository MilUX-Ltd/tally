# Digital Manufacturing Centre

> Advanced additive manufacturing facility serving aerospace, space and defence.

## What they do

The Digital Manufacturing Centre (DMC) is an advanced additive manufacturing facility offering metal and polymer production alongside engineering expertise. It serves the aerospace, space and defence sectors, among others, and has worked on UK Ministry of Defence projects. The centre is based at Silverstone Park and was established by KW Special Projects.

## Products and solutions

- **Metal additive manufacturing** - production and prototyping of metal components.
- **Polymer additive manufacturing** - polymer part production.
- **Engineering and design** - component design, consultation and training.
