# Donite Plastics Ltd

> Northern Ireland thermoforming manufacturer producing large-format vacuum-formed plastic parts and assemblies for aerospace, defence, and space customers.

## What they do

Donite Plastics specialises in batch manufacture of heavy-duty plastic mouldings using vacuum forming and CNC trimming processes. It holds AS9100 certification for the design, manufacture, and assembly of thermoformed products for the aviation, space, and defence industries, and is the first vacuum forming company in the UK and Ireland to hold both AS9100 and ISO 14001. The company operates one of the largest vacuum formers in Ireland and the UK, capable of producing parts up to 2.5 x 2 metres, and has expanded capacity significantly through recent machinery investment.

## Products and solutions

- **Vacuum-formed aerospace interiors components** - large and small-dimension moulded parts for aircraft interior programmes, produced to AS9100 standards.
- **5-axis and 3-axis CNC trimming and machining** - precision finishing of thermoformed parts, including toolmaking, with machines capable of working up to 4.8 x 1.8 x 1.2 metres.
- **Mould, trim tooling, fabrication, and assembly** - end-to-end manufacturing from design and prototyping through to finished assemblies.
