# CloudRF

> UK provider of online radio propagation planning software for military, emergency services and communications professionals.

## What they do

CloudRF offers a software-as-a-service platform and propagation API for modelling radio wave propagation. The tools support tactical radio deployment and are used by governments, industry, academia and NGOs. The software runs in a web browser and supports 1m LiDAR, soft clutter and 3D mesh terrain models. The company field tests and drive tests its software to calibrate accuracy. It is based in Gloucester, with regional servers in Canada, the UK and Germany.

## Products and solutions

- **CloudRF SaaS platform** - browser-based radio planning and coverage modelling.
- **Radio propagation API** - a 3D propagation interface for integration into other systems, applied to defence, IoT, 5G, autonomous vehicles, drones and blue-light services.
