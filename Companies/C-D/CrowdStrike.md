# CrowdStrike Holdings, Inc.

> Cloud-native cyber security vendor built around the Falcon platform, with UK operations serving government and enterprise.

## What they do

CrowdStrike is a cyber security company founded in 2011 and headquartered in Austin, Texas. Its AI-native Falcon platform unifies endpoint, cloud, identity and data security through a single lightweight sensor and cloud back end. It serves enterprise and public sector customers internationally, including UK government agencies and FTSE 100 organisations, with a network of UK-based managed service partners.

## Products and solutions

- **Falcon platform** - AI-native security platform unifying endpoint, cloud, identity and AI security in one agent and console.
- **Falcon Data Security** - data security product launched at RSA 2026 to discover, classify and stop unauthorised movement of sensitive data in real time.
