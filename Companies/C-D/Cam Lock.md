# Cam Lock

> Manufacturer of CBRN respiratory protection and life-support breathing apparatus for aircrew, military and emergency use.

## What they do

Cam Lock designs and manufactures personal protection life-support systems: breathing apparatus and respiratory protection for aircrew, military, industrial and emergency use. Its defence focus is CBRN (chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear) respiratory protection for aircrew, with core strength in elastomeric mouldings and life-support products. The company is based in Aldershot, Hampshire, is part of the Camberley Group, and operates additional facilities in the USA, Canada, Asia Pacific and the Middle East.

## Products and solutions

- **CBRN Tactical Mask (aircrew respirator)** - CBRN aircrew mask qualified across platforms including Typhoon, Gripen, Rafale, Hawk, F-15, F-16, F-18, F-22 and F-35.
- **JSAM-TA (Joint Service Aircrew Mask - Tactical Aircraft)** - supplied to the US Navy and Marine Corps, USAF, Australian Defence Force and Italian Air Force.
- **Aircrew oxygen masks**
- **FAST-mask and FAST-resQ** - escape respirator and emergency breathing apparatus.
- **Industrial respirators and firefighter breathing apparatus** - including SCBA and emergency escape sets.
