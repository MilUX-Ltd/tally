# Cambridge Consultants

> A deep-tech product development and engineering consultancy, part of Capgemini, with a defence and security practice.

## What they do

Cambridge Consultants is a technology and product development consultancy that works across multiple industries including defence and security. Its defence work spans autonomous land, air and underwater vehicles, sensing and wireless technology, signal processing, machine learning, cyber defence and quantum technologies. The company employs more than 900 staff across offices in Cambridge, Boston, Tokyo and Singapore. Since 2021 it has been part of Capgemini Invent.

## Products and solutions

- **Autonomy and uncrewed systems** - development of autonomous land vehicles, UAVs and uncrewed underwater vehicles, including collaborative UAV fleets.
- **Sensing and wireless** - radar, optical sensing for surveillance and CBRNE applications, positioning systems and electronic countermeasures.
- **Cyber and quantum** - autonomous cyber defence engines, quantum key distribution and post-quantum encryption research.
