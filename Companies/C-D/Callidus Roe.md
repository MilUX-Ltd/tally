# Callidus Roe

> UK defence engineering SME specialising in military communications system design, installation, certification, and through-life support for MOD and prime contractors.

## What they do

Callidus Roe provides engineering, project management, and technical support to the UK MOD and defence prime contractors, with a core focus on military communications systems across land and maritime platforms. Services span the full programme lifecycle: design and certification of communications systems to relevant JSPs; installation surveys and data performance analysis of legacy cabling; end-of-life decommissioning and salvage; cable harness production; and project management of testing in UK facilities and remote locations worldwide. The company operates an associate network of SQEP providing rapid-access specialist technicians, qualified armoured vehicle crews, and cable designers proficient in Harmware and RapidHarness tooling.

## Products and solutions

- **Installation, Design and Certification** - Design, certification, and installation of communications systems into land and maritime platforms to JSP standards.
- **Installation Surveys** - Survey and performance analysis of in-service land and maritime communications equipment, with harness adapter production to Mil 38999 standard.
- **End of Life Decommissioning** - Decommissioning, disposal, salvage, and reclamation of obsolete vehicles and communications infrastructure.
- **Technical Support** - Cable harness production, outsized equipment delivery, GFA management, and procurement support.
- **Equipment Testing and Trials** - Project management of testing in static and remote locations worldwide.
- **SQEP Associate Network** - Rapid-access specialist personnel for armoured vehicle movements, land comms operations, and cable design.
