# Consult Red

> Engineering partner for connected and secure electronic products and systems.

## What they do

Consult Red is an employee-owned technology development partner working on intelligent, connected and secure systems. It supports clients across the lifecycle of complex electronic products, from concept and prototyping through to production. Services span technical strategy, solution architecture, product design and development, integration, compliance and testing.

## Products and solutions

- **Embedded and connected device development** - design and development of intelligent, connected electronic products and systems.
- **Secure systems engineering** - architecture, integration and compliance work for connected and secure devices.
