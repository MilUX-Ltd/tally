# Captec Group

> Designer and manufacturer of specialised, rugged and embedded computing systems for defence and other critical applications.

## What they do

Captec designs and engineers specialised computers, integrated systems and subsystems for challenging, critical and certified applications. Its platforms combine high-performance computing, electro-mechanical design and system integration for deployment in demanding operating environments. It works with OEMs and systems integrators to deliver application-specific solutions with long-term availability, configuration control and full lifecycle support.

## Products and solutions

- **Specialised computers** - application-specific industrial, compact, embedded and rackmount computers, plus custom tablets and design-to-order builds.
- **Rugged servers** - the SRS (Specialised Rugged Server) range in 1U/2U and short-depth formats, engineered against shock, vibration, EMI, temperature and unstable power; CRS-215 Rugged GPU Server.
- **Secure Encrypted Server (SES) range** - SES-120 and SES-110 with military-grade AES-256 hardware encryption and tamper-evident chassis, developed with Viasat.
- **Integrated systems** - unified multi-configuration cabinet and enclosure platforms.
- **Subsystems engineering** - metalwork, professional finishing to BR 3939 defence standards, electro-mechanical integration and build-to-print.
- **Unified Edge** - distributed edge processing and the E-Series embedded/edge/IoT range.
- **Quadbox** - hardware platform for simulation and training applications.
