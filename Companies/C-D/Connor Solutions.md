# Connor Solutions Ltd

> Electronic manufacturing services provider serving defence and aerospace, space, medical and industrial markets.

## What they do

Connor Solutions is an electronic manufacturing services (EMS) provider taking customer designs through to manufactured product. It offers design for manufacture and new product introduction, supply chain management, PCBA and final assembly manufacturing, test and inspection, and aftermarket support. Defence and aerospace is one of its named market sectors, alongside medical and life sciences, energy and industrial, space, and interconnectivity. The company operates a 112,000 square foot facility and can produce complex multilayer RF PCBA and high-level electromechanical assemblies.

## Products and solutions

- **DFM and NPI** - design for manufacture, design for test, prototyping and new product introduction including ramp to scale.
- **Manufacturing** - turnkey PCBA and final assembly, including complex multilayer RF PCBA and electromechanical assemblies.
- **Supply chain management** - management of a global supply chain network.
- **Test and inspection** - test solutions aligned to product technology and volume.
- **Aftermarket** - support from development to end of life.
