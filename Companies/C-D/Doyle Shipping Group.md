# Doyle Shipping Group (Belfast)

> Port services, stevedoring, customs clearance and logistics operator serving defence and commercial customers from Belfast.

## What they do

Doyle Shipping Group (DSG) is a family-owned shipping and logistics company founded in 1886, operating across major ports in Ireland and the UK. The Belfast operation was acquired by DSG in 1987, originally trading as Burke Shipping. DSG Belfast provides ship agency, stevedoring and heavy lifting, customs clearance, warehousing, and project freight forwarding services. The company provides husbandry services to military vessels, submarines, and fleet auxiliary vessels calling at ports across the island of Ireland.

## Products and solutions

- **Port agency** - ship agency services covering all vessel types including naval and fleet auxiliary vessels.
- **Stevedoring and heavy lifting** - cargo handling and heavy-lift operations at Belfast and other ports.
- **Customs clearance** - import and export customs processing, including Brexit-related customs work.
- **Project freight forwarding** - logistics management for large and complex cargo movements.
- **Warehousing and distribution** - storage and distribution services linked to port operations.
