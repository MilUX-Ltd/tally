# Content Vault

> Developer of a patent-pending, device-specific encryption platform for secure media sharing, storage and distribution.

## What they do

Content Vault develops a secure content distribution platform that protects high-value media from disclosure, theft and unauthorised access. Its core approach is device-specific encryption: files are bound to an intended device and remain inaccessible without the designated device and credentials, even if shared beyond authorised recipients. The company is a member of Defence BattleLab and achieved Cyber Essentials Plus certification in March 2026.

## Products and solutions

- **Etstream** - encrypted streaming platform for secure media sharing and distribution.
- **Device-specific encryption** - end-to-end encryption extended to all file types, including compressed archive formats, binding protected files to authorised devices.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus (March 2026).
