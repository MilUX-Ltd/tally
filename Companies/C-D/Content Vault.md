# Content Vault
> Developer of maximum-security encryption software for secure media sharing and streaming.
## What they do
Content Vault develops the Etstream encrypted streaming platform, providing maximum-security encryption software to ensure media files can be shared and stored securely and viewed only by authorised recipients. The company is a member of Defence BattleLab and achieved Cyber Essentials Plus certification in March 2026.
## Products and solutions
- **Etstream** - Encrypted streaming platform for secure media sharing and distribution.
