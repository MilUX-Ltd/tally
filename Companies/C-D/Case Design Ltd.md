# Case Design Ltd

> Manufacturer of custom protective cases and transit solutions, including for defence.

## What they do

Case Design manufactures custom protective cases and transport solutions, including aluminium flight cases, road trunks and waterproof cases with precision-cut foam inserts. Services span custom foam engineering, CNC machining, aluminium fabrication and case refurbishment. Established in 1988, it serves defence alongside broadcast, film, medical and industrial customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Flight cases** - aluminium, plywood and astroboard cases.
- **Waterproof cases** - protective cases with custom foam inserts.
- **Foam engineering** - CNC-cut foam inserts and fixtures.
- **Case refurbishment** - repair and refurbishment services.
