# CellMetric

> UK designer and manufacturer of digital radio test systems, video surveillance test systems and asset-tracking solutions for government and security users.

## What they do

CellMetric designs and manufactures digital radio test solutions, video surveillance test systems and asset-tracking solutions, and acts as a systems integrator of bespoke RF test systems. The ADS directory describes the company as designing and manufacturing video surveillance test systems for terrestrial and airborne systems. The business is based in Cambridge.

## Products and solutions

- **Digital radio and RF test systems** - infotainment and broadcast RF test (FM, AM, DAB, DAB+, digital TV) and remote RF monitoring.
- **Video surveillance test systems** - test systems for terrestrial and airborne surveillance.
- **Asset tracking** - covert asset-tracking solutions.
