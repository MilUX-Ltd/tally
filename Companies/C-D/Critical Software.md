# Critical Software Ltd

> Safety- and mission-critical software and systems engineering company serving aerospace, defence, and security customers; Portuguese-headquartered with a UK entity in Southampton.

## What they do

Critical Software provides systems and software engineering services for safety, mission, and business-critical applications. The company's aerospace and defence work covers avionics software development and certification (DO-178C, DO-254, ARP-4754A), command and control systems, and homeland security software. Founded in 1998 in Portugal, the company operates from offices in Southampton (UK), Coimbra, Porto, and Lisbon (Portugal), Munich (Germany), and Boston (USA). The UK entity (Companies House 05256049) is a subsidiary of the Portuguese parent.

## Products and solutions

- **Avionics software development and certification** - DO-178C, DO-254, and ARP-4754A compliant development and verification for civil and military aviation programmes.
- **Command and control systems** - Software for mission-critical C2 environments, including defence and homeland security applications.
- **Data management services** - Solutions for managing critical operational assets and processes.
- **Critical FlyTech (joint venture)** - A 51/49 joint venture with Airbus, launched November 2025, focused on high-assurance embedded software and certifiable avionics, cabin, and connectivity systems.
