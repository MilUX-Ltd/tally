# Cosine

> Sovereign AI coding agents and frontier models for defence, national security, and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Cosine builds autonomous software engineering agents and sovereign frontier AI models designed for deployment within air-gapped, on-premise infrastructure with no external cloud dependencies. Their platform supports 38+ programming languages including legacy stacks (Fortran, COBOL, Ada, Verilog) common in UK defence and CNI systems. The company is building Lumen Sovereign, Britain's first sovereign frontier AI model, trained from scratch on proprietary datasets across regulated workflows. Co-design partners include BAE Systems, Babcock International, Leonardo UK, and Thales UK.

## Products and solutions

- **Cosine Coding Agent** - Autonomous software engineering agent that runs fully air-gapped within a customer's own infrastructure, supporting 38+ programming languages including legacy defence codebases.
- **Lumen Sovereign** - Sovereign frontier AI model being trained on Isambard-AI under the UK Government's Sovereign AI Fund; design partners include BAE Systems, Babcock International, BT, Leonardo UK, and Thales UK.
