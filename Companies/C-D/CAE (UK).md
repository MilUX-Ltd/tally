# CAE (UK) plc

> UK arm of CAE Inc, providing synthetic training equipment and aircrew training to the UK armed forces.

## What they do

CAE (UK) plc is a training systems integrator and synthetic training equipment provider, ranked as a key supplier to the UK Ministry of Defence. It provides synthetic training equipment and training services and holds Design Authority status for several British Army training systems. The RAF and Royal Navy use CAE UK for aircrew training across a range of platforms, from basic flying skills to advanced mission training and rehearsal.

CAE (UK) plc is the UK subsidiary of CAE Inc, headquartered in Montreal, Canada. The UK entity dates to 1967 and is based at Burgess Hill, West Sussex, with the Medium Support Helicopter Aircrew Training Facility at RAF Benson.

## Products and solutions

- **Medium Support Helicopter Aircrew Training Facility (MSHATF)** - six full-mission simulators at RAF Benson (Chinook, Merlin and Puma) covering conversion through to pre-deployment mission rehearsal.
- **Land training systems** - simulators for artillery fire control, direct fire gunnery, air defence engagement and target recognition; Design Authority for the Warrior Gunnery Turret Trainer, Warrior Desk Top Trainer and Artillery Fire Control Trainer.
- **Single Synthetic Environment (SSE)** - mission-rehearsal capability work for UK Strategic Command.
- **CAE London Burgess Hill** - simulator-based pilot training centre mixing civil and military rotary training.
