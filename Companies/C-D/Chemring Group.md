# Chemring Group Plc

> Listed defence technology group supplying countermeasures, energetic systems and sensors to the aerospace, defence and security markets.

## What they do

Chemring Group is a UK-headquartered defence technology business listed on the London Stock Exchange (LSE: CHG). It operates through two segments: Countermeasures and Energetics, and Sensors and Information. The group develops and manufactures expendable countermeasures, energetic materials, propellants, pyrotechnic devices, sensors and electronic systems for air, sea and land platforms.

## Products and solutions

- **Countermeasures** - expendable countermeasures including flares, chaff and decoys for air and naval platforms.
- **Energetics** - propellants, energetic materials, cartridge and propellant actuated devices, missile components and separation sub-systems.
- **Sensors and Information** - sensors and electronic systems for defence and security applications.

## Group relationship

Chemring Group Plc is the listed parent of the Chemring group of companies. UK subsidiaries include Chemring Countermeasures Limited and Chemring Energetics UK Limited.
