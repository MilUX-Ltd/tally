# DMS technologies

> Hampshire-based manufacturer of military-grade battery packs, engine-start systems and bespoke portable power solutions for defence, aviation and security customers worldwide.

## What they do

DMS technologies designs, manufactures and supplies portable and static power solutions from its 18,000 sq ft facility in Romsey, Hampshire. Founded in 1986 and ADS Group member, the company serves military, aviation, marine, rail and industrial markets. Its defence work covers engine-start systems for armoured fighting vehicles, helicopters and aircraft, as well as bespoke battery packs for body-worn equipment, generators and crew-served weapons systems. The company holds ISO 9001 accreditation.

## Products and solutions

- **Red Flash Portable Power Pack** - rugged AGM-based ground power unit designed to start military vehicles including helicopters, battle tanks, armoured fighting vehicles and light aircraft in extreme conditions.
- **Mini-Gun Slave Battery Mk2** - purpose-designed battery system to power the 6-barrel Dillon Aero M134D Mini-Gun, in service with the Royal Navy.
- **Custom battery packs** - bespoke lithium and AGM battery assemblies for body-worn equipment, field generators, aircraft starting and portable field power applications.
- **Charging systems** - military-specification standby charging and management systems to support in-service battery maintenance.
