# Consolite Technology Limited

> UK manufacturer of specialised military and naval lighting systems, including night-vision-compatible LED lighting and secure optical (LiFi) communications.

## What they do

Consolite Technology is a long-established UK company specialising in military lighting systems. Its historical focus on night-vision-goggle (NVG) lighting has expanded into broader requirements, with an emphasis on LED warship lighting. Its capabilities cover complete warship lighting systems with full EMC compliance and ATEX requirements, LED navigation light systems that are NVG-capable and COLREG-compliant, and LiFi solutions for secure communication on military platforms.

## Products and solutions

- **Naval and warship LED lighting** - complete internal and external packages, single or dual-mode (bright white or NVG-compatible), including explosion-proof variants.
- **LED navigation light systems** - NVG-compliant while remaining COLREG-compliant, with touchscreen control (Lloyd's Register Type Approval, 2022).
- **Smart Lighting System** - integrated zone-by-zone lighting control from a central point, commercially available since March 2025.
- **NVG lighting products and services** - NVG filters, cockpit and warship upgrades, NVG-compliant lamps and LEDs.
- **Military LiFi** - secure optical and wireless communication systems for military and naval platforms.
- **Lighting testing and consultancy** - environmental, EMC and explosion-protection testing.
