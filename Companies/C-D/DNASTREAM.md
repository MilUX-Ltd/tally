# DNASTREAM Limited

> Guildford-based IFS specialist consultancy delivering enterprise resource planning, asset management and digital transformation services to defence and defence-sector organisations.

## What they do

DNASTREAM Limited is an independently owned IT consultancy founded in 2006, specialising in the implementation, upgrade and support of IFS enterprise software. The company holds Specialized Partner status within the IFS partner programme, with a stated focus on defence, energy and utilities, resources, service industries and enterprise asset management. Its services cover programme and project management, technical and solution architecture, system implementation, managed support and change adoption. DNASTREAM operates from Surrey Technology Centre in Guildford and has exhibited at the Defence Procurement, Research, Technology and Exportability (DPRTE) event alongside IFS.

## Products and solutions

- **ASCEND AMS** - DNASTREAM's own suite of managed services for IFS application support, including pro support, lifecycle assurance, insights and analytics, and UK sovereign hosting.
- **IFS Applications implementation** - full delivery of IFS ERP, enterprise asset management, field service management and defence-relevant modules including aviation maintenance.
- **Transformation services** - programme management, stakeholder engagement, training and adoption work to support enterprise technology deployments.
