# Cyber Ranges

> Cyprus-headquartered military-grade cyber range platform provider; UK entity (Cyber Ranges UK and Ireland Ltd) based in Sheffield; serves militaries, governments and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Cyber Ranges designs, delivers and manages simulation-based cyber security exercises for training and capability development. The platform is used by military and law enforcement agencies, governments and critical infrastructure operators to build and validate cyber defence capabilities. Cyber Ranges is the official cyber range platform of the UN's International Telecommunication Union (ITU) for national, regional and global cyber drills. MITRE ATT&CK designated the platform its cyber range of choice in 2022. The UK entity, Cyber Ranges UK and Ireland Ltd (Companies House 05831135), is registered in Sheffield. The company's global HQ is in Limassol, Cyprus, with additional offices in the United States and Kenya.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber Range Platform** - Cloud-hosted and on-premise simulation environment for cyber exercises and capability development.
- **Military and Law Enforcement Package** - Tailored cyber training for armed forces and law enforcement agencies.
- **Workforce Development Programmes** - Transition training for personnel moving from active service to civilian cyber roles.
- **National Cyber Drills** - Design and delivery of national-level cyber exercises in partnership with the ITU.
