# Catalyst Corporation Ltd

> Defence innovation consultancy building synthetic environments and prototypes for autonomous and uncrewed systems.

## What they do

Catalyst Corporation creates real-time 3D simulations and synthetic environments to explore future technology at reduced risk and cost. It supports ideation, rapid prototyping and user testing, ranging from paper and code prototyping through to working technology demonstrators in complex system simulations. The company works on land and air systems, including drones, robotic and autonomous systems and uncrewed platforms. It is based at the Sussex Innovation Centre, University of Sussex.

## Products and solutions

- **SE2 synthetic environment** - real-time 3D environment containing uncrewed air and ground vehicle models that can be controlled directly (human in the loop) or autonomously using AI and machine learning approaches.
- **Prototyping and simulation services** - low-fidelity through to advanced prototyping for defence systems development.

Catalyst has worked with Dstl, Thales and Digital Concepts Engineering on the Land Digital Robotics and Autonomous Systems Integration Capability (L-DRIC) project, and has experimented with open electronic architecture standards such as DEF STAN GVA.
