# Dynamic Aerospace Fabrications

> Precision aerospace and defence metal fabrication and component manufacturer based in Castle Donington, operating under the US-headquartered Dynamic Aerospace and Defense group.

## What they do

Dynamic Aerospace Fabrications Ltd supplies precision machined and fabricated metal components to the aerospace, defence, automotive, and nuclear sectors. The company operates from Castle Donington, Derbyshire, and employs around 100 people. It was formed in 2019 following the acquisition of the aerospace division of Paul Fabrications Ltd, which had been manufacturing defence and aerospace components since 1937, including nose cones for Spitfire aircraft and exhaust manifolds for Rolls-Royce Merlin engines during the Second World War. The parent group, Dynamic Aerospace and Defense, is headquartered in Michigan, USA.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision Metal Fabrication** - 6-axis laser cutting, 3-, 4-, and 5-axis CNC milling, CNC turning, hot and cold forming, resistance and fusion welding, and heat treatment for aerospace and defence applications.
- **Aerospace Engine Components** - Internal LPT honeycombed seal assemblies, finger seals, shrouds, and cases for commercial and military aircraft engines.
- **Defence Structural Components** - Sheet metal and fabricated assemblies for military and civil aircraft programmes.
