# Calian UK

> UK arm of Canada's Calian Group, delivering military training, role-play exercises and simulation to the British Army and UK MoD.

## What they do

Calian UK Ltd, formerly Mabway Limited, is a prime supplier to the British Army and UK MoD for large-scale military training, role-play exercise support and simulation. The UK business specialises in defence role-play environments (actors, audiences, adversaries and specialists) that simulate operational conditions for live military exercises, alongside technical and engineering education for naval and maritime forces.

Calian UK is the UK operating company of Calian Group Ltd, a TSX-listed group headquartered in Ottawa, Canada. The UK entity dates to 2004 and is registered in Hampshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Live training and role-play exercise support** - managing realistic role-playing environments for military field exercises; prime supplier to the British Army.
- **MaestroEDE** - exercise design, development and delivery software platform for large-scale military exercises.
- **Immersive Training Technologies** - AR, VR, MR and XR training environments.
- **Audio distribution and connectivity** - systems-agnostic connectivity of field radios for exercise command and control.
- **Technical and engineering education** - for naval and maritime communities.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** EAL-recognised training provider (engineering and manufacturing qualifications).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Armed Forces Covenant signatory.
