# Cyber Guarded Ltd

> Vendor-independent cyber security testing and assurance firm; NCSC-accredited for CHECK penetration testing and Cyber Advisor services.

## What they do

Cyber Guarded provides infrastructure, cloud, IoT and application security testing and auditing services to organisations in the public and private sectors. The company is NCSC-accredited as a CHECK penetration testing provider and as a Cyber Advisor. It also operates as a Cyber Essentials Plus Certification Body, assessing and certifying organisations against the government's Cyber Essentials scheme. Clients include government departments, local authorities, health trusts, fire and rescue services, international airports and financial institutions. The company operates offices in Surrey, Belfast and Dublin.

## Products and solutions

- **CHECK Penetration Testing** - NCSC-accredited infrastructure and application penetration testing.
- **Red Team Testing** - Ethical hacking exercises simulating real-world attack techniques.
- **Cyber Audits and Reviews** - Risk and compliance-based audit of an organisation's cyber security posture.
- **Cyber Risk Management** - Strategy, policy and procedure development.
- **Cyber Essentials / CE+ Certification** - Assessment and certification body services.
