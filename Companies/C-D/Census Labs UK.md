# Census Labs UK

> UK arm of Greek cybersecurity firm CENSUS, providing IT and OT security services, product security and vulnerability research, including for defence customers.

## What they do

CENSUS is an independent, privately funded cybersecurity services provider operating since 2008. It delivers IT and OT security services to public and private organisations worldwide, including defence primes, system integrators and defence agencies, with a focus on applied security research and public vulnerability disclosure. Census Labs UK Limited is the UK operating entity, based in Chiswick, London; the parent CENSUS S.A. is headquartered in Greece.

## Products and solutions

- **Security assessments and penetration testing** - IT and OT security assessments, penetration testing and red teaming.
- **Product security** - code auditing, Security by Design, secure development lifecycle and threat modelling.
- **Vulnerability research** - firmware and embedded-system research, supported by dedicated hardware and radio laboratory facilities.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015, ISO/IEC 27001:2013, ISO 22301:2019, Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus, CREST accredited.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
