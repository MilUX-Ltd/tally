# Cross Manufacturing

> Bath-based precision manufacturer of metallic sealing components for aerospace, defence and high-performance industrial applications.

## What they do

Cross Manufacturing designs and manufactures precision-engineered metallic sealing solutions for aircraft engines, gas and steam turbines, automotive turbochargers and other high-temperature, high-stress environments. The company has been developing heat-resistant wrought alloy products since the 1930s. In 2026, Cross Manufacturing established the UK's first pilot-scale end-to-end manufacturing process for Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMCs), working with Dstl, QinetiQ, MBDA, the University of Oxford, the National Composites Centre and the UK Atomic Energy Authority.

## Products and solutions

- **Brush seals** - high-temperature sealing rings for aircraft engines and gas turbines.
- **Retaining rings** - precision retaining components ranging from 6 mm to over 2,000 mm diameter.
- **Step gap sealing rings and friction rings** - for advanced propulsion and transmission systems.
- **Ceramic Matrix Composite components** - pilot-scale production of nose cones, curved panels and high-temperature seals capable of withstanding temperatures exceeding 1,000°C; targeted at hypersonics, space and advanced propulsion.
