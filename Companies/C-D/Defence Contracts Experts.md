# Defence Contracts Experts

> Procurement, contract management and supplier relationship consultancy and training for the UK defence sector.

## What they do

Defence Contracts Experts Ltd provides commercial contract, procurement and supplier relationship management services to both public sector and private sector defence organisations. The company helps small businesses understand MoD public procurement processes and locate opportunities, and assists the public sector in managing supplier relationships. It has been operating since 2015, drawing on over 30 years of experience within the UK MoD and major defence prime contractors.

## Products and solutions

- **Procurement consultancy** - advisory support on commercial contracts, procurement strategy and supplier relationship management for MoD and defence supply chain clients.
- **Training** - courses covering procurement processes, contract management, and public sector procurement for SMEs seeking to enter or grow within the defence market.
