# C P Cases Limited

> Designer and manufacturer of protective transit cases and 19" racks for military and defence use.

## What they do

CP Cases designs and manufactures protective cases, 19" racks and bags for the transport, operation and storage of equipment, predominantly for military, industrial and defence applications. In-house capabilities span rotomoulded plastic, aluminium, laminated plywood, composite, textiles and CNC foam machining. Many products are accredited to MIL-STD-810 and Def-Stan 00-35, rated to IP65 and carry NATO stock numbers.

## Products and solutions

- **Amazon Cases and Racks** - rotomoulded polyethylene cases and racks originally designed for military markets.
- **ERack** - rugged 19" rack accredited to IP65 and MIL-STD-810F, available with EMC and TEMPEST shielding for secure communications.
- **Climate control units** - case-cooling for electronics operating in extreme temperatures.
- **Custom enclosures** - bespoke rackmount enclosures, including units supplied for the Queen Elizabeth Class aircraft carriers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** MIL-STD-810F, Def-Stan 00-35, STANAG 4340, IP65 (IEC 60529); products carry NATO stock numbers.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
