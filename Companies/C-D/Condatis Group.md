# Condatis Group

> Digital identity and access management specialist working across government, defence and the wider public and private sectors.

## What they do

Condatis designs and implements identity and access management solutions, with a focus on Microsoft Entra and a Zero Trust approach to authentication and credential verification. It works in decentralised identity and verifiable credentials, and is a member of the Decentralized Identity Foundation and the Open Identity Exchange. Condatis has delivered identity projects across UK government and the public sector, including customer identity work with Defra and a verifiable-credentials staff passport proof of value with Sellafield and Digital Catapult.

The business demerged from Sitekit Group in 2022 and was acquired by Bridgepoint in October 2024.

## Products and solutions

- **Identity and access management** - bespoke IAM delivery built on Microsoft Entra, including Zero Trust authentication.
- **Microsoft Entra Verified ID** - verifiable credentials for workforce onboarding and help-desk identity verification.
- **Decentralised identity solutions** - verifiable-credential systems such as the Sellafield Nuclear Staff Passport proof of value.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group; Decentralized Identity Foundation; Open Identity Exchange. Microsoft partner.
