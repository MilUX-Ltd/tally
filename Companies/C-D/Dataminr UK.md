# Dataminr UK

> UK entity of the New York-headquartered AI platform for real-time event detection, threat intelligence, and risk management, with confirmed UK MOD contracts and government clients globally.

## What they do

Dataminr develops AI-powered real-time event and risk intelligence software. Its platform synthesises text, image, video, audio, and sensor signals across more than one million public data sources to deliver early-warning alerts on breaking events, emerging threats, and physical and cyber risks. The UK entity (company number 09232100, incorporated 2014) is the commercial and operational presence serving UK government and commercial clients.

## Products and solutions

- **Dataminr for Public Sector** - real-time intelligence and alerting for government agencies, supporting situational awareness and crisis response.
- **Dataminr for Cyber Defense** - AI-powered cyber threat intelligence fusing external threat signals with internal telemetry, including client-tailored threat intelligence (CTTI) and autonomous threat and exposure management.
- **Physical Security Intelligence** - early detection of physical threats, critical events, and emerging risks to people, assets, and operations.
