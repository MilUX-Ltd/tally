# Digital Concepts Engineering

> UK SME designing and manufacturing robotic control systems and uncrewed ground vehicles for defence, nuclear and hazardous-environment applications.

## What they do

Digital Concepts Engineering (DCE) specialises in the design, development and production of robotic control systems and unmanned vehicles. Its products are used across defence, nuclear, health and agriculture sectors where keeping people out of hazardous environments is the primary objective. The company is based in the Midlands and has supplied government defence agencies, laboratories, prime contractors and industry partners; it participated in the British Army's 2018 Army Warfighting Experiment.

## Products and solutions

- **Marionette Universal Control System** - modular, vehicle-agnostic applique robotics toolkit providing bolt-on low-latency external control. Deployed on platforms from 250 kg to 30,000 kg including the FV510 Warrior armoured fighting vehicle, HMMWV, RMMV HX60 tactical truck, Wheelbarrow Mk7 EOD robot and the Ghost Vision-60 Q-UGV. Interfaces with cameras, weapon systems, CBRNe sensors and manipulator arms.
- **X Series** - range of high-capability, affordable, tracked all-terrain uncrewed ground vehicles covering amphibious and multi-domain roles.
- **UGV Operator Course** - training programme combining theory, legal considerations and hands-on practical demonstration for UGV operators.
