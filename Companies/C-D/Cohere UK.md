# Cohere UK

> UK subsidiary of the Canadian enterprise AI company Cohere, building secure foundation models and AI products for business and government.

## What they do

Cohere is a security-first enterprise AI company that builds foundation models and end-to-end AI products for business and government. Its platform offers private deployment so organisations can keep data behind their own firewalls, deployable across major clouds, private cloud, on-premises, or air-gapped environments. The London office supports the company's R&D growth and its sovereign-AI work for government.

## Products and solutions

- **Command** - family of large language models for text generation.
- **Embed and Rerank** - search and retrieval models.
- **North** - agentic AI platform launched August 2025 for private, on-premises, hybrid-cloud, VPC, or air-gapped deployment.
