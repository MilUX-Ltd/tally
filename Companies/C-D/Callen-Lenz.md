# Callen-Lenz

> UK developer that designs, manufactures and operates uncrewed and autonomous air systems for military customers.

## What they do

Callen-Lenz offers products, platforms and services across the uncrewed and autonomous air vehicle market. Design, development and manufacture of UAV platforms and associated avionic systems are performed in-house, alongside aviation services and technical consultancy for crewed and uncrewed aviation. All systems are manufactured in the UK, mostly from UK supply chains. The company was acquired by BAE Systems in 2024 into its FalconWorks research and development arm.

## Products and solutions

- **Koios** - compact medium VTOL platform for strategic ISR with a tactical footprint; range up to around 750 km and endurance up to around 10 hours.
- **Nyan** - one-way effector (loitering munition); subject of a UK MoD contract and tested by the British Army.
- **Fregata** - medium, high-endurance conventional take-off and landing ISR platform.
- **Prometheus** - reconfigurable UAS for mapping and autonomous navigation of GNSS-denied subterranean environments.
- **SkyCircuits** - wholly owned subsidiary producing high-integrity autopilots and mission management systems for crewed and uncrewed aviation.
