# Domo Tactical Communications (DTC)

> Tactical IP mesh, COFDM and software-defined radio systems for video, voice and data; part of Codan (Australia).

## What they do

Domo Tactical Communications (DTC), a Codan Company, designs and manufactures mission-critical tactical communications systems based on IP mesh and COFDM technologies. Its solutions secure, share and communicate real-time video, voice and data to enable shared situational awareness on land, at sea and in the air, serving military, law enforcement, counter-terror, government, broadcast, commercial and NGO users. DTC was acquired by Australia-headquartered Codan Limited in 2021; the UK registered entity is Domo Tactical Communications (DTC) Limited in Whiteley, Hampshire.

## Products and solutions

- **NETNode IP mesh radios** - IP mesh radio family, with the Phase 5 generation offering dual HD video encoders and MIMO capability.
- **BluSDR family** - software-defined radios for long-range mission-critical connectivity with MANET networking, including unmanned-platform variants.
- **SDR-H2 and SDR-M** - software-defined radio transceivers.
- **Vidar** - video compression solution for real-time streaming in low-bandwidth, contested environments.
