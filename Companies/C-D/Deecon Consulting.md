# Deecon Consulting

> Strategic advisory, programme delivery, and procurement consultancy serving defence, aerospace, and security organisations.

## What they do

Deecon Consulting is a UK-based consultancy delivering strategic advisory, programme delivery, and capability development to defence, aerospace, and security clients. The company's consultants support the Ministry of Defence, its agencies, and delivery partners across procurement, commercial management, programme governance, and capability development. Consultants hold SC and DV security clearances. The company has been named in The Sunday Times Best Places to Work 2025 list for the second consecutive year.

## Products and solutions

- **Strategic Advisory** - alignment of strategic objectives with national defence priorities and policy interpretation.
- **Programme Delivery** - leading multi-disciplinary, security-cleared programmes from inception through to completion.
- **Procurement and Commercial Assurance** - strategic procurement, value-for-money frameworks, and dispute resolution for defence and infrastructure clients.
- **Capability Development** - support to SMEs seeking to strengthen bids and scale delivery into the defence sector.
