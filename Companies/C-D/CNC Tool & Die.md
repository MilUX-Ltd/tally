# CNC Tool & Die

> Lisburn precision machining business producing components to aerospace and defence standards.

## What they do

CNC Tool & Die is a precision CNC machining business in Lisburn, Northern Ireland, part of the Coggan Group. It provides machined components to aerospace, defence and space, automotive, motorsport, pharmaceutical and medical device customers. The company runs 3- and 5-axis CNC machining centres with automation and focuses on small to medium batch work, typically up to quantities of around 50, with rapid response, new product introduction and prototyping.

For aerospace and defence it produces components such as gears, housings, sensor mounts, brackets, manifold blocks, structural panels, fittings and connectors. The business was founded in 2005 as a sister company to Coggan Engineering Ltd.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision CNC machining** - 3- and 5-axis machining of complex components to tight tolerances.
- **Aerospace and defence components** - gears, housings, sensor mounts, brackets, manifold blocks, structural panels, fittings and connectors.
- **Prototyping and NPI** - rapid response, new product introduction and prototype machining.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
