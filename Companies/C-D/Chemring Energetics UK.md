# Chemring Energetics UK

> Designer and manufacturer of energetic materials, explosives and propellants for defence, security and commercial sectors.

## What they do

Chemring Energetics UK designs, manufactures and supports energetic materials for the defence, security and commercial sectors. Work covers design, manufacture, assembly, testing and through-life support of energetic technologies, including mechanical and electronic capabilities. The company operates from the Ardeer site at Stevenston in Ayrshire, which has produced propellant for over a century.

## Products and solutions

- **Explosives and propellants** - explosive and propellant products for demanding applications.
- **Energetic devices** - cartridge and propellant actuated devices and related energetic systems.
- **Explosive ordnance disposal equipment** - EOD products and battlefield simulation products.

## Group relationship

Chemring Energetics UK Limited is a UK subsidiary of Chemring Group Plc (LSE: CHG), acquired by Chemring in 2006.
