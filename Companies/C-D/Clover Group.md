# Clover Group

> Specialist freight forwarder and logistics provider serving the defence, aerospace, security and space sectors.

## What they do

Clover Group, founded in 1993 as The Clover Shipping Company Ltd, provides international logistics, customs compliance and supply chain services to regulated sectors including defence, aerospace, security and space. Operations span the UK, US and EU. The UK office runs with security cleared personnel and proprietary digital systems. The group operates through subsidiaries covering global logistics, warehousing and distribution, managed services, consultancy and IT services.

## Products and solutions

- **Clover Global Logistics** - international freight forwarding and customs compliance.
- **Clover Distribution** - warehousing and distribution.
- **Clover Managed Services** - logistics managed services.
- **Clover Consultancy Services** - compliance and supply chain consultancy.
- **Clover I.T. Services** - IT services supporting logistics operations.
