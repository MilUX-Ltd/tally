# Delta XD Limited

> Precision metrology and engineering consultancy providing aerospace-grade 3D scanning, inspection and Digital Image Correlation services to defence, aerospace and related sectors.

## What they do

Delta XD provides precision metrology and engineering measurement services across aerospace, defence, security, energy and transport. The company specialises in 3D optical scanning, dimensional inspection, reverse engineering, fixture design, and full-field structural testing using Digital Image Correlation (DIC). It operates as ZEISS Certified Metrology Partner for the UK and Ireland, responsible for the sale, training and aftercare of ARAMIS, TRITOP, PONTOS and ARGUS systems across industry, research and academia.

## Products and solutions

- **3D Scanning and Inspection** - high-resolution non-contact dimensional measurement and production verification using optical scanning systems.
- **Digital Image Correlation (DIC)** - full-field, non-contact strain and deformation analysis for structural testing and validation.
- **Reverse Engineering** - mesh-to-CAD reconstruction and digital twin creation from physical components.
- **Fixture Design and Manufacture** - custom fixtures enabling repeatable, production-ready inspection workflows.
