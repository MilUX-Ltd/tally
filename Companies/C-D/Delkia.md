# Delkia

> Specialist engineering and technology company delivering assured C4ISR, mission systems and platform integration for defence and nuclear sectors.

## What they do

Delkia provides specialist control, engineering and instrumentation for safety-related and mission-critical systems across defence and nuclear. The company delivers C4ISR, tactical data links, combat systems, platform and propulsion engineering, and secure communications, fusing mission data from multiple sources into a common operational picture. In November 2024 Delkia acquired SyntheSys Defence Systems, a provider of Tactical Data Link and secure communications solutions with over 30 years of experience, which now operates as a subsidiary. Delkia is headquartered at the Delkia Nexus Centre in Haydock, St. Helens, where it operates an 18,000 sq ft production and testing facility.

## Products and solutions

- **Tactical Data Link (TDL) systems** - Link 11, Link 16, Link 22, VMF and JREAP interoperability and secure real-time data transmission solutions, delivered through SyntheSys Defence Systems.
- **Mission systems integration** - Platform and propulsion engineering for aerospace and maritime defence applications.
- **Defence digital systems** - Cybersecurity for mission-critical systems, data and mission management solutions.
- **Production build and power/propulsion systems** - Assembly, testing and storage of power and propulsion systems across aerospace, maritime and nuclear sectors.
