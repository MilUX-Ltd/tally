# Carl Zeiss

> UK arm of the ZEISS optics and metrology group, supplying measurement systems used in aerospace, defence and space manufacturing.

## What they do

Carl Zeiss Ltd is the UK operating company of ZEISS, the German optics and optoelectronics group headquartered in Oberkochen. In the UK it supplies coordinate measuring machines, optical and multisensor systems and metrology software used by aerospace, defence and space manufacturers for inspection and quality control. ZEISS UK operates a Quality Excellence Centre in Rugby offering demonstrations, training, and measurement and inspection services.

## Products and solutions

- **Coordinate measuring machines** - contact and multisensor systems for dimensional measurement of precision components.
- **Optical and multisensor systems** - measurement systems for high-precision inspection.
- **Metrology software** - software for measurement, analysis and quality control.
