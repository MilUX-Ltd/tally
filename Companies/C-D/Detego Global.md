# Detego Global

> UK developer of digital forensics, case management and endpoint monitoring software for military, law enforcement and intelligence agencies.

## What they do

Detego Global is the trading name of Mobile Content Management Solutions Limited, a British company founded in 2003 and headquartered in Horsham, West Sussex. The company develops digital forensics, case management and endpoint monitoring software for military units, law enforcement teams, intelligence agencies and enterprise organisations. Its solutions are used in more than 70 countries and are the de facto standard within the Five Eyes community for digital data acquisition and triage. The company holds patented technology and is an active community member of the Accelerated Capability Environment (ACE) network.

## Products and solutions

- **Detego Unified Digital Forensics Platform** - end-to-end suite of modular tools for field-based data acquisition and triage as well as lab-based automated analysis, covering computer forensics, mobile forensics, remote acquisition and AI-assisted analysis.
- **Military Deployment Pack** - ruggedised field kit designed for document and media exploitation (DOMEX), sensitive site exploitation (SSE) and tactical media exploitation (TME) operations.
- **Detego Case Manager for DFIR** - purpose-built case management platform for digital forensics and incident response workflows, launched as a standalone product in November 2025.
- **Detego Endpoint Monitor** - endpoint monitoring solution for enterprise and government environments.
- **Ballistic Imager** - high-speed forensic imaging tool for field and lab use, launched December 2025.
- **Mission Darkness Faraday Products** - RF shielding bags and solutions for device isolation during evidence handling.
