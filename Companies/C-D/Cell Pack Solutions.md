# Cell Pack Solutions

> UK manufacturer of custom battery packs and rugged rechargeable lithium power systems, marketed to security, emergency services and defence under the Tracer brand.

## What they do

Cell Pack Solutions designs and manufactures custom battery packs in the UK using a range of cell chemistries, supplying sectors including security and access control, emergency and medical, oceanographic and environmental monitoring, smart metering, surveillance, asset tracking and defence. Its Tracer Power brand provides rugged rechargeable lithium 12V and 24V battery systems engineered and manufactured in the UK. The company is based in South Shields and has supplied custom battery packs to the emergency services for over 25 years. In April 2024 it became part of the Celltech Group within Addtech Electrification, a unit of the Swedish listed group Addtech AB.

## Products and solutions

- **Custom battery packs** - bespoke packs across Lithium-Ion, Lithium Polymer and Lithium Iron Phosphate chemistries.
- **Tracer Power batteries** - rugged, lightweight portable rechargeable lithium 12V and 24V batteries marketed to security, emergency services and defence.
- **Chargers and testers** - battery chargers and testing equipment.
