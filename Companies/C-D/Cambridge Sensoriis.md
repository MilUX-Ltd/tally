# Cambridge Sensoriis

> Developer of low size, weight and power radar and sensing systems for uncrewed aerial vehicles in military and commercial use.

## What they do

Cambridge Sensoriis develops micro-radar and sensing systems for uncrewed aerial vehicles. Its radars use frequency modulated continuous wave (FMCW) methodology in a low size, weight and power form factor, supporting operation in adverse weather and GNSS-denied environments. The company was founded in 2020 and is based in Bishop's Stortford.

## Products and solutions

- **RadarAware** - a compact, power-efficient radar weighing under 500 grams, with no moving parts and electronically steered antenna arrays, for detect-and-avoid and collision avoidance.
- **ARC (Active Radar Cooperating)** - patented technology providing localisation based on direct radar range measurement, independent of satellites and resilient to jamming.
