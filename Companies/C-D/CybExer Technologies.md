# CybExer Technologies

> Estonian cyber range and live-fire cyber training platforms for defence, government and critical infrastructure.

## What they do

CybExer Technologies is a NATO-awarded Estonian cybersecurity company that builds and maintains cyber security training platforms with a focus on cyber capability development. Its cyber range is a virtual environment that can run thousands of virtual machines at once and supports highly realistic live-fire exercises, cyber skills testing, capture-the-flag competitions and security testing. The platforms are used for training across the spectrum from ordinary users and technical responders to strategic leadership.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber range** - proprietary cyber range platform, offered as SaaS and as bespoke on-premise deployments, for running live-fire exercises and skills testing.
- **Trainings and exercises** - delivery of cyber security trainings, exercises and capture-the-flag events on the range, including the Cyber Battle of Estonia.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** described publicly as a NATO-awarded company.
