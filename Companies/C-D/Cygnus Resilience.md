# Cygnus Resilience

> Specialist risk management consultancy advising governments, multinationals, and private clients on geopolitical risk, security resilience, and strategic intelligence.

## What they do

Cygnus Resilience delivers country risk analysis, security management advisory, and expert witness services in international arbitration. Their practice spans geopolitical intelligence, crisis planning, organisational resilience, and investigations. They have contributed to the Multinational Capability Development Campaign (MCDC), specifically the Defence Training Resilience Maturity Model (DTRMM), which supports allied training establishments in assessing readiness for crisis and conflict.

## Products and solutions

- **SecurityPLUS** - Consultancy and managed service covering crisis management, response planning, and organisational resilience.
- **Applied-ESG** - ESG advisory complementing security and country risk engagements.
- **Expert Services** - Expert witness and expert advisor for international arbitration, civil litigation, and alternative dispute resolution.
- **Country Risk Analysis** - Geopolitical assessment for clients operating in fragile and conflict-affected states.
