# DHELTA Limited

> Defence software startup developing a unified software supply chain to accelerate prototype warfare for high-performance edge systems.

## What they do

DHELTA describes itself as a prototype warfare accelerant, focused on reducing three bottlenecks in high-performance defence systems: complexity, latency, and integration cost. Engineers write high-level logic in Fluxtion, which DHELTA's Optimiser compiles into machine code deployed on the company's ruggedised Mongoose hardware platform. The company also fuses high-volume data streams into a single decision framework to support real-time situational awareness and faster targeting in high-threat environments.

DHELTA was incorporated in July 2025 and is registered in Cheltenham. Its Companies House SIC codes include both business software development and defence activities.

## Products and solutions

- **Fluxtion** - a high-level logic language for writing safe, optimised defence software.
- **Optimiser** - compiles Fluxtion logic into hyper-efficient machine code for low-power edge devices.
- **Mongoose** - standardised, ruggedised hardware platform for edge deployment in defence environments.
