# D-RisQ

> Malvern-based software verification specialist applying formal mathematical proof techniques to safety-critical and mission-critical embedded software in aerospace, defence, autonomous systems, and space.

## What they do

D-RisQ Ltd was incorporated in 2011, drawing on over 30 years of government-backed formal methods research. The company develops and applies automated verification tools that generate machine-checkable mathematical proof of software correctness, replacing or substantially reducing the testing burden required by civil and military certification standards including DO-178C (with the DO-333 Formal Methods Supplement) to Level A (highest integrity). Customers span aerospace, defence, autonomous systems, automotive, marine, nuclear, medical, robotics, and space. The company is based at Malvern Hills Science Park, Worcestershire.

## Products and solutions

- **Kapture** - requirements authoring tool producing unambiguous, verifiable, and traceable natural-language requirements.
- **Modelworks** - automated formal verification of Simulink and Stateflow designs against requirements.
- **CLawZ** - proves that C code generated from a Simulink/Stateflow model is a complete and correct implementation of the design; eliminates downstream unit testing at that stage.
- **FEVER** - proves that binary code correctly implements source code, catching errors introduced by the compiler.
- **System Kapture** - system-level requirements management for multi-stakeholder environments.

Known application: Eurofighter Typhoon auto-throttle and auto-pilot verification (QinetiQ-led programme).
