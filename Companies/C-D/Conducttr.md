# Conducttr

> Crisis exercise and information-operations simulation software for defence, security and corporate users.

## What they do

Conducttr provides crisis exercising software that creates a high-fidelity, realistic exercising environment for in-person or remote sessions. The platform models a simulated information environment of social media and news, and models target-audience reactions, with a particular focus on training for influence and information warfare. The company began in transmedia storytelling for entertainment and moved into immersive training for defence and security following Defence and Security Accelerator (DASA) funding. The legal entity is Transmedia Storyteller Ltd.

## Products and solutions

- **Crisis simulation platform** - software for building and running crisis and information-warfare exercises, with a Scenario Editor and ready-made content.
- **Live Sim** - facilitated live exercise simulation delivered on the platform.
- **Exercise-building service** - a service to design and build bespoke exercises for clients.
- **Kaliningrad Crucible** - a public wargame co-created with author and consultant Mark Galeotti, in which teams compete for control of the information space in a geopolitical crisis scenario.
