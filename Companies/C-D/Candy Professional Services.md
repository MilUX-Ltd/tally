# Candy Professional Services

> Defence and security consultancy advising on logistics, UAS and counter-UAS, maritime and aviation, and project management.

## What they do

Candy Professional Services is the registered entity within the Candy Group, a consultancy advising defence and security clients on logistics and supply chain development, uncrewed autonomous systems (UAS and C-UAS), maritime and aviation solutions, and project design and risk management. The group operates internationally and is an ADS member.

## Products and solutions

- **UAS and C-UAS advisory** - Strategic and tactical advice on uncrewed autonomous systems and counter-UAS responses, delivered through strategic technology partnerships.
- **Logistics and supply chain development** - End-to-end supply chain design, vendor sourcing, and operational support including compliance and ITAR.
- **Maritime and aviation solutions** - Project delivery support across maritime and aviation environments.
- **Cyber advisory** - Cyber advisory services alongside logistics and project management work.
