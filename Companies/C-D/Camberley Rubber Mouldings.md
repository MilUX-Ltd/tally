# Camberley Rubber Mouldings

> Manufacturer of bespoke rubber mouldings and custom rubber components, supplying aerospace, defence and industrial markets.

## What they do

Camberley Rubber Mouldings is a specialist technical manufacturer of bespoke rubber mouldings and custom-made rubber products, established in 1969. It produces precision rubber components and associated tooling for aerospace, electronics, pharmaceutical, industrial and military applications. Its machines support injection, compression and transfer moulding. The company is part of the Camberley Group, which also incorporates Cam Lock Ltd, a manufacturer of personal protection equipment for the aerospace, defence and industrial markets.

## Products and solutions

- **Bespoke rubber mouldings** - custom rubber components and tooling produced by injection, compression and transfer moulding for aerospace, defence and industrial customers.
