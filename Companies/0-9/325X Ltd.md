# 325X Ltd

> Venture studio focused on dual-use defence technology and cyber.

## What they do

325X is a UK venture studio that invests in, co-builds and advises founders developing dual-use defence and cyber technologies. It focuses on critical national infrastructure protection, national security and law enforcement. The company is based in London and is an appointed representative of Enterprise Investment Partners LLP.

## Products and solutions

- **Venture studio** - investment and co-building for dual-use defence and cyber startups.
- **Advisory** - management consulting for founders in national security and critical infrastructure.
