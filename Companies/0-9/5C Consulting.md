# 5C Consulting

> Defence and security consultancy delivering pre-contract business-winning support, subject matter expertise, and digital twin services for military training and procurement programmes.

## What they do

5C Consulting Ltd provides pre-contract consultancy support and post-contract subject matter expertise to the UK defence and security sector. The team carries over 100 years of combined military service and industry experience across multi-domain technical disciplines, alongside APM-accredited project delivery specialists. The company also provides 3D survey and digital twin services for defence and construction procurement, with particular focus on the Defence Training Estate.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence Consultancy** - pre-contract business-winning support and post-contract subject matter expertise for military equipment, training, and experimentation programmes.
- **Digital Twinning** - 3D capture and digital twin creation services for the Defence Training Estate and construction sector procurement.
- **Infrastructure Design** - concept to detailed design (RIBA Stages 0-4) for defence infrastructure projects.
