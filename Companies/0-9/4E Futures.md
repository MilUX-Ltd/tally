# 4E Futures

> Contract electronics manufacturer serving defence, medical, aerospace, and motorsport with low-to-medium volume, high-regulatory PCBA and box-build assembly.

## What they do

4E Futures, trading as 4E Technology, is a Salisbury-based contract electronics manufacturer providing tailor-made, value-added manufacturing solutions for technically demanding electronic products. The company focuses on highly regulated markets including defence, and offers full traceability alongside New Product Introduction support. It has been delivering specialist electronic manufacturing services for over twenty years.

## Products and solutions

- **PCBA Assembly** - surface mount and through-hole assembly, conformal coating, and hand assembly.
- **Box Build and Cabling** - full turnkey box-build and electro-mechanical assembly.
- **Test and Verification** - test and verification services for electronic assemblies.
- **NPI and Prototyping** - new product introduction support and prototype manufacture.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 13485 (medical device manufacturing)
