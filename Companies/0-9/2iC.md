# 2iC Limited

> UK software company providing digital interoperability across disparate devices, platforms and systems in the battlespace.

## What they do

2iC develops off-the-shelf software that connects users to a growing inventory of disparate devices, platforms and systems, creating a responsive and resilient network with real-time access to sensors, effectors and intelligence products. The company is the inventor of the Lean Services Architecture, a modular and open architecture standard for the battlespace tested and published by the UK Ministry of Defence. It states its software is designed for tactical and DDIL (denied, degraded, intermittent and limited bandwidth) environments.

## Products and solutions

- **2iC NODE** - enterprise licence software suite providing the digital backbone, service discovery and automation across systems and devices.
- **2iC CONNECT** - software connecting disparate devices and systems, including out-of-the-box solutions 2iC CONNECT:UNCREWED (for autonomous and uncrewed systems) and 2iC CONNECT:TAK (for TAK ecosystems).
- **2iC TRUST** - secure and flexible interoperability across different trust domains (classification, nationality or organisation).
- **2iC TOOLKIT** - software for engineers to research, develop and test modular, open and interoperable systems.
