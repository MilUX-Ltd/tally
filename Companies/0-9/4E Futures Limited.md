# 4E Futures Limited

> UK contract electronics manufacturer specialising in defence, aerospace, medical, and other regulated markets.

## What they do

4E Futures Limited, trading as 4E Technology, provides sub-contract electronics manufacturing and assembly from its facility in Salisbury, Wiltshire. The company serves low-to-medium volume production requirements in defence, aerospace, medical, automotive, and cleantech sectors. For defence clients, it manufactures PCBA assemblies, full box-build units, and complex electronic assemblies including power supplies, inverters, converters, and hybrid circuits for advanced missile controls.

## Products and solutions

- **PCBA and Box Build Assembly** - Printed circuit board assembly through to full electro-mechanical integration for defence and aerospace applications.
- **New Product Introduction (NPI)** - Prototype and trial builds, design-for-manufacture assistance, and technical data pack creation.
- **Obsolescence Management** - Component selection, product life-cycle management, and legacy defence electronics support.
- **Value-Added Services** - Conformal coating, potting, encapsulation, wiring, and project recovery for harsh-environment military products.
