# 4C Strategies

> Resilience, training and exercise management software and advisory services, with a defence customer base across NATO allied armed forces.

## What they do

4C Strategies provides organisational readiness, training and exercise management solutions built around its Exonaut software platform, alongside expert advisory and software-related services. The company works across the military, finance, public sector, healthcare and utilities. It supports more than 150 customers, including several NATO allied armed forces, and operates the brand within 4C Group AB (publ), which is listed on the Nasdaq First North Premier Growth Market.

## Products and solutions

- **Exonaut** - software platform for designing, delivering and evaluating training and exercises, with planning tools, scenario generation, MEL/MIL generation, data synchronisation and integrated assessment.
- **Exercise Manager** - software for organisations to design, deliver and evaluate exercises that test resilience and preparedness across scenarios such as fire, cyber attack or crisis.
- **Resilience Platform** - integrated solutions for risk, business continuity, and incident and crisis management, plus training and exercising.
