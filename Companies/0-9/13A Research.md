# 13A Research

> Defence and security technology advisory boutique providing expert leadership, research, and mentoring to founders and executives competing in the defence and security sector.

## What they do

13A Research is a London-based consulting boutique focused on complex challenges in the defence and security community. The firm provides expert technology leadership advice, research reporting, and mentoring to founders, CEOs, and industry leaders. It was incorporated in April 2023 and positions itself as a specialist in next-generation science and technology capabilities for defence and security.

## Products and solutions

- **Technology Leadership Advisory** - expert advice on innovation and next-generation science and technology capabilities for the defence and security sector.
- **Research Reporting** - bespoke research and analysis on defence technology topics.
- **Mentoring** - skills development for operating effectively in the defence and security community.
