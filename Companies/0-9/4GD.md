# 4GD Ltd

> Veteran-owned close-combat immersive training company specialising in urban warfare training technology.

## What they do

4GD builds instrumented, data-focused immersive training environments for close-combat and urban warfare. Their stated mission is to provide technology for training that is otherwise too dangerous, difficult or expensive.

## Products and solutions

- **SmartFacility** - a five-level immersive training environment, from the reconfigurable SimWall panel system up to the ACIES synthetic wrap.
- **UAS Simulation** - synthetic drone training inside SmartFacility environments.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group (London region).

## Recent news

- **2025-09** - Announced digitisation of the British Army urban training village at Copehill Down (DSEI 2025).
