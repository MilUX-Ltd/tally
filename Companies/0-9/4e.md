# 4e
> UK electronics manufacture and assembly specialist for low to medium volume, highly regulated and qualified production requirements.

## What they do
4e is a UK-based electronics manufacture and assembly house focused on low to medium volume production within defence and regulated industries. The company operates within the D3IP innovation community at the Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
