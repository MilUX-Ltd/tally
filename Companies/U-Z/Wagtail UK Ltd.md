# Wagtail UK Ltd

> Detection dog services provider working with UK Border Force, HMRC, Police, Trading Standards, and Armed Forces.

## What they do

Wagtail UK provides specialist detection dogs and related services for government agencies and military clients, including body detection, drug detection, and explosive search capability. The company holds a continuous contract with UK Border Force for body detection dogs in northern France and operates internationally.
