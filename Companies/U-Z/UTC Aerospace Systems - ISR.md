# UTC Aerospace Systems - ISR

> Global designer and manufacturer of ISR and space products for civil, commercial, and military applications, with a UK centre specialising in imagery processing, exploitation, and dissemination software.

## What they do

UTC Aerospace Systems' ISR and Space division designs and manufactures a broad portfolio of space, defence, airborne, and force protection systems. The UK operation at Malvern specialises in the development and integration of software for the processing, exploitation, and dissemination (PED) of multi-intelligence imagery for civil and military markets, and maintains an image reference library for the storage and retrieval of large quantities of data.

## Products and solutions

- **Multi-intelligence PED software** - Software for processing, exploitation, and dissemination of airborne and space-derived imagery for civil and military intelligence applications.
- **Image reference library** - Storage and retrieval systems for large-scale imagery datasets.
- **Force protection systems** - Airborne and ground-based force protection products.
