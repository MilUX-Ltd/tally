# Vaylia Ltd

> Security consultancy specialising in high-security projects for critical national infrastructure, defence events, and government sites.

## What they do

Vaylia provides security consultancy, security systems and technology services, and transport security services to clients operating high-security and critical national infrastructure sites. Its project portfolio spans major government events, nuclear sites, data centres, transport infrastructure, prisons, and oil and gas installations.
