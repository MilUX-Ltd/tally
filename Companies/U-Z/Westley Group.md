# Westley Group

> Foundry and engineering group supplying high-integrity copper-based and centrifugal castings to the defence and aerospace industries.

## What they do

Westley Group specialises in copper-based sand castings, ferrous and non-ferrous centrifugal castings, and proof and finish machining. It supplies defence and aerospace customers with cast components that can be delivered cast, finish-machined, or fully tested.
