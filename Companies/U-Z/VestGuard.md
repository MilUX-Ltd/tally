# VestGuard

> UK manufacturer of ballistic and stab protection, founded in 2001.

## What they do

VestGuard designs and manufactures ballistic solutions from a UK factory using advanced materials and ceramic composites. The company invests in dedicated R&D to improve ballistic test results and deliver tailored protection for individual customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Ballistic vests and panels** - personal body armour designed to exceed current ballistic protection standards.
- **Ceramic composite inserts** - advanced ceramic-composite armour plates for enhanced ballistic performance.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus, UKAS, SC21 Bronze
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
