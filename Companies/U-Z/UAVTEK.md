# UAVTEK

> UK sovereign, veteran-owned manufacturer of unmanned aerial and ground systems for military and government, known for the nano Bug drone.

## What they do

UAVTEK designs and manufactures unmanned aerial and ground systems for military and government users, from nano-drones to fixed-wing VTOL aircraft. The company describes itself as a UK sovereign, veteran-owned manufacturer and positions itself as airframe-agnostic, using a single ground controller and a common operating system across its fleet to cut training time. Its systems use a proprietary AES-256 encrypted radio for electronic warfare resistance. The registered office is in Gloucestershire (the company's brief noted Bicester; public records place the HQ in Cheltenham/Aldsworth, so the real HQ is recorded here).

## Products and solutions

- **Bug nano drone** - nano UAV developed in collaboration with BAE Systems, weighing around 196g, with a stated 40-minute battery life, 2km range and a maximum speed of about 80km/h. Selected by the UK MoD to support the UK armed forces in 2020.
- **Unmanned aerial and ground systems** - a range of UAS and UGV platforms operated through a single controller and common operating system across the fleet.
