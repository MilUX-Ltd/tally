# XPI Simulation Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of driving simulators for military, corporate, and research clients, including armoured fighting vehicle training systems.

## What they do

XPI Simulation designs and manufactures a range of driving simulators, from desktop solutions to full-scale converted vehicles, serving military, commercial, and institutional customers. For defence, the company produces driver training simulators for armoured fighting vehicles and conducts defence simulation research, including work in synthetic environments and autonomous vehicle development.

## Products and solutions

- **Military driving simulators** - Full-scale and desktop simulator systems for armoured fighting vehicle driver training, supporting defence training outcomes.
- **Research simulators** - Bespoke simulator builds for academic, motorsport, automotive, and autonomous vehicle research customers.
