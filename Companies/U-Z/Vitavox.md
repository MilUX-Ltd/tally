# Vitavox

> Division of Secomak Ltd. designing and manufacturing loudspeakers, microphones, headsets, and intercom systems for military land and naval platforms since 1930.

## What they do

Vitavox supplies tactical audio communications equipment to international army and naval customers, designing products specifically for hazardous and high-vibration operational environments. Installed platforms include armoured vehicles, ships, and other military platforms where bespoke acoustic and intercom performance is required.

## Products and solutions

- **Military loudspeakers** - Ruggedised speaker systems for armoured vehicle and naval platform interiors.
- **Headsets and microphones** - Military-grade personal communications equipment for crew use in noisy environments.
- **Intercom systems** - Bespoke vehicle and platform intercom solutions designed to operational and functional specifications.
