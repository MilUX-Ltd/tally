# Xiphos Partners
> US defence-technology company combining synthetic training simulation and sustainment analytics, with the accredited JTAC Virtual Trainer joint-fires simulator.

## What they do

Xiphos Partners is a US-based defence technology and services company combining sustainment, advanced training and synthetic simulation across aerospace, defence and energy, with an emphasis on joint fires, special operations and aviation training. Its flagship JTAC Virtual Trainer is a fully accredited joint-fires simulator, deployable as a single-station VR/XR system or a 2D laptop mode, scaling for collective training and integrating with fielded systems. A separate sustainment line provides predictive analytics and lifecycle management to lift readiness. Customers include the US Office of Naval Research and other DoD users.

## Products and solutions

- **JTAC Virtual Trainer (JVT)** - accredited joint-fires simulator in VR/XR or 2D laptop modes.
- **Sustainment analytics** - predictive analytics and lifecycle management for readiness.
