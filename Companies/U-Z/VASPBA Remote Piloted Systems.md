# VASPBA Remote Piloted Systems
> Micro company at Dorset Innovation Park providing UAS and drone training, FPV equipment supply and remote piloted systems services.
## What they do
VASPBA Remote Piloted Systems Ltd is a family-run business co-located at Dorset Innovation Park, Winfrith, adjacent to the Defence BattleLab. The company delivers UAS and drone training from introductory through advanced levels, supplies and builds FPV drone equipment, facilitates CAA Flyer ID training, and operates as a DJI reseller. The Dorset Innovation Park drone aviary is used for practical flying instruction.
## Products and solutions
- **UAS and drone training** - Introductory through advanced courses, including defence-relevant use cases.
- **FPV equipment supply** - Drone builds and components.
