# WMS Logistics & Risk Management Ltd

> Security and risk management company providing protection, guarding and surveillance services.

## What they do

WMS Risk Management provides personal and asset protection, close protection, manned guarding, surveillance and security consultancy. It deploys specialised security and monitoring equipment for corporate, event and high-net-worth clients. The company is based in London.

## Products and solutions

- **Personal and close protection** - protection for individuals and executives.
- **Guarding and asset protection** - manned guarding and physical security.
- **Surveillance and monitoring** - deployment of specialised security and monitoring equipment.
- **Security consultancy** - risk management and travel risk advice.
