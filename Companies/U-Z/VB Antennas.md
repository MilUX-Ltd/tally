# VB Antennas

> Designer and manufacturer of high-performance RF and microwave antennas for unmanned and autonomous vehicles and for security and military systems.

## What they do

VB Antennas is the antenna division of Videosys Broadcast Ltd, rebranded from the company's antenna line in June 2024. It designs and manufactures broadcast-quality RF and microwave antennas for unmanned and autonomous vehicles and for security and military applications, covering UAVs, unmanned ground vehicles and unmanned surface vessels. Antennas operate across the 1.2 GHz to 7.5 GHz range, including NATO D to H bands, and are manufactured in-house.

## Products and solutions

- **Omni-directional antennas** - for mesh radio transceivers and mobile cellular/LTE stations.
- **Flexi-antennas** - flexible designs that bend without damage.
- **Sector antennas** - 80-degree and 180-degree patterns.
- **Helix antennas** - for UAV, drone and unmanned vehicle ground control stations.
- **Blade antennas** - low-profile shark-fin design for mobile surveillance.
- **Dome antennas** - wide-area coverage for mobile and surveillance use.
- **Custom antenna design** - bespoke design service for specific frequencies and mounting arrangements.
