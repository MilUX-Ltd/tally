# WireMasters UK Ltd

> UK distributor of mil-spec wire, cable, connectors and harness management products.

## What they do

WireMasters UK supplies military and aerospace specification wire, cable, connectors and cable management products to defence and aerospace manufacturers. It is the UK branch of the WireMasters group and is based in Swindon, Wiltshire. The company is an ADS Group member.

## Products and solutions

- **Mil-spec wire and cable** - military and aerospace specification wire and cable.
- **Connectors** - military and industrial connectors.
- **Harness management** - cable management, protection and identification products.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
