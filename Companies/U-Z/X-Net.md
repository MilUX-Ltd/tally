# X-Net (Services) Ltd
> Dorset technical and business services company providing 5G, IoT, cyber ranges and hosting to defence, national security and law enforcement, and operator of the D3iP arm at Dorset Innovation Park.

## What they do

X-Net (Services) Ltd, formerly Kimcell Ltd, provides technical and business services across consulting, engineering, exploitation and hosting. It serves two customer bases: defence, national security and law enforcement through its Dorset Innovation Park operation; and public and private sector internet-services customers through its web, CRM and hosting brands. Headline capabilities include 5G, IoT, cyber ranges and virtualisation platforms. It operates the D3iP identity at Dorset Innovation Park, the defence-innovation arm that co-runs the contractor-facing side of the Defence BattleLab.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber ranges and virtualisation platforms** - for defence, national security and law enforcement customers.
- **D3iP** - Dorset Innovation Park defence-innovation operation co-running Defence BattleLab partner onboarding.
