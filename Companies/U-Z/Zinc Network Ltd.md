# Zinc Network Ltd

> Strategic communications and counter-influence consultancy working on disinformation, violent extremism, and malign influence for government and defence clients.

## What they do

Zinc Network designs and delivers programmes, interventions, and strategic communications campaigns to protect open societies from threats including disinformation, violent extremism, and malign state influence. The company operates at the intersection of behavioural science, communications, and policy, working with government and international clients on some of the most complex information-environment challenges.
