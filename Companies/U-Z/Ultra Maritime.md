# Ultra Maritime

> Undersea warfare specialist supplying sonar, sonobuoys, countermeasures and anti-submarine warfare systems to allied navies. Part of the Cobham Ultra group.

## What they do

Ultra Maritime is a defence technology business supplying advanced sonar and maritime systems for anti-submarine warfare from the air, surface and undersea. Its products include sonobuoys, countermeasures, towed arrays and transducers used by navies across NATO and allied nations. The business is part of the Cobham Ultra group, owned by private equity firm Advent International following the 2022 acquisition of Ultra Electronics; tagged backed-pe on that basis. It operates an integrated design and manufacturing centre in Greenford, west London, alongside operations across the Five Eyes nations.

## Products and solutions

- **Sonobuoys** - air-deployed acoustic sensors for submarine detection, including next-generation multistatic active receive variants.
- **Anti-submarine warfare sonar** - towed arrays, transducers and sonar processing for surface, air and undersea ASW.
- **Torpedo defence and countermeasures** - systems to protect naval platforms against torpedoes.
