# Vessel Arrest Systems

> UK manufacturer of less-than-lethal vessel-stopping and waterside security systems for coastguards, customs and military users.

## What they do

Vessel Arrest Systems is a UK company that designs, manufactures and supplies less-than-lethal vessel-stopping and maritime security products, with associated training and support. The company traces its origin to a UK Government Home Office project, deploying its first system in 2003, with further development under Home Office guidance. Its products are used by coastguards, customs authorities and military organisations to protect assets and waterside zones from unauthorised or threatening vessels.

## Products and solutions

- **Vessel Arrest Boom** - a maritime security barrier that entangles a vessel's propellers to bring it to a controlled stop and demarcates a water-based security zone.
- **Vessel Arrest Fence** - surface and sub-surface protection providing static and smart-detection capability.
- **Vessel Arrest Launcher** - an aluminium and stainless-steel launcher using compressed air to deploy an entanglement device or rescue equipment without flash, heat or noise.
