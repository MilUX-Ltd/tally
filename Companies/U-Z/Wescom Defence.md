# Wescom Defence

> Defence manufacturer producing pyrotechnics, illuminators, crowd control systems, and munitions for military and security customers.

## What they do

Wescom Defence manufactures and supplies defence materials including explosives, flares, pyrotechnics, searchlights, beacons, markers, riot control and crowd control equipment, and grenades. The company also provides bomb and explosive disposal and detection systems, and targets and decoys for training. They hold ITAR approval alongside ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and Cyber Essentials accreditations.

## Products and solutions

- **Pyrotechnics and illuminators** - Flares, searchlights, torches, beacons, and markers for military use.
- **Crowd control** - Riot and public order equipment.
- **Weapons and ordnance** - Grenades, mortars, missiles, rockets, and associated accessories.
- **Search and rescue systems** - Fire fighting and search and rescue equipment.
- **EOD/IEDD** - Bomb and explosive disposal and detection systems.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
