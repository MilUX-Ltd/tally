# Vortex Communications Ltd

> London-based developer of covert monitoring, secure transmission, and hostage negotiation communications equipment for law enforcement and security agencies.

## What they do

Vortex Communications has supplied specialist communications solutions to the enforcement community since 1987, drawing on experience in broadcast technology to develop covert monitoring systems, front-end devices, and secure transmission solutions over public networks. Products are in operational use with law enforcement and security agencies requiring reliable, discreet communications in sensitive situations.

## Products and solutions

- **Covert monitoring solutions** - Discreet audio and video capture systems for surveillance operations.
- **Secure transmission systems** - Encrypted communications over public connectivity infrastructure.
- **Hostage and victim negotiation equipment** - Specialist communications tools for critical incident response.
