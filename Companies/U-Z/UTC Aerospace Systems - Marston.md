# UTC Aerospace Systems - Marston

> HS Marston designs and manufactures heat transfer and fluids management systems for military and commercial aerospace, motorsport, and electronics markets.

## What they do

UTAS Marston provides an integrated approach to heat exchangers, oil coolers, and fluids management subsystems for aircraft and aerospace platforms. The company has a manufacturing heritage dating to 1740 and supplies both military and commercial aerospace customers.
