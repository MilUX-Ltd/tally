# Videosoft

> Developer of low-bandwidth video compression and transmission solutions for tactical surveillance, covert monitoring, and security operations.

## What they do

Videosoft produces software and hardware for bandwidth-efficient live video streaming in CCTV, IoT, covert, and tactical video surveillance applications. Solutions support secure, real-time video, audio, and data streams with adaptive compression and bespoke transmission protocols suited to constrained communications environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Low Bandwidth Video range** - components and complete units for secure live video streaming over limited bandwidth links.
- **Tactical surveillance systems** - video compression and transmission products for covert and operational security applications.
