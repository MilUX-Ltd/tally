# Ultima-Forma

> Advanced materials and platform technologies for aerospace, space, defence and energy.

## What they do

Ultima-Forma develops platform materials technologies for advanced engineering sectors, including a focus on supporting the transition to net zero. Its products span electroformed waveguides, net-shape hot isostatic pressing (HIP) cans, composite leading edge protection and hydrogen storage and pipework, serving aerospace, space, defence, energy and transport customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Waveguides** - precision electroformed RF waveguide components.
- **Net-shape HIP cans** - near-net-shape hot isostatic pressing components.
- **Leading edge protection** - composite protective materials.
- **Hydrogen storage and pipes** - liquid hydrogen storage and pipework solutions.
