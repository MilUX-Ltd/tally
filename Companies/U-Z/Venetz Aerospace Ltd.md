# Venetz Aerospace Ltd

> UK designer and manufacturer of high-reliability electric propulsion units and propellers for large UAS in civil and military applications.

## What they do

Venetz Aerospace designs and manufactures Type Certified electric propulsion systems for large unmanned air systems. All design and manufacturing is carried out in the UK, based at Goodwood Aerodrome in West Sussex.
