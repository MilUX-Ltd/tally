# Vault Cloud Ltd

> UK sovereign cloud services provider delivering secure, UK-hosted infrastructure for government, defence, and national critical infrastructure at Secret classification.

## What they do

Vault Cloud UK offers sovereign cloud infrastructure built on the technology and compliance framework of Vault Cloud Australia. Data is hosted under UK jurisdiction and managed by security-cleared UK personnel. The company holds a perpetual licence to independently accredited cloud technology and targets government, defence, and national critical infrastructure customers requiring data sovereignty at Secret.
