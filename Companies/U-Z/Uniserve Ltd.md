# Uniserve Ltd

> The UK's largest privately owned logistics and trade management provider, with dedicated aerospace sector services covering ocean, air, and road freight plus warehousing and supply chain solutions.

## What they do

Founded in 1984, Uniserve combines freight management (ocean, air, road) with intelligent warehousing, distribution, and supply chain solutions, tailoring services to the aerospace sector's specific requirements. The company works with clients to optimise supply chains and deliver technology-driven efficiencies.
