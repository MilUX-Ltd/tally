# Winslow Adaptics Ltd

> Manufacturer of interconnect and mechanical solutions for defence aerospace, industrial electronics, medical, and nuclear applications.

## What they do

Winslow Adaptics designs, develops, and manufactures interconnect adapters, test and prototyping modules, and PCB interconnect solutions, with a specialism in mitigating component obsolescence for defence and aerospace customers. The company offers both off-the-shelf and bespoke fast-turnaround solutions, including complex ASIC software retargeting.

## Products and solutions

- **Obsolescence adapters** - Footprint conversion adapters from simple 1:1 to complex ASIC software retargeting, addressing device obsolescence in defence and industrial electronics.
- **Test and prototyping modules** - Bespoke and standard modules for all device types, with a focus on fast turnaround for defence and aerospace programmes.
- **PCB interconnect** - Full range of printed circuit board interconnect solutions across device types and applications.
