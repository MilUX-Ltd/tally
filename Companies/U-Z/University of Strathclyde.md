# University of Strathclyde

> Leading international technological university with active defence and aerospace research programmes in partnership with major industry primes.

## What they do

The University of Strathclyde's engineering faculty leads research across aerospace, marine, space, and security sectors through collaborative projects and strategic programmes with partners including BAE Systems, Rolls-Royce, and Clyde Space. Research is supported by access to world-class facilities and demonstrator infrastructure relevant to defence applications.
