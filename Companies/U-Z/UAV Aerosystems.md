# UAV Aerosystems

> UAS developer focused on sovereign autonomous air capability, delivering higher-category mission performance in smaller airframe platforms.

## What they do

UAV Aerosystems designs and builds unmanned air systems with an emphasis on flight performance and autonomous capability. The company is positioned around sovereign UAS development for defence missions, operating from the Dorset Innovation Park defence and security cluster.
