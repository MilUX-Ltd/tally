# UTAC UK Ltd

> Automotive engineering, testing, and certification provider to UK MoD and the global defence industry, operating from the Millbrook Proving Ground.

## What they do

UTAC Millbrook provides automotive engineering advice and testing services to the UK Ministry of Defence and defence industry clients worldwide, with expertise spanning military vehicle experimentation, trials, and certification. Operating from a 700-acre proving ground at Millbrook, Bedfordshire, the facility contains all terrain types and surfaces required by DefStan, MilStd, and AECP tests and has tested every in-service wheeled military vehicle and most tracked vehicles for the MoD.

## Products and solutions

- **Military vehicle testing and trials** - Full range of proving ground testing against DefStan, MilStd, and AECP standards including wheeled and tracked platforms.
- **Engineering consultancy** - Defence engineering advisory services for vehicle and vehicle equipment procurement and development.
- **Training** - Driving, defensive driving, and engineering training services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** ITAR Approval; UKAS; ISO 9001; ISO 27001:2022; ISO 14001; Cyber Essentials Plus; JOSCAR Registered
