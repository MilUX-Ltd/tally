# Windracers Ltd

> Developer and operator of long-range heavy-lift cargo UAVs for defence, humanitarian, and survey missions.

## What they do

Windracers manufactures and operates the ULTRA UAV, a long-distance heavy-lift autonomous cargo platform capable of beyond visual line of sight (BVLOS) operations. The aircraft has been deployed across defence reconnaissance, Antarctic geological and wildlife survey, island logistics, and parachute medical resupply missions.

## Products and solutions

- **ULTRA UAV** - Long-range heavy-lift cargo UAV available to purchase or delivered as a service through Windracers Operations; proven in BVLOS operations across multiple theatres including Ukraine and Antarctica.
