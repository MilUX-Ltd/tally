# Ulster University

> Multi-campus Northern Irish university with research activity in science, technology, and engineering relevant to the defence and aerospace sectors.

## What they do

Ulster University is a public university based primarily at its Newtownabbey campus, with additional campuses across Northern Ireland. It conducts research across engineering, computing, and physical sciences disciplines and engages with industry and government bodies including through ADS membership.
