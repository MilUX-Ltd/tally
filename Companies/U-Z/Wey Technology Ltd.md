# Wey Technology Ltd

> KVM-over-IP and integrated workplace solutions specialist for command and control rooms across defence, aviation, public safety and utilities.

## What they do

WEYTEC provides KVM-over-IP systems that switch and distribute any information source in any combination to an unlimited number of desks within control and command rooms. The company serves air traffic control, public safety, utilities, traffic management and process-controlled industry customers, with a focus on efficiency, modernisation and cost goals.

## Products and solutions

- **KVM-over-IP systems** - Keyboard, video and mouse switching and distribution over IP for control room environments.
- **Integrated command room solutions** - Tailored workplace systems for ATC, C2 and operations centre applications.
