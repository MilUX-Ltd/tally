# UTM Limited

> Manufactures non-lethal training ammunition that converts service firearms for force-on-force military and law enforcement training.

## What they do

Ultimate Training Munitions (UTM) produces precision-engineered training ammunition designed to convert duty firearms safely for realistic force-on-force training scenarios. Its products allow military and law enforcement personnel to train with their actual service weapons without live ammunition, delivering high-fidelity training under realistic conditions.
