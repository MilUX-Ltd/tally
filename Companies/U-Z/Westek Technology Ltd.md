# Westek Technology Ltd

> Manufacturer of rugged computers, servers and integrated systems for military and industrial customers.

## What they do

Westek designs and manufactures high-availability rugged computing products built to operate reliably in harsh environments. Their range covers computer servers, workstations, integrated systems and peripherals for mission-critical military and industrial applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged servers** - High-availability server platforms qualified for demanding military environments.
- **Rugged workstations** - Hardened workstations for field and platform deployment.
- **Integrated systems and peripherals** - Complete integrated computing solutions for mission-critical use.
