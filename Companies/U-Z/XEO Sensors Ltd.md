# XEO Sensors Ltd

> UK defence and security engineering consultancy specialising in electro-optic and infrared sensor technologies, software development, and defence training analysis.

## What they do

XEO Sensors provides specialist engineering consultancy and technology development for the UK defence and security sectors, with core competencies in software development, EO/IR systems, and sensor integration for challenging operational environments. The company also delivers training analysis and design services aligned with the Defence Systems Approach to Training (DSAT) methodology, supporting clients in developing training programmes that meet MOD standards.
