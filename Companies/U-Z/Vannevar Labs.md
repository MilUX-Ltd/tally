# Vannevar Labs, Inc.

> US defence-technology company building AI software for national security and intelligence missions.

## What they do

Vannevar Labs is an American defence-technology company founded in 2019 and headquartered in Palo Alto, California. It builds AI and software products for national security and intelligence missions, including non-traditional intelligence collection, maritime awareness and countering foreign influence. It is privately held and venture-backed.

## Products and solutions

- **Decrypt** - software that helps military and foreign-policy users understand, target and respond to foreign actors.
- **Agentic AI for national security** - mission-specific AI agents pre-trained for defence and intelligence tasks, applied across maritime vigilance, intelligence collection and counter-disinformation.
