# Unilathe Ltd

> Precision engineering manufacturer specialising in welding, fabrication, and CNC machining of high-integrity components for aerospace and other demanding sectors.

## What they do

Unilathe provides CNC machining, vertical and horizontal turning, welding, and fabrication services for aerospace, mining, oil and gas, rail, and industrial equipment customers. The company handles new product introduction, low-volume short-lead-time work, and high-volume run-rate production, with machining capacity up to 6 x 2 x 2 m and turning up to 2.2 m diameter by 6 m length.
