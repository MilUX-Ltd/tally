# Unipart

> Supply chain performance improvement partner operating across aerospace and defence, automotive, rail, healthcare, and other sectors, with over 50 years of experience.

## What they do

Unipart designs, makes, moves, and improves components within its customers' supply chains, combining logistics and distribution with manufacturing and supply chain consultancy. In the aerospace and defence sector, the company addresses complex supply chain challenges using real-time data and lean operational methods developed over decades of working with major manufacturers.
