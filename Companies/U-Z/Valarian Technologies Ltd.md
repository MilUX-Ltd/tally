# Valarian Technologies Ltd

> Builds secure, compartmentalised infrastructure for organisations operating in high-trust, high-stakes environments.

## What they do

Valarian's core product, ACRA, is a secure compartmentalised backbone designed to run and protect mission-critical applications at the edge, in the cloud, and on-premise. The company was founded to address failures in segmentation, audit trail integrity, and lateral movement containment that existing infrastructure products could not resolve. It is UK-founded, UK-led, and backed by defence and national security investors.

## Products and solutions

- **ACRA** - Secure, compartmentalised infrastructure backbone for mission-critical applications; proven in portable GPU clusters at the edge as well as cloud and on-premise deployments.
