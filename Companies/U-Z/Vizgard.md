# Vizgard

> London AI software company building computer-vision tools for detection, classification and tracking in surveillance, drone and counter-drone systems.

## What they do

Vizgard is a London-based SME providing an AI software toolkit that automates detection, classification and tracking for surveillance, drone and counter-drone systems. Its FortifAI platform is a distributed visual AI system that integrates with existing camera infrastructure and uses low-power commercial edge processors to deliver real-time threat detection and autonomous camera control. The company works with the MOD, the Home Office and defence primes.

## Products and solutions

- **FortifAI** - visual autonomy software providing real-time detection, classification and tracking for PTZ cameras, drones and unmanned platforms. Detects and classifies people, vehicles, boats, drones, birds, aircraft and helicopters, with a counter-UAS detect, track and classify capability.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Customers and programmes:** Work for the UK MOD, Home Office and HMGCC Co-Creation; FortifAI deployed on the Royal Navy's Extra-Large Unmanned Underwater Vessel; Home Office contract for a portable knife-detection solution.
