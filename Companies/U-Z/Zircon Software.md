# Zircon Software

> UK sovereign SME delivering safety-critical software and systems engineering to defence, aerospace, transport, and industrial programmes.

## What they do

Zircon Software supports defence, aerospace, transport, and industrial organisations with complex software and systems engineering challenges where reliability, safety, security, and long-term sustainment matter. Founded in 1999, the company covers the full lifecycle from concept and proof of concept through to development, assurance, integration, and deployment in regulated environments.

## Products and solutions

- **ZirconSoftware.AI** - Applied machine learning and video analytics for operational defence and industrial use cases.
- **Safety software assurance and testing** - Verification and validation for safety-critical and mission-critical software.
- **Obsolescence management** - Modernisation and long-term sustainment of legacy systems in regulated environments.
