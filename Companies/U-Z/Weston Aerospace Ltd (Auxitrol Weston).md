# Weston Aerospace Ltd (Auxitrol Weston)

> Designer and manufacturer of sensors for aero-engine, airframe and aerospace-equipment applications, with marine and industrial derivatives.

## What they do

Auxitrol Weston develops and manufactures temperature, pressure, speed and mass flow sensors across the spectrum of aero-engine, airframe and aero-equipment applications. The business offers research, design, development, testing, manufacturing and aftermarket services, supported by a global network of service facilities.

## Products and solutions

- **Temperature sensors** - Engine and airframe temperature measurement for aerospace and marine platforms.
- **Pressure and speed sensors** - Precision measurement products for aero-engine and equipment applications.
- **Mass flow sensors** - Flow measurement solutions for aerospace and industrial derivatives.
