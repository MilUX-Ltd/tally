# Velox Precision Ltd

> Precision CNC machining and engineering services for defence, motorsport and aerospace.

## What they do

Velox Precision is a precision CNC machining and technical engineering services provider based in Faringdon, Oxfordshire. It offers 3, 4 and 5-axis machining, CNC turning and milling, CMM inspection, 3D printing, sub-assembly and surface treatments, serving motorsport, aerospace, marine, automotive, industrial and defence customers.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC machining** - 3, 4 and 5-axis milling and turning of precision components.
- **Inspection and finishing** - CMM inspection, grinding, heat treatment and surface treatments.
