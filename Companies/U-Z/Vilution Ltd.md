# Vilution Ltd

> UK specialist in technical surveillance countermeasures (TSCM), counter-threat detection, and security training.

## What they do

Vilution is the Kestrel TSCM Master Distributor for the UK and EMEA region, and a member of the CTSC 2026 management team. The company provides TSCM expertise, operational support, countermeasure training kits, and establishment security reviews focused on detecting and defeating modern technical surveillance threats.

## Products and solutions

- **Kestrel TSCM equipment distribution** - UK/EMEA distributor for Kestrel-branded TSCM detection systems.
- **Countermeasure training kits** - structured resources for developing TSCM team capability and consistency.
- **Establishment security reviews** - advisory and audit services for physical and electronic security posture.
