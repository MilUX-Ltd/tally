# Verotec Ltd

> Designer, manufacturer and integrator of electronic enclosures and embedded computing products including VME and VPX bus systems.

## What they do

Verotec designs and manufactures rugged electronic enclosures and associated hardware products for industrial and defence applications. Its product range includes VME and VPX bus systems, which are open-standard embedded computing formats widely used in military electronics.
