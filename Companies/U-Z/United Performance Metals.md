# United Performance Metals

> Specialty metals solutions centre supplying nickel alloys, titanium, stainless steel, cobalt alloys, and aluminium to aerospace, defence, space, and other advanced industries.

## What they do

United Performance Metals (UPM), an affiliate of O'Neal Industries, holds an extensive inventory of high-performance alloys and provides processing services including additive manufacturing support, cut-to-length, laser cutting, levelling, slitting, and custom rerolling. The UK facility in Newtownards, Northern Ireland serves the European aerospace and defence supply chain.

## Products and solutions

- **Specialty alloys** - Nickel alloys, titanium, stainless steel, cobalt alloys, cobalt chrome moly, aluminium, and alloy steel for aerospace and defence applications.
- **FIRSTCUT+ Processing Services** - Value-added processing including additive manufacturing, laser cutting, levelling, slitting, and custom rerolling.
