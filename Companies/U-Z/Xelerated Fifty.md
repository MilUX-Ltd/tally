# Xelerated Fifty

> Aerospace and defence innovation accelerator helping startups, corporations and governments source, scale and commercialise breakthrough technologies in complex, high-impact industries.

## What they do

Xelerated Fifty partners with early-stage ventures, corporations, governments and investors to accelerate breakthrough technologies across aerospace, defence, shipping and energy. The organisation was founded by the team behind Boeing's Aerospace Xelerated programme and has supported more than 40 startups across 14 countries, with portfolio companies raising over $330 million in follow-on funding.
