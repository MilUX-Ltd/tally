# WithYouWithMe UK Ltd

> Veteran-focused technology company providing digital skills training, upskilling software, and employment pathways for military veterans, military families, and underrepresented groups.

## What they do

WithYouWithMe runs a free programme to help military veterans, military spouses, women in technology, and neurodiverse individuals gain digital skills and move into employment. The company also offers software products covering recruitment, upskilling and reskilling, and talent mobility used by defence and government employers.
