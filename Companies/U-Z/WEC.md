# WEC

> Systems engineering consultancy and platform integrator, specialising in engineering complex systems into vehicle platforms.

## What they do

WEC provides systems engineering consultancy services to aerospace and defence customers, with particular expertise in designing and developing systems into vehicle platforms. The company operates its own quality management system and holds AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, and Cyber Essentials accreditations.
