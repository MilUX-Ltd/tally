# VuDu Engineering Ltd

> Defence and aerospace engineering consultancy specialising in airworthiness assurance, safety engineering, and regulatory compliance for air and space platforms.

## What they do

VuDu Engineering provides technical consultancy to the aviation, defence, and space sectors, with over 40 years of collective team experience on high-profile defence programmes. Capabilities span airworthiness assurance, safety engineering management, regulatory compliance, quality management, equipment capability assessment, and project management.
