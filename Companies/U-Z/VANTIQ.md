# VANTIQ
> US real-time AI orchestration platform enabling deployment at the tactical edge, integrating sensors, AI systems and operational tools for defence and critical environments.
## What they do
VANTIQ enables the creation, deployment and scaling of AI-powered systems that operate in real time across diverse environments. The orchestration platform connects sensors, AI systems and operational tools, with strong applications at the tactical edge. The company has a UK presence through a VP Global Defence, Security and Safety and a Senior Director based in London. Around 125 employees globally.
