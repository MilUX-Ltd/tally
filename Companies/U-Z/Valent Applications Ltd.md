# Valent Applications Ltd

> Designs and manufactures invasive technology for investigation and disposal of hazardous materials in CBRN, hazmat, and decommissioning environments.

## What they do

Valent has over 20 years of experience inventing, manufacturing, and supplying invasive technology that allows operators to drill through the outer wall of a hazardous target without releasing internal contents. The capability supports CBRN investigation, bomb disposal, nuclear engineering, and hazardous material removal, and is used by leading military and law enforcement organisations globally.

## Products and solutions

- **Invasive Technology Systems** - Equipment enabling drill-through access to hazardous containers without internal leakage, for CBRN, bomb disposal, and nuclear decommissioning applications.
