# Wincanton plc

> Leading supply chain solutions provider to the aerospace, defence and security sectors, with over 65 years of experience supporting defence primes.

## What they do

Wincanton designs, implements and manages secure, cost-efficient supply chains for leading defence primes, enabling them to operate more efficiently and safely in a compliance-driven sector. The company was the first to receive SC21 Gold accreditation, and all its sites hold SC21 accreditation.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** SC21 Gold; SC21 OE; SC21 Bronze
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
