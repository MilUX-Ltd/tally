# UKC Group

> Engineering services company offering design, prototyping, manufacturing, logistics, and testing to the defence and aerospace sectors.

## What they do

UKC Group provides end-to-end engineering services including engineering design, manufacturing, logistics and distribution, and testing and certification. It holds AS9100, ISO 9001, JOSCAR, and Cyber Essentials Plus accreditations, positioning it as a supply chain partner for prime contractors in defence and aerospace.

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR Registered
