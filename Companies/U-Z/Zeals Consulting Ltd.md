# Zeals Consulting Ltd

> Defence and security sector consultancy helping companies win government contracts and navigate procurement.

## What they do

Zeals Consulting provides subject matter expertise to companies seeking to enter or grow in the UK defence and security markets. Services include defence consultancy, CBRN consultancy, counter-terrorism and intelligence, bomb disposal incident response, logistics and asset lifecycle management, and market research, drawing on P3M-qualified practitioners with experience of MOD procurement processes.
