# Wilbourne Ltd

> Cyber security consultancy providing attack simulations, GRC services, incident response and advisory, with NCSC CHECK and CREST accreditation.

## What they do

Wilbourne, founded in 2018, accelerates clients' cyber security maturity through tailored cyber attack simulations, governance, risk and compliance (GRC) services, incident response, training and advisory. It is approved by the National Cyber Security Centre as an assured provider of penetration testing under the CHECK scheme and is accredited by CREST.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber attack simulations** - Tailored penetration testing and red team exercises under NCSC CHECK.
- **GRC services** - Governance, risk and compliance advisory to improve security posture.
- **Incident response** - Response planning and live incident support.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** NCSC CHECK assured provider; CREST accredited; IASME certification body
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
