# Unbound Orbits Ltd

> Developer of hardware and services supporting In-Orbit Servicing, Assembly and Manufacturing (ISAM) operators.

## What they do

Unbound Orbits develops hardware systems and associated services for the emerging ISAM market, enabling operators and customers to service, assemble, and manufacture in orbit. The company is based in St Albans, Hertfordshire.
