# UK Precision Ltd

> AS9100-certified sub-contract precision engineering company supplying high-quality CNC-machined components to the defence, aerospace, marine, and medical sectors.

## What they do

UK Precision machines components and assemblies to tight tolerances using CNC lathes and milling centres, working with aluminium alloys, titanium, nickel alloys, exotic alloys, and engineering polymers. It is part of the Leviathan Engineering Group and holds AS9100 and ISO 9001 certification alongside JOSCAR registration.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC Machined Components** - Precision-machined mechanical components and subcomponents for defence, aerospace, marine, motorsport, and medical customers.
- **Mechanical Assemblies** - Subcomponent and platform structure manufacture to customer drawings.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR Registered
