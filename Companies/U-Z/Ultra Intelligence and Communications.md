# Ultra Intelligence and Communications
> Electronic warfare, cyber and communications division of the Ultra group, delivering tactical comms, cryptographic equipment and EW capabilities to UK MoD, US DoD and Five Eyes customers.
## What they do
Ultra Intelligence and Communications (Ultra I&C) designs and delivers tactical communications, encryption and cryptographic equipment, command and control systems, electronic warfare effects, and cyber capabilities. The division serves UK MoD, US DoD and Five Eyes and allied customers across sites including Greenford, Fleet, Havant and Cheltenham. Ultra Cyber UK is a sub-division focused on the UK cyber portfolio. The wider Ultra group was taken private in 2022 by Cobham, itself owned by Advent International, and subsequently restructured into divisional businesses.
## Products and solutions
- **Tactical radio and secure communications systems** - Encrypted comms for military use.
- **Cryptographic and high-grade encryption products** - Certified cryptographic hardware.
- **Electronic warfare systems** - UK EWST (Electronic Warfare Systems and Threat) business line.
- **Cyber capabilities** - Offensive and defensive cyber products under Ultra Cyber UK.
