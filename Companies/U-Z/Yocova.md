# Yocova

> Aerospace collaboration platform for the exchange of ideas, data and digital solutions.

## What they do

Yocova operates a digital collaboration platform for the aerospace sector, enabling organisations to share ideas, data and digital products across the industry. The platform is operated from Rolls-Royce's offices and is positioned as a marketplace and community for aerospace data and digital solutions.
