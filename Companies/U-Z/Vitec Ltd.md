# Vitec Ltd

> Provider of video encoding, decoding, transcoding, and IP streaming solutions for defence, broadcast, and government applications.

## What they do

VITEC designs and manufactures hardware and software for video encoding, decoding, transcoding, archiving, and streaming over IP networks. Their end-to-end video solutions are used in defence ISR and C4 environments where reliable, low-latency video distribution from any source to any connected device is required.
