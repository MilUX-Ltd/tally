# Wheelabrator Group Limited

> Sub-contract shot peening and metal treatment services for aerospace structural and engine components, with OEM approvals from Airbus, Boeing and Rolls Royce.

## What they do

Wheelabrator Impact Finishers provides shot peening, peen forming, peen correction, saturation peening, plastic media paint stripping and surface preparation on a sub-contract basis. The business holds OEM approvals including Airbus, Boeing, Rolls Royce and NADCAP, processing large structural ribs, spars, landing gear and aero engine components.

## Products and solutions

- **Shot peening services** - Sub-contract peening across the full skill range including correction, forming and saturation peening.
- **Surface preparation** - Plastic media paint stripping and surface preparation for aerospace components.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** NADCAP; Airbus, Boeing and Rolls Royce OEM approvals
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
