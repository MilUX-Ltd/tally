# ZeroAvia Ltd

> Aerospace manufacturer developing hydrogen-electric powertrain technology for zero-emission commercial and defence aviation.

## What they do

ZeroAvia develops hydrogen-electric propulsion systems for fixed-wing aircraft, targeting initial commercial applications in the 10-20 seat and 40-90 seat ranges at ranges up to 500 miles. The company holds experimental certificates from both the UK CAA and the US FAA and conducts UK flight test operations from Cotswold Airport; UK programmes are supported by grants from the Aerospace Technology Institute and other UK government bodies. The company is headquartered in the USA with substantial UK operations.

## Products and solutions

- **ZA600** - hydrogen-electric powertrain for 9-19 seat regional aircraft.
- **ZA2000** - hydrogen-electric powertrain for 40-80 seat regional aircraft.
