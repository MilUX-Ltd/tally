# WholeShip
> Cornwall-based UAS test and evaluation company operating the National Drone Hub at Predannack Airfield, the UK's first CAA-accredited drone test and evaluation site.

## What they do

WholeShip Ltd is a UK company specialising in the development and testing of uncrewed aerial systems and associated technologies. It operates the National Drone Hub at Predannack Airfield in partnership with the Royal Navy and the Cornwall Space Cluster, providing facilities, expertise and access to segregated MoD airspace for rapid drone capability development. Under its operatorship the hub became the UK's first CAA-accredited drone test and evaluation site.

## Products and solutions

- **National Drone Hub** - UAS test and evaluation facility at Predannack Airfield with access to segregated MoD airspace, bringing together industry, academia, government and regulators in a single sandbox environment.
