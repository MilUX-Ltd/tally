# Viable Data

> Digital and data consultancy focused on making the UK safer and more secure.

## What they do

Viable Data is a digital and data consultancy delivering strategy, process improvement and digital services, with a focus on making the UK a safer and more secure place. Its work spans advanced analytics platforms, digital operations transformation, AI and machine learning, and security and identity assurance including biometric identity verification and border security technology. The company exhibited at Security & Policing 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **Advanced analytics** - data platforms and analytics for public sector and security operations.
- **Identity and biometric assurance** - biometric identity verification and border security solutions.
- **Digital and data consultancy** - strategy, process improvement and digital transformation.
