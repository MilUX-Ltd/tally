# Wingman AI

> AI and machine learning software company enabling autonomous operation of aerial vehicles for defence and security applications.

## What they do

Wingman AI develops software using AI and machine learning techniques to enable the autonomous operation of aerial vehicles, reducing operator burden. The company focuses on autonomous flight software for defence and security use cases.
