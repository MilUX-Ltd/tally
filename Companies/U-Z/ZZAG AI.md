# ZZAG AI

> Human-centric AI company applying narrative intelligence and qualitative AI assessment to government and defence applications.

## What they do

ZZAG AI (trading as Zig Zag Technology) develops human-centric AI tools that extract insights from narrative and qualitative data, with applications in counter-disinformation, AI ethics assessment, and decision support for government and defence. The company's approach combines natural language processing with qualitative research methodology to assess AI systems for human-centricity, bias, and suitability for specific operational contexts.

## Products and solutions

- **Narrative intelligence** - NLP-based analysis of text data to surface patterns in how people and groups describe themselves and others, with applications in counter-influence and threat assessment.
- **Qualitative AI assessment** - Model card and adversarial testing frameworks to evaluate AI applications against human-centricity, reliability, and bias criteria, applied in MOD and government AI programmes.
