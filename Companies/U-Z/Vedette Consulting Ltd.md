# Vedette Consulting Ltd

> Specialist defence and security consultancy focused on decision-making under risk and uncertainty for senior national security leaders.

## What they do

Vedette has supported UK MOD, UK Strategic Command, the Royal Navy, Royal Air Force and British Army, as well as allied governments, since 2010. The firm focuses on the human dimension of decision support, applying simulation, modelling and disruptive technologies to help commanders and their staffs operate effectively in complex, ambiguous environments.
