# Wilson Computers Ltd

> Cybersecurity-focused managed service provider delivering IT services, compliance support and security solutions to defence and regulated-sector organisations.

Wilson Computers specialises in managed IT services with a strong focus on cybersecurity, helping organisations maintain compliance, meet regulatory requirements and mitigate evolving cyber threats. The company serves defence and compliance-driven sectors from its base in Northern Ireland.
