# UM Labs Ltd

> Developer of UNICUS UOS, a cloud-native operating system providing multi-layer cyber protection across IP networks and unified communications platforms.

## What they do

UM Labs R&D builds a hardened, policy-by-design operating environment , UNICUS UOS , that applies machine learning and AI to detect and counter threats across broadband, 4G/5G, Wi-Fi, LoRa, and open RAN networks, as well as unified communications platforms including Microsoft Teams equivalents. The product provides protection, interception, and archiving (PIA) across all seven OSI layers.

## Products and solutions

- **UNICUS UOS** - Cloud-native hardened operating system with multi-level protection across network, application, and content layers using AI-driven threat detection.
