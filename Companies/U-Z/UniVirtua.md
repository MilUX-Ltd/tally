# UniVirtua

> Secure networking and application delivery specialist providing portable multi-bearer bonded connectivity for deployable command and control environments.

## What they do

UniVirtua researches, develops, and commercialises highly secure and dynamic networking and application delivery platforms. Its core capability is multi-bearer bonding, which aggregates heterogeneous transport links (cellular, LEO satellite, BGAN, terrestrial fibre, MANET radio) into a single resilient pipe for deployable headquarters and command-post use cases.

## Products and solutions

- **Multi-bearer bonded connectivity platform** - Aggregates diverse transport bearers to deliver resilient, SLA-graded connectivity for mobile and deployed HQ environments.
