# WAAM3D Ltd

> Wire arc additive manufacturing technology company serving aerospace, defence, and industrial sectors.

## What they do

WAAM3D delivers turnkey Wire Arc Additive Manufacturing (WAAM) solutions for the production of medium-to-large metal components. Their offering spans in-house developed hardware, software, materials, and services, giving customers increased design freedom and shorter lead times compared to conventional manufacturing.
