# Ukrspecsystems Ltd

> One of Europe's largest developers of unmanned aerial systems, software, and UAV components, with origins in the early years of the Ukraine conflict.

## What they do

Ukrspecsystems develops and manufactures unmanned systems, ground control software, and payloads, covering the full cycle from R&D and design through to production, training, and in-service support. The company operates as an official supplier to ministries of defence internationally and works with industry partners across the unmanned systems sector.

## Products and solutions

- **UAV systems** - Fixed-wing and multi-rotor unmanned aerial vehicles for intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance roles.
- **Ground control software** - Mission planning and control software for unmanned systems operations.
- **Payloads and components** - Sensors, cameras, and electronic components for integration into UAV platforms.
