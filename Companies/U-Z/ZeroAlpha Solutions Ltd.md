# ZeroAlpha Solutions Ltd

> Defence technology company providing hybrid energy and deployable networking solutions for the MOD and NATO.

## What they do

ZeroAlpha Solutions designs and manufactures hybrid energy systems and deployable networking capabilities for use in operational military environments, with framework contracts held within the MOD and NATO. Products are aimed at reducing the load carried by dismounted warfighters while extending operational endurance.
