# Uplift360 Ltd

> Clean-tech and advanced materials company delivering sustainable recycling technologies for defence, aerospace, and security supply chains.

## What they do

Uplift360 develops and scales low-impact recycling technologies for high-performance materials including carbon fibre composites and advanced polymers, targeting a circular economy for high-value defence and aerospace manufacturing. The company works in partnership with defence primes and original equipment manufacturers to reduce the environmental footprint of materials-intensive supply chains.
