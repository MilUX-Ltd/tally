# University of Exeter

> Russell Group research university based in Exeter and Cornwall, with a broad research portfolio spanning science, engineering, and technology relevant to the defence and aerospace sectors.

## What they do

The University of Exeter holds both Russell Group membership and a Gold award from the Teaching Excellence Framework, reflecting strength in research and teaching. Research activity spans disciplines including engineering, physics, and data science, with engagement across industry and government including through ADS membership.
