# Zero Plus Ltd

> Cyber security consultancy specialising in secure cloud adoption and Zero Trust security architecture for government and corporate organisations.

## What they do

Zero Plus advises government and corporate clients on implementing the Zero Trust Security Model for secure cloud adoption, delivering security consultancy, systems integration, and software and digital services. ADS-listed capabilities include security systems and technology covering unmanned systems and biometrics, logistics and asset lifecycle management, and testing and certification services.
