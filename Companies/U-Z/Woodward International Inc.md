# Woodward International Inc

> Aerospace and defence manufacturer providing motion control, propulsion, and weapons systems technology to airframe and weapons system manufacturers.

## What they do

Woodward supplies motion control systems and technologies for aircraft propulsion, aircraft motion control, and weapons systems, working directly with airframe manufacturers and their supply chains. The company's UK presence is based in Prestwick, Scotland, where it supports aerospace and defence programmes.
