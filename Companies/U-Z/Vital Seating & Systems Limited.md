# Vital Seating & Systems Limited

> UK manufacturer of STANAG 4569 compliant anti-blast seating and rollover protection systems for armoured and logistics vehicles.

## What they do

VSS designs and manufactures safety-critical seating for defence and security vehicles, specialising in ultra-lightweight anti-blast crew seats and rollover protection systems (ROPS). Products are supplied to vehicle manufacturers and ministries of defence worldwide, with seats weighing approximately 19 kg to allow maximum armouring without compromising payload.

## Products and solutions

- **Anti-blast crew seats** - STANAG 4569 compliant blast-attenuating seats for armoured vehicles, foldable for flexible interior configuration.
- **Rollover protection systems (ROPS)** - Personnel protection for logistics trucks operating in hazardous environments.
