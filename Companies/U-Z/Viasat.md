# Viasat, Inc.

> US satellite and tactical communications company supplying secure networking and SATCOM to military and commercial markets.

## What they do

Viasat is an American communications company headquartered in Carlsbad, California, founded in 1986. It provides high-speed satellite broadband, secure networking and tactical communications systems across military and commercial markets, with decades of experience in multi-orbit, multi-network satellite connectivity. It is listed on Nasdaq under ticker VSAT. UK operations run through Viasat UK Limited, based in Aldershot.

## Products and solutions

- **Government and defence SATCOM** - protected and anti-jam military satellite communications, including work under the Protected Tactical SATCOM-Global programme.
- **Tactical data links and networking** - secure tactical data, information assurance and battlefield networking for armed forces.
- **Cybersecurity and information assurance** - encryption, secure networking and key management products.
- **Satellite systems** - design, build and operation of geosynchronous satellites and supporting ground systems.
