# UAV Tactical Systems Ltd (U-TacS)

> Defence electronics contractor designing, developing, and manufacturing unmanned air systems for intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance roles.

## What they do

U-TacS operates across the full UAS product lifecycle , design, development, manufacture, delivery, and in-service support , with a focus on ISR capabilities for the aerospace and defence sectors. The company positions itself as a specialist in small tactical UAS for defence customers.
