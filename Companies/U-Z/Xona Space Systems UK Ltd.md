# Xona Space Systems UK Ltd

> Developer of a high-precision, protected satellite navigation constellation designed to augment and harden GPS for safety-critical and defence applications.

## What they do

Xona Space Systems is building a low Earth orbit satellite navigation constellation called PULSAR, designed to deliver precise and protected positioning, navigation and timing (PNT) services. The system integrates into existing GPS chipsets, providing accuracy and resilience for applications where GPS jamming or spoofing is a critical risk.

## Products and solutions

- **PULSAR** - LEO satellite navigation service providing precise, protected PNT, designed to integrate with existing GPS chipsets across commercial and defence platforms.
