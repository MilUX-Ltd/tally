# Videosys Broadcast Ltd

> Manufacturer of broadcast radio camera systems, RF video links and antennas, with an antenna division selling into the defence and security markets.

## What they do

Videosys Broadcast designs and manufactures RF camera systems, camera control systems, RF video links and camera backs for outside broadcast and live events, and has supplied broadcasters since 2008. Alongside the broadcast line it manufactures antennas and RF over fibre (RFoF) systems in-house. Its antenna work, run through the VB Antennas division, is sold into the defence and security markets as well as broadcast.

## Products and solutions

- **Radio camera systems** - wireless RF camera systems and camera backs for live production.
- **Camera control systems** - camera control for outside broadcast.
- **RF video links and RF over fibre (RFoF)** - video transmission over RF and fibre.
- **Antennas** - broadcast-quality RF antennas, offered to defence and security through the VB Antennas division.
