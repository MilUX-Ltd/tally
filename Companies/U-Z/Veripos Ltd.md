# Veripos Ltd

> Satellite augmentation and precise positioning service provider for maritime, land and defence customers globally.

## What they do

Veripos has delivered GNSS satellite augmentation services since 1989, providing sub-5cm positioning accuracy via communications satellites. In the marine sector the company offers a full turnkey service including hardware, installation and 24-hour support; through its TerraStar brand it extends the same accuracy to land-based defence, agriculture and airborne customers.
