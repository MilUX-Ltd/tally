# Zelim

> Maritime surveillance technology company providing AI-based detection, classification and tracking for security and safety applications.

## What they do

Zelim develops the ZOE (ZELIM Optical Enhancement) software platform, an intelligent detection, classification and tracking solution designed for maritime security operations. ZOE enables real-time detection of vessels, people and other objects that are frequently missed by traditional maritime surveillance systems such as radar or human watchkeepers, using a proprietary dataset of labelled imagery that can be customised to end-user requirements.

## Products and solutions

- **ZOE (ZELIM Optical Enhancement)** - AI-powered maritime detection, classification and tracking system for vessels, people and other targets; designed to augment or replace traditional surveillance methods.
