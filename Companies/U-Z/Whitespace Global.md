# Whitespace Global
> UK sovereign-AI company building Collective, a secure AI operating system for defence, national security and other regulated sectors, deployable from cloud to air-gapped environments.

## What they do

Whitespace, legal entity Whitespace Global Limited, is a UK technology company building Collective, an AI operating system designed for regulated environments. Collective runs on cloud, edge, on-premises or air-gapped infrastructure and surfaces four applications: Chat, Lessons, Insights and Decisions. The company works with defence, national security and government partners, with reported deployments including MOD Strategic Command and the Royal Navy.

## Products and solutions

- **Collective** - modular AI operating system for governed, high-risk environments, surfacing Chat, Lessons, Insights and Decisions applications across cloud, edge and air-gapped infrastructure.
