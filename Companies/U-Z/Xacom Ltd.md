# Xacom Ltd

> Project controls and programme management consultancy serving the defence, aerospace, maritime, and space sectors.

## What they do

Xacom provides programme and project controls, schedule assurance, and contract management services to defence and government clients. The firm develops, integrates, and challenges programme schedules to reduce delivery risk, and enhances reporting and key performance indicator visibility across complex programmes.
