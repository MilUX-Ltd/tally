# Videosoft Global Ltd

> Ultra-low bandwidth video streaming for defence, drones and critical operations.

## What they do

Videosoft Global develops ultra-low bandwidth video streaming software and hardware that compress and deliver video across constrained and unreliable networks, including satcom, LTE/5G, mesh, microwave and radio. Its solutions are used in defence and military, government and public safety, unmanned systems, maritime and critical infrastructure settings, with support for the STANAG 4609 motion imagery standard.

## Products and solutions

- **FireLight platform** - modular software suite for edge-based video encoding and delivery, compressing streams down to very low bit rates.
- **Hardware encoders** - compact all-in-one encoders optimised for size, weight and power.
