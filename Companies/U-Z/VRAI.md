# VRAI
> Dublin-based defence simulation company. The HEAT platform delivers VR training with biometric and cognitive load data capture for military crews in high-risk environments.
## What they do
VRAI Simulation develops data-driven VR training for high-risk defence environments. Founded by an Irish Army veteran and a technology entrepreneur, the company captures human performance data including heart rate, eye movement, control inputs, communications and cognitive load during simulated training. Customers include Rheinmetall and the Irish Defence Forces, where armoured crews train weekly using Varjo XR headsets with integrated biometric sensors. A EUR 5 million funding round was secured in early 2025.
## Products and solutions
- **HEAT (Hazardous Environment Awareness Trainer)** - VR training platform with integrated human performance and physiological data capture.
