# Warren Services Ltd

> Subcontract engineering manufacturer serving defence and aerospace, with fabrication, CNC machining, welding, and assembly capability.

## What they do

Warren Services provides subcontract engineering across fabrication and welding, waterjet and laser profiling, CNC milling and turning, painting, and full mechanical and electrical assembly and testing. The company holds JOSCAR registration alongside ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, and Cyber Essentials accreditations.

## Products and solutions

- **Fabrication and welding** - ISO 3834-2 comprehensive quality system and EN 1090 EXC3 for steel and aluminium structural work.
- **CNC machining** - 5-axis milling, lathe turning, and laser cutting.
- **Assembly and test** - Full mechanical and electrical assembly with integrated testing.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, Cyber Essentials, JOSCAR
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
