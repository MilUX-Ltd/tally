# WorldWide Language Resources Ltd

> Defence and intelligence language specialist providing security-cleared interpreters, cultural advisors, and language trainers to UK military and government clients worldwide.

## What they do

WorldWide Language Resources provides interpretation, cultural advisory, and language training services to UK Defence and Intelligence organisations, including support for field and combat operations across Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia. The company holds extensive international military and government experience and supplies security-cleared personnel for both UK-based and deployed operations.
