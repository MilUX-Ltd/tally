# XRC Robotics Ltd

> UK designer and manufacturer of high-performance unmanned ground vehicles for defence, security, and industrial applications.

## What they do

XRC Robotics designs and manufactures unmanned ground vehicles (UGVs) built for fast, modular, and mission-ready deployment in defence, security, and industrial environments. Its platforms are engineered for rugged terrain, tactical missions, and payload flexibility, with autonomous and remote-controlled operation modes.

## Products and solutions

- **Rhino** - The company's largest and most powerful UGV platform, engineered for speed, endurance, and payload flexibility.
- **MFP-1** - Ultra-lightweight, high-speed UGV platform built for rapid deployment.
