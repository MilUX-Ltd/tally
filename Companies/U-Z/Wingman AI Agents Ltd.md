# Wingman AI Agents Ltd

> Developer of human/AI teaming systems for autonomous ground, maritime, and aerial platforms across defence and security domains.

## What they do

Wingman AI Agents builds intelligent, adaptive autonomy software that merges semantic task-based understanding with perception systems to support goal-directed behaviour across diverse environments. The technology targets warfighter and security operator situational awareness in complex, multi-domain settings.
