# Vertical Aerospace Group Ltd

> Bristol-based developer and manufacturer of electric and hybrid-powered aircraft, including full-scale flight-certified eVTOL designs.

## What they do

Vertical Aerospace designs and builds electric and hybrid-powered aircraft with the aim of enabling a new generation of advanced air mobility. The company is based in Bristol and has progressed from scale model testing through to full-scale prototype flight, receiving ISO 9001 certification as part of its airworthiness programme.
