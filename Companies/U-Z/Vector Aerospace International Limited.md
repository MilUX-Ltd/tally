# Vector Aerospace International Limited

> UK operating entity of StandardAero, providing maintenance, repair and overhaul services for military aircraft engines and airframes.

## What they do

StandardAero (trading through Vector Aerospace International in the UK) delivers MRO, engineering and support services to government and military operators worldwide. Capabilities include OEM-authorised engine and airframe MRO, component repair, and EASA/FAA/TCAA-accredited design and engineering for civil and military modification and certification.
