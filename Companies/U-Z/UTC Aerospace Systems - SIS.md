# UTC Aerospace Systems - SIS

> Designs and manufactures inertial navigation systems, gyroscopes, and terrain-referenced navigation for military air and land platforms.

## What they do

UTC Aerospace SIS in Plymouth specialises in solid-state and rotating gyroscopes, medium to high performance inertial systems, and TERPROM terrain-referenced navigation. The product range spans guidance systems for precision guided weapons, MEMS-based inertial sensing, non-GPS navigation, and ground collision avoidance systems for aircraft.

## Products and solutions

- **TERPROM** - Terrain referenced navigation system providing non-GPS positioning for aircraft.
- **Inertial Navigation Systems** - Medium to high performance guidance, navigation and control solutions for land and air applications.
- **MEMS Inertial Sensors** - Miniaturised inertial sensing for guidance and control equipment.
