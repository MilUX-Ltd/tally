# Zenotech Ltd

> Computational fluid dynamics and cloud HPC software developer serving aerospace, defence and other engineering markets.

## What they do

Zenotech develops cloud high-performance computing (HPC) and computational fluid dynamics (CFD) software, helping organisations run faster, more accurate and more cost-effective simulation. It serves aerospace, automotive, built environment, defence, renewables and transport, with HPC resources at the Bristol and Bath Science Park. Its CFD tools are tailored to transonic aircraft, both civil and defence, and to low-speed platforms, and it provides aerodynamic design analysis and drone flight-control validation services.

## Products and solutions

- **zCFD** - GPU-accelerated high-performance CFD solver, designed to be scalable and cost-effective.
- **HiPer CFD** - scale-resolving CFD simulation product for modern GPU hardware.
- **Drone aerodynamics and flight control validation** - high-fidelity CFD analysis for drone developers and operators.
- **AirSight** - service to support safer drone and unmanned air vehicle operations.
