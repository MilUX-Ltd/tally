# Viavi Solutions UK Ltd

> Global test and measurement manufacturer with a UK division focused on military avionics, electronic warfare, and radio communications instruments.

## What they do

VIAVI Solutions' AvComm division manufactures electronic instruments and test products for military avionics, electronic warfare, and radio communications sectors. The company has a broad product range applicable to multiple market sectors and a global engineering footprint.

## Products and solutions

- **AvComm test instruments** - electronic test and measurement equipment for military avionics and EW applications.
- **Radio communications test systems** - instruments for validating and maintaining military radio communications equipment.
