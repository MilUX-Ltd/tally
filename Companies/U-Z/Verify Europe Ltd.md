# Verify Europe Ltd

> Supply chain risk management and supplier performance specialist serving aerospace, defence and other quality-critical industries.

## What they do

Verify has operated in complex aerospace and defence supply chains for over 45 years, working with OEMs, Tier 1 suppliers and government agencies across more than 50 countries. Services span the full supply chain lifecycle, from supplier qualification and risk assessment through to performance improvement and zero-defect programmes.
