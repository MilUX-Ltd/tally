# Vikela Armour Ltd

> Belfast-based manufacturer using 3D printing to produce next-generation stab and ballistic body armour.

## What they do

Vikela Armour uses additive manufacturing with patent-pending materials and structures to produce lightweight, flexible, and sustainable personal protection. The APIS stab protection range is designed to perform above current standards, and the company is focused on scaling 3D printing for mass production of life-saving equipment.

## Products and solutions

- **APIS stab protection** - 3D-printed stab-resistant body armour panels designed to exceed current protection standards.
- **Ballistic protection** - additive-manufactured ballistic solutions using advanced ceramic composite-equivalent structures.
