# Zaizi Ltd

> Government technology consultancy specialising in secure digital transformation for the public sector.

## What they do

Zaizi works alongside public sector teams to replace legacy systems and build joined-up digital services that are simpler to operate and maintain. The company covers defence consultancy, security systems and technology (including unmanned systems and biometrics), software and digital services, space consultancy, and maritime and shipbuilding consultancy.
