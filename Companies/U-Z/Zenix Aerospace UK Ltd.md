# Zenix Aerospace UK Ltd

> Aerospace manufacturer specialising in complex aerostructures and aeroengine components for OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers.

## What they do

Zenix Aerospace Weston produces complex aerostructures and aeroengine components, with in-house capabilities in both precision machining and assembly. The company has operated in the aerospace supply chain for over 25 years and serves OEMs, Tier 1 suppliers and other levels of the aerospace supply chain.
