# Xi Systems
> Somerset defence software house and UK and European market leader in ATAK plugin development, building tactical edge software and systems integration for situational awareness.

## What they do

Xi Systems specialises in tactical software development, ATAK plugin development and systems integration for situational awareness in the defence and security markets. Founded in 2010 in Somerton, Somerset, it has worked with ATAK since 2014 and is recognised as a UK and European market leader in ATAK integration. Its edge software spans MANET, HF and SATCOM radio networks using open architectures such as DDS, gRPC and MAVLink.

## Products and solutions

- **Tactical software** - edge software for MANET, HF and SATCOM radio networks across battery monitoring, fire control, quadrupeds, BVLOS UAVs and manned aircraft.
- **ATAK plugin development** - plugins for Bluetooth, UAVs, video cameras, aircraft systems and radio remote control.
- **Systems integration** - combining UAS, MANET communications and machine-speed decision making for situational awareness.
