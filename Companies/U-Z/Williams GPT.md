# Williams Grand Prix Technologies

> Formula 1-derived engineering firm developing advanced battery systems and physics-informed machine learning.

## What they do

Williams Grand Prix Technologies (Williams GPT) applies Formula 1 engineering expertise to safety-critical sectors including defence. It develops advanced energy storage systems and physics-informed machine learning for mission-critical energy and control applications in operational environments. The company is based at Grove, Oxfordshire, and is an ADS Group member.

## Products and solutions

- **Energy storage systems** - advanced battery systems, including the ES10M marine battery system.
- **PhysicsML** - physics-informed machine learning for engineering simulation and prediction.
