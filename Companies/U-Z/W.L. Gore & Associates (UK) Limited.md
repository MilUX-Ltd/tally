# W.L. Gore & Associates (UK) Limited

> UK subsidiary of global materials science company W. L. Gore & Associates, supplying advanced components and materials to the aerospace, defence, and space sectors.

## What they do

W. L. Gore & Associates is a materials science company founded in 1958, with over 11,000 employees globally and annual revenues of approximately $3.8 billion. The UK entity, based in Dundee, supports aerospace and defence customers with technically advanced materials and components including cables, filtration, and protective equipment used in demanding environments from aerospace platforms to defence systems.
