# VadaTech
> US manufacturer of embedded computing platforms for defence and aerospace, specialising in ATCA, MicroTCA, VPX and OpenVPX standards.
## What they do
VadaTech designs and manufactures embedded computing platforms built around open modular standards: ATCA, MicroTCA, VPX and OpenVPX. Products include chassis, single-board computers, FPGA boards, switches, AMCs, RTMs and integrated systems for defence, aerospace, scientific and telecom markets. The UK arm, VadaTech Defence, Aerospace and Embedded Systems, is based in the Andover area and focuses on the British defence and aerospace market, including ruggedised embedded computing for prime-led platforms and COTS-based subsystem supply.
## Products and solutions
- **ATCA and MicroTCA chassis and boards** - Open-standard embedded computing platforms for rugged environments.
- **OpenVPX systems** - Integrated systems for defence and aerospace applications.
