# Urban Hawk Data Intelligence Limited

> Bristol spatial-computing firm building real-time simulation and digital twins for defence and other sectors through its Polaron engine.

## What they do

Urban Hawk Data Intelligence is a UK spatial-computing company that turns space, aerial and ground scan data into simulation-ready digital twins. Its proprietary Polaron engine hybridises diverse data sets to simulate and analyse scenarios, risk, mitigation and causation, running in real time at the edge or via the cloud. The company positions itself as an industrial research and development firm in simulation, AI and optimisation, shaping prototypes and validating them through field trials, with applications including defence.

## Products and solutions

- **Polaron World Engine** - a real-time simulation and analytics engine creating digital twins for situational awareness, capable of testing large numbers of agents across an environment to understand flows, congestion and resilience, with applications spanning routing, logistics, evacuation and crowd movement.
