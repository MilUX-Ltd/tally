> Defence and security sector business consultancy covering all five capability domains and general business practices.

## What they do

XDI Advisory provides advisory services to businesses operating in the defence and security sector, spanning maritime, land, air, space, and cyberspace domains. The firm draws on both user operational experience and commercial defence sector knowledge to help new and established companies navigate the defence marketplace and access user-oriented technical advice.
