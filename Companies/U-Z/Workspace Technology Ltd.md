# Workspace Technology Ltd

> UK specialist in modular data centres, secure facilities, and critical infrastructure for defence, government, and enterprise clients.

## What they do

Workspace Technology designs, builds, and deploys high-performance technical infrastructure solutions, with a particular focus on Modular Data Centres and Sensitive Compartmented Information Facilities (SCIFs). Their in-house capability spans consultancy, concept design, engineering, prefabrication, and onsite integration.
