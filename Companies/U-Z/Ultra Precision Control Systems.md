# Ultra Precision Control Systems
> Cheltenham-based engineering firm providing safety and mission-critical solutions for military aircraft, ground vehicles and unmanned platforms.
## What they do
Ultra Precision Control Systems delivers engineering solutions to safety and mission-critical challenges in military and civil aviation and on the ground. Products feature in the latest military aircraft and vehicles, civil aircraft, and unmanned platforms. The firm also offers solutions optimised for dismounted soldiers. Headquartered at Arle Court, Cheltenham. ADS member.
