# Vision Engineering Ltd

> UK manufacturer of ergonomic microscopy, digital 3D visualisation, and non-contact metrology systems for defence and advanced manufacturing.

## What they do

Vision Engineering designs and manufactures precision optical and metrology instruments including stereo microscopes, digital visualisation systems, and non-contact measurement tools. Their products are used in defence manufacturing, electronics, and aerospace applications where accuracy and operator ergonomics are critical.
