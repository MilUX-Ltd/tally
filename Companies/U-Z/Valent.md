# Valent
> UK counter-disinformation startup. The Ariadne AI platform detects information operations for governments and financial institutions.
## What they do
Valent Projects develops AI-powered tools to detect and respond to online manipulation and information operations. The company was founded by a former Reuters and BBC journalist with senior advisory experience at the UK Government's Stabilisation Unit. Past work includes exposing disinformation campaigns in Sudan and the UK. In early 2026 the company raised GBP 1.1 million led by the Bayes Entrepreneurship Fund.
## Products and solutions
- **Ariadne** - AI platform for proactive detection and response to informational threats used by governments and financial institutions.
