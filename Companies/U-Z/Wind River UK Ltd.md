# Wind River UK Ltd

> Global software company specialising in mission-critical operating systems and platforms for defence, aerospace, and embedded systems.

## What they do

Wind River develops and supports real-time operating systems and software platforms for safety-critical and security-critical embedded systems across defence, aerospace, automotive, and industrial sectors. The company's UK office is in Swindon and serves the UK defence and aerospace market with software solutions, professional services, and training.

## Products and solutions

- **VxWorks** - Industry-standard real-time operating system (RTOS) for safety- and security-critical embedded systems.
- **Wind River Linux** - Carrier-grade Linux platform for communications and defence applications.
- **Wind River Studio** - Cloud-native DevSecOps platform for intelligent systems development and deployment.
