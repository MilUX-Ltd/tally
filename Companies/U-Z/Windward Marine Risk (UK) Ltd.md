# Windward Marine Risk (UK) Ltd

> Maritime AI company providing predictive intelligence and domain awareness for risk management across global shipping and security operations.

## What they do

Windward operates a Maritime AI platform that analyses vessel behaviour, identifies anomalies, and predicts evolving risks to support maritime domain awareness and risk management. Its AI-driven models continuously process datasets to flag behavioural outliers before they develop into threats, serving defence, government, and commercial maritime customers.
