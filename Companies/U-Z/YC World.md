# YC World

> International data analytics platform providing due diligence, sanctions compliance, and supply chain security intelligence across 82+ jurisdictions.

## What they do

YouControl World (YC World) is a data analytics platform that consolidates official company data, UBO networks, politically exposed persons lists, sanctions registers, and high-risk indicators into a single workspace for risk assessment and operational security. It supports due diligence, AML/CFT procedures, and supply chain security for organisations operating across international markets, including defence sector customers.

## Products and solutions

- **YouControl World platform** - Unified intelligence workspace covering 82+ jurisdictions for sanctions compliance, supply chain security, and counterparty risk assessment.
