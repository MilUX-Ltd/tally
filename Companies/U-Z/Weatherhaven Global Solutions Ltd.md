# Weatherhaven Global Solutions Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of rapidly redeployable military shelters and camp infrastructure, with additional capability in airborne systems and secure communications.

## What they do

Weatherhaven designs, manufactures, delivers, and supports military and commercial shelters and expeditionary camp infrastructure for rapid deployment. The company also delivers security, safety, and systems engineering technical services for airborne systems and secure communications.
