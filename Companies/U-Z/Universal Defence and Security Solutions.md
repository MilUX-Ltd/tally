# Universal Defence and Security Solutions

> UK defence and security advisory firm drawing on a large network of former senior UK military personnel across all five operational domains.

## What they do

Universal Defence and Security Solutions (UDSS) is a UK defence and security consultancy providing strategy, operational advice, major-project support and delivery to governments, armed forces, technology companies and private industry. It reports a network of more than 700 specialists, including former UK military personnel across the land, sea, air, cyber and space domains. The company states it works with the UK Ministry of Defence, the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office, and the Department for Business and Trade, and supports private-sector clients on physical and digital security, intelligence, supply-chain resilience and leadership development.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence and security advisory** - vision, strategy and operational advice to governments, armed forces and national-security organisations.
- **Major project and delivery support** - delivery support across defence and security programmes.
- **Corporate resilience services** - physical and digital security, intelligence, supply-chain resilience and leadership development for private industry.
