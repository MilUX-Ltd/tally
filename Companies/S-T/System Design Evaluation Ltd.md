# System Design Evaluation Ltd

> Independent technology company providing impartial assessment, design, development and safety management of platforms, munitions and weapon systems.

## What they do

Founded in 1986, SDE provides technical support across land, sea and air domains in areas including requirements engineering, trials management, test and evaluation planning, and weapons effects modelling. Multi-disciplined project teams support the full CADMID cycle for defence platforms and weapons programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Test and evaluation planning** - independent T&E support across platforms, munitions and weapon systems.
- **Weapons effects modelling** - analytical support for weapons effects and lethality assessment.
- **Safety management** - design and safety management for platforms and munitions.
