# SplashMaps
> Producer of washable, wearable, all-weather fabric maps for SERE navigation and field use.
## What they do
SplashMaps produces durable fabric maps designed for demanding outdoor and military environments, with particular application for SERE (Survival, Evasion, Resistance and Extraction) navigation. The products are proven across field and adventure use. The company is a D3IP community member at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
