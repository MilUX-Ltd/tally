# THINKPOL
> UK AI safety and grey-web intelligence platform operator serving defence, law enforcement and national security customers.
## What they do
THINKPOL operates two adjacent platforms under the same brand. The enterprise AI safety platform at thinkpol.ai gives organisations real-time monitoring, incident tracking and response workflows around generative AI usage. The grey-web intelligence platform at think-pol.com indexes over 30 billion public posts, including deleted content, at sub-300ms latency, targeting law enforcement and national security teams. The platform is EU-hosted, GDPR-native, covers Russian, Chinese and Arabic ecosystems natively, and exposes capability via a Model Context Protocol interface for agentic integration.
## Products and solutions
- **THINKPOL AI Safety Platform** - enterprise monitoring and response for generative AI interaction incidents.
- **Grey-web intelligence platform** - OSINT search across 30bn+ public posts with cross-platform correlation and linguistic fingerprinting.
