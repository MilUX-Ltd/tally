# TriCIS

> Secure integrated solutions, TEMPEST and encryption technology for defence and government.

## What they do

TriCIS designs and manufactures secure systems for military and government use, delivering NCSC-approved TEMPEST and encryption solutions, secure remote working technologies and custom engineering services. It works with government, the MOD and NATO allied partners, and describes itself as a UK supplier of CFTCS and NATO-approved TEMPEST solutions.

## Products and solutions

- **TEMPEST solutions** - NATO-approved emission-secure systems.
- **Encryption and data-at-rest** - NCSC-approved secure data solutions.
- **Secure remote working** - hardened devices with geofencing and zero-trust security.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** NCSC-approved, NATO-approved, JOSCAR registered
- **Frameworks:** G-Cloud
