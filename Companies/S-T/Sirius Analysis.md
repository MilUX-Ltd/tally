# Sirius Analysis Limited

> Independent defence analysis and digital services consultancy.

## What they do

Sirius Analysis is an independent consultancy delivering operational analysis and data science, cost engineering, systems engineering, P3MO, digital services and business case development. It provides services to the UK Ministry of Defence, the wider defence industry and NATO, using evidence-based analysis to improve the operational effectiveness and value for money of defence systems, platforms and services. The company also works across the nuclear, space and energy sectors and operates from multiple UK offices.

## Products and solutions

- **Operational analysis and data science** - evidence-based analysis to inform defence decisions.
- **Cost engineering** - cost analysis and estimation for defence projects.
- **Systems engineering** - engineering support across defence platforms and services.
- **P3MO and business case development** - portfolio, programme and project support and business case work.
- **Digital services** - digital and data services delivered to government and industry.
