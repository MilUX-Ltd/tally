# SharpCloud Software Ltd

> Visual decision-intelligence software for strategy, portfolio management and roadmapping, serving the aerospace, defence, space and security sectors.

## What they do

SharpCloud transforms complex data into interactive visual stories to support decision-making, portfolio management and strategy alignment. The platform is used by organisations across aerospace, defence, space and security to make sense of interconnected data and communicate strategy across teams.
