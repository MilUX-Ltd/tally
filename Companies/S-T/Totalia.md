# Totalia
> Cheltenham-based defence and government IT integrator specialising in TEMPEST-rated visualisation, LAN, comms and VTC systems.
## What they do
Totalia supplies specialist IT, communications and visualisation systems to MOD and HMG customers. Capability covers TEMPEST-rated emissions-secure variants of electronic screens, LAN and comms infrastructure, VTC and telephony. The Cheltenham location and TEMPEST emphasis points to a customer base spanning MOD, GCHQ-adjacent and broader UK intelligence community sites. Trades under the TIA Group banner.
