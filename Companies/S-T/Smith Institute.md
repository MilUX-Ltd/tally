# Smith Institute

> Oxford mathematics, data science and AI consultancy working across security and other sectors.

## What they do

The Smith Institute is a consultancy specialising in mathematics, data science and artificial intelligence. It builds custom analytical and AI systems covering data analysis, forecasting, optimisation, machine learning, simulation and AI assurance, taking projects from concept to production. Its security work includes autonomous cyber defence, alongside sectors such as energy, transport, healthcare and satellite communications.

## Products and solutions

- **Data science and optimisation** - forecasting, optimisation and analytics systems.
- **Machine learning and AI** - custom ML systems and AI assurance.
- **Simulation and modelling** - modelling for complex systems.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; Cyber Essentials Plus.
