# Safran Engineering Services UK Ltd

> UK arm of Safran Engineering Services, providing hi-tech engineering services to the aerospace and defence industries.

## What they do

Safran Engineering Services UK Ltd is a subsidiary of Safran Electrical & Power within the wider Safran group, based in Almondsbury, Bristol. It provides engineering services to the aerospace, energy and ground transport industries, drawing on the group's capabilities across electrical systems and platform integration.
