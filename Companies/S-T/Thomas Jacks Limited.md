# Thomas Jacks Limited

> UK distributor of military and law enforcement COTS and MOTS equipment, supplying direct to end-users across the military and emergency services markets.

## What they do

Thomas Jacks holds exclusive UK distribution rights for a range of specialist brands, offering commercial off-the-shelf, military off-the-shelf, and custom solutions for military, law enforcement, and emergency services customers. Due to ITAR and other export controls, much of their military product range is supplied directly to authorised end-users only.

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
