# Sonardyne International Ltd

> Maritime and undersea domain technology manufacturer specialising in GNSS-denied navigation, surveillance sonar, and secure acoustic and optical communications.

## What they do

Sonardyne designs and manufactures instruments and systems for the maritime and undersea domain, with particular focus on environments where satellite navigation is unavailable or denied. Their portfolio covers naval applications and critical national infrastructure protection, with increasing emphasis on autonomous and uncrewed vehicle operations.

## Products and solutions

- **Sentinel** - Diver and UUV intrusion detection sonar, described as the most widely deployed surveillance sonar of its type.
- **Hybrid navigation instruments** - Positioning systems for GNSS-denied underwater and maritime environments.
- **Acoustic and optical communications** - Secure underwater communications systems for naval and autonomous platforms.
