# Strongfield Technologies Ltd

> UK manufacturer of components and construction assemblies for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Strongfield Technologies is a manufacturing business based in Alperton, Middlesex, producing components and construction assemblies for the aerospace and defence sectors. Part of the broader Strongfield group, it focuses on product manufacturing across electronic components, subcomponents, and related construction and infrastructure products.
