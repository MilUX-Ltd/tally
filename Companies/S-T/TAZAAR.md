# TAZAAR

> Asset tracking and lifecycle management platform providing digital product passports and integrated geolocation for high-value equipment in defence and aerospace.

## What they do

TAZAAR combines Digital Product Passports (ASSETID) with integrated asset tracking (GeoTag) to give organisations visibility of where assets are, where they have been and how they are being used. The platform supports asset utilisation measurement, smarter procurement decisions and full repair, service and maintenance history tracking across complex supply chains.

## Products and solutions

- **ASSETID** - Digital product passports providing a unique digital record that travels with each asset through its lifecycle.
- **GeoTag** - Integrated tracking giving real-time and historical location data for high-value equipment.
