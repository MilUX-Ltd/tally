# Toray Advanced Composites UK Ltd

> UK manufacturing operation of Toray Advanced Composites, producing prepregs, UD tapes, and laminates in carbon fibre, aramid, and glass reinforcements for aerospace, defence, and space programmes.

## What they do

Toray Advanced Composites develops and manufactures advanced thermoplastic and thermoset composite materials at its Nottinghamshire facility, supplying the aerospace, defence, and space industries across Europe and North America. With over 40 years of heritage, their materials are used in aircraft structures, interiors, space systems, and satellite components where strength-to-weight performance is critical.

## Products and solutions

- **Prepregs** - Carbon fibre, aramid, and glass prepreg materials for structural aerospace and defence applications.
- **UD tapes and laminates** - Unidirectional tape and laminate products for thermoplastic and thermoset composite manufacturing.
