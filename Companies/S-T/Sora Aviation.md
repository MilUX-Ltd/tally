# Sora Aviation

> Bristol-based Advanced Air Mobility start-up developing a 30-seat battery electric VTOL aircraft.

## What they do

Sora Aviation is developing the S-1, a 30-seat battery electric vertical take-off and landing aircraft designed to make air mobility services affordable and widely accessible. The company is based at Future Space, Filton Road, Bristol.
