# Sora Aviation

> Bristol-based Advanced Air Mobility start-up developing a 30-seat battery electric VTOL aircraft.

## What they do

Sora Aviation is developing the S-1, a 30-seat, fully electric tiltrotor VTOL aircraft with a crew of one pilot and one cabin crew member, designed to make air mobility services affordable and widely accessible. The aircraft has a baseline weight of around 11,000kg and a stated range of up to 86 nautical miles, using distributed electric propulsion. The company's primary market is civil Advanced Air Mobility, with a route-planning and pre-order partnership in South Korea. At the Vertical Flight Society's Forum 82 in May 2026, Sora presented a conceptual hybrid-electric variant of the S-1 for naval heavy-lift applications; this is a study-stage concept, not a confirmed programme or contract. The company is based at Future Space, Filton Road, Bristol.

## Products and solutions

- **S-1** - A 30-seat, crewed, fully electric tiltrotor VTOL aircraft targeting affordable regional air mobility, with first flight of a demonstrator targeted around 2028.
