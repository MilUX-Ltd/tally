# Skyports Infrastructure Ltd

> Designs, builds, owns, and operates vertiport networks enabling urban air mobility.

## What they do

Skyports designs, builds, owns, and operates networks of vertiports to enable safe and efficient urban air transport in major markets. The company provides the ground infrastructure layer for advanced air mobility and eVTOL operations.
