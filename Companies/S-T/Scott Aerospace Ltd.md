# Scott Aerospace Ltd

> Defence and aerospace supply chain and programme management firm providing procurement, equipment management, and engineering support.

## What they do

Scott Aerospace delivers supply chain management, equipment management, and project management services to the defence and aerospace industries, as well as engineering, design, and manufacturing support for safety-critical applications. The firm works across the defence supply chain, connecting procurement and programme delivery functions with suppliers.
