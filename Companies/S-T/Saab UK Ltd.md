# Saab UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of Saab AB, delivering advanced defence and security solutions across software engineering, underwater robotics, radars and armed forces training.

## What they do

Saab UK employs over 500 people across eight sites in the United Kingdom, covering software engineering, underwater robotics, radar systems and military training. The company supports a multi-million-pound UK supply chain and works closely with customers across air, land and maritime domains.

## Products and solutions

- **Radars** - air and ground surveillance radar systems for defence customers.
- **Underwater robotics** - autonomous and remotely operated systems for the maritime domain.
- **Armed forces training** - simulation and training solutions for military users.
