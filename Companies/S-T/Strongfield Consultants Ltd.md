# Strongfield Consultants Ltd

> International group providing specialist technology consultancy across aerospace, defence, aviation, and advanced engineering.

## What they do

Strongfield is an international group with over 30 years of experience delivering technology-based products and consultancy across aerospace, defence, aviation, automotive, and advanced engineering sectors. Operating from its UK headquarters in Wembley, the group has companies in the UK, USA, Canada, India, and China, complemented by strategic partnerships globally.
