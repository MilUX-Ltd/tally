# Surface Technology International Limited

> Electronics design and manufacturing specialist for aerospace, defence, and security sectors, operating since 1989.

## What they do

STI provides printed circuit board assembly (PCBA) and full box-build electronics manufacturing for aerospace, defence, security, energy, and healthcare customers. Services span concept design, prototyping, production, and complete box-build including thermal, vibration, and full product testing.

## Products and solutions

- **PCBA and Box-Build Manufacturing** - Full electronics manufacturing from prototype to production for defence and aerospace programmes.
- **Counter-Drone and Electronic Countermeasure Systems** - Assembly and integration of counter-UAS and electronic warfare subsystems.
- **Asset Tracking Solutions** - Electronics manufacturing for RFID and smart tracking applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 13485, Cyber Essentials Plus, SC21 OE, NADCAP
