# Safran UK Ltd

> UK entity of Safran, an international high-technology group active in aerospace propulsion, aerospace equipment, and defence and security.

## What they do

Safran UK Ltd is the UK holding presence for the Safran group, which operates across aerospace propulsion and equipment, defence, and security markets. The group employs over 66,000 people worldwide and serves major prime contractors and aircraft operators globally.
