# Safran Nacelles Limited

> UK manufacturer of aircraft engine nacelle systems, part of the Safran group and one of two principal nacelle integrators worldwide.

## What they do

Safran Nacelles Limited designs, integrates, manufactures and supports nacelle systems for regional jets, business jets and commercial aircraft. The company holds over 17,000 nacelle systems in service and employs approximately 3,800 personnel globally.

## Products and solutions

- **Nacelle systems** - integration of nacelle assemblies for medium- and long-range aircraft platforms.
- **Platform structures** - nacelle and pylon subcomponents.
