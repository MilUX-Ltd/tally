# Selectamark Security Systems plc

> Asset identification and forensic marking solutions provider, supplying police forces, government bodies, and defence organisations to deter theft and support recovery and prosecution.

## What they do

Selectamark manufactures and supplies property marking and asset identification products under several brands, including SelectaDNA (forensic DNA marking), SelectaMARK (visible marking), SelectaLABEL (security labels), and BikeRegister (the UK's national cycle database). SelectaDNA is used by police forces to link suspects to crime scenes and secure convictions, including through specialist trained search dogs.

## Products and solutions

- **SelectaDNA** - Forensic DNA marking solution applied to assets; links criminals to crime scenes and enables police prosecutions.
- **SelectaMARK** - Visible property marking to deter theft and aid identification.
- **SelectaLABEL** - Asset and security labelling for property management.
- **BikeRegister** - UK national cycle registration and recovery database.
