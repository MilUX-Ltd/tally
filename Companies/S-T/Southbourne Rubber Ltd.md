# Southbourne Rubber Ltd

> OEM manufacturer of rubber products for British Aerospace, the Ministry of Defence, and Royal Naval Dockyards.

## What they do

SBR (Southbourne Rubber) manufactures rubber components as an OEM supplier to BAe Systems, the MOD, and Royal Naval Dockyards. The company produces both one-off prototype parts and large-volume orders to British Standards, covering a range of rubber products for aerospace and naval applications.
