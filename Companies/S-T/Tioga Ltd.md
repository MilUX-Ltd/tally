# Tioga Ltd

> Contract electronics manufacturer producing complex, technically advanced electronic assemblies and full turnkey solutions from its Derby facilities.

## What they do

Tioga is a UK contract electronic manufacturer offering surface mount assembly, through-hole assembly, box build, systems integration, test, and configuration services. Its customers include sectors where defence-grade reliability is required, and it holds a broad portfolio of quality and environmental certifications including AS/EN 9100.

## Products and solutions

- **Electronic assemblies** - Surface mount and through-hole PCB assembly, BGA, fine pitch, and high-speed chip shooting.
- **Full turnkey solutions** - Systems build, test, configuration, and complete product manufacture.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 27001:2022, ISO 13485
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
