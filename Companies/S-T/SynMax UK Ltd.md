# SynMax UK Ltd

> Satellite data analytics company providing maritime domain awareness and energy sector monitoring using commercial satellite imagery.

## What they do

SynMax uses commercial satellite imagery to find and monitor assets and deliver maritime intelligence. The company's Theia platform provides maritime domain awareness, while the Hyperion product monitors oil and gas production activity in near real-time using proprietary satellite data.

## Products and solutions

- **Theia** - comprehensive maritime domain awareness solution using satellite data.
- **Hyperion** - near real-time monitoring of fracking crews and oil and gas activity; underpins short-term production forecasting models.
