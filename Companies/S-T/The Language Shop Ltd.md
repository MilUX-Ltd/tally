# The Language Shop Ltd

> Language testing, interpreting and translation specialist running the MoD's language assessment service against NATO standards for the British Armed Forces.

## What they do

The Language Shop has operated as a language services and examination specialist for over 30 years, working across the public and private sectors. The firm runs the MoD's language testing service, formally assessing language capability against NATO standards across the British Armed Forces, and provides interpreting, translation, and language quality assurance consultancy.

## Products and solutions

- **Remote Language Assessment** - In-house-developed technology enabling rapid, secure assessments of language capability anytime and anywhere, used for NATO-standard testing of Armed Forces personnel.
