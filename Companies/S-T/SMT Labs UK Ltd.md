# SMT Labs UK Ltd

> Independent distributor specialising in supply-chain risk management and responsible sourcing of hard-to-find electronic components for aerospace and defence customers.

## What they do

SMT Labs UK is the UK arm of SMT, an SAE AS6081-accredited independent distributor with operations in the United States and United Kingdom. The company helps customers manage semiconductor supply-chain risk and component availability through supplier qualification, documentation review, and market intelligence, securing hard-to-find parts while maintaining quality and traceability standards demanded by aerospace and defence programmes.
