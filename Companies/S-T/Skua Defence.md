# Skua Defence

> UK firm building uncrewed air system capability for allied militaries using training and simulation proven on the Ukrainian frontline.

## What they do

Skua Defence is a UK company formed by former Ukrainian and British military officers to help allied and partner forces build uncrewed air system capability. It draws on a network in Ukraine spanning training centres, simulator developers, military units and manufacturers, and applies that frontline experience to instructor development, simulation and training design. The company also advises on protecting sites against drone attack through layered defensive systems and operator training.

## Products and solutions

- **UAS capability development** - instructor-cadre development, realistic simulation and training design intended to translate into operational capability for NATO and aligned militaries.
- **Counter-drone site protection** - risk assessment and layered defensive design to detect, respond to and recover from drone incidents, drawing on operator experience from Ukraine.
