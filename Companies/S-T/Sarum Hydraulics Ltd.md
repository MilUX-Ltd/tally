# Sarum Hydraulics Ltd

> UK designer and manufacturer of manual hydraulic equipment for aerospace, defence, offshore, and industrial applications.

## What they do

Sarum Hydraulics specialises in manual hydraulics, designing and manufacturing catalogue and bespoke hydraulic equipment for pressure testing, charging, dispensing, and actuation. Products are used across aerospace, defence, offshore, and process industries worldwide, with the company providing engineering support across a product's full life cycle.
