> European IT consulting and digital services group with a significant UK defence and aerospace portfolio spanning the full programme lifecycle.

## What they do

Sopra Steria is a European technology group offering IT consulting, digital services, and software development across 25 countries. In the UK defence and aerospace market the company covers logistics, software and digital services, training, and aerospace consultancy, with digital continuity as a central proposition.
