# Sopra Steria Ltd

> European IT consulting and digital services group with a significant UK defence and aerospace portfolio spanning the full programme lifecycle.

## What they do

Sopra Steria is a European technology group offering IT consulting, digital services, and software development across 25 countries, and has worked with the UK Ministry of Defence for over 30 years. In UK defence, the company delivers MJDI, the tri-service inventory management system used by military logisticians; BluejaySecureCollaboration, a SECRET-level collaborative working environment for MOD Strategic Command's Defence Digital organisation; and the Single Living Accommodation Management Information System (SLAMIS) for the Defence Infrastructure Organisation. Subsidiary SSCL delivers payroll, pensions, HR and administrative services for serving personnel and veterans, alongside wider cross-government shared services. In aerospace, the company's Aeroline vertical, including subsidiary CIMPA, covers the lifecycle from engineering through to maintenance, with a multi-year Airbus contract to modernise engineering methods and tools.

## Products and solutions

- **MJDI** - Tri-service inventory management system used across military logistics.
- **BluejaySecureCollaboration** - SECRET-level collaborative working environment for sharing classified information between government, industry and international partners.
- **SLAMIS** - Single Living Accommodation Management Information System covering over 130,000 single living accommodation bedspaces.
- **CIMPA** - Product Lifecycle Management services for aerospace, defence, transport and energy clients.
