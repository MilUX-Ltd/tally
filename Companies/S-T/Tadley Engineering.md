# Tadley Engineering

> AS9100-certified precision engineering manufacturer supplying machined components, fabricated assemblies and specialist items including projectiles to defence and aerospace.

## What they do

Tadley Engineering specialises in high-precision machining, sheet metal fabrication, coded welding, powder coating, wet painting and electromechanical assembly in an ISO class 7 clean room. Products range from electronics enclosures and racking systems to specialist machined components and projectiles, serving defence, aerospace and adjacent industries for over 26 years.
