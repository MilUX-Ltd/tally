# Sagentia Aviation

> Aviation and defence consultancy providing technical and engineering support across civil and military aerospace, formerly known as Osprey Consulting Services.

## What they do

Sagentia Aviation delivers specialist technical, engineering and safety consultancy to safety-critical and highly regulated aviation projects across civil and military environments. The practice draws on staff with backgrounds in the RAF and other aviation disciplines to support aerospace, air traffic management, airspace, and platform programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace consultancy** - technical and engineering support for civil and military aviation programmes.
- **Air traffic management** - systems advisory for ATC and airfield services.
- **Instrument flight procedure design** - specialised IFP design services.
- **Platform and air systems support** - programme and engineering advisory for aircraft platforms.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 27001:2022, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
