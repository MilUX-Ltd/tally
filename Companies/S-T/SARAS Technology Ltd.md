# SARAS Technology Ltd

> UK RF and microwave engineering business designing and manufacturing filters, power amplifiers, and subsystem solutions for defence and security markets.

## What they do

SARAS Technology designs, manufactures, and supplies standard and custom RF and microwave products including filters, power amplifiers, and integrated subsystems. The business operates ITAR-free and serves defence, security, mobile telecommunications, and critical communications customers, developing products to demanding specifications through in-house RF design expertise.

## Products and solutions

- **RF Filters** - standard and custom microwave filter solutions.
- **Power Amplifiers** - high-performance PA products for defence and communications.
- **RF Subsystems** - integrated microwave subsystem solutions.
