# Stirling Dynamics Ltd

> Advanced engineering consultancy delivering control systems, simulation and technical services across aerospace and marine, part of the Expleo Group.

## What they do

Stirling Dynamics is an advanced engineering company providing high-end engineering products and services to civil and military aerospace and marine programmes worldwide. It specialises in active control technologies, system monitoring, simulation and modelling, and has worked on over 70 different aircraft types. The company has a long heritage in submarine control systems and marine consultancy. It has been part of the Expleo Group since 2018.

## Products and solutions

- **Submarine Control Systems** - course autopilot and hover control systems for submarine platforms.
- **Aircraft Simulator Controls** - fixed-wing, rotary-wing, type-specific and eVTOL/AAM control loading and pilot controls.
- **Airworthy Controls and Equipment** - flight-rated cockpit controls and equipment design.
- **Engineering services** - aircraft development, modifications, landing gear systems, marine propulsion, computational fluid dynamics and design organisation approval support.
