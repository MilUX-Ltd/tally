# Sonovision Ltd

> Technical publications, Integrated Logistic Support, immersive training, and ground support equipment design for aerospace, defence, energy, and marine sectors.

## What they do

Sonovision provides technical documentation, e-learning and computer-based training, Integrated Logistic Support, and immersive design including AR and VR applications, animation, and ground support equipment design and manufacture. They serve customers across aerospace, defence (land, sea, and air), energy, and marine sectors, with offices in Letchworth Garden City and Bristol. The UK operation traces its history to 1987 and is part of the Sonovision Group, itself a subsidiary of the French Ortec Group since December 2022. Sonovision is a signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Technical publications** - Authoring and delivery of technical documentation for complex platforms.
- **ILS** - Integrated Logistic Support services across the equipment lifecycle.
- **E-learning and CBT** - Computer-based training and e-learning development.
- **AR/VR and animation** - Immersive training and visualisation applications.
- **Ground support equipment** - Design and manufacture of GSE for aerospace and defence platforms.
