# Titan Labs

> Security-cleared cyber security consultancy delivering managed detection, SIEM, and forensics services to defence and government clients.

## What they do

Titan Labs provides cyber security consultancy and managed services to defence and government customers, with security-cleared consultants experienced in SIEM, data security, managed detection and response, forensics, and information assurance. The firm also holds Defence Cyber Certification and delivers security training and certification services.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed Detection and Response (MDR)** - Continuous monitoring and threat response services.
- **SIEM services** - Security information and event management implementation and management.
- **Forensics** - Data and computer forensics services for defence and government clients.
- **Cyber Essentials / CE+ certification support** - Assessment and certification delivery.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus, IASME Cyber Essentials, IASME CE+, Defence Cyber Certification (DCC) Lv0-Lv3
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
