# tlmNexus Ltd

> Defence and security software house specialising in through-life equipment management, airworthiness and asset management.

## What they do

tlmNexus designs and operates digital representations of complex, high-value defence and security equipment, enabling maintenance and operational management decisions based on accurate, real-time data. The company solves through-life acquisition and support management issues for the defence sector, combining expertise-led consultancy with software development. It has supported the UK MOD Typhoon platform since 2007 and its airworthiness software is used across the majority of the UK's military registered aircraft, including fixed-wing, rotary-wing and uncrewed air systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Resolve** - secure airworthiness and issue management application used to track and manage airworthiness-related issues across UK military registered aircraft.
- **Derive** - requirements management.
- **Approve and Foresight** - financial planning and budgeting.
- **DaRT** - Damage and Repair Tracker.
- **Observe** - post-mission data capture.
- **Evidence Document Builder (EDB)** - structured evidence assembly.
