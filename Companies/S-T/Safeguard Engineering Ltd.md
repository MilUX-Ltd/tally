# Safeguard Engineering Ltd

> Independent safety and environmental engineering consultancy serving Defence, Maritime, Land, Aviation and commercial sectors.

## What they do

Safeguard Engineering is a privately owned consultancy established in 2013, providing impartial safety, environmental and engineering expertise to UK Government departments and commercial clients. The team includes chartered engineers across safety, marine, aviation, weapons and explosives, air traffic management and environmental sciences.
