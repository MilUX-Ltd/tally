# Techni Measure Ltd

> Supplier of sensors, instrumentation and electromagnetic shakers for measurement, structural excitation and calibration applications across defence and aerospace.

## What they do

Techni Measure supplies a wide range of sensors covering strain, vibration, acceleration, load, force, displacement, pressure, torque, inertia, orientation and temperature. The company also provides signal conditioning, wireless instrumentation systems, calibration services and electromagnetic shakers for structural excitation.
