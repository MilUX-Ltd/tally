# ServiceKey Ltd

> Defence and aerospace solutions partner specialising in service design, lifecycle optimisation and digital toolsets.

## What they do

ServiceKey helps defence and aerospace organisations design, deliver and optimise service-centric products, services and processes, embedding solution toolsets and frameworks that support continuous improvement across the solution lifecycle. Their capabilities span defence consultancy, software, data analysis, simulation and modelling, and cyber security.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR registered, IASME/NCSC Cyber Essentials
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
