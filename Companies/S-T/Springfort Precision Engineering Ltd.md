# Springfort Precision Engineering Ltd

> CNC precision engineering manufacturer serving aerospace and defence, accredited to AS9100 and ISO 9001.

## What they do

Springfort specialises in the manufacture of CNC machined components across a range of materials including aluminium alloys, titanium, Inconel, composites, and engineering polymers. They work across aerospace, defence, automotive, marine, and pharmaceutical sectors, covering design, prototyping, production runs, and finishing.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC Machining** - 5-axis milling and multi-axis CNC machining in metals, composites, and polymers.
- **Finishing services** - Buffing and precision finishing as part of the end-to-end manufacturing service.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
