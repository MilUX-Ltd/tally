# Safran Landing Systems MRO

> MRO provider for aircraft landing gear systems and hydraulic equipment, operating within the Safran group.

## What they do

Safran Landing Systems MRO provides maintenance, repair and overhaul services for landing gear systems and associated hydraulic equipment across civil and military aircraft. It also offers technical support, field service support and turnkey component solutions.
