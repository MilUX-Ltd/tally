# Stonesthro Ltd

> UK-sovereign edge cloud computing for government, justice and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Stonesthro provides UK-sovereign edge cloud computing, delivering IaaS and PaaS services alongside GPU and CPU compute, storage, networking and container orchestration for low-latency, mission-critical operations. It targets government, justice and policing and critical national infrastructure, offering sovereign cloud and cross-domain solutions with optional virtual firewall, threat protection and DDoS mitigation services.

## Products and solutions

- **Sovereign edge cloud** - IaaS and PaaS compute, storage and networking across UK infrastructure sites.
- **Cross-domain and security services** - virtual firewalls, threat protection and DDoS mitigation.
