# Synthetic Data Ltd
> Developer of synthetic datasets for AI and ML development, enabling intelligence and analyst training without GDPR constraints.
## What they do
Synthetic Data Ltd builds complex synthetic data models for AI and ML development and for training intelligence and analytical professionals. The approach uses non-GDPR synthetic datasets to create realistic but fictitious data environments for analyst training and model evaluation. The company is a D3IP community member at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
