# Telefonica Tech UK Ltd

> Digital transformation partner for the UK defence sector and critical national infrastructure, part of the global Telefonica Group.

## What they do

Telefonica Tech UK delivers consulting and managed services across cyber security, cloud, data and AI, and business applications, tailored to defence environments. With over 1,000 UK-based employees, the business combines local sector knowledge with the scale of its Spanish parent to support defence organisations through complex digital programmes.
