# SDO Associates

> Swindon consultancy providing business development and commercial support to defence, aerospace and space companies.

## What they do

SDO Associates provides outsourced business development and commercial consultancy for companies in the defence, aerospace and space sectors. Services include market entry analysis, opportunity identification, supply chain guidance, discovery reports and event support. It helps technology enterprises navigate defence procurement and Ministry of Defence supply chains.

## Products and solutions

- **Business development support** - outsourced commercial strategy and market entry.
- **Supply chain and procurement guidance** - navigating defence and MOD procurement.
- **Event and stakeholder support** - engagement and networking for defence suppliers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** JOSCAR-registered.
- **Memberships:** Make UK Defence; ADS Group.
