# True Position Robotics Ltd

> Specialist in high-accuracy robot metrology and end-of-arm tooling for aerostructure assembly and high-value manufacturing.

## What they do

True Position Robotics (TPR) designs and builds robotic drilling and inspection systems that meet six-sigma tolerance requirements for aerostructure manufacturers. Their systems provide in-process and post-process 3D geometric validation and multi-robot large-volume digital twinning to 0.1mm accuracy.

## Products and solutions

- **Robot Drilling Systems** - Lightweight, low-energy robotic drilling for small aerostructure assembly to six-sigma tolerances.
- **Robot Inspection and Validation** - In-process and post-process 3D geometric inspection for aerospace components.
- **Large Volume Digital Twinning** - Multi-robot and multi-sensor guidance systems delivering 0.1mm accuracy at 99.7% confidence.
