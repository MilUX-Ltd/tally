# SCT Technology Ltd

> IoT-based asset and shipment tracking software for military and civilian logisticians.

## What they do

SCT Technology provides turnkey asset and shipment tracing software and professional services using Internet of Things hardware. Their systems give military and civilian logisticians in-transit and fleet visibility of high-value assets and shipments.
