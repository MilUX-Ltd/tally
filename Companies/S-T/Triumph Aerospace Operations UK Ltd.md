# Triumph Aerospace Operations UK Ltd

> UK manufacturing subsidiary of Triumph Group, delivering aerospace components, hydraulic systems and sub-assemblies for military and commercial aircraft operators worldwide.

## What they do

Triumph's UK operation is headquartered in Deeside, Wales, with integrated design and manufacturing capability including CNC machining, PCB assembly and traditional hydraulics. The company supplies original equipment manufacturers and operators of military, regional and commercial aircraft with components and systems spanning propulsion, landing gear, electronics and hydraulics.

## Products and solutions

- **Hydraulic and pneumatic systems** - Design and manufacture of flight-critical hydraulic components and assemblies.
- **Electronic components and assemblies** - PCB assembly and electronic subcomponents to IPC 610 Class 3.
- **Propulsion components** - Engine and gearbox components for military and commercial platforms.
- **Weapons-related manufacturing** - Missiles, rockets and submunitions components.
