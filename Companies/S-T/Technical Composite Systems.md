# Technical Composite Systems

> UK composite manufacturer specialising in high-quality components and assemblies for the aerospace and defence sector.

## What they do

Technical Composite Systems (TCS) manufactures composite components and assemblies, combining engineering experience with material expertise across a flexible production facility in Devon. The company provides full design support alongside manufacturing solutions for aerospace and defence customers.
