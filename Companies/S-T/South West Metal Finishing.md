# South West Metal Finishing

> Surface treatment and metal finishing specialist supplying the defence, aerospace, and automotive sectors.

## What they do

South West Metal Finishing is a surface coating and technology group with over 90 years of experience in metal finishing. The company supplies the defence, aerospace, and OEM sectors, providing electroplating, abrasive blasting, chemical etching, and a range of specialist coating technologies across aluminium alloys, titanium alloys, steel alloys, and other metals.

## Products and solutions

- **Electroplating and surface coatings** - chromium, non-chromium, corrosion inhibition, and primer coatings for defence and aerospace components.
- **NADCAP-qualified finishing processes** - abrasive blasting, etching, magnetic field-assisted finishing, and peening.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, NADCAP CP, NADCAP NDT
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
