# Sellectronics Ltd

> Printed circuit board assembly manufacturer specialising in high-reliability electronics for aerospace, defence, space, and medical sectors.

## What they do

Sellectronics manufactures printed circuit boards and assemblies (PCBAs) for sectors where continuous operation in harsh environments is critical. The company offers rapid prototyping, low-to-medium volume production, box build, test, conformal coating, and X-ray inspection from its Cornwall facility.

## Products and solutions

- **PCBA manufacturing** - High-reliability printed circuit board assemblies to IPC 610 Class 1, 2, and 3 standards.
- **Box build** - Full assembly and integration services.
- **Conformal coating and X-ray inspection** - Quality and protection services for finished assemblies.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR Registered, IPC 610 Class 1/2/3
