# TFD Europe Ltd

> Consultancy and tools for product life cycle management and integrated logistics support decisions in defence.

## What they do

TFD Europe provides consultancy, tools and services that enable decisions affecting the cost, performance and readiness of system support. Their focus is Product Life Cycle Management (PLM) and Integrated Logistics Support (ILS) for complex defence and aerospace systems.
