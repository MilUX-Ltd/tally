# SafeLane Global Ltd

> Specialist in the clearance of landmines, explosive remnants of war and hazardous material threats, operating on land and in water.

## What they do

SafeLane Global counters explosive and hazardous material threats and security risks for governments and organisations worldwide. The company delivers landmine and unexploded ordnance clearance operations across land and underwater environments.
