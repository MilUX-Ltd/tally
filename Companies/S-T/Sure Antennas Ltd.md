# Sure Antennas Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of vehicle antenna solutions for military, police, and critical communications.

## What they do

Sure Antennas designs, manufactures, and supplies vehicle-mounted antenna systems for police, military, and critical communications customers, predominantly in the UK and EU. Their flagship products include the Mallard and Sandcastle multifunction vehicle antennas.

## Products and solutions

- **Mallard** - Multifunction vehicle antenna for critical and military communications.
- **Sandcastle** - Multifunction vehicle antenna for police and emergency services.
