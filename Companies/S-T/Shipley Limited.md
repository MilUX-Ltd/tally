# Shipley Limited

> Bid and proposal consultancy and training firm specialising in the aerospace, defence and security sectors.

## What they do

Shipley provides benchmarking, training and consultancy to help organisations win business in complex and competitive environments. The Shipley process is used by major aerospace, defence and security companies to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of their business development and capture activities.
