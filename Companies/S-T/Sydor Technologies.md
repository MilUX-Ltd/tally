# Sydor Technologies

> Manufactures ballistics test range systems and instrumentation for ammunition, armour and weapons testing.

## What they do

Sydor Technologies (trading as SABRE Ballistics) designs and manufactures complete ballistics test range systems, from test guns through to measurement instrumentation. Customers include the UK MOD, the US DoD, and organisations in more than twenty other countries.

## Products and solutions

- **Test guns and weapon rests** - configurable firing systems for R&D and proof testing across all calibres.
- **Ballistic instrumentation** - measurement systems for breech pressure, projectile velocity, rate of fire, fuze characteristics, accuracy and tracking.
- **Doppler radar systems** - muzzle velocity and in-flight tracking solutions.
