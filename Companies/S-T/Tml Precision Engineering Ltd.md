# Tml Precision Engineering Ltd

> CNC precision engineering subcontractor specialising in 5-axis prismatic machining, 9-axis mill-turning, and multi-axis EDM for aerospace and defence programmes.

## What they do

Tml Precision Engineering operates advanced CNC machining equipment at Hethel, Norfolk, providing precision-machined components for prototype, development, and scheduled production runs. The company offers design-for-manufacture support, kitting, sub-assembly in a clean facility, and surface treatment sourcing, and holds AS9100 accreditation.
