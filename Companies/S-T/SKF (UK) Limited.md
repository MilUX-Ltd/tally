## What they do
## What they do


SKF (UK) designs and manufactures self-lubricating spherical plain bearings, rod ends and journal bearings to customer-specific standards. The company has led the development of sliding contact bearings in the aerospace and defence sector for over 40 years, supported by in-house CNC machining and a range of specialist liner technologies and bearing materials.
