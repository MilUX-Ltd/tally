# Same Day PLC Guardian Service

> Specialist secure logistics and courier services for government, defence, and public sector organisations.

## What they do

Guardian Service has over 25 years of experience providing secure, discreet logistics solutions for government and defence clients, operating beyond the capability of standard courier services. Every delivery is planned around time, security, and environmental parameters, with round-the-clock availability for sensitive and high-value consignments.
