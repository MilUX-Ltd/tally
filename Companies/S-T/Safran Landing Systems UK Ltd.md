# Safran Landing Systems UK Ltd

> UK entity of Safran Landing Systems (formerly Messier-Bugatti-Dowty), one of the world's leading manufacturers of aircraft landing and braking systems.

## What they do

Safran Landing Systems UK Ltd designs, manufactures and supports landing gear and braking systems across the full product life cycle, serving commercial, military, business and regional airframers. The company supports more than 24,000 in-service aircraft and employs around 7,000 staff worldwide.

## Products and solutions

- **Landing gear systems** - design, manufacture and in-service support for civil and military platforms.
- **Braking systems** - advanced braking solutions across airframe types.
