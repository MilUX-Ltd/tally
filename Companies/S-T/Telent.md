# Telent

> Technology company designing, building and maintaining critical network and communications infrastructure across UK and Ireland defence and public sector.

## What they do

Telent is a UK and Ireland technology company specialising in the design, build, operation and maintenance of critical digital and communications infrastructure. It draws on decades of experience in mission-critical communications and operational technology across transport, emergency services, network providers, defence, utilities and the public sector. Its stated purpose is keeping national infrastructure connected, resilient and secure. The Telent business was formed in 2006 from the residual UK services business of Marconi, with the underlying legal entity tracing to 1961.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence** - secure communications and digital infrastructure for the armed forces.
- **Rail** - rail and metro communications and control systems, including Network Rail's nationwide SCADA programme.
- **Public safety** - emergency services communications and logistics for coastguard, ambulance, fire and police customers.
- **Traffic** - intelligent traffic systems delivered with National Highways.
- **Service providers** - network build and support for telecoms operators, including full-fibre delivery.
- **Enterprise and public sector** - managed network and IT services, cyber security, and integrated warehouse logistics systems.
