# Solid State Plc

> Value-added electronics group supplying ruggedised components, assemblies and manufactured units to industrial and military markets for use in harsh environments.

## What they do

Solid State Plc (AIM: SOLI) operates through two divisions: Steatite Ltd (system design and manufacturing) and Solsta Ltd (technical component distribution, formerly known as Solid State Supplies). The group covers ruggedised computing, power, antennas, tactical and satellite communications, and optoelectronic products, providing design-in support and component sourcing for customers operating in demanding defence, industrial and medical environments. Constituent brands include Custom Power (batteries), Q-Par Antennas, Active Silicon (imaging), Gateway and Durakool (components).

## Products and solutions

- **Steatite Communications** - Tactical and secure communications, including mobile ad-hoc networking (MANET) mesh radios and UHF SATCOM terminals and amplifiers.
- **Steatite Embedded** - Ruggedised computing, single-board computers, systems on modules, and industrial panel and box computers for harsh environments.
- **Custom Power** - Custom rechargeable and single-use lithium battery packs for UAVs, ROVs, and military and medical platforms.
- **Steatite Antennas (Q-Par)** - Microwave and RF antennas and antenna subsystems.
- **Solsta sensor range** - Includes radar sensors and infrared sensing and thermal imaging components for industrial and defence applications.
