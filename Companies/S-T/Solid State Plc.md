# Solid State Plc

> Value-added electronics group supplying ruggedised components, assemblies and manufactured units to industrial and military markets for use in harsh environments.

## What they do

Solid State Plc operates through two divisions: Manufacturing (via subsidiary Steatite Ltd) and value-added distribution (via Solid State Supplies and Pacer). The group specialises in complex engineering challenges for computing, power, communications, electronic and optoelectronic products, providing design-in support and component sourcing for customers operating in demanding conditions.

## Products and solutions

- **Ruggedised Computing** - Durable computing products engineered for harsh and military environments.
- **Power Solutions** - Power products for industrial and defence platforms.
- **Communications and Optoelectronics** - Electronic and optoelectronic assemblies for military applications.
