# TWI Limited

> Independent research and technology organisation specialising in materials joining, welding, and structural integrity, with significant work across defence, aerospace, and security sectors.

## What they do

TWI (The Welding Institute) conducts applied research and provides technology services in welding, joining, coating, inspection, and non-destructive testing. It supports defence and aerospace clients on structural integrity, materials qualification, and certification challenges.
