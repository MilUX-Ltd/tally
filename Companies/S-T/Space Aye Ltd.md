# Space Aye Ltd

> Real-time satellite imagery fused with IoT data to enable live asset and people identification from space.

## What they do

Space Aye combines real-time Earth observation with billions of globally connected IoT devices to make satellite imagery immediately actionable. Their patented approach identifies assets and people in real time from space, aimed at both commercial and government customers.
