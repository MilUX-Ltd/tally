# Telesoft Technologies

> UK cyber security and telecoms network monitoring company building high-performance network visibility and threat detection systems.

## What they do

Telesoft designs high-performance data communications technologies and systems that let communications service providers, network security organisations and large enterprises monitor, analyse and protect their networks. The company is engineering-led and describes its products as carrier-scale network visibility and threat detection. It runs a UK sovereign Security Operations Centre staffed by SC-cleared analysts and serves service providers, telecoms, government and enterprise customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Telesoft Data Analytics Capability (TDAC) Platform** - cyber security platform for full network visibility and threat detection.
- **Network Detection and Response (NDR)** - network observability and threat mitigation, with FPGA-accelerated processing.
- **Managed SOC services** - 24/7 threat detection and response from a UK sovereign Security Operations Centre.
- **IntSOC / Agentic AI NDR** - autonomous AI-led investigation and detection capability.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** UK sovereign SOC with SC-cleared analysts; supports DEFCON 658 compliance for handling MOD Identifiable Information.
