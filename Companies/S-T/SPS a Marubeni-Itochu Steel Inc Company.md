# SPS a Marubeni-Itochu Steel Inc Company

> Steel stockholder and supply chain provider serving offshore, nuclear, defence, construction, and renewables sectors; part of the Marubeni-Itochu Steel group.

## What they do

SPS supplies high yield steel for offshore fabrication and extends its stockholding and supply chain capabilities across nuclear, defence, construction, and renewables. The company holds that it was the first to lead the supply of high yield steel for offshore fabrication in the UK.
