# Trimetis Ltd

> Bristol-based research and consultancy firm applying systems thinking and human factors expertise to defence and security environments.

## What they do

Trimetis works across defence and security domains to improve performance in complex, high-consequence environments. The company combines sociotechnical systems research, design and experimentation with rigorous scientific methods, drawing on academic partnerships and a network of expert associates to deliver solutions that integrate cognitive, organisational and technological perspectives.
