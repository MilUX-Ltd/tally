# Techmodal
> Bristol-based defence data analytics, modelling and simulation consultancy; BAE Systems subsidiary since 2020.
## What they do
Techmodal provides data science, modelling and simulation, and operational analysis services to MOD customers across the Army, Strategic Command and Royal Navy. Founded in 2005, the company was acquired by BAE Systems in 2020 and retains its consultancy identity. Mandates include a multi-year MOD logistics and support operations transformation contract.
