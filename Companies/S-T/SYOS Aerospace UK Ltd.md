# SYOS Aerospace UK Ltd

> Manufacturer of utilitarian uncrewed vehicles for air, land and sea, with advanced autonomy for dual-use applications.

## What they do

SYOS Aerospace designs and manufactures uncrewed vehicles across air, land and sea domains, with advanced autonomy built in. Vehicles are designed for dual-use applications, targeting dull, dirty and dangerous operations to reduce operational risk and lower costs.
