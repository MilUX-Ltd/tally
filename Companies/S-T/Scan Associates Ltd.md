# Scan Associates Ltd

> Defence-focused managed services consultancy supporting government procurement and programme delivery.

## What they do

Scan Associates provides managed services consultancy to the defence and government sectors, delivering solutions, tooling, and suitably qualified and experienced personnel (SQEP) to support programme outcomes. The firm offers end-to-end business winning services from initial strategy through to project execution, with particular expertise in navigating government procurement processes.
