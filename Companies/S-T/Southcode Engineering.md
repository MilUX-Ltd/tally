# Southcode Engineering
> Software engineering and systems integration specialist for defence applications.
## What they do
Southcode Engineering specialises in software engineering, rapid prototyping, and systems integration and interoperability. The firm works within the D3IP innovation community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park, supporting defence technology development.
