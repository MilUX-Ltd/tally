# Thistle Design (MMC) Ltd

> Scottish encoder manufacturer supplying mission-critical motion and control components to UK defence platforms across land, sea, and air domains.

## What they do

Thistle Precision designs, prototypes, and manufactures encoders for defence and critical industrial applications, with over three decades of supply to platforms including Challenger, Ajax, Warrior, Type 45 and Type 26 ships, Astute and Dreadnought submarines, and Hawk aircraft. The company also provides build-to-print and reverse engineering services.

## Products and solutions

- **Encoders** - Custom and standard encoders for mission-critical motion and position control on armoured vehicles, warships, submarines, and aircraft.
