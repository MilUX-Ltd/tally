# Sintela

> Bristol manufacturer of distributed fibre-optic sensing systems for security, perimeter and infrastructure monitoring.

## What they do

Sintela designs, manufactures and field-supports distributed fibre-optic sensing (DFOS) systems, with particular expertise in distributed acoustic sensing (DAS). Its ONYX systems turn existing fibre-optic cable into a long-range sensing platform that detects strain, temperature and acoustic changes for security, perimeter intrusion detection and infrastructure monitoring. Applications span pipelines, telecoms and power cables, railways, highways, mining, subsea cables, geophysical monitoring and border surveillance.

## Products and solutions

- **ONYX nano** - short-range DAS interrogator (1 to under 20 km) for small sites.
- **ONYX peta** - standard-range DAS interrogator (around 20 to 75 km per fibre) for medium sites.
- **ONYX peta EX** - long-range DAS interrogator (around 75 to 200 km per fibre) for large sites.
- **Operating System Software (OSS)** - core firmware with REST API, Modbus and SNMP integration.
- **Industry Application Software (IAS)** - bespoke application-specific user interfaces, with AI detection and classification.
