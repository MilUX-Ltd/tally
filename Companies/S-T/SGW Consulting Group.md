# SGW Consulting Group

> Independent physical security, counterterrorism and surveillance consultancy for the built environment, operating in the UK, Saudi Arabia and UAE.

## What they do

SGW has provided physical security, fire, counterterrorism design and CCTV consultancy since 2003, employing consultants qualified to PSP, CPP, CSyP and MSc standard. Their defence-relevant capabilities include communications, command and control consultancy, surveillance design, security systems training and simulation and modelling services.
