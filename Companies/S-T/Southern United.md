# Southern United

> Manufacturer of aerospace and military-specification labels, nameplates, and asset-tracking components.

## What they do

Southern United designs and manufactures labels and nameplates to aerospace and military specifications, holding ISO 9001:2015 and EN 9100:2018 certification. The company offers a full in-house capability across sulphuric anodising, chemical etching, inkjet printing, and laser engraving, with fully traceable materials and a qualified contract review team that can interpret technical drawings and military specifications.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace and military-spec labels and nameplates** - produced to ISO 9001 and EN 9100 standards with traceable materials and a range of anodised and etched finishes.
- **Barcoding, RFID, and asset tracking components** - compliant labelling and tagging solutions for defence asset management.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
