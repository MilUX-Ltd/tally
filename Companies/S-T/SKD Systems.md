# SKD Systems
> European defence-technology company, trading as STARK, building VTOL loitering munitions and mission-control software, with a UK manufacturing site in Swindon.

## What they do

SKD Systems, trading as STARK and operating as legal entity SKD SE, is a European defence-technology company specialising in unmanned systems. Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Munich, it launched the OWE-V Virtus electrically powered VTOL loitering munition and the Minerva mission-control suite in early 2025. It was reported in 2025 to have secured an approximately 300 million euro contract with the German Bundeswehr. In July 2025 it announced a 40,000 sq ft factory in Swindon as its UK manufacturing foothold.

## Products and solutions

- **OWE-V Virtus** - electrically powered VTOL loitering munition (around 120 km/h cruise, 250 km/h dive, 5 kg warhead, 60-minute endurance).
- **Minerva** - mission-control software suite.
