# SGS United Kingdom Ltd

> UK subsidiary of SGS, the global testing, inspection and certification company, serving the defence and aerospace supply chain.

## What they do

SGS United Kingdom provides testing, inspection and certification services to defence and aerospace contractors, supporting supply chain integrity, quality management and compliance with military standards including AS/EN 9100, 9110 and 9120. Services cover material testing (metals, coatings, polymers), welding inspection, calibration and management system certification to ISO 9001, 14001, 27001 and ISO/IEC 17025.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** UKAS accredited; AS/EN/JISQ 9100, 9110, 9120; ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 22301, ISO 27001, ISO 45001, ISO 50001, ISO/IEC 17025, ISO 13485; Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
