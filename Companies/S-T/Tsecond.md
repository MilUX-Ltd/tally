# Tsecond
> US-based edge AI technology company providing server units and the Block AI product for remote and austere environments, with defence applications.
## What they do
Tsecond provides edge AI server solutions designed for deployment in remote and austere locations. Their hardware products include the Block AI server units and integrated workstations. The technology enables AI processing at the edge without dependence on cloud connectivity, making it relevant to defence and other high-security use cases. Distribution in Europe is handled via channel partners.
## Products and solutions
- **Block AI** - Edge AI server unit for remote and austere environments; designed for tactical and field deployment.
