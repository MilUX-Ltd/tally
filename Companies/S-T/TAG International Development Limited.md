# TAG International Development Limited

> Defence and intelligence consultancy trading as Torchlight, enabling transformation of criminal justice, defence and intelligence agencies through capability design, integration and training.

## What they do

Trading as Torchlight Solutions, TAG International Development designs, integrates and delivers operationally proven procedures, training and equipment to help government and defence organisations address criminal, terrorist and security threats. Services span C2, counter-terrorism, intelligence, forensics, incident response, security consultancy and training delivery.
