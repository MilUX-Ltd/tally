# Sirius Insight

> British AI-powered maritime surveillance specialist providing autonomous detection and alerting to defence and security authorities.

## What they do

Sirius Insight delivers AI-powered maritime surveillance systems that autonomously detect suspicious or unusual activity at sea and raise timely alerts to authorities. The company focuses on maritime domain awareness, integrating sensor data with artificial intelligence to produce actionable intelligence for decision-makers operating in the marine environment.

## Products and solutions

- **Maritime surveillance platform** - AI-powered system for autonomous detection, alerting and response support in the maritime domain.
