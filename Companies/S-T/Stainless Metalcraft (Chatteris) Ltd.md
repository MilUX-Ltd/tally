# Stainless Metalcraft (Chatteris) Ltd

> Advanced manufacturing business producing precision engineered components for mission-critical defence applications.

## What they do

Metalcraft provides advanced manufacturing solutions for highly regulated sectors, with over 150 years of experience in prototype through to serial production. The company supports defence customers domestically and internationally as part of the Avingtrans group of high-integrity, mission-critical businesses.
