# Slingsby Advanced Composites Ltd

> Manufactures advanced composite structures and complex sub-systems for military and civil platforms across air, land and sea.

## What they do

Slingsby Advanced Composites produces advanced composite structures and sub-systems to meet demanding engineering requirements across defence and civil applications. The company holds AS9100 accreditation and supplies into a range of structural and functional requirements for multiple platform domains.
