# Thermoteknix Systems Ltd

> Cambridge-based developer and manufacturer of thermal imaging and night vision systems for aerospace, defence, security and law enforcement customers worldwide.

## What they do

Thermoteknix designs and manufactures thermal imaging and night vision products including thermal clip-on systems for fused night vision, thermal binoculars, night vision goggles and target location systems. The company also licenses its patented thermal imaging technology and sensor modules to international OEMs for integration into their own platforms and products.

## Products and solutions

- **Thermal Clip-On Systems** - Fused night vision thermal clip-ons for small arms and crew-served weapons.
- **Thermal Binoculars and Goggles** - Handheld and head-mounted thermal and night vision observation devices.
- **Target Location Systems** - Thermal-based target acquisition and location equipment.
- **OEM Thermal Modules** - Patented thermal imaging modules supplied to third-party manufacturers for integration.
