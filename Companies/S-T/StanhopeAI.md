# StanhopeAI
> Deep-tech autonomy software company building edge-deployable AI agents based on Active Inference and the Free Energy Principle.
## What they do
StanhopeAI is a spinout from University College London and King's College London developing autonomy software for systems that must operate in unfamiliar, contested or comms-denied environments without GPS, prior maps or remote operators. The approach uses the Active Inference paradigm rather than LLM-style models, producing smaller, edge-deployable agents that learn and adapt in real time. The company has publicly demonstrated full-size autonomous drone flight. Backed by Frontline Ventures, Paladin Capital Group, Auxxo, UCL Technology Fund and MMC Ventures.
