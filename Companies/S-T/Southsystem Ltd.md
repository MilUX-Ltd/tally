# Southsystem Ltd

> Berkshire electronic engineering and prototyping company serving defence and other sectors.

## What they do

Southsystem, registered as Southsystem Manufacturing Limited, provides outsourced R&D, electronic engineering and product prototyping. With over 35 years of experience, it covers microprocessor electronics, electrical engineering, embedded software and hardware, automation and CNC machining, and custom enclosures. It has worked with small-scale manufacturers across sectors including defence, medical and rail.

## Products and solutions

- **Electronic product development** - concept to market-ready prototypes.
- **Embedded hardware and software** - microprocessor and electrical engineering.
- **Enclosures and machining** - 3D printing, CNC machining and laser engraving.
