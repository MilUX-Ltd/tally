# Shock-WBV

> UK manufacturer of shock mitigation suspension seats designed to reduce wave-shock and whole-body vibration impact on personnel in high-speed marine craft.

## What they do

Shock-WBV designs, tests and manufactures seat suspension systems in the United Kingdom to protect operators and passengers from wave shock and whole-body vibration in military and commercial high-speed vessels. Products span large seat systems, small seat systems, RIB and jockey seat solutions, and seat insert systems. Traded under the Sea Sure Ltd company.

## Products and solutions

- **C Series** - shock mitigation suspension for large seats in patrol and military craft.
- **P Series** - shock mitigation suspension for smaller seats and crew positions.
- **RIB/Jockey Seat systems** - solutions for rigid inflatable boat operators.
- **OMEGA Seat Inserts** - retrofit shock mitigation inserts for existing seats.
- **LT Series** - lightweight shock mitigation seat range.
