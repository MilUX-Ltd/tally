# Satellite Vu

> UK operator of high-resolution thermal infrared Earth observation satellites, trading as SatVu.

## What they do

SatVu (Global Satellite Vu Ltd) captures high-resolution thermal infrared imagery from space, building a satellite constellation intended to provide high-frequency global revisit. Its first satellite, HotSat-1, launched in 2023 and HotSat-2 followed in 2026. The company serves national security, economic monitoring and climate resilience applications, providing thermal data on infrastructure and activity worldwide.

## Products and solutions

- **Thermal imagery** - high-resolution thermal infrared data captured from the HotSat constellation.
- **Tasking** - commissioning the satellites to capture specified targets and areas of interest.
- **Catalog** - access to previously captured, archived thermal imagery.
