# Safran Helicopter Engines UK Ltd

> European Maintenance Centre Hub for Safran Helicopter Engines, supporting civil and military turboshaft engine maintenance and repair in the UK.

## What they do

The UK facility in Fareham, Hampshire is the Safran Helicopter Engines group's European Maintenance Centre Hub, performing level 1 to 3 maintenance and repairs on Arriel, Arrius, Makila and RTM322 engines for both civil and military operators. Work is carried out on-site and at customer facilities.

## Products and solutions

- **Arriel / Arrius / Makila engine MRO** - maintenance and repair for civil helicopter turboshaft engines.
- **RTM322 engine MRO** - maintenance and repair for the military turboshaft used on platforms including Apache and Merlin.
