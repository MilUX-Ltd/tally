# Shield Reply

> Defence and security operating company within the Reply group, delivering digital solutions for UK defence, intelligence and security clients.

## What they do

Shield Reply is the defence and security arm of the Reply group. It delivers digital solutions across the UK defence, intelligence and security sector, working in agile teams on Oracle, NVIDIA and Microsoft cloud technologies. Shield Reply draws on Reply's wider network to tailor enterprise digital capability for defence and security programmes. It is part of the same Reply group as RED Scientific Reply Limited, though a separate operating company.
