# TICQ
> Bath-based defence consultancy helping non-traditional suppliers and SMEs become defence-ready.
## What they do
TICQ (Technology, Innovation, Culture, Quality) was founded in October 2025. It guides startups and non-traditional suppliers through defence readiness, shaping product, processes and support systems to meet defence-buyer standards, and navigating innovation funding routes and procurement channels including DASA.
