# Tods Technology

> Designer and manufacturer of acoustic composites, sonar domes, and acoustic coatings for the Royal Navy and navies worldwide, with over 60 years of experience in the field.

## What they do

Tods Technology is the Royal Navy's preferred supplier for sonar domes, acoustic windows, acoustic coatings, and complex composite structures for surface warships and submarines. With products on over 25 navies worldwide, the company provides design, manufacture and in-service through-life support for its acoustic composite solutions.

## Products and solutions

- **Sonar domes** - Bespoke acoustic composite domes for surface warship and submarine sensor systems.
- **Acoustic coatings** - Coatings and acoustic windows applied to naval vessels for signature management.
- **Complex composite structures** - Design, manufacture and in-service support of structural composite components for maritime platforms.
