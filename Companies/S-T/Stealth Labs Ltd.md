# Stealth Labs Ltd

> AI-first engineering firm building agentic and multi-agent systems for defence, intelligence and security applications.

## What they do

Stealth Labs designs and builds agentic AI workflows and multi-agent architectures using frameworks including LangGraph and LangChain, targeting processes that previously required significant manual effort. The firm operates across counter-terrorism, intelligence, defence consultancy, surveillance and software optimisation domains.
