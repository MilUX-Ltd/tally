# Segula Technologies Ltd

> Global multi-disciplinary engineering consultancy delivering engineering, production and maintenance services for aeronautics, space and defence.

## What they do

Segula Technologies provides end-to-end engineering services spanning aircraft design, propulsion systems, subassembly definition, and manufacturing across the aeronautics, space and defence sectors. Their teams operate across both design and manufacturing phases, supporting major OEMs and Tier 1 customers with engineering and production capability.
