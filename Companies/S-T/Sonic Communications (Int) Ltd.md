# Sonic Communications (Int) Ltd

> Specialist manufacturer of radio accessories, bodyworn video surveillance equipment, and vehicle technical installation systems for police, military, government, and industrial sectors.

## What they do

Sonic Communications designs and manufactures communications accessories and video surveillance equipment, with over 45 years of service to police, military, government, and industrial customers. The company describes itself as an accessory and integration specialist rather than a radio manufacturer: its products are designed to work with all major radio makes and models and, in its own words, are agnostic to the frequency, modulation, or encryption method used by the connected radio equipment. It also operates as an approved training provider for surveillance and covert equipment use, and maintains a facility in Dubai supporting overseas installation and product support.

## Products and solutions

- **Genesis headset range** - Low-profile, noise-cancelling headsets for helmet and PPE integration.
- **Patrol and Maverick headsets** - Tactical communications headsets for general duty and firearms/rapid-intervention use.
- **Torpedo and Ring PTT** - In-line and covert push-to-talk accessories.
- **Enhanced HIU 3** - Headset interface unit with integral microphone, volume and PTT control.
- **Micro Stealth Wireless Earpiece** - Covert, low-noise wireless earpiece with a custom-fit option.
- **S-TAG (Sonic Tactical Audio Gateway)** - Interconnects radios, satellite PTT terminals and push-to-talk-over-cellular apps with smartphones.
- **Special Vehicle Operations** - Covert and overt vehicle installation of technical communications and surveillance equipment.
- **Bodyworn and mobile video surveillance** - Surveillance systems for operational and covert use, including a partnership with Digital Barriers for live video analytics.
