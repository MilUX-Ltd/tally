# Sonic Communications (Int) Ltd

> Specialist manufacturer of radio accessories, bodyworn video surveillance equipment, and vehicle technical installation systems for police, military, government, and industrial sectors.

## What they do

Sonic Communications designs and manufactures communications accessories and surveillance equipment with over 45 years of service to police, military, government, and industrial customers. Their radio accessories are compatible with all major radio makes and models, covering both covert and overt applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Radio accessories** - Bodyworn and handheld accessories compatible with all major radio platforms.
- **Bodyworn and mobile video surveillance** - Surveillance systems for operational and covert use.
- **Vehicle technical equipment** - Covert and overt installation systems for mobile platforms.
