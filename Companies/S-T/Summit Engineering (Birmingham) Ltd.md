# Summit Engineering (Birmingham) Ltd

> Precision machining and assembly house serving aerospace, defence, and oil and gas from a Solihull facility.

## What they do

Summit Engineering has delivered precision-machined components and assemblies for over half a century, serving aerospace, defence, and oil and gas sectors. The company combines advanced CNC machining, assembly, and special processes with quality systems focused on traceable, right-first-time results.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision-machined components** - CNC milled and turned components to aerospace and defence tolerances.
- **Assembly and special processes** - Component assembly with in-house special processes and quality compliance.
