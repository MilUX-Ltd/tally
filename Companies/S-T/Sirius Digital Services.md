# Sirius Digital Services
> Software engineering and simulation specialist for the UK defence sector and adjacent regulated markets.
## What they do
Sirius Digital Services provides bespoke software engineering for UK defence, covering modelling and simulation, robotics and autonomous systems, synthetic environments, and AI and ML. The company supports MOD and Defence Digital and recruits across wargaming, simulation, autonomous systems, and digital twin disciplines. It is a subsidiary of Sirius Analysis Ltd, sharing a Portsmouth footprint and customer base.
## Products and solutions
- **Bespoke software engineering** - custom development for defence and regulated industries including nuclear, space and energy.
- **Synthetic environments** - simulation platforms and digital twin solutions for defence training and analysis.
