# TYTYN Technology (UK) Ltd

> Veteran-run IT hardware and systems design house delivering products and whole-systems solutions to the defence, security, commercial, and industrial sectors.

## What they do

TYTYN designs, builds, and delivers IT hardware and integrated systems solutions for defence, security, and industrial customers worldwide. Based at the Shell Store in Hereford, it focuses on bringing engineering integrity and professionalism to complex hardware and systems design challenges.
