# SS White Technologies UK Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of customised flexible rotary drive shafts, couplings, and components for aerospace and defence applications.

## What they do

SS White Technologies designs and manufactures flexible rotary drive shafts and associated couplings for aerospace, defence, automotive, marine, and food industries. The firm is accredited to AS9100 and NADCAP Heat Treating, and holds JOSCAR registration, serving customers who require engineered flexible drive solutions to aerospace quality standards.

## Products and solutions

- **Flexible rotary drive shafts** - Custom-engineered flexible drive shafts for aerospace, defence, and marine applications.
- **Couplings and components** - Associated mechanical couplings and components designed to aerospace standards.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, NADCAP Heat Treating, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
