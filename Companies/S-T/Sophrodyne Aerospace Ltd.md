# Sophrodyne Aerospace Ltd

> Aeronautical and computer-science consultancy specialising in aerodynamic modelling and flight simulation across the full Mach range.

## What they do

Sophrodyne Aerospace provides aerodynamics and flight mechanics consultancy, building models and simulations for drones, UAVs, and full-size aircraft from subsonic to hypersonic regimes. The company has a particular specialisation in rotorcraft modelling.
