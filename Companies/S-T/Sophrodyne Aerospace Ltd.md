# Sophrodyne Aerospace Ltd

> Aeronautical and computer-science consultancy specialising in aerodynamic modelling and flight simulation across the full Mach range.

## What they do

Sophrodyne Aerospace provides aerodynamics and flight mechanics consultancy, building models and simulations for drones, UAVs, and full-size aircraft from subsonic to hypersonic regimes, with a particular specialisation in rotorcraft aerodynamics, performance, interactional effects, flight dynamics, vibration and noise. The company states it also specialises in machine learning and AI, computer vision and human-machine interface work within its computer-science practice. It works on behalf of platform developers and regulators rather than designing its own aircraft or drone platforms, including published work with the UK Civil Aviation Authority on eVTOL and helicopter downwash and outwash safety.

## Products and solutions

- **Vorticity Transport Model (VTM)** - A high-resolution computational model for predicting rotorcraft aerodynamics, including rotor and wing aerodynamic interference.
- **Innovative Aerospace Solutions** - Design validation, model-building and bespoke numerical tools for aerospace clients.
- **Expert Witness Services** - Technical input to legal proceedings involving aerospace matters.
- **Investor Due Diligence** - Technical assessment of aerospace investment targets.
