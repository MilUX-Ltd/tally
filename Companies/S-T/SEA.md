# SEA

> Independent systems engineering and software company delivering intelligent systems for the defence industry.

## What they do

SEA develops advanced systems for naval ship and fleet protection and undersea surveillance, deployed across navies globally. Their capabilities span ship and fleet protection, undersea systems, torpedo launcher systems, and communications systems, with in-service solutions supporting an ever-growing number of naval customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Ancilia** - naval ship protection system.
- **Torpedo Launcher Systems** - design and manufacture of torpedo launching equipment.
- **KraitSense** - undersea surveillance system.
- **Communications Systems** - defence-grade communications solutions.
- **Maritime Support and Services** - in-service support for deployed naval systems.
