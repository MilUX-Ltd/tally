# Telecetera Ltd

> Defence and security consultancy providing systems integration, C2, cyber security, space consultancy, software and logistics services.

## What they do

Telecetera offers management consultancy and professional services across defence, security and space, covering systems integration, command and control, cyber security, data analysis, software development and logistics and asset lifecycle management. The company is JOSCAR registered and holds Cyber Essentials Plus.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
