# Shield Space Ltd

> UK space security company developing autonomous guidance and rendezvous technologies for small satellites, supporting defence space control and in-orbit servicing missions.

## What they do

Shield Space builds an onboard autonomy system that enables spacecraft to detect, track and interact with other objects in orbit without continuous ground control. The technology targets defence space control missions as well as commercial applications in in-orbit servicing, inspection and manufacturing. Founded in 2025 and based in Lincolnshire.
