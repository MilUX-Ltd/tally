# SIG SAUER, Inc.

> Manufacturer of firearms, weapon systems, ammunition and electro-optics for military and law enforcement.

## What they do

SIG SAUER, Inc. is a US-based manufacturer of firearms, electro-optics, ammunition, suppressors, airguns, remote weapon stations and training. Headquartered in Newington, New Hampshire, with over 3,200 employees across twelve locations in three states, it blends American, German and Swiss engineering heritage. The US company forms part of the wider SIG SAUER group under Germany's L&O Holding, which also owns the German SIG SAUER GmbH & Co. KG.

## Products and solutions

- **XM7 (M7) rifle** - 6.8mm rifle selected for the US Army Next Generation Squad Weapon programme.
- **XM250 automatic rifle** - 6.8mm automatic rifle under the same NGSW programme.
- **XM8 carbine** - shorter NGSW carbine variant of the M7 rifle.
- **6.8 Common Cartridge ammunition** - family of ammunition supplied alongside the NGSW weapons.
- **Electro-optics and suppressors** - sighting systems and sound suppressors for military and law enforcement use.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Programmes:** prime contractor on the US Army Next Generation Squad Weapon (NGSW) programme.
