# STG Aerospace Ltd

> Aerospace manufacturer specialising in innovative lighting technologies and photoluminescent safety systems for aircraft.

## What they do

STG Aerospace, established in 1995, designs and manufactures lighting and safety systems for OEMs, airlines, and MROs. Products are installed across more than 9,000 aircraft, representing approximately one third of the world's passenger fleet, and are designed to meet the latest aviation safety standards and regulations.

## Products and solutions

- **saf-Tglo** - Photoluminescent floor path marking system for aircraft emergency egress.
