# System Label Technology Ltd

> Specialist manufacturer of durable labelling solutions for aerospace, defence, automotive, medical devices and heavy equipment.

## What they do

System Label Technology supplies decals, labels, overlays, membranes, metal plates and foam gaskets to Tier 1 and Tier 2 manufacturers across aerospace and defence. As a UL-approved converter for 3M, Avery and Flexcon, the company operates to ISO 9001, ISO 13485 and ISO 14001, and holds Everglow illuminated supply approval.

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 13485, ISO 14001
