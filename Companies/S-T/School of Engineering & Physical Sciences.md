# School of Engineering & Physical Sciences

> Engineering and physical sciences school at Heriot-Watt University offering contract research, consultancy, and licensing to the aerospace and defence sectors.

## What they do

The School of Engineering and Physical Sciences at Heriot-Watt University undertakes contract and collaborative research, consultancy, and technology licensing for industry partners. It has a sustained track record of engagement with aerospace and defence, including strategic alliances with AWE, BAE Systems, and Selex ES.
