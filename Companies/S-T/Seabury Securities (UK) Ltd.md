# Seabury Securities (UK) Ltd

> Independent investment banking and strategic advisory firm specialising in aerospace, defence and space.

## What they do

Seabury Securities advises aerospace and defence OEMs, Tier 1 suppliers, SMEs and financial investors on sell-side and buy-side M&A, strategic reviews, equity and debt financing, acquisition target mapping, and due diligence. The team combines backgrounds in financial services, private equity, corporate strategy, and technology and engineering within the A&D sector.
