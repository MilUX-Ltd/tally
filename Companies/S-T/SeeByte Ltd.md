# SeeByte Ltd

> Autonomy software for robotic and autonomous naval systems, with a focus on mine countermeasures and vehicle interoperability.

## What they do

SeeByte develops autonomy solutions for robotic and autonomous systems operating in environments where communication bandwidth and positional information are limited or intermittent. Their software suite has been deployed in naval applications globally for over 20 years, supporting navies in mine countermeasures and multi-asset fleet management.

## Products and solutions

- **MCM Toolbox** - integrated, qualified system delivering scalable mine countermeasures capability.
- **Vehicle Interoperability** - software enabling navies to manage assets across an entire fleet through a single integrated system.
- **SeeTrack Neptune** - maritime autonomy and command and control software.
- **ATR System** - automatic target recognition for underwater applications.
