# Smiths Detection

> Global leader in threat detection and screening technologies for aviation, ports and borders, defence, and urban security.

## What they do

Smiths Detection is a division of Smiths Group plc, a London Stock Exchange-listed global technology company. The division specialises in threat detection and screening technologies with over 40 years of operational experience, serving aviation, ports and borders, defence, and urban security customers worldwide.

## Products and solutions

- **Explosive and bomb detection** - Detection and disposal screening systems.
- **Passenger and cargo screening** - X-ray, metal detection, and passenger screening systems.
- **Chemical, biological, radiological and nuclear detection** - CBRN analysis and identification systems.
- **Contraband detection** - Scanning and monitoring systems for ports and border security.
