# Smiths Advanced Metals

> International multi-metal stockholder and supplier of metals and plastics into aerospace, defence, and other high-technology industries.

## What they do

Smiths Advanced Metals stocks and supplies a wide range of metals and plastics to aerospace, defence, oil and gas, and high-technology customers worldwide. The company offers machining services including grinding, sawing, and ultrasonic processing, alongside supply of alloys such as aluminium, titanium, nickel, and exotic alloys.

## Products and solutions

- **Metals stockholding** - Supply of aluminium alloys, titanium alloys, nickel alloys, steel alloys (inc Inconel), copper, beryllium, magnesium alloys, tungsten, and zinc alloys.
- **Plastics supply** - Specialist plastics for aerospace and defence applications.
- **Machining services** - Grinding, sawing, and ultrasonic machining capabilities.
