# Takumi Precision Engineering

> Precision manufacturer of low-to-medium volume aerospace components, supplying wing, fuselage, engine and landing gear parts to major aerospace OEMs.

## What they do

Established in 1998, Takumi operates a 22,000 sq ft facility in Raheen Business Park, Limerick, including a Class 7 clean room and white assembly area. The company specialises in precision components for the aerospace sector, with a focus on wing, fuselage, winglet, landing gear, cockpit and engine parts.
