# Spherea UK

> UK arm of the Spherea group, supplying automatic test equipment and in-service support for military, aerospace and space applications.

## What they do

Spherea Test & Services Ltd designs, manufactures, supplies, services, repairs and integrates electronic test equipment and diagnostic solutions, with related software and product development, across the full life cycle of critical applications for military, aerospace, space and other civil markets. It positions itself as a recognised partner to the UK Ministry of Defence, leading primes and international customers, citing more than 50 years of experience delivering test solutions to the UK defence sector. It is the UK entity of the wider Spherea group, headquartered in France.

## Products and solutions

- **ATEC Series 7** - generic-purpose avionic MRO test benches used by airlines, OEMs and MROs to verify conformity of critical avionic electronic equipment.
- **ATEC Dyna Series** - modular, scalable production automatic test equipment for high-throughput avionics manufacturing with parallel multi-unit testing.
- **ATEC ATMOS** - compact, open automatic test bench for electronic equipment with a reduced signal count, covering avionics and mechatronic and optronic applications.
- **U-Test** - software and simulation platform combining models from different environments with real equipment to create a digital twin.
