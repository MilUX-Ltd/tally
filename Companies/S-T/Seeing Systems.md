# Seeing Systems

> UK developer of smart, affordable unmanned aerial systems combining AI and robotics for real-world defence applications.

## What they do

Seeing Systems develops unmanned aerial systems at the intersection of AI and robotics, drawing on over ten years of experience. The product range spans agile FPV drones through to advanced UAVs built to perform in operational environments. The company is listed as a Manufacturing Organisation in the ADS Group directory and is registered in Westminster, London.

## Products and solutions

- **FPV drones** - agile first-person-view drone platforms for defence and security applications.
- **Advanced UAVs** - unmanned aerial vehicles designed for real-world operational deployment.
