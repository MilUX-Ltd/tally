# Surevine Limited

> Builder of accredited secure collaboration environments for UK Central Government and high-security organisations.

## What they do

Surevine designs, builds, and supports secure real-time collaboration platforms for organisations with demanding security and information-sharing requirements, including UK Central Government. Their systems are accredited to handle sensitive information and are used for cyber threat response, situational awareness, and cross-organisation coordination.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001:2022, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
