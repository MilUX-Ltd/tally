# SVGC Limited

> Independent systems and digital engineering consultancy serving defence, government, and security markets for over 27 years.

## What they do

SVGC provides acquisition support, capability and decision management, data science, and AI-enabled consultancy to senior decision makers across government and industry. The firm combines systems engineering discipline with digital and data analytics capabilities to help clients manage complex programmes from concept through to in-service deployment.
