# SeSys Ltd

> Manufacturer of networked vision and surveillance camera systems for defence, security and public safety applications.

## What they do

SeSys develops and manufactures networked camera systems, including the SeSys Torch Camera — a portable, standalone unit deployable within minutes and capable of transmitting to control rooms, vehicles, PCs and mobile devices. The system supports WiFi connectivity for wireless video download without interrupting recording, and is listed under ADS for components, equipment and weapons manufacturing categories.

## Products and solutions

- **SeSys Torch Camera** - Portable networked surveillance camera with WiFi connectivity, internal storage and rapid deployment capability.
