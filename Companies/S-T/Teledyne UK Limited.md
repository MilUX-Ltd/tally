# Teledyne UK Limited

> UK subsidiary of Teledyne Technologies, delivering electronics and complex sub-systems for defence, aerospace, and adjacent markets.

## What they do

Teledyne UK operates across nine UK centres of excellence employing over 1,500 people, providing non-ITAR capabilities to UK and European customers. Capability areas include radiofrequency and microwave solutions, imaging detectors and focal planes, composite structures, interconnects, energetics, and modems.
