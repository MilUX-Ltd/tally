> Crypto-agile network security company developing high-performance encryption and cybersecurity solutions.

## What they do

Sitehop builds next-generation high-performance, crypto-agile security technology for networks. The company focuses on enabling secure communications as networks evolve and cyber threats develop, with a product philosophy of making security an enabler rather than a constraint on operational performance.
