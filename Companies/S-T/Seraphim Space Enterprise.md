# Seraphim Space Enterprise

> The non-fund operating arm of the Seraphim Group, the leading SpaceTech investor, providing research, intelligence, and investment readiness programmes for the space sector.

## What they do

Seraphim Space Enterprise is the operational entity of the Seraphim Group, which manages two primary funds: the Seraphim Venture Fund for early-stage space investments and the Seraphim Space Investment Trust for growth-stage investments. The enterprise arm delivers research, intelligence, and investment readiness and growth programmes alongside the fund activity. The broader portfolio spans over 150 companies that have raised more than USD 10 billion across 32 countries.
