# Surrey Satellite Technology Limited (SSTL)

> Pioneer of small satellite design, building affordable space missions for commercial, government, and defence customers worldwide.

## What they do

SSTL designs, builds, and operates small satellites, drawing on decades of expertise to deliver cost-effective space missions for Earth observation, communications, and science. The company has a track record of disrupting conventional satellite design with pragmatic, affordable solutions for a global customer base.
