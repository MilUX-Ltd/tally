# Synchronous Science Ltd

> R&D consultancy specialising in cyber and electromagnetic activities, radar, communications systems and novel sensing techniques.

## What they do

Synchronous Science Ltd conducts research and development consultancy focused on cyber and electromagnetic activities (CEMA), including communications systems, radar detection and mitigation, novel sensing techniques, and optical surface scatter from coatings. The company undertakes both traditional long-timescale R&D and short-term applied work using COTS hardware and literature reviews, and draws on input from those with operational experience.
