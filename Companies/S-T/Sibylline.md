# Sibylline

> Strategic intelligence and risk advisory firm providing geopolitical, security and risk analysis to government, corporate and defence clients.

## What they do

Sibylline is a strategic intelligence and advisory firm founded in 2010, with offices in London, New York and Singapore. The firm provides geopolitical, security and risk analysis to over 250 organisations including FTSE 100, Fortune 500 and government clients. Core services include bespoke intelligence reporting, embedded analyst deployments, security operations centre builds (in-house and virtual), social media monitoring, due diligence and security consulting. Sibylline received the King's Award for Enterprise in International Trade in 2024 and is ranked by Chambers and Partners for political risk.

## Products and solutions

- **World Risk Register** - global geopolitical and security risk database and reporting product.
- **Embedded analyst service** - dedicated intelligence analysts deployed within client organisations.
- **GSOC capability** - security operations centre builds and virtual GSOC services for government and corporate clients.
- **Threat intelligence** - cyber threat and physical security intelligence reporting.
