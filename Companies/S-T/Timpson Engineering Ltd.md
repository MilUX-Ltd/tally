# Timpson Engineering Ltd

> Precision engineering and multi-axis CNC machining for aerospace, defence and other high-precision sectors.

## What they do

Timpson Engineering is a precision engineering and CNC machining specialist based in Oxfordshire, offering 5-axis CNC machining and complex multi-sided geometries. It serves aerospace, defence, medical, marine, motorsport and drone customers, from prototypes to medium-volume production, with in-house engraving, anodising and electroplating access.

## Products and solutions

- **5-axis CNC machining** - complex precision components for aerospace and defence applications.
- **Prototype and medium-volume production** - runs from 50 to 1000 units.
