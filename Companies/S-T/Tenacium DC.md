# Tenacium DC
> UK defence and government data company providing secure AI consulting, a proprietary situational awareness platform and applied data engineering.
## What they do
Tenacium DC helps defence and government organisations build secure data and AI foundations. Services span consulting on secure data and AI architecture, the JEDAI Situational Awareness Platform, and Innovation Labs applied engineering capability. Senior leadership includes former Royal Navy and Deloitte experience.
## Products and solutions
- **JEDAI Situational Awareness Platform** - proprietary platform for situational awareness in defence and government.
