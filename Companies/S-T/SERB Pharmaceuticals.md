# SERB Pharmaceuticals

> Global specialty pharmaceutical company supplying antidotes, medical countermeasures, and acute care medicines for CBRN preparedness, rare emergency medicine, and rare diseases.

## What they do

SERB has operated for over 30 years as a supplier of essential medicines for complex and life-threatening conditions, with a particular focus on CBRN (chemical, biological, radiological, and nuclear) preparedness. The company supplies antidotes and medical countermeasures to clinicians, healthcare systems, and governments, and operates as a fully integrated pharmaceutical manufacturer.

## Products and solutions

- **CBRN medical countermeasures** - Antidotes and treatments for chemical, biological, radiological, and nuclear exposure, supplied to governments and defence medical services.
- **Acute care medicines** - Essential medicines for rare emergency medicine and rare disease indications.
