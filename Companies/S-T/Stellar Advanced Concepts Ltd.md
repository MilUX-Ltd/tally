# Stellar Advanced Concepts Ltd

> Multi-disciplinary engineering consultancy specialising in shape-adaptive technology, morphing structures and bio-inspired mechanisms for aerospace and UXV platforms.

## What they do

Stellar AC applies academic research in adaptive structures and flow control to improve the performance of aircraft and unmanned vehicle platforms, covering drag and noise reduction surfaces, bio-inspired appendages, and landing and recovery devices. The firm integrates sensor systems, control algorithms and AI to enable shape-changing structural applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Morphing structures** - High-performance adaptive structural solutions for aerospace and UXV platforms.
- **Soft robotics and bio-inspired mechanisms** - Flexible mechanisms with integrated sensing and control for platform applications.
