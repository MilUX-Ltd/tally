# Terra Global Solutions Ltd

> International market access consultancy for SMEs supplying complex technology into defence, aerospace, and critical infrastructure markets.

## What they do

Terra Global Solutions helps technology SMEs expand into international markets through a global network of distributors and resellers, with a physical presence across APAC, EMEA, and North America. The firm specialises in complex technology products and brings local language and cultural knowledge to market entry in the defence and aerospace sectors.
