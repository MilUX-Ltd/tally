# The Nacelle Group - Resonate Testing Ltd

> Aerospace systems consultancy and ISO 17025-accredited test facility specialising in Engine Build Up integration, certification support, and structural testing.

## What they do

The Nacelle Group comprises Nacelle Systems Consultancy (NSC) and Resonate Testing. NSC specialises in systems design and integration, stress analysis, and technical validation including FAR 25/CS25 airworthiness certification support. Resonate Testing provides an in-house ISO 17025 accredited test facility that integrates directly with NSC's qualification and certification work.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems and EBU Integration** - Engine Build Up (EBU) systems design, integration, and airworthiness certification support to FAR 25/CS25.
- **Structural and Environmental Testing** - ISO 17025 accredited test facility covering fire protection, environmental, and calibration/test-rig services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; ISO/IEC 17025
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
