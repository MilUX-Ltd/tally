# Saronic Technologies UK Ltd

> Defence technology company designing and manufacturing autonomous surface vessels for maritime operations.

## What they do

Saronic Technologies designs and manufactures Autonomous Surface Vessels (ASVs) at scale for defence and commercial maritime customers. The company focuses on extending operational reach, enhancing survivability, and increasing effectiveness for the UK, the United States, and allied nations through autonomous maritime systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Autonomous Surface Vessels (ASVs)** - unmanned surface vessels for defence maritime operations.
