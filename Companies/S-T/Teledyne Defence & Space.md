# Teledyne Defence & Space

> UK manufacturer of RF and microwave components, sub-systems and systems for defence, space, electronic warfare, radar and communications.

## What they do

Teledyne Defence & Space (TDS) designs, develops and manufactures complex integrated RF and microwave solutions from its site in Shipley, West Yorkshire. It is a business unit of Teledyne Microwave Solutions and part of the US-listed Teledyne group. TDS supports defence, space, security, electronic warfare, radar and communications customers, and describes itself as a supplier of ITAR-free and ITAR-compliant microwave products with over 40 years of heritage, tracing its origins to Filtronic Components Ltd.

## Products and solutions

- **Radar Warning Receivers (RWR)** - threat-warning receiver systems.
- **ELINT and ESM receivers** - electronic intelligence and electronic support measures receivers, including the Phobos radar ESM system offering 360 degree passive detection.
- **Multi-function assemblies** - DIFM, DLVA, channelisers, converters, synthesisers and switch multiplexers.
- **Filters and components** - bandpass, suspended substrate stripline, tunable, combline filters and multiplexers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** describes products as ITAR-free and ITAR-compliant. Holder of the Queen's Award for Enterprise 2019 (International Trade) and 2020 (Innovation).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
