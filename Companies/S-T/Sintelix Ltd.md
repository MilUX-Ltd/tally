# Sintelix Ltd

> Intelligence analysis platform specialising in multilingual text analytics and data visualisation for defence and security applications.

## What they do

Sintelix provides an intelligence analysis platform with capabilities in multilingual data extraction, data amalgamation, and a range of visualisation formats. The platform is designed to improve the efficiency of intelligence operations through automated workflows and high-accuracy text analytics, and has been applied to large-scale data analysis projects across defence and security contexts.

## Products and solutions

- **Sintelix platform** - Text analytics and intelligence analysis platform with multilingual extraction, data fusion and visualisation.
