# Terrafix Ltd

> West Midlands manufacturer of components, platform systems, and weapons-related products for the defence sector.

## What they do

Terrafix manufactures components and platform systems, with ADS-listed capability covering weapons, polymers and plastics including Teflon-based materials. The company supplies into defence procurement channels as an ADS member manufacturer.
