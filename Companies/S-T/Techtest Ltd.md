# Techtest Ltd

> Manufacturer of emergency locator transmitters, personal locator beacons and search and rescue homing systems for civil and military applications, trading as part of the H.R. Smith Group.

## What they do

Techtest manufactures internationally certified Emergency Locator Transmitters (ELTs), Personal Locator Beacons (PLBs) and Automatically Deployable Emergency Locator Transmitters (ADELTs) compatible with the COSPAS-SARSAT rescue system, including GPS-equipped variants. The company also produces search and rescue homing and decoding systems for locating personnel in distress, and test equipment for aircraft antenna systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Emergency Locator Transmitters (ELTs and ADELTs)** - internationally certified, GPS-equipped beacons for COSPAS-SARSAT.
- **Personal Locator Beacons (PLBs)** - civil and military personal distress beacons.
- **SAR homing and decoding systems** - equipment for locating personnel in distress for civil and military SAR operations.
- **Aircraft antenna test equipment** - specialist test sets for aircraft antenna systems.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN 9100, AS/EN 9110, AS/EN 9120, ISO 9001, Boeing D1-4426, Cyber Essentials Plus, EASA 145, FAA 14 CFR Part 145, Part 21G, Part 21J, TCCA 145
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
