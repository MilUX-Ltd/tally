# Syensqo

> Advanced materials company supplying specialty polymers, composites and chemical solutions to the aerospace and defence sectors.

## What they do

Syensqo develops and manufactures specialty polymers, composite materials, specialty formulations, and bio-circular chemistries for industries including aerospace, defence, automotive, oil and gas, and electronics. The company employs around 13,000 people across 30 countries, with UK manufacturing and development facilities in Wrexham focused on advanced material technologies for aerospace and defence.
