# SMC Group (South Midlands Communications Ltd)

> Manufactures specialist masts, antennas, and RF communications equipment for defence, government, and emergency services.

## What they do

Established in 1958, SMC Group designs and manufactures specialist masts, antennas, and RF communications equipment built to perform in tactical and commercial environments. The company serves defence, government, emergency services, meteorological, and broadcasting customers across more than 65 years of operation.

## Products and solutions

- **Specialist masts** - Tactical and commercial mast systems for communications deployment.
- **Antenna systems** - RF antennas for defence, government, and critical operations customers.
- **RF communications equipment** - Specialist radio frequency equipment for demanding operational environments.
