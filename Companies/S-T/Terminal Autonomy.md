# Terminal Autonomy

> Designer and mass-producer of low-cost AI-guided long-range strike and loitering munitions, combat-deployed in Ukraine; founded by British and Australian military veterans and a Ukrainian engineer.

> Foreign-HQ. Headquartered and manufacturing in Kyiv, Ukraine. Founded by British and Australian military veterans, so it carries a UK founder link, but no UK-registered entity or UK office was confirmed from public sources. Formerly named One Way Aerospace; this is a different company from Flyby Technology (see Sources).

## What they do

Terminal Autonomy builds combat-tested autonomous one-way attack drones and loitering munitions with proprietary AI navigation and targeting optimised for GPS-denied and comms-denied environments, produced at scale for frontline use. The company markets ITAR-free systems and operates a production facility in Kyiv. It was founded in 2022 as One Way Aerospace and rebranded to Terminal Autonomy in late 2023.

## Products and solutions

- **AQ-400 Scythe** - long-range one-way attack drone and loitering munition with a substantial warhead payload and extended range, designed for precision strike at distance.
- **AQ-100 Bayonet** - compact loitering munition for GPS-denied and high-jamming environments, with AI terminal guidance and swarming.
- **AQV-120 Scalpel** - modular unmanned system for precision attack and surveillance.
