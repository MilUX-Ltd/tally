# Sigma Precision Components Limited

> Aerospace precision manufacturer producing pipe assemblies, ducting, fabrications, machined components and composite structures for OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers.

## What they do

Sigma Precision Components manufactures rigid pipe assemblies, sheet metal fabrications, machined items, specialist fasteners and build fixtures for aerospace customers, with facilities in the United Kingdom and China. The company also conducts R&D into lightweight composite solutions, including COMPipe composite pipes, lightweight end fittings and composite drive shafts delivering significant weight savings for airframe and aero-engine applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Rigid pipe assemblies and ducting** - precision pipe systems for airframe and engine applications.
- **COMPipe composite pipes** - composite pipe solution delivering over 50% weight saving versus metal equivalents.
- **Composite drive shafts** - lightweight drive shafts for aero-engine and airframe use.
- **Machined components and fasteners** - precision machined items and specialist fasteners for aerospace OEMs.
- **Build fixtures** - tooling and fixtures supporting aircraft assembly programmes.
