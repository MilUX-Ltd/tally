# Thales S.A.

> French aerospace, defence and security prime and the European leader in defence electronics, with a large UK arm.

## What they do

Thales is a French multinational specialising in advanced technologies for the defence, aerospace, and cyber and digital sectors, and is the European leader in defence electronics. Headquartered in the La Defense business district of Paris, it is listed on Euronext Paris under ticker HO. Thales UK has operated in Britain for over 140 years, supporting customers across defence, cyber security, digital identity and space, with its registered office at Green Park, Reading, and major manufacturing at Belfast, Glasgow, Crawley and Templecombe.

## Products and solutions

- **Missiles and air defence** - the Lightweight Multirole Missile (LMM) and Starstreak, designed and manufactured at Belfast; mounted and short-range air defence systems.
- **Naval systems** - sonar, mine countermeasures and unmanned maritime systems for the Royal Navy.
- **Communications and networks** - secure tactical and strategic military communications.
- **Cyber and digital identity** - cyber security, secure data and digital identity solutions.
- **Optronics and sensors** - radars, electro-optic sensors and surveillance systems.
