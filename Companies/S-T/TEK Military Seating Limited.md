# TEK Military Seating Limited

> UK designer and manufacturer of military vehicle seating, including blast attenuation seats and ProTEK® modular crew seating systems for legacy and new-build platforms.

## What they do

TEK Military Seating designs and manufactures OEM vehicle seating for military applications, covering driver, commander, gunner, turret, loader and crew positions across a range of suspension, pedestal, sledge, runner, riser and plinth configurations. The company's blast attenuation seating is adjustable and reusable, and the product range extends to blast floor matting and universal vehicle stowage.

## Products and solutions

- **ProTEK® military vehicle seating** - modular crew seating system covering most military vehicle types, both legacy and new build.
- **Blast attenuation seating** - adjustable and reusable blast-protective seats for armoured vehicle occupants.
- **Blast floor matting and vehicle stowage** - complementary protection and storage solutions for platform interiors.
