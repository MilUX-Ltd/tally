# Speedlink International Logistics

> Northern Ireland-based freight and logistics operator providing air, road, and sea freight services with specialist airfreight security screening capability.

## What they do

Speedlink International Logistics has provided international freight services since 1990, offering air, sea, and road freight alongside dangerous goods repacking, out-of-gauge transport, and global partner services through the WCA network. The company holds specialist capability in FREDDS (Free Running Explosive Detection Dogs) primary screening for airfreight cargo, which supports defence and security supply chains.

## Products and solutions

- **Airfreight and seafreight** - full-service international freight forwarding.
- **FREDDS primary screening** - explosive detection dog screening for airfreight cargo.
- **Dangerous goods repacking** - DG handling and repackaging services.
- **Out-of-gauge transport** - OOG cargo logistics.
