# T-SPARK Engineering
> Ukrainian electronics engineering company specialising in embedded systems, radar, RF and FPGA design for research institutions and OEMs, including NATO-aligned programmes.
## What they do
T-SPARK Engineering (trading as TechSpark) provides hardware and software engineering services covering embedded systems, IoT, DSP, SDR, FPGA, PCB design, radar and RF for startups, research institutions and OEMs. Customers include Sagentia (UK) and NATO's Science for Peace programme. The mix of radar, RF and defence-adjacent research places the firm at the dual-use edge of commercial electronics services and defence-relevant hardware development.
## Products and solutions
- **Engineering services** - embedded systems, FPGA, SDR, radar and RF design delivered as an outsourced engineering arm for OEM and research clients.
