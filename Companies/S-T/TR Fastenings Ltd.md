# TR Fastenings Ltd

> International specialist in the design, engineering, manufacture and distribution of high-quality industrial fastenings and Category C components to global assembly industries including defence.

## What they do

TR Fastenings is part of Trifast plc, listed on the London Stock Exchange since 1994. The company supplies fastenings and components to customers in around 75 countries across light vehicle, heavy vehicle, energy, infrastructure, general industrial and defence sectors.
