# SAIF Autonomy Ltd

> Software company building a control assurance layer for autonomous systems and physical AI, applied initially to aerospace and land platforms.

## What they do

SAIF Autonomy develops software that enables assured and trustworthy deployment of deterministic autonomy and AI-based control methods in safety-critical applications, with no requirement for data or communications links. The company targets autonomous systems operating in aerospace and land domains.

## Products and solutions

- **Control assurance software** - assured deployment of autonomous and AI control systems for safety-critical environments.
