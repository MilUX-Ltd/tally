# SAIF Autonomy Ltd

> Software company building a control assurance layer for autonomous systems and physical AI, applied initially to aerospace and land platforms.

## What they do

SAIF Autonomy develops software that enables assured and trustworthy deployment of deterministic autonomy and AI-based control methods in safety-critical applications, with no requirement for data or communications links. The company targets autonomous systems operating in aerospace and land domains, with deployments demonstrated on both drones and ground vehicles.

## Products and solutions

- **NEXUS** - modular, configurable Runtime Assurance software that continuously monitors system behaviour at the edge and prevents unsafe actions before they occur, for deterministic and AI-based control methods without requiring data or communications links.
