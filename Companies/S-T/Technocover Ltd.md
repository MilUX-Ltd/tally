# Technocover Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of physical security access solutions for Critical National Infrastructure protection, including LPCB-certified doorsets, covers, enclosures and modular buildings.

## What they do

Technocover designs, manufactures, installs and maintains physical security products for CNI sites across all industrial sectors. Its product range covers doorsets, covers, cabinets, kiosks, walk-in modular buildings, mesh cages, partitions, louvres, window bar sets and the UltraSecure Sentinel fire door range.

## Products and solutions

- **LPCB-certified security doorsets and covers** - physical access control products certified to Loss Prevention Certification Board standards.
- **UltraSecure Sentinel fire door range** - fire-rated secure doorsets for CNI applications.
- **Walk-in modular buildings and enclosures** - configurable secure buildings and kiosks for site protection.
