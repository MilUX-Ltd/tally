# Solid Solutions Management Ltd

> Leading UK and Ireland reseller of SOLIDWORKS CAD engineering software, supplying design tools, training and technical support to aerospace and defence customers.

## What they do

Solid Solutions accounts for approximately 80% of SOLIDWORKS CAD software sales in the UK and Ireland, serving more than 15,000 customers across engineering sectors including aerospace and defence. The company provides software sales and consultancy, training services and 3D printing solutions, backed by a SOLIDWORKS-certified support team.
