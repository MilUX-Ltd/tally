# Senior Plc

> International FTSE 250 aerospace and industrial manufacturer producing fluid conveyance systems, aerostructures, and engineered components for defence, commercial air transport, and land vehicle sectors.

## What they do

Senior plc operates two divisions: Aerospace (65% of revenues), which supplies fluid conveyance systems and aerostructures for commercial air transport, regional, bizjet, rotorcraft, and defence platforms; and Flexonics (35% of revenues), which supplies engineered products for land vehicles and industrial process control. The group operates from 31 business units across 14 countries.
