# Source Protective Solutions Ltd

> Electronic warfare, surveillance, and counter-drone solutions for military, law enforcement, and critical national infrastructure customers.

## What they do

Source Protective Solutions provides fixed, mobile, and aviation electronic warfare systems covering signals analysis and jamming, alongside surveillance and counter-UAS capabilities. The company states it promotes, delivers, integrates, trains on, and maintains systems sourced from specialist manufacturer partners rather than manufacturing hardware itself. It serves military forces, police agencies, and critical national infrastructure protection customers internationally, and offers capability development and training in signals analysis, jamming, and counter-drone operations alongside its equipment supply.

## Products and solutions

- **Broadband Jammer and Directional Jammer** - Fixed and directional radio-frequency jamming systems.
- **FAST SKY PROTECTION** - Portable, modular anti-UAV system.
- **Spectrum Analyzer** - Spectrum monitoring and analysis system, with an anti-drone variant.
- **Direction Finding System** - Radio direction-finding equipment for signals location.
- **Tactical Communications ISR/Jamming Platform** - Vehicle-based VHF/UHF/SHF system for combined signals collection and jamming.
