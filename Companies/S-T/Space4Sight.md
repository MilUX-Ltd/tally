# Space4Sight
> UK space sector advisory firm covering civil, military and dual-use activities across the full mission stack.
## What they do
Space4Sight advises governments, defence organisations and industry on space strategy, operational resilience and international partnerships. The practice spans command, control, ground segment, launch, orbit, operations, payload, recovery and security, with particular emphasis on dual-use platforms serving commercial and national security users. Leadership combines 37 years of Royal Air Force aerospace experience, including service as the principal UK officer at the White House and Pentagon, with commercial space delivery.
