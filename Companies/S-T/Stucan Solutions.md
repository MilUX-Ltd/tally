# Stucan Solutions
> Specialist defence and security consultancy serving government, law enforcement and special forces in counter-terrorism, targeting and training.
## What they do
Stucan Solutions provides counter-terrorism operations support, targeting and interdiction expertise, risk mitigation, counter-insurgency assistance and real-time training for agencies operating in targeting, interdiction and mitigation roles. The company also runs the Global Partnership Program, which supports UK and allied businesses entering the US federal and commercial market with new technologies. Founded in 2011 by Stuart Taylor, who had a 25-year British Army career including service in Commando Forces.
