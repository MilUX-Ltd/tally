# Siemens PLC

> UK subsidiary of Siemens AG, supplying industrial automation, digitalisation, energy and infrastructure technology to defence and government customers across the United Kingdom.

## What they do

Siemens PLC operates across energy, smart infrastructure, digital industries and mobility in the United Kingdom, employing thousands of people at sites including Manchester, Chippenham and Poole. The company provides technology and services to defence and government customers spanning power and energy systems, rail and transport infrastructure, industrial control systems, and product lifecycle management software used in defence platforms and programmes.
