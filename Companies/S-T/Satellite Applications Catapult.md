# Satellite Applications Catapult

> UK not-for-profit space technology innovation centre, part of the Catapult Network sponsored by Innovate UK.

## What they do

The Satellite Applications Catapult is an independent, not-for-profit innovation and technology company that helps businesses of all sizes realise the potential of space. It works with businesses, researchers, investors, government and end-users to develop satellite-based products, services and applications, taking ideas from concept to market with the aim of growing the UK's share of the global space market. It is a private company limited by guarantee, established in 2013 as one of the first Catapult centres and government-backed via Innovate UK.

## Products and solutions

- **Autonomous and Connected Earth** - work on safe adoption of autonomous systems and integration of satellite with terrestrial networks, including PNT, connectivity and resilient communications.
- **Beyond Earth** - the in-space economy and In-orbit Servicing, Assembly and Manufacturing (ISAM), supported by a robotics test facility at Westcott.
- **Resilient Earth** - Earth observation and geospatial work for environment, emissions and resilient communities.
- **Defence and security programme** - dedicated programme convening the Defence in Space community across MilSatcom, space ISR, space domain awareness, PNT and space security and cyber.
