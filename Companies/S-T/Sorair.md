# Sorair

> Buckinghamshire drone automation company for autonomous surveillance and BVLOS operations.

## What they do

Sorair is a drone automation company based at Westcott Venture Park, Aylesbury. Its hardware-agnostic Soranet platform automates drone operations for security and surveillance, with beyond visual line of sight (BVLOS) capability and AI and sensor integration. It also runs a flight school for BVLOS training and drone operations management.

## Products and solutions

- **Soranet** - hardware-agnostic drone automation platform.
- **Pilot** - automation engine for autonomous flight.
- **Sorair Flight School** - BVLOS training and operations management.
