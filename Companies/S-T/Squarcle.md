# Squarcle
> UK defence consultancy specialising in data-driven supply chain modernisation and digital transformation for MOD and prime contractors.
## What they do
Squarcle supports MOD and prime contractor clients across logistics, sustainment and support transformation, including delivery of digital control towers for joint force deployment. Founded in 2020 by a former Royal Logistic Corps officer and a former Big Four consultant, it was named the fourth fastest growing professional services firm in the 2024 UK Fast Growth Index. Board expertise includes authorship of the Defence Support Transformation Programme and the Defence Support Operating Model. The wider client base extends to FTSE 100 corporates and central government.
