# Silvus Technologies

> Developer of high-speed mobile ad-hoc network (MANET) tactical radios and mesh networking technology.

## What they do

Silvus Technologies develops software-defined MANET radio systems for secure data, video and voice communications without fixed infrastructure. Its proprietary MN-MIMO waveform creates a self-forming, adaptive mesh network able to link hundreds of nodes. Customers span military, law enforcement, public safety, autonomous systems manufacturers and commercial users worldwide. Silvus is a Motorola Solutions company following the 2025 acquisition.

## Products and solutions

- **StreamCaster MANET radios** - family of tactical mesh radios providing high-fidelity video, voice and IP data, used on the ground, in the air and at sea.
- **StreamCaster MINI 5200 (SM5200)** - compact 182-gram 2x2 MIMO MANET radio with up to two watts output power and up to 100 Mbps data throughput.
- **StreamCaster NEXUS** - chest-mounted next-generation tactical networking system for dismounted military operators, built around the SM5200 radio.
- **MN-MIMO waveform** - proprietary waveform underpinning the self-forming mesh network across the product range.
