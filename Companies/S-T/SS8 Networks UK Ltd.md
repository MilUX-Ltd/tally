# SS8 Networks UK Ltd

> UK entity of US lawful intelligence company SS8, providing AI-powered communications intelligence and digital evidence platforms to law enforcement and government.

## What they do

SS8 delivers the Intellego XT platform, which unifies communications data, location data, digital evidence, and open-source intelligence into a single investigative environment for law enforcement and intelligence agencies. The platform applies AI to surface connections between people, places, and events, and includes voice analytics, facial recognition, geofencing, and automated reporting.

## Products and solutions

- **Intellego XT** - AI-powered investigative platform unifying communications intelligence, location data, digital evidence, and OSINT.
- **Discovery** - Complementary product extending the investigative capability of the Intellego XT environment.
