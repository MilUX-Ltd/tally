# Tiberius Aerospace

> Defence technology company developing the Sceptre extended-range precision-guided 155mm ramjet artillery munition, with the UK MoD as launch customer.

## What they do

Tiberius Aerospace develops next-generation precision weapons, missile systems, autonomous platforms and AI-enabled defence capabilities for the UK, US and allied nations, applying commercial technology to defence problems. Its lead programme is Sceptre, a 155mm ramjet artillery round designed for extended range and high precision. The company emerged from stealth at Future Artillery in May 2025. It operates a UK registered company (Tiberius Aerospace Limited) parented to the US-incorporated Tiberius Aerospace, Inc., with the UK MoD as the named launch customer; tagged owned-foreign on that basis.

## Products and solutions

- **Sceptre (TRBM 155HG)** - extended-range precision-guided 155mm ramjet artillery munition. Publicly stated to reach speeds around Mach 3.5, altitudes above 65,000 feet and ranges up to 150km depending on payload, compatible with NATO-standard 155mm systems. Uses a liquid-fuelled multi-fuel ramjet (diesel, JP-4, JP-8) and is stated to deliver a circular error probability under 5m, including in GPS-contested conditions.
