# Scanna MSc Ltd

> British manufacturer of high-performance x-ray screening and detection equipment for security and defence applications.

## What they do

Scanna MSc designs and manufactures x-ray screening and detection systems used at high-threat security locations and in field-based inspection tasks. Products are used by the UK MOD, Home Office, Police and Prison services, and by military and government authorities in over 100 countries, covering applications including suspect package screening, weapons intelligence, post-blast forensics, customs vehicle checks, and TSCM work.

## Products and solutions

- **X-ray screening systems** - Portable and fixed x-ray equipment for baggage, postal, and field inspection tasks.
- **Field-based x-ray inspection** - Systems designed for military and law enforcement use in high-threat environments.
