# Strategic Proposals Ltd

> Bid and proposal consultancy serving defence, aerospace, maritime, and space sectors with a focus on improving client win rates.

## What they do

Strategic Proposals provides specialist bid and proposal consultancy, working with clients across defence, aerospace, maritime, security, and space. The firm has supported clients in nearly 40 countries and holds ADS-listed capabilities across defence, maritime and shipbuilding, security, and space consultancy services.
