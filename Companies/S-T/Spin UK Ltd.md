# Spin UK Ltd

> Worcester-based engineering company providing bespoke test facilities, data acquisition systems, and condition monitoring solutions for aerospace and defence applications.

## What they do

Spin UK Ltd, established in 1999, designs and manufactures bespoke component test benches, production engine hot test facilities, and data acquisition systems. The company offers mechanical, electrical, and software design capabilities and acts as a systems integrator, serving automotive, industrial, aerospace, and defence customers from concept through to delivery.

## Products and solutions

- **Engine hot test facilities** - bespoke production test rigs for engines and powerplants.
- **Component test benches** - custom-built test equipment for aerospace and defence components.
- **Data acquisition systems** - instrumentation and data capture solutions.
- **Plant condition monitoring** - industrial monitoring and diagnostics systems.
