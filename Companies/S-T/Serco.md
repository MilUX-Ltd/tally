# Serco Group plc

> Defence and government support services provider, listed on the London Stock Exchange.

## What they do

Serco is a British multinational services company providing support across defence, space, justice, migration, health, customer services and transport. Its defence business operates and maintains military bases, provides marine and fleet support, training and mission-support services for the UK Ministry of Defence and international customers. Serco generated around £1.89 billion of defence revenue in 2024, roughly 35 per cent of group revenue. It is a constituent of the FTSE 250.

## Products and solutions

- **Base and estate support** - operation and maintenance of RAF and Royal Navy sites including Brize Norton, Northolt, Portsmouth, Devonport and Clyde.
- **Marine services** - in-service support for Royal Navy and British Army marine fleets.
- **Training and mission support** - synthetic warfare training and operational support for defence customers in the UK and internationally.
