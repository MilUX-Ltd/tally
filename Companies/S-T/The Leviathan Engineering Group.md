# The Leviathan Engineering Group

> Integrated British sub-contract engineering group serving aerospace and defence customers across mechanical manufacturing, propulsion components, and precision machining.

## What they do

The Leviathan Engineering Group provides turnkey mechanical projects, component production, reverse engineering, repair and maintenance, and design services to premium customers in highly regulated sectors. It operates through subsidiaries sharing services to address larger, more technical projects, with particular depth in aerospace and defence supply chain work.

## Products and solutions

- **Component Production** - Mechanical components and subcomponents including pipes, hoses, springs and fans for aerospace and defence platforms.
- **Propulsion Components** - Engine and engine component manufacturing including gearbox elements and rotors/propellers.
- **CNC Machining** - 5-axis, lathe, milling, and router capabilities, with a wide range of finishing and specialist machining processes.
- **Turnkey Mechanical Projects** - End-to-end project delivery from design through production and repair.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered
