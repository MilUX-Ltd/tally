# SEPAR International Ltd

> Security and risk consultancy providing protective security, hostile environment training, logistics support, and operational advisory services globally.

## What they do

Founded in 2013 in Kabul, SEPAR International provides security and risk services to media outlets, humanitarian organisations, NGOs, and government clients operating in complex or high-threat environments. Services span security consultancy, risk assessments, hostile environment training, transport security, and logistics and asset management.

## Products and solutions

- **Security consultancy** - Risk assessments, security surveys, and protective security planning.
- **Hostile environment training** - Training programmes covering covert operations, counter-terror, defensive driving, medical, and tactical skills.
- **Transport security** - Convoy and asset protection services for high-risk environments.
- **Logistics support** - Distribution and asset management in complex operating environments.
