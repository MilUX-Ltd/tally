# Theta Technologies Ltd

> Exeter-based specialist in nonlinear acoustic non-destructive testing (NDT) of high-technology and safety-critical components for aerospace and defence manufacturing.

## What they do

Theta Technologies develops and supplies non-destructive test equipment and testing services using nonlinear acoustic methods to detect subsurface cracks and closed defects that conventional NDT often misses. Its systems are designed for safety-critical component manufacturing where material type, surface finish or geometry limits the effectiveness of standard techniques.

## Products and solutions

- **Nonlinear Acoustic NDT Equipment** - Test systems for detecting subsurface and closed defects in high-technology components.
- **NDT Testing Services** - Contract testing for aerospace and defence manufacturers requiring sensitivity beyond conventional methods.
