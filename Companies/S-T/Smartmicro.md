# smartmicro

> Designer and manufacturer of automotive, traffic and defence radar sensors.

## What they do

smartmicro designs, develops and manufactures radar solutions for traffic management, automotive and airborne applications, as well as defence. The company was founded in 1997 as a spin-off from the Technical University of Braunschweig, where it is headquartered, combining development, testing and production on one campus. Its defence offering draws on automotive and traffic radar experience and includes long-range radar and airborne applications, with all development carried out in Germany. smartmicro has subsidiaries in the United Kingdom and the United States.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence radar** - radar-based detection and tracking of small, low-flying and fast-moving objects for drone detection and infrastructure protection.
- **Automotive radar sensors** - high-performance radar sensors for automotive applications.
- **Traffic management radar** - radar sensors for traffic management and intelligent transport systems.
- **Engineering services** - radar engineering services for automotive radar sensors.
