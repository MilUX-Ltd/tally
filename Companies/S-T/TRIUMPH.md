# TRIUMPH
> US-listed aerospace tier-one supplier manufacturing aircraft components, actuation and control systems, with UK operations at Deeside (Wales), Cheltenham and the Isle of Man.
## What they do
Triumph Group (NYSE: TGI) designs and manufactures aircraft components, systems and accessories for commercial and military customers worldwide. The UK arm at Deeside, Wales covers hydraulic and electric actuation, control systems, in-house machining and PCB assembly, and HALT/HASS testing. Additional UK sites at Cheltenham and the Isle of Man. Serves both civil and military airframes.
