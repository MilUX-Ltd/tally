# Sentinels Network Ltd

> Defence and security procurement integrator providing equipment, technology, services, logistics, and operational expertise through a global supplier network.

## What they do

Sentinels (SNTLS) acts as a single point of access for complex defence and security procurement, drawing on a global network of trusted suppliers, OEMs, and specialist partners. The company covers equipment, technology, services, logistics, and operational expertise, with a structured approach designed to simplify acquisition for government and defence clients.
