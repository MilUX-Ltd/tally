# Sesanti Ltd

> Manufacturer of specialist long-range camera systems for intelligence gathering, force protection and covert surveillance.

## What they do

Sesanti designs and manufactures high-performance electro-optical and infrared camera systems capable of identification at ranges from 1 km to 30 km, covering visible, near-IR, SWIR, MWIR and LWIR wavebands. Their products are used for tactical long-range surveillance, border and coastal protection, rapid-deployment counter-terrorism and covert low-power buried installations.

## Products and solutions

- **Long-range surveillance cameras** - Ultra-low-light identification systems for overt tactical and border/coastal protection roles.
- **Covert portable systems** - Low-power systems designed for buried deployment in harsh conditions, with extended mission duration.
