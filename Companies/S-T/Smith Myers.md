# Smith Myers

> Designs and manufactures advanced radio systems including software-defined radio, covert communications, and search and rescue location systems.

## What they do

Smith Myers produces advanced radio systems for defence, search and rescue, and surveillance applications, covering protocol stacks, software-defined radio, linear RF power amplifiers, and worldwide cellular systems. The company also develops covert communication systems and search and rescue location systems for rotary, fixed-wing, manned, and unmanned aircraft.

## Products and solutions

- **Software Defined Radio** - Flexible radio architectures for military and civil communications.
- **Covert communication systems** - Specialist systems for covert and counter-terror operations.
- **Search and Rescue location systems** - SAR capability for manned and unmanned rotary and fixed-wing platforms.
- **Linear RF Power Amplifiers** - High-performance amplifiers for communications and surveillance.
- **Counter-UAV systems** - Detection and identification capability against unmanned aerial threats.
