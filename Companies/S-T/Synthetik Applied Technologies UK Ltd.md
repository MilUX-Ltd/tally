# Synthetik Applied Technologies UK Ltd

> R&D company specialising in AI, modelling and simulation, and physical protection, with particular expertise in energetic materials simulation.

## What they do

Synthetik develops advanced modelling and simulation software for energetic materials, underpinned by test-validated research. The company also delivers physical protection products including blast protection structures and systems, and applies AI and simulation to risk mitigation across defence and security domains.

## Products and solutions

- **Energetic materials simulation** - modelling and simulation code for energetic materials, described as the most advanced of its kind.
- **Blast protection systems** - protective structures, blast-resistant building components, and vehicle-mounted shelters.
- **Command and control platforms** - whole-platform C2 systems.
- **Security screening systems** - passenger and cargo screening, sensors and monitoring.
