# Super-Sharp Space Systems Ltd

> Developer of unfolding space telescopes for high-resolution thermal infrared Earth imaging.

## What they do

SuperSharp designs and manufactures compact satellite telescopes capable of capturing thermal infrared (TIR) imagery at four times greater resolution per unit cost than conventional solutions. Their technology allows large-aperture optics to fit within small satellite platforms, making persistent, affordable Earth observation accessible for defence, environmental, and agricultural applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Unfolding Space Telescope** - Deployable TIR telescope fitted to small satellites for high-resolution Earth observation at reduced cost.
