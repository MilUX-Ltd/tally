# Spaceflux Ltd

## What they do
## What they do


Founded in 2022, Spaceflux addresses space congestion by deploying a proprietary optical sensor network combined with an AI analytics platform to track and characterise space objects. Their customers include satellite operators, governments, and the insurance industry.
