# TKMS ATLAS UK Limited

> UK subsidiary of thyssenkrupp Marine Systems, supplying maritime defence systems including submarine and surface ship sensors, unmanned systems, and port security solutions to the Royal Navy and international navies.

## What they do

TKMS ATLAS UK is a defence technology business of approximately 280 staff based in Dorset, supplying the UK MoD, Royal Navy and international defence customers with underwater and surface maritime systems. The company has heritage in acoustic and underwater defence technology and operates as a centre of excellence for maritime security, unmanned systems, and through-life platform support.

## Products and solutions

- **Sonar and underwater systems** - Submarine and surface ship sensor systems with long heritage in underwater acoustic technology.
- **Unmanned maritime systems** - Unmanned platforms and systems for maritime applications.
- **Port and maritime security** - Physical and electronic security solutions for ports and maritime infrastructure.
