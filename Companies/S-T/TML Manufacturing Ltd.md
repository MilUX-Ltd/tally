# TML Manufacturing Ltd

> Sheet metal and CNC precision manufacturer supplying aerospace and defence platform components from two purpose-built facilities in Dorset.

## What they do

TML Manufacturing provides sheet metal fabrication, CNC machining, laser cutting, painting, and blasting services to defence and aerospace customers. With around 50 multi-skilled employees across two facilities, the company handles prototype, development, and production volume orders and holds ITAR approval, JOSCAR registration, NADCAP, ISO 9001, and ISO 14001 accreditations.

## Products and solutions

- **Sheet metal fabrication** - Cutting, bending, forming and assembly of aerospace and defence components.
- **CNC machining** - Milling, turning, laser cutting and plasma cutting to precision tolerances.
- **Surface finishing** - Painting, powder coating, blasting and NADCAP-approved coating processes.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ITAR, NADCAP, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
