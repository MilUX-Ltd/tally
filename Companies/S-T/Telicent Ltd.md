# Telicent Ltd

> UK technology company building open-source data platform products for secure enterprise and defence environments.

## What they do

Telicent develops CORE, an open-source platform designed to unify disconnected and classified data in a secure, standards-compliant architecture. The platform is intended to reduce the time it takes defence and enterprise innovators to deliver new capability, while maintaining security, compliance, and data integrity.

## Products and solutions

- **CORE** - Open-source data platform for aggregating dark and disconnected data in a secure, attribute-based access control environment.
