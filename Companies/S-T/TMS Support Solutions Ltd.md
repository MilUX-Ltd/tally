# TMS Support Solutions Ltd

> Through-life product support and integrated logistic support engineering consultancy serving the UK MoD and defence primes since 1991.

## What they do

TMS Support Solutions has delivered Integrated Logistic Support (ILS) engineering, reliability engineering, safety and environmental engineering, technical authoring, training, and software development to the UK MoD and prime contractors for over 30 years. The company designs and delivers tailored through-life support capabilities, balancing the support solutions envelope against whole-life cost.

## Products and solutions

- **ILS engineering** - Integrated Logistic Support planning and delivery across the equipment acquisition cycle.
- **Reliability engineering** - Reliability, availability, and maintainability analysis for defence platforms and systems.
- **Technical documentation and training** - Technical authoring, multimedia production, and technical training services.
