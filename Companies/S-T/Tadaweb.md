# Tadaweb

> Luxembourg company building a publicly available information (PAI) and open-source intelligence (OSINT) platform for defence, national security and public safety.

## What they do

Tadaweb is a Luxembourg-headquartered software company that builds a PAI and OSINT platform used by defence, national security, public safety, cyber threat intelligence and corporate security organisations. The platform ingests publicly available and commercially available information and integrates with third-party web tools and APIs, combining technology with human analysis under a stated emphasis on transparency and ethics. Its "Small Data" approach is designed to augment analysts rather than replace them, keeping the human in control rather than relying on a black box.

## Products and solutions

- **PAI and OSINT platform** - core SaaS platform that ingests publicly and commercially available information and integrates with third-party web tools and APIs.
- **Small Data approach** - combines precise data points to reduce time to insight while keeping the analyst in control.
- **Low/no-code visual query engine** - visual query capability cited by investors as a differentiator.
- **Maritime Domain Awareness** - capability for national security customers announced for launch in 2026.
