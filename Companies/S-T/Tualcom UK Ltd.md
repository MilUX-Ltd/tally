# Tualcom UK Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of RF navigation and communication systems, including GNSS anti-jamming, data links, and electronic warfare products to military standards.

## What they do

Tualcom designs and manufactures RF navigation and communication systems for defence, law enforcement, and civil customers. Product families cover GNSS anti-jamming, telemetry and data acquisition, data links, and electronic warfare equipment, all designed to military standards with size and weight optimisation.

## Products and solutions

- **GNSS Anti-Jamming Systems** - Military-grade anti-jamming receivers and controlled reception pattern antenna systems.
- **Telemetry and Data Acquisition** - Systems for capturing and transmitting operational data in defence applications.
- **Data Links** - Secure tactical data link products for platform-to-platform and platform-to-ground communications.
- **Electronic Warfare Systems** - EW equipment designed to military standards for defence customers.
