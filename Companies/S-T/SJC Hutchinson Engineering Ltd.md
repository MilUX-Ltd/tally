# SJC Hutchinson Engineering Ltd

> Northern Ireland precision fabrication and sub-assembly manufacturer serving military and defence vehicle programmes.

## What they do

Hutchinson Engineering designs and manufactures high-integrity fabrications and sub-assemblies for military and defence vehicle applications. The company operates in Northern Ireland and supplies into global machinery and defence programmes, with capabilities spanning fabrication, structural assembly and precision component production.
