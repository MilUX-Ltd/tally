# Spectrum Control Ltd

> Great Yarmouth-based manufacturer of high-reliability electronic components, optoelectronics, modules, and hybrids for defence, aerospace, space, and energy applications.

## What they do

Spectrum Control Ltd produces AS9100 and ISO 9001 certified, ESA-approved electronic components and hybrid assemblies rated for high-reliability, high-temperature environments. Products serve defence, aerospace, space, industrial, and energy sectors, and the company operates under the API Technologies group.
