# Trimax Technologies Ltd

> Specialist defence and security equipment provider delivering advanced solutions and bespoke training to defence and security partners worldwide.

## What they do

Trimax Technologies designs and supplies specialist equipment and training for defence and security customers globally, with particular focus on counter-terrorism, bomb disposal, police and tactical operations. The company holds ITAR and JOSCAR registrations and operates an in-house manufacturing capability including CNC machining, additive manufacturing and laser cutting.

## Products and solutions

- **Counter-terrorism equipment** - Specialist hardware for bomb disposal, covert and counter-terror operations.
- **Tactical training** - Bespoke training programmes for police, military and security personnel.
- **Unmanned systems** - Equipment and support for unmanned security applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** JOSCAR, Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 9001, ITAR Approval, UKAS
