# Squarcle Consulting Ltd

> Digitally native defence supply chain consultancy specialising in data science, digital innovation, and through-life support decision analysis.

## What they do

Squarcle designs and executes supply chain strategies for defence and government clients, combining data science with operational consulting to improve supply chain efficiency and support through-life decisions. The firm holds JOSCAR registration, Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 27001 and operates across defence, maritime, and space domains.

## Products and solutions

- **Supply chain optimisation** - Strategy design and execution to improve operational performance and supply chain efficiency.
- **Through-life support decision analysis** - Data-driven analysis supporting long-term asset support and sustainment decisions.
- **Simulation and modelling** - Modelling services applied to defence and maritime contexts.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 27001, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
