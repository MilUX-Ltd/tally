# Tapeswitch Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of bespoke safety systems for Ground Support Equipment and MRO operations in the aviation industry.

## What they do

Tapeswitch supplies advanced safety solutions for aviation ground support, including sensing edges, safety mats, bumpers and custom guarding systems for scissor lifts, maintenance platforms and other GSE machinery. The company focuses on protecting personnel, equipment and assets in Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Sensing edges** - Safety sensing perimeter products for GSE.
- **Safety mats and bumpers** - Personnel and equipment protection for maintenance platforms.
- **Custom guarding systems** - Bespoke safety solutions engineered to specific GSE requirements.
