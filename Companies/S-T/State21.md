# State21
> Security and resilience management company delivering training, consultancy and incident management services to defence and government clients.
## What they do
State21 delivers training, specialist consultancy and incident response services across security, emergency planning and business continuity. Training programmes cover command and control of major incidents, security exercises, investigation and interviewing techniques, and emergency response media training. Consultancy services span incident management and programme support for security operations. ADS member established in 2015, based in London.
