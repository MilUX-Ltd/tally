# Sayari

> US supply-chain and commercial-risk intelligence provider serving government, defence and national security customers.

## What they do

Sayari is a counterparty and supply chain risk intelligence provider used by government agencies, multinational corporations and financial institutions. Its network analysis platform surfaces hidden risk through integrated corporate ownership, supply chain, trade transaction and risk intelligence data drawn from primary sources across more than 250 jurisdictions. It serves government, defence, border security, law enforcement and national security customers with use cases including counter-proliferation, sanctions and export-control enforcement, forced-labour detection and supply-chain security.

## Products and solutions

- **Sayari Graph** - corporate and trade network platform for entity mapping, relationship discovery and ownership intelligence, built on primary-source records.
- **Sayari Map** - supply chain mapping and bulk risk screening covering forced labour, sanctions and ESG exposure.
- **Sayari Signal** - risk screening data platform consolidating sanctions, export control and regulatory screening, including owned-uk exposure.
- **Sayari API** - programmatic access to the risk intelligence data.
