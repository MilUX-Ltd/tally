# SII Services Limited

> UK operation of SII Group, an IT and engineering services company delivering high-value technology solutions to defence and aerospace customers from its base at Cody Technology Park, Farnborough.

## What they do

SII Services provides IT and engineering services across full project lifecycles, from design through to in-service support. The UK entity operates from Cody Technology Park in Farnborough, one of the United Kingdom's principal defence and technology campuses, and draws on SII Group's broader presence across France, Germany, Spain, the Americas, Africa and Asia.
