# Salus Cyber

> UK cyber security consultancy serving government, defence, and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Salus Cyber delivers technical, strategic, and GRC cyber security consultancy to UK Government, defence, and CNI clients. Services cover penetration testing, security consultancy, data forensics, and training, underpinned by NCSC CHECK Green Light status, CREST membership, and NCSC Approved Service Provider standing.

## Products and solutions

- **Penetration Testing** - NCSC CHECK Green Light and CREST-approved testing services.
- **Cyber Security Consultancy** - technical, strategic, and GRC advisory for defence and government.
- **Digital Forensics** - data and computer forensics services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 27001:2022, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR Registered, NCSC CHECK Green Light, CREST Member, NCSC Approved Service Provider
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
