# Snubnose Ltd

> Automated compliance evidence collection platform built for the UK defence supply chain, targeting DefStan 05-138 and Defence Cyber Certification requirements.

## What they do

Snubnose provides an automated platform that connects to suppliers' existing tooling (Microsoft 365, Entra ID, Intune, vulnerability scanners, firewalls and SIEM platforms) to collect, organise and verify security evidence across all 144 DefStan 05-138 controls. As DefStan 05-138 Issue 4 and Defence Cyber Certification move from self-assessment to independently audited compliance, the platform removes the manual evidence-gathering burden for defence supply chain companies.

## Products and solutions

- **Snubnose Platform** - Automated evidence collection and verification across 144 DefStan 05-138 controls, integrating with Microsoft 365 and security tooling.
