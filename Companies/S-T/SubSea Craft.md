# SubSea Craft

> British advanced maritime-technology company designing, testing and manufacturing high-speed surface and sub-surface and uncrewed platforms for special-forces and naval defence customers.

## What they do

SubSea Craft is a UK specialist maritime-technology manufacturer that designs, tests and builds advanced maritime platforms at pace, working with military partners to equip operators with the latest technology. Its flagship is VICTA, a dual-domain craft that transits at high speed on the surface then submerges to covertly insert and recover divers and payloads. It operates an integrated design, prototype, test and build facility at The Camber in Old Portsmouth.

## Products and solutions

- **VICTA** - flagship diver delivery unit. An optionally-manned carbon-fibre craft that runs at 40-plus knots on the surface then floods ballast tanks to submerge for covert insertion and recovery of divers and payloads in contested environments. Can be helm, umbilical or autonomously operated.
- **MARS (Maritime Autonomy Reconnaissance System)** - a compact, modular, multi-role uncrewed surface vessel for offensive operations, ISR and logistics; low-signature and mission-configurable.
- **CADDIS** - a marinised uncrewed aerial vehicle launchable underwater from a submerged VICTA or by a diver, transitioning to flight to deliver ISR and targeting information.
