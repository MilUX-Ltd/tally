# Shield AI UK Ltd

> UK entity of Shield AI, a US defence technology company building AI-powered autonomy software for military aircraft and autonomous aerial systems.

## What they do

Shield AI develops AI pilot software and autonomous systems designed to operate in contested and GPS-denied environments without reliance on communications links. Its Hivemind software platform enables aircraft and unmanned systems to fly autonomous missions, and has been deployed on a range of US Department of Defense platforms.
