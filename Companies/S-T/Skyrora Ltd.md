# Skyrora Ltd

> New Space launch vehicle manufacturer providing dedicated, responsive satellite launch services.

## What they do

Skyrora designs and manufactures satellite launch vehicles capable of delivering payloads of up to 315 kg to Polar and Sun-Synchronous orbits. The company positions itself as an all-in-one launch service provider for responsive and versatile small satellite deployment.
