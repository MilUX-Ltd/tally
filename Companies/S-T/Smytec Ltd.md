# Smytec Ltd

> Forensic science technology company developing non-contact, non-destructive evidence recovery systems for law enforcement, defence agencies and security services.

## What they do

Smytec designs and develops forensic technology that transforms physical and chemical evidence retrieval processes. Its systems are aimed at law enforcement, forensic service providers, defence agencies and security services, replacing contact-based methods with innovative non-destructive techniques that preserve evidential integrity and improve recovery rates.
