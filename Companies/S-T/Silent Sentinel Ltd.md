# Silent Sentinel Ltd

> UK designer and manufacturer of long-range surveillance platforms incorporating thermal, SWIR and ultra-low-light camera technologies for military, border and critical infrastructure security applications.

## What they do

Silent Sentinel develops and manufactures surveillance platforms for homeland security and military use, built around long-range cooled and uncooled thermal cameras, SWIR cameras, ultra-low-light cameras and illumination systems. Products are UK-manufactured and engineered for harsh operating environments, with applications spanning transport hubs, maritime security, border and perimeter security, and military surveillance.

## Products and solutions

- **Long-range thermal surveillance platforms** - cooled and uncooled thermal camera systems for fixed and mobile deployment.
- **SWIR camera systems** - short-wave infrared surveillance solutions for day and night imaging.
- **Ultra-low-light platforms** - camera systems for low-illumination environments.
- **Integrated illumination systems** - active illumination to extend surveillance range and capability.
