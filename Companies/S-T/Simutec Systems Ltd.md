# Simutec Systems Ltd

> Technical services company providing simulation, modelling and communications solutions to the defence, aerospace and energy sectors.

## What they do

Founded in 2000, Simutec Systems delivers modelling and simulation solutions for experimentation, training, mission rehearsal and operational support across the air, land and maritime domains. The company also supplies hardware and software intercom, radio and data communications systems, and undertakes systems integration linking real equipment including sensors, communications and electronic warfare assets into synthetic environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Distributed simulation** - DIS and HLA-based networked simulation for multi-domain synthetic environments.
- **Synthetic environment integration** - Linking real equipment (sensors, EW, communications) into simulation architectures.
- **Intercom and radio systems** - Hardware and software communications solutions for training and operational use.
