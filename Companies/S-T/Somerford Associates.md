# Somerford Associates

> Security-cleared consultancy building bespoke platforms for data protection, digital transformation, and real-time insights across the secure public sector and defence.

## What they do

Somerford Associates delivers security-cleared consultancy and platform integration services, with specialisms in cyber security, data analysis, computer and data forensics, and software optimisation. The company serves defence departments and secure public-sector organisations, building and integrating bespoke solutions for data protection and digital transformation.
