# Spirit AeroSystems

> Belfast-based aerostructures manufacturer providing design, manufacture, assembly, and aftermarket support for complex composite and metallic structures for major aerospace OEMs.

## What they do

Spirit AeroSystems Belfast is a premium aerostructures partner specialising in the design, test, manufacture, and assembly of wings, fuselages, and engine nacelles in composite and metallic materials. Customers include Airbus, Boeing, Bombardier, Rolls-Royce, Pratt and Whitney, and Collins Aerospace.

## Products and solutions

- **Composite aerostructures** - wings, fuselages, and nacelles in advanced composite materials.
- **Metallic aerostructures** - precision metallic structural components and assemblies.
- **Aftermarket support** - maintenance, repair, and overhaul services for aerostructures.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100, AS9110, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, NADCAP (multiple scopes including composites, welding, NDT, heat treatment), EASA 145, FAA 14CFR Pt145, Pt 21G, Pt 21J, TCCA 145, CAAC MOC
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
