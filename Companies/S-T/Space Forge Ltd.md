# Space Forge Ltd

## What they do
## What they do


Space Forge develops a returnable and refurbishable satellite platform that exploits the conditions of space to manufacture materials not achievable on Earth. Their return technology reduces the cost of re-entry and minimises space debris, with downstream applications including lighter vehicles and reduced energy loss in electronics.
