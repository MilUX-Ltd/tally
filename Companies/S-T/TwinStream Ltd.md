# TwinStream Ltd

> Cyber security and cross-domain solutions specialist, translating NCSC patterns for safe data import and export into real-world implementations.

## What they do

TwinStream builds scalable, resilient systems to solve cross-domain security problems, applying hardsec technology and cloud architecture to protect sensitive government and defence networks. It implements NCSC-approved patterns for safely importing and exporting data, helping clients maintain security posture against evolving threats.
