# Trigo ADR UK Ltd

> Global quality management and supply chain solutions provider specialising in the aerospace, defence and space sectors.

## What they do

TRIGO operates across 28 countries with around 10,000 employees, offering a complete portfolio of quality management services throughout the aerospace, defence and space supply chain. Services range from inspection and auditing through to engineering, consulting, training and technology solutions incorporating AI, virtual reality and data.
