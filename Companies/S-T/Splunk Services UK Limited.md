# Splunk Services UK Limited

> London-based UK entity of a US data analytics and security operations platform provider, serving government and defence customers with big data, SIEM, and operational intelligence capabilities.

## What they do

Splunk enables organisations to collect, analyse, and act on data generated by technology infrastructure, security systems, and business applications. The platform provides security information and event management (SIEM), observability, and operational intelligence, and is used by defence and government customers for cyber security operations and infrastructure monitoring.

## Products and solutions

- **SIEM and security operations** - threat detection, investigation, and response platform.
- **Infrastructure monitoring** - operational intelligence across IT and OT environments.
- **Big data analytics** - collection and analysis of machine-generated data at scale.
