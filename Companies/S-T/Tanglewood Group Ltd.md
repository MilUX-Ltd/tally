# Tanglewood Group Ltd

> Defence and aerospace consultancy specialising in Integrated Logistic Support, supply chain management and bid support across the product lifecycle.

## What they do

Tanglewood provides Integrated Product Support and Integrated Logistic Support consultancy to defence and aerospace clients, covering supply chain management, procurement, bid and tender support, and international business development. The company also offers workforce solutions and capability in unmanned systems and camera systems.
