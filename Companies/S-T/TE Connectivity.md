# TE Connectivity

> Global manufacturer of connectors, sensors and electronic components supplying defence platforms, weapons systems and aerospace applications.

## What they do

TE Connectivity designs and manufactures electrical connectors, cabling, harnesses and interconnect systems for defence platforms, weapons and aerospace equipment. The company's UK base is in Swindon, serving defence customers across land, air and maritime domains with components qualified to aerospace and defence standards including AS/EN/JISQ 9100 and NADCAP.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, NADCAP
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
