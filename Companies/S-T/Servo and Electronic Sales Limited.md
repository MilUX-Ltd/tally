# Servo and Electronic Sales Limited

> Franchised connector distributor and assembler for mil-aero, defence and harsh-environment applications.

## What they do

Servo and Electronic Sales distributes and assembles connectors, backshells and accessories for the military, aerospace and defence sectors, stocking over one million parts across standard and non-traceable inventory. They offer 48-hour assembly on MIL-DTL-38999 Series connectors and hold franchises from suppliers including Esterline Souriau, Fujikura and HellermannTyton.

## Products and solutions

- **MIL-spec connectors** - MIL-DTL-38999 Series I, II and III connectors with 48-hour assembly service.
- **Custom designs** - Backshells, accessories and custom connector configurations for harsh-environment applications.
