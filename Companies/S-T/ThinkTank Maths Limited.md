# ThinkTank Maths Limited

> Mathematics research organisation developing trusted reasoning and optimisation solutions for defence, space, and energy applications where standard software is inadequate.

## What they do

ThinkTank Maths provides advanced mathematical research and software for challenges in engineering and systems of unprecedented complexity. Their work spans trusted reasoning systems for UAVs and rovers, planetary re-entry modelling, and system design optimisation for defence, space, and energy sectors.
