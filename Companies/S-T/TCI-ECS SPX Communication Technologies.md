# TCI-ECS SPX Communication Technologies

> RF technology specialist designing and manufacturing tactical data links, spectrum monitoring systems, RF inhibitors and counter-UAV capabilities under the ECS and TCI brands.

## What they do

SPX Communication Technologies operates across three capability areas: Tactical Data Links, Spectrum Battlespace and Spectrum Monitoring and Management Services. Drawing on the ECS and TCI product ranges, the company designs and manufactures RF detection and location systems, jamming and RF inhibition equipment, and counter-unmanned aerial systems technology for defence customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Tactical Data Links** - Highly engineered data link systems for military communications.
- **RF detection and location systems** - Spectrum monitoring and identification equipment.
- **RF inhibitors / counter-UAV** - Jamming and countermeasures including counter-UAS capabilities.
