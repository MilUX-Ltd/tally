# Smiths Engineering Limited

> Precision engineering manufacturer producing safety-critical components for the aerospace and defence supply chain.

## What they do

Smiths Engineering (trading as Smiths Precision) specialises in CNC multi-axis machining of complex, safety-critical parts for the aerospace industry. Operating from Ballymena in Northern Ireland, the company supports the aerospace supply chain with five- and six-axis machining, a full range of finishing and welding processes, and materials capability across metals and composites.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC 5/6-Axis Machining** - Complex structural and mechanical components in metals and composites.
- **Mechanical Components and Subassemblies** - Including pipes, hoses, springs and fans for aerospace platforms.
- **Surface Finishing** - Electropolishing, buffing, mass finishing and a full range of post-machining treatments.
