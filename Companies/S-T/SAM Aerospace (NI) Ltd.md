# SAM Aerospace (NI) Ltd

> Aerospace component manufacturer based in Portadown, Northern Ireland, specialising in complex machined structures and assemblies.

## What they do

SAM Aerospace (NI) Ltd, formerly JW Kane, manufactures complex aerospace components including fuselage and wing subcomponents, nacelles, pylons, and platform interiors using advanced CNC machining capabilities including 5-axis milling. The business serves major primes including Safran and Spirit Aerosystems in Belfast, and is part of Singapore Aerospace Manufacturing (SAM), a multinational with approximately 2,250 employees.

## Products and solutions

- **Complex Structural Components** - machined fuselage, wing, and nacelle subcomponents for aerospace platforms.
- **Platform Assemblies** - final assembly of aerospace systems and subassemblies.
