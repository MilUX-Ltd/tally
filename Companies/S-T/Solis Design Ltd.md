# Solis Design Ltd

> Engineering design consultancy for aerospace, defence, automotive, and motorsport sectors, offering end-to-end mechanical, electrical, and stress engineering.

## What they do

Solis Design provides engineering design consultancy services covering aerospace, defence, automotive, motorsport, avionics, and product design. The company delivers in-house mechanical, electrical, and stress engineering, supporting customers from initial concept through to production-ready designs, working across Airbus and Boeing platforms as well as military aircraft, helicopters, military vehicles and naval platforms. The company states it owns and operates a static test facility for structural testing of seating, galleys, stowages and other cabin and defence monuments.

## Products and solutions

- **Aircraft interiors** - Mission operator workstations, server racks, galleys, monuments, partitions, seats, ceilings and stowages, from economy to VIP configurations.
- **Structural analysis** - Finite Element Analysis and Finite Element Modelling, stress calculations, weight optimisation, harness design, and composite structures.
- **Static test facility** - In-house structural testing of seating, galleys, stowages and custom monuments, including defence-specific configurations.
