# Solis Design Ltd

> Engineering design consultancy for aerospace, defence, automotive, and motorsport sectors, offering end-to-end mechanical, electrical, and stress engineering.

## What they do

Solis Design provides engineering design consultancy services covering aerospace, defence, automotive, motorsport, avionics, and product design. The company delivers in-house mechanical, electrical, and stress engineering, supporting customers from initial concept through to production-ready designs.
