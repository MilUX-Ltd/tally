# Teledyne CML Composites

> Composite component and assembly manufacturer for aerostructures and aircraft systems, with programmes including F-35, Airbus A350 and AW159.

## What they do

Teledyne CML Composites manufactures and repairs glass and carbon fibre components and assemblies for civil, business and military aircraft at its Wirral facility. Customers include Airbus, BAE Systems, GE Aviation, GKN Aerospace, Spirit AeroSystems and Senior Aerospace.

## Products and solutions

- **Composite aerostructures** - glass and carbon fibre components and assemblies for fixed-wing and rotary platforms including F-35, A350 and AW159.
- **Composite repair** - maintenance and repair of composite aircraft components.
