# Special Quality Alloys Ltd

> Sheffield-based manufacturer and supplier of forgings, precision rolled rings, and high-specification bar stock in nickel alloys, stainless, and titanium.

## What they do

Special Quality Alloys Ltd manufactures and supplies aerospace and defence-grade forgings, ring-rolled components, and bar stock distribution, specialising in nickel superalloys, duplex stainless, and titanium alloys. Founded in 1986, the company is part of the family-owned Special Steel Group and maintains in-house control of the manufacturing process, supported by group companies for heat treatment, testing, and machining.

## Products and solutions

- **Forgings and precision rolled rings** - hammer forge, ring rolling, and upset forging in nickel alloys (Alloy 718, 625, 725), duplex stainless (F51, F55), 17-4PH, and F6NM.
- **Bar stock distribution** - supply of exotic alloys, superalloys, steel alloys including Inconel, and titanium alloys.
- **CNC machining** - lathe and milling operations to finished or near-net dimensions.
