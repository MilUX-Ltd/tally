# Salt Communications

> Secure mobile communications platform with centralised enterprise management.

## What they do

Salt Communications provides encrypted voice, messaging, conference calling, broadcasting, and file transfer on mobile devices, with a web-based management platform giving IT administrators centralised control. Their customers include government, defence, and enterprise organisations that need to protect sensitive communications on the move.

## Products and solutions

- **Salt Secure Communications Platform** - encrypted voice and messaging with centralised management for enterprise and government.
