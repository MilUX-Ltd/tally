# Stealthpath Ltd

> Cybersecurity platform provider specialising in AI/ML-powered threat monitoring and Zero Trust compliance for air-gapped and distributed operational technology networks.

## What they do

Stealthpath delivers agentless network visualisation, continuous threat monitoring and Zero Trust compliance evaluation for isolated or air-gapped OT environments, operating entirely off-network. The platform has been recognised by IBM's Innovation Award and is deployed by US and allied government organisations.
