# Thorn SDS Limited

> Technical managed services provider supplying security-cleared engineers and project teams to space, defence, and security programmes in the UK and Europe.

## What they do

Thorn SDS provides integrated technical managed services ranging from individual subject matter experts to dedicated project teams, all security cleared. Core capabilities span C4ISTAR, cloud and system operations engineering, antenna and ground systems, spacecraft operations, satellite systems, and operational training.

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
