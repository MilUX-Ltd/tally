# Tannlin Ltd

> Laser cutting specialist manufacturing precision stencils, PCBA tooling and laser-cut parts for defence, aerospace, medical and electronics customers.

## What they do

Tannlin comprises two entities: Tannlin UK Ltd designs and manufactures laser-cut stencils and PCBA tooling; Tannlin Technology designs and manufactures laser cutting systems, automated production cells and laser-cut parts. The group serves defence, aerospace, medical, electronic and automotive industries from its Prestwick Aerospace Park facility in South Ayrshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Laser-cut stencils** - SMT stencils and PCBA tooling for electronics assembly.
- **Laser cutting systems** - Automated laser cutting production cells and bespoke laser-cut parts.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus, SC21 Bronze
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
