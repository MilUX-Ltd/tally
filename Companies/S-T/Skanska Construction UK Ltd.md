# Skanska Construction UK Ltd

> Major construction and infrastructure contractor delivering complex projects on live defence sites across the UK.

## What they do

Skanska Construction UK provides construction, civil engineering, and facilities management services, including specialist work in secure and classified environments. The company employs over 200 security-cleared experts and delivers end-to-end projects on active defence sites without disruption to operations.
