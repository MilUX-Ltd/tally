# Simthetiq

> Provider of military simulation, 3D models and synthetic training environments.

## What they do

Simthetiq is a Montreal-based provider of 3D solutions for the defence sector, offering visual systems, 3D model libraries, synthetic environments and virtual training. Its synthetic environments are geo-specific or geo-typical and adaptable to training requirements, supplied in standard industry formats. The 3D content is designed as plug-and-play with common simulation software and is compatible with simulation protocols such as DIS and HLA.

## Products and solutions

- **Synthetic terrains** - modular and comprehensive geo-specific and geo-typical terrains for a range of simulation activities.
- **3D model library** - simulation entity models and 3D content for training and simulation, supplied in standard industry formats.
- **Immersive training environments** - geo-specific synthetic environments and immersive classroom solutions for military end users.
