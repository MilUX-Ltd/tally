# Shadowlink Ltd

> Developer of rapidly deployable temporary fibre optic networks for defence communications, built on UK-sovereign components.

## What they do

Shadowlink has developed a rapidly deployable temporary fibre optic network designed for defence use, providing high-bandwidth, low-latency communications with a very low probability of detection or interception. The system uses UK-sovereign components and partners throughout.
