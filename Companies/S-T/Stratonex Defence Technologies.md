# Stratonex Defence Technologies

> UK and European commercialisation and distribution partner for defence technology companies.

## What they do

Stratonex Defence Technologies acts as a commercialisation and distribution partner for defence technology companies seeking to enter UK and European markets. It supports market access, business development and partnerships for overseas defence and security technology providers.

## Products and solutions

- **Commercialisation and distribution** - UK and European market access and business development for defence technology firms.
