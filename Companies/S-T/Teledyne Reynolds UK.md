# Teledyne Reynolds UK

> UK manufacturer of high-reliability interconnect solutions for defence and aerospace applications.

## What they do

Teledyne Reynolds UK designs and manufactures interconnect solutions spanning signal, power, high and low voltage, high-speed copper, RF, and fibre optics. They supply from individual connectors and cabling components up to fully integrated harness systems for demanding defence and aerospace environments.
