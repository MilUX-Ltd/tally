# Sustainable Criminal Justice Solutions CIC

> Community interest company delivering CBRN, counter-terrorism, and criminal justice consultancy for government and security organisations.

## What they do

SCJS operates as an independent delivery partner to UK government departments including the Home Office and Ministry of Justice, and leads EU Council of Europe CBRN projects with FCO support. The company provides specialist consultancy and programme delivery across CBRN, counter-terrorism, forensic services, and incident response to government agencies and private sector clients internationally.
