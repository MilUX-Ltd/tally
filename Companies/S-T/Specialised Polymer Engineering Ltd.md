# Specialised Polymer Engineering Ltd

> Southampton-based manufacturer of advanced aerospace sealing solutions, supplying Airbus, Boeing, Embraer, and other OEM aircraft programmes.

## What they do

Specialised Polymer Engineering Ltd designs and produces elastomeric and thermoplastic sealing solutions for aerospace applications, covering material and design consultation, prototype development, serial production, and aftermarket support. The company serves major airframe OEMs and supplies components qualified for existing and future aircraft platforms.
