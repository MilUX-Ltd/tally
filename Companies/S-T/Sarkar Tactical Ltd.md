# Sarkar Tactical Ltd

> Glasgow-based manufacturer of body armour, ballistic helmets, tactical uniforms, and armour plates.

## What they do

Sarkar Tactical, established in 2014, manufactures personal protective equipment and ballistic solutions including body armour, ballistic helmets, and armour plates using fully automated technology. The company operates manufacturing facilities in Glasgow covering 9,000 square feet, and has developed into a turn-key solutions provider for defence and security customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Body Armour** - ballistic vests and armour plates for personal protection.
- **Ballistic Helmets** - protective helmets for defence and security personnel.
- **Tactical Uniforms** - protective and tactical clothing solutions.
