# Spire Global UK Ltd

> Glasgow-based UK entity of a US space-to-cloud data and analytics company operating a constellation of over 100 nanosatellites to deliver earth observation, maritime, aviation, and weather data services.

## What they do

Spire Global operates a large constellation of nanosatellites and a global network of ground stations to track and analyse earth information, maritime vessel movements, aviation traffic, space weather, and terrestrial weather patterns. The UK entity is headquartered in Glasgow and provides space-derived data and analytics for defence, government, and commercial customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Maritime tracking** - global AIS vessel tracking and analytics.
- **Aviation tracking** - global aircraft surveillance and flight data.
- **Weather intelligence** - terrestrial and space weather data and forecasting services.
- **Earth observation and ISR** - satellite-derived surveillance data.
