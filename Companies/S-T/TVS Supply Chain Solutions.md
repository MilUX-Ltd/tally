# TVS Supply Chain Solutions

> Global provider of end-to-end supply chain outsourcing services, with a specialist defence practice serving the UK MoD and major prime contractors.

## What they do

TVS Supply Chain Solutions (TVS SCS) delivers supply chain outsourcing and fourth-party logistics services across defence, automotive, utilities, and beverage markets. In defence, clients include the UK MoD and Babcock International, with services spanning intelligent inventory management, logistics distribution, and operating cost reduction.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence Supply Chain Outsourcing** - End-to-end supply chain management for defence customers including the UK MoD.
- **Fourth-Party Logistics (4PL)** - Management and optimisation of complex multi-supplier logistics networks.
- **Inventory Reduction and Distribution** - Specialist services targeting delivery time reduction and inventory optimisation.
