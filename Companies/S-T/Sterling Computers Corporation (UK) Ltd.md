# Sterling Computers Corporation (UK) Ltd

> UK entity of a US-headquartered solutions integrator providing secure, mission-focused technology to UK Government and Defence.

## What they do

Sterling Computers Corporation (UK) Ltd designs, integrates, and supports technology solutions across digital workspace, modern infrastructure, connectivity, cyber security, cloud, and managed services. The company serves UK Government, Defence, and regulated public-sector organisations as a wholly owned subsidiary of Sterling Computers Corporation, established in 1996.
