# Safran Labinal Power Systems

> Safran's specialist in electrical power systems for aviation, covering the full electrical chain from generation and distribution to wiring and load management.

## What they do

Labinal Power Systems is a Safran group company focused on electrical power systems for the aviation market. Its UK site is in Pitstone, Buckinghamshire, and serves aerospace, automotive and rail sectors with solutions spanning generation, distribution, conversion, wiring, load management, ventilation, systems integration and support services.
