# SiXworks
> Secure digital solutions provider for defence and national security, specialising in high-assurance technology and digital experimentation.
## What they do
SiXworks accelerates the adoption of secure digital technology in defence and national security environments. The company focuses on fail-safe-fast experimentation with cutting-edge solutions deployed in highly secure settings. It advocates Zero Trust, Data-Centric and high-assurance architectures to protect against cyber threats, and supports customers with demanding security, assurance and privacy requirements.
