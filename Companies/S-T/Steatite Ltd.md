# Steatite Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of rugged computers, custom lithium battery systems, secure communications and advanced antennas for harsh operating environments.

## What they do

Steatite supplies ruggedised computing platforms, power and battery systems, TEMPEST/EMC-compliant electronics, thermal and CCTV imaging systems, and communications equipment to defence and security customers. The company is part of Solid State Plc (AIM: SOLI).

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged computers** - Industrial and military-grade computing platforms hardened for field deployment.
- **Custom lithium battery solutions** - High-integrity power systems for portable and vehicle-mounted applications.
- **Secure communications systems** - Audio and digital communications for defence and security platforms.
- **Advanced antennas** - Antenna systems for military and industrial communications.
