# Skylift UAV Ltd

> UK drone operator providing advanced offshore logistics, delivery, and military research UAV services.

## What they do

Skylift is an experienced UK drone operator specialising in advanced offshore logistics and delivery solutions. The company also undertakes military research and provides turnkey UAV solutions , both VLOS and BVLOS , for logistical and time-critical operational challenges.
