# TT Electronics

> Global manufacturer of engineered electronics for performance-critical aerospace and defence applications.

## What they do

TT Electronics designs and manufactures high-performance electronics for major aerospace and defence programmes, including custom power management systems, ruggedised sensors, displays, and electronic components for mission-critical applications in harsh environments. The company operates high-mix, low-volume manufacturing in Nadcap-accredited facilities.

## Products and solutions

- **Power Management and Conversion Systems** - Custom high-performance power electronics for mission-critical applications.
- **Ruggedised Sensors and Displays** - Sensors, gauges, monitoring systems, displays, and tracking/telemetry equipment.
- **Electronic Components and Subcomponents** - Circuit boards, flexible circuits, optical components, and interconnect harnesses.
- **Platform Interior Electronics** - Lighting and electronic subassemblies for platform interior fit-out.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ITAR approval, NADCAP, ISO 14001, ISO 13485, IPC 610 Class 1/2/3, UKAS, SC21 Bronze
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
