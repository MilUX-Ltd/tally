# Solstrand Technologies

> High Wycombe sub-contract precision engineering company serving aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Solstrand, trading as Solstrand Industries, is a sub-contract precision engineering company providing CNC turning, milling and finished precision components and assemblies. With over 25 years in business, it produces prototypes through to batch runs of thousands of units and is attuned to the quality requirements of the aerospace and defence sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision machined components** - turned and milled parts to specification.
- **Prototyping and batch production** - single parts through high-volume runs.
- **Assemblies and finishing** - grinding, lapping and plating via partner networks.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100; ISO 9001:2015 (LRQA); JOSCAR-registered.
- **Memberships:** Make UK; British Turned Parts Manufacturers Association (BTMA).
