# Tensor plc

> UK manufacturer of smartcard and biometric-based physical security, access control, and workforce management systems.

## What they do

Tensor designs, manufactures, and installs time and attendance, access control, visitor monitoring, and personnel systems using smartcard and biometric technologies. Product ranges also cover turnstiles, car park barriers, CCTV, ANPR, fire roll-call and evacuation reporting, and energy management, supplied to defence sites and other secured facilities.

## Products and solutions

- **Access Control Systems** - Smartcard and biometric entry management for secured sites.
- **CCTV and ANPR** - Camera and automatic number plate recognition systems.
- **Time and Attendance** - Workforce management software and hardware.
