# Stop-choc Ltd

> UK manufacturing unit of the Hutchinson group producing anti-vibration mounts, illuminated display panels, and electronics racking for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Stop-choc operates a manufacturing facility in Reading focused on the design, development, and production of illuminated display panels, anti-vibration mounts, and electronics racking. The company is part of the Hutchinson group, a global leader in vibration control, fluid management, and sealing systems technologies.

## Products and solutions

- **Anti-vibration mounts** - Vibration isolation and stabilisation components for aerospace and defence platforms.
- **Illuminated display panels** - Display panel assemblies for defence electronics applications.
- **Electronics racking** - Structural racking systems for defence electronics integration.
