# SPAICE
> Spatial-AI autonomy software for satellites, aircraft and drones operating in communications- and GNSS-denied environments.
## What they do
SPAICE builds AI autonomy software so platforms can navigate and operate when communications and GPS are degraded or denied. Products target In-Orbit Services using Generative AI and Deep Reinforcement Learning, with proprietary navigation and multitasking tools designed to integrate with existing satellite systems. The team, founded by graduates of Imperial College London, ETH Zurich and Oxford, has assembled a dataset of over one million photorealistic images of orbiting assets for model training. Pilots and endorsements have been reported with Leonardo, Astroscale, ESA and Telespazio.
