# SA Group Limited

> Wiltshire consultancy delivering cyber security, systems integration and P3M services to defence and security.

## What they do

SA Group is a technology and management consultancy specialising in cyber security and information assurance, digital integration, systems engineering, and project, programme and portfolio management (P3M). It works extensively in the defence and security sectors, including cross-domain interoperability, military medical support systems and operational support. It also provides managed services such as service desk and network operations.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber security and information assurance** - defensive security aligned to operational needs.
- **Digital integration** - secure connection of systems and applications.
- **P3M and systems engineering** - programme delivery and secure technical architecture.
