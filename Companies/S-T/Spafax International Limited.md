# Spafax International Limited

> Manufacturer of unbreakable commercial vehicle mirrors designed for military users and security services.

## What they do

Spafax developed and pioneered the first unbreakable commercial vehicle mirror, constructed from self-extinguishing materials and backed by a ten-year guarantee against lens breakage. The product is designed for military and security service use, offering rugged performance in demanding operational conditions.

## Products and solutions

- **Unbreakable vehicle mirror** - Military and security-grade vehicle mirror in self-extinguishing materials, guaranteed against breakage for ten years.
