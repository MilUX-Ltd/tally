# SystemWare Europe Ltd

> Supplier and integrator of TSCM, RF surveillance, and tactical and strategic satellite communications solutions.

## What they do

SystemWare Europe supplies, installs and supports solutions for technical surveillance countermeasures (TSCM), RF surveillance, and satellite communications across tactical and strategic domains. The company also provides a broad range of antennas for ground-to-air, satcom, laboratory, vehicle, manpack and field applications, with associated masts and tripods.

## Products and solutions

- **TSCM and RF surveillance** - detection and countermeasure solutions for technical surveillance threats.
- **Satellite communications** - tactical and strategic satcom equipment and integration.
- **Antennas and mast systems** - ground-to-air, satcom, vehicle, manpack and field antenna systems with mast and tripod support.
