# Sterling Training Ltd

> Training provider specialising in programmes that support defence engagement, operational effectiveness, and international client capability.

## What they do

Sterling Training has designed and delivered training programmes since 2015 covering instructor development, leadership and management, and cultural and linguistic training for staff working with international defence clients. Capabilities listed under ADS include covert and counter-terror training, engineering training, police training, and tactical training.
