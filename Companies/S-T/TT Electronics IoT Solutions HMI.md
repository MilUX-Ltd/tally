# TT Electronics IoT Solutions HMI

> Designer and manufacturer of ruggedised Human Machine Interface solutions for defence, aviation, marine, and industrial applications.

## What they do

TT Electronics IoT Solutions HMI has designed and manufactured HMI control assemblies for over 30 years, supplying major system manufacturers across aviation, defence, marine, rail, medical, and industrial markets. Products include membrane keypads, rugged control panel assemblies for military applications, touchscreens, and thin-film backlighting systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged Control Panel Assemblies** - Military-grade control panels for defence platform applications.
- **Membrane Keypads** - Custom membrane keypad solutions for marine bridge and industrial systems.
- **Touchscreen HMI Assemblies** - Fully customised touchscreen interfaces for harsh-environment use.
- **Thin-Film Backlighting** - Aircraft interior signage and cockpit lighting solutions.
