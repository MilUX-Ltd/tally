# STS Defence

> Defence electronics, communications and engineering company specialising in naval and air defence systems, part of the Cicor Group.

## What they do

STS Defence is a UK-based technology and engineering company specialising in mission-critical, secure communications and networks, electronic warfare and control, and instrumentation systems. It operates primarily in the naval and air defence markets, helping customers design, assemble and integrate equipment and systems and support capability through-life. It has been a wholly owned subsidiary of the Swiss-listed Cicor Group since October 2023.

## Products and solutions

- **Ships communications masts** - configurable, integrated main communications masts for naval warships.
- **UHF satellite communications** - delivery and through-life support of mission-critical UHF SatCom systems.
- **Integration and support** - system integration plus Integrated Logistic Support aligned to MoD principles, covering reliability engineering, repairs, spares and obsolescence management.
- **Electronic warfare and instrumentation** - specialist EW support and instrumentation, sensors and control systems for marine and harsh environments.
- **Electronics manufacturing** - fabrication, assembly and testing of consoles, cabinets, box builds, PCBAs and cable harnesses (via Cicor integration).
