# Systematic

> Developer of defence command and control and battle-management software, including the SitaWare suite.

## What they do

Systematic, established in 1985 and headquartered in Aarhus, is Denmark's largest privately owned software company, with solutions sold in over 50 countries. It develops software for the public and private sectors, including defence command and control. Its SitaWare suite is combat-proven commercial off-the-shelf software providing C4ISR and battle-management capabilities across land, sea, air and joint domains, with interoperability for coalition operations. SitaWare is used by more than 50 countries, including most NATO nations.

## Products and solutions

- **SitaWare Suite** - C4ISR and battle-management software offering command-and-control across the battlespace and coalition interoperability.
- **SitaWare Headquarters** - command-and-control software for planning and conducting operations; selected by NATO as its standard system for land operations.
- **SitaWare BattleCloud** - cloud-native evolution of the SitaWare Suite, extending capabilities from fixed headquarters to mobile users at the tactical edge.
