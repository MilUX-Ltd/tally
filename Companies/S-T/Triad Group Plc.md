# Triad Group Plc

> Digital technology consultancy specialising in complex transformation programmes for criminal justice, public safety, policing and other sensitive domains.

## What they do

Triad Group Plc has operated for over 35 years, deploying SC- and NPPV3-cleared consultants to lead digital programmes and projects in security-sensitive environments. The company provides strategy, leadership, agile delivery and digital solutions across criminal justice, public safety and related government domains.
