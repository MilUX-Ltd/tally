# SecureCloud+ Limited

> Managed ICT and cyber security services for defence and public sector organisations with demanding security requirements.

## What they do

SecureCloud+ develops and delivers innovative ICT systems for defence and public sector customers as end-to-end managed services. Their solutions enable customers to securely access networks, resources and applications, with capabilities spanning command and control services, simulation and modelling, cyber security, and data analysis.
