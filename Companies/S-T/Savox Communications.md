# Savox Communications

> Tactical communications and hearing-protection headsets and systems for defence and security users.

## What they do

Savox Communications designs and manufactures rugged hearing-protection and communication solutions for defence, law enforcement, fire and rescue, maritime and industrial users. The company combines in-house design, engineering and manufacturing, and supplies radio-accessory products to leading radio and personal-protection-equipment manufacturers. It has over 40 years of experience and operations spanning Finland, Germany, the United Kingdom and the United States.

## Products and solutions

- **Noise-COM 200x2** - next-generation tactical hearing-protection and communications headset for dismounted, mounted and training environments.
- **TRICS Ci4** - tactical radio and intercom controller integrating radio, satellite and data traffic, with ATAK access for digital situational awareness; built to MIL-STD 810G.
- **Savox Bridge** - digital platform for enhanced information flow across tactical teams.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Standards:** products engineered to MIL-STD 810G.
