# Thornton Tomasetti Defence Ltd

> Consulting engineers and scientists specialising in the survivability, blast protection, and vulnerability reduction of military systems and structures.

## What they do

Thornton Tomasetti Defence applies a combination of modelling and simulation alongside physical testing to harden military platforms and structures against blast, shock, fragmentation, groundshock, and vibration. Their validated software tools have been used on UK and US defence programmes for decades.
