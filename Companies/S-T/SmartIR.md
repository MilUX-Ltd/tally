# SmartIR
> Developer of graphene-based smart coatings for infrared signature management and satellite thermal control.
## What they do
SmartIR develops electro-optical devices based on a patented graphene technology that enables real-time dynamic control of infrared and visible radiation. Applications span defence signature management and space satellite thermal control. The company was spun out of the University of Manchester to commercialise its graphene research. ADS member and D3IP community participant at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
## Products and solutions
- **Smart coatings** - electro-optical devices enabling adaptive control of IR emission and absorption for signature management and thermal regulation.
