# ScioTeq

> Rugged avionics displays, consoles and visualisation systems for military and civil aircraft and air traffic control.

## What they do

ScioTeq designs and manufactures high-performance visualisation and display solutions for the avionics, defence and air traffic control markets. The company draws on a 40-year heritage in the field and operates manufacturing facilities in Belgium and the United States. Its displays are fielded on more than 150 aircraft types, civil and military.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged avionics displays** - high-performance ruggedised cockpit and mission displays for fixed- and rotary-wing platforms.
- **Avionics display computers and consoles** - mission computing and video distribution for defence platforms.
- **ATC displays** - air traffic control visualisation systems.
- **AM-817 Airborne Mission Display** - next-generation airborne mission display.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Platforms:** displays fielded on military aircraft including F-16, F-35, C-130, A400M, A330 MRTT, C-27J, P-8 and PC-21.
