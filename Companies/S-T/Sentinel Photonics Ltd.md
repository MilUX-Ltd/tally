# Sentinel Photonics Ltd

> MOD spin-out company providing laser detection and protection products and expertise to defend against the hostile use of lasers.

## What they do

Sentinel Photonics develops and supplies laser warning and protection systems, drawing on its origins as a Ministry of Defence spin-out from Porton Down. The company produces optical and electronic sensor systems focused on detecting and mitigating laser threats across military and security applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Laser detection systems** - Sensor and warning systems that identify and characterise hostile laser illumination.
- **Laser protection products** - Protective solutions and expertise to reduce the risk of laser-based attacks.
