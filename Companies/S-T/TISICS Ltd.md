# TISICS Ltd

> Advanced metal composite manufacturer producing lightweight structural components for aerospace and spacecraft programmes.

## What they do

TISICS develops and manufactures titanium and aluminium composite components using net-shape manufacturing technologies, targeting aircraft and spacecraft structures. Their materials outperform high-strength steel and titanium at significantly lower weight, with applications in landing gear, structural components, engine parts, pressure vessels, propellant tanks and spacecraft structures.

## Products and solutions

- **Titanium composite** - Stronger and stiffer than high-strength steel, used in landing gear and structural airframe components.
- **Aluminium composite** - Exceeds the strength and stiffness of titanium at 40% lower density, used in engine and spacecraft applications.
- **Pressure vessels and propellant tanks** - Net-shape composite structures for spacecraft and satellite programmes.
