# Serbus Ltd

> Secure infrastructure, communications and managed services provider for UK defence, CNI, public sector and enterprise.

## What they do

Serbus designs and deploys resilient communications infrastructure and ICT/OT networks for security-cleared sectors including defence, CNI, health and government. They provide 24/7 onsite and offsite technical services to support complex and contested communication environments.
