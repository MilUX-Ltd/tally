# Sentinel Flight Services

> UK commercial drone services operator providing UAS operations and support.

## What they do

Sentinel Flight Services is a UK UAS operator providing commercial drone services. The company is led by a veteran and former military officer and has connections to the UK defence drone and innovation community. Sentinel Flight holds a GVC (General VLOS Certificate) operating capability and has introduced partners across the UK drone sector.
