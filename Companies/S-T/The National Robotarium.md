# The National Robotarium

> Global research institute at Heriot-Watt University developing robotic, AI and automated technologies and accelerating their transfer from lab to market.

## What they do

The National Robotarium works directly with industry to test and develop robotic, AI and autonomous technologies, supporting rapid translation from research to commercial application. It also runs incubation and business acceleration programmes and delivers training in robotics, data and programming skills to develop the current and future workforce.
