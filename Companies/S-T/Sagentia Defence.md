# Sagentia Defence

> Defence science and technology consultancy, formerly TP Group, part of Science Group plc.

## What they do

Sagentia Defence, formerly TP Group, provides science and technology consultancy on mission-critical defence programmes. It positions itself as an independent, agnostic adviser and a trusted customer friend to the MOD, with defence experience spanning over 60 years. It works with national government bodies, international institutions and global prime contractors on systems engineering, feasibility analysis, enterprise transformation and programme delivery across the science and technology, maritime, air, land, joint, space and defence digital domains, across the full CADMID lifecycle. It is part of Science Group plc, an LSE-listed science and technology group headquartered near Cambridge that consolidated several consultancies under the Sagentia brand in 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **Operational analysis** - technology analysis, roadmapping, modelling and simulation, requirements and decision analysis.
- **Cost engineering** - independent cost modelling, estimating and risk analysis.
- **Project, programme and portfolio management (P3M)** - managing complex defence programmes from inception to delivery.
- **Safety assurance and certification** - through-life safety across civil and military domains.
- **Supportability engineering** - integrated logistics support and supply chain management against MOD standards.
- **Systems engineering** - full lifecycle, applying ISO 15288.
- **Analysis and innovation** - pre-concept analysis, rapid concept generation and prototyping.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; ISO/IEC 27001:2022; Cyber Essentials; Cyber Essentials PLUS; JOSCAR registered.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; INCOSE UK; APM; IET; IMechE; Operational Research Society; SCAF.
