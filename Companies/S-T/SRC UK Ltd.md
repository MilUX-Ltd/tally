# SRC UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of US defence technology company SRC Inc., delivering data solutions, analytics, and defence consultancy for intelligence, finance, and government customers.

## What they do

SRC UK Ltd provides near real-time data solutions and analytics across finance, intelligence, and government domains, with a mission focused on keeping the UK and its allies safe. As a subsidiary of SRC Inc. (Syracuse Research Corporation), the firm brings US defence technology expertise to UK government and defence customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Data solutions and analytics** - Near real-time, actionable information across government, intelligence, and finance domains.
- **Defence consultancy** - Engineering consultancy, research and development, and tactical training services.
- **Software and modelling** - Software development, optimisation, and modelling for government applications.

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
