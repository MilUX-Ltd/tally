# The Global Strategy Network Ltd

> Data-driven research and communications firm specialising in counter-extremism, counter-disinformation, and complex projects in fragile and conflict-affected states.

## What they do

Founded in 2016, TGSN works at the intersection of security, conflict and information, tackling violence and extremism and combating mis-, dis- and mal-information. The firm delivers complex projects in fragile and conflict-affected states for government and institutional clients.
