# Satcom Direct International - UK

> Global satellite connectivity provider for military, government and aviation platforms, with UK operations based at Farnborough Airport.

## What they do

Satcom Direct provides global satellite connectivity solutions for military and government aircraft, business and general aviation, and land mobile services. The UK operation is based at Farnborough Airport, Hampshire. SD is a premier Inmarsat distribution partner, Iridium service partner and ViaSat Ku preferred reseller. Divisional capabilities include SD Avionics and SD software for communications management across aviation and defence platforms.

## Products and solutions

- **Military and government connectivity** - satellite communications solutions for military, government and head of state aircraft.
- **SD Avionics** - aviation communications hardware and installation services.
- **SD software** - communications management software for aviation and defence customers.
