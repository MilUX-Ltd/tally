# Techpoint

> Electronics design, manufacturing and supply chain company serving aerospace, defence, healthcare and security sectors, from prototype through to high-volume production.

## What they do

Techpoint employs over 200 engineers and designers delivering electronics design, manufacturing and supply chain solutions. The company manufactures electronic components, circuit boards, box builds, cabling and harnesses, and electronic systems across demanding regulated sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Electronic component manufacture** - circuit boards, flexible circuits, box builds, cabling, wire harnesses and connectors.
- **Electronic systems** - complete electronic system manufacturing from prototype to high volume.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN 9100, AS/EN 9120, IPC 610 Class 1/2/3, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR Registered
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
