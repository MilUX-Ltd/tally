# Stream Defence Ltd

> UK manufacturer of thermal management, environmental control, and communications systems for defence platforms.

## What they do

Stream Defence designs and manufactures cooling and heating systems, from rugged field refrigeration to high-performance climate control in harsh environments. The company integrates physical, thermal, fluid flow, radio, digital, and acoustic disciplines to produce low-observable, reliable products that meet defence requirements and are economic over their lifetime.

## Products and solutions

- **Thermal and environmental control systems** - HVAC and climate management for defence platforms, including rugged blood fridges and field climate systems.
- **Communications and data systems** - Platform communications and C2 integration.
- **CBRN and protective equipment** - Decontamination and protective systems.
