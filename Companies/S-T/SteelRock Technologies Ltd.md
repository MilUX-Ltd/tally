# SteelRock Technologies Ltd

> Developer of UAV platforms and counter-UAV systems for military, security and humanitarian applications.

## What they do

SteelRock Technologies produces rotary UAV platforms and a dedicated counter-UAV product range, supported by long-term training, operations and maintenance services. Platforms are used for EOD, surveillance, industrial inspection and payload carriage across military and security theatres.

## Products and solutions

- **NightFighter** - Portable counter-UAV system for field deployment.
- **Odin** - Fixed counter-UAV system configurable for a range of operational environments.
- **Rotary UAVs** - Military and commercial UAS platforms for EOD, ISR and payload delivery tasks.
