# SecureCloud+ Limited

> Secure managed cloud and IT services for UK defence and national security.

## What they do

SecureCloud+ provides secure technology services for government and defence operations, with a focus on secure collaboration, data-driven decision making and secure service management. The company works with the Ministry of Defence and the wider defence industry, and deliberately limits public disclosure of client and operational detail given the sensitivity of its work.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure Defence Collaboration** - secure collaboration services for defence users.
- **Data Driven Decision Making** - services supporting data-led decisions.
- **Secure Service Management** - managed secure cloud and IT service delivery.
