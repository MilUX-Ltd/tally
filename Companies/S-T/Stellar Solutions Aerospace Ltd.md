# Stellar Solutions Aerospace Ltd

> UK subsidiary of Stellar Solutions Inc., providing systems engineering services to government and commercial space and defence customers.

## What they do

Stellar Solutions delivers high-impact systems engineering, end-to-end technical expertise and programme support to national and international clients across space and defence. The UK entity is based at the Rutherford Appleton Laboratory, Harwell Campus, with sister companies in France and the US parent founded in 1995.
