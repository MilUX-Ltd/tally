# Tonnik Consulting Ltd

> Specialist cyber security and information risk consultancy with experience in sensitive government systems and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Tonnik Consulting provides advice on information security architecture, security management, information risk, and security operations, drawing on lead consultants with experience at senior levels in government and CNI environments. The firm focuses on cyber risk reduction for clients where the stakes of a security failure are high.
