# Stratecuity Ltd

> Specialist in modelling and simulation for defence and aerospace, with particular focus on distributed simulation and mission training.

## What they do

Stratecuity provides modelling and simulation consultancy to defence and aerospace customers, with expertise in distributed simulation for concept development and mission training. The firm has particular experience with helicopters and UAS, and engages across strategy, technology evaluation, bid support, product development, and engineering research.
