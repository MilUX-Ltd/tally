# Tecknuovo Ltd

> Technology delivery partner specialising in software engineering, DevOps, cloud platforms, and data and AI for defence and public sector customers.

## What they do

Tecknuovo operates in the space between consultancy and capability development, delivering against customers' immediate software engineering, platform and DevOps needs before working with them to mature longer-term strategies. The company's Zero Dependency Framework is designed to eliminate supplier lock-in and build lasting internal capability in customer organisations.
