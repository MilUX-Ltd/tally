# STARK Defence

> European defence technology company building AI-enabled uncrewed systems and loitering munitions.

## What they do

STARK is a European defence technology company specialising in uncrewed systems across multiple domains. It develops and manufactures loitering munitions and the mission software to operate them, and supplies the German Bundeswehr and other NATO states. The company was established in 2024 and has operations across Germany, Ukraine, the United Kingdom, Greece and Sweden.

## Products and solutions

- **OWE-V Virtus** - electrically powered VTOL loitering munition. Public specifications cite a cruise speed of around 120 km/h, a dive speed of up to 250 km/h, a 5 kg warhead and an endurance of around 60 minutes.
- **Minerva** - mission control software suite for precision-strike coordination, including operation in GNSS-denied or data-link-degraded conditions, integration with battle management systems, and coordination of synchronised strike actions.
