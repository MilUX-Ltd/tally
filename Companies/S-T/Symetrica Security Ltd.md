# Symetrica Security Ltd

> Develops and manufactures radiation detection and identification systems for defence, border security and cargo screening.

## What they do

Symetrica designs radiation detection and identification products including handheld, backpack and mobile systems, as well as fixed and demountable portal monitors. Products are supplied directly and through OEM partners, with command and control software for radiation detection integrated with high-energy cargo X-ray screening capability. The company has its R&D headquarters in Southampton and manufacturing and sales operations in both the UK and Massachusetts, USA.

## Products and solutions

- **Handheld and backpack detectors** - portable radiation identification for security and military personnel.
- **Portal monitors** - fixed and demountable systems for checkpoint and border screening.
- **C2 software** - command and control software for radiation detection and integrated cargo X-ray screening.
