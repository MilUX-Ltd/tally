# Key Plastics Ltd

> Irish plastics and precision metal manufacturer serving the aerospace, medical and engineering sectors.

## What they do

Key Plastics Ltd, based in Bray, Co. Wicklow, provides custom-manufactured components in plastic and metal, covering the full project lifecycle from concept, prototyping and validation through to mass production. The company combines mould design, mould manufacture and precision engineering with injection moulding, including a Class 7 moulding cleanroom, and serves aerospace, medical device, telecoms and engineering customers in the UK, Europe, North America and Asia-Pacific as a single-source procurement partner.

## Products and solutions

- **Injection moulding** - custom plastic component manufacture, including cleanroom moulding for medical and precision applications.
- **Precision metal machining** - precision-engineered metal components alongside its plastics capability.
- **Mould design and manufacture** - in-house tool design and mould-build capability supporting new product introduction.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS 9100 (aerospace), ISO 13485 (medical device), ISO 9001.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
