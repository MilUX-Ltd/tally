# Integrated Security Manufacturing

> Technology company delivering intelligent command and control solutions for high-security environments and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Integrated Security Manufacturing (ISM) has over 30 years of experience reducing costs and improving efficiency in high-security and critical infrastructure environments. The company specialises in smart technology that bridges legacy and modern systems, providing command and control integration, business intelligence, and compliance support across disparate security systems. ISM describes itself as offering manufacturer-agnostic integration of third-party systems within high-security environments, serving sectors including nuclear sites, airports, ports, utilities, data centres, hospitals, custodial estates, and venues and stadia.

## Products and solutions

- **Genesys** - command and control / PSIM (Physical Security Information Management) platform unifying CCTV and video management, access control, perimeter intrusion detection, building management systems, drone detection, and over 150 third-party integrations into a single interface, with automated incident-management workflows and situational awareness reporting. Marketed at critical national infrastructure operators.
- **Genesys Nano (Custodial)** - a variant of Genesys for police custody suites and HM Prison & Probation Service estates, covering automated detainee logging and life-sign monitoring for vulnerable persons, plus a "Smart Cell Kiosk" for telephony, self-service, and e-learning.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 9001:2015, ISO 27001:2022, CAPSS (Cyber Assurance of Physical Security Systems) approval for Genesys, Secured by Design (Police Preferred Specification)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
