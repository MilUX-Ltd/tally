# Newton Europe Ltd

> Operational management consultancy serving defence, government, energy and health sectors.

## What they do

Newton Europe, trading as Newton, is an operational management and strategic delivery consultancy operating across central government, defence, energy, health and integrated care, housing, local government, regulated utilities, retail and transport. Its defence practice states a focus on implementing UK defence strategy and improving defence affordability, combining operational insight, digital capability and change delivery across the defence enterprise. Newton states that it puts its implementation fees at risk against agreed delivery outcomes. The company cites involvement in every major UK MOD maritime build programme over roughly the last 15 years.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence maritime build support** - digital transformation and programme delivery support for MOD maritime build programmes.
- **Front Line Command availability improvement** - operational improvement work aimed at equipment and platform availability for Front Line Commands.
