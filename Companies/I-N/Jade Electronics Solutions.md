# Jade Electronics Solutions Ltd

> Provider of end-to-end electronics manufacturing services (EMS) for the defence, aerospace, medical and industrial sectors.

## What they do

Jade Electronics Solutions is a provider of professional contract and extended electronics manufacturing services (EMS), based in Exeter, Devon and incorporated in November 2022. The company offers high-reliability electronics manufacturing across sectors including MOD defence and national security, aerospace and avionics, communications, medical and industrial. It describes itself as a UK sovereign capability and has received recognition from the Ministry of Defence for its commitment to the Armed Forces.

## Products and solutions

- **Electronics manufacturing services (EMS)** - end-to-end contract electronics manufacture.
- **Electromechanical services** - box builds, cable harnessing and mechanical assembly.
- **Cable assemblies** - aerospace and defence-grade cable assemblies.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015, with a quality framework aligned to AS9100D aerospace practices.
