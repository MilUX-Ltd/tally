# Inflatable Structures

> UK SME designing and manufacturing inflatable shelters, decoys, and rapid-deployment structures for military and defence clients.

## What they do

Inflatable Structures Limited designs and manufactures inflatable products for defence, civil, and commercial customers from its base in Ipswich. The defence-facing product range covers inflatable shelters and tents, military decoys, rapid-deployment cover, and logistic and welfare structures for use in exercises and operations.

## Products and solutions

- **Inflatable military shelters** - rapid-deployment shelters and tents for field use in exercises and operations.
- **Military decoys** - inflatable decoy structures for use in training and operational environments.
- **Logistics and welfare structures** - inflatable structures supporting field logistics and personnel welfare.
