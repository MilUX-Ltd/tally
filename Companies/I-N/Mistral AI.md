# Mistral AI
> Paris-based foundation-model company building open and proprietary LLMs, with a growing defence vertical targeting European sovereign AI workloads.

## What they do
Mistral AI is Europe's leading AI lab, developing open and proprietary large language models and positioning itself for sovereign and defence AI workloads across European NATO nations. The company has formed a strategic partnership with Helsing focused on vision-language-action models for next-generation defence systems. A framework agreement with the French Ministry of Defence was announced in January 2026.

## Products and solutions
- **Mistral LLMs** - Open and proprietary large language models, including frontier models intended for sovereign and classified deployment.
