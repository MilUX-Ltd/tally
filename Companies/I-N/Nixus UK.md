# Nixus UK

> UK manufacturer of rapid-deployment inflatable shelters and field structures for military use, the defence brand of Inflatable Structures Ltd.

## What they do

Nixus UK is the military and defence brand of Inflatable Structures Ltd, and the UK distributor for Nixus2protect.com. It designs and supplies rapid-deployment inflatable shelters and field structures: high-pressure inflatable tents, command posts, expandable containers and metal-frame field buildings, engineered to deploy across extreme environments from desert heat to Scandinavian winters and built to meet EU and NATO material, safety and transportation standards. The structures pack down small and erect quickly, and support base camps, command posts, field hospitals, CBRN facilities, workshops and storage.

## Products and solutions

- **PRO Heavy Duty Tent** - high-pressure inflatable shelter for extreme-weather military use.
- **PRO X Command Post** - command centre combining three PRO tents in an ISO-container configuration.
- **ERA Inflatable Military Tent** - inflatable accommodation with near-vertical sides for efficient use of space.
- **PGK General Purpose Tent** - inflatable shelter for temperate-climate operations.
- **RIBS Large Span Inflatable Building** - large-span structure up to roughly 12.5m wide and 23m long.
- **HUB and FOLD Metal Frame Tents** - multipurpose field buildings for workshops, stores, gyms, maintenance and hangars.
- **ECOS Expandable Container** - container-based deployable structure.
- **STRETCH Canopy** - rapid-cover canopy structure.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
