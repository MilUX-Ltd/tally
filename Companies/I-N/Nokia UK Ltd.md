# Nokia UK Ltd

> Global network technology company providing communications infrastructure, software and services to defence and government customers.

## What they do

Nokia designs and delivers mobile, fixed and cloud networks, with a significant portfolio serving defence and government customers including tactical communications, private wireless networks and mission-critical connectivity. Research and intellectual property development is led by Nokia Bell Labs.
