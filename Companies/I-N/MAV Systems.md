# MAV Systems

> Manufacturer of ANPR cameras and surveillance systems for police, military, and government clients worldwide.

## What they do

MAV Systems designs and manufactures automatic number plate recognition cameras, CCTV systems, radar countermeasures, and road traffic control systems. The company has over 15 years of experience supplying law enforcement, military, and government departments globally.
