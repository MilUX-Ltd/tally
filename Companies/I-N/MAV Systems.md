# MAV Systems

> Manufacturer of automatic number plate recognition (ANPR) camera systems for law enforcement, military, and government clients worldwide.

## What they do

MAV Systems designs and manufactures ANPR camera systems, serving police, military, government, parking operators, and transport authorities. Founded in 2007, the company states it has more than 30,000 cameras deployed across over 80 countries. MAV Systems is part of The Traffic Group, which also includes sister companies working in radar-based vehicle detection and traffic signals; MAV Systems' own products are ANPR and camera based.

## Products and solutions

- **MAV AiQ** - flagship ANPR camera stated to offer over 99% recognition accuracy, tamper and clone detection (GhostPlate), recognition across more than 150 country plate formats, dual 1080p colour and infrared imaging, and IP68-rated housing for operation from -40C to 60C.
- **AiQ Lite** - lower-tier variant of the AiQ camera range.
- **MAV Mobile** - vehicle-mounted ANPR camera launched January 2026, using the company's FlexiAi software with NVIDIA Jetson processing.
- **BAYiQ** - additional camera model in the current range.
