# LPE Ltd

> Belfast-based additive manufacturing and rapid prototyping specialist with over 30 years' experience serving aerospace and defence manufacturers.

## What they do

LPE is the UK and Ireland's longest-established 3D printing partner, operating from a 25,000 sq ft facility in Belfast with more than 20 machines and 45 materials including plastics, rubbers, metals and specialist-grade materials. The company serves aerospace and defence manufacturers across rapid prototyping and low-volume production, applying processes including stereolithography, selective laser sintering, digital light processing and direct metal laser sintering.

## Products and solutions

- **Stereolithography (SLA)** - High-accuracy photopolymer prototyping for complex geometries.
- **Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)** - Nylon and engineering-grade polymer parts for functional testing.
- **Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS)** - Metal additive manufacturing in aluminium, steel, titanium and Inconel alloys.
- **Hybrid Photosynthesis (HPS)** - LPE states it was the first 3D printing service in the UK and Ireland to offer the HPS process.
- **CNC Machining** - 5- and 6-axis milling for precision metal components.
- **Surface finishing** - Electroplating, electropolishing, abrasive blasting and polishing services.
