# Inspired Act Ltd

> Creative performance company providing professional actors and trained specialist roleplayers for immersive training simulations and assessment events across defence and public sectors.

## What they do

Inspired Act supplies roleplay and simulation support for military and government training exercises, providing trained actors who portray realistic scenarios for counter-terrorism, intelligence, and general operational training. The company also consults on drama-based communication, interactive teaching solutions, and media support.

## Products and solutions

- **Roleplay Training Simulations** - Immersive exercise support using professional actors and specialist roleplayers for military and government training events.
- **Assessment Events** - Structured roleplay for personnel assessment and selection events.
- **Drama-based Communication Coaching** - Interactive learning solutions covering public speaking and media handling.
