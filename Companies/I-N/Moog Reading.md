# Moog Reading

> UK components and systems manufacturing operation of Moog Inc., supplying defence and aerospace platforms.

## What they do

Moog Reading is the components-focused UK facility of Moog Inc., based in Suttons Business Park, Reading. The site supplies manufactured components across a broad range of defence categories including platform systems, propulsion, weapons and structures.
