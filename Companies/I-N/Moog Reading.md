# Moog Reading

> UK components and systems manufacturing operation of Moog Inc, supplying slip rings and power/data transfer solutions to defence, aerospace and space platforms.

## What they do

Moog Reading Limited, trading as part of Moog's Power and Data Division, is based at Suttons Business Park, Earley, Reading. The site's registered scope covers the design, manufacture and repair of electrical sliding contacts, slip ring separates and packaged units, and fibre optic rotary joints. Moog describes the wider Power and Data Division as supplying power and data transfer solutions, motion control and electronic products designed to meet the technical and environmental requirements of the defence and space markets, with products used on armoured vehicles, electro-optic and infrared systems, helicopters and space satellites.

## Products and solutions

- **Slip rings** - electro-optic system twist capsules, helicopter slip rings, miniature slip ring capsules, propeller slip rings and vehicular slip rings, used in applications including target acquisition systems and tank turrets.
- **Fibre optic rotary joints** - rotary joint products for data transfer across rotating interfaces.
- **Electrical sliding contacts and packaged units** - components supplied under the site's certified manufacturing scope.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** EN 9100:2018 / ISO 9001:2015 (BSI-certified quality management system for the Reading site).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group (listed as Moog Components Group Ltd).
