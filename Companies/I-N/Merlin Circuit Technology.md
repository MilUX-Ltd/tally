# Merlin Circuit Technology

> High-technology PCB manufacturer producing prototype, NPI and medium-volume multi-layer printed circuit boards for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Merlin Circuit Technology operates a purpose-built 45,000 sq ft factory in North Wales manufacturing 2 to 32 layer PCBs, including advanced HDI micro-via and impedance-controlled product. The company holds AS9100 Rev D accreditation and serves aerospace and defence customers with quick-turn and standard service options supported by experienced engineering teams.

## Products and solutions

- **Multi-layer PCBs** - 2 to 32 layer boards including HDI micro-via technology.
- **Impedance-controlled PCBs** - precision-engineered boards for high-reliability applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100 (AS9100 Rev D); ISO 9001; ISO 14001; Cyber Essentials; Cyber Essentials Plus; SC21 OE; SC21 Bronze
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
