# JOTA Holdings

> Engineering group applying motorsport-derived precision and composites expertise to defence, automotive and marine sectors.

## What they do

JOTA Holdings operates across endurance motorsport, advanced engineering and composites manufacturing, with subsidiaries JOTA Advanced Engineering and JOTA Composites serving defence, automotive and marine customers. JOTA Advanced Engineering, incorporated in 2015 and registered in Tunbridge Wells, manufactures fully inspected, complex components in aluminium, stainless steel, special alloys and plastics. JOTA Composites operates from a 15,000 sq ft facility in West Oxfordshire, producing complex autoclave-cured components in carbon, aramid and sustainable fibres for automotive, defence and marine customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision-machined components** - complex, fully inspected parts in aluminium, stainless steel, special alloys and plastics (JOTA Advanced Engineering).
- **Composite components** - autoclave-cured carbon, aramid and sustainable-fibre parts, including design, tooling and assembly (JOTA Composites).
