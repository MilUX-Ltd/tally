# JOTA Holdings

> Engineering group applying motorsport-derived precision and composites expertise to defence, automotive and marine sectors.

## What they do

JOTA Holdings operates across endurance motorsport, advanced engineering and composites manufacturing, with subsidiaries JOTA Advanced Engineering and JOTA Composites serving defence, automotive and marine customers. The group designs complex solutions and develops specialist technologies from two facilities in West Oxfordshire and Hastings.
