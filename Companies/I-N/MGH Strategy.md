# MGH Strategy Ltd

> Defence sector strategy, capture and bid management consultancy with a focus on aerospace and space.

## What they do

MGH Strategy provides consultancy in strategy formulation and execution, capture and bid strategy, and management and governance support through to contract award. The firm focuses on the defence sector with particular specialties in aerospace and space.
