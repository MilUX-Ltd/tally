# Logically

> AI company providing narrative and threat intelligence to detect and counter mis- and disinformation for government, defence and enterprise.

> [!warning] Status
> The original UK legal entity (THELOGICALLY LTD, Companies House 10850644) entered administration on 2025-06-27. Its assets, brand and core technology were sold in a pre-pack administration deal to Kreatur Ltd in July 2025, preserving around 50 roles. The Logically brand continues to trade under the new owner.

## What they do

Logically provides narrative decision intelligence for government and enterprise. It combines AI with human analysis to monitor the online information environment, detect and cluster harmful narratives and disinformation, and deliver alerts, reporting and decision support. The platform is positioned for national security, defence, public safety and government missions, with enterprise risk and communications use cases second.

## Products and solutions

- **Logically Intelligence** - threat-detection platform that monitors public and open-source information, uses machine learning to assess source and content credibility, detects propaganda, and clusters emerging narratives with alerts and reporting.
- **PRISM** - scoped intelligence system offering simulation, action support and integration with customer workflows for missions beyond standard monitoring.
