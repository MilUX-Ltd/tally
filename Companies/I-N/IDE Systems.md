# IDE Systems

> Safety engineering and systems engineering consultancy for highly regulated industries including defence, nuclear, and aerospace.

## What they do

IDE Systems provides safety analysis, safety case development, and systems engineering services to clients operating in safety-critical environments. Founded in 2004, the company is independent and privately owned, working across defence, nuclear, and aerospace sectors.
