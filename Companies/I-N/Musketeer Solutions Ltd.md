# Musketeer Solutions Ltd

> MOD-registered independent airworthiness consultancy supporting military large transport and ISTAR aircraft platforms since 2006.

## What they do

Musketeer Solutions Ltd (MSL) delivers independent airworthiness support to the MOD and military aircraft industry primes, specialising in large transport and ISTAR platforms. The company is MOD FATS-STS/D&E registered and ISO 9001 certified, with a track record of airworthiness review, on-aircraft system interconnectivity inspection, structural repair assessment, and zonal survey work delivered on time and on cost.

## Products and solutions

- **Airworthiness review services** - independent airworthiness support using a range of review methodologies for MOD platforms.
- **Structural and systems inspection** - on-aircraft interconnectivity inspection and structural repair assessment.
- **Safety and engineering consultancy** - health and safety, logistics, research and training services for the aerospace and defence sector.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001; MOD FATS-STS/D&E Registered
