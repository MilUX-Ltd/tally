# MindLink Software

> Ultra-secure chat and messaging platform for classified communication in national security and defence.

## What they do

MindLink provides a secure mission information-sharing platform that lets organisations and their mission partners establish a global communication backbone for mission execution. It offers encrypted, real-time chat using data classification and user entitlements to restrict access on a need-to-know basis. The platform uses a data-centric, end-to-end encryption approach with attribute-based access control, and is deployed at scale in air-gapped environments. The company is based in London.

## Products and solutions

- **MindLink Platform** - ultra-secure chat and messaging for international and inter-agency mission operations across defence and intelligence.
- **Data-centric security engine** - multi-layered access control based on secret attributes, roles and security clearances.
- **FRNIX federation protocol** - federation protocol designed to share classified information only with those holding the appropriate need-to-know.
