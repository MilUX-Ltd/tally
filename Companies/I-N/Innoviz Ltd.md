# Innoviz Ltd

> Cranfield-based aerospace consultancy, engineering services firm, and parts distributor operating across the UK, US, and India.

## What they do

Innoviz operates across three verticals: aerospace and IT recruitment, aerospace design and engineering consultancy, and aerospace parts distribution. Based at Cranfield University Technology Park and mentored by the Bettany Centre, the company holds AS9100 and AS9120 certification and is JOSCAR registered.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace Design and Engineering Services** - Engineering consultancy and design for aerospace customers.
- **Aerospace Parts Distribution** - Stockist and distributor of aerospace components.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered
- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100; AS/EN/JISQ 9120; Cyber Essentials Plus
