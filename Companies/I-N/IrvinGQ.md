# IrvinGQ

> Manufacturer of aerial delivery systems, personnel parachutes, rescue and survival equipment, and naval decoy systems with over a century of heritage.

## What they do

IrvinGQ designs and manufactures specialist life-saving and aerial delivery equipment for military and civil customers under the Irvin and GQ Parachutes heritage brands. Product lines span personnel parachutes, aerial delivery platforms, rescue and survival systems, and naval decoy systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerial delivery systems** - Military cargo and precision aerial delivery platforms.
- **Personnel parachutes** - Military and specialist parachute systems.
- **Rescue, safety and survival equipment** - Safety harnesses, deployable slides, and fire control and detection systems.
- **Naval decoy systems** - Countermeasure decoys for naval platforms.
