# Mini Gears

> Precision subcontract engineering company manufacturing high-precision machined parts, gears and mechanical components for the aerospace and defence supply chain.

## What they do

Mini Gears operates a 35,000 sq ft factory in Stockport with over 100 machines and 90 staff, supplying precision-machined components to aerospace, defence and industrial customers globally. Established over 58 years ago, the company holds AS/EN/JISQ 9100 and ISO 9001 certification and is Cyber Essentials Plus accredited.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision machined parts and gears** - CNC machined components including 5-axis, milling, turning, hobbing and grinding across metals, nylon and TPU.
- **Mechanical assemblies and subcomponents** - Platform structures, propulsion components, engine parts, seating subcomponents and lifting equipment.
