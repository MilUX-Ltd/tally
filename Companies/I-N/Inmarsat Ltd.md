# Inmarsat Ltd

> Global mobile satellite communications provider delivering broadband data and voice connectivity for government, maritime, and aviation customers worldwide.

## What they do

Inmarsat operates a global satellite network providing mobile communications services where terrestrial coverage is unavailable. Its government division supplies secure connectivity to military and government customers; it also provides maritime and aviation safety services as well as commercial broadband. The company was acquired by Viasat in 2023 and now operates as part of that group.
