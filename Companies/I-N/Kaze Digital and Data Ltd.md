# Kaze Digital and Data Ltd

> Digital resilience and cyber consultancy serving defence, government and complex commercial environments.

## What they do

Founded in 2019 and trading as Kaze Consulting, the company delivers digital transformation, cyber resilience and decision engineering for organisations in complex, high-trust environments. Its team combines commerce, technology and armed services experience, working across governance, risk and compliance, digital product ownership and AI-driven decision support. Kaze operates from Cheltenham with additional offices in Cambridge and London.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber resilience and GRC** - design and mobilisation of organisation-wide cyber transformation strategy, governance, risk and compliance, and cyber risk management.
- **Digital transformation** - user-centric capability development, digital product ownership and adoption of agile approaches to operational planning.
- **Decision engineering** - AI, machine learning and data analytics solutions to build trust in data and augment decision-making, including the in-house KATO AI product.
