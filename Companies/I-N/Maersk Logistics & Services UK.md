# Maersk Logistics & Services UK

> UK entity of the global Maersk logistics group, providing integrated supply chain and logistics solutions to defence and government customers.

## What they do

Maersk Logistics & Services UK is the British arm of A.P. Moller-Maersk, delivering integrated logistics across ocean, land, air, customs and port services. The company has a dedicated Government and Defence business development function and holds ITAR approval alongside ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 27001 and Cyber Essentials Plus certifications, positioning it for work across defence supply chains and government logistics contracts.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** ITAR, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 27001:2022, Cyber Essentials Plus
