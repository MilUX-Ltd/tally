# Maersk Logistics & Services UK

> UK entity of the global Maersk logistics group, providing integrated supply chain and logistics solutions to defence and government customers.

## What they do

Maersk Logistics & Services UK is the British arm of A.P. Moller-Maersk, delivering integrated logistics across ocean, land, air, customs and port services. The company has a dedicated Government and Defence business development function and holds ITAR approval alongside ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 27001 and Cyber Essentials Plus certifications, positioning it for work across defence supply chains and government logistics contracts. The wider Maersk group also operates a customised government and defence transportation programme, including chartered logistics support for defence and public sector customers and status as a Civil Reserve Air Fleet partner in the United States.

## Products and solutions

- **Government and defence logistics services** - customised transport and logistics support for government and defence customers, spanning ocean, land, air, customs and port services.
- **Contract logistics and warehousing** - UK contract logistics base at the East Midlands Gateway, Derby, supporting warehousing and distribution.
- **Time-definite shipping services** - scheduled express and standard freight services for time-critical consignments.
- **Customs brokerage** - clearance and compliance services liaising with relevant government agencies.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Armed Forces Covenant signatory (signed 2016).
- **Accreditations:** JOSCAR, ITAR, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 27001:2022, Cyber Essentials Plus.
