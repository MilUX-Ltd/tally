# Kraken Technology Group

> UK builder of high-speed boats and low-cost, open-architecture uncrewed surface vessels.

## What they do

Kraken Technology Group is a UK company applying precision design and advanced engineering to the performance marine market, with a focus on uncrewed surface vessels. It develops cost-effective, open-architecture USVs for surveillance, force protection and multi-domain operations. The company is based in Fareham, Hampshire.

## Products and solutions

- **K3 Scout** - high-performance, low-cost uncrewed surface vessel with a fully modular payload bay, adaptable for reconnaissance, communications relay, electronic warfare, decoy, logistics or strike tasks. The 8.4 m composite hull can reach around 55 knots with a stated range of 650 nautical miles at 25 knots.
