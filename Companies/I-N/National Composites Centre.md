# National Composites Centre

> UK national R&D facility providing industry access to composite engineering capability for aerospace, defence and advanced manufacturing sectors.

## What they do

The National Composites Centre (NCC) is a research and development facility based in Bristol that enables companies to develop and scale composite manufacturing technologies through access to state-of-the-art engineering equipment and expertise. It supports programmes across aerospace, automotive, marine and defence applications.
