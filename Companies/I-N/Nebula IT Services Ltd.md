# Nebula IT Services Ltd

> Managed IT and cybersecurity services provider for SMEs and businesses in the UK defence supply chain.

## What they do

Nebula IT is a managed service provider offering IT support, managed IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and compliance advisory services to SMEs, with a stated focus on organisations in regulated sectors and the UK defence supply chain. Services span day-to-day managed IT support, cyber resilience work including Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus preparation, and consultancy including a Virtual CTO offering.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed IT Support** - Remote and onsite server and workstation support, telecoms supply and support, and IT department cover.
- **Virtual CTO Services** - Board-level technology strategy, procurement advice and IT risk management, scoped to client need.
- **Managed SOC** - Monitoring of cybersecurity events day and night, delivered via a third-party managed SOC partner, covering patch and update management and incident response.
- **Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus support** - Gateway and server testing, vulnerability assessment, and bespoke audits to help client organisations achieve accreditation.
- **Email and device security** - Email threat detection and DMARC implementation, plus endpoint protection.
- **Staff awareness training** - Phishing simulation and cyber awareness training for client staff.
- **Cyber advice** - Advisory delivered by NCSC-certified Cyber Advisor staff.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus, IASME Cyber Assurance, NCSC Assured Service Provider, NCSC Cyber Advisor scheme
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
