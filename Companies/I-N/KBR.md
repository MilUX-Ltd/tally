# KBR

> Engineering, construction and programme management firm delivering infrastructure and support services to UK defence and government.

## What they do

KBR's UK operation, headquartered in Leatherhead with around 3,000 staff (rising to over 4,000 nationally including Frazer-Nash Consultancy, a KBR company), delivers estate management, engineering and construction services to defence and public sector clients. Work includes rehousing British Army personnel in modern accommodation and managing large-scale infrastructure programmes. KBR's UK focus spans defence, policing and home affairs, and energy, and it describes itself as a world leader in programme and project management, engineering, procurement and construction, logistics, services and training.

## Products and solutions

- **Heavy Equipment Transporter (HET) support** - provision, operation and maintenance of the British Army's Heavy Equipment Transporter fleet, including movement of Challenger 2 tanks, under the Materiel Distribution Land 2025 programme.
- **Combat Water Supply System (CWSS)** - design, manufacture, supply and storage of potable water systems for deployed UK forces, plus reimbursable maintenance, repair and operational support.
- **Operational support to Permanent Joint Headquarters (PJHQ)** - embedded operational support capability for UK military headquarters.
- **Defence estate and infrastructure management** - engineering, procurement and construction services across the British Army estate and other public sector infrastructure.
- **Transforming Forensics programme delivery** - delivery partner to the National Police Chiefs' Council and Association of Police and Crime Commissioners, providing programme management, design, communications and change expertise for forensic services reform.
- **MOJ National Service Hub (Swindon)** - facilities and estate support across Ministry of Justice probation offices, approved premises, training centres and Home Office estate.
- **Submarine and maritime engineering (via Frazer-Nash Consultancy)** - engineering support across the UK submarine programme, including work for Rolls-Royce Submarines' nuclear reactor design, analysis, manufacture, assurance and in-service support.
- **Cyber defence research (via Frazer-Nash Consultancy)** - AI and machine-learning-based self-defending, self-recovering cyber defence concepts developed with Dstl under the Autonomous Resilient Cyber Defence (ARCD) programme.
- **Defence engineering delivery (via Frazer-Nash Consultancy)** - engineering delivery partner support for rapid deployment of military equipment to the front line, as part of the Aurora Engineering Partnership.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** Management Consultancy Framework Four (MCF4), Aurora Engineering Partnership / Engineering Delivery Partner (EDP) programme (via Frazer-Nash Consultancy).
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001 (processes, procedures and work instructions), ISO 44001 (collaborative business relationships).
- **Recognition:** Gold Award, UK Defence Employer Recognition Scheme; signatory of the Armed Forces Covenant since 2014.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
