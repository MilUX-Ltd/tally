# KBR

> Engineering, construction and programme management firm delivering infrastructure and support services to UK defence and government.

## What they do

KBR's UK operation, headquartered in Leatherhead with around 3,000 staff, delivers estate management, engineering and construction services to defence and public sector clients. Work includes rehousing British Army personnel in modern accommodation and managing large-scale infrastructure programmes.
