# Infrastructure Projects Partners Ltd

> North West programme management and controls consultancy specialising in complex infrastructure delivery, including nuclear and defence projects.

## What they do

Founded in 2011, IPP provides project, programme and portfolio (P3M) management and controls services across utilities, nuclear, and defence sectors. The company embeds within client teams and operates across the full project lifecycle from strategic advisory and business case development through to on-site delivery support.

## Products and solutions

- **Strategic Advisory Services** - Investment appraisal, organisational change, vendor assurance, due diligence, and event recovery for national infrastructure programmes.
- **Tactical Delivery Support** - Hands-on programme management, PMO design, and controls support for major tenders and live programmes.
