# London Machine Works Ltd
> UK R&D and manufacturing SME providing in-house design, build, and prototyping capability for defence and engineering customers from its Farnborough base.
## What they do
London Machine Works Ltd is a UK defence engineering and manufacturing company based at Abbey House, Farnborough, with an R&D office in Kingston. It offers in-house mechanical and electrical installation, in-house and offsite manufacturing, prototyping, and 3D scanning with sub-millimetre accuracy for reverse engineering and replication. The company provides an alternative to offshoring for defence and industrial customers needing UK-based design and build capability. It is a signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant.
## Products and solutions
- **Manufacturing and prototyping** - In-house mechanical and electrical manufacturing, prototyping, and component replication for defence programmes.
- **3D scanning** - Sub-millimetre accuracy scanning for reverse engineering and replication of components.
