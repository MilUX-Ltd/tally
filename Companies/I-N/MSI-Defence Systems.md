# MSI-Defence Systems Ltd

> UK land and naval weapon systems specialist, the only UK-based medium calibre naval gun system manufacturer.

## What they do

MSI-Defence Systems (MSI-DS) is a UK-based land and naval weapon systems specialist and a world leader in small and medium calibre naval gun systems. It is the only UK-based medium calibre system manufacturer. The company supplies lightweight automated weapon mounts to the Royal Navy and to over 40 armed forces worldwide, and traces its engineering heritage back more than a century.

## Products and solutions

- **SEAHAWK** - multi-role naval weapon system available as the DS 25/30mm mounting and the lightweight LW 20mm mounting, in service with numerous navies.
- **DS30M Mark 2** - 30mm automated small calibre gun mounting in service with the Royal Navy.
- **TERRAHAWK** - land-based VSHORAD (very short range air defence) system.
- **Stabilised systems** - stabilised mounts and radar mast systems.
