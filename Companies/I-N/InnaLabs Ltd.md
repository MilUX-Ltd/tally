# InnaLabs Ltd

> Dublin-based manufacturer of high-performance inertial sensors for defence and aerospace guidance, navigation, and stabilisation applications.

## What they do

InnaLabs designs and manufactures tactical-grade Coriolis vibratory gyroscopes and navigation-grade accelerometers. Its products target precision guidance, stabilisation, navigation, and orientation applications across defence and aerospace platforms. The company supplies into UK defence through a UK-based regional sales presence.

## Products and solutions

- **Coriolis vibratory gyroscopes** - Tactical-grade inertial sensors for guidance and navigation.
- **Navigation-grade accelerometers** - High-precision accelerometers for orientation and stabilisation.
