# Materialise UK

> UK subsidiary of Belgian additive manufacturing group Materialise, supplying software and manufacturing services to aerospace, government and defence customers.

## What they do

Founded in 1990 and headquartered in Belgium, Materialise brings additive manufacturing software and industrial 3D printing services to defence contractors, aerospace organisations and government customers. The UK office is located at Southampton Science Park. The company combines software development for additive manufacturing with large-scale production capabilities, supporting supply chain resilience through rapid prototyping and localised manufacturing.
