# Materialise UK

> UK subsidiary of Belgian additive manufacturing group Materialise, supplying software and manufacturing services to aerospace, government and defence customers.

## What they do

Founded in 1990 and headquartered in Belgium, Materialise brings additive manufacturing software and industrial 3D printing services to defence contractors, aerospace organisations and government customers. The company combines software development for additive manufacturing with large-scale production capabilities, supporting supply chain resilience through rapid prototyping and localised manufacturing. Its industrial and aerospace additive manufacturing services operate from the Advanced Manufacturing Park, Sheffield.

## Products and solutions

- **Magics** - build-preparation software for additive manufacturing, with geometry-fixing, marking and measurement/reporting tools.
- **3D printing services** - metal 3D printing, selective laser sintering, Multi Jet Fusion, fused deposition modelling and other processes, used for rapid prototyping, small-series production and on-demand spare parts.
- **Certified aerospace materials** - flame-retardant additive manufacturing materials, including Airbus-certified grades, meeting FAR/CS 25.853 heat-release, flame, smoke and toxicity requirements.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** EN 9100 (polymer and metal additive manufacturing), EASA Part 21.G Production Organisation Approval, Airbus AIPI standards certification, ISO 9001, ISO 14001.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
