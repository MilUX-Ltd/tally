# Meliorem HR Consultancy

> Strategic HR consultancy supporting organisations in regulated, complex and high-assurance environments, including aerospace, defence, security and advanced engineering.

## What they do

Meliorem HR advises UK and international organisations on people strategy, workforce risk and organisational change, with over a decade of experience in defence and aerospace-adjacent sectors. The consultancy works with leadership teams to align HR strategy with operational, regulatory and commercial priorities across the full HR lifecycle.
