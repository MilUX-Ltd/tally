# K39 Consulting
> Australian veteran-owned defence consultancy delivering strategic advisory across guided weapons, maritime sustainment, and programme delivery, with a UK presence from 2025.
## What they do
K39 Consulting is a professional services consultancy founded in Sydney in 2018 by Darren Owen, a dual British/Australian Army veteran. It provides strategic advisory, commercial and procurement support, engineering, and technical assurance across Australian and UK defence, with specialist expertise in Guided Weapons and Explosive Ordnance (GWEO) and maritime sustainment. The team numbered approximately 44 staff as of mid-2026, over 40% of whom hold UK citizenship. K39 expanded into the UK in late 2025, establishing a London presence at Farringdon Street.
## Products and solutions
- **Strategic advisory** - Capability development, procurement, and commercial advisory for defence programmes.
- **GWEO support** - Guided Weapons and Explosive Ordnance expertise across Australian and UK programmes.
- **Maritime sustainment** - In-service support and sustainment advisory for maritime platforms.
- **Programme delivery** - Project and programme management and technical assurance for complex defence programmes.
