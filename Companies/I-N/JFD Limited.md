# JFD Limited

> Underwater capability provider serving defence and commercial markets with diving, submarine rescue and hyperbaric solutions.

## What they do

JFD delivers capability across special operations diving, submarine rescue, submarine systems and commercial diving, drawing on over 40 years of experience in the underwater environment. The company designs and manufactures equipment ranging from diving systems and hyperbaric chambers to submarine rescue vehicles and platform launch-and-recovery systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Special Operations** - diving and equipment solutions for special forces and military diving roles.
- **Submarine Rescue** - submarine rescue vehicles and systems operated under contract with defence customers.
- **Submarine Solutions** - hardware, systems integration and through-life support for submarine platforms.
- **Commercial Diving** - equipment and services for the offshore and commercial diving industry.
