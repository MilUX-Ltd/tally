# Indicatura Ltd

> Asset management and investment consultancy for critical infrastructure, including defence and nuclear programmes.

## What they do

Indicatura advises clients on high-value physical asset investment decisions using its proprietary KBEE (Knowledge Based Economic Engineering) methodology alongside AR&M and Asset Management Services. The firm works across commercial, defence, and nuclear sectors, focused on maximising value and reducing capital expenditure waste in highly regulated environments.
