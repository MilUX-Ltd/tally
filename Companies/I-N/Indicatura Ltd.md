# Indicatura Ltd

> Asset management and investment consultancy for critical infrastructure, including defence and nuclear programmes.

## What they do

Indicatura is an infrastructure and asset management consultancy founded in 2012 and based in Manchester. It advises clients on high-value physical asset investment decisions, maximising the value of current and future investments by optimising performance, risk, cost and sustainability through the life of the investment. The firm works across manufacturing, commercial real estate, chemical and petrochemicals, critical infrastructure, defence and nuclear. In defence and nuclear programmes, it specialises in uncovering and mitigating risks associated with asset Availability, Reliability and Maintainability (AR&M), citing work on a £100 million defence asset storage project that identified a 45% reduction in capital expenditure and a 63% reduction in lifetime operational expenditure.

## Products and solutions

- **KBEE (Knowledge Based Economic Engineering)** - proprietary methodology for identifying whole-life value opportunities in physical asset investment decisions.
- **AR&M (Availability, Reliability and Maintainability)** - techniques and analytics for uncovering and mitigating asset performance risk across the investment lifecycle.
- **Asset Management Services** - optimisation and implementation of through-life management strategies, from acquisition to disposal or upgrade.
