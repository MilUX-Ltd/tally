# JET Connectivity

> UK developer of secure sovereign 5G mesh networks delivered on floating buoy platforms for maritime and remote environments.

## What they do

JET Connectivity delivers secure 5G/6G mesh networks with integrated backhaul and positioning, navigation and timing capabilities for maritime and expeditionary environments. The company, spun out from JET Engineering in 2020 and trading under the JET Connectivity name from October 2022, has built its own hardware and software stack. It positions its sovereign networks as land and sea-based alternatives to satellite services for offshore, coastal and remote sites.

## Products and solutions

- **5G connectivity buoys** - floating buoy platforms hosting bespoke 5G networks, sharing coverage between buoys and relaying data to shore.
- **Pop-up 5G IAB mesh** - secure mesh capability designed for littoral, offshore and remote environments, including GPS-denied conditions.
- **Platform range** - a compact buoy for rivers and coastal areas, a mid-size platform for wider maritime zones, and an offshore station for year-round connectivity miles from shore, with onboard sensors collecting sea state, weather and video data.
