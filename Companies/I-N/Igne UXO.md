# Igne UXO

> UK provider of unexploded ordnance survey, risk assessment and clearance services, formerly BACTEC.

## What they do

Igne UXO Ltd is the unexploded ordnance arm of Igne, a UK subsurface services group formed in 2023 by consolidating several specialist businesses. The UXO business, formerly BACTEC, specialises in unexploded ordnance risk mitigation and explosive ordnance disposal, with land and marine coverage and a nationwide geospatial UXO risk database. It is one of the few UK contractors licensed to acquire and keep ordnance.

## Products and solutions

- **UXO risk assessments** - desk-study preliminary risk assessments using a nationwide UXO risk database, structured to CIRIA C681 guidance.
- **UXO surveys** - non-intrusive, intrusive and drone-borne magnetometer surveys to detect ferrous anomalies.
- **EOD services** - explosive ordnance disposal: search, clearance, disposal and on-site watching briefs.
- **Marine UXO** - offshore, inshore and nearshore UXO and chemical-munition risk mitigation for renewables, cabling and port operators.
- **UXO training** - CPD-accredited UXO safety training and toolbox talks.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, PAS 2080:2023, UKAS-accredited testing.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, British Drilling Association, plus industry schemes including Constructionline Gold, CHAS and RISQS.
