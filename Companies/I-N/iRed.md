# iRed

> UK drone, UAV, and thermal imaging services firm providing surveys, training, and equipment to defence, government, and public safety clients.

## What they do

iRed (trading as iRed Ltd) has delivered drone and thermal imaging technology, training, and remote sensing surveys since 2002. Clients include AWE, the CAA, the Home Office, and the Ministry of Defence. Training is delivered by ex-forces members and covers drone operation and thermography to ISO standards.

## Products and solutions

- **Remote sensing surveys** - Aerial inspection, search and rescue support, and industrial surveillance using UAV and thermal imaging platforms.
- **Drone pilot and thermography training** - Accredited training programmes delivered in-location across the UK and via remote learning.
- **Equipment sales** - Drone and thermal imaging hardware through the company's online store.
