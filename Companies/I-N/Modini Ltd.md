# Modini Ltd
> UK SME specialising in the design, development, test, evaluation and operational delivery of complex uncrewed systems for government and commercial clients.

## What they do
Modini delivers end-to-end capability across the full UxS lifecycle, from analysis and design through to operational delivery. The firm works with government and commercial clients worldwide, with expertise spanning avionics, flight control, propulsion and ground support equipment. It is based at Salisbury Road Business Park in Pewsey, Wiltshire.

## Products and solutions
- **Uncrewed system development and delivery** - Full-lifecycle UxS engineering from concept analysis through to operational fielding.
- **Uncrewed platforms** - Multi-rotor and fixed-wing platforms using electric, jet or gas turbine propulsion, developed for austere operating conditions across land, maritime and air domains.
