# MKC Training Services Ltd

> Defence-focused training provider delivering construction, engineering and project management programmes from foundation to degree level, primarily for the MOD.

## What they do

MKC Training Services delivers construction and engineering training at the Royal School of Military Engineering and other MOD sites, covering technical trades from foundation through to degree level. The company also provides project, programme and risk management training, facilities management, health and safety, e-learning and bespoke course design. It is a wholly owned subsidiary of Mid Kent College.
