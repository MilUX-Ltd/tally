# MKC Training Services Ltd

> Defence-focused training provider delivering construction, engineering and project management programmes from foundation to degree level, primarily for the MOD.

## What they do

MKC Training Services designs, manages, delivers and assesses artisan, technical and professional training and qualifications, from foundation level to Master's degree, primarily for the British Army's Royal Engineers. It delivers training at the Royal School of Military Engineering, Brompton Barracks, Chatham, as part of the Holdfast Training Services consortium under a Ministry of Defence public-private partnership. It is a wholly owned subsidiary of MidKent College, with profits reinvested into the college and local community. The company states it designs bespoke training solutions end to end, and also delivers face-to-face, virtual and classroom-based instruction directly, including training the trainers and assessing trainees.

## Products and solutions

- **APM Project Fundamentals Qualification and Project Management Qualification** - introductory and full project management qualifications based on the APM Body of Knowledge.
- **PRINCE2 and PRINCE2 Agile (Foundation and Practitioner)** - project management qualifications.
- **Managing Successful Programmes (MSP)** - programme management qualification, Foundation and Practitioner.
- **AgilePM and APMG Change Management** - agile project management and organisational change qualifications.
- **Management of Risk (M_o_R)** - risk management qualification.
- **Construction and engineering courses** - including scaffolding inspection, AutoCAD and additive manufacturing introductions, delivered at foundation to degree level.
- **Bespoke training design** - e-learning, blended and immersive (AR/VR/MR) training solutions, plus train-the-trainer services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001; City & Guilds Approved Centre; PeopleCert Accredited Training Organisation (PRINCE2, PRINCE2 Agile, M_o_R, MSP).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
