# Jaltek Systems Ltd

> Electronics design and manufacturing services for aerospace, defence, space and security.

## What they do

Jaltek is a Luton-based electronics manufacturing services (EMS) provider offering combined design and manufacturing. Its work spans hardware, PCB and software design through to new product introduction, PCB assembly, test and box build, serving the aerospace, defence, space, marine, medical and security sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **Design services** - hardware development, PCB layout, software and firmware, and mechanical engineering.
- **Electronics manufacturing** - new product introduction, PCB assembly, test, box build and supply chain management.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100; ISO 9001; ISO 14001; ISO 27001; ISO 13485; Cyber Essentials Plus; IPC.
- **Memberships:** Make UK Defence; Bedfordshire Chamber of Commerce; Silverstone Technology Cluster; Medilink Midlands.
