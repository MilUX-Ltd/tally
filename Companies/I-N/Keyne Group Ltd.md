# Keyne Group Ltd

> Defence and facilities management bid and sales consultancy helping organisations win more work and differentiate in competitive markets.

## What they do

Keyne Group is a work-winning and growth consultancy for the facilities management, defence and government services sectors. Formed in 2023, the firm states it has supported over £15 billion of complex bids across hundreds of opportunities over the last decade. It covers bid management, service solutioning, technical writing and commercial and pricing support, alongside post-award contract retention, rather than bid writing alone.

## Products and solutions

- **Work Winning** - sales methodology and bid strategy support to identify and secure opportunities, using established bidding frameworks.
- **Bid support** - end-to-end bid management, technical bid writing, service solution development and commercial and estimating support, including contract legal reviews.
- **Keyne Continuum (contract retention)** - a "Win-Again" framework for retaining live facilities management and service contracts through renewal, including in central government and defence.
- **Operational improvement** - ongoing monitoring of facilities management sector innovation and continuous improvement support for live contracts.

Sector coverage stated on their site includes defence bidding (estate management, training, equipment support, secure facilities and logistics, UK and international), government bidding (including the RM6232 facilities management framework and Crown Commercial Service frameworks), and commercial and technical bidding (data centres, manufacturing, banking and power generation, including nuclear).

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** bid support under the RM6232 Crown Commercial Service facilities management framework (as bid consultancy, not framework supplier).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; signatory to the Armed Forces Covenant (September 2025).
