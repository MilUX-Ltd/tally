# NMS UK

> UK-based manufacturer of Protected Patrol Vehicles for the British Army and export markets.

## What they do

NMS UK, the UK arm of Nurol Makina, is a UK-based supplier of Protected Mobility Vehicles combining proven vehicle expertise with British engineering and manufacturing capability. The company manufactures combat patrol, command and control, ambulance and specialist vehicle variants, and aims to serve export markets as well as UK Armed Forces requirements. In September 2024 the company opened a new manufacturing facility at Tachbrook Park, Leamington Spa.

## Products and solutions

- **DRAGON 4x4** - Protected Patrol Vehicle, standard variant.
- **DRAGON EWB** - Extended-wheelbase variant of Dragon.
- **NOMAD 4x4** - Tactical light armoured vehicle.
- **LURCHER 4x4** - Light tactical armoured vehicle.
- **NMS EWB 4x4** - Extended-wheelbase tactical armoured vehicle.
- **Mission Solutions range** - Combat 4x4, Combat Support 4x4 and Combat Service Support 4x4 variants.
