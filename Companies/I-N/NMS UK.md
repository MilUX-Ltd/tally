# NMS UK

> UK-based manufacturer of Protected Patrol Vehicles for the British Army and export markets.

## What they do

NMS UK is established as a UK-based supplier of Protected Patrol Vehicles (PPVs), combining proven PPV expertise with British engineering and manufacturing capability. The company manufactures combat patrol, command and control, ambulance and specialist vehicle variants, and aims to serve export markets as well as UK Armed Forces requirements.
