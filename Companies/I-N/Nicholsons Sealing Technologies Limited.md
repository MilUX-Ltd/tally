# Nicholsons Sealing Technologies Limited

> Manufacturer of precision resilient metal seals, gaskets and ancillary components for extreme-environment applications, with NADCAP and aerospace quality accreditations.

## What they do

Nicholsons Sealing Technologies designs and manufactures resilient metal seals and gaskets capable of withstanding extreme compression, temperature and pressure. Based in Stanley, County Durham, it offers a complete engineering service from prototype through to full-volume production, covering standard, optimised and bespoke sealing solutions. In-house capabilities include machining, rolling, forming, heat treatment and chemical processing.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, AS9100 series, IATF 16949, NADCAP
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
