# Nicholsons Sealing Technologies Limited

> Manufacturer of precision resilient metal seals, gaskets and ancillary components for extreme-environment applications, with NADCAP and aerospace quality accreditations.

## What they do

Nicholsons Sealing Technologies designs and manufactures resilient, high-temperature metal seals and gaskets, headquartered in Stanley, County Durham, with design, sales and manufacturing sites across Europe and the USA. It offers a complete engineering service from design and prototyping through to full-volume production, covering standard, optimised and bespoke sealing solutions. Its seals handle media ranging from helium gas to heavy oil, across environments from cryogenic to high temperature and vacuum to high pressure. Sectors served, as stated on its own site, include aerospace, automotive, turbochargers, oil and gas, power generation, nuclear and medical applications. In-house capabilities include machining, rolling, forming, heat treatment and chemical processing.

## Products and solutions

- **Corruseals and Reflexseals** - crush-type gaskets using a triangular sealing corrugation, in single or multi-ply construction.
- **Corruplus** - high-temperature, low-load sealing solution for small cavities.
- **Flexible Metal Seals, C-Seals, Boss Seals, E-Seals** - standard metal seal ranges for extreme-environment sealing.
- **Special Products range** - machined seals, formed seals, pipe-end seals, bellows, complex assemblies, shims, baffles, clamps and clips.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015, AS9100D, IATF 16949:2016, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, Nadcap (Chemical Processing, Non-conventional Machining, Welding).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
