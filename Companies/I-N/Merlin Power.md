# Merlin Power

> Designer and manufacturer of electrical products and systems for defence vehicles, from silent watch solutions to custom battery packs and high-current EW power supply.

## What they do

Merlin Power designs, manufactures, supplies and installs electrical products and systems for military ground vehicles, meeting MIL-STD and DEF-STD requirements. Products are deployed across platforms including Abrams, Bradley, Stryker, Jackal and CV-90, covering silent watch capability, platform reliability and power supply for modern electronic warfare systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Silent watch power systems** - solutions to extend silent watch periods on military platforms.
- **Custom battery solutions** - bespoke battery packs built to military specifications.
- **High-current power supply** - power delivery systems for EW and other high-demand payloads.
