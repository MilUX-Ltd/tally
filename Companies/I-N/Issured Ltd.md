# Issured Ltd

> Defence and security management consultancy offering C2, cyber, intelligence, and digital transformation services to government and defence customers.

## What they do

Issured provides management consultancy and professional services to the defence and security sector, with capabilities spanning command and control, counter-terrorism intelligence, security consultancy, cyber security, data analysis, and organisational design. The company also delivers training in engineering, security, software, and systems, and holds a range of government-relevant accreditations.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence and security consultancy** - Strategic and operational consultancy across C2, intelligence, and security disciplines.
- **Cyber security and systems integrity** - Cyber advisory and technical assurance services.
- **Digital and software services** - Data analysis, software development and optimisation, and ICT.
- **Training** - Courses in engineering, security, software, and systems for defence and government clients.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 27001:2022, ISO 22301, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
