# Key-Tech Electronic Systems

> Scottish electronics contract manufacturer specialising in low-to-medium volume, high-mix production for aerospace, defence and security.

## What they do

Key-Tech provides electronic contract manufacturing (ECM) services from its facility in Fife, covering PCB assembly (SMT, through-hole and mixed technology), box build, system integration, cable and harness assembly, prototyping, NPI support, and inspection and test. It focuses on complex, mission-critical applications where reliability and full traceability are essential.

## Products and solutions

- **PCBA and box build** - Surface-mount, through-hole and mixed-technology PCB assembly through to full box-build and system integration.
- **Cable and wire harness assembly** - Custom harness assembly for defence and aerospace applications.
- **Prototyping and NPI** - New product introduction support from prototype through to low-volume production.

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 13485
