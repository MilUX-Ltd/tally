> Space systems engineering consultancy delivering modular, cloud-native architectures for mission-critical space programmes.

## What they do

NuSpace Engineering specialises in designing modular, scalable, and interoperable space systems that move away from monolithic approaches. The company combines rigorous systems engineering with modern software delivery practices to produce space architectures capable of rapid, low-risk capability evolution.
