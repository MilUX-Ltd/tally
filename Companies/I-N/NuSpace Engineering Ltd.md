# NuSpace Engineering Ltd

> Space systems engineering consultancy delivering modular, cloud-native architectures for mission-critical space programmes, based in Reading.

## What they do

NuSpace describes itself as a highly specialised micro-SME founded on more than 25 years of combined expertise in space systems and software engineering. It applies agile development approaches, cloud technologies and space systems engineering expertise to create space architectures that are modular, scalable and interoperable, in place of traditional monolithic approaches. The company states its mission as helping to revolutionise the UK space sector by applying best-in-class technologies and modern engineering practices to space systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Space systems engineering** - systems engineering expertise drawn from experience across the commercial and government space sector.
- **Agile software development** - development patterns for rapidly building and prototyping systems.
- **Cloud solutions** - microservices, Kubernetes orchestration and infrastructure-as-code to build secure, automated and scalable space systems.
- **Novel mission systems** - event-driven architectures for next-generation space architectures.
