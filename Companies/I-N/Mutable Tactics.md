# Mutable Tactics
> UK defence-tech startup founded in August 2024, developing AI decision-layer software for coordinated autonomy of uncrewed defence systems.

## What they do
Mutable Tactics builds software described as a "decision-layer" that sits between a human operator and multiple uncrewed systems, allowing air, maritime and ground platforms to coordinate and make local decisions in communications-denied or GPS-denied environments. The company's stated aim is to move beyond the model of one operator controlling one uncrewed system, enabling a single operator to direct multiple platforms at once.

## Products and solutions
- **Decision-layer software** - AI-enabled coordination software for uncrewed aerial, maritime and ground systems operating with degraded communications or navigation.

## Funding
- Closed a pre-seed funding round of approximately £1.6 million ($2.1 million / €1.8 million) in March 2026, led by Seraphim Space with participation from the UK's National Security Strategic Investment Fund, Koro, Entrepreneurs First and Transpose.
