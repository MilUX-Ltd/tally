# Insight Direct (UK) Ltd

> UK arm of global IT solutions provider Insight Enterprises, supplying hardware, software, and managed services including to defence and government customers.

## What they do

Insight Direct (UK) provides IT hardware, software, and services from its Sheffield base, acting as a single-source technology partner for public sector and commercial customers. Its ADS-listed capabilities include ICT hardware, systems integration, communications systems, and security consultancy, with ISO 27001 and Cyber Essentials Plus certification.
