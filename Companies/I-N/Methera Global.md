# Methera Global

> Secure and resilient satellite communications underpinned by space domain awareness.

## What they do

Methera Global develops secure and resilient satellite communications infrastructure for dual-use civilian and defence networks and critical national infrastructure. Its systems are underpinned by space domain awareness, enabling organisations to operate in an increasingly contested domain. The company is based at Harwell Campus in Oxfordshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Satellite communications** - secure, resilient connectivity for contested environments.
- **Space domain awareness** - situational awareness underpinning the network.
