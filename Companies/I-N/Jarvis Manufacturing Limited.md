# Jarvis Manufacturing Limited

> Bespoke textile manufacturer producing military and defence-specification soft goods from its factory in Eastbourne.

## What they do

Jarvis Manufacturing designs and produces bespoke textile products for defence and wider applications from a dedicated facility in Eastbourne employing around 28 staff. It describes itself as a one-stop shop for textile products, able to manufacture items ranging from a small lanyard to a large vehicle cover, combining current textile technology with over 40 years of experience. The company's origins are in fashion belt manufacturing before moving into military helmets and wider defence soft goods; it now serves military, medical, emergency services, law enforcement and commercial customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Vehicle covers** - Bespoke textile covers for military and specialist vehicles.
- **Lanyards and soft components** - Manufactured textile accessories for military and commercial use.
- **PPE and safety textiles** - Personal protective equipment and associated soft goods, including soft helmets.
- **Load-carrying equipment** - Tactical ops bags, bergens, heavy-duty rucksacks, camo daysacks, holdalls, tool bags, utility and ammo pouches, haversacks, radio pouches, tactical carriers and rifle cases.
- **Design and manufacturing services** - product design, development and prototyping, UK cutting, sewing, hardware fitting, printing and embroidery with no minimum order quantity, plus offshore sourcing and supply chain management for higher-volume orders.
- **Contract cutting** - CNC cutting of webbing, plastics, foam, Velcro and PVC.

Fabric and finishing capabilities stated by the company include rot and fungal resistance, flame retardancy, UV stability, infrared reflectivity and water-resistant treatments for extreme climate conditions.
