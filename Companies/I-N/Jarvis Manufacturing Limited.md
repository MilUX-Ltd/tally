# Jarvis Manufacturing Limited

> Bespoke textile manufacturer producing military and defence-specification soft goods from its factory in Eastbourne.

## What they do

Jarvis Manufacturing designs and produces bespoke textile products for defence and wider applications from a dedicated facility in Eastbourne employing around 28 staff. The company's capability spans lanyards, vehicle covers, PPE, and other soft-goods components, serving both small one-off requirements and extended production runs.

## Products and solutions

- **Vehicle covers** - Bespoke textile covers for military and specialist vehicles.
- **Lanyards and soft components** - Manufactured textile accessories for military and commercial use.
- **PPE and safety textiles** - Personal protective equipment and associated soft goods.
