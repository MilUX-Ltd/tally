# Lloyd's Register

> Independent engineering assurance and professional services organisation providing classification, inspection and certification across maritime, energy and defence sectors.

## What they do

Founded in 1760 as a marine classification society, Lloyd's Register has grown into one of the world's leading providers of professional services for engineering risk management. The organisation delivers classification, compliance, testing, inspection and certification services to operators of complex engineered assets, including naval vessels and defence platforms.
