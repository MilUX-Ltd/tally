# Integrated Training Solutions (Global)

> UK defence training consultancy delivering operational-level command and staff training and Fusion planning practice to military colleges worldwide.

## What they do

Integrated Training Solutions (Global) LLP is an Oxfordshire-based training consultancy delivering command and staff training at the operational level to defence colleges and national military institutions internationally, including in Bangladesh, Nigeria, and South Korea. The team comprises experienced military and civilian planners who advise, train, and mentor on planning in complex, contested, and coalition environments. ITS positions itself as a leading UK Defence and cross-government Fusion planning practice, with courses aimed at officers at major to lieutenant colonel rank. Signed up to the Armed Forces Covenant.

## Products and solutions

- **Command and staff training** - operational-level courses for command and staff colleges and national defence colleges, delivered in the UK and internationally.
- **Fusion planning** - training in joint, coalition, and inter-agency planning across complex and contested environments.
