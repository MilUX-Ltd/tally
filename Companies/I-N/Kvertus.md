# Kvertus

> Ukrainian developer and manufacturer of electronic warfare, SIGINT and counter-UAS systems.

## What they do

Kvertus is a Ukrainian product company specialising in the development and manufacture of signals intelligence and electronic warfare systems designed for UAV detection and suppression. Its products are in service with the Ministry of Defence of Ukraine and are codified to NATO standards. Since the start of the full-scale invasion the company has manufactured over 20,000 EW units deployed across the Ukrainian Defence Forces.

## Products and solutions

- **Atlas** - integrated SIGINT and electronic warfare complex; detects fixed-wing UAVs to around 115 km and neutralises targets to around 30 km. Basis of the "anti-drone wall" concept developed with Brave1.
- **AD Berserk** - mobile EW system on a ground robotic platform; entered serial production July 2025.
- **AD Cerberus** - modular vehicle-mounted EW system protecting vehicles from radio-controlled UAVs.
- **AD Counter FPV** - single-band FPV-drone jammers, including a backpack variant.
- **AD G-6+** - handheld anti-drone jamming gun.
- **MS Azimuth and Aero Azimuth** - electronic intelligence systems locating enemy drone crews, including an airborne variant.
- **LTEJ Mirage** - next-generation EW system presented May 2025.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** products codified to NATO standards and in service with the Ukrainian Ministry of Defence.
- **Memberships:** National Defence Industry Association of Ukraine (NAUDI).
