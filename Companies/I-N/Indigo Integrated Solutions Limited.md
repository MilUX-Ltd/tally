# Indigo Integrated Solutions Limited

> Secure managed IT services and cyber security consultancy serving defence, NHS, and education.

## What they do

Indigo ICS (Indigo Information and Communication Solutions) was incorporated in 2010 and is based in Poole, Dorset. It delivers secure managed IT services and cyber security consultancy to small and medium businesses, education, defence and NHS customers, taking a defence-in-depth approach aligned to national and international cyber security frameworks and NCSC guidance. The company states it is 100% British: it operates entirely from Great Britain, is 100% owned by British citizens, and operates without third-party investment.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure managed IT services** - on-premise and cloud-based managed services for security-sensitive customers.
- **Cyber security consultancy** - consultancy aligned to NCSC guidance and a defence-in-depth security approach.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 27001
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
