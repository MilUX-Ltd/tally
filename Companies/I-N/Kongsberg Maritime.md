# Kongsberg Maritime

> Norwegian maritime technology company providing navigation, automation, sensors and simulation systems for naval, merchant and offshore customers.

## What they do

Kongsberg Maritime supplies navigation, positioning, automation and sensor systems for vessels across naval, merchant marine, offshore, subsea, fisheries and coastal marine markets. The company also provides maritime simulation solutions for crew training.
