# Marshall ADG

> Independent British aerospace and defence company providing mission-critical support services to a global customer base, established in 1909.

## What they do

Marshall ADG is one of the UK's oldest independent aerospace and defence businesses, founded in Cambridge in 1909, and is the aerospace and defence arm of the wider Marshall Group. The company provides deep maintenance, repair, overhaul, and modification services for military and civil aircraft platforms, alongside engineering services, precision manufacturing and through-life support. Its customer base spans armed forces and government operators in the UK and internationally.

## Products and solutions

- **Military MRO** - maintenance, repair and overhaul across military and civil aircraft platforms, including Hercules and Foreign Military Sales support work.
- **Engineering services** - modernisation programmes, capability enhancement, certification and testing, and through-life management for aircraft and land platforms.
- **Precision manufacturing** - complex subsystem assembly, parts and components manufacture, including sonar array production for the Royal Navy under a long-term Thales UK contract.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, AS9100D/EN 9100, UK CAA Part 21G/21J/145 approvals, UK Military Aviation Authority DAOS and MRP Part 145 MAOS, Nadcap (Heat Treating, NDT, Chemical Processing, Welding), Export Control Compliance certification.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
