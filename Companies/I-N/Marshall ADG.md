# Marshall ADG

> Independent British aerospace and defence company providing mission-critical support services to a global customer base, established in 1909.

## What they do

Marshall ADG is one of the UK's oldest independent aerospace and defence businesses, founded in Cambridge in 1909. The company provides deep maintenance, repair, overhaul, and modification services for military and civil aircraft platforms, alongside specialist conversion and integration work. Its customer base spans armed forces and government operators in the UK and internationally.
