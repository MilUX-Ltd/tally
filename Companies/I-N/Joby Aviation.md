# Joby Aviation

> Developer and manufacturer of all-electric vertical take-off and landing aircraft for commercial passenger and defence applications.

## What they do

Joby Aviation designs and manufactures an all-electric eVTOL aircraft capable of carrying a pilot and four passengers at speeds up to 200 mph on a single charge, with a range exceeding 150 miles. The company has UK operations and is engaged with defence customers as part of the broader military air mobility market alongside its commercial passenger service ambitions.
