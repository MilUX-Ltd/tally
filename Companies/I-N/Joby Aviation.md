# Joby Aviation

> Developer and manufacturer of all-electric vertical take-off and landing aircraft for commercial passenger and defence applications.

## What they do

Joby Aviation designs and manufactures an all-electric eVTOL aircraft capable of carrying a pilot and four passengers at speeds up to 200 mph on a single charge, with a range exceeding 150 miles. The company has UK operations, registered in Altrincham, Cheshire, and states it is exploring a new aircraft class for defence applications: a gas turbine hybrid VTOL aircraft designed for low-altitude missions that is optionally piloted, enabling both crewed and fully autonomous operation.

## Products and solutions

- **Joby S4** - the company's core all-electric eVTOL air taxi, piloted, carrying four passengers, in delivery to the US Air Force under the Agility Prime programme.
- **Defence VTOL aircraft (with L3Harris)** - a gas turbine hybrid VTOL aircraft built on the S4 platform, described by Joby as optionally piloted for crewed or fully autonomous operation, with L3Harris providing sensors, effectors, communications and collaborative autonomy integration.
- **Autonomous flight technology** - Joby states the aircraft uses its own SuperPilot autonomous flight technology, following its 2024 acquisition of the autonomy division of Xwing.
