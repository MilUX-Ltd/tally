# MonsterCAM Composite Systems Ltd
> UK defence composites manufacturer based at the Daedalus enterprise zone, specialising in composite materials and component manufacture for the defence sector.

## What they do
MonsterCAM Composite Systems manufactures composite materials and components for defence clients from its site on the Daedalus enterprise zone at Lee-on-the-Solent. The business has veteran involvement at leadership level and is geographically aligned with the Solent and South East defence cluster. It sits within a wider maritime, aviation and defence SME community on the former HMS Daedalus site.
