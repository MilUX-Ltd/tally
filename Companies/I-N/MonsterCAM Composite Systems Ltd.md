# MonsterCAM Composite Systems Ltd

> UK composite tooling and component manufacturer based at the Daedalus enterprise zone, Lee-on-the-Solent, serving the defence, marine and industrial sectors.

## What they do

MonsterCAM Composite Systems Ltd operates a large-scale five-axis CNC machining facility at Daedalus Drive, Lee-on-the-Solent, Hampshire, offering end-to-end composite tooling and component solutions. The company describes itself as specialising in the defence, marine and industrial sectors, building composite components ranging from uncrewed aerial vehicles to submarines. It sits within a wider maritime, aviation and defence SME community on the former HMS Daedalus site, with direct slipway access for transporting oversized work by barge.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC machining** - five-axis CNC machining of patterns, moulds and tooling.
- **Composite mouldings** - carbon fibre and epoxy/resin infusion builds, including pre-preg processes.
- **Metrology** - dimensional inspection and quality verification of composite components.
- **Painting and finishing** - surface finishing for composite parts.
- **Tooling** - design and build of composite tooling for defence and marine programmes.
- Named defence-sector work described by the company includes composite hulls and structures for uncrewed underwater and surface vessels, survey submarines, and minesweeper tooling and build.
