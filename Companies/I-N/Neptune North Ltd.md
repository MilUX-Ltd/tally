# Neptune North Ltd

> Software development and data engineering consultancy serving the defence, financial services, and alternative investment sectors.

## What they do

Neptune North is a technical consultancy providing data engineering, AI, software engineering, and quantitative/economics consultancy to clients in defence, financial services, and alternative investment. It describes two engagement models: augmentation, embedding engineers within a client's existing team, and full consultancy, where Neptune North owns a defined scope of work end-to-end. The company was founded in 2024 as a joint venture between Oliver Wyman and Rokos Capital Management, and delivers from a Newcastle upon Tyne base alongside smaller locations in Spain, the United States and New Zealand.

## Products and solutions

- **Data engineering** - Designing and managing systems for data collection, storage, processing and analysis.
- **AI engineering** - Identifying opportunities for AI implementation and integrating tailored solutions into client business processes.
- **Software engineering** - Design, testing, implementation, deployment and maintenance of software applications and systems.
- **Quants and economists** - Mathematical models, statistics and software development applied to financial markets, risk and opportunity analysis.
