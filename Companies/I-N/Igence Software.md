# Igence Software

> UK supplier of bespoke software and systems engineering to defence, aerospace, maritime and radar sectors.

## What they do

Igence Software is a UK supplier of bespoke software and systems engineering, primarily to defence, aerospace, maritime, radar and adjacent industrial sectors. It delivers custom software for proof of concept, bid support, emulators and simulators, prototypes and demonstrators, control systems and systems integration, and also offers associate and contract engineers. The company has delivered defence software since 1999, citing over 200 defence-related projects with the Armed Services and major defence primes.

## Products and solutions

- **Igence Radar** - radar product and capability (separate site igenceradar.com).
- **Autonomy and autonomous systems** - including autonomous maritime work, such as the BAE Systems P38 Nautomate project.
- **Bespoke software engineering** - control systems, remote ground station control, simulation and training systems, and mapping and GIS systems.
- **Associate Contract Engineers** - supply of engineering contractors to defence and industry.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Delivered on the MOD DART-accredited DISX network to OFFICIAL (including SENSITIVE). Previously held List X certification.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
