# JAG Secure Ltd

> Cyber security and penetration testing consultancy serving defence and government clients.

## What they do

JAG Secure delivers penetration testing and cyber security services with qualifications from CREST, The Cyber Scheme, and Offensive Security (OffSec). The firm offers tailored assessments covering digital infrastructure, data integrity, and security resilience for customers including those in the defence sector.

## Products and solutions

- **Penetration testing** - CREST and Cyber Scheme qualified testing across networks, applications, and systems.
- **Cyber security consultancy** - Tailored assessments of digital infrastructure and security posture.
- **Data analysis and systems integrity** - Software and digital services supporting cyber resilience.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus; CREST; The Cyber Scheme; Offensive Security (OffSec)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
