# Knight Precision Wire Limited

> Specialist manufacturer and stockholder of precision wire, strip metal and resistance wire in a wide range of high-performance alloys.

## What they do

Knight Precision Wire is part of the Knight Group and processes and stocks precision wire, strip metal, tube and electrical resistance wire in nickel alloys, stainless steels, titanium, copper alloys and other special-performance materials for aerospace, defence and industrial applications. Manufacturing processes include wire drawing, strip slitting, recoiling, blanking, straightening and metal stamping.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision wire and resistance wire** - Specialist wire products in nickel chrome, cupro-nickel, titanium and a range of exotic alloys including Inconel and superconductors.
- **Strip metal and tube** - Precision strip slitting and recoiling in carbon spring steel, stainless steel and copper alloys.
- **Electrical components and cabling** - Wire and cable products for electrical interconnect and harnessing applications.
