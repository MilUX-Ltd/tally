# Lionbridge
> Global content solutions partner with over 30 years of defence, aerospace, and maritime experience across technical publications, training content, and AI-data services.
## What they do
Lionbridge supports defence, aerospace, land, marine, digital, and industrial organisations across the full Integrated Product Support (IPS) lifecycle. Services cover technical publication analysis and design, validated publications, training content, AI-data services, and in-service documentation management. The company combines deep domain expertise with global delivery capability and frames its offer around reducing programme risk and accelerating delivery of mission-ready information in complex, multi-stakeholder environments.
## Products and solutions
- **Technical publications** - Creation, management, and delivery of critical technical information across the IPS lifecycle.
- **Training content** - Training material design and production for complex defence programmes.
- **AI-data services** - Data labelling, annotation, and AI training dataset services for defence applications.
