# iDirect

> VSAT satellite communications provider delivering IP-based broadband for critical enterprise and military applications.

## What they do

iDirect develops IP-based satellite communications systems, providing enterprise-quality broadband over VSAT networks for defence and enterprise customers. The company's platforms are used in applications where reliable, high-performance connectivity is required in austere or remote environments.
