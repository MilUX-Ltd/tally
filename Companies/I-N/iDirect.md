# iDirect

> VSAT satellite communications provider delivering IP-based broadband for critical enterprise and military applications.

## What they do

iDirect develops IP-based satellite communications systems, providing enterprise-quality broadband over VSAT networks for defence and enterprise customers. The company's platforms are used in applications where reliable, high-performance connectivity is required in austere or remote environments. The UK entity, ST Engineering iDirect (UK) Limited, is part of the ST Engineering iDirect group, formerly VT iDirect, itself part of Singapore's ST Engineering.

## Products and solutions

- **iDirect Intelligent Platform** - the company's core software-defined VSAT platform for enterprise, government and military connectivity.
- **iQ Series remotes** - software-defined satellite remotes, including the iQ Desktop+ and iQ 200 Series, supporting DVB-S2/S2X.
- **Dialog, Velocity and Evolution hub platforms** - VSAT hub systems for network operators, with Newtec modems compatible across all three.
