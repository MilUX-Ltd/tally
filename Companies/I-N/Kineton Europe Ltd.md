# Kineton Europe Ltd

> Italian engineering and software consultancy with a UK presence, providing design, test and validation services for aerospace, automotive and defence.

## What they do

Founded in 2017, Kineton provides engineering and software solutions across aerospace, automotive and related sectors, with capabilities spanning vehicle electronics, mission software, simulation and modelling, cyber security, data analysis and systems integration. The company works with OEMs and suppliers on system design, testing and validation, and operates offices in the UK, Italy, Germany and the USA.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems design and engineering** - Engineering consultancy and design services for complex aerospace and defence systems.
- **Software development and optimisation** - Software engineering, cyber security and digital services for mission-critical applications.
- **Simulation and modelling** - Simulation services supporting system validation and test.
- **Test and certification** - Testing services to industry standards for aerospace and automotive platforms.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 50001
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
