# Networkology Ltd

> Professional services company delivering networking and cyber capability to defence, government and public sector clients.

## What they do

Networkology provides architecture and engineering services focused on networking and cyber within highly regulated environments, including defence and government. Its offer is structured around three service pillars: networks and communications, data solutions, and performance management. A separate Application Performance Management (APM) division supports end-user experience monitoring, baselining and auditing, troubleshooting, and SLA compliance for the same client base.

## Products and solutions

- **Networks and communications** - Network architecture and engineering delivered through named partner practices including Juniper Networks (including Juniper Mist AI wireless), Allied Telesis, and Teltonika Networks for industrial cellular connectivity.
- **Application Performance Management (APM)** - Proactive monitoring, emulation and testing, traffic management, and digital experience monitoring, built substantially on Splunk Observability and Splunk Enterprise Security.
- **Data solutions** - Data pipeline and observability capability, including named Splunk Edge Hub and Cribl offerings.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** G-Cloud (Crown Commercial Service), Digital Outcomes and Specialists (DOS)
- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001:2022, ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, Cyber Essentials Plus, Splunk Elite Partner, Allied Telesis Platinum Partner
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Armed Forces Covenant signatory, Defence Employer Recognition Scheme Gold
