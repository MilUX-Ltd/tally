# Networkology Ltd

> Professional services company delivering networking and cyber capability to defence, government and public sector clients.

## What they do

Networkology provides architecture and engineering services focused on networking and cyber within highly regulated environments, including defence and government. It also operates an Application and Performance Management division supporting end-user experience for those clients.
