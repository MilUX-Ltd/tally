# Novatech

> UK custom-computing manufacturer building bespoke PCs, servers and rugged COTS hardware, including defence training and simulation systems.

## What they do

Novatech designs, assembles, supports and repairs custom-built computer hardware from its Portsmouth headquarters, serving home, business, education and public-sector customers. Alongside its mainstream PC and server business, it supplies specialist and adaptable hardware including custom rack design and build, high-performance commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) PCs and servers, and ruggedised solutions. Its engineering team delivers complex rack and compute systems that power simulators for defence and aviation customers, and the company states it has supplied engineered COTS hardware into the MOD through primes including Thales, BAE Systems and Airbus Defence and Space.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence, Training and Simulation** - a dedicated sector division supplying engineered computing and simulation systems.
- **Motion Sims** - motion-simulator systems for training, including powerboat and RHIB trainers.
- **Custom Server and Rack Solutions** - bespoke rack design and build, configurable tower and rackmount servers.
- **Design and Engineering** - in-house engineering of COTS PCs, servers and ruggedised solutions, including high-performance AI and deep-learning compute workstations.
