# Mbryonics

> Irish photonics company developing advanced optical interconnect technology for space, satellite, and high-performance computing applications.

## What they do

Mbryonics specialises in low-latency optical interconnectivity from millimetre scale to inter-planetary scale. Target applications include LEO satellite broadband constellations, 6G networks, quantum computing infrastructure, AI and HPC platforms, edge networks, and autonomous transport systems.
