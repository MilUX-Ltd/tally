# Mbryonics

> Irish photonics company building free-space optical communications terminals and ground infrastructure for satellite and space applications.

## What they do

Mbryonics designs and manufactures free-space optical communications (FSOC) systems, optical ground terminals and adaptive optics systems, describing its work as building an end-to-end optical solution connecting terrestrial networks to orbital constellations across LEO, MEO, GEO and cislunar space. Its photonics technologies are deployed on satellite systems for secure communications links, quantum key distribution, data relay, satellite optical mesh networks, optical feeder links, and direct-to-Earth and deep space missions. The company also provides rapidly deployable, high-throughput free-space optical links for terrestrial last-mile and 5G+ network applications. Mbryonics was selected as the optical terminal provider for the US Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency's Space-BACN programme, developing the optical terminal, including the space telescope, pointing/acquisition/tracking system and optical amplifiers, to support interconnection between government and commercial satellite systems.

## Products and solutions

- **STARCOM** - optical terminal product line for inter-satellite and Earth links.
- **STARGATE** - optical ground station connecting space and terrestrial networks.
- **STARLIGHT** - integrated digital optical modem.
- **TeraBIX** - radiation-hardened silicon photonic transceiver for high-speed, low-power data transmission.
- **PakPIC** - high-precision packaging solutions for photonic integrated circuits.
