# Nasmyth Bulwell

> Precision aerospace manufacturer producing machined parts, kits, airframe assemblies and sub-assemblies from its facility in Pinxton, Nottinghamshire.

## What they do

Nasmyth Bulwell specialises in precision machining of steel, aluminium, nimonics and super alloys using up to 5-axis CAD/CAM technology, producing quality-assured components and assemblies for aerospace and defence customers. Capabilities include fine tolerance grinding, water jet cutting, heat treatment, anodising, non-destructive testing, and paint and plasma finishing.
