# Nasmyth Bulwell

> Precision aerospace manufacturer producing machined parts, kits, airframe assemblies and sub-assemblies from its facility in Pinxton, Nottinghamshire.

## What they do

Nasmyth Bulwell specialises in precision machining of steel, aluminium, nimonics and super alloys using up to 5-axis CAD/CAM technology, producing quality-assured components and assemblies for aerospace and defence customers. Capabilities include fine tolerance grinding, water jet cutting, heat treatment, anodising, non-destructive testing, and paint and plasma finishing. The company operates as part of the Nasmyth Group, drawing on the group's supply chain management to deliver airframe assemblies and kitted parts directly to customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision parts, kits and airframe assemblies** - machined and assembled components and sub-assemblies for aerospace and defence platforms.
- **Wet-assembly aerostructure components** - captive nut installation, heat-and-shrink bush installation, and proof-load testing.
- **Welded assemblies** - coded and approved welding for fuel pipes, ground support equipment, satellite drill fixtures, and military vehicle and lightweight rotary aircraft products.
- **Electro-mechanical assemblies** - flight simulator and flat panel trainer seat systems, and repair and overhaul of flight column and throttle box control assemblies.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100 Rev D, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, NADCAP Heat Treatment, NADCAP Non-Destructive Testing, UKAS BS/EN ISO 17025 (calibration systems).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
