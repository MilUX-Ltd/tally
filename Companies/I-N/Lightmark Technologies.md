# Lightmark Technologies
> UK deep-tech startup building capture-layer authentication for visual media, using optical fingerprinting to prove provenance at the moment of recording.
## What they do
Lightmark Technologies develops capture-layer content authentication. The technology embeds verifiable optical fingerprints into a lit environment using controlled light modulation, so cameras present record an invisible authenticity signal at the moment of capture. Verification is binary: the optical signature is either present or absent. This approach sits earlier in the content lifecycle than detection, watermarking, or C2PA metadata, all of which act after the file exists. Dual-use applications are in development with Bifrost Defence. Lightmark is the flagship deep-tech initiative within GIF Solutions LTD, which has traded since 2020. A patent is pending.
## Products and solutions
- **Optical fingerprinting** - Light-modulation system embedding authenticity signals into video and imagery at the moment of capture.
- **Verification infrastructure** - Real-time decoding and verification of captured optical signals.
