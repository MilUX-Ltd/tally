# Kingsbury UK

> Machine tool supplier with over 60 years of experience providing German-built equipment to UK manufacturing industries including aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Kingsbury UK supplies precision machine tools from leading German manufacturers, covering turning machines, vertical milling, multi-axis grinding, and related equipment. The company provides applications support and a trained technical service team, serving industrial customers across sectors that include aerospace and defence.
