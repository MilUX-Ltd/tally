# iCOMAT

> Bristol composites manufacturer whose Rapid Tow Shearing technology steers fibres without defects for aerospace, defence and automotive parts.

## What they do

iCOMAT is a manufacturer of advanced composite components for the aerospace, defence and automotive industries. It spun out of the University of Bristol in 2019 to industrialise its proprietary Rapid Tow Shearing (RTS) process, which steers composite fibres without defects to enable lighter, optimised structures at high production rate. The company operates a production facility, Factory One, and has been building a further automated facility in Gloucester.

## Products and solutions

- **Rapid Tow Shearing (RTS)** - proprietary fibre-steering process using in-plane shearing rather than bending, enabling defect-free curved fibre paths for optimised composite parts.
- **Advanced composite components** - turnkey supply from tooling to finished parts for aerospace, defence and automotive customers.
