# Morshead Consulting Ltd

> Defence analysis, experimentation and programme transformation consultancy working with Dstl, the UK Armed Forces and industry partners.

## What they do

Morshead Consulting delivers operational analysis, experimentation and transformation services across Land, C4ISR and Cyber domains. The company supports Dstl and the UK Armed Forces in evidencing the case for capability change and in exploring new technologies and ways of working.
