# Morshead Consulting Ltd

> Defence analysis, experimentation and programme transformation consultancy working with Dstl, the UK Armed Forces and industry partners.

## What they do

Morshead Consulting is an independent consultancy delivering analysis, experimentation and programme transformation to Dstl, the UK Armed Forces and industry partners, working across Land, C4ISR and Cyber domains. The company states it was founded in 2020 to provide high-quality analysis, experimentation and transformation for the UK MOD, industry and government agencies, positioning itself as a supplier of advice and support rather than technology or product.

## Products and solutions

- **Operational analysis** - concept assessment, business cases and cost-benefit analysis, and Defence Lines of Development (DLOD) analysis for programmes spanning command and control, electronic warfare and one-way-effector systems, for customers including Dstl and Army HQ.
- **Experimentation** - warfare development, modelling and simulation, and wargaming support, including concept of employment and tactics, techniques and procedures development, for customers including Dstl, Army HQ and the Land Warfare Centre.
- **Programme transformation** - programme start-up advice, benefits realisation, enterprise architecture and embedded "customer friend" support for defence transformation programmes.
