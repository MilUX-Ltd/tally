# Northrop Grumman Corporation

> Global US aerospace and defence prime operating across air, space, defence and cyberspace, with established UK operations.

## What they do

Northrop Grumman is a global aerospace and defence technology company working across space, aeronautics, defence and cyberspace. It designs, develops and manufactures systems for national security and commercial customers across the air, land, sea, space and cyber domains, and is one of the largest contractors to the US federal government. It is listed on the New York Stock Exchange under ticker NOC, headquartered in Falls Church, Virginia, and maintains UK operations through Northrop Grumman UK Limited.

## Products and solutions

- **Aeronautics Systems** - manned and unmanned air systems, including military aircraft, autonomous systems and aerospace structures; lead contractor for the B-21 Raider next-generation stealth bomber.
- **Defense Systems** - modernisation and sustainment, battle command systems, directed energy, tactical weapons and armaments.
- **Mission Systems** - cyber-secure, software-defined systems including sensors, radars, electronic warfare and C4ISR.
- **Space Systems** - space and launch systems for national security, civil and commercial customers.
- **UK operations** - cyber and technology facilities in Cheltenham, London, Manchester and New Malden; the New Malden site specialises in marine navigation systems, radars, integrated bridge systems and integrated platform management systems.
