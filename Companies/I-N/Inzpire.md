# Inzpire

> Defence managed services and mission systems company, staffed by veterans from the Royal Navy, British Army, and Royal Air Force.

## What they do

Inzpire supplies the UK MOD with managed services and mission systems, developed by personnel with front-line military flying and ground experience. Their GECO family of tablet-based mission systems provides capability for pilots and dismounted soldiers, focused on improving flight safety and operational performance.

## Products and solutions

- **GECO Mission Systems** - Tablet-based mission management platform for pilots and dismounted soldiers; supports flight safety and operational performance enhancement.
