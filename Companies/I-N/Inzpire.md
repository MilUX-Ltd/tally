# Inzpire

> Defence managed services and mission systems company, staffed by veterans from the Royal Navy, British Army, and Royal Air Force.

## What they do

Inzpire supplies the UK Ministry of Defence with operational training and mission systems, developed and delivered by personnel with front-line military flying and ground experience. Around three-quarters of its revenue comes from airborne training and evaluation services, primarily for the Royal Air Force, with the balance from the sale of aviation mission system products ranging from standalone tablets to full mission support systems integrated with aircraft avionics. Inzpire is a joint-services organisation founded in 2005, with QinetiQ as majority shareholder following a strategic investment completed in 2026.

## Products and solutions

- **GECO Mission Support System** - Fully integrated mission support system combining software, purpose-built hardware and customer service, covering the full military air mission cycle from pre-flight planning and briefing through in-flight execution to post-mission debrief; in service with UK armed forces since 2009 and used on over 22 fixed-wing and helicopter types.
- **Flying training services** - Rotary, fixed-wing and UAS operational training in synthetic and live environments, including adversary air services and Qualified Weapons Instructor training support.
- **Collective training** - Live, synthetic and blended live-synthetic collective training exercises across air, land and maritime domains.
- **Synthetic and simulation systems** - Development of synthetic training devices and deployable simulators, including a mixed reality gunnery trainer prototype for the UK MOD.
