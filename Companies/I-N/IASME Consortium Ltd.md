# IASME Consortium Ltd

> UK cyber security certification body, sole delivery partner for the National Cyber Security Centre's Cyber Essentials and Cyber Advisor schemes, and delivery partner for the MOD's Defence Cyber Certification scheme.

## What they do

IASME Consortium Ltd is a UK certification and accreditation body, not a practitioner consultancy: it designs and administers cyber security certification schemes and licenses a network of over 300 Certification Bodies and Assured Service Providers who carry out assessments and offer advice against those schemes. IASME is the sole delivery partner for the National Cyber Security Centre's (NCSC) Cyber Essentials and Cyber Advisor schemes, and one of two delivery partners for the NCSC's Cyber Incident Exercising and Cyber Incident Response (Standard Level) industry assurance schemes. Through its network, IASME also delivers the Defence Cyber Certification (DCC), developed jointly with the Ministry of Defence as an organisation-wide certification providing assurance of a supplier's cyber resilience across the UK defence supply chain.

Beyond its NCSC and MOD-linked roles, IASME runs its own IASME Cyber Assurance standard (formerly IASME Governance), designed as an accessible alternative to ISO 27001 for small and medium-sized organisations, plus IoT device certification schemes (IASME IoT Cyber Baseline and IoT Cyber Assurance) and the Maritime Cyber Baseline scheme for commercial vessels. It is also a Delivery Partner for the Civil Aviation Authority's ASSURE scheme. IASME Consortium Ltd is registered at Companies House as a private limited company (number 07897132, incorporated 2012), headquartered in Malvern with additional offices in Belfast and Dalgety Bay, near Edinburgh. IASME Community (previously the UK Cyber Security Forum) is IASME's separate community interest and social enterprise arm, running cyber security training and workforce-entry programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber Essentials / Cyber Advisor** - delivered on behalf of the NCSC; Cyber Essentials is the UK Government's baseline certification against common cyber attacks.
- **Defence Cyber Certification (DCC)** - developed with the MOD; an organisation-wide certification scheme for the UK defence supplier base.
- **IASME Cyber Assurance** - IASME's own flagship standard (formerly IASME Governance), covering cyber security, privacy, and data protection for organisations of all sizes.
- **IASME IoT Cyber Baseline / IoT Cyber Assurance** - certification for internet-connected devices, aligned to the Product Security and Telecommunications Infrastructure Act 2022 and the ETSI EN 303 645 standard.
- **Maritime Cyber Baseline** - cyber security certification for commercial marine vessels and operators worldwide.
- **Civil Aviation Authority ASSURE** - delivery partner role providing an audit mechanism for cyber security risk in the aviation sector.
