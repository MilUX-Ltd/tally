# IASME Consortium Ltd

> UK cyber security certification body operating a network of over 900 accredited assessors, and the delivery partner for the MoD's Defence Cyber Certification scheme.

## What they do

IASME works with organisations of all sizes to improve and demonstrate their cyber security posture, operating through a national network of certified assessors. It also certifies connected devices and commercial maritime vessels globally, and developed the Defence Cyber Certification in partnership with the MoD to strengthen cyber resilience across the UK defence supply chain.
