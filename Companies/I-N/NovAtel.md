# NovAtel

> Hexagon brand providing assured GNSS positioning, navigation and GPS anti-jam technology for defence, aerospace and maritime applications.

## What they do

NovAtel, part of Hexagon's positioning division, develops and manufactures GNSS receivers, GPS anti-jam technology and positioning systems for defence and regulated sectors. The UK office is based in Aberdeen, Scotland, serving military and defence customers from the Veripos House facility.

## Products and solutions

- **GAJT (GPS Anti-Jam Technology)** - protects against jamming and interference to maintain GPS position, navigation and timing availability.
- **OEM7 GNSS Receivers** - multi-constellation, multi-frequency receivers with field-upgradeable software for defence and precision applications.
- **GRIT (GNSS Resilience and Integrity Technology)** - technology for maintaining GNSS integrity and resilience in contested environments.
- **Navigation and Mapping Systems** - end-to-end navigation, GPS and surveying solutions.
