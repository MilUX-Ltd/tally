# Lyonlight Consulting Ltd

> Boutique consultancy providing advisory, analysis, technical, and commercial services to the space and defence sectors, with a focus on dual-use applications.

## What they do

Lyonlight offers strategic advisory and analysis for organisations operating in or seeking entry to the space and defence market, including funding strategy, technical requirements for dual-use capabilities, and commercialisation of space-sector products and services. The firm works across defence consultancy, space consultancy, and market research.
