# ISTEC Services Ltd

> Manufacturer of precision-engineered small-arm and medium-calibre weapon mounts and weapon ancillaries for the defence sector.

## What they do

ISTEC Services designs and manufactures weapon mounting systems and ancillary equipment for small arms and medium-calibre platforms. The company supplies high-quality, precision-engineered products to the defence market from its base in Hertfordshire.
