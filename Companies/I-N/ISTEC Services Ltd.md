# ISTEC Services Ltd

> UK manufacturer of precision-engineered small-arm and medium-calibre weapon mounts and weapon ancillaries for land, sea and air platforms.

## What they do

ISTEC Services Limited, formed in 1989, is the UK's principal supplier of high-quality, precision-engineered small-arm and medium-calibre gun mounts and weapon ancillaries. The company designs, manufactures and supports complex weapon integration solutions for military platforms, specialising in fully integrating a customer's existing inventory of crew-served weapons, either retrospectively onto vehicle platforms or by design at the concept stage. ISTEC states its mounts and ancillaries are operated by users worldwide and are supplied to major defence contractors and directly to the UK Ministry of Defence and US Department of Defense.

## Products and solutions

- **Ring mounts** - a range of mounts for 5.56mm, 7.62mm, 12.7mm and 40mm Grenade Machine Gun weapon systems.
- **Enhanced Close Range Protection (ECRP)** - shield wall system providing increased physical protection on vehicle platforms.
- **Smoke Discharger Systems (SDS)** - vehicle-mounted smoke discharger equipment.
- **Softmount systems** - including the Mk2 Softmount for the M2 Heavy Machine Gun.
- **Universal Gun Mounts (UGM)** - traverse control mechanisms and swing arm systems for weapon platforms, including for the Supacat Jackal 3 Extenda.
