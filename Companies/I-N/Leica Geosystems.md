# Leica Geosystems

> Part of Hexagon, Leica Geosystems provides precision measurement, survey and geospatial solutions used across defence, engineering and construction sectors globally.

## What they do

Leica Geosystems, a Hexagon company, develops and manufactures instruments and software for measurement and survey, including total stations, laser scanners, GNSS systems and geospatial software. Its products are used in defence, infrastructure, construction and public safety applications.
