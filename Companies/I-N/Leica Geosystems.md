# Leica Geosystems

> Part of Hexagon, Leica Geosystems provides precision measurement, survey and geospatial solutions used across defence, engineering and construction sectors globally.

## What they do

Leica Geosystems, a Hexagon company, develops and manufactures instruments and software for measurement, survey and geospatial data capture, including total stations, laser scanners, GNSS systems and reality-capture and mapping software. The company states that geospatial intelligence and information are key enablers for military forces, and markets a defence and situational-awareness offer built around its reality-capture, mobile-mapping and GIS software portfolio.

## Products and solutions

- **GNSS systems** - survey-grade GNSS antennas and receivers, including tilt-compensation and integrated IMU, with centimetre-level positioning; supported by the HxGN SmartNet GNSS correction network.
- **Total stations and laser scanners** - robotic total stations and 3D laser scanners for precision survey and reality capture.
- **Geospatial and mapping software** - software for processing, visualising and disseminating geospatial data, marketed into defence situational-awareness use cases alongside public safety and construction.
