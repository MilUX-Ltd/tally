# Marlborough Engineering

> Precision engineering manufacturer supplying aerospace and defence companies worldwide, with 35 years of experience in CNC machining and weapons component production.

## What they do

Marlborough Engineering produces precision-machined parts and one-off specialist components across a range of materials, serving primary aerospace and defence customers directly and as a sub-tier supplier. The company's weapons manufacturing capability covers guns, ammunition and accessories, knives and bayonets, and targets and training equipment, alongside broader aerospace structures work. It operates in the UK, US, Asia, and mainland Europe.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC machining and fabrication** - five-axis, lathe, and milling machining capability in aluminium, titanium, nickel, and exotic alloys, with welding and surface treatment.
- **Weapons components** - production of guns, ammunition and accessories, bladed weapons, and training targets for defence customers.
