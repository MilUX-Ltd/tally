# ITSUS Consulting Limited

> Cardiff-based network and cyber security consultancy delivering secure communications and digital transformation programmes for defence, aerospace and public sector clients.

## What they do

ITSUS Consulting, founded in 2008, provides network and cyber security services for clients operating mission-critical communications systems. The company collaborates with international aerospace, defence and public sector organisations to deliver complex digital transformation programmes, working across three broad capability areas: networks, cyber security, and data science. ITSUS states it is a validated cyber security supplier to HMG. Named public-sector and defence clients include the Ministry of Defence, Dstl, Leonardo, General Dynamics, and Airbus Defence and Space.

## Products and solutions

- **Network engineering** - design, build and support of secure communications networks, including next-generation firewall deployments.
- **Cyber security services** - services covering zero trust architecture and cyber security culture, delivered to HMG-validated standards.
- **Data science** - analysis and interpretation of complex data to provide actionable insights.
- **Technology partnerships** - certified partner for Cisco, Palo Alto Networks and Keysight Technologies, delivering network security solutions built on these vendors' products.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered.
- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 9001, ISO 27001:2022.
