# Newbury Electronics Ltd

> UK PCB manufacturer with over 65 years of experience, serving defence and aerospace clients from facilities in Newbury, Berkshire.

## What they do

Newbury Electronics designs, fabricates and assembles printed circuit boards at its manufacturing plants in Newbury. It holds aerospace and defence quality approvals including AS9100D, EN9100 and JOSCAR registration, and offers contract electronics manufacturing across a broad range of complexity levels.

## Products and solutions

- **Flexible and flexi-rigid PCBs** - Flexible and rigid-flex circuit boards for space-constrained or high-vibration applications.
- **Multi-layer PCB fabrication** - Boards from one to twelve layers, including sequential build-up with buried and blind vias.
- **Aluminium-based copper-clad and controlled impedance PCBs** - Metal-core boards for thermal management and boards built to controlled impedance specifications.
- **Box build and mechanical assembly** - Full box build, conformal coating (selective, CNC and automated), laser-cut solder paste stencils, laser micro-machining, in-house X-ray inspection, and CNC de-panelling.
- **Newbury Innovation** - A separate division covering electronic, firmware, software and enclosure product development alongside core PCB manufacture.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100D, EN9100, JISQ9100, ISO 9001, ISO 13485, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, ISO 27001, IPC-610-A Class 3, JOSCAR, Underwriters Laboratory, Cyber Essentials Plus, Apple MFI
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
