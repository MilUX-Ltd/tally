# Newbury Electronics Ltd

> UK PCB manufacturer with over 65 years of experience, serving defence and aerospace clients from facilities in Newbury, Berkshire.

## What they do

Newbury Electronics designs, fabricates and assembles printed circuit boards at its manufacturing plants in Newbury. It holds aerospace and defence quality approvals including AS9100D, EN9100 and JOSCAR registration, and offers contract electronics manufacturing across a broad range of complexity levels.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100D, EN9100, JISQ9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, JOSCAR, Underwriters Laboratory, Cyber Essentials Plus, IPC specialist certifications
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
