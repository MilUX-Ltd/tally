# Nvisiq Ltd
> UK defence-tech company developing passive 3D sensor technology for autonomous vehicles, ISR and covert surveillance applications.

## What they do
Nvisiq develops passive 3D sensor systems positioned for use in autonomous vehicles, intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance, and covert surveillance roles. The company is a member of the D3IP community at the Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park. Further company details were not publicly available at the time of capture.
