# Moyola Precision Engineering

> Precision machining and assembly manufacturer supplying structural components, aerospace platform parts and weapons sub-assemblies to the defence and aerospace sectors.

## What they do

Moyola manufactures and assembles precision components and sub-assemblies using 5-axis and 6-axis CNC machining across aluminium alloys, titanium, steel and exotic alloys. The company supplies fuselage and wing structures, nacelles, mechanical sub-assemblies and weapons components including missile and rocket parts and infrared sights.

## Products and solutions

- **5-axis precision machining** - Components up to 3.8m x 2.5m for aerospace and defence platforms.
- **Weapons sub-assemblies** - Missiles, rockets, submunitions and infrared/thermal sighting components.
- **Platform structures** - Fuselage, wing and nacelle subcomponents for aerospace programmes.
