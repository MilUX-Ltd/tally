# Infrastar Ltd

> Gloucestershire-based IT and security specialist providing cyber, communications, and defence-grade technology solutions to SMEs, public sector, and government bodies.

## What they do

Infrastar provides computer infrastructure, networking, and security solutions from its base in Gloucestershire's cyber hub. Its ADS capability set spans TEMPEST, biometrics, unmanned systems, cyber security, software modelling, and simulation, serving defence and government customers alongside the broader public sector.

## Products and solutions

- **TEMPEST solutions** - Specialist emissions security consultancy and solutions.
- **Cyber security services** - Cyber Essentials Plus-certified security consultancy and systems integrity work.
- **Unmanned systems** - Capability in UAS-related technology and biometrics/imagery.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** ITAR approval; Cyber Essentials Plus
