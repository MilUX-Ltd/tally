# IPC Mouldings

> Injection moulding and engineered assembly manufacturer serving the aerospace and defence supply chain from Northern Ireland.

## What they do

IPC Mouldings designs and manufactures injection moulded parts and engineered assemblies for demanding aerospace and defence applications. Capabilities span mould tool design, CNC machining, and injection moulding in a range of engineering polymers.

## Products and solutions

- **Injection moulded components** - Platform interior parts including mechanical components, seating elements, lighting fixtures, storage and stowage systems, and sanitary equipment.
- **CNC machined parts** - Milling and router machining in metals and polymers.
- **Engineered assemblies** - Adhesive-bonded and fastened sub-assemblies for aerospace platforms.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, SC21 Silver OE, SC21 Gold
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
