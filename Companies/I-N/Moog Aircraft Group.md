# Moog Aircraft Group

> Manufacturer of precision motion control products and systems for aerospace and defence platforms.

## What they do

Moog Aircraft Group is the aerospace and defence operating division of Moog Inc., a US-headquartered precision motion control company. The UK operation at Tewkesbury, Gloucestershire, supports aerospace business development and manufacturing for aircraft and defence platforms.
