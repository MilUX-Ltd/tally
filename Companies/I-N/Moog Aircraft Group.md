# Moog Aircraft Group

> Manufacturer of flight control systems and precision motion control products for military and commercial aircraft.

## What they do

Moog Aircraft Group is the aerospace and defence operating group of Moog Inc, a US-headquartered precision motion control company. The group describes itself as a designer, manufacturer and integrator of flight control systems for military and commercial aircraft, with products used across the majority of the commercial aircraft market. Its UK site at Tewkesbury, Gloucestershire, houses the Aircraft Controls Group alongside a separate Industrial Systems Group, and covers design, development, test, certification and in-service support for flight control equipment.

## Products and solutions

- **Primary and secondary flight controls** - actuation systems covering the full "stick to tail" control chain for civil and military aircraft.
- **High lift controls and control electronics** - systems supporting aircraft high-lift surfaces and their associated electronics.
- **Servo valves and manifolds** - electrically operated servovalves and manifolds controlling hydraulic fluid flow for precise actuator position, velocity, pressure and force control; military servo valves are designed and manufactured in-country, with civil servo valves qualified at Tewkesbury for final-phase manufacture and assembly.
- **Fuel metering interface devices** - components for aircraft engine fuel systems.
