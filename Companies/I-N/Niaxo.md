# NIAXO

> UK data analytics and digital innovation SME delivering secure analytics and software to defence, national security and public-sector customers.

## What they do

NIAXO delivers innovation, process improvement and software solutions using business analysis, data science, software development and business change. The company states it enables organisations to gain insight from data and provides business transformation through technology, covering process, hardware and software. Its work spans UK defence, national security and wider public-sector projects, with a focus on secure analytics platforms and security operations.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure Platform for Analytics (SPA)** - scalable hybrid-cloud, open-architecture analytics platform running across AWS and Azure.
- **Situational Awareness Tool (SAT)** - real-time dashboards for evidence-based decision-making, built with Elastic.
- **Dynamic Intelligence Reporting (DIR)** - automated briefing packs and situational-awareness dashboards drawing on open-source datasets.
- **InkWell** - AI-powered strategy software.
