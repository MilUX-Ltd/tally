# Missiles & Space Batteries Ltd

> Manufacturer of batteries and power systems for missile and space applications.

## What they do

Missiles & Space Batteries Ltd manufactures specialist power components for defence and space programmes, including weapons systems, platform systems and subsistence equipment. The company operates from Coatbridge in Lanarkshire and is part of the ASB Group.
