# Missiles & Space Batteries Ltd

> Manufacturer of thermal batteries and power components for missile, munitions and space applications.

## What they do

Missiles & Space Batteries Ltd (MSB) designs and manufactures thermal batteries for defence and space programmes. The business traces back to the MSA Catalyst Division established in Scotland in the 1950s and became part of ASB Group in 1996, taking the Missiles & Space Batteries name. It operates from Coatbridge in Lanarkshire as one of the ASB Group's battery businesses, alongside ASB and ATB. Thermal batteries are primary lithium batteries that remain inert until activated and are maintenance-free over long storage periods, a property suited to munitions and weapons that may sit in storage for years before use. The site holds design, manufacture and logistics capability on one location, including a dry room area for powder processing, pellet manufacture and battery build.

## Products and solutions

- **Thermal batteries** - power sources for tactical missiles and guided rockets, supplying seekers, control electronics, safety and arming units, actuators, telemetry and self-destruction systems.
- **Munitions and platform power** - battery power for bomb kits, artillery, countermeasures, underwater defence torpedoes (UDT), ejection seats and emergency power systems.
- **Space launcher batteries** - thermal batteries qualified for space launch applications, including use on the Ariane 6 and Vega-C launchers.
