# Nightball Technologies Ltd

> Independent cyber security specialist delivering zero-trust and cyber-resilient solutions for defence and critical operational environments.

## What they do

Nightball provides end-to-end digital solutions focused on cyber resilience for high-risk and critical operational environments. The company offers defence consultancy, communications and command and control services, systems integration, and cyber security and systems integrity software, with a specialism in zero-trust architecture.
