# Nightball Technologies Ltd

> Independent cyber security specialist delivering zero-trust and cyber-resilient solutions for defence and critical operational environments.

## What they do

Nightball, based in Basingstoke, provides end-to-end digital solutions focused on cyber resilience for high-risk and critical operational environments. The company states that cyber problems cannot be resolved by technology alone, and that its approach covers people, process, environment and technology. Its stated areas of expertise include privileged access management, adopting the Principle of Least Privilege for enterprise environments; classified and high-threat environment solutions, including public sector classified and multi-national work; high-performance solutions for high-density, scalable workloads supporting AI/ML and end-user compute; system design and integration of classified secure systems; zero-trust architecture across public and private cloud, including separation and continuous authentication; and rapid prototyping of new concepts through its Nightball Lab.

## Products and solutions

- **CRIW (Cyber Resilient Integrated Workspace)** - trademarked, patent-pending zero-trust, modular system providing cyber resilient distributed and disconnected cloud operating environments, positioned for critical national infrastructure.
- **CLVR** - joint venture providing secure, platform-agnostic distributed sensing capabilities, performing multi-modal detection, identification and tracking with edge analytics from distributed sensors.
