# Municipal Security Ltd

> Manufacturer and supplier of specialised physical and ballistic protection equipment for public sector organisations, utilities and high-security sites.

## What they do

Municipal Security Ltd designs and manufactures customised ballistic protection and physical security equipment, with a flexible factory facility enabling bespoke solutions in equipment and locking systems. The company also operates an online retail division (1010security.com) for safe retro-parts and conversion kits, and provides installations and servicing from its distribution centre in West Sussex.

## Products and solutions

- **Ballistic and physical protection equipment** - customised solutions for public sector and utilities customers.
- **Locking systems** - bespoke locking equipment manufactured to specification.
- **Safe retro-parts and conversion kits** - supplied via the 1010security.com online store.
