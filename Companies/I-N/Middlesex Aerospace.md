# Middlesex Aerospace

> UK precision manufacturer of safety-critical machined components and assemblies for fixed-wing and rotary-wing aerospace platforms.

## What they do

Middlesex Aerospace manufactures build-to-specification complex machined components, fully tested assemblies, and bespoke product kitting for the aerospace and defence sectors. They produce Class 1 safety-critical parts for undercarriage, transmission, actuation systems and hub assemblies across both fixed-wing and rotary-wing markets.
