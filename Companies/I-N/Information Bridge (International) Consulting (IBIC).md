# Information Bridge (International) Consulting (IBIC)

> Specialist C4ISR consultancy focused on NATO joint intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance.

## What they do

IBIC provides consultancy services in the defence C4ISR domain, with a specific focus on NATO C4ISR. Since its formation in 2008, the company has built up extensive knowledge of NATO processes and procedures. Recent activities have included a key leadership role in the planning and execution of major NATO JISR trials and management roles in several NATO multi-national industry technical feasibility studies.

## Products and solutions

- **NATO JISR trials leadership** - planning and execution of major NATO Joint Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance trials.
- **NATO multi-national technical feasibility studies** - management of industry feasibility studies supporting C4ISR interoperability across NATO member nations.
- **C4ISR consultancy** - advisory support drawing on the company's knowledge of NATO processes and procedures, built up since 2008.
