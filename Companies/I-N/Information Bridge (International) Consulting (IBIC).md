# Information Bridge (International) Consulting (IBIC)

> Specialist C4ISR consultancy focused on NATO joint intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance.

## What they do

IBIC has led NATO JISR trials and managed multi-national industry technical feasibility studies since its founding in 2008. The company advises on C4ISR architecture, interoperability, and capability development across the NATO alliance.
