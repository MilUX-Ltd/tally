# Mobifon Communications Ltd

> Secure and discreet mobile communications solutions for personnel operating in hostile environments.

## What they do

Mobifon provides secure mobile communications and J6 advisory services for defence and government clients, drawing on over 25 years of experience in the telecommunications sector. The company was founded by ex-service personnel who identified critical shortcomings in mobile communications and J6 policy during deployments to hostile environments. Beyond defence, Mobifon operates as a business telecommunications provider based in Tamworth, Warwickshire, delivering hosted telephony, mobile networks, mobile device management and managed connectivity services to enterprise, education, health and government customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure mobile handsets and Cypher SIMs** - encrypted mobile devices and SIM products for military units, defence contractors and organisations operating in hostile or high-risk environments, including deployments in Ukraine.
- **J6 advisory services** - communications policy and planning advice for defence and government clients, drawing on the founders' operational J6 experience.
- **Hosted telephony and managed connectivity** - cloud-hosted telephony, mobile device management, multi-network mobile services and business connectivity for the wider enterprise market.
