# Mobifon Communications Ltd

> Secure and discreet mobile communications solutions for personnel operating in hostile environments.

## What they do

Mobifon provides secure mobile communications and J6 advisory services for defence and government clients, drawing on over 25 years of experience in the telecommunications sector. The company was founded by ex-service personnel who identified critical shortcomings in mobile communications and J6 policy during deployments to hostile environments.
