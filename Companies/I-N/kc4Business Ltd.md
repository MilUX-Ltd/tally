# kc4Business Ltd

> Business development and sales support consultancy for the aerospace and defence sector.

## What they do

kc4Business is a business development and sales support consultancy that acts as an extension of its clients' sales teams without the cost of full-time staff. It works with aerospace and defence suppliers, supporting campaigns and bids. The company is a member of the Farnborough Aerospace Consortium and exhibits at defence events such as DSEI.

## Products and solutions

- **Business development** - outsourced business development acting as an extension of a client's sales team.
- **Sales support** - campaign and bid support for aerospace and defence suppliers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** Farnborough Aerospace Consortium (FAC).
