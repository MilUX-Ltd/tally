# Netscout Systems UK Ltd

> UK entity of NETSCOUT, providing network detection and response and real-time network visibility to enterprises, service providers, and government agencies.

## What they do

NETSCOUT protects critical digital infrastructure from cyberattacks and performance disruptions using deep packet inspection at scale technology. The company serves government agencies alongside enterprises and service providers, providing network performance management, service assurance, and threat detection capabilities across cloud, on-premise, and hybrid environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Omnis Cyber Intelligence / Omnis CyberStream** - Network detection and response built for SOC teams, integrating with SIEM, XDR, SOAR and EDR workflows for continuous monitoring.
- **Adaptive Threat Analytics** - Threat investigation and incident response capability aimed at reducing detection-to-response time, launched 2025.
- **Performance management and service assurance** - Visibility and monitoring across network, cloud, and service environments, built on deep packet inspection at scale.
