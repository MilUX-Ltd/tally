# Intelliconnect (Europe) Ltd

> Interconnect product supplier providing connectors, cabling, harnesses, and electronic subcomponents to defence and aerospace platforms.

## What they do

Intelliconnect (Europe) designs and supplies interconnect solutions including connectors, cables, wire harnesses, circuit boards, and box-build electronic assemblies. The company serves defence and aerospace customers with tailored products for platform interiors, communications, and power systems, drawing on decades of industry experience and holding several quality and defence supply chain accreditations.

## Products and solutions

- **Connectors and Harnesses** - Custom cables, wiring harnesses, and interconnect assemblies for defence platforms.
- **Electronic Subcomponents** - Circuit boards, flexible circuits, and box-build assemblies.
- **Additive Manufacturing** - 3D printing capability supporting rapid prototyping and low-volume production.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, Cyber Essentials Plus, UKAS, SC21 Silver
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR Registered
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
