# MDS Technologies

> UK sovereign SME delivering HMG Assured hosting, cloud, and managed services to central and regional government.

## What they do

MDS Technologies, trading as Assured Digital Technologies, combines the experience of established IT solutions providers MDS Technologies and Bristol IT Company, which merged in 2020. The company provides assured managed cloud and infrastructure solutions primarily to government and public sector organisations, positioning itself as a sovereign UK provider with access to high-security data centre facilities at Spring Park, Corsham, and Cody Technology Park, Hampshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Scalable Cloud** - custom cloud platforms, including business continuity, disaster recovery, and cloud virtualisation optimisation.
- **Enhanced Workforce** - secure, AI-supported workplace tools covering productivity, collaboration, process automation, and business intelligence.
- **CyberSecurity and Assurance** - layered security services covering users, devices, data, and cloud environments, including CISO as a Service.
- **Managed Services** - general IT support and managed IT.
- **Enterprise Networking and Telecoms** - secure mobile, resilient internet, and cloud telephony services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 9001, ISO/IEC 27001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group. Technology partnerships with Microsoft, Oracle, Cisco, HP, Dell, Veeam, Datto, VMware, and Ubiquiti.
