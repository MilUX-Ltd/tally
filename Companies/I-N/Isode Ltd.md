# Isode Ltd

> UK software company supplying secure communications software for military, civil aviation, and government customers worldwide.

## What they do

Isode develops and supports software for secure military messaging (email and instant messaging conforming to ACP127, STANAG 4406, SMTP, MMHS, and XMPP), secure civil aviation message exchange (flight plans and ATC messages), and directory and security infrastructure. The company supplies gateway software to convert messages between different technical and national-specific formats.

## Products and solutions

- **Military messaging software** - Secure email and instant messaging conforming to ACP127, STANAG 4406, MMHS, and XMPP military standards.
- **Civil aviation messaging** - Secure exchange of flight plans and ATC messages.
- **Directory and security infrastructure** - Directory services and PKI supporting secure communications.
