# James Walker UK

> Specialist designer and manufacturer of high-performance sealing and bolting systems for defence, aerospace, and marine applications.

## What they do

James Walker designs and manufactures sealing and bolting solutions for critical applications across naval, ground, and air defence platforms, as well as civil aerospace and marine environments. The company's capability in elastomers, polymers, composites, and engineering plastics underpins products spanning multiple generations of UK and international defence programmes, including a stated submarine portfolio.

## Products and solutions

- **Sealing systems** - Off-the-shelf and bespoke seals for naval, aerospace, and ground defence platforms.
- **Bolting systems** - High-performance fastening solutions for critical structural applications, including RotaBolt tension-indicating fasteners.
- **Vibration and noise control** - Mounts, stabilisers, and noise attenuation components.
- **CBRN and nuclear engineering components** - Materials and components for hazardous environment applications, including the Shieldseal elastomer range for gamma-radiation environments.
- **Pressure control and valves** - Precision valves and pressure management components for platform systems.
- **Critical metallic sealing** - Metaflex spiral-wound gaskets, Metakamm kammprofile gaskets and ring joint gaskets, precision machined in high-nickel alloys, stainless steel and titanium.
- **Expansion joints** - Fabric, metallic and rubber bellows expansion joints.
