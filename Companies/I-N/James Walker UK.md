# James Walker UK

> Specialist designer and manufacturer of high-performance sealing and bolting systems for defence, aerospace, and marine applications.

## What they do

James Walker designs and manufactures sealing and bolting solutions for critical applications across naval, ground, and air defence platforms, as well as civil aerospace and marine environments. The company's capability in elastomers, polymers, composites, and engineering plastics underpins products spanning multiple generations of UK and international defence programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Sealing systems** - Off-the-shelf and bespoke seals for naval, aerospace, and ground defence platforms.
- **Bolting systems** - High-performance fastening solutions for critical structural applications.
- **Vibration and noise control** - Mounts, stabilisers, and noise attenuation components.
- **CBRN and nuclear engineering components** - Materials and components for hazardous environment applications.
- **Pressure control and valves** - Precision valves and pressure management components for platform systems.
