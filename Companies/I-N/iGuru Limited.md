# iGuru Limited

> Specialist IT security consultancy focused primarily on defence clients and government departments.

## What they do

iGuru provides IT security consultancy services to defence customers and other government departments, including defence supply chain organisations. The company's work spans security consultancy, cyber and systems integrity, digital services, data analysis, and digital forensics.

## Products and solutions

- **Enterprise architecture and systems integration** - security-led architecture and integration services for defence and government clients.
- **Risk management and compliance** - risk management, compliance and policy development services.
- **Vulnerability assessment and incident management** - vulnerability assessment and incident management services.
- **OT and industrial control systems security** - specialised security services safeguarding critical infrastructure and industrial control systems.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001:2022, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
