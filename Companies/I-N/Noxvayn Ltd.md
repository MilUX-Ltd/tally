# Noxvayn Ltd

> Bespoke counter-UAS and security systems for high-value properties, critical infrastructure, and rural environments.

## What they do

Noxvayn designs, builds, and deploys discrete detection and response systems for drone, maritime, and complex security threats. Their systems are engineered for specific operational environments rather than sold off the shelf, combining AI, electronics, radar, and thermal imaging technologies.

## Products and solutions

- **Counter-UAS systems** - Detection and response solutions for drone threats across high-value and critical asset environments.
- **Maritime threat detection** - Systems addressing maritime security challenges alongside aerial threat monitoring.
- **Surveillance and tracking** - Thermal/infrared camera systems, radar, and telemetry-based tracking for surveillance applications.
