# JENOPTIK UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of the Jenoptik technology group, supplying ANPR camera systems and traffic enforcement solutions to police and security customers.

## What they do

JENOPTIK UK is part of Jenoptik's Smart Mobility Solutions division, a globally operating technology group present in more than 80 countries. In the UK, the company deploys ANPR camera systems, back-office data platforms, and mobile enforcement applications to police forces and public safety customers, with thousands of operational cameras installed across the UK and internationally.

## Products and solutions

- **VECTOR2 ANPR camera** - HD automatic number plate recognition camera with motorised zoom and integrated overview imaging.
- **TraffiData back office (BOF)** - Back-office platform for processing and managing ANPR data.
- **TraffiMobile** - In-car and mobile ANPR application for law enforcement use.
