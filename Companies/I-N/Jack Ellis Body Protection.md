# Jack Ellis Body Protection

> Manufacturer of body protection, PPE and structural components for defence and safety applications, based in Kirriemuir, Angus.

## What they do

Jack Ellis Body Protection designs, certifies and manufactures body armour and PPE for defence and safety markets, including bullet, ballistic, fragmentation, knife, spike and stab-resistant protection. Its capability set as listed with ADS Group also spans platform structures and components across a range of safety applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Body armour** - bullet, ballistic, fragmentation, knife, spike and stab-resistant vests, designed to meet Home Office Scientific Development Branch and NIJ standards, available in standard sizes and made to measure.
- **Combat and safety vests** - protective vests for military and security applications.
