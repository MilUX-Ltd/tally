# Logan Risk
> UK specialist defence and cyber security consultancy providing independent information assurance and technical advisory to MOD, FCDO, and Home Office.
## What they do
Logan Risk Ltd is a UK cyber security and defence consultancy. Its experts assess cyber security risk and provide independent specialist technical services on security controls and technology selection, scaled in proportion to the identified risk. Services span information assurance, cyber risk assessment, and niche solutions development, conducted against industry security standards and relevant legislation. Customers include the UK MOD, Foreign Commonwealth and Development Office, and Home Office, alongside private sector organisations in financial services, healthcare, and renewables.
## Products and solutions
- **Cyber risk assessment** - Independent technical assessment of cyber security risk and control selection for defence and government programmes.
- **Information assurance** - IA advisory supporting customers to manage information risk through project lifecycles.
- **Niche solutions development** - Specialist technical services for complex and sensitive security requirements.
