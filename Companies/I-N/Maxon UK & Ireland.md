# Maxon UK & Ireland

> UK and Ireland arm of Maxon, supplying high-precision DC drives and motor systems for space, aerospace, and defence applications.

## What they do

Maxon designs and manufactures high-precision DC motors and drive systems rated to operate across temperatures from -55°C to +85°C and to withstand the vibration environment of launch and landing. Products are used in space missions, aerospace platforms, and defence systems.
