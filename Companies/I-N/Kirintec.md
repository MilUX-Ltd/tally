# Kirintec

> UK manufacturer of electronic countermeasures, IED jamming and EOD equipment for defence and counter-terrorism operations.

## What they do

Kirintec designs and manufactures electronic countermeasure (ECM) systems, IED jammers and EOD tools for defence and security customers. The product range includes vehicle-mounted and portable jamming systems, rigging line cables, flying knives, spikes and wire cutters, supported by in-house training and advisory services.

## Products and solutions

- **Mercury jammer** - Electronic countermeasure system for IED defeat.
- **Rebus jammer** - Portable ECM system for counter-IED operations.
- **EOD tools** - Specialist equipment including flying knives, spikes and wire cutters for explosive ordnance disposal.
