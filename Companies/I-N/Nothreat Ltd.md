# Nothreat Ltd

> Autonomous, preemptive cybersecurity powered by self-learning AI.

## What they do

Nothreat delivers autonomous, preemptive cybersecurity powered by self-learning AI, aiming to stop unknown threats before they compromise systems. Its platform spans continuous threat exposure management, deception, real-time threat scoring and protection for IoT and edge devices. The company is based in London.

## Products and solutions

- **Nothreat Platform (CTEM)** - continuous threat exposure and management.
- **CyberEcho Deception** - patented automated decoy technology.
- **ThreatShield** - real-time threat scoring and automated blocking.
- **AIoT Defender** - on-device AI firewalls for IoT and edge devices.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type I, Cyber Essentials.
