# Northside Precision Tooling Ltd

> Glasgow-based precision manufacturer of microwave hardware, filters and electronic housings for defence and aerospace applications.

## What they do

Northside Precision Tooling manufactures high-accuracy microwave hardware and filters, precision electronic housings and chassis hardware, with all machining and surface finishing carried out in-house. The company provides controlled depositions, selective plating, anodising and conversion coatings for defence and aerospace customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Microwave Hardware and Filters** - high-accuracy machined microwave components and filter assemblies.
- **Precision Electronic Housings and Chassis** - machined and surface-finished enclosures for electronic sub-systems.
- **Surface Finishing** - selective plating, anodising and conversion coatings applied in-house.
