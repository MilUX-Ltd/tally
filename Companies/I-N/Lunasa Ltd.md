# Lunasa Ltd

> UK-based space systems company developing in-space satellite servicing missions for defence and commercial customers.

## What they do

Lunasa is a UK entity owned by Infinite Orbits, focused on advanced in-space satellite servicing missions delivering reliability and security for defence and commercial satellite operators. The company develops the systems and services needed to extend satellite life and maintain orbital assets.
