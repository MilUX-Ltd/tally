# MacLennan Associates

> Programme, project and business change management consultancy serving the UK defence and security sectors.

## What they do

MacLennan Associates delivers programme management, project management and business change consulting to clients in the defence and security sectors. The firm operates across complex, demanding environments and states its specialisms as programme management, business change (including benefits management), project management, cyber security advisory, strategic sourcing and procurement, supply chain and logistics, and through-life capability management.

## Products and solutions

- **Programme and project management** - management of complex defence and security programmes, including transition from programme delivery to business-as-usual.
- **Business change management** - business realignment and benefits management support.
- **Cyber security advisory** - described by the company as protecting the business rather than just information technology, covering procedural recommendations and supplier and partner assurance.
- **Strategic sourcing and procurement** - category management and supply chain and logistics support.
- **Through-life capability management** - support across the capability lifecycle for defence and security clients.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group, BCS (The Chartered Institute for IT), IPSE.
