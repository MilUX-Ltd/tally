# Meadow3

> Business development and bid consultancy helping aerospace and defence companies sharpen competitive edge and win new work.

## What they do

Meadow3 works with capture teams in the aerospace and defence sector to develop win strategies, structure bid processes, and produce written and designed proposals. The consultancy combines business development methodology with design thinking to connect a company's proposition to customer requirements.
