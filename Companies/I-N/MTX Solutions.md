# MTX Solutions
> UK specialist delivering defence software engineering, simulation, embedded systems and technical consultancy through a Defence Technology as a Service model.

## What they do
MTX Solutions blends operational insight with technical expertise, providing defence customers with software engineering, modelling and simulation, embedded systems and mission-ready systems via a Defence Technology as a Service (DefTaaS) model. The firm draws on a trusted partner network including academia and specialist SMEs. It is based at the Sci-Tech Daresbury innovation campus in Cheshire.

## Products and solutions
- **DefTaaS** - Defence Technology as a Service delivery model combining game engine, simulation and digital technology development for defence products.
