# Ionic Technologies International Ltd

> Rare earth element processing company producing separated, high-purity rare earths from recycled permanent magnets, supporting domestic supply chains for defence and advanced manufacturing.

## What they do

Ionic Technologies has developed a long-loop process for recycling end-of-life rare earth permanent magnets to produce high-purity, separated rare earth elements including Dysprosium and Terbium. The process is flexible on feedstock quality and status, and the company states it is the only organisation outside China capable of separating and producing individual Heavy Rare Earths at this level, enabling domestic supply chains in the UK and internationally.
