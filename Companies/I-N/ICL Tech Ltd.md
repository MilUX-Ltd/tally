# ICL Tech Ltd

> Scottish polymer products manufacturer serving aerospace, defence, security and space sectors, with vacuum forming, CNC machining and 3D printing capabilities.

## What they do

ICL Tech manufactures bespoke plastic components and products for aerospace, defence, security and space customers, covering everything from concept design and prototyping through to turnkey production. Its TECHPEACE division specialises in personal protective equipment and security products.

## Products and solutions

- **Structural polymer components** - Vacuum-formed and CNC-machined plastic parts for platform interiors and structures.
- **Blast protection products** - Structural components for protective applications.
- **TECHPEACE division** - Personal protective and security equipment.
