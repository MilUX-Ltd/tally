# MAIAR

> Agile UK defence SME providing specialist subject matter expertise, systems engineering, and intelligence-as-a-service for MOD intelligence, operations and communications programmes.

## What they do

MAIAR was formed in December 2023 when Level 7 Expertise acquired RJD Technology and the combined business rebranded as MAIAR. It describes itself as an agile SME working primarily in the UK MOD intelligence and research arenas, and with a range of customers and partners across NATO. It provides specialist technical consultancy across intelligence (J2), operations (J3), and communications and information systems (J6), operating in complex and classified environments on high-tempo projects.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems engineering** - lifecycle support covering concept, design, solutions, architectures, test, delivery, assurance and transition, delivered to MOD and other government departments.
- **Intelligence as a Service (IaaS)** - delivery of intelligence outputs in sensitive environments, using military-grade intelligence tools and skills, with a broader all-source offering in development for the unclassified arena.
- **Geospatial and open-source analytics** - delivered in partnership with Ursa Space Systems, combining commercial satellite imagery and data fusion with MAIAR's understanding of UK MOD and NATO requirements.
