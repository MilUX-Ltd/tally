# MAIAR

> Agile UK defence SME providing specialist subject matter expertise and systems engineering for MOD intelligence, operations and communications programmes.

## What they do

MAIAR was formed from the combined operations of RJD Technology and Level 7 Expertise. The company works primarily in UK MOD intelligence and research environments, as well as across NATO and supporting nations, providing specialist systems engineering and subject matter expertise in intelligence, foundation information, operations, and communications and ICT systems. It operates in complex and classified spaces on high-tempo projects.
