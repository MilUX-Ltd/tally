# Infor (UK) Ltd

> Global provider of industry-specific cloud ERP software, with a dedicated Aerospace and Defence vertical.

## What they do

Infor builds and delivers cloud-based enterprise resource planning and industry suite software for over 65,000 organisations worldwide. Its Aerospace and Defence offering addresses rigorous compliance requirements and complex production and supply chain management for defence manufacturers and contractors.

## Products and solutions

- **Infor Velocity Suite (A&D)** - Industry-specific ERP suite for aerospace and defence manufacturers, covering compliance, supply chain, and production management.
