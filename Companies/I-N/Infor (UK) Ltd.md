# Infor (UK) Ltd

> Global provider of industry-specific cloud ERP software, with a dedicated Aerospace and Defence vertical.

## What they do

Infor builds and delivers cloud-based enterprise resource planning and industry suite software for over 65,000 organisations worldwide. Its Aerospace and Defence offering addresses rigorous compliance requirements and complex production and supply chain management for defence manufacturers and contractors, and Infor states its A&D solutions are used by over 800 aerospace and defence companies.

## Products and solutions

- **Infor CloudSuite Aerospace & Defense** - industry-specific cloud ERP suite for aerospace and defence manufacturers, covering compliance, supply chain and production management, built on Infor LN and available through AWS GovCloud with FedRAMP Moderate authorisation to support contractors' CMMC 2.0 compliance.
- **Infor OS** - underlying platform layer providing modern user experience, Coleman AI, and Birst analytics for CloudSuite deployments.
