# Nova Systems

> One of the largest Australian-owned defence and security companies, a global provider of capability assurance, test and evaluation and systems integration, with a UK arm based in Bristol.

> Australian-owned. Global HQ in Adelaide, South Australia. The UK arm is Nova Systems (International) Ltd, registered at Filton, Bristol (Companies House 03752187).

## What they do

Nova Systems is a 100% Australian-owned engineering services and technology solutions company partnering with clients in national defence, essential services, border control, cyber security and civil aerospace. It is a provider of test and evaluation, certification and systems assurance, and delivers specialist systems engineering advice alongside digital technology, software and systems integration. It operates from offices across Australia, New Zealand, the UK, Ireland, Singapore and Norway.

## Products and solutions

- **Test and evaluation** - certification, systems assurance and a Test and Evaluation Centre of Excellence bringing together defence, industry and academia.
- **Systems integration** - integration of complex platforms and systems.
- **Software and digital solutions** - digital technology and software delivery.
- **Aircraft modifications** - including special-mission aircraft integration.
- **Advanced training** - training services.
- **Advisory and engineering services** - specialist systems engineering advice and professional services.
