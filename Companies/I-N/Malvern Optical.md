# Malvern Optical

> Electro-optics and electromagnetics R&D and independent test house working primarily for UK MOD, government agencies and the defence industry.

## What they do

Malvern Optical is a research, development and independent test and measurement company focused on the electro-optics (EO) and electromagnetics (EM) domains. Based at Pershore Airfield in Worcestershire, the company works primarily for UK MOD, government agencies and the UK defence industry across platforms, systems and sub-systems. It holds UKAS accreditation to ISO 17025:2017 as an independent test house, and its three core technical teams cover Electromagnetics, Sensors and Materials.

## Products and solutions

- **EO and EM testing and measurement** - independent UKAS-accredited test services across optics and electromagnetics.
- **Simulation and modelling** - including software modelling and TEMPEST.
- **Communications, command and control services** - consultancy and engineering.
- **Design and prototyping** - including additive manufacturing and materials work.
- **Surveillance systems** - CCTV and video analytics.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** JOSCAR, ITAR, Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO/IEC 17025, ISO 27001
