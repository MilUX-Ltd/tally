# MilUX

> A defence-focused user-centred design consultancy, helping defence organisations become fit for purpose.

## What they do

MilUX is a user-centred design consultancy working in the UK defence sector. It helps defence businesses and programmes improve the design and delivery of mission-critical solutions, keeping the military end-user at the centre. The strapline is "From Insight to Impact".

## Products and solutions

- **Design, Delivery, Training and Coaching** - consultancy practices around user-centred design and delivery for defence, including human factors integration and design assurance.
- **AI-driven decision tools** - applying AI to defence knowledge work and decision support.
- **Training design and delivery** - courseware and instruction in agile and delivery methods, including Kanban, Scrum and DevOps practices.
- **Founder and scale-up support** - tools and support for defence-sector founders, including the Foothold vault pack and Tally directory.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered.
- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus.
