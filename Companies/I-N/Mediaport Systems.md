# Mediaport Systems
> UK micro-SME designing and manufacturing ruggedised bonded-connectivity solutions for defence, CNI, and field deployment.
## What they do
Mediaport Systems designs and manufactures secure connectivity and edge-compute solutions for rapid deployment in challenging environments. Founded in 2024, the company draws on engineering heritage from its predecessor Wired Broadcast Ltd (1989 to 2022). Its flagship SONJA product aggregates bandwidth from up to four 5G networks and four LEO satellite links, or any combination of 5G, 4G, Wi-Fi, satellite, fibre, and DSL, in a WAN bonding router built around high-grade 5G modems and Bondix software. A ruggedised field variant, SONJA Rugged, is available in an IP67-rated milled aluminium enclosure optimised for size, weight, and power. Target verticals are defence, critical national infrastructure, security, and broadcast.
## Products and solutions
- **SONJA** - Portable WAN bonding router aggregating up to four 5G networks and four LEO satellite links for reliable connectivity in contested or remote environments.
- **SONJA Rugged** - Field variant in IP67-rated milled aluminium enclosure, optimised for SWaP and rough handling.
