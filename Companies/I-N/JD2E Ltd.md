# JD2E Ltd

> Military ISR training and simulation company delivering Mission Commander and Sensor Operator courses from its academy in Lincolnshire.

## What they do

JD2E specialises in intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (ISR) training for military and civil customers, including Mission Commander and Sensor Operator qualifications. The company delivers blended training programmes at its Lincolnshire academy and deploys experienced ex-military instructors to customer locations in the UK and overseas, supported by a Learning Management System for standalone or integrated use.

## Products and solutions

- **ISR operator training** - Mission Commander and Sensor Operator courses for military and civil customers.
- **Learning Management System (LMS)** - Standalone or integrated platform for managing and delivering training content.
- **Mobile training teams** - Deployable ex-military instructor teams for in-country delivery.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
