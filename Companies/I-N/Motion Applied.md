# Motion Applied
> UK sovereign-capability company delivering electrification, connectivity and control systems for defence, with heritage in motorsport and aerospace.

## What they do
Motion Applied applies race-proven, mission-ready technologies to extreme defence environments, spanning power electronics, telemetry, sensor systems and secure real-time data exchange across dispersed platforms. The Surrey facility produces thousands of components annually with a high UK content to support supply chain security. Defence offerings emphasise connected and interoperable platforms reliable in harsh conditions.

## Products and solutions
- **Power electronics and electrification systems** - Defence-grade power and control systems derived from motorsport engineering.
- **Sensor and telemetry systems** - Sensor packages and real-time data exchange for dispersed unit operations.
