# Level Peaks Associates Limited

> Soldier systems specialist designing, sourcing and supplying integrated clothing, PPE, surveillance, uncrewed systems, weapons, ammunition and tactical equipment to UK MOD, UK Police and NATO forces.

## What they do

Level Peaks Associates represents a portfolio of market-leading defence brands, supplying integrated soldier systems and tactical equipment to defence and security customers. The company covers the full soldier systems stack from personal protective equipment and specialist clothing through to tactical missile systems, unmanned systems, lethal and non-lethal weapons, ammunition and specialist armoured vehicles.

## Products and solutions

- **Integrated Soldier Systems** - clothing, PPE and load carriage solutions for front-line personnel.
- **Surveillance Technologies** - ISR equipment including biometrics and imagery systems.
- **Tactical Unmanned Systems** - UAS and uncrewed platforms for defence and security use.
- **Lethal and Non-Lethal Weapons** - small arms, ammunition and non-lethal capability.
- **Specialist Armoured Vehicles** - protected mobility and tactical vehicle solutions.
