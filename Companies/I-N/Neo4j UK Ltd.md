# Neo4j UK Ltd

> UK entity of Neo4j, the leading graph database platform used for connected-data analytics, knowledge graphs, and AI applications.

## What they do

Neo4j provides graph database technology that models and queries relationships between entities at scale. The platform supports use cases including analytics and artificial intelligence, fraud detection, real-time recommendations, and knowledge graph construction, with applications across government and defence.

## Products and solutions

- **Neo4j Graph Database** - Native graph database for storing, querying, and analysing highly connected data.
- **Knowledge graphs** - Relationship-first data modelling for intelligence, logistics, and decision-support applications.
