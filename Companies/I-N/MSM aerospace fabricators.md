# MSM aerospace fabricators

> Specialist SME manufacturer of complex aerospace components in high-temperature metals, with 75 years of heritage in forming, welding and repair.

## What they do

MSM aerospace fabricators manufactures and repairs aerospace components including bleed air ducts, anti-ice piccolo tubes, ozone converters, nacelle fabrications, and fuel, oil and water tanks. The company holds EASA Part 145 approval and offers specialist capabilities in hydroform pressing, bulge forming in titanium and Inconel, 5-axis laser profiling, and laser and resistance welding.

## Products and solutions

- **Complex aerospace fabrications** - forming and welding of high-temperature metals including titanium and Inconel for military and civil aerospace platforms.
- **Bleed air and anti-ice components** - piccolo tubes, bleed air ducts and related thermal management fabrications.
- **EASA Part 145 MRO** - maintenance, repair and overhaul of aerospace components under EASA approval.
