# Lampata

> Cambridge-based research and engineering team applying geospatial AI and earth observation to defence, research and sustainability problems.

## What they do

Lampata describes itself as working in geospatial AI, earth observation and open-science engineering, helping space agencies, research institutions and enterprises turn geospatial data into decision-ready intelligence. Its public-facing work centres on cloud-native geospatial data engineering and open, standards-based workflows rather than named off-the-shelf products; the company is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.

## Products and solutions

- **Geospatial AI engineering** - applied research and delivery combining geospatial AI, machine learning and earth observation data for organisations including space agencies and research institutions.
- **Cloud-native geospatial data workflows** - design and delivery of open, standards-based (FAIR/open-science) geospatial data pipelines, replacing legacy proprietary stacks.
