# Lampata

> Research and engineering team applying geospatial AI and machine learning to critical problems in defence and humanitarian domains.

## What they do

Lampata is a research and engineering team focused on geospatial AI and ML, actively seeking out critical, hard problems to apply this capability to. The company is a member of the D3IP community at Defence BattleLab, Dorset Innovation Park.
