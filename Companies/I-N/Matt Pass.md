# Matt Pass

> London-based cyber security and web application development consultancy serving security, defence and government clients.

## What they do

Matt Pass Ltd provides web application development and cyber security services, with a focus on security and defence sector clients including UK and US government bodies. The company has been active since 1999 professionally and has delivered cyber security training to over 250,000 users online and in person, with the aim of developing future cyber security professionals.
