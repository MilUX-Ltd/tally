# NP Aerospace

> Coventry-headquartered armour manufacturer and military vehicle integrator delivering survivability and protection solutions for defence and security customers worldwide.

## What they do

NP Aerospace describes itself as a world-leading armour manufacturer and vehicle integrator delivering high-performance defence and security solutions and engineering services, drawing on close to 100 years of experience producing lightweight survivability solutions from advanced materials. Its Coventry Advanced Armour Systems facility houses engineering, design, composites and armour manufacturing alongside a UKAS-accredited Ballistics Centre of Excellence. Its vehicle systems team handles full military vehicle integration, through-life support, repairs and refurbishment, and acts as Engineering Authority for the UK MoD's Protected Mobility fleet.

## Products and solutions

- **Composite vehicle armour systems** - lightweight add-on and integrated armour for military vehicles.
- **Vehicle integration and through-life support** - full military vehicle integration, repairs, refurbishment and spares; Engineering Authority for the UK MoD Protected Mobility fleet.
- **Ballistic helmets and shields** - personal protection products.
- **Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD) suits** - protective equipment for ordnance disposal.
- **Body armour plates** - personal survivability systems.
- **LC300 armoured vehicle** - upgraded armoured platform; entered full-rate production from January 2026.
- **Ballistics testing services** - independently UKAS ISO/IEC 17025 accredited laboratory.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 (Coventry Advanced Armour Systems facility); UKAS ISO/IEC 17025 accredited Ballistics Centre of Excellence.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group member.
