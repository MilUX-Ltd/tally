# Intelligent Energy Ltd

> Fuel cell engineering company producing lightweight, high-efficiency hydrogen fuel cell systems for UAV, automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications.

## What they do

Intelligent Energy designs, manufactures, and commercialises hydrogen fuel cell systems with a focus on zero-emission propulsion and power generation. Their product range spans sub-1 kW to 100 kW and covers UAV propulsion, automotive, aerospace, marine, rail, telecoms, and materials handling sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **UAV Fuel Cell Systems** - High power density hydrogen fuel cells for unmanned aerial vehicles, described as the world's highest power density in class.
- **Industrial Fuel Cell Systems** - Scalable power generation systems from sub-1 kW to 100 kW for aerospace, marine, rail, and telecoms applications.
