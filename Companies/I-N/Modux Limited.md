# Modux Limited

> Cyber security, software development and simulation consultancy serving UK defence, national security and government programmes.

## What they do

Founded in 2008, Modux is a technical consultancy delivering cyber security, software development, and 3D simulation and serious games across defence, government and private industry. The company employs technical specialists with backgrounds in large-scale government and defence programmes, and works across defence and aerospace, finance and telecommunications. Modux is an NCSC-approved CHECK company, delivering penetration testing and security assessment services under the government-accredited CHECK scheme, alongside broader technical consulting and assurance work.

## Products and solutions

- **Penetration testing and assurance** - CHECK-accredited penetration testing and security assessment of IT systems, covering application, infrastructure and mobile security, delivered by consultants holding CREST and Offensive Security certifications.
- **Red team simulation** - realistic enterprise attack simulations, using both publicly available and in-house tools to model the methods of external attackers and malicious insiders.
- **Software development** - bespoke software for systems used across defence, government and private-sector programmes, delivered directly and through industry partners.
- **3D simulation and serious games** - interactive simulation and gaming environments supporting training, data analysis and simulation for enterprise, critical national infrastructure and defence clients.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
