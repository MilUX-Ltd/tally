# MH International

> UK manufacturer of military bridging, trestling and engineering support solutions with over 60 years of heritage.

## What they do

MH International, trading name of Mabey Hire Limited, designs and manufactures rapidly deployable bridging, trestling and force-protection systems for military applications, covering both planned tactical operations and disaster relief. The company states it works with defence professionals to establish line-of-communication routes and to support damaged structures, with products built to military-grade specifications.

## Products and solutions

- **Heavy Vehicle BeamBridge (HVBB)** - pre-assembled bridge spanning up to 20m, rated to MLC 150, available in flat-top or rollover deck formats.
- **Heavy Vehicle Trench Crossing Units (HVTCU)** - short-span bridging for rapid deployment in combat zones.
- **Heavy Vehicle OverBridge (HVOB)** - short-gap crossing and ramp system for overbridging.
- **Trestling (MAT125)** - modular propping system rated to 125 tonnes per prop, used for bridge support and back-propping.
- **Modular Protective System (MPS)** - prefabricated force-protection shell, deployable subterranean or above-ground.
