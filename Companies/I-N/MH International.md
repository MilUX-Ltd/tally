# MH International

> UK manufacturer of military bridging, trestling and engineering support solutions with over 60 years of heritage.

## What they do

MH International, and its parent Mabey Hire Limited, design and manufacture rapidly deployable bridging and trestling systems for military applications, including tactical operations and disaster response. Their products establish line-of-communication routes and support damaged structures, with designs built to military-grade specifications.
