# Mico-Tindall Engineering Ltd

> UK manufacturer of high-security locking systems rated for physical attack, blast, ballistic and fire performance.

## What they do

Mico-Tindall Engineering designs and manufactures the UK's most extensively tested and approved range of high-security locking solutions. Products are independently tested and approved for performance against physical attack, blast, ballistic and fire threats, serving defence, security and critical infrastructure customers.
