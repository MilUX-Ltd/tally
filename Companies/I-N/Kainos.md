# Kainos Group

> London-listed digital technology company delivering software, data and AI services to public sector, defence and healthcare.

## What they do

Kainos Group plc is a digital technology company headquartered in Belfast, Northern Ireland, listed on the London Stock Exchange (LSE: KNOS) and a constituent of the FTSE 250. Founded in 1986 as a joint venture between Fujitsu and Queen's University Belfast's business incubation unit, it develops information technology for businesses and organisations. The group operates three divisions: Digital Services, Workday Services and Workday Products, and is a UK supplier of digital, data and AI capability to public sector, defence and healthcare.

## Products and solutions

- **Digital Services** - digital transformation, software development, data and AI delivery for government, healthcare and commercial clients.
- **Workday Services** - implementation and support partner for Workday enterprise applications.
- **Workday Products** - software products extending and automating Workday deployments.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** Crown Commercial Service Digital and IT Professional Services (DIPS); G-Cloud; Defence Innovation and Procurement frameworks.
