# KISPE Ltd

> Programme and systems engineering company specialising in electronics, telecommunications and space.

## What they do

KISPE works with customers from early business planning and concept design through to manufacture, integration, test and operations, delivering programme and systems engineering across the electronics, telecommunications and space sectors. The company also provides specialist training and development alongside its consultancy and engineering services.
