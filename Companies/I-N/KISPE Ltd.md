# KISPE Ltd

> Programme and systems engineering company specialising in electronics, telecommunications and space.

## What they do

KISPE works with customers from early business planning and concept design through to manufacture, integration, test and operations, delivering programme and systems engineering across the electronics, telecommunications and space sectors. The company also provides specialist training and development alongside its consultancy and engineering services. KISPE is based at Farnborough's Cody Technology Park, with a further site in Evesham, and has over 25 years of combined industry experience across its team. The company has two subsidiaries: KISPE Space Systems Ltd, covering space-related programme management and systems engineering, and Applied Space Solutions Limited, focused on space-related research, analytics, applications and services.

## Products and solutions

- **Space systems engineering** - system design, development, procurement, implementation and deployment for space missions, plus spacecraft design, development, manufacture, integration, test and operation.
- **Launch procurement and support** - experience working with launch service providers for ride-share and dedicated payload delivery to orbit.
- **Next Generation Microsatellite Platform** - a satellite platform in development aimed at delivering small-satellite performance at CubeSat price points, using a common integrated platform avionics design across missions.
- **Satellite communications engineering** - design and development of satellite-based communications systems, payloads and equipment, including low-earth-orbit store-and-forward communications, machine-to-machine satellite communications, in-orbit spectrum monitoring, telecommunications and navigation payload design, in-orbit test and characterisation, and geostationary communications satellite design.
- **Electronics design and manufacture** - digital and RF electronic design for terrestrial and satellite applications, covering microelectronics and digital signal processing, analogue electronics, RF and telecommunications, software-defined radio, and embedded/application firmware and software.
- **Satellite Learning Laboratory (SATLL)** - a modular satellite engineering toolset (satellite simulator, ground station simulator, courseware and support equipment) used for workforce development, training and hands-on experimentation, sold via KISPE's training arm.
- **Training and courseware** - courses spanning introductory space and satellite topics, mission design, spacecraft systems engineering, verification and validation, human spaceflight, project management for space systems, and business modelling and planning.
- **Consultancy** - business, management and technical consultancy drawing on the same engineering and programme experience.
