# Machina Engineering Ltd

> Automated CNC precision machining company producing high-tolerance 5-axis components for critical industries including aerospace and defence, operating lights-out 24/7 from a 30,000 sq ft Yorkshire facility.

## What they do

Machina Engineering specialises in high-precision CNC machining using state-of-the-art Hermle automated pallet pool machines, enabling 24/7 lights-out production for tight-deadline and cost-sensitive programmes. The company works across aerospace, defence, and other critical industries, and is pursuing AS9100 (EN9100) certification with BSI.
