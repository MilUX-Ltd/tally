# Machina Engineering Ltd

> Automated CNC precision machining company producing high-tolerance 5-axis components for critical industries including aerospace and defence, operating lights-out 24/7 from its North Yorkshire facility.

## What they do

Machina Engineering, based in Thirsk, North Yorkshire, specialises in high-tolerance simultaneous 5-axis CNC machining for critical industries including defence and aerospace. The company uses automated Hermle pallet-pool machining centres to run 24/7 unattended production, and machines materials including titanium, Inconel alloys and ceramic matrix composites. It states it is working towards EN9100:2018 (AS9100) certification with BSI.

## Products and solutions

- **5-axis simultaneous CNC machining** - components up to 850mm x 850mm, with stated machine positional accuracy of 6 microns.
- **Hermle C400 (HS Flex) machining centre** - automated pallet-pool machine with an 18,000rpm spindle, supporting unattended lights-out production.
- **Precision tool pre-setting** - a ZOLLER Redomatic 400 pre-setter combined with automatic stick-out measurement and induction heat-shrinking.
- **CAM programming** - toolpath generation and collision checking using hyperMILL CAM software.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001 and EN9100 (AS9100), both stated as in progress with BSI, not yet achieved.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
