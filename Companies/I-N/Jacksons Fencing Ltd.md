# Jacksons Fencing Ltd

> UK manufacturer of perimeter security systems for defence, government, and critical national infrastructure.

## What they do

Established in 1947, Jacksons designs, manufactures, and installs security fencing, acoustic barriers, gates, access control systems, and hostile vehicle mitigation (HVM) solutions. The company serves defence, government, and critical national infrastructure customers across the UK and internationally.

## Products and solutions

- **Security fencing** - High-security certified fencing systems for defence and government sites.
- **Hostile vehicle mitigation (HVM)** - Bollards, barriers, and related perimeter protection products.
- **Gates and access control** - Integrated gate and turnstile solutions for controlled-access environments.
- **Acoustic barriers** - Noise attenuation barriers for infrastructure and defence applications.
- **Barb and razor wire** - Perimeter deterrent systems.
