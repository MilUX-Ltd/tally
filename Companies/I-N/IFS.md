# IFS

> Global cloud enterprise software company with a dedicated aerospace and defence offering spanning ERP, MRO, logistics, and field service management.

## What they do

IFS develops and delivers cloud enterprise software built around manufacturing, asset management, and service operations. Its aerospace and defence platform covers ERP, maintenance, repair and overhaul (MRO), logistics, field service, and digital and data services, and is used by defence primes, operators, and MRO providers worldwide.

## Products and solutions

- **IFS Cloud** - Integrated enterprise platform covering ERP, asset lifecycle management, and service operations for defence and aerospace customers.
- **IFS Maintenix** - MRO and aviation maintenance management solution.
