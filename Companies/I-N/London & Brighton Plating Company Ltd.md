# London & Brighton Plating Company Ltd

> Metal finishing specialist providing electroplating and surface treatment to the aerospace and defence supply chain.

## What they do

Established in 1946, London & Brighton Plating draws on decades of experience in metal finishing to deliver surface treatment services including electroplating, plating, polishing and finishing. The company operates from Portslade in Sussex and serves aerospace and defence customers with a focus on product quality, turnaround speed and environmental performance.
