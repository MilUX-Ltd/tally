# Indigofitness Ltd

> Designer and manufacturer of deployable strength and conditioning solutions for military and elite sport.

## What they do

Indigofitness designs and manufactures commercial gym equipment with a specialist range for military deployment, drawing on experience from Iraq and Afghanistan campaigns. Products include the Deployable Training Platform (DTP) for small teams and Tactical Boxes, which are ISO container-based gyms in 10, 20, and 40-foot configurations.

## Products and solutions

- **Deployable Training Platform (DTP)** - Compact strength and conditioning system sized for small deployed teams.
- **Tactical Box** - 10, 20, and 40-foot ISO container gyms providing a complete training environment in a deployable format.
