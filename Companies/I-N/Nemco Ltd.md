# Nemco Ltd

> Contract electronics manufacturer specialising in complex, high-reliability PCBA production and test for the defence and aerospace sectors.

## What they do

Nemco manufactures printed circuit board assemblies (PCBAs) and provides test services for high-mix, low-to-medium-volume defence and aerospace electronic products. The company operates six SMT lines alongside conventional assembly capabilities, with a quality management system aligned to the requirements of aerospace, defence, and high-reliability industry sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **PCB Assembly (PCBA)** - Surface mount and conventional assembly for complex defence and aerospace electronics.
- **Test services** - Full test packages tailored to aerospace and defence, covering functional and in-circuit test.
