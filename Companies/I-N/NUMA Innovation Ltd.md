# NUMA Innovation Ltd

> Engineering simulation consultancy specialising in computational fluid dynamics and heat transfer for the aerospace and biomedical sectors.

## What they do

NUMA Innovation provides CFD, heat transfer analysis, and simulation code development as a service to aerospace and biomedical clients. The company also offers consultancy on specific engineering projects and training on open-source simulation tools.

## Products and solutions

- **CFD simulation service** - Computational fluid dynamics analysis for aerospace design and validation programmes.
- **Heat transfer analysis** - Thermal modelling and simulation for aerospace and engineering applications.
- **Open-source software training** - Training and support for open-source simulation and modelling tools.
