# Iceye UK Information Services Ltd

> UK arm of ICEYE, operator of the world's largest synthetic aperture radar (SAR) satellite constellation, delivering sovereign ISR from space.

## What they do

ICEYE operates the world's largest and most advanced synthetic aperture radar (SAR) satellite constellation, providing persistent, all-weather Earth monitoring. Its UK entity supports government, defence and intelligence customers with SAR imagery, tactical tasking and mission systems, including sovereign ISR capabilities. Services span defence and intelligence, disaster response and natural catastrophe monitoring.

## Products and solutions

- **SAR satellite imagery** - high-resolution radar imaging with rapid tasking and delivery.
- **Mission systems** - sovereign SAR mission capabilities for governments and defence.
- **ISR tasking** - tactical access and urgent tasking for defence and intelligence users.
