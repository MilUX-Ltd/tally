# Lock On
> UK aerospace and defence consultancy staffed by former fast jet instructor pilots, delivering capability development and operating procedure support across 4th and 5th generation platforms.
## What they do
Lock On provides consultancy services for the aerospace industry with a defence specialism. Founded in 2018 and based at The Officers' Mess, Duxford Airfield, Cambridgeshire, its consultants are former fast jet instructor pilots with combat experience on 4th and 5th generation platforms and backgrounds in strategy, capability development, and operational delivery. The company works with UK, US, European, and Middle Eastern clients through a network of specialists, placing a premium on information security.
## Products and solutions
- **Capability development** - Strategy and capability advisory for aerospace and defence customers.
- **Operating procedure development** - Specialist procedure writing and analysis for complex air systems.
- **Specialist aerospace advice** - Technical advisory across the procurement chain for UK and international clients.
