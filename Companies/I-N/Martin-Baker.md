# Martin-Baker

> The world's longest-established manufacturer of ejection seats, operating from Denham in Buckinghamshire with international facilities in France, Italy and the United States.

## What they do

Martin-Baker designs and manufactures ejection seats and related escape systems for military aircraft, and has developed crashworthy seats for helicopters alongside fixed-wing crew and mission seats for maritime patrol and reconnaissance platforms. Recent seat designs incorporate on-board electronic sequencers that optimise escape sequencing based on speed and altitude.

## Products and solutions

- **Ejection seats** - aircraft escape systems for fast jets and trainer aircraft, fitted across numerous air forces globally.
- **Crashworthy helicopter seats** - energy-absorbing seats designed to reduce occupant injury in survivable helicopter impacts.
- **Mission and crew seats** - fixed-wing crew seats for maritime patrol, reconnaissance and other mission aircraft.
