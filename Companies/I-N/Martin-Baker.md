# Martin-Baker

> The world's longest-established manufacturer of ejection seats, operating from Denham in Buckinghamshire with international facilities in France, Italy and the United States.

## What they do

Martin-Baker designs and manufactures ejection seats and related escape systems for military aircraft, and has developed crashworthy seats for helicopters alongside fixed-wing crew and mission seats for maritime patrol and reconnaissance platforms. Founded in 1946, the company states it has delivered over 90,000 ejection seats to 93 air forces and saved over 7,700 lives to date. Recent seat designs incorporate on-board electronic sequencers that optimise escape sequencing based on speed and altitude.

## Products and solutions

- **Ejection seats** - aircraft escape systems for fast jets and trainer aircraft, including the US16E (F-35), US18E (F-16 Block 70/72), US14A/NACES (US Navy T-45 Goshawk) and Mk16 (T-6 Texan II), fitted across numerous air forces globally.
- **Crashworthy helicopter seats** - energy-absorbing seats designed to reduce occupant injury in survivable helicopter impacts, including the Multi-Functional Operator's Seat and helicopter passenger seats fitted on the Sikorsky S-92 and Airbus EC145.
- **Mission and crew seats** - fixed-wing crew seats for maritime patrol, reconnaissance and other mission aircraft, including armoured crew seat variants.
- **Aircrew safety equipment** - escape-system components including water-activated release systems and inertia reels.
