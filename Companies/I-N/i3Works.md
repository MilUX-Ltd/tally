# i3Works Ltd

> Project, programme and portfolio management (P3M) and digital consultancy delivering high-security change programmes across defence and the public sector.

## What they do

i3Works is a P3M and digital consultancy with experience delivering high-profile and high-security projects and transformations across the UK, notably within defence, rail and public sector environments. Its teams are embedded alongside military personnel, civil servants and third party suppliers to deliver complex change to operational capabilities in secure settings. Founded in 2014, the company has diversified across the public sector, with around half of its work delivered outside defence.

## Products and solutions

- **P3M consultancy** - project, programme and portfolio management, Agile delivery, project controls and assurance, and PMO development.
- **Transformation and target operating model design** - TOM design and implementation, organisational change and IT service management.
- **Digital services** - digital strategy, service design, product design, UX design, GDS discovery and delivery services, and user research.
- **Cyber security and information assurance** - information assurance and cyber security advisory.
