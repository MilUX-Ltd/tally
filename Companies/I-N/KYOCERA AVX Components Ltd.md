# KYOCERA AVX Components Ltd

> International manufacturer of advanced electronic components for aerospace, defence and high-reliability applications.

## What they do

KYOCERA AVX designs and manufactures a broad portfolio of electronic components, including capacitors, inductors, filters, resistors, sensors, interconnects and antenna solutions, with a focus on high-reliability products for demanding environments. The company operates 33 research, design, manufacturing and customer support facilities across 16 countries, with its UK office in Frimley, Surrey.

## Products and solutions

- **Capacitors and inductors** - passive components for high-reliability and aerospace-qualified assemblies.
- **Sensors and transducers** - position, speed, temperature and pressure sensing components, including NTC temperature sensors, Hall angle sensors and pressure sensors, for automotive, industrial and specialist applications.
- **Connectors and interconnects** - interface solutions for ruggedised and high-reliability platforms.
- **Antenna solutions** - antenna and RF connectivity products for communications and navigation.
- **RF/microwave components** - capacitors, couplers, filters, inductors and resistive products for high-frequency, aerospace, defence, radar and satcom applications.
- **MIL-spec and DLA components** - ceramic capacitors, tantalum capacitors, RF/microwave capacitors and inductors, and EMI filters built to MIL-SPEC and DLA Qualified Products Database requirements, including the MIL-PRF-32535 BME NP0 MLCC series.
