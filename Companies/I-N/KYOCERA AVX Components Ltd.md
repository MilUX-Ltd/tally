# KYOCERA AVX Components Ltd

> International manufacturer of advanced electronic components for aerospace, defence and high-reliability applications.

## What they do

KYOCERA AVX designs and manufactures a broad portfolio of electronic components — capacitors, inductors, filters, resistors, sensors, interconnects and antenna solutions — with a focus on high-reliability products for demanding environments. The company operates 33 research, design, manufacturing and customer support facilities across 16 countries, with its UK office in Frimley, Surrey.

## Products and solutions

- **Capacitors and inductors** - passive components for high-reliability and aerospace-qualified assemblies.
- **Sensors and transducers** - sensing solutions for specialist and defence-grade applications.
- **Connectors and interconnects** - interface solutions for ruggedised and high-reliability platforms.
- **Antenna solutions** - antenna and RF connectivity products for communications and navigation.
