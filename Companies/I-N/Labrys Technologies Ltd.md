# Labrys Technologies Ltd

> Managed service and SaaS provider supporting defence, national security and humanitarian organisations operating in complex, limited-access environments.

## What they do

Labrys Technologies offers both managed service and Software as a Service solutions to clients running complex operations in limited-access locations globally. The company's customer base spans defence and national security, other government departments, the humanitarian and international development sector, and commercial clients.
