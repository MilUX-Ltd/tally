# Labrys Technologies Ltd

> UK-founded, veteran-led defence technology company building digital verification and workforce coordination infrastructure for contested and limited-access environments.

## What they do

Labrys Technologies is a UK-founded defence technology company, founded in 2020 by veterans of the Royal Marines and British Army, building digital verification infrastructure for contested and limited-access environments. Its Axiom platform cryptographically verifies users and media at the point of capture, coordinates distributed teams across languages and geographies, and enables secure payments where traditional infrastructure cannot reach. Labrys offers both a managed service, where it uses Axiom and its own global access to run client programmes, and a Software as a Service offering, where clients run their own teams and programmes on the platform. Its customer base spans defence and national security, other government departments, the humanitarian and international development sector, and commercial clients, and it states operational deployment validated in defence and government contexts, including work with NATO nations.

## Products and solutions

- **Axiom** - mobile-first platform providing cryptographic verification of users and media, distributed team coordination, and secure payments for organisations operating in contested or limited-access environments.
- **Axiom for Protection** - security and risk use case of the Axiom platform.
- **Axiom for Impact** - humanitarian and development use case of the Axiom platform.
- **Axiom for Understanding** - government use case of the Axiom platform.
- **Program Delivery, powered by Axiom** - managed service using Axiom and Labrys's own global access to run client programmes.
