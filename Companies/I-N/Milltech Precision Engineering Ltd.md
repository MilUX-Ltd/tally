# Milltech Precision Engineering Ltd

> Norwich-based precision CNC machining and subcontract manufacturing specialist for aerospace, defence and energy supply chains.

## What they do

Milltech Precision Engineering has manufactured high-tolerance components and assemblies for OEM and Tier 1 / Tier 2 supply chains since 1993. Operating from a modern facility with advanced multi-axis CNC capability, the company delivers 3-axis, 4-axis and 5-axis machining across metals and engineering polymers, from prototype through to production. The company has been part of the Swedish industrial group Indutrade AB since 2015.

## Products and solutions

- **Valves and manifolds machining** - dedicated cell operating around the clock for hydraulic and instrumentation components.
- **CNC turning and machining centres** - over 30 vertical, horizontal and turning centres across 3, 4 and 5-axis capability, including live tooling, twin-spindle and sliding-head turning.
- **Component assembly** - sub-assembly and full assembly service, with finished products supplied direct to customers.
- **Aerospace and defence components** - named applications include avionics housings, electronic assemblies, aircraft seating structures, and structural fixtures and fittings.
