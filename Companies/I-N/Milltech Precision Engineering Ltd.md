# Milltech Precision Engineering Ltd

> Norwich-based precision CNC machining and subcontract manufacturing specialist for aerospace, defence and energy supply chains.

## What they do

Milltech Precision Engineering has manufactured high-tolerance components and assemblies for OEM and Tier 1 / Tier 2 supply chains since 1993. Operating from a modern facility with advanced multi-axis CNC capability, the company delivers 3-axis, 4-axis and 5-axis machining across metals and engineering polymers, from prototype through to production.
