# Martin's Rubber Company

> Southampton-based rubber products manufacturer with over 150 years' experience supplying the aerospace and defence sectors.

## What they do

Martin's Rubber Company manufactures bespoke rubber mouldings, seals, grommets, gaskets, washers and arm rests to aerospace specification, drawing on more than 150 years in the rubber trade. The company also offers technical consultancy including Finite Element Analysis, and prototyping services alongside volume production. It supplies defence customers directly, including submarine, missile and EOD-related components, and manufactures ITAR-restricted products under licence.

## Products and solutions

- **Moulded rubber components** - seals, grommets, gaskets, washers and bespoke mouldings to aerospace specification.
- **FRlite** - a proprietary lightweight, fire-retardant silicone material developed for aerospace weight-saving.
- **Technical consultancy** - Finite Element Analysis and design support for rubber applications.
- **Defence components** - battery support mats, acoustic rubbers for noise suppression, submarine torpedo tube seals, cruise missile control cable covers, and EOD remote handling equipment tracks.
