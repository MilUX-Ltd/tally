# Martin's Rubber Company

> Southampton-based rubber products manufacturer with over 150 years' experience supplying the aerospace and defence sectors.

## What they do

Martin's Rubber Company manufactures bespoke rubber mouldings, seals, grommets, gaskets, washers and arm rests to aerospace specification, drawing on more than 150 years in the rubber trade. The company also offers technical consultancy including Finite Element Analysis, and prototyping services alongside volume production.

## Products and solutions

- **Moulded rubber components** - seals, grommets, gaskets, washers and bespoke mouldings to aerospace specification.
- **Technical consultancy** - Finite Element Analysis and design support for rubber applications.
