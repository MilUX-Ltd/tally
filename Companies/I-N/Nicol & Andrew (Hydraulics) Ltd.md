# Nicol & Andrew (Hydraulics) Ltd

> Glasgow-based hydraulic cylinder and ram repair and manufacturing company, serving defence and industrial clients from its Hillington facility.

## What they do

Nicol & Andrew provides hydraulic cylinder and ram repair, overhaul and manufacture from its facility on the Hillington Industrial Estate, Glasgow. It handles both routine and emergency repair of severely damaged cylinders, with in-house machining (lathe and milling) and stocks of quality replacement components. UKAS-accredited testing supports quality assurance on repaired and manufactured units.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, UKAS, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
