# Nicol & Andrew (Hydraulics) Ltd

> Glasgow-based hydraulic cylinder and ram repair and manufacturing company, serving defence and industrial clients from its Hillington facility.

## What they do

Nicol & Andrew (Hydraulics) Ltd operates from Hillington Industrial Estate, Glasgow, tracing its roots to a parent company founded in 1952. It provides hydraulic cylinder and ram repair, overhaul and manufacture, with in-house machining and stocks of chrome-plated rod material. All cylinders are pressure tested and repairs carry a warranty. The company states it serves marine, offshore and subsea customers, including bow door cylinders for RO/RO ferries, deck winches, cranes, offshore platform equipment, subsea ROVs and pipe-handling equipment, alongside naval and defence sector work including naval ship lifting equipment, watertight door actuators and valve actuators.

## Products and solutions

- **Hydraulic cylinder and ram repair and manufacture** - routine and emergency repair, overhaul and manufacture for industrial, marine and defence customers.
- **Naval and defence equipment support** - naval ship lifting equipment, watertight door actuators and valve actuators.
