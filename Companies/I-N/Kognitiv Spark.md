# Kognitiv Spark

> Augmented reality technology company specialising in secure remote mentoring and mixed reality solutions for defence and industrial maintenance.

## What they do

Kognitiv Spark builds secure, hands-free augmented and mixed reality solutions that connect frontline workers with remote experts in real time. Its flagship platform, RemoteSpark, allows field personnel wearing AR headsets to receive live guidance, view documents and 3D model overlays, and complete complex maintenance and repair tasks regardless of location or bandwidth. The company is headquartered in Fredericton, Canada, with a UK presence at Harwell Campus, Oxfordshire, and serves customers across defence, energy, manufacturing and marine sectors.

## Products and solutions

- **RemoteSpark** - hands-free AR/mixed reality platform connecting field workers with remote subject-matter experts via secure, low-bandwidth audio-visual calls, holographic annotation, document viewing and 3D model overlays. Runs on HoloLens 2, Magic Leap 2, and Windows PC, web and mobile.
- **RemoteSpark Use Case Development Kit** - a self-contained field kit combining an AR headset, mini server, handheld PC and secure cellular connectivity, allowing organisations to trial use cases without IT integration.
- **KognitivU** - a self-paced, modular training and onboarding programme for RemoteSpark users.
- **MiRRAS (Mixed Reality Remote Assistant Support)** - the branded RemoteSpark deployment used for Royal Canadian Navy vessel maintenance and repair trials.
