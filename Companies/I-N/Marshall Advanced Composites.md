# Marshall Advanced Composites

> Specialist composite structures manufacturer for aerospace and defence, based in North Yorkshire.

## What they do

Marshall Advanced Composites designs, manufactures and qualifies composite structures for aerospace and defence platforms, operating from a secure facility in Kirkbymoorside, Yorkshire. The company has been active since 1931 and holds the distinction of designing and manufacturing the first CAA-certified all-composite airframe, the Slingsby Firefly.
