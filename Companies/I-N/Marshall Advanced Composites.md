# Marshall Advanced Composites

> Specialist composite structures manufacturer for aerospace and defence, based in North Yorkshire.

## What they do

Marshall Advanced Composites designs, manufactures and qualifies composite structures for aerospace and defence platforms, operating from a facility in Kirkbymoorside, Yorkshire. The company has been active since 1931 and holds the distinction of designing and manufacturing the first CAA-certified all-composite airframe, the Slingsby Firefly. It was sold by Marshall Group to Swedish holding company Mangohojden AB in July 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **Submarine composite structures** - composite structures for submarines, including optronic mast housings, designed to reduce acoustic, visual, radar and thermal signature.
- **Naval decoy launcher tube structures** - supplied in partnership with IrvinGQ to the Royal Navy, US Navy and other international customers.
- **Aircraft trim panels** - composite trim panels for aircraft interiors.
