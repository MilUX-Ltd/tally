# Metrohm UK Ltd

> High-precision analytical instrumentation for chemical detection, identification and monitoring in defence and security applications.

## What they do

Metrohm UK supplies high-precision analytical instruments to defence and security organisations, including Raman spectroscopy, ion chromatography and electrochemical analysis systems. These are deployed for counter-CBRN applications, forensic investigations and environmental monitoring.

## Products and solutions

- **Raman spectroscopy** - Rapid identification of hazardous and controlled substances in field and laboratory environments.
- **Ion chromatography** - Chemical analysis for CBRN and environmental monitoring applications.
- **Electrochemical analysis** - Precision electrochemical instrumentation for defence and security users.
