# Light Coatings Ltd

> Specialist thin-film coatings manufacturer serving defence, aerospace and photonics markets from its Sci-Tech Daresbury facility.

## What they do

Light Coatings designs, specifies and manufactures coated components and applies bespoke thin-film coatings to customer-supplied substrates. The company specialises in small-batch, high-flexibility production using techniques including sputter deposition, chemical vapour deposition and optical coating processes across a wide range of materials and substrate types.

## Products and solutions

- **Thin-film coatings** - custom optical, conductive and non-chromium coatings applied via CVD, sputter deposition and related processes.
- **Coated components** - design-to-manufacture service for coated parts used in optics, sensors and specialist defence applications.
- **Prototyping and R&D** - fast-make prototype coatings with iterative specification support.
