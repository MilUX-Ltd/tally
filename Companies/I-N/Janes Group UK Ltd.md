# Janes Group UK Ltd

> Defence intelligence and open-source data provider with over 125 years of military OSINT expertise.

## What they do

Janes provides foundational military OSINT data sets and analysis to militaries, governments, and global defence organisations. Its data is compiled through more than 500,000 analyst hours annually and covers equipment, order of battle, and threat information across any operational environment or platform type.
