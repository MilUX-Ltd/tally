# Janes Group UK Ltd

> Defence intelligence and open-source data provider with over 125 years of military OSINT expertise.

## What they do

Janes provides foundational military open-source intelligence (OSINT) data sets and analysis to militaries, governments, and global defence organisations. Its data is compiled through more than 500,000 analyst hours annually and covers military capabilities, orders of battle, events and country intelligence, equipment, and defence markets, programmes and forecasts, across any operational environment or platform type. The company was founded in 1898 and today trades as Janes Group UK Limited.

## Products and solutions

- **Janes Fighting Ships** - reference yearbook and database of naval platform specifications, photographs and silhouettes for ships in development, production and service worldwide.
- **Janes Defence Weekly** - weekly defence news publication covering equipment, military geopolitics, forces, organisations, markets and business.
- **Janes Terrorism and Insurgency Centre (JTIC)** - online database tracking terrorism and insurgency incidents and groups.
- **Janes C4ISR products** - coverage of C4ISR and mission systems worldwide, including emerging technologies, market forecasts and technical reference data.
- **Janes Markets Forecast** - verified data and insight across global defence markets, with ten-year forecasts and historical data on awarded programmes.
- **Janes Intelligence Platform** - an API-enabled knowledge graph interconnecting structured intelligence data sets (equipment, sensors and weapons, military capabilities, country and CBRN profiles, defence budgets, programmes and forecasts), using machine learning for classification, deduplication and entity detection, and supporting fusion with other intelligence disciplines.
- **Tradecraft Online** - Janes' OSINT training programme.
