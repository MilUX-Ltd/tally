# Lockheed Martin Corporation

> Global US aerospace and defence prime spanning air, space, missiles and rotary systems, with established UK operations.

## What they do

Lockheed Martin is a global aerospace, defence, arms, security and advanced technologies company, formed in 1995 from the merger of Lockheed Corporation and Martin Marietta. It develops and manufactures combat aircraft, missiles and fire control systems, space and satellite systems, rotary and mission systems, and is one of the largest contractors to the US federal government. It is listed on the New York Stock Exchange under ticker LMT, headquartered in Bethesda, Maryland. UK operations run through Lockheed Martin UK Limited, headquartered in London with around 30 sites across the country, including a major facility at Ampthill, Bedfordshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Aeronautics** - combat and military aircraft, including the F-35 Lightning II Joint Strike Fighter.
- **Missiles and Fire Control** - air and missile defence, tactical missiles, fire control and sensor systems; the UK Ampthill site supports land systems and fire control.
- **Rotary and Mission Systems** - rotary-wing aircraft, naval and radar systems, training and logistics.
- **Space** - military and civil space and launch systems; supports the UK Trident strategic missile programme and operates a UK space technology office at Harwell.
- **UK programmes** - delivery of the Trident strategic missile, F-35 work and participation in the Military Flying Training System through the ASCENT team.
