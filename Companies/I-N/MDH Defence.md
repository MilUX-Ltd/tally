# MDH Defence

> Wiltshire manufacturer of CBRN filtration and collective protection systems for armoured and protected military vehicles.

## What they do

MDH Defence has over 50 years of experience designing and manufacturing vehicle CBRN filtration systems, crew cooling units, unhardened collective protection systems, shelter CBRN systems, and associated filter equipment. The company supplies bespoke solutions to systems integrators and vehicle manufacturers using standard parts and sub-systems, with fielded technology on platforms including the Force Protection Cougar and FNSS ACV 300/350.

## Products and solutions

- **Vehicle CBRN Filtration Systems** - NBC filtration for armoured and protected vehicles.
- **Vehicle Crew Cooling Units** - thermal management for vehicle crew environments.
- **Unhardened Collective Protection Systems (UCPS)** - deployable collective protection for field use.
- **Shelter CBRN Systems** - CBRN protection for fixed and deployable shelter infrastructure.
