# MDH Defence

> Wiltshire manufacturer of CBRN filtration and collective protection systems for armoured and protected military vehicles.

## What they do

MDH Defence is a world leader in the provision of CBRN collective protection, fresh air filtration and environmental control for military platforms. With over 50 years of experience designing and manufacturing CBRN filtration and crew cooling and heating, the company supplies bespoke solutions to systems integrators and vehicle manufacturers using standard parts and sub-systems. MDH Defence equipment is in service worldwide on a range of military vehicles, ships, and rigid and tented shelters, with fielded technology on platforms including the Force Protection Cougar and FNSS ACV 300/350.

## Products and solutions

- **Vehicle CBRN Filtration** - collective and individual NBC protection for armoured and protected vehicles.
- **Shelter CBRN Filtration** - collective protection for rigid and tented shelters.
- **Fresh Air Filtration** - fine dust filtration for a range of military vehicles and shelters.
- **Environmental Control** - crew cooling and heating for military vehicles.
- **Consumables and Spares** - filter equipment and associated spares supporting the fielded product range.
