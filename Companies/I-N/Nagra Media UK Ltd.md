# Nagra Media UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of the Kudelski Group, delivering digital security, content protection and cybersecurity solutions for media, telecoms and mission-critical sectors.

## What they do

Nagra Media UK Ltd, based in Cwmbran, is part of the Kudelski Group and trades publicly as NAGRAVISION. It provides advanced encryption, cybersecurity and resilience solutions protecting digital platforms, connected devices and communications infrastructure. Its mission-critical portfolio serves sectors where data integrity and secure communications are operationally significant, delivered through the group's cybersecurity division, Kudelski Security.

## Products and solutions

- **Kudelski Security Managed Detection and Response** - monitoring and threat detection delivered from Cyber Fusion Centers in the US, Switzerland and Spain.
- **Incident response and digital forensics** - incident response retainer and digital forensics services.
- **Penetration testing and red teaming** - penetration testing, red teaming and purple teaming services.
- **NAGRA Venturi** - intelligence-led streaming security platform.
