# IceMOS Technology Ltd

> US-headquartered semiconductor manufacturer with wafer fabrication in Belfast, producing proprietary sensing elements and Power MOSFET switches for defence, aerospace and industrial markets.

## What they do

IceMOS is an equity-financed, privately held semiconductor company and manufacturer of Silicon MEMS-based Power MOSFETs and sensing device technology. The company designs, manufactures, markets and sells proprietary sensing elements and energy-saving Power MOSFET switches to customers across North America, Asia and Europe, with applications spanning safety-critical electronics and energy-saving power conversion. IceMOS has R&D in Arizona, design and development in Japan, and its own wafer fabrication factory at the top of the Falls Road in Belfast, which the company states employs around 70 people.

## Products and solutions

- **Sensing elements** - proprietary MEMS-based sensors for safety enhancement in electronic systems.
- **Super-junction Power MOSFETs (mSJMOS)** - high-voltage (600V-730V) power switching devices for energy-saving applications, developed from over 70 patents combining Silicon MEMS manufacturing with mature-node CMOS super-junction structures.
- **Advanced Engineered Substrates** - bonded silicon and silicon-on-insulator (SOI) substrates.
