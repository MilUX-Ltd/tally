# IceMOS Technology Ltd

> US-headquartered semiconductor manufacturer with wafer fabrication in Belfast, producing proprietary sensing elements and Power MOSFET switches for defence, aerospace and industrial markets.

## What they do

IceMOS designs, manufactures and sells sensing elements and Super-junction Power MOSFET switches, with applications spanning safety-critical electronics and energy-saving power conversion. The company has R&D in Arizona, design and development in Japan, and its own wafer fabrication factory in Belfast serving customers across North America, Asia and Europe.

## Products and solutions

- **Sensing elements** - Proprietary sensors for safety enhancement in electronic systems.
- **Super-junction Power MOSFETs** - High-efficiency power switching devices for energy-saving applications.
