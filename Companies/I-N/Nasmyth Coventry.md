# Nasmyth Coventry

> Advanced assembly manufacturer specialising in low-volume, complex precision structures for aerospace, marine and engineering sectors.

## What they do

Nasmyth Coventry employs around 100 engineers, sheet metal technicians and welding specialists producing large-scale aluminium, stainless steel, titanium and rare alloy assemblies. The company handles structural and turned components to the largest sizes its customers require, and is fully conversant with reverse engineering techniques.
