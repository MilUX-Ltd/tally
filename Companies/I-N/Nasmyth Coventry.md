# Nasmyth Coventry

> Advanced assembly manufacturer specialising in low-volume, complex precision structures for aerospace, marine and engineering sectors, part of the Nasmyth Group.

## What they do

Nasmyth Coventry employs around 100 engineers, sheet metal technicians and welding specialists producing large-scale aluminium, stainless steel, titanium and rare alloy assemblies. The company handles structural and turned components to the largest sizes its customers require, and is fully conversant with reverse engineering techniques. It is a long-established manufacturer of complex sheet metal, welded and machined assemblies serving the aero engine, airframe, space and industrial gas turbine sectors, and also provides precision engineering and metal treatment for maritime customers, including assembly and test of complex maritime systems and precision machined components up to two metres in diameter.

## Products and solutions

- **Sheet metal and welded assemblies** - large-scale aluminium, stainless steel, titanium and rare alloy structures for aero engine, airframe and industrial gas turbine customers.
- **Machined and turned components** - precision machining to customer-specified sizes, including reverse engineering of legacy parts.
- **Maritime system assembly and test** - electro-mechanical assembly, system integration and test of maritime systems and fabrications.
