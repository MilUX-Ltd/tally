# Law Enforcement International Ltd

> UK manufacturer and supplier of small arms, ammunition and related accessories for defence and law enforcement markets.

## What they do

Law Enforcement International manufactures and supplies small arms, ammunition and associated products and accessories, serving defence and law enforcement customers from its Hertfordshire base. Trade-press coverage describes the company as a NATO-codified contractor supplying to a large number of countries, and as holder of a UK Section 5 firearms dealer licence. No company website was found; the company's own public presence is limited to its ADS Group members-directory listing.

## Products and solutions

- **Sound suppressors** - design and manufacture of firearm suppressors, including for rifle and pistol calibres, reported by trade press.
- **Small arms and accessories** - custom and specialist firearms and accessories for defence and law enforcement clients.
- **Ammunition supply** - procurement and supply of ammunition and related products to military and police customers.
