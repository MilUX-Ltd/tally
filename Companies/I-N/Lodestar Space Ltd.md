# Lodestar Space Ltd

> Developer of integrated hardware and software for on-orbit servicing, inspection and repair of space assets with national security applications.

## What they do

Lodestar Space is developing a hardware and software suite to provide physical and dexterous manipulation capability in orbit, enabling the protection, inspection and repair of space assets. The company frames its work around safeguarding national security today and enabling large-scale in-orbit construction in future.
