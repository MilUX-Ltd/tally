# Lodestar Space Ltd

> Developer of integrated hardware and software for on-orbit servicing, inspection and repair of space assets with national security applications.

## What they do

Lodestar Space is developing a hardware and software suite to provide physical and dexterous manipulation capability in orbit, enabling the protection, inspection and repair of space assets. The company frames its work around safeguarding national security today and enabling large-scale in-orbit construction in future. Its public positioning centres on UK and allied defence use cases, including space domain awareness and protect-and-defend missions for high-value satellites, alongside a secondary ambition to serve commercial in-orbit servicing, assembly and manufacturing missions.

## Products and solutions

- **Robotic arm** - a modular, dexterous robotic arm for contact-dependent operations in orbit such as inspection, capture and repair, designed to be platform-agnostic with swappable end-effectors.
- **Mithril** - described by the company as a fully autonomous, AI-powered on-orbit payload that detects, classifies and responds to other spacecraft in orbit, combining machine-vision sensors, LiDAR and an edge-compute platform to assess objects and support docking or servicing without human intervention.
- **In-orbit servicing, assembly and manufacturing (IOSM) testbed** - a ground-based facility at the company's London site used to test vision and sensing systems under simulated in-orbit visual conditions.
