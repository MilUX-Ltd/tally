# Malloy Aeronautics Ltd

> UK designer of all-electric heavy-lift uncrewed aircraft systems for military and commercial logistics.

## What they do

Malloy Aeronautics designs and supplies all-electric uncrewed aircraft systems for civil and military customers. Its heavy-lift quadcopters carry payloads from around 68 kg to several hundred kilograms over short to medium-range missions, with zero-emission operation at point of use. The company is based in Berkshire and is a BAE Systems owned entity within BAE's FalconWorks research and development division.

## Products and solutions

- **T-150** - heavy-lift drone with around 68 kg maximum payload, in service with the UK Royal Navy, Royal Marines and US Marine Corps.
- **T-400** - heavy-lift platform with around 165 kg maximum payload and extended range.
- **T-650** - next-generation aircraft under development for requirement-led concepts.
- **TRV-150** - trials variant of the T-150 used in capability demonstrations.
