# NKT Photonics Ltd

> Advanced fibre laser and photonics manufacturer supplying defence and security applications on mobile platforms.

## What they do

NKT Photonics develops advanced fibre laser technology for use in laser systems deployed on mobile platforms including aircraft, ships and ground vehicles, with a focus on security and asymmetrical threat applications. The UK entity is based in Southampton and supports European and US special project requirements.
