# NKT Photonics Ltd

> Advanced fibre laser and photonics manufacturer supplying defence and security applications on mobile platforms.

## What they do

NKT Photonics develops advanced fibre laser technology for use in laser systems deployed on mobile platforms including aircraft, ships and ground vehicles, with a focus on security and asymmetrical threat applications. The UK entity is based in Southampton and supports European and US special project requirements. The company states that its multi-kilowatt single-frequency fibre lasers can be spectrally combined to exceed 20 kW at near diffraction-limited beam quality, suited to high-energy laser applications, and that it also supplies laser technology for tracking and illumination at eye-safe wavelengths.

**Note on parent company.** Following Hamamatsu Photonics' acquisition of NKT Photonics A/S (completed June 2024), the Danish parent was formally renamed Hamamatsu Photonics A/S around June 2026, operating as the "Lasers & Fibers" business unit; its former nktphotonics.com website now redirects accordingly. The UK entity continues to be listed as NKT Photonics Ltd in public directories at the time of writing.

## Products and solutions

- **SuperK series** - supercontinuum white-light lasers.
- **Koheras series** - single-frequency fibre lasers, including variants used in high-power spectrally-combined configurations for directed energy applications.
- **Ultrafast lasers (aeroPULSE, ORIGAMI)** - ultrafast laser sources.
- **Optical fibres and gain modules** - including ytterbium-doped double-clad gain fibres for laser amplification.
