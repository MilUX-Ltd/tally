# Nasmyth IEC

> Specialist aerospace, defence and marine assembly and CNC machining business based in Rustington, West Sussex, part of the Nasmyth Group.

## What they do

Nasmyth IEC manufactures complex parts and assemblies for aerospace, defence, marine, instrumentation, energy, medical and mobility markets, working in nickel alloys, Inconel, titanium, magnesium and high-performance plastics. The company holds a major specialist assembly and test facility for systems used in marine and submarine environments.
