# Nasmyth IEC

> Specialist aerospace, defence and marine assembly and CNC machining business based in Rustington, West Sussex, part of the Nasmyth Group.

## What they do

Nasmyth IEC specialises in assembly, systems engineering and CNC machining, manufacturing complex parts and assemblies for the aerospace, defence, marine, instrumentation, energy, medical and mobility markets, working in nickel alloys, Inconel, titanium, magnesium and high-performance plastics. The company describes itself as serving space, air, land and sea applications for the defence sector, and holds a specialist facility for assembly and test of systems used in marine and submarine environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Multi-axis milling and turning** - three, four and five-axis high-speed CNC milling and turning, including multi-spindle lathes, in hard and soft metals for aerospace applications.
- **Electro-mechanical and cable and harness assembly** - low-volume, high-mix electronics manufacturing, cable and harness assembly, and PCB assembly for aerospace, defence and oil and gas customers.
- **Assemblies and testing** - aerostructure, welded, electrical and mechanical assemblies including fuel pipes, ground support equipment, satellite drill fixtures, military vehicle components and flight simulator seat installations.
- **Maintenance, repair and overhaul** - repair and overhaul of components including flight control systems and sea-mine countermeasures equipment, with an aircraft-on-ground response capability.
- **Design, reverse engineering and supply chain integration** - CAD/CAM-supported design and reverse engineering, and global supply chain sourcing for machined products, forged rings and castings.
