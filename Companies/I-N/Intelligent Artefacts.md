# Intelligent Artefacts

> AI middleware providing intelligent, adaptive agents for next-generation defence training simulations.

## What they do

Intelligent Artefacts builds AI middleware for training simulation environments, drawing on deep expertise in navigation and behaviour systems for large-scale virtual worlds. Their middleware product gives simulated entities the ability to navigate, adapt, and behave intelligently, producing training environments that more closely replicate real-world operational conditions while reducing development time and cost. The company is an ADS member and is based in London.

## Products and solutions

- **Intelligent agent middleware** - navigation, adaptation, and behaviour systems for entities in training simulation environments, enabling realistic and responsive virtual opposition forces and supporting characters.
