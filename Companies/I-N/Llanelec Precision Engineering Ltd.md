# Llanelec Precision Engineering Ltd

> Precision CNC machining specialist for military vehicle running gear, drivetrain components and survivability structures.

## What they do

Llanelec specialises in precision CNC machining of running gear, final drive parts, road wheels and survivability components for military vehicles. Operating from a manufacturing facility at Neath Abbey Business Park in South Wales equipped with more than 60 CNC machine centres, the company offers machining, welding and fabrication, painting and rubberising under one roof. It states over 35 years in the MOD supply chain and describes itself as a UK MOD approved supplier.

## Products and solutions

- **Challenger 2 / CRARRV components** - crank axle track tensioner, gun housing, track tensioner bracket, hydrogas cylinder, axle arm, road wheel and related running gear parts.
- **Warrior components** - road wheel hubs, final drive oil seal, final drive hub and shaft assembly, track tensioner assembly, hydraulic ram and mechanical housings.
- **Bulldog and CVRT components** - hydraulic rams, brake calipers, road wheels and axle arm assemblies.
- **Llanelec 1450** - a proprietary rubber compound for solid compound wheels used on MOD platforms.
