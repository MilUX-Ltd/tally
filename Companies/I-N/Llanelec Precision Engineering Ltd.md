# Llanelec Precision Engineering Ltd

> Precision CNC machining specialist for military vehicle running gear, drivetrain components and survivability structures.

## What they do

Llanelec specialises in precision CNC machining of running gear, final drive parts, road wheels and survivability components for military vehicles. Operating from Neath Abbey Business Park in South Wales, the company offers machining, welding and fabrication, painting and rubberising under one roof.
