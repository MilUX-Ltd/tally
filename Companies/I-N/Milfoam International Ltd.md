# Milfoam International Ltd

> UK-headquartered manufacturer of barrel cleaning systems for military and civilian firearms, used in over 40 countries.

## What they do

Milfoam International supplies eco-friendly, high-precision barrel cleaning solutions for the defence and civil sectors, combining advanced foam technology with NATO-approved chemicals. Initially adopted by the Finnish Defence Forces, their systems are now deployed across more than 40 countries and are designed to enhance weapon performance and extend barrel lifespan.

## Products and solutions

- **Advanced Cleaning Foams** - Remove carbon, copper, brass and plastic residues from weapon barrels.
- **Synthetic Oils and Pneumatic Brushes** - Clean, lubricate and protect barrels to extend service life.
- **Vibrating Pneumatic Brushes** - Accelerate and standardise the cleaning process for military and commercial users.
