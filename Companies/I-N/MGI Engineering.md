# MGI Engineering Ltd

> Unmanned autonomous systems design, prototyping and manufacture for defence and security, drawing on over 25 years of Formula 1 engineering.

## What they do

MGI Engineering delivers high-quality consultancy and unmanned autonomous systems design, prototyping and manufacture, applying engineering disciplines developed over two decades in Formula 1 motorsport. The company serves the defence and security sector alongside other high-performance industries. Its defence arm, MGI Defence, designs and delivers autonomous platforms for land, air, and maritime operations intended for contested and electronically denied environments.

## Products and solutions

- **TigerShark** - long-range autonomous one-way effector, ground-launched and salvo-capable, with a modular payload and a guidance suite combining inertial navigation with GPS-free terrain mapping for operation in GPS-denied and spoofed environments.
- **SkyShark** - medium-range autonomous one-way effector strike drone with a modular payload.
- **SeaGlide** - autonomous foiling uncrewed surface vessel, described as a swarm-mothership able to carry and launch UAV swarms simultaneously or in sequence, using hydrofoil autonomy for navigation without reliance on external guidance or satellite communications.
- **TigerShark II / LeopardShark** - additional platforms in the company's Autonomous Collaborative Platforms line.
