# MGI Engineering Ltd

> Unmanned autonomous systems design, prototyping and manufacture for defence and security, drawing on over 25 years of Formula 1 engineering.

## What they do

MGI Engineering delivers high-quality consultancy and unmanned autonomous systems design, prototyping and manufacture, applying engineering disciplines developed over two decades in Formula 1 motorsport. The company serves the defence and security sector alongside other high-performance industries.

## Products and solutions

- **UAS design and prototyping** - Custom unmanned aerial and autonomous systems designed and prototyped for defence requirements.
- **Engineering consultancy** - Technical consultancy drawing on advanced motorsport engineering methods.
