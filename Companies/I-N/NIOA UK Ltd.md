# NIOA UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of NIOA, a family-owned global supplier of firearms, weapons and munitions to defence and security customers.

## What they do

NIOA is the largest privately-owned supplier of weapons and munitions in Australia, with operations spanning NIOA Australia, NIOA New Zealand, NIOA UK, the Australian Missile Corporation, Barrett Firearms (USA) and a joint venture with Rheinmetall. The UK entity is based in Bristol and supports NIOA's European and global defence customer base.
