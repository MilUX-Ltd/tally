# NIOA UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of NIOA, a family-owned global supplier of firearms, weapons and munitions to defence and security customers.

## What they do

NIOA is the largest privately-owned supplier of weapons and munitions in Australia, with operations spanning NIOA Australia, NIOA New Zealand, NIOA UK, the Australian Missile Corporation, Barrett Firearms (USA, acquired 2023) and a joint venture with Rheinmetall. NIOA UK Ltd was incorporated in June 2024 with a registered office in Bristol, and opened an office at Abbey Wood, home of the MOD's Defence Equipment & Support organisation. The company states this office is also intended to serve as NIOA Group's European headquarters, supporting closer ties with DE&S and UK industry and exploring munitions manufacturing and military supply opportunities in the UK and Europe.

## Products and solutions

- **Small arms** - supply of small arms to commercial, law enforcement and military markets.
- **Munitions** - supply of medium and large calibre munitions, special-function grenades and pyrotechnics, under Australia's Major Munitions Deed covering more than 50 munitions types.
- **Rheinmetall NIOA Munitions joint venture** - 155mm artillery shell manufacturing (Maryborough, Queensland) and 120mm tank round, 30mm and 35mm cannon ammunition manufacturing (Benalla, Victoria).
- **Optics and carriage solutions** - pistol systems, optics and associated carriage equipment.

No UK-specific manufacturing contract or MOD supply agreement has been publicly confirmed for NIOA UK Ltd to date; public statements describe the UK office as exploring opportunities rather than delivering against a confirmed contract.
