# M Subs Ltd

> Independent company specialising in manned and unmanned submarines and underwater vehicles for defence, research, and commercial survey applications.

## What they do

M Subs develops and supplies manned and unmanned submarine and underwater vehicle systems for defence, research, and commercial survey customers. The company operates independently and focuses on underwater domain solutions including UUVs.
