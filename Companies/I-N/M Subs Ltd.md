# M Subs Ltd

> Independent company specialising in manned and unmanned submarines and underwater vehicles for defence, research, and commercial survey applications.

## What they do

M Subs Ltd designs, manufactures and operates manned and unmanned submersibles for military and commercial markets, operating from Plymouth alongside its sister company Submergence Group. The company states it has designed, built and operated specialised submersibles supporting diver lock-out and long-endurance operations since 1986. Its unmanned underwater vehicle (UUV) hull designs are derived from its manned submersible designs, so most systems and components sit within the pressure hull, allowing the vehicles to be maintained and replenished without recovery. UUVs can be operated manned or unmanned using Submergence Group's SUBNAVSYS integrated bridge system.

## Products and solutions

- **Manned submersibles** - crewed submersible platforms supporting diver lock-out and long-endurance scientific research and military operations.
- **Unmanned underwater vehicles (UUVs)** - UUV platforms sharing hull and system design with the company's manned submersibles, operable manned or unmanned.
- **Extra Large Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (XLAUV)** - the company states it delivered the UK Royal Navy's XLAUV under the Cetus programme, launched at its Plymouth site.
