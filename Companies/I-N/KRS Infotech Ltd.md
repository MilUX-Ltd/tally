# KRS Infotech Ltd

> UK-based technology consultancy and resource partner delivering IT and engineering capability to aerospace, defence, and critical infrastructure programmes.

## What they do

KRS Infotech supplies skilled professionals and managed delivery expertise to prime contractors, technology vendors, and mission-critical programmes across infrastructure, software engineering, and enterprise transformation. Their focus is on reliability and compliance within the aerospace, defence, and critical infrastructure sectors, covering cyber security, data analysis, quantum computing, and systems integration.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed IT and engineering delivery** - specialist resource and team-based delivery for defence programmes.
- **Cyber security services** - systems integrity and security capability for defence and critical infrastructure clients.
- **Data analysis** - data analysis capability for defence and critical infrastructure clients.
- **Quantum computing** - listed as a service capability alongside systems integration work, per their ADS Group member capability listing.
- **Software and systems training** - technical training for software and engineering teams.
