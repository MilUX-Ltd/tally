# Nortal

> Global digital transformation and cyber security company with over 20 years' experience delivering high-assurance projects for government and defence.

## What they do

Nortal partners with government and regulated-sector clients to design and implement secure, scalable digital solutions including cyber security, software engineering, and data analysis. The company operates across Europe, North America and the Middle East, with a UK office in London serving defence and national security customers.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber Security and Systems Integrity** - secure software design and assurance for high-assurance government environments.
- **Data Analysis and Software Modelling** - analytical and modelling services for complex defence and government programmes.
