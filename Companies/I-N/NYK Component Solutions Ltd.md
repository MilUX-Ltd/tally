# NYK Component Solutions Ltd

> Franchised distributor of military and aerospace-grade circular connectors and backshells, AS9100 and QPL approved.

## What they do

NYK Component Solutions distributes high-end military and aerospace circular connectors and backshells under full franchise and QPL approval arrangements, based in Hedge End, Southampton. The company holds stock for immediate supply, offers long-term supply agreements, and provides bespoke engineering solutions for non-standard interconnect requirements. It trades as part of Apollo Aerospace Components, itself part of Supply Technologies LLC (a Park-Ohio Holdings company).

## Products and solutions

- **Military circular connectors** - QPL-approved circular connectors to standards including MIL-DTL-38999, MIL-DTL-5015, MIL-DTL-26482 (Series II), M83723, EN2997 and ESC10, stocked for fast delivery.
- **Backshells** - Connector backshell accessories across standard and bespoke configurations, including franchised ranges from Compaero and EMCA Electro-Mechanic.
- **Bespoke interconnect solutions** - Custom interconnect assemblies and specials for requirements outside standard series.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
