# NYK Component Solutions Ltd

> Franchised distributor of military and aerospace-grade circular connectors and backshells, AS9100 and QPL approved.

## What they do

NYK Component Solutions distributes high-end military and aerospace circular connectors and backshells under full franchise and QPL approval arrangements. The company holds stock for immediate supply, offers long-term supply agreements, and provides bespoke engineering solutions for non-standard interconnect requirements.

## Products and solutions

- **Military circular connectors** - QPL-approved circular connectors to military and aerospace standards, stocked for fast delivery.
- **Backshells** - Connector backshell accessories across standard and bespoke configurations.
- **Bespoke interconnect solutions** - Custom interconnect assemblies and specials for requirements outside standard series.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100; QPL (Qualified Products List) approval
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
