# Linwave Technology Ltd

> Custom RF and microwave product specialist serving defence, avionics, marine and other sectors from its Lincoln facility.

## What they do

Linwave designs and manufactures novel custom RF and microwave modules and subsystems, working from bare die level upward to minimise size, weight and power. The company provides external engineering enterprise solutions that allow systems designers to integrate RF and software-hardware functions without expanding internal resource.

## Products and solutions

- **Custom RF and microwave modules** - bespoke assemblies from die level, optimised for SWaP across defence, avionics and marine platforms.
- **Surveillance electronics** - RF subsystems for surveillance applications.
- **Radar and countermeasure systems** - electronic subsystems for radar and EW platforms.
