# NOVA Defence Intelligence Systems
> Bristol-based startup building an agentic AI platform that automates the full UK DSAT lifecycle for Allied defence training.

## What they do
NOVA automates the Defence Systems Approach to Training (DSAT) lifecycle, including Training Needs Analysis, design, delivery and evaluation, mapped to UK JSP 822, US TRADOC 350-70 and NATO Bi-SC 075-003 doctrine. The platform is built on a doctrine corpus of over 78,000 searchable passages and generates SCORM and xAPI-compatible packages with citations back to source. It positions itself as the first agentic Allied Defence Training platform.

## Products and solutions
- **Force Multiplier** - Four autonomous AI agents covering the Analysis, Design, Delivery and Assurance stages of the DSAT cycle.
- **Command** - Full platform including Slides Creator, One-Click Course and cross-doctrinal fusion tools; unlimited AI generations.
- **Enterprise** - On-premises or air-gapped deployment for IL4/IL5 environments, with coalition data partitioning and SSO.
