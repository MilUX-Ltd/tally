# Morrison Satcom Ltd

> Specialist provider of transportable and deployable satellite communication systems for defence, emergency services and remote operations.

## What they do

Morrison Satcom Ltd, established in 2018 and based in Teesside, North East England, is a specialist service provider in transportable and deployable satellite communication systems. The company offers system integration, maintenance, repair and upgrade support to ensure reliable performance throughout the full lifecycle of tactical satcom equipment, supporting UK and international clients across defence, emergency services and remote operations.

## Products and solutions

- **System integration** - integration of transportable and deployable satcom systems.
- **Maintenance, repair and upgrade** - lifecycle support for tactical satcom equipment, including resolving faults and upgrading obsolete components.
- **Field-based and remote technical support** - support delivered on-site or remotely to extend system capability in line with evolving operational needs.
