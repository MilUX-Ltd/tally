# Morrison Satcom Ltd

> Specialist provider of transportable and deployable satellite communication systems for defence, emergency services and remote operations.

## What they do

Morrison Satcom supports clients across the full lifecycle of tactical satcom equipment, covering system integration, maintenance, repair and upgrade. The company provides both field-based and remote technical support to UK and international customers.
