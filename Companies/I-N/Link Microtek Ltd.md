# Link Microtek Ltd

> Designer, manufacturer and supplier of RF, microwave and optical components, systems and instrumentation for aerospace, defence and related sectors.

## What they do

Link Microtek develops and supplies RF and microwave components and systems, drawing on engineering heritage from GEC, Marconi, Racal and Filtronic. The company operates from sites in Basingstoke and Southampton, serving UK and international customers across aerospace, defence, telecommunications, scientific and medical markets.

## Products and solutions

- **Optical secure communications systems** - fibre and optical-path communication solutions for secure defence applications.
- **Microwave rotating joints** - precision rotary joints for radar and antenna platforms.
- **Antennas and combiners** - custom RF antenna designs and combining networks.
- **Material analysis equipment** - microwave-based instrumentation for materials characterisation.
- **RF and microwave components** - broadband passive and active components across the microwave spectrum.
