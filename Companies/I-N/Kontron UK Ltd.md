# Kontron UK Ltd

> Rugged embedded computing and avionics hardware manufacturer serving defence and aerospace platforms.

## What they do

Kontron manufactures ruggedised computing systems, avionics hardware, and in-flight entertainment hardware for aerospace and defence platforms. Their UK operation supports customers across the South East with embedded computing, display systems, and software integration spanning avionics, ICT hardware, and mechanical assemblies.

## Products and solutions

- **Ruggedised computing systems** - hardened hardware for defence and aerospace platform integration.
- **Avionics computers and displays** - certified avionics hardware including display and monitor systems.
- **In-flight entertainment hardware** - platform interior systems for defence and commercial aircraft.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
