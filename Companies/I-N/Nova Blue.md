# Nova Blue

> Cyber security advisory and technical services company, founded by former senior UK and Canadian government and military experts, specialising in defence cyber resilience.

## What they do

Nova Blue provides cyber security advisory and technical services to defence supply chain and government customers, drawing on decades of experience in defence and government contexts. The company focuses on the UK MOD Cyber Security Model and the Defence Cyber Certification scheme, helping organisations meet compliance requirements and improve cyber posture.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence Cyber Readiness Assessment** - instant snapshot tool measuring readiness against the UK MOD Cyber Security Model and Defence Cyber Certification (Lv0–Lv3).
- **Cyber Advisory Services** - strategic and technical advisory to build cyber resilience in defence and government environments.
- **Communication Systems Security** - cyber security services applied to communication systems and command and control.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials; Cyber Essentials Plus; IASME / NCSC Cyber Essentials; Defence Cyber Certification (DCC) Lv0–Lv3
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
