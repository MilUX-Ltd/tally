# NBOUND
> US defence advisory firm specialising in business development, defence acquisition strategy and programme management for dual-use startups, primes and academic institutions.
## What they do
NBOUND provides defence acquisition strategy, SBIR/STTR advisory, and programme management to companies seeking access to the US defence market. The team is composed of former military officers with experience across federal acquisitions, defence programmes and DoD operations.

NBOUND has supported more than 100 companies through SBIR and STTR programmes, reporting over $75M in R&D funding secured for clients. Services cover the full acquisition cycle: SBIR/STTR, FAR-based procurement, Commercial Solutions Openings (CSO) and Phase III contracts. Clients span pre-seed to Series C, across most defence-adjacent industries.
