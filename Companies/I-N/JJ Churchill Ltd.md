# JJ Churchill Ltd

> Precision engineering manufacturer producing high-quality components and assemblies for the aerospace, defence and power generation sectors.

## What they do

JJ Churchill operates as a first-tier sub-contractor, taking supply-chain management responsibility and applying advanced CNC machining processes to high-value aerospace and defence components. The business works across engine components, weapon system parts and structural assemblies, using specialist materials including titanium, nickel and aluminium alloys.

## Products and solutions

- **Precision-machined components** - 5-axis CNC milling and turning of complex aerospace and defence parts.
- **Propulsion components** - engine components, rotors and propellers for aerospace platforms.
- **Weapon system components** - parts for missiles, rockets and torpedo/anti-submarine systems.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, NADCAP (CP, EDM, NDT, WLD)
