# Nuvia UK

> Engineering and services partner for regulated nuclear, defence and industrial environments.

## What they do

NUVIA is an international engineering and services company specialising in highly regulated environments across nuclear, defence, healthcare and industrial facilities. NUVIA UK, part of the VINCI Group, delivers engineering, procurement and construction (EPC), radiation monitoring and lifecycle services across civil and military nuclear and defence programmes. Its UK head office is at Birchwood Park, Risley, Warrington.

## Products and solutions

- **Engineering and EPC services** - design, construction, operation and dismantling of regulated facilities.
- **Radiation monitoring** - detection and safety technologies.
- **Professional and technical services** - independent verification and lifecycle support.
