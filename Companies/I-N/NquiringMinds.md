# NquiringMinds

> Southampton-based trusted AI and cyber security company building secure data-sharing and analytics for defence, government and industry.

## What they do

NquiringMinds describes itself as a trusted AI company building advanced analytics on top of solid cyber security principles. Founded in 2010 by AI and cyber security experts from the mobile and defence research industries, it builds secure data-sharing solutions for central government, the wider public sector, and industrial manufacturing and agriculture. Its stated areas of expertise are AI-powered data analytics, secure data sharing and cyber-resilient Internet of Things, with customers across smart cities, Industry 4.0, defence, agriculture, telecoms and health and social care.

## Products and solutions

- **TDX (Trusted Data Exchange)** - cloud-based secure data-sharing and AI-powered analytics platform built for security and scale, handling large-scale and real-time IoT data with owner-controlled access and selective sharing.
- **InterliNQ** - secure IoT hub technology for small, low-power devices, providing security from hardware through to a PKI certificate-based application layer using a peer-to-peer cryptographic model.

Related consortium initiatives include TAIBOM (a trustable AI bill of materials, led by NquiringMinds with partners including BAE Systems and BSI, funded by the UKRI Technology Missions Fund) and Secure Networking by Design work with TechWorks and the University of Oxford.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** Participant in the Defence and Security Accelerator (DASA) innovation programme; UKRI Technology Missions Fund (TAIBOM); research collaborations with the University of Oxford and TechWorks.
