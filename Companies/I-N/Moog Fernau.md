# Moog Fernau Ltd

> UK supplier of ground-based navigation aids and defence communications and datalink systems, part of Moog Inc.

## What they do

Moog Fernau, based in Luton, is a supplier of ground-based navigation aids with experience across all aspects of navigation. Its products and systems have been installed for civil aviation, maritime authorities and military forces worldwide, with stated customers including the US Navy, UK MOD, US Air Force and US Marine Corps. Trading as part of Moog Inc, the business descends from Fernau Avionics, which became part of Moog in 2009.

## Products and solutions

- **DME** - distance measuring equipment, a ground-based navigation aid.
- **VHF/UHF DF and Naval DF** - direction-finding systems for aviation and maritime use.
- **Auto-Triangulation (Auto-T)** - automatic triangulation for locating transmissions.
- **TACAN** - fixed tactical air navigation, with man-portable, mobile and deployable variants.
- **Mode-S radar transmitters** - secondary surveillance radar transmitters.
- **Tarsier** - automatic foreign object debris (FOD) detection system for civil and military airfields.
