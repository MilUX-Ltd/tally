# K Sharp

> Human factors and human science research consultancy working across defence, security and public safety.

## What they do

K Sharp provides strategic and applied human sciences research and human-centred design to help clients understand and predict user behaviour, optimising the safety and usability of systems, processes, products and training. The team describes over 12 years of delivery into defence, alongside security, public safety, health, aviation, space and smart technology, and has worked with MOD, Dstl, BAE Systems, QinetiQ, General Dynamics, Leonardo, Raytheon and the UK Space Agency.

## Products and solutions

- **Human factors design and assessment** - human-centred design of software, hardware, control rooms and human-machine interfaces, plus usability, workload and situational awareness assessment.
- **Human factors integration and project management** - human factors integration, agile and risk management support across programme lifecycles.
- **Training** - ergonomics and human factors training, manual handling, simulation and synthetic blended training, and training needs analysis.
- **Research and analysis** - task and systems analysis, human error and reliability analysis, and applied research into human behavioural sciences, command and control, and information superiority.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR; Digital Outcomes and Specialists (DOS); Sell2Wales; BAE Systems ASC and Human and Social Science Research Capability (HSSRC) panels.
- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus.
- **Memberships:** Chartered Institute of Ergonomics and Human Factors (CIEHF), registered consultancy; Aerospace Wales Forum.
