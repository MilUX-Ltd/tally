# K Sharp

> Human factors and human science research consultancy working across defence, security and public safety.

## What they do

K Sharp provides strategic and applied human sciences research and human factors consultancy, specialising in cultural and behavioural analysis and technology adoption. The team uses human-centred design to help clients understand and predict user behaviour and adopt the right technologies. It describes more than a decade of delivery into defence, alongside health, aviation, space and smart technology.

## Products and solutions

- **Human factors consultancy** - human-centred design and human factors integration across the product lifecycle, from research through to design and delivery.
- **Applied human science research** - cultural and behavioural analysis, technology adoption studies and user research.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** Chartered Institute of Ergonomics and Human Factors (CIEHF); Aerospace Wales Forum.
