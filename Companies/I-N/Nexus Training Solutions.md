# Nexus Training Solutions
> Australian training and capability-management company serving military, government and commercial customers globally.
## What they do
Nexus Training Solutions (NTS) designs and delivers training using a user-centred approach, blending technical instruction with collective and immersive methods. Capability spans joint fires training, JTAC/JFO programme development, simulation development, e-learning, and government and emergency services training packages.

Clients include the Australian Federal Police, Trinidad and Tobago specialist security forces, Georgian CBRN units and Jordanian JTAC capability development. NTS LLC in North Carolina is the US subsidiary of Nexus Training Solutions Pty Ltd, Australia.
