# Micropol Fiberoptic Ltd

> Swedish manufacturer of military-grade fibre optic connectors and data transmission products, with a UK presence in London.

## What they do

Micropol Fiberoptic develops and manufactures battle-tested fibre optic products refined over 30 years in close cooperation with the Swedish Armed Forces. Their product range enables high-efficiency data transmission in the toughest environments, including MIL-spec expanded beam connectors capable of 40 Gbit/s throughput compliant with MIL-DTL-83526.

## Products and solutions

- **Falcon Expanded Beam Connectors** - 12-channel junior connector with collimated light beam, compliant with MIL-DTL-83526, supporting up to 40 Gbit/s.
