# Meta Mission Data Limited

> Airborne surveillance and ISTAR consultancy specialising in Tactical Data Links, communications and information assurance.

## What they do

Meta Mission Data provides consultancy and airborne surveillance capabilities across the aerospace, defence and security sectors. Their subject matter experts cover ISTAR, Tactical Data Links, communications, information assurance, UAS, AIS and cyber security.

## Products and solutions

- **ISTAR and surveillance systems** - Systems engineering and advisory across airborne surveillance and intelligence, surveillance, target acquisition and reconnaissance.
- **Tactical Data Links** - Technical consultancy and capability support for TDL integration.
- **Cyber and information assurance** - Consultancy on encryption, cyber protection and data security.
- **UAS capabilities** - Unmanned systems and remote-controlled systems products and advisory.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 27001:2022, Cyber Essentials Plus, ITAR Approval, Def Stan 05-135
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
