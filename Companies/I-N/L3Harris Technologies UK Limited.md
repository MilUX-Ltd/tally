# L3Harris Technologies UK Limited

> Major US-headquartered defence prime with over 1,400 people across 12 UK sites, delivering ISR, weapons integration, sensors and pilot training capability.

## What they do

L3Harris Technologies UK delivers capabilities across air, land, sea, space and cyber for military, security and commercial customers worldwide. Core activities include strategic and tactical ISR through the RC-135-based Airseeker/Rivet Joint programme, carriage and release solutions for integrating weapons onto crewed and uncrewed platforms, and commercial aviation pilot training using high-fidelity full-flight simulators.

## Products and solutions

- **ISR systems** - intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance capability including Airseeker/Rivet Joint.
- **Weapons and stores integration** - carriage, release and interfacing solutions for crewed and uncrewed air platforms.
- **Flight simulation and pilot training** - full-flight simulators and flight data analysis services.
- **Optical and sensor components** - optical subcomponents, sensors, transducers and stabilisation systems.
- **Countermeasures and covert systems** - electronic countermeasures and specialist covert technologies.
