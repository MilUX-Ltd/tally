# Kuehne + Nagel

> Global logistics and supply chain provider with specialist aerospace and defence freight and contract logistics capability.

## What they do

Kuehne + Nagel is a leading global freight and logistics business operating across seafreight, airfreight, contract logistics, and overland transport in more than 100 countries. In aerospace and defence, they provide integrated supply chain solutions covering time-critical freight, warehousing, and IT-enabled logistics for OEMs and their supply chains.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace airfreight** - time-critical and high-value air cargo solutions for defence and aerospace customers.
- **Contract logistics** - warehousing and fulfilment integrated with defence supply chain requirements.
- **Integrated supply chain solutions** - IT-backed logistics platforms for demand-driven aerospace and defence operations.
