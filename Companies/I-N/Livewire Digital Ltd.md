# Livewire Digital Ltd

> Developer of RazorLink hybrid connectivity software enabling mission-critical satellite and terrestrial communications for mobile defence deployments.

## What they do

Livewire Digital specialises in software that accelerates and bonds multiple terrestrial and non-terrestrial network links into a single resilient connection. Its RazorLink technology has been developed over 30 years of enabling satellite and terrestrial communications for mobile and extreme-environment deployments across land, sea and air.

## Products and solutions

- **RazorLink** - hybrid connectivity software that bonds satellite and terrestrial networks to deliver fast, resilient communications for mobile and deployed platforms.
