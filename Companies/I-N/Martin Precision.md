# Martin Precision

> Scottish precision engineering manufacturer supplying components and assemblies to aerospace and defence customers.

## What they do

Martin Precision, established in 1993, manufactures precision engineered components and assemblies for quality-critical industries including aerospace and defence, operating from the Caldwellside Industrial Estate in Lanark. The company focuses on on-time delivery and demanding quality standards, supported by continual investment in equipment and processes.
