# Martin Precision

> Scottish precision engineering manufacturer supplying components and assemblies to aerospace and defence customers.

## What they do

Martin Precision, established in 1993, manufactures precision engineered components and assemblies for quality-critical industries including aerospace and defence, operating from the Caldwellside Industrial Estate in Lanark. The company focuses on on-time delivery and demanding quality standards, supported by continual investment in equipment and processes. It became employee-owned in 2023 and was acquired by Swedish investment firm Evity Invest AB in March 2026.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC machining and turning** - 5 and 9-axis milling and multi-spindle turning of hard and soft alloys, including titanium, steel, aluminium and superalloys.
- **Component repair and refurbishment** - restoration of precision components alongside new manufacture.
- **Kits and assemblies** - sub-assembly build from own and third-party components, including product development and new product introduction support.
- **Non-destructive testing** - in-house NDT capability since 1995, alongside clean room facilities and inspection.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100 Rev D, BS EN ISO 9001:2015, Nadcap (Non-Destructive Testing, First Article Inspection).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
