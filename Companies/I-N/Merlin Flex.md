# Merlin Flex

> Manufacturer of flexible and flex-rigid multilayer printed circuit boards and interconnection systems for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Merlin Flex combines the heritage of two original UK flexible circuit manufacturers, Flex-Ability Ltd and MBM Technologies Ltd, with over 33 years of manufacturing experience. The company operates a 35,000 sq ft facility in Hartlepool producing flexible and flex-rigid multilayer PCBs, managing turnkey projects from design through manufacture and assembly.

## Products and solutions

- **Flexible PCBs** - single and multilayer flexible printed circuits for high-reliability applications.
- **Flex-rigid multilayer PCBs** - combined flexible and rigid circuit constructions.
- **PCB assemblies** - turnkey assembly and interconnection systems.
