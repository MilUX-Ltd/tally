# Mercury Computer Systems

> Defence electronics company delivering secure processing technologies, mission computers and RF subsystems across land, sea, air, space and cyberspace domains.

## What they do

Mercury designs and manufactures secure embedded processing modules, mission computers, rugged rack-mount servers, safety-critical avionics, RF components and multi-function subsystems purpose-built for aerospace and defence. Their solutions are deployed on more than 300 platforms and programmes, adapting commercial silicon technology for mission-critical applications.

## Products and solutions

- **Secure embedded processing modules** - ruggedised compute for mission-critical environments.
- **Mission computers** - purpose-built platforms for aerospace and defence applications.
- **RF components and assemblies** - RF subsystems for radar, EW and ISR.
- **Safety-critical avionics** - airworthy computing and avionics systems.
