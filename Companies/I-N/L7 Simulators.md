# L7 Simulators

> Ukrainian developer of laser- and VR-based combat training simulators for military forces.

## What they do

Since 2019 L7 has built combat training simulators for the Armed Forces of Ukraine, the National Guard of Ukraine and other security forces. The company develops the full simulator development cycle, from concept and software through to physical modelling of combat scenarios. Using laser and VR technologies it simulates a range of combat scenarios in mobile, compact systems deployable at unit locations. The brand was renamed from PSS by Logics7 to L7 Simulators in June 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **UNITS ecosystem** - combat training platform with 50+ modules simulating weapons in service, supporting individual and multi-group training across small arms, grenade launchers, MANPADS and anti-tank weapons.
- **LT Laser Simulator** - laser-based live firing simulator.
- **Complex UNITS VR** - VR training complex.
- **Vartovyi (Watchmen)** - training simulator for interceptor drone crews; interface mimics standard ground control stations.
- **Dronobii and Dvobiy** - FPV drone shooting and training simulators.
- **Citadele** - multimedia classroom training system.
