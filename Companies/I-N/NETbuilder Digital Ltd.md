# NETbuilder Digital Ltd

> Cyber, digital, and data consultancy with a skills enablement academy, serving government and defence alongside commercial markets for 25 years.

## What they do

NETbuilder Digital provides consultancy, managed services, and workforce transformation across cyber, infrastructure, cloud, data, and test disciplines to government and defence clients. Alongside project delivery, the company operates a skills academy that supports clients in building internal teams and sustaining service continuity across cyber, firmware, cloud, and related domains. The company states it is an NCSC-approved Cyber Advisor for UK small and medium-sized enterprises.

## Products and solutions

- **Penetration testing** - security testing delivered by a dedicated pen-testing team.
- **Cyber Security Blueprint** - frameworks, leadership models and roadmaps for building and growing SOC protection.
- **Digital Forensics and Incident Response** - a dedicated DFIR team investigating threats and managing containment, eradication and recovery.
- **Legacy Transformation** - SIEM migration services, with more than 100 SIEM migrations stated as completed.
- **Reset & Relaunch** - course-correction for stalled or underperforming cyber security programmes.
- **Health Checks** - evaluation of an organisation's cyber stack performance.
- **The Academy (SkillsNow)** - a hire, train, deploy model delivering bespoke technical training academies; the company states over 10,000 engineers trained and eight of the top ten global IT service providers among its customers.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 9001, ISO 27001:2022, ISO 14001, JOSCAR Registered
- **Memberships:** ADS Group; NCSC Cyber Advisor scheme member
