# Marlborough Communications

> Supplies advanced RF communications and signals intelligence technology to defence customers, drawing on over 30 years in the sector.

## What they do

Marlborough Communications Ltd (MCL) designs and supplies electronic and surveillance technology for RF communications and signals intelligence applications. The company develops solutions in-house and sources complementary products from partners worldwide, supporting equipment through its full service life. Its customer base spans defence and security organisations, where it holds preferred supplier status with a number of long-term clients.
