# Indeloy IT Solutions Ltd

> IT procurement and services specialist for the defence and security sector.

## What they do

Indeloy provides hardware, software, configuration, and managed services to defence and security customers, drawing on over 20 years of experience across vendors, distributors, and resellers. Partners include Microsoft, Dell, Cisco, Lenovo, HP, Adobe, and VMware.
