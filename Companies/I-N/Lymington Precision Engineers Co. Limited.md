# Lymington Precision Engineers Co. Limited

> Precision engineering manufacturer supplying machined components to the defence, space, marine, nuclear, telecommunications, and oil and gas sectors since 1980.

## What they do

Founded in 1980, Lymington Precision Engineers (LPE) operates a 92,000 sq ft facility specialising in the manufacture, inspection, and supply of precision engineered components. Capabilities include circumferential and TIG welding, EDM, fabrication, pressure testing, assembly, kitting, and project management across defence, space, marine, and nuclear programmes.
