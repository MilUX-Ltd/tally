# Infinity Systems Engineering Ltd

> Systems and safety engineering consultancy serving the UK defence industry.

## What they do

Infinity Systems Engineering is a systems and safety engineering consultancy based in Bristol, focused on the successful delivery of engineering projects across multiple sectors. The company began operating in the defence sector in 2019, working with defence companies on airborne systems, and its engineers and subject matter experts support integrated safety and systems engineering work across UK defence and aerospace programmes, with customers based across the UK and Europe. It also provides safety engineering input for the launch and recovery of micro satellite systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems engineering** - systems engineering support across defence, aerospace and space programmes.
- **Safety engineering** - safety management, hazard identification and analysis, safety case reports and functional safety assessments, including work to Defence Standard 00-56, IEC 61508, IEC 61511, IEC 62061 and IEC 13849.
- **Reliability engineering** - reliability engineering support for defence and aerospace platforms.
- **Environmental engineering** - environmental engineering and legislation compliance assessments, including UKCA/CE marking.
