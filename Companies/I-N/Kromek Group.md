# Kromek Group plc

> AIM-listed developer of radiation and biological threat detection technology based on cadmium zinc telluride sensors.

## What they do

Kromek Group supplies and develops radiation detection solutions based on cadmium zinc telluride (CZT) and other materials, for the medical imaging, civil nuclear, security screening and homeland security markets. It also works with OEMs, governments and other agencies to design and build new solutions. The company is headquartered at NETPark in Sedgefield, County Durham, and operates across two continents.

## Products and solutions

- **D3S** - handheld radioisotope identification devices for CBRN detection, including the D3S ID and personal radiation detector variants.
- **D5 RIID** - high-accuracy, ruggedised handheld radioisotope identifier.
- **AARM** - autonomous airborne radiation monitoring system for UAV-based survey.
- **Biological threat detection** - bio-security detection products developed for government and defence customers.
- **CZT gamma detectors** - detectors and modules for SPECT medical imaging and advanced imaging applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
- **Other:** Listed on the London Stock Exchange AIM market (ticker KMK). Registered on the UK Government's Radiological Nuclear Detection Framework since 2024.
