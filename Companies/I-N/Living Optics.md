# Living Optics

> Developer of hyperspectral cameras targeting defence, security and commercial sensing applications.

## What they do

Living Optics develops hyperspectral imaging cameras designed to bring spectral sensing capability to a wider market. Their devices capture information across the electromagnetic spectrum invisible to conventional cameras and the human eye, enabling applications in surveillance, reconnaissance and target identification.
