# Living Optics

> Developer of hyperspectral cameras targeting defence, security and commercial sensing applications.

## What they do

Living Optics develops hyperspectral imaging cameras that capture the full colour spectrum, not just red, green and blue, at every pixel, revealing material and environmental information invisible to conventional cameras or the human eye. Its core product captures 96 spectral bands across 440 to 900 nanometres alongside a 5-megapixel RGB image at video frame rate. The company states target markets spanning food, healthcare, agriculture and general industry alongside security and national security, where it positions the camera for tactical decision-making, anomaly and camouflage detection, and next-generation surveillance.

## Products and solutions

- **Living Optics Camera** - the core snapshot hyperspectral sensor, capturing spatial and spectral data simultaneously at video frame rates.
- **Camera Kit** - the camera plus interface card and cables, for customers supplying their own edge-compute hardware.
- **Development Kit** - the camera bundled with an NVIDIA Jetson AGX Orin edge-compute platform, preloaded software and SDK, described by the company as sufficient to build production-grade applications from day one.
- **Living Optics Software / SDK** - a software suite for camera control, data acquisition, visualisation and analysis, with a developer API.

The company's security market page states the camera includes local compute capable of computer vision inference at the network edge, framed for tactical use, and describes applications including camouflage and low-observable material detection and target identification, tracking and reacquisition.
