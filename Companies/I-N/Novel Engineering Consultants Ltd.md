# Novel Engineering Consultants Ltd

> Aerospace, defence and eVTOL engineering consultancy providing systems and equipment services to OEMs and prime aircraft manufacturers, based in Nailsworth, Gloucestershire.

## What they do

Novel Engineering Consultants is a high-niche engineering consultancy founded by experienced engineers for aerospace engineering customers. It works across three capability areas: eVTOL, defence and civil aviation. Its engineers describe centuries of combined aerospace design and certification experience, gained as experts and chief engineers at various airframers and Tier 1 suppliers, and the company states a client portfolio that includes major aerospace OEMs and primes such as Airbus, Mitsubishi, Safran and Turkish Aerospace.

## Products and solutions

- **Safety and certification-led system architecture design** - eVTOL systems engineering support, working alongside a customer's own team to break down aircraft complexity and design for certifiability.
- **Certification gap analysis** - design audit of an eVTOL aircraft or system against certification standards, with a report on gaps and suggested improvements.
- **Guidance and training** - informal and formal training in Novel's technical specialisms, delivered alongside engineering engagements.
- **Defence systems engineering** - mechanical and electrical system design, providing analytical and safety support for defence customers.
- **Civil aerospace certification** - aerospace system and equipment certification, including product acceleration from TRL 3 through to certification.

Stated technical specialisms include safety-led design, certification knowledge, flight control systems design, electric propulsion systems, electric actuation systems design, hybrid power systems, and equipment and system sizing and design.
