# Neros Technologies

> US drone manufacturer building tactical, attritable one-way attack drones for Western military forces, with a new UK subsidiary.

## What they do

Neros makes tactical, attritable, one-way drones intended to give Western military forces an asymmetric advantage. The company says it was founded to onshore the manufacturing of drones critical to a modern arsenal, building first-person-view (FPV) systems and AI-enabled mission software with a secure supply chain free of critical Chinese components. It is headquartered in El Segundo, California, with manufacturing in California and additional offices including a UK subsidiary.

## Products and solutions

- **Archer** - mass-produced FPV drone built for modular payloads and resilient communications, with an NDAA-compliant supply chain; selected for the US Defense Innovation Unit's Blue UAS list.
- **Archer Strike** - kinetic-enabled FPV variant for lethal effects at range in contested environments.
- **Archer Fiber** - fibre-optic-controlled FPV drone, jam-resistant with no radio-frequency signature, launched with partner Kela Technologies and marketed as an NDAA-compliant fibre-optic FPV drone.
- **Crossbow** - tactical ground control station with electronic-warfare resistance at range.
- **Flatbow** - ground control system named alongside Archer in the US Army Purpose-Built Attritable Systems selection.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Blue UAS listed (US Defense Innovation Unit); NDAA-compliant supply chain.
