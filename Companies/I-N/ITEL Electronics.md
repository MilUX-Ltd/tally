# ITEL Electronics Limited

> Family-owned manufacturer of cable harness systems and electrical interconnect solutions for aerospace, space, defence, marine and commercial applications.

## What they do

ITEL Electronics is a family-owned business based in Swindon, Wiltshire, designing and supplying electrical interconnect systems. The company designs and manufactures high performance cable harness systems and electrical boxes for aerospace, space, defence, marine and commercial applications, working closely with the aerospace and defence sectors on cable harness design, cable assemblies and wiring harness manufacture.

## Products and solutions

- **Cable harness systems** - design and manufacture of high performance cable harnesses and wiring harnesses.
- **Cable assemblies** - bespoke interconnect assemblies for aerospace, space, defence and marine applications.
- **Electro-mechanical boxes** - electrical box builds and electro-mechanical assemblies.
