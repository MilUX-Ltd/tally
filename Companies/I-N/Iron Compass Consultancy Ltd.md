# Iron Compass Consultancy Ltd

> Defence market-entry consultancy helping US and Canadian defence and space firms access UK, European, and allied government markets.

## What they do

Iron Compass was founded in 2023 by consultants with operational military, diplomatic attaché, and security assistance backgrounds. It helps small and medium-sized US and Canadian defence and space companies engage with governments and industry across the Atlantic in support of allied priorities. In 2025 the firm expanded its reach through a network of US-based partner consultants.
