# MDA Space and Robotics

> UK arm of Canadian space technology company MDA, delivering space robotics, sensors, geo-intelligence, and satellite communications systems.

## What they do

MDA Space and Robotics Limited operates from the Harwell Campus in Oxfordshire as the UK entity of MDA, a company with a 50-year track record in space systems. Capabilities span space-based LIDAR, infrared cameras, laser range finders and positioning systems, space robotic servicing, satellite communications infrastructure, and geo-intelligence services.

## Products and solutions

- **Space-based sensors** - LIDAR, IR cameras, and laser positioning systems for satellite and planetary applications.
- **Space robotics** - robotic servicing systems for on-orbit operations.
- **Geo-intelligence** - earth observation and spatial data services.
- **Communications systems** - satellite communications for programmes including the ISS and Lunar Gateway.
