# MDA Space and Robotics

> UK arm of Canadian space technology company MDA, delivering space robotics, sensors, geo-intelligence, and satellite communications systems.

## What they do

MDA Space and Robotics Limited operates from the Harwell Campus in Oxfordshire as the UK entity of MDA, a company with a 50-year track record in space systems. Capabilities span space-based LIDAR, infrared cameras, laser range finders and positioning systems, space robotic servicing, satellite communications infrastructure, and geo-intelligence services. The Harwell site supports robotics and on-orbit servicing programmes, including work with the European Space Agency, alongside laser rangefinder development for lunar lander missions.

## Products and solutions

- **Space-based sensors** - LIDAR, IR cameras, and laser positioning systems for satellite and planetary applications.
- **Space robotics** - robotic servicing systems for on-orbit operations, including European Space Agency programmes supported from the Harwell site.
- **RADARSAT-2 and MDA CHORUS** - synthetic aperture radar earth observation satellites and constellation, providing wide-area coverage through to very high resolution imagery.
- **Geo-intelligence** - earth observation and spatial data services, including maritime domain awareness and dark vessel detection.
- **Communications systems** - satellite communications for programmes including the ISS and Lunar Gateway.
