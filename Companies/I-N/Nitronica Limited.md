# Nitronica Limited

> Contract electronics manufacturer based in Northern Ireland serving defence, aerospace and industrial customers across the UK, Ireland and Europe.

## What they do

Nitronica (trading name of Hinchtech Limited) provides contract electronic manufacturing services from its site in Ballynahinch, County Down, covering customers in the UK, Republic of Ireland, Europe and beyond. The company holds SC21 Bronze and Operational Excellence accreditations alongside ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and IPC 610 Class 3 standards.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 13485, IPC 610 Class 3, SC21 Operational Excellence, SC21 Bronze
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
