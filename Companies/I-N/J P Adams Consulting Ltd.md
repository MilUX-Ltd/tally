# J P Adams Consulting Ltd

> Engineering consultancy providing systems engineering, system safety and cyber expertise to the MoD and defence contractors, with a focus on maritime platforms and electronic warfare.

## What they do

J P Adams Consulting delivers traditional and model-based systems engineering, system safety, and safety-critical software support to the MoD and its supply chain. The practice has specialist expertise in maritime applications (ships and submarines) and in radar and electronic warfare systems.
