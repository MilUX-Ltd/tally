# i3 Gen Ltd

> Hampshire-based specialist in narrative wargaming, strategic planning and strategic communications for defence, government and commercial clients.

## What they do

i3 Gen's core capability is narrative wargaming: immersive, competitive simulations that enable senior leaders to rehearse strategic decisions in realistic but safe-to-fail environments. Unlike rule-bound games, its approach blends structured planning, expert adjudication and compelling narrative to stress-test policies, explore future risks and surface hidden assumptions.
