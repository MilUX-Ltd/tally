# i3 Gen Ltd

> Hampshire-based specialist in narrative wargaming, strategic planning and strategic communications for defence, government and commercial clients.

## What they do

i3 Gen's core capability is narrative wargaming: immersive, competitive simulations that enable senior leaders to rehearse strategic decisions in realistic but safe-to-fail environments. Unlike rule-bound games, its approach blends structured planning, expert adjudication and compelling narrative to stress-test policies, explore future risks and surface hidden assumptions.

## Products and solutions

- **Narrative wargaming** - an enhanced and extended matrix wargaming approach following a five-stage methodology (Understand, Design, Develop, Deliver, Report) that combines structured planning, wargaming and story-crafting.
- **Strategy wargaming** - facilitator-led competitive scenario exercises for senior leaders to rehearse strategic decisions.
- **Strategic planning** - turning insights into objectives, actionable plans and aligned leadership direction.
- **Strategic communications** - narrative development for strategy, including information operations, story-crafting informed by behavioural psychology, and digital and social media strategy.
