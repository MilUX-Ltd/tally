# Morson Praxis

> Engineering consultancy delivering integrated design, analysis and industrial capability across aerospace and defence, from concept through to certification and in-service support.

## What they do

Morson Praxis is an engineering consultancy delivering integrated design, analysis and industrial capability across aerospace and defence, marine and manufacturing, civil and energy, and digital sectors. The company supports programmes from concept through to certification, production and in-service support, operating within highly regulated environments including Part 21J and military frameworks. Morson Praxis was formed from the consolidation of legacy Morson Group engineering brands, including Morson Projects, Ematics, Waldeck, Orange Solutions and Astraeus.

## Products and solutions

- **Aerospace and defence engineering** - structural design and stress analysis, tooling development, composite structures and systems integration across the product lifecycle; manufacturing engineering; high-security site engineering including MOD installations.
- **Marine and naval engineering** - naval architecture, structural finite element analysis, hydrodynamic assessment, signature modelling, blast and impact analysis, and ship stability analysis, with named work on the Dreadnought submarine, Type 26 frigate and Type 45 destroyer repowering programmes.
- **Submarines and underwater systems** - naval architecture, structural assessment, materials selection, corrosion analysis, hydrodynamics and signatures, electrical/control and instrumentation (EC&I) integration, shock analysis and control software development.
- **Land systems** - military land systems design, vehicle platform engineering, soldier systems, defence infrastructure, through-life support and signature management.
- **Civil aviation certification** - UK CAA Part 21J Design Organisation Approval, covering major and minor modifications, supplementary type certificates, structural analysis and electrical/avionic design certification.
- **Electrical and avionic design** - specialist RF, EMC and WiFi support, and ground, flight and EMC testing.
- **Systems engineering and integration** - cargo handling, flight controls, fuel systems, HVAC, hydraulic assemblies and landing gear, for clients including Airbus, BAE Systems, Rolls-Royce and Leonardo.
- **Manufacturing engineering** - CNC programming, tooling design and manufacture, and collaborative robot (cobot) integration.
- **Digital and technology consulting** - data strategy and governance, analytics, AI and machine learning (predictive modelling, computer vision, generative AI), cyber security and assurance, software engineering, and digital capture including UAV aerial surveys and a 3D digital twin visualisation environment (EVE).
- **Supportability engineering** - Integrated Logistics Support (ILS) across defence and industrial programmes.
