# Morson Praxis

> Engineering consultancy delivering integrated design, analysis and industrial capability across aerospace and defence, from concept through to certification and in-service support.

## What they do

Morson Praxis provides multi-disciplinary engineering teams covering structural, systems, electrical, manufacturing and certification disciplines. The company operates within highly regulated environments including Part 21J and military frameworks, supporting programmes across aerospace, marine, civil and digital sectors.
