# NCC

> The National Composites Centre , a High Value Manufacturing Catapult organisation bridging composites research and industrial application across the defence and aerospace sectors.

## What they do

NCC is part of the High Value Manufacturing Catapult and the University of Bristol. It translates advanced composites research into industrial impact across the entire product lifecycle, from concept to end-of-life, working with companies of all sizes including defence and aerospace primes and their supply chains.
