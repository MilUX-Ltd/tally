# Intertek

> Global testing, inspection, and certification company providing quality assurance and equipment testing services to defence and armed forces customers.

## What they do

Intertek provides testing, inspection, and certification across a broad range of sectors, with a dedicated defence capability covering the testing and certification of equipment for demanding operational environments. The company partners with defence organisations and armed forces supply chains to ensure equipment is safe and effective. Intertek operates over 1,000 laboratories and offices across more than 100 countries, employing over 40,000 people.
