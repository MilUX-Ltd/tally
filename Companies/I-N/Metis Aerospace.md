# Metis Aerospace Limited

> Independent technical advisory and products across SIGINT, Electronic Warfare, CEMA, counter-UAS and UAV systems.

## What they do

Metis Aerospace provides independent technical advice and support to military and commercial customers. The company draws on a high proportion of ex-military specialists to deliver in the fields of Signals Intelligence, Electronic Warfare, Cyber and Electromagnetic Activities, counter-UAS detect/track/identify, and UAV systems.

## Products and solutions

- **Counter-UAS (DTI)** - Detection, tracking and identification solutions for counter-drone applications.
- **SIGINT and EW systems** - Signals intelligence and electronic warfare advisory and equipment.
- **CEMA support** - Cyber and Electromagnetic Activities services and consultancy.
- **UAV systems** - Unmanned aerial systems products and advisory.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
