# Mission Systems Wimborne Ltd (Eaton)

> World-leading designer and manufacturer of hose and drogue aerial refuelling systems and defensive aids sub-systems for fixed-wing, rotary and UAV platforms.

## What they do

Mission Systems Wimborne is the largest global supplier of hose and drogue air-to-air refuelling systems, covering design, development, qualification, certification and manufacture for defence customers worldwide. The company also produces actuation equipment, refuelling probes, launch systems and Defensive Aids Sub-Systems (DASS) for a range of fixed-wing, rotorcraft and UAV platforms.

## Products and solutions

- **Hose and drogue aerial refuelling systems** - Design, development and manufacture for fast jets, tankers and multi-role aircraft.
- **Refuelling probes** - Receiver-side probe systems for fixed-wing and rotary platforms.
- **Defensive Aids Sub-Systems (DASS)** - Integrated defensive systems for combat aircraft.
- **Actuation equipment** - Control surface and systems actuation for fixed-wing, rotorcraft and UAVs.
