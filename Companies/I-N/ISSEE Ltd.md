# ISSEE Ltd

> Specialist training provider for security, explosives, and defence skills, covering EOD, C-IED, CBRN, and protection disciplines to an externally accredited standard.

## What they do

ISSEE delivers externally verified and accredited training across the full spectrum of security and explosives disciplines, from national-level homeland security through to operational EOD, C-IED, IEDD, forensic exploitation, marksmanship, and CBRN environment training. The company also supports the safe management and disposal of munitions and explosives for government and commercial clients.

## Products and solutions

- **EOD and C-IED/IEDD training** - Operator and team-level courses in explosive ordnance disposal and counter-IED techniques.
- **CBRN training** - Training for operating in chemical, biological, radiological, and nuclear environments.
- **Specialist protection and marksmanship** - Advanced protection and sniper training programmes.
- **Explosives storage and management** - Compliance and safe-handling training for government and commercial munitions users.
- **Forensic awareness and technical exploitation** - Intelligence-exploitation training for specialist teams.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, Cyber Essentials, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
