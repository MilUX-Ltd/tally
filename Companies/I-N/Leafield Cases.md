# Leafield Cases

> UK designer and manufacturer of tough, waterproof rotational-moulded protective cases and packaging for use in extreme environments, including defence applications.

## What they do

Leafield Cases, a division of Leafield Environmental, designs and manufactures custom protective cases and packaging using rotational moulding technology. Products are built to withstand extreme environments and are supplied to defence and other demanding sectors; all design and manufacture is carried out under one roof in Corsham, Wiltshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Custom Protective Cases** - bespoke waterproof, impact-resistant cases sized and tooled to customer specification using modular rotational moulding.
