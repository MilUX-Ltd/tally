# inink

> Defence engagement consultancy specialising in communications, branding and copywriting for deep-tech companies selling into defence and national security.

## What they do

inink is a communications consultancy working wherever deep tech meets defence and national security. Founded in 2023 by two specialists who previously worked together at Improbable Defence (now Skyral), it helps technology developers take innovations from the technological frontier to the front line, articulating complex capabilities in ways that land with defence decision makers, technical evaluators, end users, and investors. It plugs into a client's existing commercial and marketing functions on a flexible, call-off basis rather than filling full-time roles, and works with venture builders, founders, start-ups and SMEs across the UK, the US and the wider NATO alliance.

## Products and solutions

- **Narrative and messaging** - product and service definition, narrative development and core messaging.
- **Positioning** - market positioning, audience identification and segmentation, and value-proposition definition.
- **Brand development** - naming, campaign ideation, brand and creative direction, tone of voice, and brand and product marketing strategy.
- **Audience engagement** - launch presentations, pitch decks, web copy, content planning, landing pages, brochures, technical blogs, think pieces and case studies.
- **Sales enablement** - tech- and sales-demo design, explainer-video scripts, and sales-enablement assets.
- **Hitchhiker's Guide to the Valley of Death** - a public interactive guide for deep-tech founders on crossing the gap between a promising technology and adoption at the front line. [guide](https://inink.io/hitchhikers-guide-to-the-valley-of-death/)
