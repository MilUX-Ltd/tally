# inink

> Communications consultancy helping deep-tech developers get from the technological frontier to the front lines of UK defence and national security.

## What they do

inink helps developers get from the technological frontier to the front lines of UK defence and national security. The companies that work with it arrive with great technology and a messaging problem; they leave with the positioning, the narrative and the assets they need to engage government buyers and industry partners.

inink diagnoses where the message is breaking down, builds the framework that fixes it, and delivers the assets that carry it into the market. JOSCAR-registered and fluent in the sector, it gets up to speed fast, plugging into existing teams without adding to permanent headcount.

Typically inink works alongside founders who need a positioning foundation before they can scale their BD and sales efforts; marketing and sales leads who need a concise, consistent and compelling message reaching the right people at the right time; and VCs whose portfolio companies need to gain and maintain commercial traction while staying focused on engineering.

## Products and solutions

- **Narrative and messaging** - product and service definition, narrative development and core messaging.
- **Positioning** - market positioning, audience identification and segmentation, and value-proposition definition.
- **Brand development** - naming, campaign ideation, brand and creative direction, tone of voice, and brand and product marketing strategy.
- **Audience engagement** - launch presentations, pitch decks, web copy, content planning, landing pages, brochures, technical blogs, think pieces and case studies.
- **Sales enablement** - tech- and sales-demo design, explainer-video scripts, and sales-enablement assets.
- **Hitchhiker's Guide to the Valley of Death** - a public interactive guide for deep-tech founders on crossing the gap between a promising technology and adoption at the front line. [guide](https://inink.io/hitchhikers-guide-to-the-valley-of-death/)
