# J & D Wilkie Ltd

> Scottish manufacturer of specialist technical textiles for the Ministry of Defence, including camouflage materials and ballistic protection fabrics, established 1868.

## What they do

J & D Wilkie manufactures and supplies specialist technical textile materials to the MoD and its contractors. Its product range includes thermal, urban and multispectral camouflage materials, anti-shine and shadow fabrics, heat shields, and ballistic protection textiles, supported by in-house design and making-up facilities for bespoke requirements.

## Products and solutions

- **Camouflage Textiles** - Thermal, urban and multispectral camouflage fabrics; anti-shine and shadow materials.
- **Ballistic Protection Fabrics** - Textile-based ballistic protection for personnel and platform applications.
- **Heat Shields** - Specialist thermal management textile products.
- **Bespoke Textile Products** - In-house design and manufacturing for tailored requirements.
