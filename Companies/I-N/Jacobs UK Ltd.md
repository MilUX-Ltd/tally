# Jacobs UK Ltd

> Global engineering and technical services firm with a specialist defence, aerospace, and security practice.

## What they do

Jacobs is a global engineering consultancy with a significant UK presence, ranking among the largest providers of technical, professional and construction services to government and industry defence clients. Its specialist UK services include facility cyber security, sustainability and resiliency planning, environmental services, and feasibility and master planning for aerospace, defence and security applications. Jacobs also holds a long-standing role supporting Royal Navy naval nuclear propulsion research and technical support, and is a joint delivery partner to the Ministry of Defence's procurement organisation, DE&S.

## Products and solutions

- **Facility and OT cyber security** - secure operational technology network design and SCADA integration, OT-managed security services, governance, risk and compliance, and advanced OT cyber security services including penetration testing and threat hunting, delivered jointly with PA Consulting.
- **Naval nuclear propulsion support** - research and technology services and through-life technical support for Royal Navy submarine reactors, including work on future reactor development.
- **MOD programme and project delivery** - joint delivery partner (with Turner & Townsend, as the Equinox joint venture) on the Defence Equipment and Support Project Delivery Partner-Successor framework, covering project and operational delivery across MOD departments.
- **Environmental services and master planning** - environmental planning, remediation, EHS consulting and feasibility/master planning for aerospace, defence and security facilities.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** MOD Digital and IT Professional Services (DIPS) framework, Lot 3 (cyber security, crypto, security operations and integrated systems); DE&S Project Delivery Partner-Successor (PDP-S) framework.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
