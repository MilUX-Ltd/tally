# Jacobs UK Ltd

> Global engineering and technical services firm with a specialist defence, aerospace, and security practice.

## What they do

Jacobs provides engineering, technical, professional, and construction services to government and industry defence clients. Its specialist services in the defence and security space include facility cyber security, sustainability and resiliency planning, environmental services, and feasibility and master planning for aerospace, defence, and security applications.
