# McGreevy Engineering

> Northern Ireland precision engineering company manufacturing components for the aerospace and defence supply chain, including missile and rocket subassemblies.

## What they do

McGreevy Engineering operates CNC 5-axis milling and lathe facilities alongside CNC CMM inspection capability. The company manufactures mechanical components and subassemblies for missiles, rockets, and submunitions, working with aluminium, titanium, Inconel, and other exotic alloys for customers in the aerospace and defence sectors.
