# Iturri Ltd

> Manufacturer and distributor of technical clothing, footwear, PPE, CBRN equipment and vehicles for defence, security and personnel protection.

## What they do

Iturri supplies tailored, innovative and sustainable protective solutions to the defence and security sectors. Its product range covers technical clothing and footwear, PPE (including CBRN and explosive/chemical protective systems), riot control equipment, and specialist vehicles, with an emphasis on high-specification, technically demanding products.

## Products and solutions

- **Technical Clothing and Footwear** - Specialist garments and safety footwear for defence and security personnel.
- **PPE Systems** - Protective clothing including CBRN, fire, explosive and chemical protection; safety harnesses; high-visibility clothing.
- **CBRN and Decontamination Equipment** - Detection and decontamination solutions for hazardous environments.
- **Riot Control Equipment** - Crowd control and public order products including shields and batons.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
