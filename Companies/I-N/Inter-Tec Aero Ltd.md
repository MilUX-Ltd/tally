# Inter-Tec Aero Ltd

> Aerospace engineering consultancy holding EASA Part 21J Design Organisation Approval, serving OEMs, MROs, and operators globally.

## What they do

Inter-Tec Aero provides specialist engineering, design, and structural analysis services to the aviation sector. The company holds EASA Part 21J Design Organisation Approval and was established to continue the EASA-approved design services previously delivered under Inter-Tec Services in SW Scotland, now serving a global customer base from Shannon, Ireland.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** EASA Part 21J Design Organisation Approval
