# Inter-Tec Aero Ltd

> Aerospace engineering consultancy holding EASA Part 21J Design Organisation Approval, serving OEMs, MROs, and operators globally.

## What they do

Inter-Tec Aero provides specialist engineering, design, and structural analysis services to the aviation sector, describing itself as offering practical EASA-approved engineering solutions for clients needing modifications or repair solutions. The company holds EASA Part 21J Design Organisation Approval and was established to continue the EASA-approved design services previously delivered under Inter-Tec Services in SW Scotland, now serving a global customer base from Shannon, Ireland, alongside a continuing UK office at Prestwick International Aerospace Park.

## Products and solutions

- **Structural repair solutions** - identification, assessment, and removal of common damage profiles including corrosion, cracking, and disbond, covering engine nacelle intake cowls, fan cowl doors, thrust reversers, exhaust nozzle components, airframe secondary structure, radomes, control surfaces, and landing gear.
- **Interiors** - management of cabin interior development, renewal, refurbishment, seat reconfiguration, and role changes across passenger, cargo, and air ambulance configurations.
- **Specialist role equipment** - support for helicopter emergency medical services and search and rescue operations, including structural provisions and bespoke mounting configurations.
- **Exteriors** - approved design solutions and material provisioning for aircraft exterior refurbishment.
- **Avionics integration** - bespoke equipment installation designs and retrofitting of instrument panels and electrical systems.
- **Consultancy** - compliance verification, project management, and stress analysis services, including maintenance and inspection planning advice.

The company states its design solution catalogue, covering aircraft types from the DHC-6 and BN2 Islander through to Airbus A320-family and Boeing widebody aircraft, ATR, SAAB, and a range of Airbus Helicopters, Leonardo, Sikorsky, and Bell rotary types.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** EASA Part 21J Design Organisation Approval, UK CAA Part 21J DOA, AS/EN 9100, ETSO authorisations covering APU and engine nacelle structural repairs and aircraft economy seats
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
