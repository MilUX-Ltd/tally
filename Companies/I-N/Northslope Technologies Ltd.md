# Northslope Technologies Ltd

> Leading global Palantir partner delivering data and software engineering to UK defence and security customers.

## What they do

Northslope Technologies is the leading global Palantir partner, founded in collaboration with Palantir as a new type of engineering company. Over half of the company's Forward Deployed Engineers are Palantir alumni, and the UK-based teams have delivered in demanding defence and national security environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Palantir Deployment and Engineering** - forward deployed engineering and implementation of Palantir platforms for defence and security use cases.
- **Data Analysis and Software Modelling** - data engineering, analytical modelling and software optimisation services.
