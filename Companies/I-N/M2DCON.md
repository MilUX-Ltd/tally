# M2DCON

> CBRNe decontamination and collective protection specialist supplying advanced decontamination systems and consumables to defence and emergency services worldwide.

## What they do

M2DCON develops and manufactures CBRNe decontamination solutions with over 40 years of experience in the field. Products include immediate decontamination consumables and gaseous decontamination systems using modified vaporised hydrogen peroxide for sensitive equipment, vehicles, and enclosed spaces, supplied to the US Department of Defense and agencies globally.

## Products and solutions

- **Joint Service Equipment Wipe (JSEW/M333/CBR Wipes)** - Immediate decontamination consumable in service with the US Department of Defense.
- **General Purpose Decon for Hardened Military Equipment (GPD-HME/M334/Decon PLUS)** - Hard surface decontamination for military equipment.
- **Gaseous Decontamination Systems (GDS)** - Modified vaporised hydrogen peroxide systems for thorough decontamination of sensitive equipment and enclosed spaces.
