# ITP Aero UK Ltd

> Aerospace engine component manufacturer serving the civil and defence aviation sectors, operating from a former Rolls-Royce site in Hucknall, Nottinghamshire.

## What they do

ITP Aero UK manufactures complex fabrications, combustors, casings and outlet guide vanes for civil and defence aviation programmes. The Hucknall site, integrated in 2021, houses three manufacturing plants with a skilled team specialising in design and advanced manufacturing processes including CNC machining, welding, superplastic forming and electron beam processes.

## Products and solutions

- **Complex Fabrications** - Structural and thermal components for aero engines, produced across arc, laser, electron beam and resistance welding processes.
- **Combustors** - Engine combustion system components for civil and defence aviation programmes.
- **Casings and Outlet Guide Vanes** - Precision-machined and formed casings and vane assemblies for turbine engines.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, NADCAP (welding, EDM, heat treatment, NDT, surface enhancement)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
