# KRG Specialist Engineering Services Ltd

> Precision CNC machining and engineering manufacturer serving aerospace, defence, and marine sectors.

## What they do

KRG Specialist Engineering Services delivers precision machining, including 5-axis CNC milling and turning, alongside arc and specialist welding for clients in aerospace, defence, oil and gas, marine, and renewables. They offer asset new build and lifecycle solutions from their Coatbridge facility, providing an integrated manufacturing service from first article through to ongoing production.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC machining (5-axis)** - precision milled and turned components for aerospace and defence programmes.
- **Welding services** - arc and specialist welding for structural and platform components.
- **Lifecycle engineering** - new build and in-service maintenance manufacturing solutions.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 3834
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
