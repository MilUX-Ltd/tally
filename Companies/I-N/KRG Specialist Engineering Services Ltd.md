# KRG Specialist Engineering Services Ltd

> Precision CNC machining and engineering manufacturer serving aerospace, defence, and marine sectors.

## What they do

KRG Specialist Engineering Services delivers precision machining, including 5-axis CNC milling and turning, alongside arc and specialist welding for clients in aerospace, defence, oil and gas, marine, and renewables. They offer asset new build and lifecycle solutions from their Coatbridge facility, providing an integrated manufacturing service from first article through to ongoing production.

## Products and solutions

- **CNC machining (5-axis)** - precision milled and turned components for aerospace and defence programmes.
- **Welding services** - arc and specialist welding for structural and platform components.
- **Lifecycle engineering** - new build and in-service maintenance manufacturing solutions.
- **Refurbishment and upgrade** - aftermarket maintenance, refurbishment, repair and certification of critical assets.
- **Special purpose manufacture** - precision-engineered custom machinery and automation.
- **Non-destructive testing (NDT)** - inspection for component integrity, performance and safety.
- **CMM and quality management** - precision quality assurance using CMM inspection and traceable processes.
- **Surface treatments** - coating solutions applied by NORSOK-trained, NACE-certified specialists.
- **Assembly and testing** - precision assembly with FAT, SIT and high-pressure testing.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR (Hellios JOSCAR register)
- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 3834, Cyber Essentials Plus (achieved January 2026)
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Make UK Defence
