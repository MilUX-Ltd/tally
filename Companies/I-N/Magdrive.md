# Magdrive

> UK space propulsion company developing high-thrust, high-efficiency thrusters for satellite servicing and orbital operations.

## What they do

Magdrive is developing next-generation spacecraft propulsion systems based in Harwell Campus, Oxfordshire. Its thruster technology is designed to deliver higher thrust and efficiency than existing in-space propulsion, targeting applications in satellite servicing, orbital assembly and interplanetary transport.
