# Magdrive

> UK space propulsion company developing high-thrust, high-efficiency plasma thrusters for satellite servicing and orbital operations.

## What they do

Magdrive is developing next-generation spacecraft propulsion systems based at Harwell Campus, Oxfordshire. Its thruster technology uses solid metal propellant to deliver higher thrust and efficiency than existing in-space electric propulsion, targeting applications including satellite servicing, orbital assembly, constellation management and interplanetary transport. The company states defence-relevant applications including manoeuvring satellites unpredictably to make them harder to track, and constellation management in increasingly congested low Earth orbit.

## Products and solutions

- **Rogue** - compact high-thrust electric propulsion system for CubeSat-class satellites, stated thrust range 0.1 to 10mN and specific impulse of 1,800 to 3,500 seconds.
- **Warlock** - higher-power propulsion variant, stated thrust range 0.1 to 50mN.
- **Paladin** - higher-thrust system described by the company as the next stage in its propulsion line, with a stated maximum thrust above 1N.
