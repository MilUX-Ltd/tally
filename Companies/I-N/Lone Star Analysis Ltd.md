# Lone Star Analysis Ltd

> Predictive and prescriptive analytics and guided AI provider helping defence and commercial customers make faster, better-informed decisions under uncertainty.

## What they do

Lone Star Analysis Ltd is the UK subsidiary of Lone Star Analysis, a provider of predictive and prescriptive analytics and guided artificial intelligence (branded Evolved AI) for decision-making under uncertainty. The company states its European client base includes the UK Ministry of Defence, the Norwegian Ministry of Defence, defence prime contractors and major offshore operators, and that its technology supports fleet readiness and availability analysis for military aircraft operators. Founded in the US in 2004, the company applies its analytics to operational and financial performance challenges across defence, aerospace, energy, logistics and healthcare.

## Products and solutions

- **TruNavigator MAX** - a predictive decision analysis platform used for planning and real-time operational predictive and prescriptive analytics.
- **Evolved AI** - the company's branded artificial intelligence approach underpinning its analytics products.
- **MaxUp Operational Analytics / MaxUp Readiness Solutions** - operational analytics aimed at maximising asset fleet readiness, availability and sustainability.
- **AnalyticsOS** - the company's analytics operating system for automated, intelligent analytics solutions.
