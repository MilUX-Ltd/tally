# Lincad Ltd

> UK designer and manufacturer of specialist batteries, chargers and power systems built to defence and military standards.

## What they do

Lincad designs and manufactures batteries, battery chargers and associated power electronics for defence, security and other specialist applications where military-grade performance is required. Products are developed in accordance with UK Defence Standards and US Military Standards, with proven capability in ruggedised designs for demanding environmental and EMC conditions.

## Products and solutions

- **Specialist batteries** - military-grade primary and rechargeable battery packs for defence and security platforms.
- **Battery chargers** - ruggedised chargers designed to UK Def Stan and US MIL-STD requirements.
- **Electrical power systems** - integrated power and battery management solutions for land and dismounted applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, Cyber Essentials Plus, JOSCAR
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
