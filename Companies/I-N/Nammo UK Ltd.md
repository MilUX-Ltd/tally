# Nammo UK Ltd

> UK site of Nammo, a Norwegian defence and aerospace manufacturer specialising in rocket propulsion and ammunition.

## What they do

Based at Westcott Venture Park in Buckinghamshire, Nammo UK develops and manufactures chemical propulsion solutions for space and defence applications. The site has over 70 years of experience in rocket engine design, development, manufacture and test.
