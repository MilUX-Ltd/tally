# Nammo UK Ltd

> UK site of Nammo, a Norwegian defence and aerospace manufacturer specialising in rocket propulsion and ammunition.

## What they do

Based at Westcott Venture Park in Buckinghamshire, Nammo UK develops and manufactures chemical propulsion solutions for space and defence applications. The site has been occupied since 1946 and moved into spacecraft rocket engine manufacture in the mid-1990s; Nammo describes itself as a leading provider of spacecraft propulsion solutions for Earth orbit, Moon and interplanetary missions, offering everything from individual components to bespoke, vertically integrated propulsion solutions.

## Products and solutions

- **LEROS rocket engine family** - apogee engines and thrusters, with over 90 flown missions for the LEROS Apogee engines and more than 100 for LEROS thrusters.
- **LEROS 1C** - dual-mode apogee engine supplied to Lockheed Martin for NASA's Lucy mission to the Trojan asteroids.
- **LEROS 4-ET** - provided descent orbit insertion propulsion for Firefly Aerospace's Blue Ghost lunar lander.
- **RELIANCE** - next-generation throttleable bi-propellant engine, developed at Westcott and selected as the main engine for ESA's Argonaut lunar lander.
- **LMP-103s monopropellant propulsion system** - supplied to Astroscale for the ADRAS-J space debris removal mission.
- **Test facilities** - operates the miniHATF, F-Site and J-Site test facilities, and the UK Space Agency's National Space Propulsion Test Facility.
