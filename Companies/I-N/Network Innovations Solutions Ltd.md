# Network Innovations Solutions Ltd

> UK entity of Network Innovations, a global provider of satellite and connectivity solutions for government, defence, and remote operations.

## What they do

Network Innovations designs, builds, and operates always-available connectivity solutions for government and defence clients, with particular strength in satellite communications and remote-area coverage. Founded in 1989 and headquartered in Calgary, the company maintains regional offices in the UK, USA, EU, Singapore, and Australia, supported by a partner network of more than 200 organisations worldwide.

## Products and solutions

- **Satellite communications** - Design and delivery of SATCOM solutions for government, defence, and critical infrastructure.
- **Connectivity solutions** - Always-available connectivity for remote, maritime, and deployed operations.
