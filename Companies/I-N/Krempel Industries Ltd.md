# Krempel Industries Ltd

> Specialist composites and polymer manufacturer with defence capability in radomes, antenna shrouds, and structural components across land, sea, and air domains.

## What they do

Krempel Industries manufactures composite and polymer products for aerospace, defence, automotive, and industrial markets globally. Their UK operation in Bideford, Devon specialises in composite radomes and antenna shrouds for land, sea, and air platforms, alongside structural tubes and components produced through a range of composite manufacturing processes.

## Products and solutions

- **Composite radomes and antenna shrouds** - protective enclosures for radar and antenna systems across platform domains.
- **Composite and polymer tubes** - structural and functional tubular components for defence applications.
- **Structural composites** - fibre-reinforced components including pressure control, sensors, and blast protection elements.
