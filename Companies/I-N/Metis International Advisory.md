# Metis International Advisory Ltd

> Strategic advisory for defence industry clients covering geopolitical analysis, bid strategy and government engagement.

## What they do

Metis International Advisory provides tailored strategic advisory to defence industry clients, covering geopolitical trends analysis, how government works, and positioning for contracts and market growth. Core services include bid strategy, US and UK institutional fluency, market and product relevance reviews, strategic engagement planning and executive coaching.

## Products and solutions

- **Geo-strategic insights** - Tailored analysis of geopolitical trends relevant to defence market positioning.
- **Capture and bid strategy** - Business strategy design and delivery for defence contract pursuit.
- **Government engagement programmes** - US and UK institutional fluency education and executive coaching.
