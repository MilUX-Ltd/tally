# JET Engineering System Solutions

> Developer and manufacturer of floating, pop-up 5G connectivity solutions for the marine and offshore environment.

## What they do

JET Engineering System Solutions designs and builds in-house 5G radio equipment hardened for harsh offshore and ultra-remote environments, enabling high-speed internet and real-time data streaming at sea. Their solutions support safety-critical emergency communications and sensor data streaming for offshore operations.
