# JET Engineering System Solutions

> Developer and manufacturer of floating, pop-up 5G connectivity solutions for the marine and offshore environment.

## What they do

JET Engineering System Solutions (trading as JET Connectivity) designs and builds in-house 5G radio equipment hardened for harsh offshore and ultra-remote environments, enabling high-speed internet and real-time data streaming at sea. The company describes its offering as the world's first pop-up, floating 5G connectivity solution at sea, combining 5G, AIS and RF networks to support civilian, defence, green-shipping and renewables customers. For defence, the company states it is developing a deployable mobile multi-radio IP/5G network capability for littoral and offshore environments, intended to provide high-bandwidth, low-latency, multi-domain integrated communications and data with high resilience.

## Products and solutions

- **Floating 5G platform** - pop-up, sea-based 5G connectivity node combining 5G, AIS and RF networks for offshore data transfer.
- **Deployable multi-radio IP/5G network** - mobile network capability for littoral and offshore defence environments, supporting high-bandwidth, low-latency, multi-domain communications.
- **Offshore safety and sensor communications** - links supporting safety-critical emergency communications and real-time sensor data streaming for offshore operations.
