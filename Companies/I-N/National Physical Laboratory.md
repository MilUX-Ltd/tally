# National Physical Laboratory

> The UK's National Measurement Institute, providing the measurement standards and science that underpin aerospace, defence, and security applications.

## What they do

NPL is a world-leading centre of excellence in measurement standards, science, and technology. It works with businesses across the aerospace, defence, and security sectors to develop improved technology and products, providing R&D, test, training, and consultancy services in areas including harsh-environment sensors and materials, through-life monitoring and prognostics, structural health monitoring, and high-temperature performance materials.

## Products and solutions

- **Measurement and calibration services** - Traceable measurement standards for defence-sector component and system testing.
- **Harsh environment sensors** - Research and development into sensor performance under extreme conditions.
- **Structural health monitoring** - Through-life monitoring and prognostics for platforms and infrastructure.
