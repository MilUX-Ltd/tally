# Mavinci

> Technology and software developer serving defence, nuclear safety and security.

## What they do

Mavinci designs, develops and produces technology and software solutions across information and communication technology (ICT), nuclear safety, security and defence. It provides bespoke software development and engineering delivered by experienced teams. The company is based at Cambridge Science Park.

## Products and solutions

- **Bespoke software development** - tailored software for defence, nuclear and security clients.
- **ICT and systems solutions** - engineering and integration across regulated sectors.
