# Loki Dynamics Ltd

> UK marine equipment and consultancy specialist supplying shock-mitigating seating, propulsion and crew wellbeing solutions, with a defence and commercial vessel customer base.

## What they do

Loki Dynamics is a UK marine equipment and consultancy company organised around four product areas: marine products and consultancy, shock-mitigating seating, engines and drives, and crew wellbeing technology. Its shock and vibration control seating is used to protect crews and equipment aboard commercial, defence and leisure vessels operating in demanding sea states. The company also acts as a UK sales and consultancy partner for allied marine technology suppliers.

## Products and solutions

- **SHOCK-WBV seat bases** - a shock and whole-body-vibration seat suspension system, designed and manufactured in the UK, reducing wave shock and providing lateral stability for crew comfort and endurance on commercial and defence craft.
- **OMEGA cushion insert** - a patented, modular cushion insert reducing shock and vibration exposure, integrated into cushions across marine and land-based applications.
- **Engines and drives** - hybrid-ready diesel and electric propulsion solutions for commercial, defence and leisure vessels, delivered with partner powertrain manufacturers.
- **SPIIND Defence & Maritime VR** - a wellbeing technology offering drug-free relief from seasickness, intended to support crew focus and performance at sea.
- **Marine consultancy** - advisory support for boatbuilders, operators and OEM partners on integrating marine technology and optimising vessel performance.
