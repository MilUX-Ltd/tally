# Loki Dynamics Ltd
> UK specialist in shock and vibration control for maritime and land environments, supplying equipment for defence applications.
## What they do
Loki Dynamics Ltd provides shock and vibration control solutions for maritime and land defence environments. The company designs and supplies maritime equipment under its own brand, focused on protecting systems and personnel from shock and vibration in demanding operational conditions.
