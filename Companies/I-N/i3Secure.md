# i3Secure

> Cyber security and information assurance consultancy serving the defence, public and private sectors.

## What they do

i3Secure is a cyber security and information assurance consultancy working to protect the confidentiality, integrity and availability of information and data across the defence, public and private sectors. Its consultants design, develop and implement information security management systems to recognised frameworks and guide organisations to certification. The company signed the Armed Forces Covenant on 3 November 2021.

## Products and solutions

- **ISO consultancy** - design and implementation of information security management systems to ISO 27001 and related standards.
- **CISO as a Service** - outsourced chief information security officer capability.
- **DPO as a Service** - outsourced data protection officer capability.
- **Audit and assessment** - security audits, risk management and technology risk assessment.
- **Cloud security** - digital and cloud security services.
- **Accreditation services** - end-to-end systems and network accreditation in line with JSP and NIST, including RMADS development.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks worked to:** NIST, ISO 27001, CIS, ISF, PCI DSS, Cyber Essentials.
