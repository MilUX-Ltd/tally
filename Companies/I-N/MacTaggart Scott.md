# MacTaggart Scott

> Scottish naval engineering company making mechanical handling and motion systems for submarines and ships.

## What they do

MacTaggart, Scott & Company is a privately owned Scottish engineering company, founded in 1898 and based in Loanhead near Edinburgh. It specialises in naval mechanical handling equipment, designing and manufacturing systems for surface ships and submarines for navies worldwide. The company supports demanding maritime environments with a long-standing reputation for quality and reliability.

## Products and solutions

- **Aircraft elevators and lifts** - deck lifts for aircraft carriers and ships.
- **Helicopter handling systems** - flight-deck handling and traversing gear.
- **Arresting gear** - a dedicated naval product line.
- **Submarine mast systems** - hoistable and mast-raising systems, including the Quiet Modular Mast.
- **Towed-array handling systems** and **emergency propulsion units** for submarines.
- **Hydraulic power units, pumps and actuators** for surface ships and submarines.
