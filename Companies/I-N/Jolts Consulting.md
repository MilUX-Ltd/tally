# JOLTS Consulting

> UK veteran-owned organisation performance consultancy delivering training, Integrated Logistic Support and decision support to defence, maritime and aviation clients.

## What they do

JOLTS Consulting Limited is a veteran-owned and operated UK consultancy specialising in organisation performance, working predominantly in high-risk and safety-critical environments across defence, maritime and aviation. The company combines data-driven insight with people-centred leadership across four service lines: training, consultancy, Integrated Logistic Support (ILS) and creative digital services. It has provided training needs analysis for UK Defence's Joint Fires Synthetic Trainer (JFST) and has supported the British Army through experimentation activity to inform capability development and acquisition decisions.

## Products and solutions

- **Training services** - analysis, design and delivery of training solutions, including training needs analysis and desktop simulation to optimise organisational performance.
- **ILS and engineering support** - Integrated Logistic Support and Management consultancy covering reliability and maintainability analysis, obsolescence management, asset and supply chain management, and technical publications, built to UK Def-Stan/Def-Con, JSPs, the Defence Logistics Framework, international AQAPs and NATO MIL-STDs.
- **Consultancy services** - bespoke business and market intelligence for defence and security clients, and decision support through experimentation and evidence-based analysis.
- **Digital services** - immersive 2D and 3D visualisation and serious gaming solutions.
