# MilScrum
> UK delivery consultancy applying agile and Scrum methodologies to defence, public sector and manufacturing programmes.

## What they do

MilScrum is a UK delivery and coaching consultancy providing embedded Agile consultants and coaching for defence, public sector and manufacturing organisations. It positions itself at the intersection of military-derived delivery discipline and modern Agile methods. Public material describes a shift in emphasis toward coaching manufacturing businesses in people, product and systems delivery, alongside its original defence and public sector programme work.

## Products and solutions

- **Embedded consultants** - Scrum Masters and Agile team leads placed on defence, public sector and manufacturing delivery programmes.
- **Agile training and coaching** - masterclasses and coaching in Scrum, Kanban and Agile delivery, including a structured coaching programme for manufacturing teams.
