# MilScrum
> UK defence delivery consultancy applying agile and Scrum methodologies to defence and public sector programmes, founded by a REME veteran.
## What they do
MilScrum is a UK delivery and development consultancy founded by Wayne Smallman, a former REME soldier. It provides embedded Agile consultants (Scrum Masters and Agile team leads) for defence and public sector programmes, alongside training and coaching in Scrum, Kanban, and Agile delivery. The firm positions itself at the intersection of military strategy principles and modern delivery methods. Active in defence innovation networks, including facilitating the Defence BattleLab Logistics and Supply Chain Hackaday in October 2025.
## Products and solutions
- **Embedded consultants** - Scrum Masters and Agile team leads placed on defence and public sector delivery programmes.
- **Agile training** - Masterclasses and coaching in Scrum, Kanban, and Agile delivery for complex environments.
