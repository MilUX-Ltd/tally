# Kahootz Ltd

> MOD-accredited secure SaaS collaboration platform for sharing information up to Official Sensitive with external partners.

## What they do

Kahootz provides a cloud-based workspace tool that allows organisations to set up secure, accredited environments for bids, procurement, projects, stakeholder engagement and knowledge management within minutes. The platform is accredited for use at Official Sensitive level and is used within the MOD and defence supply chain.

## Products and solutions

- **Kahootz Secure Workspace** - SaaS collaboration environment accredited to Official Sensitive, supporting external partner sharing for defence and government use cases.
