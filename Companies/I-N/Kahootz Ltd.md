# Kahootz Ltd

> MOD-accredited secure SaaS collaboration platform for sharing information up to Official Sensitive with external partners.

## What they do

Kahootz provides a cloud-based workspace tool that allows organisations to set up secure, accredited environments for bids, procurement, projects, stakeholder engagement and knowledge management within minutes. Its Defence Share platform is the MOD's primary collaboration tool and remains the only platform of its type accredited by the MOD to store and share information marked up to Official-Sensitive, supporting collaboration across the wider defence supply chain. The company is certified to ISO 27001 and listed on G-Cloud 14.

## Products and solutions

- **Kahootz Secure Workspace** - SaaS collaboration environment accredited to Official Sensitive, supporting external partner sharing for defence and government use cases.
- **Defence Share** - the MOD's branded instance of the Kahootz platform, used defence-wide for supply chain collaboration and information sharing.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** G-Cloud 14.
- **Accreditations:** ISO 27001.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
