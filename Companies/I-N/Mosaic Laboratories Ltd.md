# Mosaic Laboratories Ltd

> Research and development organisation specialising in metal forming, chemical processes and related engineering activities.

## What they do

Mosaic Laboratories conducts R&D in metal forming and chemical engineering processes. The company is based at Ditton Priors Industrial Estate near Bridgnorth, Shropshire, and holds Cyber Essentials Plus accreditation. The company has applied to Shropshire Council and the Health and Safety Executive for a licence to manufacture explosive substances and articles at the site, including shaped charge warheads, research rocket motors and pressed pellets.

## Products and solutions

- **Metal forming and chemical processes R&D** - engineering research and development activities.
- **Explosive manufacturing (licence application stage)** - proposed production of shaped charge warheads, research rocket motors and pressed pellets, subject to HSE and local authority approval.
