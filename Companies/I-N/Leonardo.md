# Leonardo S.p.A.

> Italian aerospace, defence and security prime with a large UK presence across helicopters and defence electronics.

## What they do

Leonardo is an Italian multinational specialising in aerospace, defence and security, headquartered in Rome and operating across some 180 sites worldwide. It designs, builds and supports military and civil helicopters, defence electronics, sensors, cyber and security systems, and works across the air, land, sea, space and cyber domains. The company is listed on the Borsa Italiana. In the UK it is one of the largest suppliers of defence and security equipment to the Ministry of Defence, with the British arm Leonardo UK Ltd headquartered at 1 Eagle Place, London, and major sites at Yeovil, Edinburgh and Luton.

## Products and solutions

- **Helicopters** - design, manufacture and support of military and civil rotorcraft at Yeovil, including the AW149, AW159 Wildcat and AW101 Merlin.
- **Defence electronics** - airborne, land and naval sensors, radars, electronic warfare and electro-optic systems; the UK business is a leading manufacturer of sensors and electronic systems for advanced aircraft.
- **Cyber and security** - secure communications, cyber resilience and security solutions.
- **Counter-drone and laser systems** - counter-UAS technology, military communications, lasers, satellite communications and navigation.
