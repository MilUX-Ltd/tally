# Marble Aerospace

> Designs, builds and operates fleets of high-speed VTOL drones with AI vessel detection for maritime surveillance, delivered as an end-to-end service.

## What they do

Marble builds hardware and software to protect maritime environments. It designs, manufactures and operates its own fleet of high-speed vertical take-off and landing drones, paired with computer-vision sensors and AI-assisted vessel detection, and manages the full deployment chain including beyond visual line of sight regulatory approvals, planning and operations. Clients include government agencies, coast guards, maritime police and environmental organisations, with applications across fisheries protection, anti-piracy and drug-trafficking surveillance, infrastructure protection and environmental and ice monitoring.

## Products and solutions

- **MRB5** - fifth-generation proprietary drone. Fixed-wing VTOL lift-and-cruise design with four lift rotors and a single pusher propeller, built for harsh maritime environments and able to break down to fit in regular airline luggage for rapid deployment.
- **Surveillance as a service** - end-to-end managed maritime monitoring offering, covering deployment, BVLOS approvals, AI vessel detection and real-time intelligence.
