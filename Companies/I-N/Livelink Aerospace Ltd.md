# Livelink Aerospace Ltd

> Developer of airspace intelligence and counter-drone systems integrating hardware, firmware and software for defensive and pro-aviation applications.

## What they do

Livelink Aerospace produces its Airspace Intelligence System (AIS), a modular solution for securing restricted airspace against unauthorised drone and UAV activity. The system uses bespoke hardware, firmware and software control components that can operate as a standalone deployment or integrate with incumbent security and C2 infrastructure.

## Products and solutions

- **Airspace Intelligence System (AIS)** - counter-drone detection and response system combining optical, thermal, sensor and C2 elements for local and large-scale airspace security.
- **Sensor and optical subsystems** - thermal cameras, CCTV, infrared sensors and optical components for surveillance platforms.
- **Kinetic effectors** - weapons-class effector integration within the AIS architecture.
