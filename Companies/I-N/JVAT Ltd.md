# JVAT Ltd

> Risk, engineering and assurance consultancy serving the defence, transportation and energy sectors.

## What they do

JVAT provides professional services across risk management, engineering assurance, and safety in complex regulated environments, including systems assurance, safety engineering, systems engineering, enterprise risk management, human factors and cyber security. Founded in Australia in 2017, the firm has grown to over 70 employees and expanded into the UK (JVAT Ltd, Bristol office opened February 2022) and the United States (JVAT Inc).

## Products and solutions

- **Systems and safety assurance** - systems engineering, safety engineering and safety programme management for regulated environments.
- **Enterprise and programme risk management** - bid, programme and project risk management, resilience and business transformation advisory.
- **Human factors and technical solutions** - human factors, software assurance and management system development services.
