# IDV Defence Vehicles UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of Italian armoured vehicle manufacturer IDV, supplying uncrewed ground vehicles and AI autonomy systems to the global defence industry.

## What they do

IDV Defence Vehicles UK supplies uncrewed ground vehicles (UGVs) and integrates payloads and systems for operational capability. The company offers the VIKING UGV platform alongside the MACE AI autonomy kit, and supports customers from concept through to urgent operational requirement programmes. It is the UK arm of IDV, a leading Italian armoured military vehicle manufacturer.

## Products and solutions

- **VIKING UGV** - All-terrain, multirole, six-wheeled diesel-electric hybrid uncrewed ground vehicle with a 750 kg payload capacity, adaptable for remote weapon stations, ISTAR, CBRNE, logistics and troop support roles.
- **MACE AI Autonomy Kit** - Modular Autonomous Control Equipment, a full-stack autonomy system for retrofit or new-build uncrewed and autonomous vehicles, including route-following, convoy-following and independent search or mapping modes.
- **ATLAS navigation system** - AI-based navigation system for GNSS-denied environments, using neural networks for terrain perception, object detection and recognition, and map database creation.
