# IDV Defence Vehicles UK Ltd

> UK subsidiary of Italian armoured vehicle manufacturer IDV, supplying uncrewed ground vehicles and AI autonomy systems to the global defence industry.

## What they do

IDV Defence Vehicles UK supplies uncrewed ground vehicles (UGVs) and integrates payloads and systems for operational capability. The company offers the VIKING UGV platform alongside the MACE AI autonomy kit, and supports customers from concept through to urgent operational requirement programmes. It is the UK arm of IDV, a leading Italian armoured military vehicle manufacturer.

## Products and solutions

- **VIKING UGV** - Uncrewed ground vehicle platform for defence applications.
- **MACE AI Autonomy Kit** - AI-based autonomy system for integration onto ground vehicle platforms.
