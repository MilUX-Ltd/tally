# Mission Decisions Ltd
> UK data intelligence company using AI and machine learning to augment human decision-making for government and defence.

## What they do
Mission Decisions delivers human-machine teaming services, integrating AI and machine learning into existing workflows to improve decision-making, operational safety, efficiency and productivity. The company works across government and industry, with a focus on data analysis, software modelling and digital services. It is headquartered at Bristol & Bath Science Park.

## Products and solutions
- **Data intelligence platform** - AI and ML-driven analysis tools designed to enhance the effectiveness of operators working with complex datasets.
