# Kinsetsu

> Belfast-based IoT platform company delivering real-time asset management and utilisation intelligence for defence and other critical sectors.

## What they do

Kinsetsu's IoT platform assigns a digital identity to physical assets using intelligent sensors, enabling automated task management and real-time visibility across complex operations. The platform is in use with the Royal Navy and defence supply chain partners, as well as NHS hospitals, local authorities and utility organisations, reducing time to task-completion and improving compliance and service utilisation.

## Products and solutions

- **IoT asset management platform** - Sensor-based platform providing real-time asset identity, tracking and task automation for defence and critical infrastructure.
- **Data analysis and reporting** - Software tools for operational data analysis and decision support.
