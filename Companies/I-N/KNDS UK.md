# KNDS UK

> Tactical military bridging manufacturer supplying armed forces worldwide, formerly WFEL Limited.

## What they do

KNDS UK designs, manufactures and supports rapidly deployable military bridging systems for land forces and disaster relief operations, drawing on over a century of engineering heritage. The product range includes the Medium Girder Bridge (MGB) and Dry Support Bridge (DSB) systems, with in-service support covering inspection, repair and maintenance, spares and field training.

The company was formerly known as WFEL Limited, operating from the same Heaton Chapel site in Stockport since 1915. It is part of KNDS, the Franco-German land defence group formed from the association of Krauss-Maffei Wegmann (KMW) and Nexter, with the unified KNDS brand adopted across the group's subsidiaries from June 2023. Alongside bridging, KNDS UK's Stockport facility carries out fabrication, assembly, integration and test work on the Boxer armoured vehicle programme for the British Army, and manufactures the Boxer drive module that underpins the British Army's RCH 155 self-propelled howitzer.

## Products and solutions

- **Medium Girder Bridge (MGB)** - Rapidly deployable tactical bridging system in service with armed forces across more than 40 countries.
- **Dry Support Bridge (DSB)** - Modern military bridging system for vehicle crossing of gaps in operational and disaster relief environments.

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
