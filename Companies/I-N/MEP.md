# MEP

> Precision manufacturer of complex metal and plastic components and assemblies for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

MEP, founded in 1972 as Moulded and Engineered Plastics, manufactures complex machined parts through to multi-part assemblies and platforms, serving global aerospace and defence primes as a preferred supplier. The company describes itself as a supply chain and R&D risk-sharing partner across aerospace, defence, space, medical, motorsport, and rail sectors, and also operates a US entity, Making Everything Possible LLC, in Virginia.

## Products and solutions

- **Multi-axis machining** - manual and semi-CNC through to five-axis milling and seven-axis mill-turn, across ferrous, non-ferrous, and polymeric materials.
- **Plastics moulding** - thermoset compression, thermoplastic injection, thermoset transfer, and hand lay-up, including in-house metallic insert encapsulation.
- **Insulations** - stamped and machined engineering laminates for mission-critical applications.
- **Assemblies** - simple through to complex, higher-level assemblies.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR, SC21 (Silver level, multiple awards).
- **Accreditations:** AS9100:2018 Rev D, ISO 9001, ISO 14001:2015, Cyber Essentials Basic and Plus, First Article Certification (FAC).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Make UK Defence, Aerospace Technology Institute (ATI) partner, NATEP partner.
