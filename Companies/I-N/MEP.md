# MEP

> Precision manufacturer of complex metal and plastic components and assemblies for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

MEP manufactures complex machined parts through to multi-part assemblies and platforms, serving global aerospace and defence primes as a preferred supplier. The company focuses on quality and on-time delivery across injection and transfer moulding, precision CNC machining, and fabrication.
