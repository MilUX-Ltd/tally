# McAndrew Martin Ltd

> Multi-discipline design and build and security consultancy for regulated, high-assurance environments.

## What they do

McAndrew Martin delivers multi-discipline design and build solutions in regulated environments where safety, compliance and security are critical, including Ministry of Defence estates and critical national infrastructure. It covers architecture, structural, civil and mechanical and electrical engineering, statutory compliance, Principal Designer services and physical, technical and cyber security consultancy. Work is delivered in line with the UK Government Secure by Design policy.

## Products and solutions

- **Design and build** - architecture, structures, civils and M&E engineering across RIBA Stages 0 to 7.
- **Security consultancy** - physical, technical and cyber security.
- **Compliance and Principal Designer services** - statutory compliance and lifecycle project delivery.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ISO/IEC 27001, Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 45001.
