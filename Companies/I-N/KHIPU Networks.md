# KHIPU Networks

> International cyber security specialists delivering managed security, networking and wireless solutions.

## What they do

KHIPU Networks are a privately owned, international cyber security specialist delivering network, wireless and security solutions across multiple sectors. They run 24x7 Network and Security Operations Centres in the UK and South Africa, serving education, healthcare, government and the private sector. Their stated approach is to partner with customers, understand their challenges and design fitted solutions.

## Products and solutions

- **XMDR (eXtended Managed Detection and Response)** - 24x7 managed detection and response service delivered through KHIPU's SOC, providing threat hunting, triage, investigation and response.
- **Managed SOC and NOC services** - integrated network, NOC and SOC delivery.
- **Cyber Essentials consultancy** - guides organisations through Cyber Essentials certification.
- **Simulated phishing and security awareness training** - specialist simulated phishing and cyber awareness training.
- **Networking and wireless security** - network access, wireless and remote access security.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** Royal Warrant by Appointment to His Majesty The King; NCSC-recognised (certified under the Cyber Incident Response scheme); ISO 27001.
