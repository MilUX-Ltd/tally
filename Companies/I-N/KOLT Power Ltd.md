# KOLT Power Ltd

> European manufacturer of power conversion solutions for defence land, sea, airborne and space programmes.

## What they do

KOLT Power designs and manufactures power conversion products to DEF Standards and MIL standards, serving defence programmes across land, sea, airborne and low earth orbit domains. The company uses a modular design approach to reduce custom development overhead, with full custom design available, and operates from a European sales and design-in support office in Fareham, Hampshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Digitally controlled power conversion units** - PCB-level and chassis power solutions meeting DEF Stan and MIL-STD requirements for defence platforms.
- **Modular power systems** - Platform electrical and power systems for land, naval, airborne and LEO applications.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS/EN/JISQ 9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, IPC 610 Class 3, ISO/IEC 17025, Cyber Essentials Plus
- **Frameworks:** JOSCAR registered
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
