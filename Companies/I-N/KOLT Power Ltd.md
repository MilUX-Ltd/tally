# KOLT Power Ltd

> European manufacturer of power conversion solutions for defence land, sea, airborne and space programmes.

## What they do

KOLT Power designs and manufactures power conversion products to DEF Standards and MIL standards, serving defence programmes across land, sea, airborne and low earth orbit domains. The company uses a modular design approach to reduce custom development overhead, with full custom design available, and operates from a European sales and design-in support office in Fareham, Hampshire.

## Products and solutions

- **Digitally controlled power conversion units** - PCB-level and chassis power solutions meeting DEF Stan and MIL-STD requirements for defence platforms.
- **Modular power systems** - Platform electrical and power systems for land, naval, airborne and LEO applications.
- **Brick Modules** - Isolated DC-DC converters in quarter-brick and half-brick form factors, up to 500 W, digitally controlled, rated to -55°C, available as PCB-mount modules or packaged plug-and-play units, with associated EMI filter modules.
- **SOSA VPX power supplies** - SOSA-aligned VPX power modules and multi-channel VPX power chassis, including AC/DC and DC/DC VPX variants.
- **Power Modules** - Single-phase and true three-phase PFC modules and isolated DC/DC converter modules, from 2.8 kW per module, used as building blocks for AC/DC power supplies and battery chargers.
- **AC/DC power supplies** - Single-phase and three-phase AC/DC power supplies for defence platforms.
- **DC/DC power supplies** - Including armoured fighting vehicle DC/DC power converters and isolated ruggedised and multi-output DC/DC power supplies.
- **Battery chargers** - Single-phase and three-phase AC/DC battery chargers.
