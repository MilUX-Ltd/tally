# Mott MacDonald Ltd

> Global engineering, management and development consultancy delivering programme management, capability risk analysis and digital consultancy across defence and security.

## What they do

Mott MacDonald supports defence and security clients through requirements definition, capability risk analysis and systems-engineered delivery. Core services include programme and project management, delivery partnership, commercial and cost management, infrastructure advisory, design and engineering, and digital consultancy including TEMPEST-rated software work.
