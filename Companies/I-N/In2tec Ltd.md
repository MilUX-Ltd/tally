# In2tec Ltd

> UK electronics design, test and manufacturing company specialising in high-density component integration for defence platforms.

## What they do

In2tec is a UK electronics design, test and manufacturing company based in Kettering, with over 25 years of experience delivering end-to-end electronic assemblies from concept through to full-scale production. All work is carried out in-house without subcontracting. Its core technology, Flexi-hibrid, integrates high-density components onto flexible substrates at a fraction of the weight of traditional rigid FR4 boards, producing assemblies for platforms where every gram of weight and every millimetre of space matters.

## Products and solutions

- **Flexi-hibrid technology** - high-density component integration onto flexible substrates, covering dynamic circuits, rigidised flexible circuits and conductive contouring.
- **ReUSE and ReCYCLE** - patented technology enabling full board disassembly and component recovery, supporting obsolescence management on long-lifecycle defence programmes and reducing prototyping costs.
- **Conformal and structural electronics** - printed harnesses, operator interfaces and conformal electronics sealed into structural housings.
