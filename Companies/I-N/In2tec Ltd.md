# In2tec Ltd

> UK electronics design, test and manufacturing company specialising in high-density component integration for defence platforms.

## What they do

In2tec's core technology is Flexi-Hibrid, which integrates high-density components onto flexible substrates to reduce weight and space on defence platforms. The company has over 25 years of experience and carries out all work in-house without subcontracting.
