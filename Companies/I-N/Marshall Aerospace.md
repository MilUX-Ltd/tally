# Marshall Aerospace

> British aerospace and defence company specialising in military MRO and platform modification, headquartered in Cambridge.

## What they do

Established in 1909, Marshall Aerospace provides military maintenance, repair and overhaul (MRO) services and platform modification, with particular depth on the C-130 Hercules — a relationship spanning from 1966. The company holds a broad portfolio of design organisation approvals and works directly with aircraft operators and prime contractors to sustain and extend platform capability.

## Products and solutions

- **Military MRO** - maintenance, repair and overhaul across fixed-wing military aircraft including the C-130 Hercules.
- **Platform modification** - design and integration of structural and systems modifications across multiple aircraft types.
- **Platform integration** - whole aircraft integration and systems installation, including avionics, electrical and structural work.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** ITAR, AS/EN 9100, AS/EN 9110, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, EASA 145, FAA 14CFR Pt145, NADCAP (CP, NDT, WLD, Heat Treatment), Boeing D1-4426
- **Memberships:** ADS Group
