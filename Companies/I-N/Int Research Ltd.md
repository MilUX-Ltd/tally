# Int Research Ltd

> Specialist surveillance solutions provider delivering products, training, and services to military, police, and government customers to close operational capability gaps.

## What they do

Int Research states it has provided covert surveillance support, equipment and training to the law enforcement, military and business world for over 20 years. The company describes itself as a bespoke surveillance solutions provider delivering products, training and services (including projects) for military, police, UK and foreign governments, helping close operational capability gaps. It states it sits on many government, police and military procurement databases and is located close to London Heathrow.

## Products and solutions

- **Custom concealments** - bespoke hard and soft concealment design for covert equipment.
- **Product design and manufacturing** - custom audio and video system builds, end-user focused vehicle fits (video, photography and ANPR), tracking, triggering and cueing device manufacture, antenna manufacturing, and power source solutions.
- **Equipment supply and procurement** - intelligent procurement and creative manufacturing of deployable surveillance products for covert policing and government/military end users.
- **Training and technical support** - bespoke training with subject matter and duration tailored to client requirements, plus extensive after-sales technical support.
- **Investigations and risk management** - discreet investigations, confidential risk assessments, covert surveillance, asset tracking, and evidential capture for corporate, legal, financial and high-net-worth-individual clients.
