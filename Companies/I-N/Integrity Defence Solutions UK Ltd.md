# Integrity Defence Solutions UK Ltd

> Defence technology consultancy delivering secure, resilient systems that enhance operational capability across defence and mission-critical environments.

## What they do

Integrity Defence Solutions UK (IDS UK) is a recently established consultancy focused on systems integration, software and hardware engineering, and technical consultancy for defence and mission-critical clients. Its public material describes service lines spanning artificial intelligence and machine learning, mission systems integration, command and control, and resilient communications, applied to operations, battle management, and intelligence purposes for government and defence agencies. The company positions itself around a Modular Open Systems Approach to architecture and acquisition, aiming for open, interoperable, scalable systems. It is the UK subsidiary of the US firm Integrity Defense Solutions, Inc.

## Products and solutions

- **AI and machine learning** - described as developing systems to support decision-making and operational efficiency.
- **Mission systems integration** - integrating hardware, software and network components into scalable systems, including acquisition management support, Modular Open Systems Approach design, and product support and sustainment planning.
- **Command and control (C2)** - described as combining operational practice and technology to deliver C2 capability using Model-Based Systems Engineering, covering architecture design, digital engineering, and software integration and delivery, including modernisation of legacy C2 infrastructure.
- **Resilient communications** - described in general terms as advanced and emerging communication technologies; no named product or specific technology stated publicly.
