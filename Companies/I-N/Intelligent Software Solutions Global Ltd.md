# Intelligent Software Solutions Global Ltd

> Software company providing intelligence management systems for defence, law enforcement, and international organisations, originating in counter-IED and EOD operations.

## What they do

ISS Global develops and delivers the DFuze Product Suite, a COTS intelligence management platform deployed in over 40 countries. The company originated in counter-IED, EOD, and command and control communities and has expanded into security and law enforcement, handling sensitive data across multiple classification domains.

## Products and solutions

- **DFuze Product Suite** - COTS intelligence management system for defence, law enforcement, intelligence agencies, and international organisations; covers data analysis, forensics, biometrics and imagery, surveillance, and incident response.
