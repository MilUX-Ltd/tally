# i-4p Consulting Ltd

> Kent-based consultancy helping companies grow their capability and customer base in defence, national security, risk management and disaster recovery.

## What they do

i-4p supports businesses seeking to enter or grow in the defence and national security market, providing industry knowledge, sector credibility and help building relationships with procurement decision-makers. The firm combines technology tools with a human network to match products and services to relevant programmes and procurement cycles.
