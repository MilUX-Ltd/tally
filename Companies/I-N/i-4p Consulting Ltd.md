# i-4p Consulting Ltd

> Kent-based consultancy helping companies grow their capability and customer base in defence, national security, risk management and disaster recovery.

## What they do

i-4p describes itself as a defence industry and critical national infrastructure consultancy, partnering with commercial and government organisations. The firm supports businesses seeking to enter or grow in the defence and national security market, whether taking first steps in selling into the sector, growing existing commercial relationships, or revising products and services to meet current and future industry demands. i-4p states a team of more than 40 experts, many of whom have held senior roles in the UK military and associated defence industries, all working as practitioners able to get directly involved in a client's sales effort.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence and CNI sales capability and effectiveness consulting** - advisory support to improve a client's sales capability into the defence and critical national infrastructure markets.
- **Product and service alignment review** - review of a client's offering against MOD requirements.
- **Outsourced or delegated business development** - business development delivered on a client's behalf.
- **Equipment and solution procurement support** - support navigating equipment and solution procurement.
- **Opportunity-matching platform** - a proprietary large language model that identifies relevant MOD opportunities for a client's business, combined with the firm's own human network and expertise to match products and services to relevant programmes and procurement cycles.
