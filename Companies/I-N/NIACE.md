# NIACE

> Northern Ireland Advanced Composites and Engineering Centre; a technology hub for research and development in advanced materials and engineering, serving aerospace, automotive and defence sectors.

## What they do

NIACE is a purpose-built R&D centre based at Belfast International Airport, providing collaborative research and technology development in advanced composites and engineering materials. It operates as a shared hub for member companies across aerospace, automotive and defence, offering access to specialist facilities and expertise not typically available to individual SMEs.
