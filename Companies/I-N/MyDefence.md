# MyDefence

> Counter-drone and counter-UAV systems manufacturer combining RF detection technology with rapid-response neutralisation capabilities for military and critical infrastructure protection.

## What they do

MyDefence develops next-generation counter-UAS solutions built around advanced radio frequency detection and electronic neutralisation. The company works directly with frontline forces to incorporate real-time operational insights into product design, producing systems certified to ISO 9001 and intended for military operations as well as the protection of airports, corporate facilities and high-security sites.

## Products and solutions

- **Counter-drone detection and neutralisation systems** - RF-based systems for detecting and defeating drone threats in military and critical infrastructure environments.
