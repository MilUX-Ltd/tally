# NSYM Ltd

> NeuroSymbolic AI systems designed to deliver explainable, adaptive decision-making in high-stakes operational environments.

## What they do

NSYM builds sovereign, operational-by-design NeuroSymbolic AI systems that model human-like reasoning, for critical defence, security and commercial use cases. Founded by the team behind Origami Labs, the company matures research-stage capabilities into operationally deployable products that offer explainable conclusions rather than opaque model outputs.
