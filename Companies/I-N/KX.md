# KX

> Real-time streaming analytics platform built on the kdb+ time-series database, deployed in defence for high-velocity sensor and operational data processing.

## What they do

KX develops KX Streaming Analytics, a high-performance platform built on the kdb+ time-series database, capable of ingesting and processing data at extreme speed from multiple sources and formats. Originally adopted across financial markets, the platform is also deployed in defence for real-time decision support, ISR data fusion, and operational analytics across high-data-volume environments.

## Products and solutions

- **KX Streaming Analytics (kdb+)** - real-time time-series database and analytics engine for high-velocity data ingestion and processing.
- **Data forensics and analysis tools** - capabilities for cyber security, data forensics, and operational intelligence.
