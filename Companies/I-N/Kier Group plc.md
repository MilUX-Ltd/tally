# Kier Group plc

> UK infrastructure services and construction company and strategic supplier to the UK Government, with a dedicated defence sector practice.

## What they do

Kier Group has around 100 years of experience delivering infrastructure and construction projects across education, healthcare, custodial, transport and defence. In defence, the company delivers and maintains built infrastructure for the UK Armed Forces, alongside broader work on roads, rail, water, energy and telecoms networks.
