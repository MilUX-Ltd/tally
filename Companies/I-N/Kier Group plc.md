# Kier Group plc

> UK infrastructure services and construction company and strategic supplier to the UK Government, with a dedicated defence sector practice.

## What they do

Kier Group plc is a UK infrastructure services, construction and property development group, headquartered in Salford and listed on the London Stock Exchange. It employs around 10,000 people and describes itself as a strategic supplier to the UK Government, with over 400 live projects across the UK. Kier operates a "develop, design, build and maintain" model across sectors including defence, education, healthcare, justice, nuclear, water, highways, rail and aviation. In defence, Kier delivers build, refurbishment and estate-optimisation projects for the Ministry of Defence across naval bases, air stations, army bases and defence research sites, and states it has delivered around 20 defence-sector projects since 2019, worth approximately £350 million.

## Products and solutions

- **Defence Estate Optimisation Portfolio** - Kier is one of seven contractors appointed to the Ministry of Defence's Strategic Alliance Contract, a 10-year, £5.1 billion programme, delivering a major project at RAF Cranwell and a regional package for the British Army estate in the south of England.
- **Single Living Accommodation and Technical Infrastructure Programme** - new-build accommodation and technical infrastructure for the Army, including a contract to rebuild Keogh Barracks, Aldershot, running to 2030.
- **Defence Infrastructure Organisation National Framework** - general build and refurbishment work under the DIO's national contracting framework.
- **Facilities management** - Kier states it maintains more than 5,500 buildings across its portfolio, alongside modern methods of construction and in-house design and engineering services.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Frameworks:** Defence Infrastructure Organisation National Framework; Ministry of Defence Strategic Alliance Contract (Defence Estate Optimisation Portfolio).
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001 (certified under Kier's Safety, Health and Environmental Management System); listed on the CIPS Corporate Ethics Register.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
