# Laser Optical Engineering Ltd

> UK manufacturer of bespoke laser-based imaging, detection and safety products, active in defence and industrial markets since 1996.

## What they do

Laser Optical Engineering develops and manufactures laser-based products and systems for imaging, non-destructive testing, explosive residue detection, and material processing. Services include laser shearography for design analysis, diffractive optical element (DOE) beam shaping, and laser safety testing and consultancy.

## Products and solutions

- **Laser Shearography System** - non-destructive testing using laser-based imaging for structural analysis.
- **Diffractive Optical Elements (DOE)** - custom beam shaping components for laser applications.
- **Explosive Residue Detection** - detection equipment for security and defence applications.
- **Laser Safety Products** - testing, certification, and consultancy services for laser safety compliance.
