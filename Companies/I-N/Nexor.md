# Nexor Limited

> UK cyber security company specialising in secure information exchange and cross-domain solutions.

## What they do

Nexor is a privately held UK cyber security company based in Nottingham, providing products and services to safeguard government, defence and critical national infrastructure systems. It specialises in secure information exchange, or cross-domain, solutions that enforce the controlled flow of data between separate security domains, applying data validation, sanitisation and policy enforcement so only authorised, clean information is exchanged. Nexor states more than 30 years of work alongside the UK MOD and NATO; the company traces its origins to X-Tel Services Limited, founded in 1989, later renamed Nexor.

## Products and solutions

- **Nexor GuarDiode** - secure, uni-directional data transfer to safely import and filter data from untrusted networks.
- **Nexor Guardian** - reduces risk of malware attacks and data loss across domains.
- **Nexor Data Diode** - hardware-enforced one-way transfer preventing data leaking back to an untrusted network.
- **Nexor Sentinel** - prevents information loss via email and reduces malware risk.
