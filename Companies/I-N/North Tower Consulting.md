# North Tower Consulting
> Cheltenham-based specialist technology and software consultancy delivering cyber security, cloud and digital services to National Security and Defence clients.

## What they do
North Tower Consulting provides professional expertise in cyber security, cloud computing, lifecycle management and customised software solutions. The firm delivers outcome-based digital and cyber services enabling National Security and Defence clients to manage risk, harness data and drive digital transformation. Its Cheltenham location places it within the UK's national security and intelligence cluster.
