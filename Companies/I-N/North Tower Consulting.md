# North Tower Consulting

> Cheltenham-based specialist technology and software consultancy delivering cyber security, information assurance and digital services to National Security and Defence clients.

## What they do

North Tower Consulting is a management consultancy based in Cheltenham, founded in 2019, providing expertise across the cyber technology sector. The company states its work spans software solutions, cyber security and lifecycle management services through to project delivery, describing itself as "executing exceptional cyber and digital transformations". Its key client base is government defence. The company's Cheltenham base places it within the UK's national security and intelligence cluster, and it maintains a strategic partnership with CDS Defence & Security for joint cyber security and information assurance delivery.

## Products and solutions

- **Cyber Security Specialists** - cyber risk assessments, strategic security planning, incident response and system hardening, cyber assurance, regulatory compliance, threat mitigation and cyber security leadership.
- **Information Assurance Professional Services** - governance, compliance, risk management and security assurance, including tailored frameworks and support for accreditation and audit processes.
- **Bespoke System Development and Support** - software development across the full lifecycle, from concept through to deployment.
- **Systems Engineering** - end-to-end systems engineering support.
- **Service Management, Business Analysis and Portfolio Management (P3M)** - delivery management, PMO services, operations management, planning and risk support, and agile coaching.
