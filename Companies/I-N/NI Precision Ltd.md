# NI Precision Ltd

> Northern Ireland precision CNC machining company serving aerospace and defence, with automated 5-axis manufacturing across two sites in Co. Armagh and Co. Antrim.

## What they do

NI Precision operates automated 5-axis and 6-axis CNC machining cells on a 24/7 basis from its sites in Portadown and Lisburn, Northern Ireland. It produces mechanical components and sub-components in aluminium alloys, titanium alloys, steel alloys, copper and engineering polymers, serving aerospace and defence customers.

## Products and solutions

- **5-Axis CNC Machining** - Automated production of complex aerospace and defence components using DMG Mori cells.
- **Welding and Moulding** - Arc welding and rotational moulding for defence-sector subcomponents.
