# Keltech IoE

> Irish infrastructure technology company engineering hybrid power-and-data network solutions for rapidly deployable defence, disaster-relief and telecoms infrastructure.

## What they do

KelTech IoE ("Internet of Everything") Networks, based in Dublin, designs hybrid dual distribution energy and data network (DDN) solutions that integrate power and passive fibre and copper data in a single deployable system. The product set targets rapidly deployable infrastructure use cases including expeditionary command nodes, temporary headquarters and field sites, as well as civilian 5G, smart city and microgrid rollouts, where running separate power and data cabling is impractical. The company positions its defence offer around applications spanning benign to non-benign environments and mobile/deployed through to fixed infrastructure, and also serves the disaster-relief and humanitarian sector.

## Products and solutions

- **DDN (Dual Distribution energy and data Network)** - hybrid cable and closure system integrating power and passive fibre/copper data distribution in a single run, supporting rapid deployment of communications infrastructure.
- **LOC (Label on a Cable)** - ruggedised NFC-tag asset identification and management platform giving real-time visibility of infrastructure assets via mobile app and cloud platform.
