# Keltech IoE
> UK company engineering hybrid power-and-data cables for rapidly deployable defence and industrial infrastructure.
## What they do
Keltech IoE ("Internet of Everything") designs single lightweight hybrid cables carrying both power and data in one run. The product targets rapidly deployable infrastructure use cases including temporary headquarters, expeditionary command nodes, and field sites where running separate power and data cabling is impractical. The defence offer is framed around speed and safety of deployment.
