# Landguard Systems Limited

> Designer and manufacturer of bespoke ISTAR radio systems and software defined radios for government, defence and law enforcement customers.

## What they do

Landguard Systems designs and manufactures custom radio systems that enable government, defence and law enforcement customers to track, locate and protect operational assets. The company describes its offer as intelligence, surveillance, target acquisition and reconnaissance (ISTAR) radio systems, alongside software defined radios (SDRs) built to open industry frameworks. It is based in Fareham, Hampshire, with a US subsidiary, Landguard Systems Inc, in Clearwater, Florida. Landguard became part of Chemring Group in 2025.

## Products and solutions

- **ISTAR radio systems** - custom radio systems providing tracking and locating capability across satellite, cellular and RF networks for government, defence and law enforcement customers.
- **Software defined radios (SDR)** - small size, low power SDRs built to open source frameworks such as OpenCPI and OpenVPX.
- **Support services** - after-sales support, 24/7 operational assistance and training.
