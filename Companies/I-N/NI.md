# NI

> Test and measurement technology company providing hardware and software platforms for aerospace, defence and government applications, now part of Emerson.

## What they do

NI (formerly National Instruments) supplies modular instrumentation, data acquisition hardware, and automated test software , including LabVIEW, PXI platforms and software-defined radio to customers in aerospace, defence and government. Its defence-relevant focus areas include radar test, electronic warfare, avionics communication buses and RF signal analysis.

## Products and solutions

- **PXI Platform** - High-performance modular test platform used for aerospace and defence system validation.
- **LabVIEW** - Graphical programming environment widely used in defence test and measurement workflows.
- **Software Defined Radios (FlexRIO)** - Reconfigurable RF instruments supporting radar and EW test applications.
- **VeriStand** - Hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) test development environment.
