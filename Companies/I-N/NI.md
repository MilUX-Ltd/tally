# NI

> Test and measurement technology company providing hardware and software platforms for aerospace, defence and government applications, now part of Emerson.

## What they do

NI (formerly National Instruments) supplies modular instrumentation, data acquisition hardware, and automated test software , including LabVIEW, PXI platforms and software-defined radio to customers in aerospace, defence and government. Its defence-relevant focus areas include radar test, electronic warfare, avionics communication buses and RF signal analysis. Its Electromagnetic Spectrum Operations materials state that its solutions support radar, communications and navigation system testing across radar, EW and SIGINT applications. NI's role is as a supplier of test, prototyping and validation equipment rather than of operational EW or radar effectors.

## Products and solutions

- **PXI Platform** - High-performance modular test platform used for aerospace and defence system validation.
- **LabVIEW** - Graphical programming environment widely used in defence test and measurement workflows.
- **Software Defined Radios (FlexRIO, USRP)** - Reconfigurable RF instruments supporting radar and EW test applications; USRP devices are marketed for real-time direction finding and spectrum monitoring.
- **VeriStand** - Hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) test development environment.
- **Radar and EW system test** - dedicated test solutions for radar warning receivers, jammers and the wider spectrum of electronic warfare systems.
