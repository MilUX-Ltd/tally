# NVIDIA Ltd

> GPU and AI computing platform company with a UK subsidiary based in Reading.

## What they do

NVIDIA designs GPU hardware and AI software platforms used across defence, simulation, autonomous systems, and data-intensive applications. Their platform stack spans chip-level compute through to enterprise AI deployment tools, including NVIDIA AI Enterprise and NVIDIA Omniverse for simulation and digital twin applications.

## Products and solutions

- **NVIDIA GPUs** - High-performance graphics and AI processing hardware used in simulation, ISR processing, and autonomous systems.
- **NVIDIA AI Enterprise** - Enterprise AI development and deployment platform.
- **NVIDIA Omniverse** - Real-time simulation and digital twin platform with applications in training, testing, and mission rehearsal.
- **NVIDIA NIM microservices** - Microservices for accelerated AI model development and inference.
