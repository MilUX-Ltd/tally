# Mills CNC Ltd

> UK importer and distributor of Doosan CNC machine tools, supplying aerospace, defence and industrial manufacturers.

## What they do

Mills CNC is the exclusive UK and Ireland agent for Doosan Infracore CNC machine tools, a relationship spanning 35 years. Operating from a technology campus in Leamington Spa, the company imports around 480 machine tools annually and supplies them across manufacturing sectors including aerospace and defence, supported by showroom, turnkey and machine storage facilities.
