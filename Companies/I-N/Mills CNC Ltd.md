# Mills CNC Ltd

> UK importer and distributor of Doosan CNC machine tools, supplying aerospace, defence and industrial manufacturers.

## What they do

Mills CNC is the exclusive UK and Ireland agent for Doosan Infracore (DN Solutions) CNC machine tools, a relationship spanning 35 years. Operating from a technology campus in Leamington Spa, the company imports around 450 to 480 machine tools annually and supplies them across manufacturing sectors including aerospace and defence, supported by showroom, turnkey and machine storage facilities. The installed UK and Ireland base exceeds 4,500 machines.

## Products and solutions

- **Lynx and Puma lathe ranges** - CNC turning centres, including vertical turning models.
- **MX and SMX mill-turn ranges** - multi-tasking machining centres, including twin-turret and twin-spindle models.
- **DVF series** - 5-axis machining centres with Fanuc or Siemens controls.
- **NHP horizontal machining centres** - horizontal machining with linear pallet system options.
- **SYNERGi automation** - automation systems supplied alongside machine tools.
- **Applications engineering and training** - support services delivered alongside machine sales from the Leamington Spa technology campus.
