# Nasmyth Group Ltd

> Group of precision engineering businesses delivering design, manufacturing, assembly and product support services across mechanical and electro-mechanical engineering for aerospace and defence.

## What they do

Nasmyth Group offers customers an integrated solution spanning machining, welding, coatings and non-destructive testing, with in-house capability across multiple specialist sites in the UK. The group holds NADCAP accreditations across chemical processing, conventional testing, heat treatment and NDT alongside ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certification.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, NADCAP CP, NADCAP CT, NADCAP Heat Treatment, NADCAP NDT
