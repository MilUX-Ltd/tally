# Nasmyth Group Ltd

> Group of precision engineering businesses delivering design, manufacturing, assembly and product support services across mechanical and electro-mechanical engineering for aerospace, defence and marine.

## What they do

Nasmyth Group describes itself as a full-capability supplier of precision engineering addressing requirements for global aerospace, defence, marine, medical, instrumentation and specialised automotive markets. It brings together a group of customer-orientated precision engineering and metal treatment businesses to offer a single-source solution across design, manufacture, assembly, testing and product support. The group is headquartered in Coventry and employs over 500 people across sites in the UK, Europe and Asia Pacific.

## Products and solutions

- **Complex machining** - precision machining of high-performance metals, including multi-axis milling, turning and mill turning, and CNC grinding, in titanium, aluminium, nimonics and superalloys.
- **Fabrication and welding** - complex sheet metal, welded and machined assemblies and structures, including hydraulic press forming, fusion and resistance welding, and precision fabricated structures up to five tonnes.
- **System integration** - electro-mechanical systems, cable and harness assembly, PCB assembly and electronic test, including assemblies for flight simulator seat installations and control unit repair and overhaul.
- **Metal treatments** - surface finishing and chemical and thermal treatments, including anodising, alkaline zinc, chromic passivation, shot peening and thermal barrier coating.
- **Design, reverse engineering and MRO** - CAD/CAM-supported design and reverse engineering, non-destructive testing, and maintenance, repair and overhaul including AOG support and FAR/EASA Part 145 compliant repairs.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Midlands Aerospace Alliance
- **Accreditations:** ISO 9001, AS9100 Rev C, ISO 14001, EASA/FAR Part 145, CAA Parts 145 and 21, NADCAP (chemical processing, conventional testing, heat treatment, NDT)
