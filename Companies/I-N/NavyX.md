# NavyX

> The Royal Navy's Autonomy and Lethality Accelerator, developing and testing autonomous and novel technology for naval operations.

## What they do

NavyX develops, tests, and accelerates autonomous and lethality-focused technologies on behalf of the Royal Navy. It acts as a challenge setter and experimentation node, working with industry and academia to bring emerging technology into naval use.
