# NightSearcher Ltd

> British designer and manufacturer of portable rechargeable LED lighting and portable power, supplied to the armed forces among other professional users.

## What they do

NightSearcher designs and manufactures premium rechargeable and portable LED lighting and portable power solutions. Its floodlights, worklights, searchlights, flashlights, head torches and inspection lamps are used by the armed forces, police, fire and rescue, construction, oil and gas, and rail sectors worldwide. The company also produces ATEX-certified intrinsically safe lighting and battery-powered portable power stations as an alternative to fuel generators.

## Products and solutions

- **StratoStar** - rechargeable multi-directional tower floodlight with four light-heads and 360 degree adjustable illumination, marketed for sectors including military operations.
- **Sigma ATEX range** - intrinsically safe flashlights, head torches, hand lamps and floodlights for hazardous environments.
- **GenZero** - rechargeable portable power stations for off-grid and remote power.
- **Flashlights, head torches and searchlights** - rechargeable LED hand-held and head-worn lighting.
