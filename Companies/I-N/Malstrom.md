# Malstrom

> Specialist applied data science company providing DV-cleared analytics, machine learning and geospatial data engineering to UK defence and government.

## What they do

Malstrom is a defence-focused applied data science company providing DV-cleared embedded staff, advanced analytics, custom tool development, data engineering and applied research. The team brings experience operating in sensitive environments, with specialisms in applied machine learning, big data systems and complex defence geospatial data.

## Products and solutions

- **DV-cleared embedded staff** - specialist personnel embedded with defence and government customers in sensitive environments.
- **Advanced analytics and custom tool development** - bespoke analytical tools and applied machine learning for defence data problems.
- **Data engineering** - big data systems and pipeline work supporting defence and government customers.
- **Applied research** - research and development in defence geospatial data and related fields.
