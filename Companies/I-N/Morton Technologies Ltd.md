# Morton Technologies Ltd

> Defence and security software company specialising in simulation, synthetic training environments, EM modelling and AI-enabled decision support.

## What they do

Morton Technologies develops software solutions for the defence and security markets, with particular focus on simulation and training, electromagnetic spectrum modelling, systems engineering and research and innovation. The company holds a portfolio of work across defence and aeronautics, including AI-driven decision support tools and virtual and augmented reality training systems.

## Products and solutions

- **EIDOS** - Electromagnetic Informed Decision Support system combining AI, data analytics and EM spectrum modelling.
- **Simulation and Training** - Virtual, mixed reality and cloud-based training environments for defence and aeronautics.
