# Naval Solutions Ltd

> A systems engineering consultancy specialising in naval technology solutions for military and commercial applications.

## What they do

Naval Solutions Ltd describes itself as an engineering consultancy delivering engineering and technology solutions across the defence, nuclear and commercial maritime sectors, with a stated focus on marine systems and naval architecture. It supports the full design lifetime of naval ships and submarines, from initial concept development and design assessment through to systems integration, testing, in-service support and disposal.

## Products and solutions

- **Engineering services** - design, integration and support of complex marine systems, including propulsion, power generation and distribution, platform systems and environmental control.
- **Safety and regulatory compliance** - safety case development and regulatory assurance for naval and nuclear platforms.
- **Programme management** - lifecycle and delivery support for naval and maritime programmes.
- **Specialist consultancy** - naval architecture, technical assurance and peer review.
- **Published case studies** - Type 31 Frigate certification-first compliance support; Dreadnought Class submarine Whole Boat Safety Case work; dock integration de-risking at HM Naval Base Clyde; engineering calculations and design substantiation for a nuclear berthing facility supporting Astute and Trafalgar Class submarines.
