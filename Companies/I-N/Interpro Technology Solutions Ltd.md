# Interpro Technology Solutions Ltd

> Defence-focused IT support and managed services company, delivering secure IT solutions through security-cleared engineers to the UK defence sector.

## What they do

Interpro Technology Solutions provides IT support, systems integration, and cyber security services exclusively to the UK defence sector. Their engineers and consultants hold security clearances, and the company focuses on safeguarding sensitive data, maintaining operational integrity, and meeting defence compliance requirements.
