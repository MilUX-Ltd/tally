# Interpro Technology Solutions Ltd

> Defence-focused IT support and managed services company, delivering secure IT solutions through security-cleared engineers to the UK defence sector.

## What they do

Interpro Technology Solutions is a Fareham-based managed IT services provider founded in 2005, delivering IT support, systems integration, and cyber security services to small and medium businesses generally, alongside a dedicated defence practice. Its defence-facing engineers and consultants hold BPSS and SC security clearances, and the company states it has delivered IT support to organisations including QinetiQ and Aquila Air Traffic Management Services, focusing on safeguarding sensitive data, maintaining operational integrity, and meeting defence compliance requirements.

## Products and solutions

- **Managed IT support** - fully managed and monitored IT support with service-level agreements, alongside pay-as-you-go support and IT health checks.
- **Defence IT support** - a dedicated service line covering secure communications, defence-aligned cloud, and cyber protection for defence contractors and organisations in the defence supply chain, including secure hosted telephony, Microsoft 365 for Defence, secure cloud backups, and high-speed defence connectivity.
- **Cyber security services** - managed firewalls and threat filtering, antivirus and endpoint protection, email security and phishing protection, ransomware defence, vulnerability scanning, risk management and security audits, cloud security, and multi-factor authentication implementation, plus support for clients pursuing Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus certification.
- **Cloud and telecoms** - cloud computing and hosted systems, dedicated servers, cloud backup, business email hosting, hosted VoIP phone systems, and business broadband.
