# Innovation DB and Forum Ltd

> Technology commercialisation platform linking academic and research innovation to industry, with a classified-contract dataset developed for UK Army Future Capability Command.

## What they do

Innovation DB describes itself as maintaining the world's largest database of novel, commercially useful technologies created by academic institutions, used by an international client base of multinational corporations, venture capital firms, innovative SMEs, and patent and innovation consultancies. Its database organises content into Offers, Projects, Patents and Products, drawing on research output from around 5,000 academic institutions worldwide. For defence clients, the company states it is working under a classified contract for the UK Army Future Capability Command, using its own AI to build an enhanced dataset focused on defence technology scouting.

## Products and solutions

- **Innovation DB database** - core subscription platform covering Offers, Projects, Patents and Products from academic and research institutions worldwide, used to identify licensable and partnerable technologies.
- **Aigis defence dataset** - an enhanced, defence-focused dataset developed under a classified contract for the UK Army Future Capability Command, using the company's in-house AI to multiply its technology-scouting capability.
- **Market and technology reports** - published reports on innovation-driven markets and emerging technology sectors (for example digital healthcare, graphene, imaging, and plastic electronics), available to subscribers.
- **Innovation Forum R&D community** - a community and LinkedIn group connecting subscribers with peers and collaboration partners across corporates, SMEs and universities.
