# Innovation DB and Forum Ltd

> Surrey-based technology commercialisation platform linking technology owners to partners, with active work for UK MOD and Dstl on military-grade AI and data intelligence.

## What they do

Innovation DB operates what it describes as the world's largest database of technologies available for licence, drawing on content from approximately 5,000 academic institutions. In partnership with UK MOD Army Futures Command and Dstl, the company is developing military-grade neural-network AI and machine learning code for a global data intelligence system, which feeds back into its core commercialisation product.
