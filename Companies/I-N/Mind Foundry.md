# Mind Foundry

> Oxford-spinout applied machine learning company building responsible AI for defence and national security.

## What they do

Mind Foundry develops responsible AI and machine learning solutions for high-stakes applications in defence and national security. It was spun out of the University of Oxford and turns complex, multi-modal sensor data into trusted insight, supporting human operators while preserving human autonomy in mission-critical environments. The company is based in Oxford.

## Products and solutions

- **Sentry: Sensor Fusion** - real-time fusion of data from multiple sensors into a single evolving picture for situational awareness, making existing hardware and command-and-control software smarter.
- **Nightingale: ASW** - acoustic software for anti-submarine warfare, delivering automated tonal analysis and classification to turn maritime sound into actionable intelligence.
- **Continuous Metalearning** - platform approach in which AI adapts its own learning to real-world change.
