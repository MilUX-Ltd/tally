# NiTech Solutions Ltd

> Developer of dual-use continuous manufacturing technology for chemical and defence applications, supported by DASA.

## What they do

NiTech Solutions, based in Edinburgh, develops patent-protected continuous manufacturing technology for chemical reaction and crystallisation processes, replacing traditional batch processing. Its core technology superimposes an oscillatory flow on the net flow through jacketed pipework, combining heat and mass transfer with uniform mixing, so kinetic reaction times proven at laboratory scale can be reproduced at pilot and production scale. The company describes its process solutions as dual-use, applicable to both civil chemical manufacturing and defence-related energetics and propellant production. Stated application areas include speciality and fine chemicals, pharmaceuticals, battery materials, food and drink, and energetics and propellants.

## Products and solutions

- **Continuous Oscillatory Baffled Reactor (COBR)** - continuous-flow reactor technology for chemical reaction processes.
- **Continuous Oscillatory Baffled Crystalliser (COBC)** - continuous-flow crystallisation technology, offered across a range of pipe diameters.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Programmes:** DASA-funded, supporting demonstration of the technology in the energetics sector.
