# NiTech Solutions Ltd

> Developer of dual-use continuous manufacturing technology for chemical and defence applications, supported by DASA.

## What they do

NiTech Solutions develops patent-protected continuous process-based manufacturing technology operating across a range of reaction and crystallisation processes. The technology has been validated commercially and received DASA funding to enable defence applications, with dual-use potential across speciality chemicals and defence manufacturing.
