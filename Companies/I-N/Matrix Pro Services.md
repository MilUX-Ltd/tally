# Matrix Pro Services

> Defence-focused wargaming, modelling and simulation support company based in Surrey, sister organisation to Matrix Pro Sims.

## What they do

Matrix Pro Services provides wargaming support services including operational analysis, scenario planning, logistics consultancy, and modelling and simulation specialists. The company primarily supports Matrix Pro Sims' Command Pro Edition — a whole-world, 3D physics-based wargame used across the US DoD, NATO partners and Western armed forces — but lends expertise to other M&S software environments for most nations.

## Products and solutions

- **Wargaming support services** - operational analysis, scenario planning, exercise logistics and facilitation for professional serious wargames.
- **Modelling and simulation consultancy** - specialist support across simulation environments for training, operational planning and analysis.
