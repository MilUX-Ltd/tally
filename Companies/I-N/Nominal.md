# Nominal

> US software company building a unified test and mission data platform for defence, aerospace and other complex hardware programmes.

## What they do

Nominal builds a unified software stack for hardware engineering teams, letting them test and iterate on physical systems as quickly as software teams ship code. Its platform captures, organises, monitors, visualises and securely shares high-rate test and operational data from complex hardware including aircraft, hypersonics, satellites and autonomous vehicles. The company has roots in aerospace and defence, founded by engineers from Anduril, Lockheed Martin and Palantir, and is headquartered in Los Angeles with offices including London.

## Products and solutions

- **Nominal Core** - secure cloud workbench for managing, monitoring and analysing high-rate sensor data, logs, video, simulation results and procedures in one time-aligned, collaborative view, with trend analysis, anomaly detection and synchronised post-test replay.
- **Nominal Connect** - Python-first edge platform for building custom hardware-in-the-loop applications, handling sensor streams at kilohertz rates and streaming large volumes of test and video data from a test bench into Nominal.
