# National Nuclear Laboratory

> The UK's national laboratory for nuclear science and technology, serving defence, civil nuclear, and homeland security customers.

## What they do

NNL covers the complete nuclear fuel cycle, from fuel manufacture and power generation through to reprocessing, waste treatment, and disposal, including defence applications and homeland security. The laboratory provides commercial technologies and trusted technical advice to government, supported by a range of specialist facilities and links with international research organisations and academia.
