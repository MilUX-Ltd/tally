# Integrate Systems Consulting Ltd

> Technical consultancy in systems engineering and marine engineering, supporting defence, energy, and infrastructure clients.

## What they do

Integrate Systems Consulting provides technical consultancy in systems engineering and marine engineering, delivering requirements definition, acceptance work, and general engineering advisory to clients across defence, energy, and infrastructure. The company states it applies systems engineering across military and civil maritime platforms, marine infrastructure, air platforms and equipment, transport infrastructure, energy, military and civil land systems, and nuclear power and propulsion. It was started in 2023 and is built around a principal consultant with a network of affiliated systems engineers for larger projects.

## Products and solutions

- **Systems engineering** - requirements elicitation, development and management, Systems Engineering Management Plans, acceptance planning, and verification and validation across user, system, and infrastructure requirement sets.
- **Marine engineering and maritime consultancy** - military and civil marine platforms and equipment, marine infrastructure, Integrated Full Electric Propulsion and traditional power and propulsion systems, and ship and equipment test, acceptance and trials planning.
- **Bid support** - bid planning and estimation, proposal writing, contract estimation, and management of large and complex bids.
