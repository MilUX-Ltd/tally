# Metrea Aerospace

> Effects-as-a-Service solutions for defence customers across air, electromagnetic and digital domains.

## What they do

Metrea provides Effects-as-a-Service solutions to defence partners, delivering capabilities as commercially owned and operated managed services. It operates across the air domain (airborne ISR, air-to-air refuelling, special mission aviation and training), the electromagnetic and cyber domain (SIGINT and electronic warfare training, secure communications) and the digital and synthetic domain (military simulation, space domain awareness and data fusion). It maintains UK operations in London, Harwell and Lincoln.

## Products and solutions

- **Air domain services** - airborne ISR, air-to-air refuelling, special mission aviation and rotary wing training.
- **Electromagnetic and cyber** - SIGINT and EW training, secure communications and cyber operations support.
- **Digital and synthetic** - military simulation (NOR, TACNOR, OPNOR), space domain awareness and data fusion.
