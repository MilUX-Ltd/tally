# LiveU UK Ltd

> UK entity of LiveU, a developer of live video transmission and streaming solutions for unmanned aerial and ground vehicles used in defence, public safety and broadcasting.

## What they do

LiveU develops bonded cellular and satellite video transmission technology enabling live, low-latency video streaming from UAVs, drones and ground vehicles to any internet-connected device. The company serves government departments, public safety agencies and defence customers globally, with its UK entity registered in Nuneaton, Warwickshire.

## Products and solutions

- **UAV and drone video streaming** - live, low-latency video feed from unmanned aerial platforms using bonded multi-network transmission.
- **Defence situational awareness streaming** - mission-critical video transmission for policing, military and public safety operations.
