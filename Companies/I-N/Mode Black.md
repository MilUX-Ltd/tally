# Mode Black
> UK SME developing covert Free Space Optics communications technology for the defence market, immune to Electronic Warfare.

## What they do
Mode Black produces a covert communications product based on Free Space Optics, designed to be compact, adaptable and immune to Electronic Warfare. The company is positioned for both UK defence and Critical National Infrastructure applications, with longer-term commercial ambitions across NATO countries. It spun out of Optect Ltd in October 2022 and closed an angel investment round in 2024.

## Products and solutions
- **FSO covert comms system** - Free Space Optics communications device configured for defence and CNI use, offering EW-immune, covert data transmission.
