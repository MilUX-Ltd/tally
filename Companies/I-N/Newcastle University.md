# Newcastle University

> Research-intensive university with defence-relevant expertise in naval architecture, marine engineering, aerospace engineering and intelligent sensing.

## What they do

Newcastle University's School of Engineering covers naval architecture and marine engineering, aerospace engineering, electrical and electronic engineering, and geospatial engineering, among other disciplines. It undertakes research and industry partnerships across maritime, mobility, microsystems and sensing, with applications relevant to defence.
