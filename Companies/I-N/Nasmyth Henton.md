# Nasmyth Henton

> Precision ring component manufacturer serving aero engine, marine engine and satellite programmes, part of the Nasmyth Group.

## What they do

Nasmyth Henton describes itself as a leading supplier to the aero engine, marine and satellite industries, manufacturing ring-machined components. Based in Hinckley, Leicestershire, and part of the Nasmyth Group since 2008, it specialises in the machining and fine grinding of large, complex, sectioned ring and prismatic components, typically 200mm to 1.8-2m in diameter, working with customers from design and material sourcing through to manufacture, assembly and test.

## Products and solutions

- **Ring machined components** - compressor seals, compressor casings, satellite mounting rings, adapter flanges and bearing housings, produced on a five-axis CNC machining facility.
- **Multi-axis milling and turning** - three, four and five-axis CNC milling and turning, including multi-spindle lathes, in titanium, nimonic, stellite, aluminium and other high-temperature alloys.
- **CNC grinding and non-destructive testing** - precision grinding of machined components, plasma coatings, lapping and honing, plus in-house magnetic particle and penetrant inspection.
- **Marine and submarine systems assembly and test** - a specialist assembly and test facility for systems used in marine and submarine environments.
