# Nasmyth Henton

> Precision ring component manufacturer serving aero engine, marine engine and satellite programmes, part of the Nasmyth Group.

## What they do

Nasmyth Henton manufactures ring-machined components from 200mm to 1.8m diameter in titanium, nimonic, stellite and lightweight aluminium, working closely with customers from design and material sourcing through to manufacture, assembly and test. The company operates a specialist assembly and test facility for systems used in marine and submarine environments.
