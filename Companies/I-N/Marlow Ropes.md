# Marlow Ropes

> British rope manufacturer with 200 years of history, specialising in defence and special forces products used in fast-rope insertion, extraction, and harsh-environment operations worldwide.

## What they do

Marlow Ropes designs and manufactures specialist ropes and cordage for defence, special forces, and safety applications, exporting to customers in some of the world's most demanding environments. Its product range spans fast-rope insertion and extraction systems, dynamic and static ropes, and platform and equipment structures. The company is known for British-designed engineering with a track record of fielded use in combat operations.

## Products and solutions

- **Fast Rope insertion and extraction series** - pioneering fast-rope systems used by special forces and airborne units for tactical insertion and extraction from aircraft.
- **Dynamic and static ropes** - specialist ropes designed for PPE, safety, and load-bearing applications in defence and industrial environments.
