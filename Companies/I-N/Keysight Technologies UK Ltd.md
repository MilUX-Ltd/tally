# Keysight Technologies UK Ltd

> UK entity of Keysight Technologies, a global designer and manufacturer of electronic test and measurement equipment and systems.

## What they do

Keysight Technologies designs, manufactures and supplies electronic design and test equipment, systems, software and associated calibration and repair services. The parent company, Keysight Technologies Inc., is listed on the NYSE and headquartered in Santa Rosa, California; Keysight Technologies UK Ltd is its UK operating entity, with sites including Winnersh, Fleet, Telford and Edinburgh. The company serves aerospace and defence, communications, semiconductor and automotive and energy sectors, and states its aim in defence is to support electromagnetic spectrum operations, secure communications, space and satellite, and radar programmes.

## Products and solutions

- **Oscilloscopes** - including the Infiniium and Pro XR8 series, used across aerospace, defence and RF applications.
- **Signal and spectrum analysers** - including the UXA and PXA analysers, for radar, electronic warfare and wideband signal analysis.
- **Vector and agile signal generators** - including the M9484C VXG and UXG, for electronic warfare threat and radar scenario simulation.
- **FieldFox handheld analysers** - portable RF and microwave test equipment for field use, spectrum monitoring and interference-finding.
- **Vector network analysers** - the PNA-X and ENA-X ranges, for component and satellite-link characterisation.
- **EW Advanced Simulation Platform (EWASP)** - scalable electronic warfare test environments for training, reprogramming and research and development.
- **PathWave software suite** - design and test software, including SystemVue and Advanced Design System, for RF, radar and phased-array systems.
- **Aerospace and defence automated test equipment (ATE)** - systems and services for test automation across aerospace and defence programmes.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** SC21 Silver Award (ADS Group's Supply Chains for the 21st Century programme, held since 2013); ISO 27001:2013; ISO 27701:2019; Cyber Essentials Plus; ISO 17025 (calibration and testing).
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
