# North Product Design Ltd

> Product design and development consultancy taking complex hardware briefs from concept through to manufacture for defence primes and technology companies.

## What they do

North Product Design Ltd works with defence primes, technology companies and manufacturers who need to turn proven software, IP or component specifications into manufacturable, field-deployable physical products. Based in Preston, Lancashire, the company operates in demanding, regulated environments and supports clients from concept through daily iteration to production.

## Products and solutions

- **Design and Prototyping Services** - end-to-end hardware design and rapid prototyping for defence and regulated applications.
- **Engineering Consultancy and Design** - engineering consultancy covering concept, development and manufacture readiness.
