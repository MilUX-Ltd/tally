# Kraken Robotics Services UK Ltd

> Marine technology company producing advanced underwater sensors, synthetic aperture sonar systems, and robotic platforms for defence and scientific applications.

## What they do

Kraken Robotics designs and manufactures ultra-high-resolution Synthetic Aperture Sonar (SAS) systems for seabed imaging, sub-seabed survey tools, and precision 3D LiDAR sensors. They also supply high-performance subsea batteries for autonomous underwater vehicles and remotely operated vehicles, supporting defence, commercial, and scientific operators.

## Products and solutions

- **Synthetic Aperture Sonar (SAS)** - ultra-high-resolution seabed imaging for mine countermeasures and ISR.
- **Sub-seabed survey tools** - precision instruments for sub-surface mapping.
- **3D LiDAR sensors** - precision optical sensing for underwater environments.
- **Subsea battery systems** - high-performance power solutions for AUVs and ROVs.
