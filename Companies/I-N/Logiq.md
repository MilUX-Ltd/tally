# Logiq
> NCSC Assured UK cyber security consultancy and managed services provider serving defence, government, and highly regulated sectors from its Bristol base.
## What they do
Logiq Consulting Limited is a Bristol-based cyber security consultancy and SaaS managed services provider. It helps defence, government, and regulated organisations protect critical assets through security consultancy and a managed service offering. Teams are SC-cleared; approximately 60% hold DV clearance. The company holds NCSC Assurance and is active in the defence sector, with a presence at DSEI 2025.
## Products and solutions
- **Cyber security consultancy** - Risk assessment and security advisory for defence and government programmes.
- **Managed security service** - Assured managed service for ongoing protection of critical information assets.
## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships
- **Accreditations:** NCSC Assured cyber security and digital consultancy; Secure by Design.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group.
