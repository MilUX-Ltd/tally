# Infoblox UK Ltd

> DDI (DNS, DHCP, IP address management) and cloud-native network security specialist with a UK entity serving defence and government.

## What they do

Infoblox is a 20-year-plus leader in DDI infrastructure, providing DNS, DHCP, and IP address management at scale. Its BloxOne Threat Defence platform is a cloud-native SaaS security solution that uses DNS to detect and block threats across on-premise and hybrid environments.

## Products and solutions

- **BloxOne Threat Defence** - Cloud-native SaaS network security platform using DNS intelligence for threat detection and response.
- **DDI** - Integrated DNS, DHCP, and IP address management for enterprise and government networks.
