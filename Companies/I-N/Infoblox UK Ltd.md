# Infoblox UK Ltd

> DDI (DNS, DHCP, IP address management) and cloud-native network security specialist with a UK entity serving defence and government.

## What they do

Infoblox is a 20-year-plus leader in DDI infrastructure, providing DNS, DHCP, and IP address management at scale. Its BloxOne Threat Defence platform is a cloud-native SaaS security solution that uses DNS intelligence, firewalling and behavioural analytics to detect and block threats such as malware, phishing and data exfiltration at the DNS layer, across on-premise and hybrid environments, and integrates with SIEM, SOAR and SOC tools to enrich alerts with DNS, network and device context.

## Products and solutions

- **BloxOne Threat Defence** - cloud-native SaaS network security platform using DNS intelligence for threat detection and response, with add-ons including SOC Insights, Log Export, Lookalike Domain Monitoring and the Dossier threat investigation portal.
- **DDI** - integrated DNS, DHCP, and IP address management for enterprise and government networks.
- **TIDE (Threat Intelligence Data Exchange)** - machine-readable threat intelligence data aggregation and distribution service.
