# Juma Communications Ltd

> UK manufacturer and distributor of professional radio communications accessories for military, police and emergency services.

## What they do

Juma Communications manufactures and distributes audio accessories for professional radio users, including earpieces, headsets, throat microphones, speaker microphones and covert communications equipment. Products cover military and tactical, semi-covert, and emergency services applications, with supply to UK police forces and distribution to customers worldwide through a reseller network.

## Products and solutions

- **Military and tactical audio** - throat microphones, tactical headsets and push-to-talk units for military use.
- **Covert communications** - covert earpiece hubs and accessories for law enforcement and security roles.
- **Headsets and speaker mics** - professional radio accessories for police, industry and emergency services.
