# Kinected Solutions Ltd

> Defence and security SME providing business development, bid capture and technical solutions to UK and international markets.

## What they do

Kinected Solutions supports defence and security primes and SMEs through the enable, qualify and capture phases of business development, drawing on experience with major NATO partners including the UK and US, as well as export markets across Africa, the Middle East and Europe. The firm also provides programme delivery support through specialist training and technical services.
