# Marshall Land Systems

> Designer and manufacturer of deployable containerised infrastructure and vehicle-mounted systems for defence, security and humanitarian operations.

## What they do

Marshall Land Systems specialises in deployable operational infrastructure delivered through standardised containerised solutions, integration of sub-systems, and managed support services. The company serves defence, renewable energy, humanitarian relief and security customers worldwide, with capability spanning command and control, medical, surveillance and ground support equipment.

## Products and solutions

- **Containerised solutions** - standardised deployable containers configured for C2, medical, laboratory and operational support roles.
- **Combat and specialist vehicles** - integration of systems onto APC, patrol and command vehicle platforms.
- **Managed Support Services** - engineering consultancy and asset support for Marshall and third-party equipment.
