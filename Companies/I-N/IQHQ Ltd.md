# IQHQ Ltd

> High-technology radio communications company developing advanced RF systems for the UK armed forces.

## What they do

IQHQ researches, develops, and produces advanced radio communications technology for military use, based at Malvern Hills Science Park. Founded in 2009, the company supports the communications, intelligence, surveillance and security markets with bespoke waveforms on custom-designed hardware, and states expertise in military radio communication systems, waveform design and development, FPGA platforms, digital signal processing, and RF and microwave engineering up to 60 GHz.

## Products and solutions

- **Software-defined radio platforms** - Custom radio communication and waveform development for military and civilian users, including COFDM waveform design.
- **High-resilience receivers** - Developed with Centre for Defence Enterprise funding, for strategic and critical military infrastructure such as backbone networks and ISR datalinks, designed for high immunity to jamming and interference.
