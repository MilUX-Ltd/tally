# IQHQ Ltd

> High-technology radio communications company developing advanced RF systems for the UK armed forces.

## What they do

IQHQ researches, develops, and produces advanced radio communications technology for military use, based at Malvern Hills Science Park. Founded in 2009, the company has built a body of intellectual property and product prototypes in radio technology and holds emerging export plans.
