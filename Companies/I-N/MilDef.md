# MilDef Group AB

> Rugged IT systems integrator and full-spectrum provider for defence, government and critical infrastructure.

## What they do

MilDef is a systems integrator and full-spectrum provider specialising in rugged IT for military, government and critical infrastructure sectors. Since 1997 it has designed, tested, qualified, manufactured, sold and supported rugged computing products for military applications in harsh environments. It supplies hardware, software and services across multiple countries including Sweden, Norway, Finland, Denmark, the United Kingdom, Germany, Switzerland, the United States and Australia. MilDef Group AB has been listed on Nasdaq Stockholm since 2021.

## Products and solutions

- **Rugged computers and electronics** - small form factor computers, mobile computers, displays, panel PCs and special electronics for the harshest conditions.
- **19"/2 rugged IT platform** - rack-mounted rugged computing supporting modular defence systems.
- **Command and Control (C2) software** - C2 systems delivered to defence customers including the Swedish Navy.
- **Integration services** - system design and implementation alongside hardware delivery.
