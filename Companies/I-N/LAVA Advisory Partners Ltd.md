# LAVA Advisory Partners Ltd

> London-based M&A advisory firm with defence and aerospace sector experience, providing buy-side, sell-side and pure advisory services across the lower-mid-market.

## What they do

LAVA Advisory Partners is a London-based mergers and acquisitions advisory firm founded in 2020, working across the lower-mid-market on buy-side, sell-side, financing and standalone advisory mandates. It states extensive defence and aerospace experience among its sector specialisms, alongside education, energy, construction and technology. LAVA is structured as an Employee Ownership Trust and is a certified B Corp.

## Products and solutions

- **Buy-side and sell-side M&A advisory** - mandates for defence and aerospace sector clients, among others, covering deal origination through to completion.
- **Financing and standalone advisory** - support raising investment and standalone strategic advice outside a live transaction.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Memberships:** ADS Group
- **Accreditations:** Cyber Essentials Plus, Certified B Corporation
