# M-CNC Ltd

> AS9100 and Cyber Essentials Plus certified CNC precision machining company supplying safety-critical and mission-critical components to aerospace, defence, and related industries.

## What they do

M-CNC specialises in 2, 3, 4, 5, and 9-axis high-precision CNC machining in heat-resistant alloys including titanium, Inconel, Hastelloy, Nitronic 60, stainless steel grades, aluminium alloys, and PEEK. The company machines safety-critical and mission-critical components for aerospace and defence programmes, alongside rail, Formula 1, and oil and gas customers.

## Products and solutions

- **High-precision CNC machining** - Multi-axis machining in titanium, Inconel, and other exotic alloys for aerospace and defence components.
