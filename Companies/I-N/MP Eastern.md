# MP Eastern

> Metal finishing specialist providing anodising, zinc plating, zinc nickel and related surface treatment processes for aerospace, defence and precision engineering customers.

## What they do

MP Eastern, trading since 1982 and based in Lowestoft, provides anodising and electroplating solutions to customers in oil and gas, aerospace and defence, automotive and motorsport, packaging machinery and precision engineering, across the UK and Europe. The company states it combines technical knowledge with operational experience to address surface engineering requirements for demanding environments.

## Products and solutions

- **Anodising** - including coloured, hard, PTFE, sulphuric and titanium anodising.
- **Zinc and zinc-nickel plating** - including passivation and trivalent finishes, and corrosion- and wear-resistant zinc-nickel alloy coating.
- **Chromate conversion coating** (Alocrom 1000, Alocrom 1200, Iridite) - for corrosion resistance and paint pre-treatment.
- **Nickel and copper plating** - bright and electroless nickel, and cyanide copper plating.
- **Chemical blacking and passivation** - on steel and stainless steel.
- **Phosphating** - zinc and manganese variants.
- **Hydrogen de-embrittlement and stress relieving**, and **salt spray testing**.

## Frameworks, accreditations and memberships

- **Accreditations:** AS9100, ISO 9001, ISO 14001.
- **Memberships:** ADS Group, Surface Engineering Association.
