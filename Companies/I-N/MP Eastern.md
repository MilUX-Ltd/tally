# MP Eastern

> Metal finishing specialist providing anodising, zinc plating, zinc nickel and related surface treatment processes for aerospace, defence and precision engineering customers.

## What they do

MP Eastern offers a range of metal finishing treatments including anodising, zinc plating, Surtec 650, zinc nickel and dual-finishing with masking and selective processes. The company holds AS9100 accreditation and serves customers across aerospace, defence, oil and gas, subsea engineering and precision engineering industries.
