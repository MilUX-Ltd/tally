# Multiple Access Communications Ltd

> Radio and wireless systems consultancy with deep expertise in military communications, counter-terrorism interception, and spectrum engineering across GSM through to 5G and satellite.

## What they do

MAC Ltd has specialised in radio frequency consultancy since 1986, working across military and government communications programmes including TETRA, satellite systems, and successive cellular generations from 2G to 5G. The company provides independent consultancy in wireless system specification, design and implementation, with capabilities spanning C2 communications, counter-terrorism intelligence and interception, simulation and modelling, and space systems.
